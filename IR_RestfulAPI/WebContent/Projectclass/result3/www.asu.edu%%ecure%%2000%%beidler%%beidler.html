Susan Beidler
Connecting Administrative and Instructional Management Systems for Extended Education

Bio

Susan Beidler is the Director of Product Strategy for the Learning Solutions Division of PeopleSoft. She is responsible for initiatives that seek to leverage the use of PeopleSoft’s Student Administration and Advancement products in new ways, such as integration to learning management systems, development of the Higher Education portal, creation of an Education Marketplace as well as wireless deployment of product capabilities. Susan has been involved with the development of the Student Administration product for the past three years, with a focus in the Enrollment Services area, and prior to that was the Manager for HRMS education at PeopleSoft University.
