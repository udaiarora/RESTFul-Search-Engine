Combining and Modifying SAS Data Sets: Examples

Discover through examples how you can easily perform specific tasks to combine and modify SAS data sets. These examples demonstrate how to use the SET, MERGE, and MODIFY statements and the SQL procedure for combining and modifying SAS data sets. This book is written for intermediate to experienced SAS programmers.
