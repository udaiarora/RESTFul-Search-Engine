What levels of information can you find in an index?

Full Text Indexes

Full text indexes include the complete articles covered, in addition to citation information. Sometimes these indexes will include the full text of only some of the articles, with just the citation information for the remaining. Examples of full text indexes include:
  • Academic Search Premier (EbscoHost)
  • Lexis-Nexis Academic
