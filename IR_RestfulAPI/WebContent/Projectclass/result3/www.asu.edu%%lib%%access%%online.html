Access Services

Online Do-It-Yourself

 

VIEW your library account

By viewing your account you can verify due dates, check for recalls or fines

From the Libraries' web page, start in CATALOG, click on MY ACCOUNT and follow instructions.

 

RENEW your books (while viewing your Circulation Record)

From the Libraries' web page, start in CATALOG, click on MY ACCOUNT and follow instructions.


You may renew all items that qualify for renewal unless your account is blocked due to outstanding fines, overdue recalls, expired registration or renewal limits exceeded.


You can Renew All or Renew Selected Items. Confirm your renewals by scrolling down through the entire list of items checked out.


For detailed Renewal Policy information, contact Circulation or visit our ACCESS SERVICES web page.


EMAIL Add/Modify:

From the ASU Libraries Web page, click on MY ACCOUNT and follow instructions.


Click the Add/Modify Email button. This will open a new window.


Update your email address in this window and click Submit.


RECALL Checked-out items or REQUEST Shelf Status Books from the West campus or Polytechnic campus

Search the On-Line Catalog for items.


Once an item is located, click on the REQUEST button.


Enter the required Patron Information.


Select a Pick-up Location.


Scroll down to SUBMIT ABOVE INFORMATION button and click.


Click on REQUEST SELECTED ITEM button.


Make sure that you recieve the confirmation message: YOUR REQUEST FOR THIS ITEM HAS BEEN SENT TO THE LIBRARY.



**Note: Not all items qualify for RECALL/REQUEST


Most items checked-out from the general stacks collection may be recalled.


Check with Circulation for complete Recall Policy Information.


Notification is sent when Recall items are returned. When viewing your account On-line, these items will have a status of READY FOR PICK-UP.



You may only request shelf status items for delivery between campus (i.e. from Tempe campus to West campus), not between branches (i.e. from Noble to Hayden).


Law Library material is not requestable online.
