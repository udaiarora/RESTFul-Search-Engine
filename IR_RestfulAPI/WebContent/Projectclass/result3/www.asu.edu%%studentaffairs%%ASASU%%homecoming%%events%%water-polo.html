Homecoming - Inner Tube Water Polo

Why Come to ASU Homecoming? Plenty of Reasons!

Overview:  Water polo with a twist!  There will be an inner tube water polo tournament on Sunday, October 15th for all student organizations on campus.  It will be a single elimination tournament with basic water polo rules, running from 10:00am-5:00pm at the SRC pool.  Teams can be 6-10 players, with 6 playing at all times.

Registration:  Every participant MUST fill out a risk and indemnity form before they will be allowed to play!  See below for team registration and risk form. Registration fee is $50/team, and must be payed by check or cash to the ASASU Business Office.  All payment and risk forms are due FRIDAY OCTOBER 13TH BY 5:00PM to the ASASU Business Office (3rd floor of the Memorial Union).

Rules:  SRC Inner Tube Water Polo Rules.  Signing the risk and indemnity form indicates that each participant has read and agreed to follow these rules.
