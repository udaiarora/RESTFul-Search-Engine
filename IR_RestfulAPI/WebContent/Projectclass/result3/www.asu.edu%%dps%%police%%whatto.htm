What to expect when the crime is reported to the ASU Department of Public Safety.
1.
Our Officers will meet privately with you in a location of your choice.
2.
They will act thoughtfully without prejudging or blaming you.
3.
You will be treated with courtesy, sensitivity, dignity and understanding.
4.
They will accommodate your request to speak to an officer of the same gender.
5.
Your name and description will be withheld from the public and the press.
6.
Our officers will help you in arranging hospital or other assistance.
7.
They will help you obtain counseling services or other assistance.
8.
They promise to diligently investigate each case and keep you informed of all progress in your case.
9.
They will make themselves available at any time to answer your questions and to explain the criminal justice system and its processes.
10.
Your case will be handled seriously, regardless of your sex or the sex of the suspect.

The above is adapted from Michigan State University Police Promises.
