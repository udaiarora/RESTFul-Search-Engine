Museums/Galleries/Collections




Art Museum Network

    "The official web site of the world's leading art museums." An extremely helpful utility at this site is "excalendar" which is "The official exhibition calendar of the world's leading museums." This database is searchable by artist's name, exhibition title, city, keyword, museum name and geographical region. Each successful search yields information concerning the exhibition such as: official title, museum and city where the exhibition is to take place, and the dates that the exhibition is scheduled to be shown. Hot links are provided the home pages of the museum and exhibition for further information. This site is sponsered by the Association of Art Museum Directors.

Virtual Library Museum Pages

    A gateway to large number of all types of museums from around the world organized by country or region. This source includes selected virtual exhibitions, art galleries, library exhibitions and much more. This site is serviced by Jonathan Bowen and is supported by the International Council of Museums.
