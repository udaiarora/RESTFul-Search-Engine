Getting Materials from Storage



  • To request materials located in STORAGE STACKS, click on the “Request” button at the top of the catalog screen if the volume you need is listed on the record.
  • If you are not able to do an online request, please click here or contact Circulation at 480-965-3605 or 480-965-2600, and a request will be made for you. Normal delivery time is 1-2 business days.
  • Items whose location says LUHR'S READING STORAGE can only be obtained by contacting the Archives & Special Collections Reference Services located on level 4 of the Hayden Library. They can be reached at (480) 965-4932.
  • ASU affiliates with complete and specific citation may request articles through Interlibrary Loan: http://www.asu.edu/lib/ill/
  • If the entire volume is needed for review, Interlibrary Loan should not be used.
  • Users not affiliated with ASU cannot use Interlibrary Loan-Article delivery service. The entire volume must be requested by clicking here or contacting Circulation at one of the numbers above.
