What Is IMS?

Open Interoperability through Open Standards

PeopleSoft is an IMS Investor and Advisory Board member. We also lead the work group defining standards for the interoperability of internet learning systems with Enterprise Systems (Student Administration, Human Resources, Financial Systems, Library Man

  • IMS is a global coalition of educational, commercial and government organizations working to define open standards for Internet learning technology.

11

Obstacles IMS Wants to Overcome

  • Lack of standards for locating and operating interactive platform-independent materials

  • Lack of support for the collaborative and dynamic nature of learning

  • Lack of incentives and structure for developing and sharing content

13

IMS Meta-data Work Group

  • Worldwide participation of IMS community

    • IMS Investor Members
    • IEEE
    • ARIANDE
    • GESTALT

  • Version 1 of the meta-data released to the public August 20, 1999

  • Each data element described with 5 pieces of information

14

IMS Meta-data Descriptions

  • Name

    • Spelling of the meta-data

  • Explanation

    • Definition of the element

  • Multiplicity

    • How many elements are allowed ad if order os significant

  • Type

    • Whether value is textual, numerical, or date
    • size and format constraint

  • Rationale

    • Why element included and guidelines for use
