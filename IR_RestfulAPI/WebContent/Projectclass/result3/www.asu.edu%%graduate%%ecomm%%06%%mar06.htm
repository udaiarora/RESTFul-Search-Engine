University Graduate Fellowship (UGF) Block Grant Program
As presented at the recent graduate directors and chairs meetings and in individual meetings, the new University Graduate Fellowship (UGF) Block Grant program is replacing the Graduate Tuition Fellowship (GTF) program. UGF funds will be used to provide academic units with an annual allocation to be used to support the recruitment, retention, and completion of the very best graduate students. Allocation notices are in the process of being sent to academic units. Please direct questions to dgs-financial@asu.edu.

Interactive Program of Study (iPOS) — Live!
As of March 1, the Graduation Office and the Division of Graduate Studies no longer accepts paper Program of Study forms. Students who have not already submitted a paper POS must complete the iPOS online through myASU. If a student has submitted a paper Program of Study, they should not complete an iPOS online. Only one Program of Study may be filed with the DGS. Additionally, if a student has an approved paper POS, they will need to file paper petitions, committee changes or course changes using the printed forms. For more information, visit www.asu.edu/graduate/whatsnew/iPOSupdate.htm.

Graduate Student Appreciation Week
The Graduate and Professional Student Association will honor graduate students during the Graduate Student Appreciation Week from March 20-24. This week of activities is designed to recognize graduate students for their scholarly and creative achievements as well as the many contributions they make toward furthering ASU's educational mission. Encourage students in your academic unit to attend. For a complete listing of events, contact GPSA at (480) 727-9870.

CONACYT Fellowships. ASU is proud to present its partnership with El Consejo Nacional de Ciencia y Tecnología (CONACYT) in Mexico. This historic agreement grants 75 fellowships for Mexican graduate students to study at ASU, and provides for other types of research and exchange funding. For more information, go to www.asu.edu/opi/conacyt.

DGS Forms. Two forms have been revised due to the launch of iPOS. Both forms are available online: www.asu.edu/graduate/forms.
  • The Report of Final Master's Written or Oral Exam form is now called the Report of Final Master's Culminating Experience form and now includes sections to report the final result of an applied project, capstone course, and more than one written or oral exam. Please note that the results on the form must match the degree requirements included on the approved Program of Study.


  • The Committee Approval Request form has also been updated. The form now includes a section to indicate if the approval is part of a student's paper POS, iPOS, or a committee change.

gPortal Summer Assistantships. Effective immediately, you may begin using gPortal to post RA/TA awards for summer 2006. The same input screen that you have been using during the past year to input assistantships for Fall and Spring now allows you to identify new awards for summer. To do this:
  1. Locate the student that you need to add a summer assistantship for;
  2. Click on the Add Award link;
  3. Fill out the information requested (Award Type, Academic Year, Hiring Department, Faculty ASURITE ID, Term and % of Award)
    1. The academic year for Summer, 2006 is 2005–2006
    2. The choices for term are either Academic Year (Fall/Spring) or Summer
    3. The Summer 1 FTE and Summer 2 FTE percentages are entered as whole numbers. For example, a full-time assistantship for the summer is entered as 50.
  4. Be sure to follow-up with the necessary HPR form to Human Resources.
If you have questions concerning summer assistantships, please contact us at dgs-financial@asu.edu.
