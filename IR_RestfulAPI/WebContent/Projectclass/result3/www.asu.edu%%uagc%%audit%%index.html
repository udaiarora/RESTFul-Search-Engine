Mission Statement

Internal Audit and Management Services will promote University Administration's mission and strategic goals by providing objective and independent evaluation services of University operations and finances that are responsive to the University's stakeholders. Internal Audit and Management Services provides in-depth customer focused audit engagements, state of the art management reviews, and interactive educational programs to University departments.

If you require assistance, please call Internal Audit and Management Services at (480) 965-1801.
