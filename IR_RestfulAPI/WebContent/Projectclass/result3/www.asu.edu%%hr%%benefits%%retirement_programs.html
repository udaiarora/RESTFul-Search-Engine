RETIREMENT PLAN:


Classified Staff
As one of a number of benefits, Arizona State University provides a retirement program. Participation in the program is required if you work at least 20 weeks in a fiscal year and 20 or more hours per week, as provided in Arizona Revised Statutes, Section 38-711.22 (b).

You must participate in the Arizona State Retirement System (ASRS).

You will contribute 8.6% of your gross salary (through payroll deduction)

for your retirement benefit and an additional .5% for long-term disability.

The university matches both of these contributions.

NOTE:  Although your participation in the retirement program begins on

your first day of eligible employment, you MUST complete the

enrollment paperwork within 30 days.
