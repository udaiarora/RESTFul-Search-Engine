ASU Human Resources

HYPER Listserv

A little background: Hyper was named for Hypertext which was an early buzz-word used to describe the ability for an html page to link directly to other pertinent content or other documents from within the page's text. Hyper is an unmoderated discussion group at Arizona State University dealing specifically with WWW development and support issues.

Students, faculty, and staff are invited to join. Subscribing to the Hyper listserv is as easy as filling out this form! If you have any questions regarding the Hyper Discussion Group, email the ASU Web Consulting Team for more information.

WebDev
The Web Developers Group at Arizona State University exists to help develop the abilities, enhance the quality of work, and improve the inter-communication of those who create and maintain web pages at ASU. The group holds meetings regularly that feature demonstrations of the latest web technologies, answers to audience questions, and other Internet and computing information of interest. Anyone affiliated with ASU (student, faculty, or staff) is invited to attend (no RSVP necessary).
Web-Q Consulting Service

Web consulting services for ASU are provided via email by the Web and Messaging Applications Team in Information Technology to facilitate resolution of technical problems encountered by customers of "central" IT-hosted ASU web services. These services include (but are not limited to) public webspace, department webspace on www.asu.edu, public CGI applications, and administrative web applications.
