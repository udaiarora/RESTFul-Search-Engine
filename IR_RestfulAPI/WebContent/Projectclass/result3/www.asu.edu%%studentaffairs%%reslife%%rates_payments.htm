Residential Life Room & Board Rates for 2006 - 2007 Academic Year*

Listed below are Residential Life's room and board rates for the 2006-2007 academic year for Tempe and Downtown Phoenix campuses. The pricing includes the cost of the meal plan plus the room rate. See the bottom of this page for more information regarding the meal plans*.

 

Room and board rates for 2006-2007 are inclusive of housing and meal plan costs. Meal plan exemptions are available to students. Those students who would like more information regarding an exemption or other financial options may contact Campus Dining at 480.727.DINE.

Room and board payments can be made in one of two installment plans:

  1. One payment per semester: This payment plan is suggested if you are paying with financial aid. Payments are due on July 25, 2006 and December 20, 2006.
  2. Three payments per semester: Payments are due July 25, 2006; September 25, 2006; October 25, 2006, December 20,2006; February 25, 2007 and March 25, 2007. There is an additional $50 administrative charge per academic year for this payment option.

Meal Plan Pricing
                 

Meal plans are selected during the student housing application process and are included in the room and board rate listed in the charts above. Room and board rates for 2006-2007 are inclusive of housing and meal plan costs. Meal plan exemptions are available to students. Thos students who would like more information regaeding an exemption or other financial options may contact Campus Dining at 480-727-DINE.

ASU is committed to minimizing financial barriers for students in the pursuit of their ASU degree. If you feel that the inclusive room and board rate offered by ASU causes a financial barrier and would like to discuss your options, please contact Campus Dining. They will assist you in understanding the exemption process and dedicated forms of assistance that include Campus Dining's Work-Credit Scholarships and special grants from Student Affairs for meal plan expenses. Campus Dining will also connect you with ASU's Student Financial Assistance for information about the university's financial aid program.
