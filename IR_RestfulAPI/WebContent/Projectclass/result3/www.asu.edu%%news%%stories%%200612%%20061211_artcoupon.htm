December 11, 2006

Art Museum offers coupon for gift buyers

It's that time of the year again when shoppers are looking for unique gifts for family, friends and loved ones.

To make this holiday easier, the ASU Art Museum is happy to present the 2006 ASU Art Museum's holiday coupon. To get one, visit the ASU Art Museum store's Web site (http://asuartmuseum.asu.edu/museumstore) and print a copy of the coupon. To use the coupon, take it along when shopping at the museum store to receive the discount.

The ASU Art Museum Store, located at the southeast corner of Mill Avenue and Tenth Street in Tempe, is open during regular museum hours. Free parking is available on the northeast corner of Mill Avenue and Tenth Street in front of the ASU Art Museum 's Ceramics Research Center .

For more information, call (480) 965-2787.
