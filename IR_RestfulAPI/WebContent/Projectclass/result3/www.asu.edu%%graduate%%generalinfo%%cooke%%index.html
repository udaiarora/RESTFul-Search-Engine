Jack Kent Cooke Foundation
2006 Graduate Scholarship Program
ASU LWFONSA application deadline: February 15, 2006


The Jack Kent Cooke Foundation will award graduate scholarships to help students with exceptional promise and demonstrated financial need reach their full potential through education. Awards cover tuition, room and board, books, and other required fees for the length of the degree program, up to six years. The maximum award per student is $50,000 per year. Candidates may not apply to the Foundation directly; they must be nominated by their undergraduate institution.
