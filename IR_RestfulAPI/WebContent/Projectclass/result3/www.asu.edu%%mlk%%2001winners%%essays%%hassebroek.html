2001 Essay/Poetry Contest

Jordan Hassebroek, Grade 2
Teacher: Mrs. Connie Spencer
Mesa Elementary
Mesa District

Second Place
Primary (Grades K-2)

 

My Mom

She cooks for my family, buys us clothes when we don't have any money. She works at the library and she checks out books for people and even me. She helps people when they're sick. At the library she tells people where books and movies are. When we get other people's mail she gives it back.
