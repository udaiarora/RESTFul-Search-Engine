Kathryn - Justice Studies and Political Science

"My academic experiences at ASU have stretched far beyond what is taught in the classroom."

Kathryn is focused on reaching her career goal of working with the FBI, Secret Service or White House staff. She has been accepted into the law school of her choice with plans to study administrative or constitutional law. At ASU she has a double major, because she liked the classes offered by both departments. With an academic advisor for each major, Kathryn meets with them regularly to help her stay on track.  

Kathryn earned the 2005 Sun Devil Star Award, which recognizes students for their campus involvement and contributions to the university. She enjoys giving tours as a Devil's Advocate and is also involved in R.E.A.C.H (Research, Educate, Advise, Counsel, Help) and is a START (Student Admissions Relations Team) member.

What she likes best about ASU is that it's constantly evolving. "I came to ASU to receive an education, and will leave here with inspiration for the future, and a better understanding of who I am."
