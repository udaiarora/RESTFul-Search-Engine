Student Recreation at ASU
www.asu.edu/src

 



The Student Recreation Complex at ASU is for the use of the ASU community and offers an extensive assortment of activities and memberships:
  • Aquatics
    The SRC offers a wide vaiety of aquatic classes, some of which include: swim lessons, lifeguard instructor, CPR/AED for the professional rescuer, first aid, and water safety instructor. During the cooler months of the year, the pool temperature is kept close to 82 degrees; summer temperature is closer to 80 degrees.

  • Children and Family
    The SRC offers two programs to members with children: Sun Devil Kids Camp and Family Swim.



  • Group Fitness
    Weekly classes include aqua, land, and yoga, with 3 aerobic gyms equipped with steps, step risers, yoga mats, body bars, exercise balls, hand weights, stretch mats, mini-hurdles, jump ropes, boxing gloves, boxing mitts, heavy bag, exer-tubes, dynabands, and exer-rings. Cardio Jams are offered each semester as a special event for all participants. SRC also offers an in-house instructor training program as needed and hosts national workshops for training and certification by the American Council on Exercise and Aerobic fitness Association of America.

  • Open Recreation Program
    SRC members may participate in a variety of activities on a drop-in basis including: basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, racquetball, squash, tennis and lap swimming. The weight room and fitness areas feature selectorized resistance machines, computerized cardiorespiratory equipment and 14 tons of free weights.

  • Intramurals
    This program offers a wide range of recreation and sport opportunities. The sports leagues are designed with both the competitive and recreational players in mind.

  • Sports Clubs
    A sport club is a voluntary group of students, faculty or staff at ASU that have organized for the purpose of furthering its interest in a common sport or recreational activity. There are two types of sport club programs at ASU: Competitive and Recreational. Competitive Clubs have highly organized, formal sport contests or matches, on an individual or team basis. They are usually characterized by the involvement of at least one coach, scheduled practice times and competitions, and a referee, umpire, or other type of rules judge.

  • Outdoor Recreation
    The SRC offers organized trips for everyone from the avid hiker to the first-time camper. Got kids? Bring them along! Or plan your own trip and have the SRC provide you with quality, affordable equipment, along with pictures and instructions to help you get the most out of your adventure. Get ready to EXPLORE!

  • Adaptive Recreation
    A wide variety of accessible equipment is available for use in a mainstreamed environment. Programs and services are designed for the individual or small groups, including recreational outings, sporting events, water exercise therapy, wheelchair tennis instruction, therapeutic kinesiology, wheelchair table tennis, and visually impaired stokers. Support is also offered to international student groups in cooperation with the International Students Office.

  • Sun Devil Fitness and Yoga Program
    This program offers a variety of classes to accommodate as many people as possible. Classes are offered all year long but change along with the academic calendar and holidays. There are free classes the first week of each semester, winter break, and during interim periods. Classes are taught in the small gyms on the 2nd floor of the SRC. All equipment is provided.

  • Wellness & Therapy
    Make healthy choices! The SRC invites you to take care of yourself by providing opportunities to experience and learn about movement, nutrition, rest and relaxation, and self care. Explore and enjoy!

  • Safety Education
    Would you know how to react in an emergency? Are you aware that you are more likely to perform life-saving first aid on a family member than a stranger? Prepare yourself for the unexpected by taking on the SRC safety classes. All classes are American Red Cross courses and are offered at various times throughout the year.
