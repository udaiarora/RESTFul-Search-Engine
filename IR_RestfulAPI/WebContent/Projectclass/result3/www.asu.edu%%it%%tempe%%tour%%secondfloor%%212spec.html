Room Specifications - Kaleidoscope Classroom

Service Description

The Kaleidoscope Classroom, seating a maximum of 135, was designed to promote collaboration and teamwork within a flexible learning environment. The Kaleidoscope Classroom is a space where faculty are encouraged, by the physical deployment of the furniture, to use different teaching methodologies, to give a large class the chance to enjoy some of the benefits of smaller classes, to work in 4 or 5 person groups. A "spoke" configuration with space in the center for the instructor and for a lectern, reminiscent of a "theater in the round," is the most typical arrangement. As inferred by its name, the Kaleidoscope has many configurations due to flexible furnishings (moveable tables, seating, lectern) and infrastructure, to support rapidly-changing technologies.
