GAMMAGE EVENTS PARKING

Gammage Event Parking Map

Events at Grady Gammage Auditorium


The following ASU parking lots are affected

Lots 3, 17 and Tempe Center. Lot 3 will be closed at 4 pm for each Gammage event. Only Lot 3 parking decals and event patrons will be allowed in Lot 3 after 4 pm. Tempe Center and Lot 17 are used for overflow parking. People attending the event should plan on arriving one hour early, as parking next to Gammage Auditorium is limited.

Disabled parking is available in Lot 3 (next to Gammage Auditorium).

All Residence Hall parking lots require residence hall parking decals. No event parking is permitted in any Residence Hall parking lot.
