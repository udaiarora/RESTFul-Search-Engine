October 21, 2004

Language Analysis Technology Reveals The Choreography of Campaigns' "Mud Dances"

A comprehensive objective analysis using a sophisticated language analysis tool has given hard evidence supporting something that many political reporters have long suspected: the Kerry and Bush teams are framing their campaign messages quite differently.

Here as some basic conclusions derived from the tool's objective analysis of the two campaigns' current releases and web blogs:

    · Kerry's messages frame Bush using complex arguments; Bush's frame Kerry using simple adjectives.
    · Kerry's messages have emphasized issues more than Bush.
    · Kerry's messages are focused on Bush's policies and outcomes; Bush's are focused on Kerry's attributes.
    · Bush's messages have emphasized local issues in swing states more than Kerry's.

While pointing out these basic differences in the campaigns' marketing strategies, the tool (known as "The Crawdad Campaign Dashboard") also tracks the complex choreography of attack and response between the two campaigns. One of the tool's most important features, the "Mud Meter," has documented strategic surges and withdrawals in the amount of negative anti-candidate rhetoric the two campaigns produce, with the Kerry Campaign generally being reactive to the Bush Campaign's strategic moves, at least until recently.

The technology is the product of two Arizona State University professors, Steven Corman from the Hugh Downs School of Human Communication and Kevin Dooley from the W.P. Carey School of Business. The Crawdad Campaign Dashboard ( < http://www.crawdadtech.com/campaign > ) provides an unbiased and comprehensive analysis of campaign communications from the Bush and Kerry official web sites and blogs, updated daily.  The Dashboard uses identical technology to analyze both message streams, and provides information concerning the tone, intensity, and focus of campaign communications, key words from each campaign, and image nets that show how the candidates are semantically framing themselves and the other.  Additionally, the Crawdad Mud MeterT provides an objective measure of political mud-slinging that has never been available before.  The Dashboard is provided by Crawdad Technologies, LLC.  For a detailed explanation of how the Dashboard works, visit: <http://www.asu.edu/feature/campaign/details.htm>.

For a detailed summary of current Campaign Dashboard findings, see: <http://clas.asu.edu/newsevents/pressreleases/crawdad10-5.htm >
