PCS 409: Receiving Loaned Equipment

Purpose

To properly receive equipment loaned to ASU by external agencies

Sources

Property Control
Purchasing and Business Services

Policy

ASU assumes no responsibility for equipment on loan or demonstration unless the user has submitted an Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form to Purchasing and Business Services and Property Control.

Note:If the form is not submitted, the Arizona Department of Administration’s Risk Management Division may not insure the equipment.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Department
  1. Prepare an Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form before accepting the equipment.
If equipment will be on campus fewer than 30 days:
  1. Send a photocopy of the form to Purchasing and Business Services.
Purchasing and Business Services
  1. Send a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.
If equipment will be on campus for more than 30 days:
Department
  1. Send a photocopy of the form to Purchasing and Business Services and Property Control.
Purchasing and Business Services
  1. Send a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.
Property Control
  1. Tag the equipment and add it to the property control system at zero dollars.

    Note:Property Control does not scribe loaned equipment.

endtable

Cross-Reference

For more information on equipment on loan or demonstration to the university, see the Purchasing and Business Services Policies and Procedures Manual—PUR 502–06, “Equipment on Loan or Demonstration.”
