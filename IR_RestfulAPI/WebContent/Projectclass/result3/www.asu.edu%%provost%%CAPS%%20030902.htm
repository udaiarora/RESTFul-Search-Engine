Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

September 2, 2003

Members Present:  Sue Hunter (Chair), Barbara Trapido-Lurie (webmaster), Beth Luey and Paul Scowen (acting recorder) 

Excused:  Carol Behl; Patricia Feldman; Victoria Trotta

Committee organization

Chair Sue Hunter introduced Alternate Member Paul Scowen to the committee.  Paul will serve on the committee through December 2003, the duration of Victoria Trotta?s sabbatical leave.  Because several members were absent from this meeting, the committee agreed to select a Secretary for 2003-2004 at the October meeting.  Paul Scowen agreed to serve as Secretary for this meeting. Barbara Trapido-Lurie volunteered, previous to the first meeting, to take on the role of Webmaster and she has updated the site appropriately.

Sue will inquire about the procedure of ensuring the record of activities for CAPS are maintained for archival purposes.

Old business

Sue Hunter updated the committee on the status of the Task Force on Academic Professionals.  She explained that the tentative plan is for CAPS to focus on developing ACD policy language, together with the Task Force, to operationalize Task Force recommendations.  Sue indicated that she would represent CAPS at the Task Force?s initial meeting on September 18, and would report on the meeting to CAPS.

New business

The committee discussed the importance of staying informed of Task Force activities, in order to be prepared for its own role in revising the Academic Policies and Procedures text.   The committee proposed that CAPS members be included on Task Force email correspondence. 

Paul Scowen proposed setting up a CAPS Distribution List (DL.CMTE.CAPS), to facilitate communication between CAPS members as well as between the Task Force and CAPS.

The committee discussed its traditional role of organizing Spotlight Speakers.  It was proposed that President Crow be formally invited to address the APs in the Fall, with the Task Force Chair providing a response in the Spring.  Discussion of this topic will continue at the October meeting.

Meeting adjourned at 11am.

Submitted by Paul Scowen and Barbara Trapido-Lurie

Minutes were approved 10/1/03
