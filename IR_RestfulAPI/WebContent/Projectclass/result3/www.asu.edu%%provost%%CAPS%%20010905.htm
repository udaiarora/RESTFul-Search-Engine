Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

September 5, 2001

Present: Carol Behl, Patty Feldman, Annie Platoff, Linda Shackle, Santos Vega
Absent: Stefan Myhajlenko

CAPS met in Room 214 of the Memorial Union, at 10:00 a.m.

CAPS members for 2001-2002 are:

  • Carol Behl, Manager, Advising Services Computer Science and Engineering (2001-2003) (Academic Associate rep.)
  • Patty Feldman, Director, Academic and Professional Programs, College of Extended Education (2001-2004)
  • Stefan Myhajlenko, Associate Director, Center for Solid State Electronics Research (2000-2002)
  • Annie Platoff, Web Master/Instruction Librarian, University Libraries (2001-2004)
  • Linda Shackle, Science Reference Team Leader/Chemistry Librarian, University Libraries (1999-2002)
  • Santos Vega, Ph.D., Coordinator, Community Art & Research Outreach (CARO), Hispanic Research Center (1999-2002)

The group discussed the positions that needed to be filled: CAPS Chair, CAPS Vice-Chair, and CAPS Secretary. Nominations were made and the following people were elected to office:

  • CAPS Chair: Patty Feldman
  • CAPS Vice-Chair: Linda Shackle
  • CAPS Secretary: Annie Platoff

Carol explained why there is an Academic Associate on the committee. She is creating a distribution list of Academic Associates and would like to use the list to inform that group about the activities of CAPS. Many people on the list do not know that they have a representative on CAPS. Currently academic voting privileges are limited to those in continuing-status track positions. This excludes most of the Academic Associates so they cannot vote directly for their CAPS representative.

There was a discussion of possible speakers for this academic year. Topics proposed were:

  • Arizona Regents University
  • Grievance Process
  • CAPS Spotlight on Academic Associates
We will select topics and invite speakers as the semester progresses.

The CAPS web site is located at http://www.asu.edu/provost/CAPS. The site has not been updated to include all of last year's meeting minutes. It will be updated in the future.

CAPS regular business meetings will be on the first Wednesday of each month. The next one will be Wednesday, October 3 at 10 a.m. in Room 214 of the Memorial Union. We will need a substitute secretary for the meeting in November.

Annie Platoff, Secretary
