Receive Your Financial Aid Notification (FAN)

The FAN lists a student's financial aid offer for the nine-month academic year (August-May). Offered awards are contingent upon full-time enrollment and are based on your academic level, estimated cost of attendance, eligibility, and application completion date. Awards listed on your FAN will be disbursed equally between the fall and spring semesters. If enrollment changes, ASU pro-rates your awards through the 21st class day of the semester.

Our primary method of communication with students is via their @asu.edu e-mail address. If you are a new student, the university will send you a paper FAN and a hard-copy of the FAN Guide as well as e-mail notification once your financial aid offer is determined. If you are a continuing student, you will receive an e-mail notification directing you to ASU Interactive to view your online FAN.

The awarding process for new students usually begins late-March, and mid-May for continuing students.

 

View the FAN Guide

What can affect your awards?

  • Less than full-time enrollment
  • Auditing a class
  • Taking a correspondence course
  • Receipt of an additional award
  • Residency Change
  • FAFSA corrections\updates
  • Defaulting on a student loan
  • Exceeding annual or aggregate student loan limits
  • Owing a grant overpayment to the federal government
  • SAP violations

Important

Carefully review your FAN and award details. If you received, or will receive, awards that are not reflected on your FAN, please contact Student Financial Assistance.
