CLEP

College Level Examination Program


Information
CLEP examinations cover material taught in courses that most students take as requirements in the first two years of college. A college usually grants the same amount of credit to students earning satisfactory scores on the CLEP examination as it grants to students successfully completing that course. You may obtain a CLEP bulletin in our office or by visiting www.collegeboard.com/clep.
CLEP for DANTES
DANTES program candidates must register in person at our office, present a proof of eligibility (military or approved governmental identification card) and pay the $15.00 registration fee by personal check or money order. Cash or credit card payments are not permitted. Mail in registrations are not permitted.
Restrictions
CLEP credit is not given at ASU when any of the following situations apply:
  1. It duplicates credit previously earned by the student at the university or accepted by the university for work done elsewhere.
  2. It is more elementary than a course in the same field in which the student has already received credit.
  3. An ASU student has already earned more than 100 semester hours of comprehensive credit (proficiency exams).
Schedule
  • February 8, 13, 15, 20, 27 at 9:30 am
  • February 13, 14, 15, 20, 21, 27, 28 at 2:00 pm
Cost
Each College Level Examination Program test costs a total of $75.00. You must pay $15.00 at the time of registration with a check or money order made out to Arizona State University. The remaining $60.00 is paid to CLEP and is not collected until the day of the test. However, the method of payment must be indicated at the time of registration. For the $60.00 CLEP payment you may use check, money order, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover or JCB. (Note: CLEP exams for which you select an optional essay will require an additional $10.00 fee payable to CLEP.)
Time
The exam is 90 minutes long but examinees should allow 2 hours for the total appointment time.
Register
Registrations must be received by 4:30 pm one working day before the requested test date.
In Person: Complete the CLEP registration form and submit it along with payment to an UTS staff member in 301 Payne Hall. At that time you will be given an admission ticket.
By Mail: Complete the CLEP registration form and submit it along with payment and a self addressed stamped envelope to:
Arizona State University
University Testing Services
PO Box 870511
Tempe, AZ 85287-0511
Reschedule
The $15.00 registration fee is non-refundable and non-transferable.
Retake
You must wait 6 months before retaking the same CLEP exam.
Admission
Examinees must pre-register; walk-ins on the day of the exam are not permitted. Exam entrance requires an admission ticket, payment to CLEP, and two forms of valid, unexpired ID. The name on your ID must match the name on your admission ticket. Your primary ID must be a government-issued ID that includes your name, signature and a recognizable photograph. Acceptable forms of primary identification include:
  • Current driver's license
  • Current state or federal ID card
  • Current passport
  • Tribal ID card
  • Naturalization card or certificate of citizenship
Your secondary ID must contain either a photo or a signature. Acceptable forms of secordary identification include:
  • Current employee ID
  • Current student ID
  • Social Security card
  • Military ID
  • An unused ID from the primary choices above
DANTES-Funded CLEP military examinees must present a current military photo identification card AND a second form of government-issued photo ID with name and signature.
  • The military ID (Common Access Card) must indicate that you are a member of the "Armed Forces of the United States." Retired service members are not eligible for DANTES-funded CLEP testing.
  • For Authorized Reserve component spouses that receive DANTES-funded testing, your ID must indicate "United States Uniformed Services."
  • If you are a Government-funded civilian who is an employee performing military functions, you must present your government or state-issued photo ID's
Scores
Immediately after completion of your exam, you will receive your official score report. Official results are mailed from CLEP directly to the institution you indicate at the time of the test. There is a $20.00 fee for each additional transcript.
