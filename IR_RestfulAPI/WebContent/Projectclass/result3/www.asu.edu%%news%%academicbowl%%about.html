| ACADEMIC BOWL


 

Academic Bowl KICKS OFF OCTOBER 12

ASU’s first annual Academic Bowl competition kicked off with opening-round matches on Oct. 12. The second half of the opening round will be held Oct. 19.

The competition pits 16 teams of undergraduate students in an intellectual competition for scholarship prizes of $4,500 – a full year’s tuition – for each of the four members of the winning team, as well as the President’s Cup trophy. The winning team’s alternate members each will receive a 1,000 scholarship.

The competition, sponsored by the offices of Public Affairs and Student Affairs, is modeled after the national College Bowl. Four-member teams will square off in a question-and-answer format on a wide range of subjects, including current events, history, science, sports and culture.

The semifinals and final competition will be broadcast by Eight/KAET-TV, Channel 8.

The opening round, which will be held in the Pima Room of the Memorial Union on the Tempe camps, has been divided into two groups:

Group A – Oct. 12

• 7 p.m. – Match 1: The Herberger College of Fine Art versus the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering.

• 7:40 p.m. – Match 2: University College versus College of Science and Technology.

• 8:20 p.m. – Match 3: East College versus New College of Interdisciplinary Arts and Sciences.

• 9 p.m. – Match 4: College of Teacher Education and Leadership versus College of Liberal Arts and Sciences Team A.

Group B – Oct. 19

• 7 p.m. – Match 5: School of Global Management and Leadership versus Mary Lou Fulton College of Education.

• 7:40 p.m. – Match 6: College of Human Services versus Morrison School of Management and Agribusiness.

• 8:20 p.m. – Match 7: College of Design versus the W. P. Carey School of Business.

• 9 p.m. – Match 8: College of Liberal Arts and Sciences Team B versus Walter Cronkite School of Journalism and Mass Communications.

 

The complete Academic Bowl schedule is:

 

Group A Opening Round Oct. 12, 7 p.m.
Memorial Union, Pima Room
Group B Opening Round Oct. 19, 7 p.m.
Memorial Union, Pima Room
Quarter Finals Oct. 25, 7 p.m.
Memorial Union, Pima Room
Semifinals and Finals Nov. 6, 7 p.m.
KAET Studio
