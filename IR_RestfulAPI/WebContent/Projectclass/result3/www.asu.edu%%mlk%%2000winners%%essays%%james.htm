2000 Essay/Poetry Contest

Brittany James, Grade 2
Teacher: Mrs. Sandra Matsuura
Pueblo del Sol Elementary
Sierra Vista Unified District

Second Place — Primary (Grades K-2)

 

I think Emily Spencer is a good leader because she plays fair and she isn't mean to us and she does good things. She leads us where we need to go. She makes up good games to play. Then we play fun games instead of boring. She leads us. That's why we want to follow her because shes a good friend. She shows us how to play the game she makes up. She's fair when we play new games. She doesn't hurt us when we're playing. She is the leader. She is a nice leader to me.
— This is student work, transcribed as the student presented it.
