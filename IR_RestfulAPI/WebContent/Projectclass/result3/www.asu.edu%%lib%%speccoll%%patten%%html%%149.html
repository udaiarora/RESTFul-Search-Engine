Wurzburger, Marilyn

Botanical Treasures from the Doris and Marc Patten Collection: Catalog of an Exhibition.

Introduction by Donald H. Dyal. Tempe, AZ: Special Collections Dept., Arizona State University Libraries, 1994. "Catalog of an exhibition, Arizona State University Libraries, November 1994."--Cover. Bibliography. In a custom-made folder with a copy of the invitation to the exhibition and reception and a reception program.

This exhibition catalog features detailed annotations as well as illustrations of many of the works from the Patten Collection now housed at ASU.

Subjects: Botany--Pre-Linnean works; Bibliography; Exhibitions; Herbals; Rare books.
