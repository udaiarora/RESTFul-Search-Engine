ASU Listed as One of the Best Workplaces for Commuters

October 18, 2005 (Tempe, Ariz.)

Best Workplaces for Commuters has named Arizona State University to its annual list of employers whose commuter benefits reach a national standard of excellence.

ASU is one of only 1,279 companies and government agencies nation-wide, and one of 95 from Arizona, awarded the distinction as a “Best Workplace for Commuters.”

Established by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT), Best Workplaces for CommutersSM recognizes employers that provide commuter benefits which help address limited parking, reduce traffic congestion, improve employee recruiting and retention and minimize the environmental impacts associated with drive-alone commuting.

ASU earned the distinction as one of the Best Workplaces for Commuters by offering an employer-based commuter program, an ozone awareness program, ridesharing through www.ShareTheRide.com, preferred parking for carpools, compressed work schedules and a commuting awards program.

For more information on commuting programs at ASU contact the Travel Reduction Office at (480) 965-1072 or visit www.asu.edu/dps/pts/travel.

To view the national Best Workplaces for Commuters list visit www.bwc.gov.
