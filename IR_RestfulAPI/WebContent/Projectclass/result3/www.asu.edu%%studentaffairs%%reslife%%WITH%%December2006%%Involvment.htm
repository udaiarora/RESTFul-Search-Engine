NRHH (National Residence Hall Honorary)

National Residence Hall Honorary is a sister organization to RHA comprised of the top 1% of students living in residence halls across the country. Students who have made significant contributions to their university are nominated for this prestigious organization by their peers and staff members. This fall, 19 new students were inducted into NRHH. Congratulations to all of the inductees!
