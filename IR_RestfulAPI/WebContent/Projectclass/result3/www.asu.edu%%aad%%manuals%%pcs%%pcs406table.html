The department forwards to Property Control a list of equipment being transferred from another institution. Appraised or estimated values for each item should be included.

If the equipment does not pertain to a sponsored grant or contract, the department advises the sponsored property coordinator accordingly.

The sponsored property coordinator tags the equipment and adds it to the property control system and records the grant or contract number in the property control system.
