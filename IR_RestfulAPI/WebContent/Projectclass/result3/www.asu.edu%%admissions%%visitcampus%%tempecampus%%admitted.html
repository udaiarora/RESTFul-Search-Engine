Visit ASU at the Tempe campus: Next steps for admitted freshman students

Congratulations on being admitted to ASU! You are on your way to making a great choice that will help ensure your academic success!

Explore ASU
Further explore ASU, including your major and the College or School that offers your program. Once you are admitted, you are eligible to speak with an academic advisor. You may also explore ASU's resources for students and student activities and involvement opportunities. And get answers to your additional questions.

Steps to Attending ASU
Understand the Steps to Attending ASU and complete all steps that pertain to you to ensure your smooth transition to enrolling at ASU.

Attend ASU Orientation
All admitted incoming freshman students are expected to attend ASU Orientation. Please note that if you are scheduled to attend ASU Orientation, you will receive academic advising and a campus tour as part of this program.

Visit ASU
Schedule a visit to ASU. Learn more about ASU's academics and student life in person. Visiting campus is one of the best ways to get a glimpse of what life is like at ASU. Schedule your visit today.
