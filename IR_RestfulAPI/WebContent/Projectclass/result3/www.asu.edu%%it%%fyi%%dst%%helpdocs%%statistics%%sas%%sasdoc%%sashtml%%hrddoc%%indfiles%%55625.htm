An Array of Challenges Test Your SAS Skills

by Robert Virgile

Take a deep breath and prepare to be challenged. This brain teasing book is designed for you to assess your programming savvy through short, FUN, and puzzling SAS programming problems. These problems do not require any hardware, only your mind. New opportunities to test your knowledge of fundamental programming tools such as arrays, merging data sets, DO loops, reading raw data, and lengths of variables are provided on every two pages along with suggested solutions. Each advanced problem includes the proposed "time to beat". The text is witty yet the problems require concentration. You're encouraged to have fun while enhancing your programming skills. Guaranteed to challenge the best programmers!

192 pages.
