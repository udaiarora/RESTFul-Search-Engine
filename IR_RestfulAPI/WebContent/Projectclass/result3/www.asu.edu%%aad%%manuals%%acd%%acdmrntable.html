ACD 506–06: Evaluation for Tenure and Promotion. Revised to identify the president or designee as the final decision maker

ACD 506–08: Notification of Consideration for Tenure, Promotion, or Retention. Revised to update the deadlines for notification in compliance with the schedule of personnel actions

ACD 508–01: Nonreappointment. Revised to update the deadlines for notification in compliance with the schedule of personnel actions

ACD 509–01: Ombudsperson Committee. Revised to change “Student Affairs vice presidential area” to “University Initiatives vice presidential area”

ACD 509–02: Grievance Policies and Procedures for Faculty. Revised to update the mail code and the location for submission of documents

ACD 710: Parental Leave with Pay. New policy to provide faculty and academic professionals with salary continuance for a period of absence from work for the birth or adoption of a child
