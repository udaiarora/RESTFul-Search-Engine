About Tutoring

What kinds of tutoring are available?
 
FREE scheduled small-group tutoring. Meet with the same tutor at the same time each week.

FREE drop-in tutoring. Come in for tutoring during site hours without an appointment.

FREE online tutoring. Sign up for real-time help with math, physics, and Spanish.

FEE-BASED One-on-one tutoring. Sign up in person for a one-on-one daytime tutoring appointment.

What to bring to tutoring
  Bring your syllabus, books, and notes to tutoring
  Have questions for your tutor
  Be ready to participate!
What to expect in tutoring
  Help from your tutor in learning how to do the work
  Tips about how to study the subject, where and how to find information, and how to check your own work
  Encouragement and friendly help
  Techniques and strategies that can help you to work with your classmates and on your own in your subject
How to sign up for small-group tutoring
  Go to the schedule, search for your course, click on ?Enroll,? and fill out your name and e-mail address. Click on ?Enroll.? When you see the confirmation, you have signed up for that session.
Canceling tutoring appointments
  Please call us at 480.965.6254 or at 480.965.7728 or e-mail TheLRC@asu.edu if you have to miss a scheduled session or need to cancel your weekly appointment. Students who miss 3 sessions without canceling in advance are dropped from their scheduled tutoring times.
