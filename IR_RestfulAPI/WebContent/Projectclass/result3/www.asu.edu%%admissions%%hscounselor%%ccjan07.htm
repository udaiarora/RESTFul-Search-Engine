Spring Welcome Program
Students who are admitted for spring 2007 at the Tempe Campus are able to attend the Spring Welcome Program Friday, January 12, 2007. For more information, please see the Web site.

2007-2008 FAFSA
The 2007-2008 FAFSA is now available online at www.fafsa.ed.gov. ASU has a priority filing date of March 1, 2007.

College Goal Sunday, February 11, 2007
ASU will host three sites for College Goal Sunday, where financial aid professionals will help students and families complete the FAFSA. For more information on location and what to bring, please refer to ASU's financial aid web site at www.asu.edu/fa/cgs.

Future Freshman Reception
Eligible students will be sent an invitation this month to attend a reception in their area to learn important steps to becoming an ASU Sun Devil and to meet other students in the area who plan to attend ASU this fall.
