Directions to Parking Structure 1 (Located on the south side of campus, Apache Blvd. and College Ave.)

Driving Directions to ASU at the Tempe campus, Parking Structure 1

From Sky Harbor Airport

All major airlines fly into Sky Harbor International Airport in Phoenix,  just a 15-minute drive from campus.

  • Take Sky Harbor Blvd. east to Loop 202.
  • Take Loop 202 east to Scottsdale/Rural Road (Exit 7).
  • Turn right onto Rural Road and proceed to Apache Blvd.
  • Turn right (west) onto Apache Blvd.
  • Turn right (north) on Normal Avenue, just past McAllister Avenue.
  • Follow the signs to Visitor Parking in Structure #1.

From Tucson / Southern Arizona

  • Take Interstate Highway I-10 west to US Highway 60 east.
  • Take US Highway 60 east to the Rural Road exit.
  • Turn left (north) onto Rural Road and proceed to Apache Blvd.
  • Turn left (west) onto Apache Blvd.
  • Turn right (north) on Normal Avenue, just past McAllister Avenue.
  • Follow the signs to Visitor Parking in Structure #1.

From Flagstaff / Northern Arizona

  • Take Interstate Highway I-17 south to Interstate Highway I-10 east (exit 200A).
  • Take Interstate Highway I-10 east to Loop 202 east (Exit 147).
  • Take Loop 202 east to Scottsdale/Rural Road (Exit 7).
  • Turn right (south) onto Rural Road and proceed to Apache Blvd.
  • Turn right (west) onto Apache Blvd.
  • Turn right (north) on Normal Avenue, just past McAllister Avenue.
  • Follow the signs to Visitor Parking in Structure #1.

Directions to the Fulton Center (Located on the north side of campus, University Dr. and College Ave.)

Driving Directions to the Fulton Center at ASU at the Tempe campus

Parking: Visitor parking is available in the parking structure directly north of the Fulton Center. Take a ticket upon entering the structure. The rate is $2/hour with a maximum exit fee of $8. Visitors should park on levels 1 and 2. Please take your ticket with you. Upon leaving the building, you will need to pay the cashier (or present your validated ticket) to obtain an exit code. This exit code is valid for 10 minutes. Please note that you will not be able to exit the parking structure without an exit code. The cashier booth is located between the north doors and the parking structure.

From Sky Harbor Airport

All major airlines fly into Sky Harbor International Airport in Phoenix,  just a 15-minute drive from campus.

  • Take Sky Harbor Blvd. east to Loop 202.
  • Take Loop 202 east to Scottsdale/Rural Road (Exit 7).
  • Turn right onto Rural Road and proceed to E. University Dr.
  • Turn right (west) onto E. University Dr.
  • The Fulton Center is located on the northeast corner of University Drive and College Avenue at 300 E. University Dr., Tempe AZ 85281
  • From E. University Dr., turn right (north) on S. College Ave.
  • Turn right into the Fulton Center parking structure.
