PTS 102: Temporary Parking Permits

Purpose

To provide temporary parking permits for short-term parking needs

Source

Parking and Transit Services

Policy

Decal Holders

Faculty, staff, and students who possess a valid ASU at Tempe campus parking decal and whose vehicle becomes inoperable may obtain a free temporary parking permit for a loaner vehicle for up to two work weeks. The temporary parking permit authorizes parking in the same lot that the regular decal authorizes.

Non-decal Holders

A temporary parking permit may be purchased by non-decal holders for $6.00 a day or $30.00 a week in designated surface parking lots.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Individual
  1. Provide ASU ID card (or driver’s license), license plate number, and vehicle make, model, and color when requesting a temporary parking permit from Decal Sales, Parking and Transit Services (PTS).
Decal Sales Office
  1. Verify the requestor’s ownership of a valid ASU decal.

  2. Issue a temporary parking permit for the authorized lot, charging non-decal holders, and provide a duplicate of the temporary permit to the Appeals Section, PTS.
Individual
  1. Display the temporary parking permit, as directed, in the vehicle.
endtable
