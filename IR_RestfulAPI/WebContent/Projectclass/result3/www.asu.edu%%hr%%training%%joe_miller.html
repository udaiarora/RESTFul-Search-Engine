Human Resources - Professional Development

Joseph Miller

Joseph Miller leads the Learning and Performance Solutions Group. His major roles include overseeing all programs, business operations, strategic planning, technology-based services, and entrepreneurial activities.

The Learning and Performance Solutions Group’s purpose is to develop the competencies and effectiveness of ASU employees, to support the Office of Human Resources consulting platform and its clients with services relating to consulting, facilitation, training, and organizational development. The group delivers strategic professional development programs in leadership, continuous improvement, team effectiveness, new employee orientation, employee recognition, and technology, as well as high-quality learning management services.

Joseph has worked in the training and professional development industry for twelve years, including eleven in ASU's Human Resources Department. He has an MBA from the W. P. Carey School of Business at ASU, a B.A. in Spanish from ASU, and has completed other graduate coursework in Educational Technology, Distance Learning, Global Technology and Development, and Research Statistics. He is also currently pursuing a Six Sigma Black Belt certification through the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering at ASU.
