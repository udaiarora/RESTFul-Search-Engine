Advising Office Hours:

  • Appointments taken Tuesday through Friday year-round.  Please call the Department of Languages and Literatures main office to make an appointment:  480-965-6281.
  • You will need to make an appointment if:  1) you are a Chinese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Russian or Spanish major with 87 or more credits.  This means you must complete a Declaration of Graduation form before you will be permitted to register for the next semester; or, 2) you are a transfer student majoring in one of the languages we offer and have already had an evaluation of your transfer credits completed by one of the CLAS advisors in CLAS Academic Programs, Foundation Building, 1120; or, 3) you want to participate in a Study Abroad or Exchange program.  Go first to the International Programs Office to pick up the program information materials and the application that includes an advisor recommendation form.
  • Most other questions can be answered over the phone or via e-mail.
