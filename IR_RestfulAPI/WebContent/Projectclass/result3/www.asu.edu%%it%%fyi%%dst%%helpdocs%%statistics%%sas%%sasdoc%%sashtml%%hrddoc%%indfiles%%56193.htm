SAS Applications Programming: A Gentle Introduction

by Frank C. DiIorio

This is the perfect guide for new and intermediate users looking for a practical, user-friendly approach to the basics of the SAS System. Numerous real-world examples, drawn from the author's experience using SAS software, are provided. This book is organized to introduce you to the more complex topics when appropriate, allowing you to master the SAS System at a reasonable pace. After completing this book you'll be able to perform the following with ease: manage multiple data sets, read complex raw data, perform complex calculations quickly, and write custom reports.
