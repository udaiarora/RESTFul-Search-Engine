Human Resources - New Employee

Benefits-Eligible Employee Orientation

This university-wide orientation program is coordinated by the ASU Office of Human Resources and is offered to all new benefits-eligible employees (a regular employee who is scheduled to work at least 50% time and who is expected to be employed for six months or more).

Prior to attending a session, new employees should obtain a Benefits-Eligible New Employee Envelope from their hiring department or from ASU Human Resources Department to assist in the successful completion of initial employee paperwork.

**An employee is generally eligible for benefits on the first of the month following both the date of eligibility and the date enrollment paperwork is received.  Therefore, participation in Orientation is recommended as soon as possible so that the employee can hear the benefits overview and make enrollment decisions.**

Time & Location

The Benefits-Eligible Employee Orientation is held in the University Services Building, Room 1302 on Mondays (except holidays) from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with a lunch break around noon.  To register for orientation, the hiring manager or the new employee should call (480) 965-4751 or email lps@asu.edu and provide the following information: the employee's name, employment category (faculty, staff, post doc, academic or service professional), department, and a contact phone number. 

For a map showing the routes to USB click here - Campus Map. For information about the shuttle to USB click here http://www.asu.edu/dps/pts/shuttle/usb.html

Visitor parking is available in the USB visitor lot #45.  New benefits-eligible employees may park in Lot 45 while attending Orientation and can obtain a free temporary parking permit at the beginning of the Orientation session that allows them to park in Lot 45 that day.

The visitor lot at USB is an unattended “Pay by Space” lot. The fee is $1.00 per hour up to 12 hours. The machine is located near the main entry to USB.  Pay by Space parking hours are from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

 Agenda

Payroll, ASU ID, & Parking

During the first 30 minutes of the session new employees may complete and submit their New Employee Payroll Packet, which includes their I-9 verification (a must for getting on payroll). A temporary ASU Identification card will be issued.  New employees may obtain a free temporary parking permit during the orientation session that will allow them to park in USB Lot 45 and in other specified lots for two weeks.  Employees who have been entered into the Human Resources database prior to Orientation may also obtain a permanent parking decal and a Sun Card.

ASU Overview

New faculty and staff will receive an overivew of ASU's culture, goals, and objectives. Additional information is offered on a wide range of resources, programs, and services. In order to comply with Arizona Public Service Orientation policy requirements, policies and procedures that govern our employment at ASU will also be addressed.

Benefits Overview The final segment of the orientation session will provide new employees with an overview of the ASU HR Benefits programs, including benefit and retirement options and benefit-related university policies. Information can also be accessed via the HR Benefits home page.  You have 30 days (for retirement) or 31 days (for health, life and disability) from your eligibility date to enroll in retirement and benefit programs.
