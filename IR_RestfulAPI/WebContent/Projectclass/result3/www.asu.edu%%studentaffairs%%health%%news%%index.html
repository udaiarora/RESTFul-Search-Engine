9/05/2006
Meningitis and Immunization

The Meningitis vaccine has arrived.
We have had many inquiries from students and their parents regarding immunizations. While the only immunization required by ASU is the MMR (measles vaccine), we also strongly encourage you to be immunized against meningococcal disease and to make sure that your other immunizations are up to date. You may obtain any of these immunizations from the ASU Campus Health Service. Please call 480-965-3349 to schedule an appointment with the nurse clinic.

8/04/2006
STD Testing at ASU
We have frequent questions about our prices for "STD Testing."For most students who wish this service, the tests we recommend are blood tests for HIV and a syphilis serology (RPR) plus urine tests for chlamydia and gonorrhea. You may optain any of these tests from the ASU Campus Health Service located on Palm Walk & University Dr.

8/24/2006
Student Advisory Committee
A new student health committee is hoping to convince students that illness isn't the only reason to stop by the Campus Health Service.

To promote the health center and receive more student input, a student advisory committee has been formed this semester.
