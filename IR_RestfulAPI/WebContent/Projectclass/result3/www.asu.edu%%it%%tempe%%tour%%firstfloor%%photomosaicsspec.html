Computing Commons

Room Specifications - Gallery

Service Description

The Computing Commons Gallery provides a modern facility for multi-disciplinary, multi-media, technology-based exhibits. It demonstrates to techies as well as technophobes the innovative and alternative applications of technology. The Gallery has provided the Computing Commons with a human element, underscoring the idea that technology is very much a part of life in today's society. Twenty exhibitions have been installed since 1993, with sponsorship by corporations such as AT&T, Silicon Graphics, and MCI.
