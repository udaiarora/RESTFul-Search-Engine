Danforth Chapel

Danforth Meditation Chapel, located on Cady Mall northwest of the Memorial Union and south of the entrance to Hayden Library, serves the university community as a place for quiet rest and meditation. Built in 1947, the Chapel was funded with an original gift from William H. Danforth and additional gifts from 75 Valley individuals, churches, civic and business organizations. Danforth Chapel is currently used for worship services sponsored by student religious groups, weddings, memorial services as well as for individual or group meditation, prayer or rest.

Danforth Chapel is open Monday through Friday from 7:00 am to 9:00 pm and on the weekends by reservation only. The Chapel accommodates approximately 68 individuals and may be scheduled for use by calling (480) 965-3570.

We invite you to visit the chapel.
