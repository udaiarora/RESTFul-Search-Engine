Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)
APForum Listserv

While the chair of CAPS will occasionally send notes to all academic professionals through the university?s distribution list, APForum provides a vehicle for interactive discussion among academic professionals at Arizona State University.

Any subscriber may send a note to the list.  Possible topics include information or questions about personnel practices across the university and announcements of events or opportunities of interest to the AP community.

 

To subscribe to the list, ?APForum? use the form below:

  Send me general listserv help.
 

OR:

  • Send a note to:  Listserv@asu.edu
  • In the body of the message type: Subscribe apforum
  • Leave the note's subject line blank, and remove your signature block from the body, if necessary.
  • You will soon get a note back, confirming that you are subscribed.

To send a note to APForum:

  • Address the note to APForum@asu.edu

To view APForum archives:

  • http://lists.asu.edu/archives

To sign off APForum, or for other listserv options:

  • Click on ?Subscriber?s Corner? in upper left corner of http://lists.asu.edu/archives
