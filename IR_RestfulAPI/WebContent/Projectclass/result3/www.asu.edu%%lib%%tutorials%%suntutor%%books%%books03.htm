Getting Started

Why use a catalog?

Couldn't you just browse the shelves of the library to find a book? You could, but

  • The library has a very large collection of books, over 3 million. That's a lot of books to sort through!
  • Books that contain information on a particular topic may be shelved in several different areas of the library, in special collections, or even in different libraries altogether.
  • Books are shelved in the library in call number order, so you'd have to be very familiar with the Library of Congress call numbers to find the small area devoted to your topic.
