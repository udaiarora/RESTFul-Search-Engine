EXCEPTIONS AND EXCLUSIONS
1.Courses devoted primarily to developing a skill in the creative or performing arts are to be disallowed. This includes courses that are primarily studio classes in the College of Fine Arts.
2.Courses devoted primarily to developing skill in the use of a language are to be disallowed. However, courses that emphasize cultural study and the study of literature are to be allowed.
3.Courses which emphasize the acquisition of quantitative or experimental methods are to be disallowed.
4.Courses devoted primarily to teaching skills are to be disallowed.
