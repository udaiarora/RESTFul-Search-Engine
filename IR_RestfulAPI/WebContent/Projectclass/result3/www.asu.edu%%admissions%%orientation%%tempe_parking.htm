ASU at the Tempe campus: Tempe, AZ

Check-In Information
All Orientation programs at ASU's Tempe campus are from 8:00 a.m. to approximately 3:00 p.m. Check in begins at 8:00 a.m. Optional campus tours will be offered in the afternoon.

Important Notes
All students planning to take the Calculus Placement exam should arrive at 7:15 a.m. with their photo ID to have enough time to be checked-in for the exam.

All students admitted to the Barrett Honors College need to attend the 8:30 a.m. Barrett Honors College Welcome.

Students must participate in the full day on-campus Orientation program to complete the registration process.

Accommodations
For information on hotels close to ASU, contact the Tempe Convention and Visitors Bureau: 800.283.6734; www.tempecvb.com. Or look for accommodations at www.phoenixcvb.com and www.scottsdale.com.

Parking and Directions
Parking for Orientation participants is provided in the ASU's Parking Structure 4.

  • From the Airport: (All major airlines fly into Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport, which is a 15-minute drive by car from the Tempe campus.) From the airport, follow signs for Loop 202 East. Take 202 East to the Scottsdale/Rural Road exit. Turn right onto Rural Road (going South). Follow Rural Road and turn right on Terrace Road. Turn left into Parking Structure 4, Visitor Parking Area.
  • From I-10: Exit at Broadway and proceed East. (You will know that you are going East if you cross Priest Drive within a relatively short period of time.) Turn left (going North) onto Rural Road. Follow Rural Road and turn left on Terrace Road. Turn left into Parking Structure 4, Visitor Parking Area.
  • From the Superstition Freeway (U.S. 60): Exit Mill Avenue. Turn so that you are going North onto Mill Avenue. (You will know that you are going North if you cross Southern Avenue within a relatively short period of time.) Continue on Mill Avenue until you reach University Drive. Turn right onto University Drive. Take University Drive to Rural Road. Turn right on Rural Road. Follow Rural Road and turn right on Terrace Road. Turn left into Parking Structure 4, Visitor Parking Area.

Important Note
ASU Orientation provides parking validation/pass only for one vehicle per party in Parking Structure 4, Visitor Parking Area (student and his/her guests constitute a party). Therefore, the party is responsible for parking fees for any additional vehicles or if the party decides to park in another location besides Structure 4, Visitor Parking Area.

Please allow plenty of time to drive to ASU's Tempe campus and park. ASU and the City of Tempe are currently completing a number of construction projects. Please be aware that some area streets around and within the Tempe campus may have limited access and/or may be closed. Also, local area public transportation may also be affected as routes are modified or changed. For more information, and recent updates, please refer to the following Web sites before attending your Orientation program:

ASU's Parking and Transit Services Web stile: www.asu.edu/dps/pts
ASU's Public Affairs Web site: www.asu.edu/ia/construction/projects.htm
City of Tempe Web site: www.tempe.gov/traffic/construction.htm

Once you have parked in Structure 4, proceed to the Memorial Union by walking on Terrace Road toward the center of campus. Cross McAllister Avenue and continue down Orange Street. (Once you have crossed McAllister, Terrace Road becomes Orange Street.) You will pass the ASU Bookstore on your left. Orange Street will become Orange Mall. The Memorial Union will be on your left. Proceed to the 2nd floor of the Memorial Union.
