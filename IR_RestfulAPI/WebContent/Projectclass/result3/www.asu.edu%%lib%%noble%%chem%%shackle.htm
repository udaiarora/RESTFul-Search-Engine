Current Position

Team Leader for Noble Library Reference Services. Provides information and reference services for the sciences, health sciences, and engineering; instruction in the use of libraries and resources for biochemistry, chemistry, and related subjects; liaison to the Biodesign Institute, the Centers for Meteorite Studies, Solid State Science, and the Study of Early Events in Photosynthesis and for the Department of Chemistry and Biochemistry.
