The reason that I pledge Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Inc was to become a founding brother of a chapter that would make me a better man. Lambda Theta Phi introduced me to another family that I would cherish for the remainder of my life. The fraternity would introduce me to aspects of life that would help fulfill my college experience and enhance my overall enjoyment of life. These aspects would be; Chivalry, Respect for all Cultures, Unity, Brotherhood, and for me most importantly PRIDE.
Lambda Theta Phi has introduced me to brothers of all nationalities and personalities. Through Lambda, I have been able to travel across the nation and be welcomed with open arms from brothers unknown by face, but bonded by name.
