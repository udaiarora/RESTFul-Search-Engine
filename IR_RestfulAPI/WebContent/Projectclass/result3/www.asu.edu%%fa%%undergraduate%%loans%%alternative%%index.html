Alternative Student Loans

As you pursue your educational goals, we understand that meeting the cost of your education can be a major concern.

We first encourage you to complete a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) to determine your eligibility for federal, state, and institutional aid.

If your awards are lower than your cost of attendance, you may want to consider an alternative loan. Some courses of study such as the Aeronautical, Architecture, and Law Programs are more costly.

Your loan application should reflect the amount of funds you wish to borrow for your actual period(s) of enrollment. If you plan to enroll for the academic year (fall and spring), then your approved loan amount will be disbursed half in the fall and half in the spring. If you only plan to enroll for a single semester in the academic year, please apply for a single term loan. The approved loan will disburse in that semester.

If you are a fall/spring student and you apply for a fall only loan, funds will be disbursed as a fall/spring loan (half in the fall and half in the spring). Students attending one term only are the only students eligible to apply for a single term loan.

How to Apply

To begin the application process with an ASU Preferred Lender online, click the name of the lender of your choice below, or call the lender directly.

If you prefer to apply for an alternative loan with a different lender, contact that lender directly.
