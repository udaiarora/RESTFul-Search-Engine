POLYTECHNIC CAMPUS GOVERNANCE GRIEVANCE COMMITTEE

The committee hears faculty complaints alleging unfair treatment because of absence or misinterpretation, misapplication, or violation of policies or procedures by other faculty or administrators. Membership: members are elected by the Academic Assembly. (Three-year term.)
