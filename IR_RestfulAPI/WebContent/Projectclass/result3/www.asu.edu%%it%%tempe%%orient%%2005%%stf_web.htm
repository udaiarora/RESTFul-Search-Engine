myASU

ASU’s portal, myASU, is a customizable and personalizable Web page that gives users access to a mix of news, services, resources, and on-line courses. myASU is also where students can access their ASU e-mail mailbox. To use myASU, all you have to do is log in with your ASURITE UserID and password.

ASU on the Web

ASU’s Web site has a wealth of information useful to students, faculty, and staff. Several services are available online, including checking your grades, viewing your class schedules, and checking financial aid status.

ASU E-mail

Every staff member is automatically given an ASU e-mail address in the form of firstname.lastname@asu.edu. This address is used for official ASU e-mail correspondence. You must have this e-mail address directed to an ASU Exchange (Outlook) account, an ASU Webmail (IMAP) mailbox, or any e-mail mailbox you may already have such as AOL, Hotmail or Yahoo.

If you did not provide an e-mail address when activating your UserID, you'll need to do so at my.asu.edu. After logging into myASU, select ASU Interactive , then select Update Your E-mail Address Information. Your ASU e-mail address is listed in the ASU Online Directory unless you request otherwise.

If you would like to use ASU e-mail as your primary mailbox, you will need to subscribe to ASU Exchange (Outlook E-mail) service, or Student E-mail (IMAP). You can add e-mail service by going to www.asu.edu/asurite. For more information about e-mail services go to www.asu.edu/mailbox.

Jukebox Web Site

The Jukebox site offers Web news, solutions, and suggestions with some sections specifically tailored to the Web environment at ASU.
