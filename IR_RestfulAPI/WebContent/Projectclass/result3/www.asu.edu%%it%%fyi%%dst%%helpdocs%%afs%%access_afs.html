How to access AFS space

There are different ways to access your personal AFS space. Here are a few:

  1. Access is available through one of the ASU UNIX clusters. For example use a client such as SSH to connect to "general.asu.edu" and after logging in with your ASURITE id and password you should have access to AFS.

  2. Access is available through one of the Microsoft Windows PCs or Apple Macs in the ASU computing sites. Please check with the site staff regarding assistance with this option.

  3. Access is available through a browser via the WWW. Point your browser to https://webmail.asu.edu/emma/, login with your ASURITE id and password and then select AFS Manager.
