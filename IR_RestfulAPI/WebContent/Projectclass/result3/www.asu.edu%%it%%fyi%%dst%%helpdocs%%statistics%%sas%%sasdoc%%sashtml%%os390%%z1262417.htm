Using Version 7 Engines

The V7 engine is identical to the V8 engine. SAS identifies V7 libraries and members thereof as having the V8 engine format. Members created with the V7 and V8 engines may coexist in the same library. This information also applies to the V7TAPE and V8TAPE engines.
