THE FOLLOWING ERRORS ARE UNFIXED IN RELEASE 4.1

In CROSSTABS, IN GENERAL MODE, THERE ARE TWO "PROBLEMS."

  1. If the upper left cell of the table is empty, the Kappa statistic, its ASE1, and t-value are computed incorrectly.
  2. When one of the cells in the first column is empty the risk statistic is incorrectly computed.

Work-around: Use INTEGER MODE CROSSTABS.

To obtain detailed information on unfixed errors in release 4.1 of SPSS, code the statement INFO ERRORS in the SPSS job statements. The following list indicates procedures or commands for which there are know errors of one sort or another.
