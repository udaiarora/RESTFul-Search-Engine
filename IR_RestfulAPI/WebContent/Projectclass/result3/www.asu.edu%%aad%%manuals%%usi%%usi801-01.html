USI 801–01: General Building Rules

Purpose

To ensure that the property and facilities of the Memorial Union are used appropriately

Sources

Arizona Revised Statutes § 36.601.02
Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 5-106; -108; -303
ASU Department of Public Safety

Policy

The property and facilities of the Memorial Union (MU) are intended for the educational, cultural, and social benefit of the university community. All other uses inconsistent with this purpose are prohibited.

Banned Items and Activities

Illegal drugs, firearms, and gambling in any form are prohibited in the MU.

Roller blades, roller skates, skateboards, bicycles, and the use of those items within the MU are prohibited.

Use of candles and incense is prohibited, except for ceremonial purposes and only upon receipt of a permit from Event and Meeting Services.

Smoking

Smoking is not permitted in the MU. Designated outdoor seating areas are provided for smokers’ convenience.

Alcohol

Alcohol may be served or consumed only at designated special events as authorized by Arizona Board of Regents’ policy 5-108(A)(4), “Sale and Consumption of Alcoholic Beverages on Campus” and USI 106–03, “Alcohol and Other Drugs on Campus.”

Children

When in the MU, children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult who will assume responsibility for their conduct and welfare.

Animals

Service animals and dogs used in law enforcement are the only animals permitted in the building.

Equipment and Furnishings

Equipment and furnishings belonging to the MU may not be removed from or loaned out of the building.

Art, paintings, pictures, and other displays may not be moved or removed under any circumstances.
