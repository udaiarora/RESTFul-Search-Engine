The department:

  1. prepares an Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form before accepting the equipment

  2. if equipment will be on campus fewer than 30 days, sends a photocopy of the form to Purchasing and Business Services

    Note: Purchasing and Business Services sends a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.

  3. if equipment will be on campus for more than 30 days, sends a photocopy of the form to Purchasing and Business Services and Property Control.

Purchasing and Business Services sends a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.

Property Control tags the equipment and adds it to the property control system at zero dollars.

Note: Property Control does not scribe loaned equipment.
