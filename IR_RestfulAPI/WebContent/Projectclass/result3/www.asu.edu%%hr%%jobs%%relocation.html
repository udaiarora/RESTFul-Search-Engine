Relocation Information

Staffing Services is pleased to offer the services provided by Arizona Insights to assist your candidates with their relocation needs.

Arizona Insights Relocation Center is available to assist hiring departments in the recruiting process by acquainting out-of-state employment candidates with the Tempe/Phoenix area and helping to ensure a smooth relocation. The service is free of charge both to the University and to the candidates. Upon notification from your department, an Arizona Insights Consultant will call your candidates in their hometowns and begin to acquaint them with the Tempe/Phoenix area while assessing the relocation needs of the entire family.
