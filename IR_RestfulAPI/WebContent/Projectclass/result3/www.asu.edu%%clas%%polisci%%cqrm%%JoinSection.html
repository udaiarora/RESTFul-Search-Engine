QUALITATIVE METHODS - APSA

JOIN THE SECTION

 
        

      

       

 

The membership of the qualitative methods section of the American Political Science Association is made up of individuals. (This is in contrast to CQRM, the subscribers to which are institutions). Members of the qualitative methods section must also be members of APSA.

If you are a member of APSA, and know your APSA member number, log in to MY APSA. After logging in, scroll down to "apply for additional services," and click on "organized sections." Click on "continue" to get to the second page, and check the box for section 37: qualitative methods. Again click "continue" and proceed as prompted. Click here to begin.

 

If you are a member of APSA but can't remember your APSA member number, use the "name search" database to locate your information and have it emailed to you. Then log into MY APSA. After logging in, scroll down to "apply for additional services," and click on "organized sections." Click on "continue" to get to the second page, and check the box for section 37: qualitative methods. Again click "continue" and proceed as prompted. Click here to begin.

 

If you are not yet a member of APSA, or were but your membership expired, and would like to join the Association and the qualitative methods section, please click here to begin.
