ONLINE INDEXES

             

Eighteenth Century Collections Online  Provides access to the digital images of every page of more than 150,000 books published during the 18th Century. With full-text searching of approximately 33 million pages, the product allows researchers new methods of access to critical information in the fields of history, literature, religion, law, fine arts, science and more.

 

British Humanities Index (BHI): 1962-Current
The database is an online index to (1962+) humanities journals and weekly magazines published in Great Britain and other English speaking nations including newspapers in UK

 

Historical Abstracts (1954+) Covers the world’s scholarly literature in history.  Includes article abstracts and bibliographic citations of books and dissertations on the history of the world from 1450 to the present, except for the United States and Canada.

 

Humanities Abstracts (2/1984+) Indexes articles in the fields of archeology and classical studies, film, folklore, gender studies, history, journalism, communications, language, literature, literary and political criticism, performing arts, philosophy, religion, etc.

 

 <![endif]>JSTOR Includes long runs of backfiles of scholarly journals.  Subjects covered include Anthropology, Asian Studies, Ecology, Economics, Education, Finance, History, Mathematics, Philosophy, Political Science, Population Studies, and Sociology

 

.Periodicals Contents Index (1770-1990/91) PCI Web is an electronic index to the contents of thousands of periodicals in the humanities and social sciences from their first issues to 1990/1991.  Every article is indexed.

 

 

SELECTED INTERNET SOURCES

 

Yahoo – History – France – French Revolution  

The beginning site for almost all History searches on the Internet

 

EuroDocs: Primary Historical Documents From Western Europe 

An excellent and comprehensive site for locating primary sources

 

LIBERTY, EQUALITY, FRATERNITY: EXPLORING THE FRENCH REVOUTION   "This site provides an accessible and lively introduction to the French Revolution as well as an extraordinary archive of some of the most important documentary evidence from the Revolution, including 338 texts, 245 images, and a number of maps and songs."

 

Internet Modern History Sourcebook

Fantastic history site!  Provides links to other Internet sourcebooks covering much of recorded history.  Contains a separate section on French Revolution resources.
