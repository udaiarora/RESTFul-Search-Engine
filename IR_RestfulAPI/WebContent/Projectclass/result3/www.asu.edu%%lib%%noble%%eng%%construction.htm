Books

    Construction books are on the 3rd floor of Noble Science and Engineering Library and in the Architecture Library  in the call number areas TA (Civil Engineering) and TH (Building Construction). However, some important reference books are kept in the Science Reference Collection or Science Reserve on the first floor. Government Documents are in Hayden Library.

To find construction books in Noble Library use the ASU Online Catalog.

  • To find a specific book, use the TITLE or AUTHOR search.

  • To find books about , use the SUBJECT HEADINGS search such as:
        Buildings
        Concrete construction -- Handbooks, manuals, etc.
        House Construction
        Masonry
        Structural Engineering
  • To find more books on your topic, use a KEYWORD search such as:
        adobe and construction
        building and estimates

Journals and Journal Articles

Construction journals are on the 2nd floor of Noble Science and Engineering Library, mainly in the call number TH 
  • Current issues are on the first floor in the Current Periodicals  Room.
  • Microforms are in the northeast corner on the first floor.
  • Older volumes are kept on the east side of the floor. Some older volumes are kept in storage; request these items from either the Noble Library Circulation Desk or Noble Library Information Desk.
  • For online availability, see the Alphabetical List of Electronic Journals. NOTE: electronic journals are only accessible from ASU computers.

To find journal articles about Construction use one or more of the following journal indexes:

  • Applied Science and Technology Abstracts 
    Contains references to articles. Abstracts (1993+); Contains some full text (1997+)
  • Compendex Web 
    References to over 2,600 journals, conference papers and technical reports. A few links to full text
  • Avery Index to Architectural Periodicals
    References to journal articles in more than 700 US and foreign journals
