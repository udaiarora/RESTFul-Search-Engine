Marketing Resources
MARKETING BIBLIOGRAPHIES and "WEBLIOGRAPHIES"
  1. Asia & Japan Business Information Sourcebook. New York: Wiley, 1994.
      HF 3820.5 .E64x 1994 Hayden Reference

    This comprehensive bibliography includes a profile on the country and its peoples and cultures. The work tries to provide at least one reference source for each of the areas of doing business. The appendices in the back also provide lists of relevant organizations and programs on Asia.

  2. Asian Markets: A Guide to Company and Industry Information Sources. Washington, D.C.: Washington Researchers, 1988.
      HF 54.52 .A785 A84 1988 Hayden Reference

    The purpose of this book is to help researchers get the information needed for business in the 10 countries covered. The first section covers the strategies and how the book works. The next part covers the organizations that produce/collect information. These range from the U.S. government to international organizations and associations. The middle section is the actual sources such as books and databases. The final section is agencies and sources in each of the countries.

  3. Dickinson, John R. Bibliography of Marketing Research Methods. 3rd ed. Lexington, MA: Lexington Books, 1990.
      HF 5415.2 .D5x 1990 Hayden Reference

    This comprehensive bibliography covers articles and books on every aspect of doing marketing research. It is especially good for those areas of primary research that are stumbling blocks for those who have not used those techniques before.

  4. Compendium of Marketing Information Sources. London: Euromonitor, 1989.
      HC 256.6 .C65x Hayden Reference

    A directory of the basic indexes, databases, journals, books, organizations and agencies, libraries, and market research companies of the UK. Sources are divided into official and non-official sources.

  5. Complete Direct Marketing Sourcebook. New York: Wiley, 1992.
      HF 5466 .F665 Hayden Reference

    Step by step procedures and worksheets or checklists for every part of developing a direct marketing program are the strenght of this work. Master copies of the worksheets and checklists are in an appendix in the back of the book. Another nice feature is the bibliography of additional helpful reference books on various aspects of direct marketing.

  6. Ganly, John V. Data Sources for Business and Market Analysis. 4th ed. Metuchen, NJ: Scarecrow, 1994.
      HF 5415.124 .F7 1994 Hayden Reference

    This outstanding guide lists virtually all of the major sources of secondary marketing information. Arranged by issuing agency (Census Bureau, trade association, etc.) and by publication type, each publication is described. Useful background information introduces each section.

  7. European Directory of Marketing Information Sources, 1987. London: Euromonitor, 1987.
      HF 5415.12 .E85 E97x 1987 Hayden Reference

    This is more than just a directory of the basic books, indexes, journals, and databases; it includes organizations and agencies, libraries, market research companies, marketing associations, and other important contacts. In the back, just before the indexes, there is a section with a socioeconomic profile for each country covered by this directory.

  8. International Directory of Marketing Information Sources. London: Euromonitor, 1988.
      HF 5415.124 .I57x 1988 Hayden Reference

    A companion volume to the European Directory of Marketing and Information Sources, this covers countries of the world EXCEPT those in Europe and the U.S.S.R. In addition to covering the agencies, libraries, associations, marketing research companies, important contacts and the usual books, journals, and indexes, a list of other guides to sources is included in the preface pages.

  9. Goldstucker, Jac L. and Jose R. de la Torre, Jr. International Marketing. Chicago: American Marketing Association, 1972.
      Z 7164 .C8G64 HAYDEN STACKS

    Cavusgil, S. Tamer and John R. Nevin. International Marketing: An Annotated Bibliography. 2nd ed. Chicago: American Marketing Association, 1983.
      HF 1009.5 .C37x 1983 HAYDEN STACKS

    Cavusgil, S. Tamer and Tiger Li. International Marketing: An Annotated Bibliography. 3rd ed. Chicago: American Marketing Association, 1992.
      HF 1009.5 .C37x 1992 Hayden Reference

    These three editions of the AMA bibliography on International Marketing covers the years up to 1991. Designed to be the articles of lasting value and useful in reviewing the literature of the decades covered by each edition, these work provide a solid long term overview of the field of international marketing.

  10. Herold, Jean. Marketing and Sales Management: An Information Sourcebook. Phoenix, AZ: Oryx, 1988.
      HF 5415.13 .H47x 1988 Hayden Reference

    An excellent, comprehensive bibliography of sources of information on all aspects of marketing.

  11. Marketing Information: A Professional Reference Guide. Atlanta, GA: Business Publishing Division, College of Business Administration, Georgia State University, 1982- . Biennial. (ASU owns latest year only.)
      HF 5415 .M2989x 1987 Hayden Reference

    Another extremely comprehensive guide to sources of marketing information. Part I, intended as a directory, is a guide to associations and organizations involved in the marketing process; Part II is a bibliography, briefly annotated, of printed sources of information arranged by general subject, e.g., international marketing, marketing research, new product development, channels of distribution, pricing, sales forecasting, computer-related marketing materials, and specialized areas of marketing such as services, nonprofit, and ethnic-group marketing.

  12. Research Resources: Electronic Reading Room for Advertising-Related Materials. Austin, TX: Texas Advertising, Department of Advertising, University of Texas at Austin, 1995- .
      http://advertising.utexas.edu/research/Topics.html

    This bibliography is a bibliography to online resources selected by the Advertising faculty at the University of Texas. Several good online bibliographies, jokes, slogans, and quotations are among the selections included.

  13. Sources of Unofficial UK Statistics. 2nd ed. Brookfield, VT: Gower, 1990.
      HA 37 .G7 M665 1990 Hayden Reference

    A bibliography that provides the address to associations and organizations that do surveys and collect data for the appropriate related businesses and/or consumers. Many of the associations listed collect market data. There is a title index and a subject index in the back.

  14. University Research in Business and Economics. [Morgantown, WV]: Bureau of Business Research, College of Business and Economics, West Virginia University, 1950- . Annual. (ASU owns 1950- . 1981- in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HC 103 .I5x Hayden Reference

    Formerly known as the AUBER Bibliography, this annual compilation of school-sponsored research indexes books, monographs, journals, newsletters, working papers, bulletins, and conference proceedings. Arranged by broad subject classification and by institutional sponsor. Useful for theoretical/research aspects of marketing and case studies. Most of the materials indexed are not covered by any other source.
