RSP 502–03: Project-Related Effort and Documentation

Purpose

To outline the process for documenting the effort and pay of university employees paid from a sponsored project, including contributed effort designated as cost sharing

Sources

United States. Office of Management and Budget. OMB circular A-21 (revised), Cost Principles for Educational Institutions
Office for Research and Sponsored Projects Administration

Policy

The payment of salary and the documentation of contributed effort must reflect the actual effort performed for the sponsored project during that payroll period or activity distribution reporting period.

All faculty and those graduate students and staff compensated from sponsored accounts document their effort regardless of the actual hours worked. The Activity Distribution Report (ADR) form is used to comply with federal regulations.

Cross-References

For further information on ADRs, see the Academic Affairs Policies and Procedures Manual—ACD 303, “Activity Distribution Reports.”

For further information on personnel on sponsored projects, see RSP 502–01, “Personnel Employed on Sponsored Projects.”
