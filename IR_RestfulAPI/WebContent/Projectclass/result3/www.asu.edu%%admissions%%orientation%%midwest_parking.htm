Midwest: Chicago, IL

Hotel Information
Hyatt Regency O'Hare Hotel
9300 West Bryn Mawr Avenue
River Road at Kennedy Expressway
Rosemont, IL 60018

Phone: 847.696.1234 or 800.233.1234
Fax: 708.698.0139
Web: www.hyatt.com

Check-In Information
Check in begins one hour before the program.

Morning Session: 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.
Afternoon Session: 12:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m.

Make sure to review the Checklist to ensure you are thoroughly prepared for your ASU Orientation program.

Students must participate in the full Off-Campus Orientation program to complete the registration process.

Hotel Accommodations
If overnight accommodations are desired, please call the Hyatt for reservations. Accommodations are not included in the program fee.

Parking
Hotel parking is available for program participants at a reduced rate. Parking is not included in the program fee.

Map and Directions

From O'Hare International Airport: Continuous complimentary shuttle service. Or, take I-90 East to River Rd. South exit. Hotel is on left.

From Midway Airport: Take Cicero Ave. north to I-55 South to I-294 North. Follow directions to O'Hare. Exit River Rd. South. Hotel is on left.

For the most up-to-date information, please return to this page before attending your ASU Orientation program for the latest details, or contact New Student Orientation with any questions you may have.
