W. P. Carey MBA - Technology Program Offering Additional Professional Certificates This Fall

Students entering the W. P. Carey MBA - Technology Program this fall will have the opportunity to earn one of four professional certificates while they are completing the requirements of the W. P. Carey MBA.

The W. P. Carey MBA - Technology Program is designed for professionals working in technology companies, and is offered part-time at the Arizona State University Research (ASU) Park in Tempe. Starting last fall the program began offering Six Sigma Black Belt certificates in conjunction with the W. P. Carey MBA. This fall three additional certificates are being offered: software engineering, systems engineering and entrepreneurship/intrapreneurship.

"Company leadership in the Valley are telling us at ASU that they want their technical teams to remain technically astute while preparing themselves to manage growing, successful business units," said Professor Dan Shunk, faculty director of the W.P. Carey MBA - Technology Program. "The Technology Program is designed to accomplish this mission. It is a unique blend offering the working professional a chance to do both. By adding a technical certificate to the world-class MBA, a unique career advancement opportunity exists."

Each of the certificates represents five courses built into the two-year schedule of the program. For example, the Six Sigma - Black Belt Certificate is fully supported by the university Applied Statistics faculty as one of the most valuable and one of the most rigorous Black Belts offered in the United States. Students who choose the entrepreneurship/intrapreneurship certificate may tailor their program for a management view, with an emphasis on strategy and creativity, or a technology view, which focuses on new product development.

The W. P. Carey MBA - Technology Program is a robust collaboration between the W. P. Carey School of Business and the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering, utilizing the faculty resources of both to deliver a distinctive degree that addresses the challenges of the technology marketplace from the perspective of both disciplines. For more information see wpcarey.asu.edu/mba/tech/index.

W. P. Carey School of Business

The W. P. Carey School of Business provides quality undergraduate, masters and doctoral programs that draw upon a dynamic learning environment to prepare students at all levels for the future. Internationally recognized faculty enrich the curriculum with their latest research findings, and deliver this knowledge in a technology-rich environment. Ranked 21st in the nation by U.S. News & World Report , the undergraduate program features Business Honors comprised of some of the best students in the country. The MBA, ranked 14th in the nation among public programs, combines business fundamentals with marketplace issues and trends, giving graduates the knowledge and experience to excel in a global economy. W. P. Carey School of Business alumni are lifelong learners connected to the school and each other through an active alumni community and executive education opportunities.
