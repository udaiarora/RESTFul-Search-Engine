ASU Residential Life Dormitories

As an AECP student, you may apply to live in an ASU residence hall. Dormitory applications with the application fee should be sent directly to the Residence Life office.

Prices for dormitory rooms vary and are based on two people sharing a room. Single rooms usually are taken by ASU students. There is no married or family housing available on campus. The minimum stay is eight weeks. You may purchase a meal plan to eat in the cafeterias or restaurants on campus.

Your dormitory application and a $75 application fee should be sent directly to the Residence Life office. They will not assign you to a room without the application fee. Since space is limited, you should send your application six to eight weeks before you come. If you are attending the August session, you should apply before April. You can print an application from the Residence Life Web site. Contact ASU Residential Life for additional details.

Adelphi Commons Campus Dormitories

These are new dormitories located next to AECP. They are open year-round. During the summer they offer single rooms at shared-room rates. Contact Adelphi Commons for details.
