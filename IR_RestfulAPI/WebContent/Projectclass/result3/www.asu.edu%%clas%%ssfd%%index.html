The Future is Now!
     

SSFD's Center for Population Dynamics is Officially Approved!

Social and Family Dynamics is a problem-focused, research-intensive school that is devoted to the study of individual, family, and social systems and institutions. This new interdisciplinary school is designed to be a force of world-class excellence for children and families in Arizona and beyond. It was formed by merging the program in Family/Human Development together with the program in Sociology. Students will benefit from the wider array of courses, faculty, and perspectives offered. Undergraduate and graduate degrees in Family/Human Development and Sociology continue to be offered!
