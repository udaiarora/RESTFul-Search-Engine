Café Biblioteca by Charlie
Grand Opening Brings International Flavor to the Library

(posted 10/12/04)


Café Biblioteca by Charlie, the new international coffeehouse located at the entrance of Hayden Library, will hold its Grand Opening Celebration on October 18-19. On those dates, there will be raffles and free giveaways to café customers.

The café hours are Monday -Thursday from 8:30 a.m. to 9 p.m.; Fridays from 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. and Sundays from 12 p.m. to 8 p.m. In addition to coffee, customers can purchase bottled water, juice, cappuccino, sandwiches, and pastries from the café (see full menu below)

The new café enables students to relax with a cup of coffee and a delicious pastry on the main entry levels of the library while studying and conducting research. With indoor and terrace seating and wireless access, the café also serves as a common grounds meeting place for faculty and students.

Charlie Mitchell is also the owner of three other cafés at ASU located in the Architecture Building, College of Law, and Honors College Buildings.


Friendly staff members at the Café Biblioteca by Charlie are waiting to serve.

Cold beverages and sandwiches are available in addition to coffee, espresso drinks and pastries.

Café Biblioteca by Charlie provides both indoor and outdoor seating, with access to the wireless Internet.
