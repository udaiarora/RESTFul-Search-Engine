Event Planning

The Memorial Union (MU) serves as the primary meeting center on the campus of Arizona State University for registered student organizations, campus departments, and, at limited times of the year, organizations and business from the larger community. The MU is an ideal setting for meetings, conferences, or special events due to its central location, 30+ meeting rooms, event amenities, and catering services.

Event and Meeting Services schedules the designated "meeting and/or event" space in the MU and dedicated portions of Cady, Orange, and Tyler Malls, Palm Walk, Student Services Lawn, and Hayden Lawn. Open weekdays from 8a-5p, please contact the Event and Meeting Services office to reserve space for your next event:

  • Phone at 480-965-3406
  • E-mail Event and Meeting Services
  • Fax request to 480-965-5834
  • Visit the office located on the main level of the MU behind the Information Desk

Distinctive Catering, Arizona State University's licensed catering partner, provides a variety of menu options with one stop shopping convenience. From meetings to wedding receptions, the Distinctive Catering staff can assist with all your food service needs. For more information or to request catering services please contact Distinctive Catering by phone at 480-965-6508 or visit the catering sales office located on the main level of the MU behind the Information Desk.
