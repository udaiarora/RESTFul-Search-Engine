Online Consulting System

The online consulting system (OLCS) is one of the many "help" resources available to the ASU community. It includes several consulting groups that are monitored by full-time and part-time Information Technology staff members. The consultants respond to email messages sent to these groups generally within 24-hours during business days. The email addresses and topics covered by these groups are:

  1. Comm-q

    2. Email address is comm-q@asu.edu. Deals with questions and problems with communications software (PPP, eXodus, etc.), communications between computers (sftp, ssh, x-term, etc.) and other communications problems

  2. Email-q

    3. Email address is email-q@asu.edu. Deals with questions and problems with electronic mail and conferencing systems (Outlook, Pine, netnews, etc.)

  3. Help Desk

    4. Email address is helpdesk@asu.edu. For faculty, staff, and students needing help with the following: File/Disk/AFS space problems; minor communications, email, and web page questions; ASU system/network status and service/equipment problem referral; microcomputer and general unix questions.

  4. client-server

    5. E-mail address is helpdesk@asu.edu. Provides support for UNIX ssh and OpenAFS clients, and IT UNIX servers and mainframe. For more detailed information please see http://www.asu.edu/it/fyi/dst/.

  5. Web Pages

    6. Email address is webpages@asu.edu. Deals with questions and problems with ASU web, creating personal web page, and about Web development and publishing at ASU.

  6. Other

    7. Assistance is available for a variety of other topics, too, such as computer accounts, data warehouse, etc. An extensive list of email addresses along with a description of the type of help available from each is shown at http://www.asu.edu/help.


Posting a message to an appropriate Online Consulting Group

Decide which group is the appropriate one for your email message then complete the following details and press the Send Message button to send it:

Your name
Your email address
Phone number, if you prefer phone contact
ASU Affiliation
Select group to send to
Enter subject header for email message

Please provide complete details of the problem into the box below, pressing RETURN or ENTER at the end of each line. The details such as the exact nature of the problem or error message, Mac or Windows, operating system type, name of ASU computer system, name of software, or any other pertinent information would help to expedite a response from the consultants.

When finished, press Submit Message to send your message. To clear the form and start over, press Reset.
