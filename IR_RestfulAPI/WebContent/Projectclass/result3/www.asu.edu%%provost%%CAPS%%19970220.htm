Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

February 20, 1997

1. It is with regret that Gary has accepted Christine West's resignation from CAPS. The committee would like to thank Christine for her hard work and contribution to AP governance. We will ask the AS Committee on Committees for the name of the alternative who will be appointed to replace Chris.

2. The committee approved the draft letter and ballot regarding changes to the CAPS bylaws; these documents will be made ready for distribution to campus APs.

3. Discussion of ACD 507-05 revisions: A draft revision was distributed for discussion and comment. This draft was accepted by the committee so the CAPS members who form part of the subcommittee (3 CAPS members and three members of the AS) will take this document forward to the meeting on Monday, February 24.

4. Request for revision of ACD 506-09 (regarding time frame for requesting and receiving letters of reference): It is the unanimous opinion of the members present that CAPS should work on the proposed revision.

5. Announcements: A call for nominations for the Commission on the Status of Women; a positions listing for the Director of the Intergroup Relations Center (faculty or academic professional); and discussed a letter to Campus Reps from Ubiquity about their concern over proposed legislation.
