ATP NOTES

  1. Transferable as lower division credit only.
  2. Students should select a course to meet the general studies area [SB or L] not met by COM course completed.
  3. Students are encouraged to select HU, SB and elective courses that also carry awareness designations such as global [G], historical [H] or cultural [C].
  4. Starting in Fall 2006, the BS degree in Molecular Biosciences & Biotechnology at ASU will no longer require 2nd language proficiency. Therefore students following this ATP agreement starting in Spring 2006 will be encouraged to change their catalog year to 2006-2007 once that catalog is released.
