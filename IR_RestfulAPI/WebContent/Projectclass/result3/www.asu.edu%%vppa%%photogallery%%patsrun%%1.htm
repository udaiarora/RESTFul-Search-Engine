Pat's Run

5,578 participants start down Rio Salado Parkway for Pat's Run, a 4.2 mile race in honor of ASU alum and Arizona Cardinal player Pat Tillman. The race was held in Tempe April 16, and completed on the 42- yard line inside Sun Devil Stadium. 

For more information on how you can contribue to the Pat Tillman Foundation, visit www.pattillmanfoundation.org
