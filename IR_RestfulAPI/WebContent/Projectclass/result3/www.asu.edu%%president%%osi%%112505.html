Cambrian College Announces Plans for Sustainable Energy Centre

EFS West

Cambrian College recently announced plans for a Sustainable Energy Centre at Cambrian's Barrydowne campus. This new facility will provide a focus for the study and development of alternative energy systems for residential homes and small buildings and, at the same time, will become a laboratory for students in Cambrian's new three-year Energy Systems Technology program.
