Intergroup Relations Center
In the Media

Local Recognition

October 2005

Dr. Lori B. Girshick joins the Center as the new Program Coordinator, Sr., for faculty development and research coordination. See State Press article.

September 2005

The IRC participates in a national, 3-year study on intergroup dialogue pedagogy. See State Press article, and the study's website.

June 2004

Dr. Delia Saenz, is the new director of ASU's Intergroup Relations Center (IRC), effective July 10. See Insight article.

April 2004

ASU researchers with the Women of Color Research Collective (WoCRC) based within the Intergroup Relations Center have garnered a $300,000 Ford Foundation grant to study diversity efforts in American universities. See Insight article.

February 2003

The IRC's conflict de-escalation work is featured in the ASU Insight.

October 2002

IRC mentioned by ally as strength of ASU in Arizona Republic's Public Pulse commentary.

September 2002

IRC 5th anniversay featured in the ASU Insight (employee publication). Visit www.asu.edu/news/printedition/pdfs/092702.pdf and scroll to page 3.

National Recognition

March 2003

IRC highlighted in the Association of American Colleges & Universities' Diversity Digest.

October 2000

IRC featured in issue of Matrix Unbound: The Online Magazine for Leaders in Higher Education!
