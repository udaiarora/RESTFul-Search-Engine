SAS/AF Software: Class Dictionary

Overview

The Block class enables you to create blocks, which are rectangular, text-based objects with three components. Blocks are useful for representing menu choices, or for representing icons on systems that do not support icons.

Blocks are composed of three parts: the rectangular area that contains the label, the label itself, and a shadow that makes the block appear three dimensional.

Parent:

Class:
