Voluntary Separation and Retirement Incentive Program

All participants in the VSRIP (Voluntary Separation and Retirement Incentive Program) are invited to attend one of the following retirement meetings.  The purpose of the meeting is to discuss your retirement options for medical, dental, and life insurance; the payout of accrued sick leave (RASL); and other ASU-specific retiree benefits. 

The meetings have been organized by retirement plan type. Please select the meeting according to your availability and whether you are retiring from the ASRS (Arizona State Retirement System) or the ORP (Optional Retirement Plan). 

MEETING SCHEDULE

Arizona State Retirement System (ASRS):
Thursday, March 25, 2004 at 8:30
Monday, March 29, 2004 at 2:30

Optional Retirement Plan (ORP):
Friday, March 26, 2004 at 8:30
Tuesday, March 30, 2004 at 2:30
Wednesday, March 31, 2004 at 2:30

The meeting will last about one hour and all meetings will be held in the Memorial Union, Room 209, Yavapai Room.

RESERVATIONS ARE REQUIRED. Each meeting can accommodate 20 people so we ask that you reserve your meeting date with us.  Please call Rachel Montoya, 480-965-8568.

General Information

ASU is implementing a Voluntary Separation and Retirement Incentive Program (VSRIP) for eligible employees beginning August 15, 2003. The class of eligible employees that may participate in the plan is limited to tenured faculty (including those currently holding administrative appointments) and academic professionals with continuing status who, by December 1, 2003, have attained, or will attain, the age of 55 and above with 10 years of completed service to ASU.

Information was distributed to eligible participants on August 15. This packet included:

  • The VSRIP Policy Statement (Microsoft Word document)
  • Contact List for the VSRIP
  • Questions and Answers about the VSRIP (Microsoft Word document)
    – Addendum to Q&A - Revised November 5, 2003 (Microsoft Word document)
  • VSRIP Agreement and Full and Final Release of All Claims (Microsoft Word document)
  • Instructions for Enrolling in the VSRIP (Microsoft Word document)
