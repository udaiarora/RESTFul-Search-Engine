Are you the and SOL of ASU?
 

 

ASU Student Orientation Leaders (SOLs) are a diverse group of dedicated, enthusiastic individuals who love ASU. They serve as role models and mentors to new students, and help educate new students and their families about ASU and ASU traditions.

If this sounds like you and you would like to become a SOL for this summer, please fill out an application and return it to Dale Pelz, Jr. in the office of New Student Orientation, Student Services Building, Room 110 by 5:00 p.m., Friday, April 14, 2006.
