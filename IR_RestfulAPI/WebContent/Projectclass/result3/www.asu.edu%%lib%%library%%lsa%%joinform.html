Letter of welcome and introduction

Welcome. We would like to offer you the opportunity to join the ASU Library Staff Association. The LSA is a non-profit social and fundraising organization run by officers elected from members of the Association and assisted by member volunteers who chair and help with various committees.

The annual dues for the LSA are $5.00, $2.50 for an employee joining after January 1st. Dues for student employees are $3.00, and $1.50 after January 1st.

"The purpose of this organization shall be to promote camaraderie, improve morale in terms of social atmosphere and enhance the environment of the staff lounges." The LSA holds a variety of annual or semi-annual events such as a Spaghetti Lunch, a Sunrise Breakfast, a Spring Tea (where 5-plus year employees and retirees are honored), a Holiday Dinner and events such as While Elephant sales, bake sales, and lunch-time potlucks and programs. The Association also sponsors a book sale on the 1st Thursday of each month, the profits from which greatly support the nominal membership dues toward the additional services the LSA provides.

The staff association provides to Library staff refrigerators, microwave ovens, hot water dispensers and toaster ovens in all the staff lounges. We provide the full kitchen and kitchen supplies in the Hayden main lounge, and this year we will cover the cost of the Holiday celebration. The LSA is the sole provider of these services. Without membership dues and support, these services would not exist.

There are over 100 members of the ASU Libraries Staff Association. This includes the elected board: President, President-elect (who also chairs the Program Committee), Secretary, Treasurer and Past-President. It also includes the chair and members of the various committees: the Social Committee, the Book Sale Committee, the Courtesy Committee, the Staff Lounge Committee, the Membership Committee, and the Bylaws Committee.
