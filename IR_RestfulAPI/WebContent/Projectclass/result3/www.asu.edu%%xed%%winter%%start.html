Getting Started

Winter Session is an intensive program offered between fall and spring semesters. Students may earn up to three semester credit hours that will be recorded on fall 2006 transcripts. College-approved overload petitions must be submitted for registrations of more than three credit hours. (Law students are limited to a maximum of two credit hours.)

Easy as 1-2-3

ASU Students. Meet with your advisor for course approval and ensure your selected course fulfills your degree requirements.

New or Readmitted Students. Apply for admission online.

Prerequisites. Check the general catalog or ask the course instructor if you have a question regarding course prerequisites.
New and readmitted students born after Dec. 31, 1956, must provide proof of measles (rubeola) and rubella immunity or two immunizations (at least one of which was given after Jan. 1, 1980). The immunization requirements can be waived for the Winter Session term only for students taking an online Winter Session course. Request this waiver online.

Online registration requests made through Dec. 1 will be acknowledged with an e-mail, showing payment due to the Winter Session registration office by 4pm, Dec. 1. Students wishing to pay Winter Session charges with spring financial aid cash awards can indicate release of funds at the time of the registration request. Beginning Dec. 4, registration is taken by phone or walk-in only. Courses not meeting minimum enrollment by Dec. 8 may be cancelled.

Online Requests. Requests will be processed in the order received.

  • Registration
  • Drop/add
  • Withdrawal

Registration Requests: Oct. 2 – Dec. 21.
Drop Requests: through Dec. 21.
