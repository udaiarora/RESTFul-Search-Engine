Room Specifications - Auditorium

Service Description

The auditorium is designed as a highly - mediated presentation facility for meetings, seminars, vendor presentation, etc. The auditorium is not utilized as a student lecture hall. The auditorium can function as an overflow facility for the adjacent video conferencing facility, or as a point of origin for a video conference or satellite uplink.

Infrastructure

  • Telecommunications
    • 2 Data connections
    • 1 Voice connection
  • Lighting
    • Dimmable incandescent recessed downlights throughout
    • Floodlights
  • Sound
    • 20 Ceiling mounted speakers
    • Walls lined with fabric-covered, acoustical/tackable panels

Equipment

  • Computer
    • 1 Pentium class PC
    • 1 Powermac class Mac
  • Multimedia
    • 1 Proxima Pro AV DP9300 (located in control room)
    • 1 Overhead projector
    • 1 AMX Control panel with 10 presets
    • 1 HiFi Stereo VHS VCR
    • 1 Wireless microphone

Special Amenities

  • Furniture
    • 2 Instructor lecterns (Egan Visual "Mobile Instruction Centre")
    • 1 Table with grommet and wire management channel 60"W x 29"H x 30"D
    • 1 Utility table 36"W x 29"H x 24"D
    • 60 Executive task chairs
    • 2 Stools
  • Visual Communications
    • 1 Dry erase marker board 206"W x 48"H
    • 1 Ceiling mounted electric projection screen 120"W x 120"H
    • 1 Ceiling mounted electric projection screen 240"W x 120"H
  • Millwork
    • Tiered millwork provides writing surface in audience area. Millwork has grommets and wire management channels.
  • ADA
    • 2 Exterior ramp leading to exterior door of auditorium, making the presentation/speaker area wheelchair accessible as well as the first tier of the audience area ( up to 10 seats).
    • Two wheelchair accessible locations at the rear of the audience area.
