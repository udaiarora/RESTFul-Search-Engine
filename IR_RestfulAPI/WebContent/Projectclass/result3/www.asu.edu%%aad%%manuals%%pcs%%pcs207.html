PCS 207: Transfers of Equipment to the University

Purpose

To identify and record equipment transferred from other institutions and agencies

Source

Property Control

Policy

Individuals must notify Property Control of equipment transfers from another institution or agency to ASU. Unless otherwise stated, title of equipment vests with the university at the time of the transfer.

Exception

Gifts and donations

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Department personnel
  1. Notify Property Control when equipment is transferred from another institution to ASU.
Property Control
  1. Tag the equipment and add it to the property control system.

  2. Prepare a journal voucher (JV) to add the equipment to the financial system.
If a request for acknowledgement is received from the institution that transferred the equipment:
  1. Sign the request and mail it to the originator.
endtable

Cross-Reference

For information on gifts and donations to the university, see PCS 206, “Acquisition of Gifts and Donations to the University.”
