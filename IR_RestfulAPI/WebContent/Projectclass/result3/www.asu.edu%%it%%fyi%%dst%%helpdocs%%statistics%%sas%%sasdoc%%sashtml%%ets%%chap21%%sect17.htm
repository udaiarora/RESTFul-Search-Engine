TABLES Statement

TABLES tablenames;
The TABLES statement prints the tables specified in addition to the tables that are printed as a result of the PRINTOUT= option in the MONTHLY or QUARTERLY statement. Table names are listed in Table 21.3 later in this chapter.

To print only selected tables, omit the PRINTOUT= option in the MONTHLY or QUARTERLY statement and list the tables to be printed on the TABLES statement. For example, to print only the final seasonal factors and final seasonally adjusted series, use the statement 

   tables d10 d11;
