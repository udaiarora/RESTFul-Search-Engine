The recipient’s supervisor obtains approval:

  1. from an administrative official within the recipient’s department to use compassionate transfer

    and

  2. obtains approval for initial and subsequent transfers of leave from Human Resources (HR).

    3. Note: The request must include a copy of a physician’s statement documenting the illness or injury.

The HR director or designee approves or disapproves the request to transfer leave in conformance with policy and notifies the recipient’s supervisor.

The contributing faculty member or academic professional completes the Request for Compassionate Transfer of Leave form (available in HR) and notifies the supervisor of the donation.

HR:

  1. determines the dollar value of the vacation hours contributed

  2. converts that value to sick leave hours for the recipient and notifies the recipient’s supervisor of the hours to be credited

  3. increases the recipient’s sick leave balance

  4. decreases the contributor’s vacation balance

    and

  5. retains a copy of the Transfer of Leave form for the Absence Report file and places copies in the contributor’s and recipient’s personnel files.

When the employee returns to work or receives long-term disability benefits:

  1. the recipient’s supervisor notifies HR

    and

  2. HR removes any remaining hours from the recipient’s accrued sick leave balance and credits them to the contributor.
