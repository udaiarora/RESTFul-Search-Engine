How to Order Official Transcripts
How to Order Unofficial Transcripts

Official and unofficial academic transcripts are available through the Registrar's Office. Financial aid transcripts are available through the Student Financial Aid Office (480) 965-3355. An official transcript is a copy of the student's permanent academic record and lists, in chronological order, all courses taken at ASU. It includes all grades received. It is signed and dated by the registrar and displays the seal of the university. There is a fee for each copy. Official transcripts are not issued for students with financial obligations to the university. Official transcripts cannot be ordered by e-mail or fax.

An unofficial transcript is an uncertified copy of the student's academic record which is provided at no cost. Unofficial transcripts also include additional information such as: line numbers, restricted withdrawal counts, note records regarding grade changes, semester gpa's and probation / disqualification status.

If you have questions about which type of transcript you need, 
please consult the recipient before ordering.

How to Order Official Transcripts

Official transcripts can be requested in person , by mail or via ASU Interactive. The fee for official transcripts is $10.00 per copy. "Rush" transcripts (requested to be printed and picked up the same day) will cost $10.00 in addition to the total cost of the transcripts ordered. Special delivery processing, instead of regular mail, is available via FedEx or U. S. Express Mail at $19.50 per U. S. States delivery address. Express deliveries to addresses outside the U. S. States, is available via International FedEx Express or International Express Mail at $38.00. The express costs are in addition to the cost of the transcript(s). (Please note that FedEx Express will not deliver to a PO Box, and actual FedEx deliverable street address is required.) Fees are subject to change without notice.

By Mail:

Official transcripts cannot be sent by e-mail or fax; however, a few destinations receive official transcripts electronically.

Students may order an official transcript by submitting a Request for Official Transcript Form (available on the Registrar Forms page) or a SIGNED letter including the following information: 
  • Name *
  • Former names (if applicable)
  • Arizona State University identification number (or SSN)
  • Date of birth
  • First and last dates of attendance
  • Current Return address
  • Daytime phone number
  • Specific mailing address for each transcript ordered
  • Select one of the following options to be displayed on the transcript:
    • ASU ID only
    • Social Security Number (SSN) only
    • Both ASU ID and SSN displayed
    • Neither ASU ID nor SSN displayed
      (These options are not available for Pre-1980 records nor rush, i.e., same day transcript requests.)

* Currently enrolled students may submit a Name Change Form by mail, fax or in person. All requests require a photo identification (if submitting via mail or fax, include copy of the photo identification) and one of the following; marriage license/certificate, adoption papers, court order, divorce decree, social security card or passport/permanent visa. Names are subject to a 25 character limit. Academic records for formerly enrolled students will not be changed. Certain exceptions apply. For further assistance call (480) 965-7276.

 

REQUESTS BY MAIL MUST BE SIGNED BY THE STUDENT. 

Pre-payment is required. See charges for official transcripts above. Make check or money order payable to Arizona State University. 

Send request to:

University Registrar's Office
P.O. Box 870312
Arizona State University
Tempe, AZ 85287-0312
