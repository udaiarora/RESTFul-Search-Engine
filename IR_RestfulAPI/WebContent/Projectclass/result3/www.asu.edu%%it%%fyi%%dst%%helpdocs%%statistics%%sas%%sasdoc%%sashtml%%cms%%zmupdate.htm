VSAMUPDATE

VSAMUPDATE


Enables or disables the updating of VSAM data sets by modifying or erasing existing records or by adding new records

Default: NOVSAMUPDATE
Valid in: configuration file, SAS invocation, OPTIONS statement, OPTIONS window
CMS specifics: all

Syntax
Details

Syntax

VSAMUPDATE | NOVSAMUPDATE

VSAMUPDATE
enables the updating of VSAM data sets.

NOVSAMUPDATE
disables the updating of VSAM data sets.

Details

VSAMUPDATE implies the specifications of VSAMREAD.
