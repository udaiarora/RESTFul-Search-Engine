Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

The Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS) shall act to improve the effectiveness and professionalism of all academic professionals at Arizona State University. Incorporating the tenets of academic freedom and respective professions, the Committee on Academic Professional Status shall endeavor to:

  • foster and develop the role of the academic professional within the academic community emphasizing both the academic and the professional nature of this role,

  • develop policies and procedures relating to academic professional service and representation that assures fair treatment to all members of this diverse class, and
  • provide leadership and governance of the academic professional community.

For more information see: Who are academic professionals and what do they do?

Meetings
