Who we are:
The Phoenix Union Devils (PUD) is a community service based organization consisting of ASU students, many of whom are graduates from the Phoenix Union High School District (PUHSD).

What we do:
The purpose of PUD is to inspire and enable high school students, especially those from the PUHSD, to realize their full potential and to encourage them to continue their education.  PUD is committed to serving the various needs of its members, the students of the PUHSD, and the ASU community.  All ASU students are welcome to join!
