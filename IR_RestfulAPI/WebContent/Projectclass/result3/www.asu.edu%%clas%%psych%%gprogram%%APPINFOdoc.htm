Dear Applicant:

 

Thank you for your interest in Arizona State University and the Department of Psychology.

 

There are a number of steps necessary in order to complete the application process.  They are as follows:

 

1.         The admission form including the $50.00 application fee, official transcripts, and official GRE scores must be sent directly to the Graduate College.

 

2.         A current Graduate Catalog may be requested through the Graduate College at (480) 965-6113.

 

3.         Documents available from our Department website include:

·       Applicant Response Form/Information Sheet

·       Letter of Recommendation Forms

·       Statement of Purpose Form

·       Application for Graduate Assistantship form. (For additional financial information, contact the Financial Aid Office at 480-965-3355).

·       Information describing our programs and faculty at http://www.asu.edu/clas/psych/gprogram.                                                 

4.         Send the following materials directly to the Graduate College for processing:

·       The Graduate College Application, the $50.00 application fee, and relevant official transcripts

·       Official GRE Aptitude Scores are required for all areas, **Advanced Test scores are strongly encouraged for Clinical applicants.

 

5.         Submit the following materials directly to the Department of Psychology:

·       Unofficial GRE scores

·       Unofficial copies of your transcripts

·       3 letters of reference

·       Statement of Purpose

·       Applicant Response Form/Information Sheet

·       Application for Graduate Assistantship Form

·       Stamped self-addressed post card for notification of receipt of materials

 

Your file will be complete when the Graduate College and the Psychology Department materials are combined to make an application packet.  We hope this information will help you in the submission of your application.  Our deadline is December 15th for applicants to the Clinical Program and January 5th for applicants to the other programs.  Preference will be given to those applicants who have completed their files by the deadline.

 

 

Arizona State University

Department of Psychology
