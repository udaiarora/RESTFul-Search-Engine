9.  TERMINATION

	This Lease can be terminated by Lessor or 
Lessee at any time by giving ten (10) days' written 
notice to the other to that effect.

	If either party shall utilize the services of 
an attorney or commence any legal proceedings against 
the other for default of any term or provision of this 
Lease, the prevailing party in each and every instance 
shall be entitled to receive from the other party all 
expenses in connection thoewith, including reasonable 
attorneys' fees and costs as fixed by the Court.
