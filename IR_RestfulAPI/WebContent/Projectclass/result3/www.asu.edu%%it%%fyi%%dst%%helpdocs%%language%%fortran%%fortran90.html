FORTRAN 90 compilers

Q. Which ASU UNIX systems have the FORTRAN 90 compilers?

The systems are:

Medusa: (SGI -Power Challenge) 

  
    f90   (man f90 <---  will give more information)

Research Cluster: (IBM/RS/6000 

  xlf    (man xlf  <---- will give more information on options you 
	                 needed to compile programs, but it does not 
			 have all the features yet.)

Note: we plan to upgrade the cluster to AIX 4.1 and at that point the Research cluster will also have FORTRAN 90 complier (xlf90 or xlF ). This will occur in the next 6 to 7 months.

Presently the xlf options for Fortran 90 are:

xlf -Pk -Wp,-translate=av -O progra.f

or

xlf -Pk -Wp,-gvn myprogram.f

For more information on those options, please refer to page 195 of the XLF User Guide manual available in COMPASS.
