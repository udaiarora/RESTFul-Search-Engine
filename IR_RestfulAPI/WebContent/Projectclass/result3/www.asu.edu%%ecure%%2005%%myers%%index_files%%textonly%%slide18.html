Where We Are Now . . .

Electronic Records Working Group (ERWG)

  • Formed 2002

  • Representatives from KDLA, COT, and the Offices of the Attorney General and Auditor of Public Accounts

  • Develops suggested policies, guidelines, and recommendations for addressing electronic records issues.

Continues the 20+ year of dialog on electronic records issues.

  • KISK, KIRM, Empower

Designed to be small and manageable.

Focuses on the major issues in recordkeeping: Historical, IT, Legal, Fiscal

Serves as an Advisory Committee recommending to other groups for action.

Gives us more expose in state government

  • Opportunity to address the CIO Advisory Council, Web Standards Committee, Architecture & Standards

  • Able to address the Gov. Executive Cabinet

  • By attaching our guidelines to the Architecture Standards, more IT will see it.

Gives more weight to our Guidelines and Standards

  • Part of the Architecture

  • Has the approval of Auditors and AG
