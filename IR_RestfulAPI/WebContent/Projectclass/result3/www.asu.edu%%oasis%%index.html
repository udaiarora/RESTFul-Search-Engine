The OASIS Project

The OASIS (Online Administrative & Student Information System) project will replace and enhance the current Human Resources and Student Information Systems at ASU with Oracle's PeopleSoft Human Capital Management and Student Administration systems software. ASU staff from multiple colleges and campuses will be working to phase in the new software for the Fall 2007 semester for student processes, with Human Capital Management to follow.
