mindBODY - Instructors

Julie Dreyer
Picture Coming Soon!
Julie has loved yoga since she discovered the transformational practice in 2001. With the belief that yoga can benefit everyone, she has created classes for many different populations, including her corporate co-workers, pre-natal women, teenagers, kids, seniors, kayakers, surfers, hikers, runners, even couch potatoes. She brings enthusiastic energy to her classes as she encourages fellow yogis to simply "be what they are."
 
 
Connie Engel
Picture Coming Soon!
Connie found yoga in 2001, and noticed the deep connection between body and mind from the start. This connection inspired her to continue practicing and observing the effects of yoga in her life. In 2004, she began teacher training at Inner Vision Yoga, continuing with the 500 hour certification which she is currently completing. Her classes infuse dynamic movement, deep awareness, and playfulness, into the practice. She seeks to inspire students to explore and experience their bodies and spirits and their interconnection with all that is around them.
 
 
Sherri Eshkibok
Picture Coming Soon!
Yoga came into my life about 8 years ago, as a perfect balance for my high-stress, and less-than-holistic life style.  I fell in love with the physical, mental and spiritual aspects of yoga, and chose to become certified to teach in 2003 at Southwest Institute of Healing Arts.  Yoga has transformed my life on many levels and teaching allows me to pass on the gift of yoga to others.  My philosophy includes creating an open, safe, fun and challenging environment that encourages all students to find and "work" their edge while always honoring their body.

Jeannie is graduate of ASU with a degree in Physical Education.  She did her Yoga Teacher Training at the South West Institute of Healing Arts in Tempe and got her Pilates Certification through the National Endurance and Sports Trainers Association.  She is a member of Yoga Alliance and AFAA has been teaching Pilates and Yoga since 2003. Jeannie believes in and demonstrates a holistic approach to fitness, she understands the benefits of a mind-body connection and her mission is to share that message with you.

Danae's yoga journey began with a Bikram class in 1999 wherein a brief love affair ensued and from there she began to explore the many other forms of yoga. She completed her formal yoga training in magical Sedona , Arizona and finds such pleasure in sharing her knowledge of yoga with others.
