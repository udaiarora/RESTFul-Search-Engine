Librarians' Council


Minutes of Meeting


October 18, 2000


Meeting called to order at 3:05 p.m.

 

Chair: Pat Etter

Secretary: Gordon Dutrisac

A quorum was present.


I. Approval of Minutes

 

Minutes of September 2000 meeting were approved as written.

 

II. Chair & Executive Committee Report

 

No report

 

III. Committee Reports

 

Personnel Committee - Helen Seaton

No report

Review Committee - Marcia Anderson

No report

Research and Professional Development Committee

Tom Turner reported that the Committee has received the allotment of funds in support of travel outside Maricopa County for this academic year. Individual allotments will not be known until after the November 3rd deadline for travel fund applications. Tom reminded department heads to inform the Committee of any pending hires that may need funding during the fiscal year.

Compensation Committee - Ken Furuta

No report.

Academic Senate - George Bell

No report.

Committee on Academic Professional Status

Helen Seaton reported that CAPS will host three brown bag lunches this academic year to highlight the achievements of Academic Professionals at ASU. The first of these will occur on Wednesday November 1 from 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM in the Apache Room (#221) of the Memorial Union. The speaker will be Richard Filley of the College of Engineering and Applied Science.

Web Master - Annie Platoff

Annie Platoff reported that forms (equipment requests, personnel action forms, etc.) are now on the Library's web site (http://www.asu.edu/lib/admin/forms.htm). Links to the forms are found on the Librarians’ Council and Library Administration web pages.

 

V. Old Business

 

  • Revisions to the Bylaws of the Librarians’ Council

    Annie Platoff proposed a friendly amendment to the revisions to the Bylaws and to section C.2.1. She proposed that the title of Web Master be replaced by the title Page Master. Annie explained that the title Page Master better fit the responsibilities asked of this position. The amendment passed unanimously.

    After a brief discussion the revisions to the Bylaws outlining the roles and responsibilities of the Parliamentarian and Page Master were unanimously passed as amended.

  • Revisions to Librarians' Handbook Section C.2.1 (Standing Rules and Committees - Executive Committee)

    It was pointed out that the Procedures paragraph of section C.2.1 was inconsistent with the proposed revisions. All present agreed that these differences were a matter of housekeeping and asked that the Executive Committee clean up section C.2.1. The revisions to section C.2.1 formalizing the role of the Parliamentarian and Page Master in Executive Committee meetings were unanimously passed as amended.

VI. New Business

 

None

 

Meeting Adjourned at 3:23 p.m.

Gordon Dutrisac, Secretary
