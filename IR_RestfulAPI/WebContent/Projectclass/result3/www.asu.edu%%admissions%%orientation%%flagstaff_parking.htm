Northern Arizona: Flagstaff, AZ

Hotel Information
Radisson Woodlands Hotel
1175 West Route 66
Flagstaff, AZ 86001

Reservations: 800.333.3333
Fax: 928.773.0597
www.radisson.com/flagstaffaz/

Check-In and Schedule Information
The program runs from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Check-in begins at 9:00 a.m.

Make sure to review the Checklist to ensure you are thoroughly prepared for your ASU Orientation program

Students must participate in the full Off-Campus Orientation program to complete the registration process.

Hotel Accommodations
If overnight accommodations are desired, please call the Radisson for reservations. Accommodations are not included in the program fee.

Parking
Hotel parking is available for program.

Map and Directions

From Tuba City: Take Interstate 40 West. Exit north onto 89A/S. Beulah Blvd. S. Beulah Blvd. ends at Milton Avenue. Turn left on Milton to Route 66 West. A Barnes & Noble Bookseller will be your landmark on the left-hand side. Turn left on Route 66 West. The Radisson is at 1175 West Route 66, on your left.

From Sedona (Airport): Travel north on State Route 89A. 89A becomes S. Beulah Blvd., and ends at Milton Avenue. Turn left on Milton to Route 66 West. A Barnes & Noble Bookseller will be your landmark on the left-hand side. Turn left on Route 66 West. The Radisson is at 1175 West Route 66, on your left.

From the West: Take Interstate 40 East. Exit north onto 89A/S. Beulah Blvd. S. Beulah Blvd. ends at Milton Avenue. Turn left on Milton to Route 66 West. A Barnes & Noble Bookseller will be your landmark on the left-hand side. Turn left on Route 66 West. The Radisson is at 1175 West Route 66, on your left.
