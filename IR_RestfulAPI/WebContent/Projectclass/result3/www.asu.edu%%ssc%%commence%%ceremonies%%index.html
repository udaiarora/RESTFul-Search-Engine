Other ASU Ceremonies

University Ceremonies are celebrations in the life of the university. They keep ceremonial traditions alive in the modern academic institution.

Commencement ceremonies and graduation represent the culmination of a student's academic achievement. ASU also celebrates the begining of each academic year and honors faculty achievement with another ceremony:

New Student Convocation

Friday, August 18, 2006
9:00 am
Wells Fargo Arena

Welcome to the annual opening new student convocation, which officially begins ASU's academic year. Please be sure to arrive on time; then plan to attend your college activities immediately following. For more information, visit http://www.asu.edu/studentaffairs/mu/transitions/Sundevil101/must_attend.htm
