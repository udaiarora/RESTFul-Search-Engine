Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

February 5, 2003

Members Present: Patricia Feldman, Carol Behl, Stefan Myhajlenko, Victoria Trotta
Excused Absent: Sue Hunter, Barbara Trapido-Lurie

1. Minutes from 12-04-02 Meeting previously approved and posted to 
the  CAPS website.  

2. OLD BUSINESS

i) Chair Patti Feldman reported that Academic Senate President George Watson 
accepted the CAPS letter requesting that the issue of voting rights  for 
Academic Associates be reviewed.  Professor Watson indicated that the Academic 
Senate is undertaking a review of the Constitution and Bylaws and this request is 
timely. Tory Trotta will report back to CAPS on the Academic Senate Committee 
that is spearheading the review. We will be sure that the letter/request is forwarded 
to that committee as well. [The University Affairs Committee, chaired by Karen Dwyer 
in English is the group in charge.]

ii) The Chair had raised with Vice Provost Gail Hackett the issue of whether or 
not there would be an AP representative on the Promotion and Tenure Task Force. 
That Task Force has been constituted and there is no AP representative. 
Discussion ensued.

iii) Spotlight Speaker Series. CAPS felt that the Fall Spotlight Speaker was a 
huge success. We would like to hold a Spring Spotlight Speaker event. CAPS 
discussed inviting Vice Provost Hackett to address the group.  Dates were discussed. 
If Dr Hackett declines, it was decided to post a message to the AP distribution 
list for speaker ideas. Possible dates are 3/5, 4/2 [not good for academic advisors 
but a later date in April will work], 5/7 or 6/4. The Chair will ask Dr Hackett if she is 
available.

 

 3. NEW BUSINESS

i) The committee discussed whether or not a recommendation should be made 
concerning the term of committee alternates, which currently is for one year only.

ii) The Chair asked the committee to transmit recommendations on AP?s to stand 
for office. She also asked the committee to send recommendations to her on serving 
as an election teller. This is a short-term assignment. 6 are needed.

iii) Tory Trotta gave a report on the Committee on Committee meeting she attended 
to represent the CAPS Chair, a member of that committee.

 

The meeting was adjourned at 10:45 a.m.

Respectfully submitted, Victoria K. Trotta (Secretary)

Minutes were approved 2/11/2003
