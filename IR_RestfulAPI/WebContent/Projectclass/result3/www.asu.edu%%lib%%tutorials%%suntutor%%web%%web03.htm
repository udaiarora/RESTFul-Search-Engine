How to find information on the Web

Browsing and Searching

Why browse?

There are many reasons that you might want to browse for information on the Web:

  • You don't know enough about a subject to develop an effective search.
  • Your subject is too broad for a keyword search to be effective.
  • You can define your topic as you go along by selecting from lists of subcategories.
  • You like to browse.
