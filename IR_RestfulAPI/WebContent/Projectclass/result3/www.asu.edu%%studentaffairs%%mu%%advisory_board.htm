The Memorial Union Advisory Board’s mission is to ensure the successful growth and development of the Memorial Union as the hearthstone of the university community. The purpose of the MUAB is to initiate, formulate, and review Memorial Union (MU) policies and practices including budget, marketing, food service, facilities, programming, and planning.  The MUAB will make recommendations to the Executive Director of the MU on behalf of and inclusive of ASU students, faculty, staff, alumni, and community, while fully supporting the missions of the MU and Arizona State University (ASU) and assessing the needs of the ASU community

There are six committees co-chaired by our board members. The committees represent the comprehensive make-up of the MU. Each committee will soon be recruiting individuals to join their group to review different aspects of the Memorial Union and determine how it can better serve the ASU community.

If you are interested in applying for board membership, please fill out a student application or a staff/faculty application. Applications are due Friday, September 8, 2006 at 5pm. Please turn completed applications in to the Memorial Union Information Desk.
