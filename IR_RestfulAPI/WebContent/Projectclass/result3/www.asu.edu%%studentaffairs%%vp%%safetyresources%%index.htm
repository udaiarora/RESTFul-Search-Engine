Services

Counseling and Consultation, 480-965-6146
Provides consultation and outreach services to the university community. Will consult with academic departments on student issues and concerns.

Department of Public Safety, 480-965-3456 (in emergency call 911)
ASU's police department responds to criminal and emergency situations and provides prevention assistance.

Employee Assistance Program, 480-965-2271
Provides confidential employee counseling resources.

Safety Escort Services, 480-965-1515
Provides escorts between campus buildings, residence halls and parking facilities during evening hours.

Student Life, 480-965-6547
Student Judicial Affairs promulgates student code of conduct issues. Student Advocacy and Assistance provides assistance to students with life issues affecting their academic experience.

ASU Hotline, 877-SUN-DEVL (877-786-3385)

 

Policies and Guides

Faculty Guide for Identifying Students in Distress
This Web page, created by Counseling and Consultation, can assist faculty in determining if a student needs assistance.

Disruptive Student Behavior (Word file)
Published by Student Life and General Counsel, provides information for faculty on how to address disruptive students in and outside the classroom.

Critical Incident Response Guidelines
Published by Student Life and Student Affairs, provides information for faculty and staff on how to respond to individual student crises, including mental health crises and harassment. Available online, and also in print upon request.

Emergency Flipchart
DPS publishes this guide to responding to large campus emergency situations.

Student Affairs Policies and Procedures
Policies that address student conduct issues and administrative procedures as well as other campus safety issues.

  • Handling Disruptive Individuals on Campus
  • Hazing Prevention
  • Protocol for Response to Student Victims of Sexual and Relationship Violence
  • Involuntary Withdrawal from the University in Special Circumstances
  • Death of a Student
