How to Apply - Summer 2007                                                                                                

 

If you are interested in applying to the summer program, contact your high school counselor or mathematics/science teacher for an application packet or download the application packet.

 

Submit the following information -

 

  • Application Form

 

  • Personal/Career Statement

 

  • Recommendation form from a mathematics teacher, science teacher, or school counselor

 

  • Official high school transcript (include fall 2006 semester)

 

  • Consent for access to education records form

Incomplete Applications will not be considered.

 

Mail Application Materials To:

 

Math-Science Honors Program

Arizona State University

PO Box 871804

Tempe, Arizona 85287-1804

 

** Complete applications must be postmarked no later than Friday, March 2, 2007
