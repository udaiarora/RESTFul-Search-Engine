PTS 105: Replacement of Lost, Damaged, or Stolen Decals and Gate Access Cards

Purpose

To define the procedure for replacing lost, damaged, or stolen parking decals and gate access cards

Sources

Arizona Revised Statutes § 13–2907.01
Parking and Transit Services

Policy

Decal holders should report lost or stolen ASU parking decals and gate access cards to the Decal Sales Office, Parking and Transit Services (PTS) as soon as possible.

Decal holders pay a replacement fee for lost, damaged, or stolen decals or gate access cards. PTS sets all fees in accordance with established university policy. The Decal Sales Office replaces lost, damaged, or stolen decals and gate access cards after proper verification with an ASU ID card (or driver's license) and payments.

ProceduresD

Lost or Stolen Decal and Gate Access Cards

Responsibility
Action
Decal holder
  1. Report a lost or stolen decal or gate access card in person to the Decal Sales Office, Parking and Transit Services.

    Note:The office is located on Fifth Street between Forest and College Avenues. Decal holders may also call 480/965–6124 to request the necessary forms to be sent by mail.

  2. Complete a Parking Decal Application for a replacement decal.

  3. Read, complete, and sign the Lost/Stolen Parking Decal Affidavit.

  4. Present an ASU ID card (or driver's license) and pay the replacement fee(s) for the decal or gate access card.
Decal Sales Office
  1. Verify:

    1. the identity of the person requesting the new decal or gate access card

      and

    2. the requestor’s current ownership of the decal or gate access card in question.

  1. Accept replacement fee(s) for lost or stolen decal or gate access card.

  2. Enter the transaction into the parking management database.

  3. Deactivate the lost or stolen decal or gate access card.

  4. Place lost or stolen decal numbers on the appropriate list for enforcement officers.

Damaged Decal or Gate Access Card

Responsibility
Action
Decal holder
  1. Turn in identifiable remnants of the decal, i.e., at least two of the decal numbers, or the gate access card to the Decal Sales Office.

  2. Complete a Parking Decal Application for a new decal.

  3. Present an ASU ID card (or driver's license) and pay the replacement fee(s) for the decal or gate access card.
Decal Sales Office
  1. Verify:

    1. the identity of the person requesting the new decal or gate access card

      and

    2. the requestor’s current ownership of the decal or gate access card in question.

  1. Accept replacement fee and issue replacement decal or gate access card.

  2. Enter the transaction into the parking management database.

  3. Deactivate the damaged decal or gate access card.
endtable
