Room Specifications - Storage Room

Service Description

This room provides stoarge for multiple Information Technology units. Telecommunications has one locakacble cage for wiring and equipment. The computer store utilized another lockable cage as the receiving/storage of software, hardware and perfirals. The center of the room is used for the storage of paper, supplies and repair/extra furniture. A double set of door leads to the loading dock. Lastly a small portion of the room is used as a modest breakroom by Information Technology and Computer Store staff for lunches and breaks.
