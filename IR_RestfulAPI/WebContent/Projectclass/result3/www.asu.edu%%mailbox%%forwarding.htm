E-Mail Forwarding

Students are given an asu.edu e-mail address upon applying to ASU. You can choose to have e-mail sent to you at this asu.edu e-mail address to a personal mailbox you already own such as a AOL, Hotmail, Yahoo or GMail account. If you prefer you can subscibe to an ASU IMAP mailbox. See instructions for both below:

I want to receive my asu.edu e-mail in my personal account. How do I register my external e-mail account address with ASU ?

To have e-mail sent to you@asu.edu go to your external e-mail account address (such as hotmail, yahoo, cox, etc.):

  1. Log in to myASU (http://my.asu.edu/, choose 'emma' link on top right) with your ASURITE UserID and passphrase. This will open a new window of a program called emma
  2. Choose EPO Update from the main menu to the left
  3. Under Destination Address, enter the e-mail address and click on Update
    (Note: It usually takes 15-30 minutes for the changes to come into effect)

I want to receive e-mail in an ASU mailbox. How do I subscribe for an ASU E-Mail account/mailbox and other ASURITE services? *

Once the ASURITE UserID has been activated and the student has registered and paid for classes, he or she can add services, including getting a free ASU e-mail mailbox (IMAP e-mail). To add this (and other services) follow these instructions:

  1. Go to http://asu.edu/asurite
  2. Log in using your ASURITE UserID and passphrase
  3. Choose the Add Services button
  4. Select Student E-Mail Service from the available options (And any other service you may wish to add at this time)
  5. For Student E-Mail Service, be sure to accept the defaults so that your destination address updates accordingly
  6. Be sure to save any changes and use the logout button when you are finished
  7. To access this new account, please go to http://my.asu.edu and choose the WebMail tab once you have logged in
    (This process can take several hours to take full effect)

Accessing your ASU E-mail **

ASU provides a web-based e-mail interface to your ASU IMAP e-mail account which allows you to check your ASU e-mail from anywhere you can gain access to an Internet connection:

  1. Open up a browser and go to http://my.asu.edu.
  2. Enter your ASURITE UserID and passphrase
  3. Click the tab link that reads Webmail
  4. Your IMAP E-mail account Inbox view will appear in the bottom frame
