        Sample Scholarly Publications by Dr. Croft

Croft, Lee B. "The Method to Madness in a Poem by Chinnov" in SLAVIC AND EAST EUPROPEAN JOURNAL (Vol. 17, No. 4 (Winter 1973)), pp. 408-413. In the same journal, see also "The Mnemonic Use of Linguistic Iconicity in Teaching Language and Literature" (Vol. 22, No. 4 (Winter 1978)), pp. 509-18.

Croft, Lee B. "The Expression of Modality in English and in Russian: A Contrastive Analysis" in RUSSIAN LANGUAGE JOURNAL (Vol. XXIX, No. 104 (Fall 1975)), pp.5-25.

Croft, Lee B. "Charlie Chaplin and Olesha's ENVY" in CLA JOURNAL (JOURNAL OF THE COLLEGE LANGUAGE ASSOCIATION) (Vol. XXI, No.4 (June 1978)), pp. 525-37. In the same journal, see also "Spontaneous Human Combustion in Literature: Some Literary Uses of Popular Mythology" (Vol. XXXVII, No. 3 (March 1983)), pp. 335-47.

Croft, Lee B. "Triplicity and Textual Iconicity: Russian Literature Through a Triangular Prism" in SYNTACTIC ICONICITY AND LINGUISTIC FREEZES: THE HUMAN DIMENSION (Marge E. Landsberg, ed.). Mouton de Gruyter Publishers. Berlin and New York. 1995. pp. 249-265.
