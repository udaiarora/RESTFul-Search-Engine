Provost Communications Group

Mission: To support the efforts of upper level administrative offices by providing professional communication services, including web, print and presentation materials, with a standard of performance that leads the university in excellence.

Vision:  To provide the offices we serve with the highest quality communication products that effectively, efficiently and elegantly communicate their messages through inspired design, content and use of branding.

Members of this communications team primarily serve the research and academic affairs vice presidential areas of the university, but also design, develop and maintain the Office of the President's web site, among others.

Our main focus is to relay significant information to ASU's many audiences through well designed and written communication. Prospective and current students, industry leaders, ASU faculty and staff, theatre goers, sports fans and K-12 teachers are among the varied audiences that interact with ASU daily.

Today's audiences are savvy, they make instant judgments based on their impressions of a web site or brochure and they want to quickly find the desired information.

Reaching and keeping their interest is our goal. Design and content are our tools.

In other words:
We combat the ugly and confusing by striving for the beautiful and inspiring.
