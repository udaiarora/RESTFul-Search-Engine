Instructions for New Account Application form

  1. Org Title

    The title of the new agency/org needs to describe its responsibilities and activities; limited to 30 characters.

  2. Purpose

    The purpose of the requested new agency/org needs to include, as appropriate for each situation, a description of:

    1. the departmental activities requiring establishment of a new agency/org

    2. the state of development these activities have reached

    3. the remaining steps for the activities to become fully operational, and when these are expected to occur

      and

    4. why the existing agency/org(s) cannot meet the department’s accounting needs.

    Attach additional page(s) if necessary.

  3. Use of Any Existing Agency/Org to Satisfy Needs

    If yes, please discuss this further with the fund accountant responsible for your other agency/org(s). If no, the purpose statement should explain why the existing agency/org(s) cannot meet your accounting needs.

  4. Start Date/Ending Date

    The start date is the date the agency/org will begin to incur expenditures or generate revenue.

    The ending date is the date financial activity will cease. If the ending date is indefinite, indicate this on the form.

  5. Source(s) of Revenue

    The sources of revenue for the proposed agency/org, including revenue that may be generated by the agency/org (e.g., sales and service income), must be listed. Indicate all sources of revenue considering the following restrictions:

    1. Grants or Contracts: If the agency/org is for an externally funded project, i.e., a grant or contract, and/or special reporting to a donor is required, do not complete this form. Contact the Office of Research and Creative Activities (ORCA).

    2. Departmental Gifts: All gifts to the university must be processed through the Development Office (see COM 303, “Gift Deposits,” for additional, complete information).

  6. Type(s) of Expenditure(s)

    Indicate the types of expenditures that will be incurred by this new agency/org (see COM 430, “Expenditure Coding,” for additional information).

  7. Estimated Level of Expenditures

    If expenditures are estimated at more than $100,000, an annual budget must be submitted to University Fiscal Planning and Analysis for subsequent years.

  8. Function classification

    Choose the function classification that fits the purpose of the account.

  9. Establish New Sub-Org(s)

    If the department desires to account for revenues or expenditures at a level of detail lower than the agency/org or object/sub-object code (i.e., accounting for maintenance expenses by facility), the department must establish sub-org(s) and/or function codes for the agency/org. The establishment of sub-org(s) and/or function codes should be discussed with your accountant in the Comptroller’s Office.

  10. ABOR Approval

    Arizona Board of Regents policies require specific approvals for establishment, merger, transfer or disestablishment of the University's academic units. If ABOR approval is required, check "Yes" and attach copy of ABOR minutes.

After the form has been completed, forward the application to the appropriate department chair/director, to the dean (if appliicable), and to the provost/vice president/vice provost for approval. Once all approvals have been obtained submit the New Account Application form with an Advantage Form A (available at http://www.asu.edu/fs/forms/forms.html) to Financial Services at mail code 0103.

For questions regarding this form contact your accountant at Financial Services (Comptroller).
