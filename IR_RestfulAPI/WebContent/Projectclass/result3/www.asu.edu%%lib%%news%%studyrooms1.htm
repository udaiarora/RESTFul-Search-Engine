Individual Study Rooms Now Available in Both Hayden and Noble Libraries!

(posted 2/28/06)

More Quiet Study-ers Wanted!

The libraries have received such overwhelming response to the opening of individual study rooms in Noble Library that we have expanded this service to Hayden Library as well. We now have study rooms available for both daily and hourly use. Current ASU Students, Faculty and Staff can check out a key to one of these rooms at the Circulation/Check Out desk located on the Lower Concourse level of Hayden Library.

 

There are 12 individual study rooms available to students and faculty on the Lower Level of the Hayden Library. The rooms are located on the northern wall downstairs, and can be seen through the long aisles of bound journals. The room numbers available include the range from A2 to C3. These rooms will be available each day from 7am to midnight on a first come, first served basis.

 

Study rooms are located in the northern wall of the Lower Level of Hayden Library

Study rooms are just large enough for you and your books!

 

Please contact Circulation (circ@asu.edu; 965-3605) with any comments, questions or suggestions.
