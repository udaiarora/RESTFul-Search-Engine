Coordinates and supervises the staff and activities of a University Registrar's Office section (Graduation, Recording/Readmissions or Records Information). Provides a full range of supervision of staff, including selection, training, performance evaluation, discipline, schedule development and work prioritization. Provides directions and information to diverse populations, including students, advising departments, staff and general public. Coordinates special activities and projects. Develops and recommends process improvements to supervisors. Recommends and manages wage budget. Initiates and/or coordinates the preparation and/or maintenance of reports, forms, procedures, manuals, and other written materials. Directs and participates in cross-training activities including procedures such as customer service, academic transactions, and data entry, to assist with variable peak processing demands.  
Six years of office/clerical experience which includes two years of lead experience; OR, Associate's degree in Office Administration, Business Administration or a related field AND four years of office/clerical experience which includes two years of lead experience.  
OR, any equivalent combination of education and/or experience from which comparable knowledge, skills and abilities have been achieved.  
 
ALL: Experience with generating correspondence and report writing. Experience working with various university departments and advising units. Experience in word processing (e.g. MS Word). Experience with spreadsheets, E-mail, and reporting software (e.g. MS Excel, MS Outlook and BrioQuery). User level experience on a mainframe computer (e.g. ASU's Student Information System and Control D). Evidence of direct customer service experience. Demonstrated knowledge of general office practices. Effective verbal and written communication skills.

Graduation: Experience with university graduation and records policies and procedures. Experience with a university degree audit reporting system, e.g. DARS.

Recording/Readmissions: Experience with university admission and readmission policies and procedures. Experience with a university degree audit reporting system, e.g. DARS.

Records Information: Experience with university registration policies and procedures.
