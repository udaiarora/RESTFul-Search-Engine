Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

February 1, 2005 ? Called to order 10:02 am

Members Present:  Barbara Trapido-Lurie (Chair), Beth Luey, Bill Sharp, Dan Stanton, and John  Wheatley

Absent:  Victoria Trotta

Barbara Trapido-Lurie introduced John Wheatley, who has replaced Elizabeth McNeil.

Old business

Barbara reported on the December meeting of the Academic Senate, at which policy revisions permitting the creation of multiyear rolling contracts for academic professionals were approved and sent on to the Arizona Board of Regents. These revisions have not yet been placed on the ABOR agenda; they must be approved at the East and West campuses before ABOR can consider them.

 

Beth Luey and Bill Sharp reported on the progress of the implementation task force. It seems unlikely that these revisions will be completed before the end of the academic year. The question of continuing CAPS representation next fall, when Luey and Sharp?s terms expire, was discussed.

 

Although Victoria Trotta was absent, she had submitted reports on PAC-20 meetings by e-mail.

New business

The committee discussed topics for the spring forum. Barbara will pursue these and report back.

 

The committee discussed the number of vacancies that will occur this year and ways to encourage nominations.

Meeting adjourned at 11:00 am
