PERSONAL MONEY MANAGEMENT/
CREDIT COUNSELING
(A service for ASU employees)

ASU Work/Life Balance Programs in partnership with American Family Credit Counseling, Inc. a non-profit public service, offers personal money management assistance for ASU employees. If you are feeling over-extended and overwhelmed, "drowning" in debt, or have too few checks and too many bills, this service may be just what you need.

Services include:

  • How to get a credit report/how to correct credit report errors
  • Learn to budget and balance a checkbook
  • Set up affordable re-payment plans with creditors
  • Stop collection activity
  • Learn how to improve credit rating
  • Bankruptcy counseling
