IT : Mac —   How to unmount AFS


When installing/upgrading an application, you may witness its installer program endlessly spinning its wheels. The likely reason for this behavior is that it is transversing the various cells in the AFS hierarchy.

An appropriate remedy to this problem is to unmount AFS and this can easily be done with a utility called unmountAFS.

Download unmountAFS.sit and double-click on unmountAFS. A dialog box appears, then another and you will be prompted for your password. Finally, a message will be displayed that The AFS unmounting is now complete.

To re-mount AFS, simply reboot the Mac.
