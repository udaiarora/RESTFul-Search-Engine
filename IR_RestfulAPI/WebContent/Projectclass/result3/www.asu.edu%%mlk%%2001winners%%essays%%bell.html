2001 Essay/Poetry Contest

Rachel Bell, Grade 1
Teacher: Mrs. Sandra Matsumara
Pueblo del Sol Elementary
Sierra Vista District

Third Place
Primary (Grades K-2)

 

Laure is a good leader because she is honest and she knows the right thing for me. She helps me and if I fall she asks me if I'm ok. If I'm doing something wrong, she helps me fix it. She is smarter than I am and she plays with me. She is the best.
