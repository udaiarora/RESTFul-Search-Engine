Renewing Your Decal for 2006-2007

Current ASU decal holders will be able to renew their parking decal from April 10 – 21 via Online Parking Services* or in person at the PTS Decal Sales office at each campus with a current ASU ID.

* An ASURITE ID is required. Decals will be available for pickup 48 business hours after online purchase.

Be sure you do not have an outstanding balance on your ASU account; if you do, you will not be able to renew your decal.


Important Renewal Information

1. Current ASU decal holders are eligible to renew their decal location for the 2006-07 year or purchase a new decal that is of lower value based on the new 2006-07 parking rates (downgrade option).

Example: John currently has a decal for Parking Structure 5. During the April 10-21 renewal period, John may either renew his PS-5 decal at the new rate of $480 or choose the downgrade option and purchase a decal of less value.

2. Decal holders who select the downgrade purchase option must do so in person at the PTS Decal Sales office during the April 10-21 renewal period.
