Advisory & Consultative Committees

[ CIO > Committees >

Three committees meet quarterly (or more frequently as needed) to advise and consult with the chief information officer: the Instruction and Research Computing Advisory Committee (IRCAC), the Administrative Systems Project Advisory Committee (ASPAC), and the CIO Advisory Committee (CIOAC). The CIO also uses ad hoc groups like the Computing Consultants Cooperative to communicate IT–related issues to the university community.
