Service Interruptions

The computing systems are subject to periodic service interruptions. Situations may arise that necessitate interrupting service at times other than those listed below. Every effort will be made to keep these to a minimum.

Major Interruptions

There may be service interruptions several times a year, usually during the semester breaks when major changes are made. These interruptions will be scheduled at specific times, typically for one or two days (on weekends during a semester, on weekdays between semesters).

Maintenance Interruptions

Preventive maintenance and system work is done during the semester as necessary. The times during which this may occur are as follows:

  • IBM MVS (Academic) system
    Saturday noon - Sunday noon monthly (* see footnote)

Up-to-date Schedule Information

Up-to-date information about the service interruption schedules are available from the following sources:

  • the "Scheduled Service Interruptions" screen which is accessed via the IBM system selection screen for IBM schedules
  • system status message and HELP Desk at (480) 965-6500

(*) Monthly interruptions are usually scheduled for the third weekend of the month, with none during April and November. To accomodate environmental maintenance, occassionally the monthly interruptions may be extended to all day Saturday through 6 PM Sunday.
