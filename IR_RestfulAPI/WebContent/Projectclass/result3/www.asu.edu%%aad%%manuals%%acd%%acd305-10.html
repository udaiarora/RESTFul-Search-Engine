ACD 305–10: Comprehensive Examinations for Advanced Placement

Purpose

To describe faculty responsibilities for advanced placement examinations

Source

President’s Working Group

Applicability

Faculty

Policy

Neither the instructor who prepares and grades a comprehensive examination for undergraduate advanced placement and credit nor the department chair or division director will receive payment for such services. The conduct of comprehensive examinations for advanced placement is thus equated with the services of faculty members in preparing and grading other examinations, such as proficiency examinations.

Department chairs and division directors will exercise care in the assignment of comprehensive examinations so no faculty member is unduly encumbered with an excessive and burdensome number.

Cross-Reference

For further information, see the University Student Initiatives Policies and Procedures Manual—USI 202–05, “Comprehensive Examinations for Undergraduate Course Equivalence.”
