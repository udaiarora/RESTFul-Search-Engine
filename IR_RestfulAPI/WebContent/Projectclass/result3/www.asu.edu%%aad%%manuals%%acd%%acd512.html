ACD 512: Payroll—Pay Dates and Deductions

Purpose

To describe pay dates and deductions

Sources

Office of the Executive Vice President for Administration and Finance
Human Resources

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

Pay Dates

Faculty and academic professionals are paid on a semimonthly pay schedule. Pay dates are on the 15th and the last day of the month, except if the 15th or last day of the month is on a holiday or weekend; then payday is on the last working day before the holiday or weekend. Payroll checks are distributed by departments based on the warrant mail code submitted on the Personnel Action form (PAF) for each employee.

Employees may ask to have their payroll checks deposited directly to any financial institution which is a member of the Arizona Clearing House Association. Employees may authorize direct deposit by completing a direct deposit request in Human Resources. Direct deposit funds will be available at 8:00 a.m. on payday, and a payroll stub will be sent to the employee at his or her department.

Deductions

New employees are required to complete forms for mandatory deductions, such as federal and state withholding, Social Security (FICA), and retirement (TIAA, VALIC, or Arizona State Retirement System). Voluntary deductions may be authorized by the employee for health insurance, supplemental life insurance, tax-sheltered annuities, U.S. savings bonds, auto insurance, short-term disability insurance, and United Way charitable contributions.
