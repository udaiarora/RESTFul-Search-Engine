USI 201–01: Registration for Classes—General Policy

Purpose

To allow eligible students to register for classes

Source

University Registrar’s Office

Applicability

In addition to other restrictors, a student must be one of the following to be eligible to register for classes at ASU:

  1. a continuing student (a student enrolled in ASU classes during the previous semester)

  2. a newly admitted student (a new ASU student who is officially admitted to the university)

    or

  3. a newly readmitted student (a former ASU student who is officially readmitted to the university after an absence of one semester or more).

Students may verify enrollment eligibility on the ASU Interactive Web site.

Policy

Registration

To register for classes, eligible students must follow the appropriate ASU Interactive (Web) or in-person registration procedures (listed below under “Procedures”).

A late fee is assessed during late registration, which begins on the first day of classes.

Processing Priority

Registration requests are processed on a first-come, first-served basis during registration.

Schedule of Classes

Refer to the Registration and Tuition Guide or the Summer Sessions Bulletin for registration dates, times, and fees.

Procedures

Preregistration

To register for classes during preregistration, a student must:

  1. meet with the assigned advisor to obtain advising sign-off and course approval, if required by the university, college, or department

  2. complete a Preregistration Course Request form, including the schedule line numbers and course prefixes and numbers (or submit a preregistration request online at the ASU Interactive Web site)

  3. submit the Preregistration Course Request form to the advising office or any registrar site (the Records Information counter at the Tempe campus, Records & Registration at the West campus, or Registration Services at the Polytechnic or Downtown Phoenix campus)

    4. and

  4. verify enrollment using the ASU Interactive Web site after the preregistration period is over and the results have been posted.

Registration

To register for classes during the registration period, a student must:

  1. meet with the assigned advisor to obtain advising sign-off and course approval, if required by the university, college, or department

  2. submit a registration request online at the ASU Interactive Web site or complete a Registration Request form, including the schedule line numbers and course prefixes and numbers, and submit it and a photo ID to any registrar site (the Records Information counter at the Tempe campus, Records & Registration at the West campus, or Registration Services at the Polytechnic or Downtown Phoenix campus)

    Note:If a form is submitted in person, wait for a schedule/billing statement to be issued before leaving the registrar site.

    3. and

  3. verify enrollment by viewing the student class schedule online at ASU Interactive.

Cross-References

For information on late registration, see USI 201–05, “Registration and Drop/Add after the Published Deadline.”

For information on readmission, see the policies in USI 202, “Readmissions and Course Equivalencies.”
