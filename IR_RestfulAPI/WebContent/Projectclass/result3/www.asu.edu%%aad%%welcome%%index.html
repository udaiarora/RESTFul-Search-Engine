Mission

Academic and Administrative Documents provides editorial leadership and technical expertise to improve, produce, and maintain accurate, consistent administrative manuals and general and graduate catalogs on the World Wide Web for Arizona State University and its local, regional, and global communities.

Academic and Administrative Documents (AAD) has created a book on how to write a manual, Creating Administrative Manuals (CAM). AAD is available for informal consultation with university administrative units on the development and maintenance of internal manuals such as departmental or desk manuals.

AAD can be contacted at 480/965-2320, or mail code 4211. We are located in Tempe Center, Room 195, 951 S. Mill Ave., Tempe.

Print catalog distribution

The 2007–2008 editions of the ASU General Catalog and Graduate Catalog will be the first online-only editions.

AAD provides older editions of the catalogs in print to employees, students, and others according to available supplies. Employees may request copies of the 2006–2007 catalogs using an Employee Request form.

Catalog editions from 1994 through 2006 are available in multiple formats. The online versions of these editions can be accessed from the catalog archives.
