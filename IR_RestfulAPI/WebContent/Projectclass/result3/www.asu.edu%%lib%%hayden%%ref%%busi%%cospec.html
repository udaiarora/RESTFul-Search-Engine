Marketing Resources

COMPANY INFORMATION -- SPECIALIZED DIRECTORIES
  1. American Wholesalers & Distributors Directory. Detroit, MI: Gale Research, 1992- . Biennial. (ASU owns 1992-2003. Latest edition in Reference; earlier editions in Journals.)
    HF 5421 .A43x HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Each entry looks like a typical business directory with the company name, address, telephone, product line, SIC codes, sales, number of employees, and officers. The uniqueness of this work is that the companies included are all wholesalers or distributors in over twenty major product lines. The work is aranged by product lines. SIC, revenue rankings, geographic, and alphabetic indexes are included.

  2. Bradford's international directory of marketing research agencies. Washington, DC : Business Research Services, 1954/55- . Biennial. (ASU owns 1954/55, 1963/64- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    HF 5415 .A2B7 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    The geographically arranged directory of agencies is most useful with its subject listing by type of research included in the back.

  3. Directory of Business to Business Catalogs. Lakeville, CT: Grey House Pub., 1991- . Annual. (ASU owns 1991, 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    HF 5437 .A2D57x HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Directory of Mail Order Catalogs. New York: Grey House Pub, 1981- . Annual. (ASU owns 1984, 1994. Latest edition in Reference; earlier editions in Journals.)
    HF 5465.5 .D58 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Mail Order Business Directory: a Complete Guide to the Mail Order Market. Coral Springs, FL: B. Klein Publications, 1955- . (ASU owns 1966, 1975- . Latest in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    HF 5466 .M28 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    These three directories are basic company directories in the mail order and catalog industry.

  4. Franchise Annual. Lewiston, NY: INFO Franchise News, 1980- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    HF 5429.235 .U5F73x HAYDEN REFERENCE

    This international directory of franchises available for purchase does focus on mostly U.S. and Canadian opportunities. There is a small handbook section in the front that does provide some guidance on selecting a franchise and who to contact about state regulations. Arranged by industry, it does provide basic address information, product line, year established, number of independent and company owned franchises, fees and royalties, inventory size, and the availability of financing.

  5. Bond, Robert E. and Jeffrey M. Bond. Bond's Franchise Guide. Homewood, IL: Business One Irwin, 1985- . Annual. (ASU owns 1985,1988- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    HF 5429.235 .U5 S66 HAYDEN REFERENCE
    HF 5429.235 .U5 S66 WEST REFERENCE

    Formerly called the Source Book of Franchise Opportunities, this work takes the middle road in the amount of information provided. It has more detail than the Franchise Annual, but less than the Worldwide Franchise Directory in some ways. This is arrange by industry then by company. Each industry has a table of statistics on that industry. The company entries have the usual basic directory information along with the history, financial data, franchiser training/support, and expansion plans. These four sections are in a tabular form. The introductory parts of this are very useful in getting an overview of franchising with some industry statistics and history. A bibliography for further reading is also included.
