Minutes of Meeting


May 15, 2002

 

Meeting called to order at 3:05 PM

 

Chair: John Barnard
Secretary: Mimmo Bonanni
A quorum was present.

 

I. Approval of Minutes

 

Minutes of April 17, 2002 meeting were approved as written.

 

II. Chair & Executive Committee Report

 

As parliamentarian, JoAnn Mulvihill explained to council that as result of both the resignation of John Barnard and Ken Furuta, Chair and Chair-Elect, there would be a vacancy for the Chair-Elect position.  When Ken Furuta resigns on June 7, 2002 as Chair-Elect, Julie Tharp will become Chair-Elect.  Then, a week later, John resigns as Chair so Julie will assume the role of Chair creating a vacancy for Chair-Elect.  According to the by-laws, Executive Committee does not have the power to appoint the position of Chair or Chair-Elect, so there must be a election.

 

IV. Committee Reports

 

Personnel Committee - Scott Herrington

Scott announced that for the final project, the Committee is in the process of finalizing the Marion the Librarian 6th Year review letter.

Review Committee - Kathy Krzys

No report.

Research and Professional Development Committee - Dan Stanton

The committee announced that it currently has funds that have been returned or unused. Dan also mentioned that since it is a difficult budget year, the committee would give the monies back to Administration to spend as they see fit.  For our next fiscal year, the Dean has agreed to allot 54,000 dollars for professional development, and 3,000 dollars for research.

Compensation Committee - Lois Schneberger

No report.

Academic Senate

No report.

Committee on Academic Professional Status - Linda Shackle

No Report.

Page Master

No report.

 

V. Old Business

 

None.

 

VI. New Business

 

Nominations and Elections Committee - JoAnn

 

As mentioned at the beginning of the meeting, the Nominations Committee proposed an election for the position of Chair-Elect.  JoAnn Mulvihill, reporting in Pat Etter's absence, presented the slate for Chair-Elect, which was made up of one candidate, Scott Herrington.  JoAnn asked for nominations from the floor.  There were no nominations.  JoAnn stated that a ballot would be issued and sent out to all Academic Professionals.

 

Meeting Adjourned at 3:22 pm

 

Mimmo Bonanni, Secretary
