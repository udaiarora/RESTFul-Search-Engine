ANNOUNCEMENTS/REMINDERS
2004 Graduate College Summer Assistantship Program is no longer accepting applications due to expended funds. Congratulations to the 82 students receiving them.

New Application Fees: Effective July 1, the application fee for all graduate applications, regardless of type, will be $50. Please encourage your nondegree and re-entry students to apply before this date to avoid the increased cost.

Fulbright Scholarships: Students planning to apply for a Fulbright Scholarship next year should contact Dr. Janet Burke to immediately begin the process. The scholarships are for a year's study/research in a foreign country. Applicants must be graduating seniors (next year) or graduate students and U.S. citizens. Please contact Suzie Balamenti, 5-5894 or suzanne.balamenti@asu.edu, to schedule an appointment.

GPSA's new Student Bill of Rights is now available at asu.edu/gpsa/documents/bill_of_rights.pdf
