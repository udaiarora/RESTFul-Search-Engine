Got Virus?

Is your computer running slow? Do you get popup ads? Does it seem like your computer is just not behaving correctly?

You may have a computer virus or even spyware. Computer viruses and spyware can be contracted many ways through many different web sites, even My Space, or Facebook. The most common way to get a computer virus or spy ware is through downloading. If you download a file sharing program like Limewire not only is it illegal it can cause major problems with your computer. One of the best ways to try to clean up your computer is to use one or both of the following programs. Spybot and Adaware are two of the best more trusted search and destroy programs for spy ware and viruses. You can download Spybot for free at http://www.spybot.com/ and Adaware for free at http://www.lavasoft.com/ .

If you have questions about computer viruses or spy ware fell free to contact the Reslife Helpdesk, we would be happy to help.

Have fun surfing and be safe,
