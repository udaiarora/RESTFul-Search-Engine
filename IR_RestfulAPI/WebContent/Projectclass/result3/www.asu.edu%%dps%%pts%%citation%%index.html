Citation Appeals

If you have received a citation and believe that the citation has been issued in error or that there are grounds for an appeal, we encourage you to submit an appeal. We are here to help!

Appeals may be submitted through our on-line services, in person, mail, or fax within 14 calendar days of the citation date. You can also pay a citation through our on-line services.
