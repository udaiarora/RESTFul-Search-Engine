Week 1: January 14 - January 20

January 14

Memorial Union Concert
7:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.
Alumni Lounge, Memorial Union

January 15

Martin Luther King, Jr. Holiday Observed

Honoring Great Civil Rights Leaders: Gone, But Never Forgotten
Art Café, Memorial Union Einstein Bros. Bagels
7:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.
"True leadership must be for the benefit of the followers:"- Robert Townsend
Join fellow Sun Devils as we gather to remember great leaders who have persevered in their fight for civil rights. Remembering the past, discussing our present and planning for a stronger future. This event is open-mic and light refreshments will be served.

January 16

Discovery Series Tour
4:00 p.m. - 5:30 p.m.
Ira A Fulton School of Engineering
Visit the Laboratory of Energetic Flow and Turbulence. Research is focused on experimental detonation, development of advanced instrumentation for experimental fluid dynamics and fundamental studies of turbulent flows. Co-sponsored by City of Tempe Economic Development Office. Reservations required: (480) 965-3493. Further inquiries, including locations and times, can be e-mailed to herb.f@asu.edu

NPHC Yard Show and Informational
5:00 p.m. - 7:00 p.m.
SSV Lawn and Amphitheater
ASU's Black Greek-Lettered Organizations host a step show and informational where students can meet members of the National Pan-Hellenic Council and learn how to get involved.

January 17

Professor of the Year Nominations
Nominations can be submitted online at www.asu.edu/pty
ASU students, faculty and staff are encouraged to nominate outstanding tenured professors who teach at the undergraduate level for the ASU Parents Association 2007 Professor of the Year Award. Undergraduate teaching excellence is recognized at a celebrations event held on April 16. The 2007 Professor of the Year will receive a cash award of $10,000 plus an additional $10,000 to fund an undergraduate student assistant. Nominations are accepted from January 17 through February 23.

Palo Verde Beach
7:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.

January 20

MLK Day of Service
9:00 a.m.-1:00 p.m.
UMOM New Day Centers
Come serve in the spirit of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. As a part of the larger University MLK celebration, students will be going to UMOM New Day Centers to serve residents there and do some general clean-up. UMOM is a New Day Centers is a homeless shelter for families. Please visit www.asu.edu/mlk for information on additional ASU events celebrating Dr. King.
