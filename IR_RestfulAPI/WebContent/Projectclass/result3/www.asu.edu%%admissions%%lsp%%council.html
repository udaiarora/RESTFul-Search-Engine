LSP Alumni Council

Join us and make a difference for current and future Leadership Scholars.

The LSP Alumni Council, formed in April 1999, is composed of alumni from the inaugural class through the 1992 cohort. Our work focuses mostly on:

  • raising funds for the program
  • providing a network to keep alumni informed about ASU and LSP events, and
  • mentoring current Leadership Scholars

Our current projects include the Homecoming Reunion, supporting the Leadership Scholarship Endowment Fund, and creating new ways -- such as this web site -- to communicate with other LSP alumni.

The LSP Alumni Council is currently in the process of becoming an official chapter of the ASU Alumni Association.
