University Design Team Reports and Recommendations

The University Design Team was appointed in the Spring of 2003. Members were asked to develop a five-to-ten year road map for ASU which would, enhance the campus identities, accommodate the anticipated growth in student demand by building attractive programs on the east, west and downtown campuses and maximizing opportunities to enhance the quality and performance of all University programs.
