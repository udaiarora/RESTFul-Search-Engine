All incoming freshmen are expected to attend Orientation.

About the Program

The focus of the full-day, ASU Orientation - Downtown Phoenix program is to enable you to receive academic advisement for your course of study and register for classes. At this ASU Orientation program you will also discuss academic expectations with academic advisors, faculty, and staff, and meet current ASU students.

Your Major: In order to provide you with the appropriate academic advising, it is important that we have the correct information about the major you have chosen. If the major displayed in your reservation confirmation is incorrect, or has changed, please contact New Student Orientation or submit an Orientation Change Request to update your major's information at least five business days before your scheduled ASU Orientation program. Note: Changes to your major cannot be made during your Orientation session. Requests for changes to the major are updated every 24 hours. If you have not already made your Orientation reservation, please contact New Student Orientation to change your major before making a reservation.

Check-In: Arrive early for your program to check in. Review and print the Parking and Directions information before attending your program.

Agenda: Generally, your ASU Orientation agenda consists of:

  • Explore campus involvement opportunities.
  • Discover resources to help you succeed and learn about student success programs.
  • Attend a group academic advisement session.
  • Register for fall classes.
  • You, your family, and guests will also be able to meet with representatives from various campus departments.

What Your Program Includes: As a part of your reservation for the ASU Orientation - Downtown Phoenix, on-campus program:

  • You, the student, will receive group academic advising, an Orientation Student Handbook, an ASU General Catalog, Schedule of Classes, lunch, parking in designated lots and a campus tour.
  • Your guest reservation includes a special family session, an Orientation Family Guide, lunch, parking in designated lots and a campus tour.

AP, IB and CLEP Test Scores: In order to receive college credit for Advanced Placement (AP) courses, the International Baccalaureate (IB) program, and/or College-Level Examination Program (CLEP), request that your official test scores be sent to ASU. Results from these exams will also be used by your academic advisor to place you in appropriate courses.

Program Dates and Fees

Dates: Orientation program availability varies by ASU campus and major. When you log into the Orientation Reservation System, you will see the final list of available dates for your major.

Fees: ASU Orientation - Downtown Phoenix program fees are:

  • $60.00 per student
  • $40.00 per guest

Reservation Information and Reminders

Please be aware of the following important information for students and guests attending ASU Orientation - Downtown Phoenix programs.

  • Please do not secure travel arrangements and accommodations until you have received the online confirmation of your reservation.
  • Reservations will be accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • For the best opportunity to receive a preferred program date, be sure to make your reservation online three or more weeks before your earliest preferred date.
  • Reservations will be accepted beginning early February, 2006.
  • In order for you to provide you with the appropriate academic advisement, it is important that we have the correct information about the major you have chosen. If your major has changed, please immediately contact New Student Orientation. Notes: Changes to your major cannot be made during your Orientation session. Requests for changes to the major are updated every 24 hours.
  • Upon successfully reserving online, you will receive an online confirmation. Be sure to print out your Orientation program confirmation, Program Checklist as well as Parking and Directions information upon completing your reservation. You will not be mailed confirmation information.

Program Payment, Cancellations or Changes, and Special Needs

Program payment: Students requesting the check payment option have 10 business days to remit their payment to New Student Orientation. If payment is not received in 10 business days, your ASU Orientation reservation will be forfeited. Your balance will still be due and your ASU Orientation reservation will need to be rescheduled.

Cancellations or Changes:

  • If you would like to change your program date or cancel your ASU Orientation session, you must notify New Student Orienation in writing (by mail, fax or online) so that the cancellation or Orientation Change Request is received at least five business days before your scheduled Orientation date.
  • Each change to your program date or cancellation will be assessed a $15 processing fee.
  • A $15 processing fee will be charged for all returned checks and denied credit cards.
  • If you wish to add a guest or substitute a guest, you must submit your Orientation Change Request in writing or online.
  • Any Orientation refund requests will be processed in July 2006 upon conclusion of the ASU Orientation season with required documentation. Please note that in order to receive a refund for a cancellation you must submit your request in writing (by mail, fax or online) 5 business days before the date of the program you wish to cancel.
  • In order to provide you with the appropriate academic advising, it is important that we have the correct information about the major you have chosen. If the major displayed in your reservation confirmation is incorrect, or has changed, please contact New Student Orientation or submit an Orientation Change Request to update your major's information at least five business days before your scheduled ASU Orientation program. Note: Changes to your major cannot be made during your Orientation session. Requests for changes to the major are updated every 24 hours. If you have not already made your Orientation reservation, please contact New Student Orientation to change your major before making a reservation.

Special Needs: If you have special needs call 480.965.2880 or e-mail at least 10 business days before your scheduled program. Special needs include: disabled parking, assistance with tours, language interpretation and/or a special meal.
