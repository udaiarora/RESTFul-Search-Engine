Subscribe to the Tours Podcast

  1. Download iTunes 6, which now supports podcasting
  2. Once you have installed iTunes 6, subscribe to the Library Channel Tours within the Podcast Directory of the iTunes Music Store.

    or

    Click on this:

    or

    Open iTunes and select Advanced

    Figure 1: Select Advanced


  3. In the drop down menu select "subscribe to podcast" and enter the following url: http://feeds.feedburner.com/TheLibraryChannelTours

    Figure 2: Select "Subsribe to Podcast"


  4. It will automatically subscribe you and load the content as a subtrack under "The Library Channel Tours " in your podcasts playlist.

While listening to the podcast you can file through and see the "cover image" (with an iPod photo or better), see the chapters and also skip ahead or back, as well as read the text information that was downloaded with the audio.


For more information see: How to download and listen to MP4 audio in iTunes
