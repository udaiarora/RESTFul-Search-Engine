Administrative Services

MISSION STATEMENT

To provide excellence in administrative services to the University and community in support of Arizona State University, the New American University.

GENERAL ADMINISTRATION

Plans, organizes and directs the Department of Administrative Services and reports to the Deputy Executive, Vice President, University Services. In addition, is responsible for the division's organizational development initiatives and compensation classification program and position management.

EMPLOYEE RELATIONS

The University Services, Administrative Services, Employee Relations Unit administers the division's progressive disciplinary program, provides guidance in resolution of workplace conflicts, educates and guides employees, administrators, managers and supervisors on a variety of employee relations issues, consults in matters related to staff personnel policies, procedures and programs, and develops and implements new programs and policies.

MANAGEMENT SUPPORT SERVICES

Management Support Services assists University Services management with all facets of the employment process from the initial job posting through the recruitment process until a hire is made. These services encompass all staffing needs, including temporary and student employment. All employee leaves of absence are coordinated by this group, which provides consultation to both supervisors and employees regarding available types of leave.

In addition to leave coordination, employee time and attendance records are processed to ensure correct payment for all leaves as well as accurate compensation for time worked.

The division performance management program is coordinated by this group to assist managers and employees with timely evaluation of performance.

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

The Research and Development Group (R&D) is responsible for researching, analyzing and directing complex strategic plans.  The R&D Group coordinates and manages process improvement initiatives for University Services Departments. 

The R&D Group manages the division-wide goal/accomplishment program, ABOR audits, University Services Web Pages and develops and maintains comprehensive databases and web applications to accommodate University Services business and operational needs
