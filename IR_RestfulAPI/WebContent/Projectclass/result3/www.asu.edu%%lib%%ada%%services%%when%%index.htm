ADA Services

Access for Disability Accommodations


When?

 

ADA Services facilities are open during all Library Hours.

If individual assistance is needed, we encourage
making an appointment to ensure our availability.

ADA Services staff is available during the following scheduled hours:


Fall and Spring Semester Hours
Day Time
Monday to Thursday 8:00 am to 8:00 pm
Friday 8:00 am to 5:00 pm
Saturday 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm
Sunday 1:00 pm to 8:00 pm


Summer Hours
Day Time
Monday to Friday 10:00am to 3:00 pm
Saturday 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm

Hours may vary. Contact Us for updated information, or to make an appointment.
