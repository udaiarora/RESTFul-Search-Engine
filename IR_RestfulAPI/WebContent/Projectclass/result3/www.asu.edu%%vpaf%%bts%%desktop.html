Desktop & server Support Group

The Server & Desktop Support Group Supports a veriety of equipment:

- Approximately 900 desktop PCs and associated software;
- Laptops;
- Blackberry and various PDAs;
- Network printers; and
- an extensive file-sharing network used by all University Administration and Business and Finance staff.


The Group also partners with the University Technology Office (UTO) to support 16 servers, two network-attached storage filers and two redundant firewall appliances. In addition, the staff provides file and account access permissions setup and maintenance, and keeps track of Ethernet jacks and hardware inventories.

The Group is responsible for reviewing the PC/laptop and software needs of the University Administration and Buisness and Finance Departments so that it can recommend appropriate purchases and obtain best price quotes. When necessary, the staff installs the software and sets ups all new hardware, in addition to relocating equipment when units or staff move to new locations.
