Julia Rosen to serve as director of economic affairs

ASU's Office of Economic Affairs has appointed Julia Rosen to the newly-created position of director of economic affairs. Rosen reports to Rob Melnick, associate vice president for economic affairs and director of ASU's Morrison Institute for Public Policy.

Rosen has broad experience in technology transfer, venture capital and economic development. She previously worked in the ASU technology transfer office, where she established a series of innovative programs and services for ASU-based entrepreneurs. As an economic development official at the city of Tempe, she created the Tech Oasis to attract and retain technology companies for the city.

Rosen also headed business development efforts for a local venture capital firm. She holds a master of business administration degree from ASU and a master of international management degree from Thunderbird, the American Graduate School of International Management.

"I look forward to working with talented faculty, staff and business leaders to increase ASU's contributions to the economic well-being of the state," Rose said.
Rosen will represent ASU at numerous business and economic development organizations. She will also serve as a liaison to companies that desire access to ASU's intellectual and programmatic resources.

Leland, with the Morrison Institute for Public Policy, can be reached at (480) 965-4525 or (karen.Leland@asu.edu).
