NewCu Discussion Forums

Dear Colleagues,

The Task Force on the Curriculum for the New American University has moved on from general principles to a series of learning outcomes and will be discussing early drafts of those outcomes and listening for new input from the university community at several open forums.

Conceptualization of these outcomes (listed below) is still in process; brief descriptions will be available on the Newcu website on Friday (www.asu.edu/provost/newcu/outcomes.html).

  1. Knowledge categories (general knowledge in specific content areas)
  2. Communication
  3. Critical thinking and quantitative and qualitative analysis
  4. Self-awareness
  5. Technology
  6. Transnational and global perspective
  7. Interdisciplinary perspective
  8. Being positive community member
  9. Ethical perspective
  10. Creativity
