Liger, Louis

1658-1717.

Le Nouveau Theatre d'Agriculture et Menage des Champs.
. .

Paris: Chez Damien Beugnie, 1723. "Contenant la maniere de cultiver
& faire valoir toutes sortes de Biens a la Campagne. . ." Includes
index. References: NUC pre-1956; BM. Ms. annotations throughout.

This early work on French agriculture also includes recipes and table
arrangements.  ?The work is very detailed and even includes instructions
and calculations necessary for the determination of the number of perches
needed for a certain number of birds.  The author also details the
costs involved for such items as cement,carpentry, iron, and paint. 
All types of animals are discussed from pigeons, deer, and horned animals. 
After three sections on agriculture, the work deals with five different
types of gardens, namely kitchen gardens, fruit gardens, flower gardens,
ornamental gardens, and herb gardens.  The work has numerous plates
with illustrations of a variety of topics, such as tools, planting techniques,
and table settings.  The fifth and final section includes a wide variety
of recipes.  The work concludes with an index to which hand notes
have been added to include additional headings.

Subjects:
Agriculture--France; Gardening; Cookery, French.
