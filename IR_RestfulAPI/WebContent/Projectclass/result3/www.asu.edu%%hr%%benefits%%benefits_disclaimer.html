Benefits Program Highlights

Arizona State University receives its core benefits from the Arizona Department of Administration. They are the contract holders for the medical and dental insurance, the Standard life and disability insurance, and the Long Term Disability insurance. Information on this web site provides brief summaries of the State’s official plan documents and contracts that govern the plans along with data for plans that are unique to ASU. If there is any discrepancy between this information and the official documents, the official documents will always govern.

The State of Arizona and Arizona State University reserve the right to change or terminate any of its plans, in whole or in part, at any time. Participation in any of the State’s employee or retiree/LTD participant benefit plans should not be considered a contract.
