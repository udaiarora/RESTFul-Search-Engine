April 8, 2004

One University in Many Places
Presentation (PowerPoint) | White paper: HTML | PDF

January 2004

Led by President Michael Crow a team of ASU faculty and ASU Foundation board members traveled to mainland China and Taiwan to follow up on a series of “scouting” visits in previous months. The broad goal of the enterprise was to connect with various potential academic, financial, and research partners and to discuss sustainability science, biosciences, science policy, business education, economic and trade related research, and technology transfer in the context of ASU’s vision for a “New American University.”
