Sarah's Story

For some students, the path to their career can be extremely focused. "I have known since I was in 7th grade that I want to be a doctor," says Sarah. "ASU's Biology and Society program and the Barrett Honors College appealed to my interests."

Sarah's parents have frequently discussed college choice and finances with Sarah and her three younger siblings. Sarah, a senior at ASU, limited her college search to Arizona in consultation with her parents because of the affordability of the degree. (Her younger twin sisters followed Sarahâ??s lead and are now enrolled at ASU as well.)

Because of Sarah's high school performance, she was awarded a President's Scholarship. She also applied for many private scholarships, and she received one from a community organization.

In addition to her scholarships, Sarah accepted a Unsubsidized Federal Direct loan in her financial aid award. Sarah also works part-time to supplement her aid package.
