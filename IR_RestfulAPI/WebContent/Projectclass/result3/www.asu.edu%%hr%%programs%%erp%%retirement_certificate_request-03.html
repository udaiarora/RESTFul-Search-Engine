Retirement Certificate Request Form

Departments may obtain framed retirement certificates for employees who are retiring with five or more years of service to ASU. Retirement certificates may be requested at any time during the year; however, we ask that you allow 2 weeks preparation time.

To request a retirement certificate:

Complete the form below and send to Mail Code 5612 or FAX this form to 480-727-6209.

For more information regarding ASU Retirement Certificates, please call Joan Faherty at (480) 965-0033.

Sun Devil Service Award Luncheon

Human Resources relies on individual departments to help identify retirees who are eligible to attend this special luncheon. Faculty and staff with 25, 30, 35, 40, 45 and 50 years of service and those who retire with 25 or more years of service to ASU will be invited to a luncheon hosted by the President's Office in March. For more information about the Sun Devil Award for Service program, please visit the web at http://www.asu.edu/hr/programs/erp/employee_rec_programs.html or contact Joan Faherty at (480) 965-0033.
