PCS 1002–03: Redistribution of Equipment to University Departments

Purpose

To provide university departments with the opportunity to utilize excess used property

Sources

Arizona Constitution Article IX, § 7
Property Control
Surplus Property and Recycling
University policy

Policy

After property is transferred to Surplus Property and Recycling, it is available to university departments, at no charge in most cases, for 15 calendar days. After 15 calendar days, it is displayed for sale to the general public. At the discretion of the Surplus Property and Recycling manager, some items may not be held for 15 days. Once property is received by Surplus Property and inventoried, it becomes the property of Surplus Property.

Procedure D

Responsibility
Action
Department desiring property
  1. Acquire hold tag from warehouse staff.

    Note:An ASU photo ID is required for hold tags.

  2. Tag property for distribution back to campus.

  3. Complete required information on hold tag.

  4. Complete a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form, obtain the immediate supervisor’s signature, and send the PRA to Surplus Property and Recycling within 15 calendar days of applying holding tag.

    Note:If PRA form is not received within 15 calendar days, property will be returned to warehouse floor for public sale.

If the Department of Facilities Management movers are not needed:
  1. Forward the completed original to Surplus Property and Recycling.
Surplus Property and Recycling
  1. Permit department employee to remove equipment from Surplus Property and Recycling. Property must be transported in a state vehicle.
  1. Update records to indicate the new custodial unit.
Property Control
  1. Randomly verify that equipment was delivered to campus.
If the Department of Facilities Management movers are needed:
Surplus Property and Recycling
  1. Upon receiving the completed PRA form, move property listed on form to holding area.

  2. Forward the original PRA form to the Department of Facilities Management.
Department of Facilities Management
  1. Pick up the equipment from Surplus Property and Recycling.

  2. Verify that the ASU property control numbers are on the PRA or write them on the original.

    Note:Obtain the Surplus Property and Recycling signature before moving the equipment.

  3. Coordinate movement of the equipment with the department acquiring the equipment.
Surplus Property and Recycling
  1. Update records to reflect the new custodial unit, location, and other pertinent data.

endtable
