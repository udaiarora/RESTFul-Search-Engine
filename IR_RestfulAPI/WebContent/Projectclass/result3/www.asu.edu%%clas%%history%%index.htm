Alumni Information Request

We're introducing a new Public History alumni database and invite your participation. We want to provide a way for alumni to maintain contact with the program and with their friends from the program. We also want to make it possible for you to see who else has gone through the program and the expertise that is there. We want to encourage new networks! We think you will appreciate being able to link to one another easily. We also want to be able to tell prospective students and employers about your collective accomplishments. In addition, the university often asks us for up-to-date information about alumni and what they are doing. This will be an easy way to accomplish all of these goals. For more information, please visit our Alumni Page.
