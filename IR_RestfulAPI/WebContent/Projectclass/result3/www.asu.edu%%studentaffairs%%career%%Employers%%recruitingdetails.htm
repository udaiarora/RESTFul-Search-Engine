Career Event Descriptions

Click on the event name to see more details; location is Tempe campus except as noted. View the Calendar of Events for specific dates.

Event attendance summaries from recent years

Arizona Collegiate Job Fair - a Focus on Diversity is co-sponsored by Career Services at ASU with other Arizona campuses in the late spring. This large job fair gives employers an opportunity to showcase their organization and interview students and alumni for immediate career positions and summer internships; a heavy emphasis is placed on promoting this event to a diverse candidate pool statewide.

Career Fiesta, ASU's "signature" career event, is held early fall and allows employers the opportunity to present career information over a two-day period of time, to thousands of ASU students and alumni in a relaxed, non-interviewing setting. Of additional benefit to participating employers is the campus exposure built by involvement in this campus-wide event in anticipation of employer career and internship recruiting.

Community Career Mixer is a collaborative event in the spring sponsored and promoted by a number of campus departments to bring not-for-profit, government and healthcare employers together with students interested in internship and career employment with these industries.

Nursing Career Day is held early Spring semester on a Saturday, and includes Nursing and students from other healthcare-related majors from all ASU campuses, as well as the Maricopa County Community Colleges.

Polytechnic campus Fall and Spring Career Fairs provides the opportunity for employers to network with many of the Polytechnic campus 6,000+ students and recent graduates as they explore their career options and begin the journey toward professional employment. This is an excellent forum for employers to promote their full-time career positions in addition to any internship opportunities they have available.

Recruiter Roundup provides education employers the opportunity to interview candidates mid-summer for late openings for the fall. Teacher and administrative candidates are present from all sections of the country and represent all areas of education, K-12.

Small/Local Business Career Mixer is an informal event that will bring students together with employers from the Metro Phoenix area to network and discuss local internship and career options.

Summer/Camp Day on the Mall is a new opportunity for camps and parks to informally distribute materials on Cady Mall in the center of the Tempe campus, to students interested in summer and recreation-type positions.  Interview rooms will be available to participating employers who wish to conduct same-day and next-day interviews.

Super Recruiting Days are now being held during recruiting season, to enable employers to meet with and interview students in a larger environment than regular on-campus interviewing.  Super Recruiting Days are geared toward employers interviewing on-campus for the first time or otherwise wanting to establish a presence on campus.  Some have a specific focus:

Fall Career and Internship Super Recruiting Day October
Career, Internship and Seasonal Super Recruiting Day November
Spring Career and Internship Super Recruiting Day February
Career, Internship and Summer Super Recruiting Day April

Summer Career Mixers, new in 2005, are held in May, June, and July and are intended for those employers with newly-created or unfilled positions after Spring Semester classes and formal recruiting have ended.  These Mixers are intended to be smaller, more informal networking opportunities for employers and students/recent grads.

West campus Career Fest, which is held both in late fall and spring, encourages networking between employers and the more than 7000 students on the West Campus.

What's Good About Education is a career information event designed for Arizona school systems to meet informally with current student teachers, to distribute application materials, and to promote their districts.

If you are interested in participating in or requesting additional information for any of these events, please email Career Services, call 480/ 965-2355, or access Sun Devil CareerLink through the Employer login above.
