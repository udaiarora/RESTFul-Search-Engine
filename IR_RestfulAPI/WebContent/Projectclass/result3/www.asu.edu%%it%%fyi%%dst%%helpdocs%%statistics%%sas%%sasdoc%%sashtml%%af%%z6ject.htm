Scatter Control: colorRangeObject

Scatter Control: colorRangeObject


the range object to use as a source for colors for the colorVariable

Type: Character
Valid Values:
Editor: sashelp.classes.rangeentryeditor.scl

Details

Details

You may specify as many ranges as the limitations of the range object allow, but no more than 64.

The Scatter control does not support the color attributes (Blinking, Highlight, Reverse, Underline, HiRev, and None) that are available on a colorRangeObject.
