DPS 201–01: Operation Identification

Purpose

To provide guidelines for protecting personal property

Sources

University policy
Property Control

Policy

Identifying personal property reduces the chance of theft and significantly assists in the identification and retrieval of missing property. The Police Services Division (PSD) provides a service known as Operation Identification to any individual or group desiring to have personal property marked. The ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) encourages property owners to mark or engrave all items with individual driver’s license numbers or state ID card numbers. An ASU Police number also may be engraved to assist in the identification of items in case of theft. Groups can receive engravers on loan from ASU DPS to conduct a large-scale engraving activity with assistance from PSD.

ASU DPS reminds all university personnel that lost or stolen personal property is not covered for insurance purposes by State Risk Management. University faculty, staff, and students are responsible for obtaining their own insurance coverage.
