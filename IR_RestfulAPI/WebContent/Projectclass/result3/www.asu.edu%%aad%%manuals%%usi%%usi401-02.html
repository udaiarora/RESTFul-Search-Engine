USI 401–02: Transfer Credit Appeals

Purpose

To provide channels of appeal for transfer applicants who feel they have been unjustly denied credit for courses taken at another college or have been unjustly denied admission to the university

Source

Undergraduate Admissions

Policy

Transfer students who feel they have been unjustly denied credit for courses taken may appeal to the standards committee of the college in which they have enrolled. (This does not apply to community college transfer credit over the 64-hour maximum. See USI 401–01, “Transfer Credit Evaluation.”) The decision of the committee is final.

Cross-Reference

For more information on evaluation of transfer credit, see USI 401–01, “Transfer Credit Evaluation.”
