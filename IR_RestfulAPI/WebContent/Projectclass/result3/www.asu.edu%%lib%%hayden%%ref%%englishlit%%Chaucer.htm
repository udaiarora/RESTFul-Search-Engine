Online Library Resources:

MLA Bibliography (1963+) Indexes articles in the subjects of literature, language, linguistics, and folklore from journals, books, proceedings, dissertations, etc.


Humanities Abstracts (2/1984+) Indexes articles in the fields of archaeology and classical studies, film, folklore, gender studies, history, journalism, communications, language, literature, literary and political criticism, performing arts, philosophy, religion, etc.


Essay and General Literature (1985+) Cites essays & articles contained in collections of essays and miscellaneous works. Focuses on humanities & social sciences, including history, economics, psychology, religion, political science, folklore, film, music, drama, literature, etc.


Literature Online (600+) Includes fully-searchable texts, reference works, bibliographies, catalogues, & links to relevant websites. Includes American Poetry; English Bible; Shakespeare; English Drama and Poetry; Biographical Encyclopedia; Encyclopedia; Dictionary, etc.


PCI Full Text (1770-1990/91) PCI Web is an electronic index to the contents of thousands of periodicals in the humanities and social sciences from their first issues to 1990/1991. Every article is indexed.


Web Resources:
The Geoffrey Chaucer Website Homepage

Larry Benson's Chaucer site, mounted for Harvard University's Chaucer courses,  includes background information on Chaucer, Derek Pearsall's Bibliography (1970-2000), Chaucer's language (with audio clips for pronunciation), translations of the Canterbury Tales, and general information on the Middle Ages.


Chaucer MetaPage

This is a great starting point for navigating Chaucer-related informational sites on the Web. Includes links to electronic versions of the Canterbury Tales.


A Basic Chaucer Glossary

A list of terms from Chaucer's texts with modern equivalents.
