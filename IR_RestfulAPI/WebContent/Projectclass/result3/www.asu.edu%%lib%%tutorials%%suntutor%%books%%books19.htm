How to find books

Understanding your results

What if the book you want is checked out?

If the book you want shows a due date under the "Status" area it is checked out. To request that it be returned for you to use, click on the "Request" button on the catalog information screen.

If prompted, enter your ASURITE ID and password .

You then need to select the library where you want to pick up the book when it becomes available. Then click on "Submit".
