Division of Graduate Studies
Financial Support Office

The Division of Graduate Studies (DGS) provides financial support services including processing and allocation of DGS awards, processing of TA/RA benefits, and assistance in identifying and obtaining other forms of support for graduate students. Graduate students seeking loans or financial aid counseling should contact the main ASU Student Financial Aid Office in the Student Services building at (480) 965-3355 or by email to financialaid@asu.edu.

The DGS Financial Support Office processes:
  • TA/RA benefits (tuition remission and health insurance)
  • Graduate Grant-In-Aid (need-based) Fellowships
  • Travel awards
  • Recruitment Airfare Awards
  • Other DGS fellowships and awards
The DGS Financial Support Office assists students applying for graduate (including dissertation) and postdoctoral fellowships by providing information regarding:
  • DGS fellowships and awards
  • Federal and other national fellowships
  • Dissertation/thesis fellowships
  • Postdoctoral fellowships
  • Links to grant search engines
  • Copies of successful fellowship applications for review
  • Assistance in strengthening your application
The Division of Graduate Studies also provides information and targeted financial support for international students and to support diversity, as well as awards for conferences. Furthermore, the DGS has the Outstanding Mentor Award where graduate students may nominate an individual faculty member who places high value on and excels in mentoring doctoral and MFA students.
  • Research Conference Award
  • Outstanding Mentor Award


Information regarding the cost of attending ASU may be found at www.asu.edu/fa/coa.


Please send an email to dgs-financial@asu.edu with any questions you may have.
