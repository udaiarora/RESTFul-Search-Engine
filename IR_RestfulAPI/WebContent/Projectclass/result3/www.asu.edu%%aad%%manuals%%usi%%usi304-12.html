USI 304–12: Student Employee Grievances

Purpose

To allow recourse for university student employees who have employment-related complaints or grievances

Source

Student Financial Assistance Office/Student Employment

Policy

University student employees who have employment-related complaints or grievances may bring such matters to the attention of their supervisors. Supervisors should make reasonable efforts to respond to or resolve the grievances.

If students are not satisfied with the resolution from their immediate supervisors, they may discuss their problems with individual department hiring officials. Hiring officials will render written decisions within seven days after receipt of the specific grievances. Their decisions are final.

Exception

Employment grievances based on discrimination or sexual harassment must be submitted to the Office of Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action (EO/AA).
