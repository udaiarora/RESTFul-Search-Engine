Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

October 2, 2002

Present: Patricia Feldman, Chair; Carol Behl, Sue Hunter, Stefan Myhajlenko, Barbara Trapido-Lurie, Victoria Trotta.

1.  The Chair formally welcomed new Committee Member Barbara Trapido-Lurie, from the Geography Department. Ms. Trapido-Lurie has agreed to serve during the 2002-2003 year to replace Beth Luey.

2.  The Minutes from the September 4, 2002 meeting were formally approved. They will appear on the CAPS website shortly.

3.  OLD BUSINESS 

  1. Now that Barbara Trapido-Lurie has confirmed her willingness to serve this year, Carol Behl will circulate a draft welcome message 
    for the AP listserv.  

  2. CAPS will attempt to approve meeting minutes electronically so 
    that they may be posted more timely to the CAPS website.

  3. Status of Academic Associates voting rights in Academic Senate
     ? The Chair was scheduled to meet with Vice Provost Gail Hackett on this issue but the meeting was moved to next week. Chair will report back to CAPS.

  4. Spotlight Series ? The Chair has obtained the tentative acceptance of Gaye Murphy, Assistant Director of University Budget  Planning and Management to address the AP gathering on the issue of University self-insurance, which the Arizona Legislature has recently mandated. Ms. Murphy serves as Chair of the University Committee on Insurance and Retirement. CAPS has set the date for the Fall Spotlight Series event for Wednesday, November 13, 2002,  11:00 am ? noon.  The venue  is the Memorial Union Alumni Lounge. CAPS is confident that this is an issue of widespread interest and that the attendance might be larger than usual. Marketing will be: via listserv and flyer preparation (Carol Behl) and via the CAPS website (Sue Hunter). The AP listserv will solicit questions from AP s who cannot attend the session; CAPS members will record minutes of the gathering; the Secretary will put them all together, and they will be posted to the CAPS listserv subsequent to the event.

4. NEW BUSINESS

  1. Some of the past   CAPS minutes have not been posted  to the CAPS website. The Chair will forward missing past CAPS minutes in her possession to Tory Trotta who will have them scanned and forwarded to Sue Hunter for inclusion in the CAPS website. This will augment the website collection of past minutes already on the website.  

  2. The Chair is unable to attend the first meeting of the Academic Assembly Committee on Committees to be held on October 3, 2002 at 3:30-4:30 in Dixie Gammage Hall. Tory Trotta will attend the meeting in her stead.  

  3. The next CAPS meeting is scheduled for November 6, 2002, 10:00 a.m. MU Gila 214.

The meeting was adjourned at 10:45 a.m.

Respectfully submitted, Victoria Trotta, Secretary

Revised 10/16/2002; Approved 11/13/02
