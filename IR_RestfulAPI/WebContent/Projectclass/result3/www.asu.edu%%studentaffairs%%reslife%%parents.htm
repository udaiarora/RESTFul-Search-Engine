The federal Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (also known as FERPA, and/or the Buckley Amendment) affords students certain rights with respect to their education records. It is important that you read and understand access and treatment of your student’s education records. Please visit the ASU Registrar's site for more information.

New program set to offer students late night and weekend activities right in their residential community…

Devil's after Dark offers students free programs and events around campus. Concerts, movie nights, open mic nights, stand-up comedy, hypnotists, and sports tournaments are available to all students. Who needs to go off campus when everything you want to do is right here? When you see the Devils After Dark logo, you know it’s worth going to!
