If equipment will be on campus for fewer than 30 days, the department:

  1. obtains an Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form from Purchasing and Business Services and prepares it before accepting the equipment

    and

  2. sends a photocopy of the form to Purchasing and Business Services.

Purchasing and Business Services sends a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.

If equipment will be on campus for 30 days or more, the department:

  1. prepares an Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form before accepting the equipment

    and

  2. sends a photocopy of the form to Purchasing and Business Services and Property Control.

Purchasing and Business Services sends a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.
