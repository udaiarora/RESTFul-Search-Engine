Service Description

Service Phone: 965-9364
Parts Phone: 965-9128
E-mail: computer.service.center@asu.edu
Web: http://tc2.inre.asu.edu/techshop
The Computer Service Center (CSC) is the campus unit that provides installation, maintenance, and application support services to the university community for computer terminals, micro-computers, workstations, and data communication equipment. CSC employs a staff of professionally trained CTIA A+ Certified engineers and technicians who are equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools.
