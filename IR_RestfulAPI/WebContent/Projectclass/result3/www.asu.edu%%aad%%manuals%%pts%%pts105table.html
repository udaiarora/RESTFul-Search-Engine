Lost or Stolen Decal or Gate Access Cards

The decal holder:

  1. reports a lost or stolen decal or gate access card in person to the Decal Sales Office, Parking and Transit Services

    Note: The office is located on Fifth Street between Forest and College Avenues. Decal holders may also call 480/965–6124 to request the necessary forms to be sent by mail.

  2. completes a Parking Decal Application for a replacement decal

  3. reads, completes, and signs the Lost/Stolen Parking Decal Affidavit

    and

  4. presents an ASU ID card (or driver's license) and pays the replacement fee(s) for the decal or gate access card.

The Decal Sales Office:

  1. verifies the identity of the person requesting the new decal or gate access card and the requestor’s current ownership of the decal or gate access card in question

  2. accepts replacement fee(s) for the lost or stolen decal or gate access card

  3. enters the transaction into the parking management database

  4. deactivates the lost or stolen decal or gate access card

    and

  5. places lost or stolen decal numbers on the appropriate list for enforcement officers.

Damaged Decal or Gate Access Card

The decal holder:

  1. turns in to the Decal Sales Office identifiable remnants of the decal (i.e., at least two of the decal numbers) or the gate access card

  2. completes a Parking Decal Application for a new decal

    and

  3. presents an ASU ID card (or driver's license) and pays the replacement fee(s) for the decal or gate access card.

The Decal Sales Office:

  1. verifies the identity of the person requesting the new decal or gate access card and the requestor’s current ownership of the decal or gate access card in question

  2. accepts the replacement fee and issues the replacement decal or gate access card

  3. enters the transaction into the parking management database

    and

  4. deactivates the damaged decal or gate access card.
