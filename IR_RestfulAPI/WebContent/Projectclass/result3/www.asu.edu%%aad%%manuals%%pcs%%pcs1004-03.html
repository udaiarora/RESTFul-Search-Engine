PCS 1004–03: Sponsor-Titled Equipment

Purpose

To ensure the proper transfer of sponsor-owned equipment on active or inactive grants or contracts in accordance with federal and university requirements

Sources

United States. Office of Management and Budget. OMB circulars A-21 and A-110
Federal Acquisition Regulation § 45.6
Arizona Constitution Article IX, § 7
Property Control

Applicability

Faculty or principal investigators transferring equipment

Policy

University personnel are responsible for notifying the ASU sponsored projects officer and sponsored property coordinator when sponsored-owned equipment is being transferred to another university.

Equipment is generally transferred because faculty members move themselves and their research to other universities. Shipping or freight charges will be paid for by the university receiving the property.

Procedure D

Responsibility
Action
Department/Principal investigator
  1. Request equipment transfer authorization from the sponsored projects officer.
Sponsored projects officer
  1. Request, in writing, equipment transfer from the sponsor, and copy the sponsored property coordinator on the memo.
If equipment transfer is approved:
  1. Forward the authorization letter to the sponsored property coordinator.
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Determine from the department the equipment transfer departure date.

  2. Remove ASU property control numbers from the equipment prior to the equipment departure date.

  3. Remove the equipment from the property control system.
endtable
