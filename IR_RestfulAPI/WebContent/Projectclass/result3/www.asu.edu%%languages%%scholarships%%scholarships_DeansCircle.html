Department of Languages & Literatures Dean?s Circle Scholarship

Academic Year 2007-08

 

The DLL Dean?s Circle Scholarship is $1,000, to be awarded on an annual basis

 

Eligibility:

  • For academic year 2007-08, applicant must be an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Chinese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Russian or Spanish.
  • Minimum ASU Cumulative GPA 3.50
  • No residency requirement
  • Recipients must be enrolled full-time (minimum 12 hours) and maintain full-time enrollment to stay eligible for the scholarship
  • Financial need may be considered by the DLL Selection Committee, but is not a requirement. Note: Receiving this scholarship may affect the recipient?s financial aid.
  • If the applicant is in a concurrent degree program, he/she may be a candidate in both departments.  However, the student may accept only one Dean?s Circle departmental scholarship.

 

 

Application (deadline February 23, 2007):

Selection will be based upon:

  • the completed scholarship application, including one-page typed essay describing academic goals, 
  • one copy of student transcripts, including transfer work (unofficial copies of transcripts, or DARS reports are acceptable),
  • one recommendation letter from a faculty member.

Application and essay must be typed. Essays should be no longer than one page, double-spaced, 12-pt font, with one-inch margins.
