Billy - Business and Chinese

"ASU has prepared me to take on any challenge."

Billy, a Flinn scholar, wants to try everything. ASU has provided some exciting opportunities toward this goal.

Whether it's drafting speeches for the Governor through his ASU internship, traveling in China on an ASU study-abroad program, learning Chinese, or serving lattes at Starbucks, Billy says that all of these experiences have helped him grow, both as a student and as an independently thinking adult.

Billy says that his experiences at ASU will help him rise to any occasion and take on any challenge. He wants to pursue a graduate degree in business and/or law and hopes to put his increasing knowledge of the Chinese language to good use in the future.

He says that ASU has given him a profound appreciation for education, and he aims to embrace college life in its entirety.
