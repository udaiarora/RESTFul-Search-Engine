Renewing your books

If you need your books longer than your initial check out period, you can renew them online through your library account.

Go to the library's web site and click on the "My Account" link in the upper right hand corner of the screen.


If prompted, sign in with your ASURITE ID and password.


On the next screen, click on "Checked Out Items " to get the list of books you have checked out.


From the list, select the books to renew by clicking on the "Renew" box, then click on "Renew selected items". If you want to renew all of the books on the list, click on "Renew all".
