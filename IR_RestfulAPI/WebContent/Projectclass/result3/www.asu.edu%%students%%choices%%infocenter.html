Choices: Information Center

We have created this site to provide students in transition with a wide range of resources, tools, and strategies for development. Our mission is to empower you, the student in transition, to make informed academic decisions based on your goals. We will assist you as you prepare for success at ASU and help you define your academic and career goals.

On these pages, you will find:

  • University-wide resources for students on a full range of services, from advising to recreation and health care.
  • The majors offered at Arizona State University
  • Information on career paths, career development, and career services
  • Dates and locations of workshops designed specifically for students in transition
  • Contact information for advisors who can help you
  • Resources for anyone feeling lost in the system

Transitions can be difficult, but they are an opportunity for growth. Throughout the University you will find trained professionals who want to help you realize your ambitions, explore options, make informed choices, and recognize the multiple paths to your various destinations.
