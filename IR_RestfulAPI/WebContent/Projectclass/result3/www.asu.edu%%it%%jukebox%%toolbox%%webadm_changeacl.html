Webadmin - Change ACL

Example Screen:

Access list for /afs/asu.edu/www/YOURDIRECTORY is
Normal rights:
  system_www:YOURDIRECTORY rlidwk
  system:administrators rlidwka
  system:anyuser rl
User_ID or group:
 

What You Can Enter:

Enter the ASURITE User_ID or group of the ACL you would like to change. Then enter the ACL's you would like the User_ID or group to have. This will then change the ACL's for that User_ID or group.
