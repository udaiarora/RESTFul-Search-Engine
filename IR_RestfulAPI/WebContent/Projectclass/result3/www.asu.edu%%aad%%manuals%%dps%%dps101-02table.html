The person reporting an emergency situation locates a call box in a parking lot, structure, or high traffic area by looking for a bright blue light, and follows the instructions on the box.

The ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) Communications Center acknowledges the activated call box alarm and gives the caller instructions on how to speak into the box.

The person reporting an emergency situation:

  1. pushes the speaker button

  2. speaks into the speaker/microphone, using a normal tone of voice

    and

  3. tells the dispatcher the nature of the emergency, the location, any injuries, and other information as requested.

The ASU DPS Communications Center dispatches the necessary emergency assistance.
