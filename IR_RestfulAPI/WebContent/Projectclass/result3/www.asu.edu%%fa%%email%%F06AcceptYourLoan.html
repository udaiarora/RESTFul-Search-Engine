Have You Accepted Your Federal Direct Loan?

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this e-mail notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

According to our records, you were offered a Federal Direct Subsidized or Unsubsidized Student Loan for Fall 2006 but have not yet accepted it. Please use ASU Interactive to accept or decline this award.

If you are a first-time ASU Direct Loan borrower and you accept this loan, you will also need to complete the Direct Loan Master Promissory Note (MPN) and Direct Loan Entrance Counseling online.

To view the electronic MPN Website effectively, click here for browser instructions. If you are using an older browser that is not supported, your interaction with this site may be impacted. You will need your U.S. Department of Education issued Personal Identification Number (PIN).

Please contact us or visit one of our locations if you have any questions about this notice.
