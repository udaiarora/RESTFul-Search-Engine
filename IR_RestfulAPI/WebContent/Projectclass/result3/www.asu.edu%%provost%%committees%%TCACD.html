TEMPE CAMPUS ADVISORY COUNCIL ON DIVERSITY

The council is advisory to the executive vice president and university provost on development of policies and procedures to enhance campus diversity, particularly with regard to faculty and staff issues. It provides a vehicle for communication across academic and support units on the Tempe campus and serves as a forum for raising issues and strategies for enhancing diversity. The council is appointed by and responsible to the executive vice president and university provost.
