Definition

ASU_ID

.

Char

9

A unique nine character numeric code used as an identifier for students or employees at Arizona State University. The customary entry for a person is Social Security Number, but the system can assign a block ID number if the SSN is not available at the time of record creation, or if the person requests it.

AFFILIATE_ID

.

Char

10

A unique ten character alphanumeric code that identifies a person, organization, or entity doing business with the Arizona State University. This number can be used to uniquely identify students rather than using their ASU ID. BE CAUTIOUS in the STUDENT database as we are in the process of converting old tables, which contained only an Affiliate ID field. This Affiliate ID field actually contained ASU ID. New tables will have both ID's

COMPETENCY_START_SEMESTER

.

Char

5

The beginning semester of the two year time period given to the students to meet BOR competency requirements.

COMPETENCY_END_SEMESTER

.

Char

5

The ending semester of the two year time period given to the students to meet BOR competency requirements.

COMPETENCY_DISMISSAL_CODE

.

Char

1

Indicates the competency status of the student.

   D - Dismissed, competencies not met by end semester

   E - Extended, end semester extended for meeting Competencies

   M - Met competencies

   N - No competency record on system

   R - Rescinded, dismissal voided

HIGH_SCHOOL_CLASS_RANK_PERCENT

.

Decimal

4,2

This field is calculated from the student?s class rank divided by class size.  A student ranked 100 out of a class size of 1000 would have a class rank percent of 10%.

HIGH_SCHOOL_GPA

.

Decimal

3,2

A three digit numeric field showing the high school GPA which has been calculated for the student by the appropriate admissions office.

ABOR_HS_GPA

.

Decimal

3,2

The GPA calculated on courses that are used to fulfill competency requirements.  The general aptitude requirements for admission are based on this GPA rather than the overall High School GPA.

ARIZONA_RESIDENCY_CODE

.

Char

1

A code indicating whether a student is a legal resident of Arizona for tuition purposes.  Domicile status is assigned for tuition purposes by the appropriate Admissions offices. Y indicates an Arizona resident. N indicates they are not an Arizona resident.

RECORD_ON_SIS_FLAG

.

Char

1

Indicates if the record existed on the SIS at the last data load. Y indicates yes. N indicates no. Most common reason for no is a record where the student has changed their ASU ID number. The record with the old number will have an N while the record with the new number will have a Y.

This flag would also be set to N after 1 year if the student applies to ASU but doesn?t attend.  Applications for these folks are deleted from SIS.
