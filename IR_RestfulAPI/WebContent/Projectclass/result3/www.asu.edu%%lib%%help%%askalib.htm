Ask a Librarian

What is your question?

Ask a Librarian provides e-mail reference service primarily for ASU students, faculty and staff. Questions are normally answered within twenty-four hours. Questions submitted during weekends and holidays may require a longer response time. See the Ask a Librarian policy statement for a full description of our policy.
