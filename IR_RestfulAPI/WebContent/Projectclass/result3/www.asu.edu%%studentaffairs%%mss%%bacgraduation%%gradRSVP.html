  • Doors will open to guests at 5:30p.m. Tickets are required for admission. Tickets are FREE and must be picked up at the Multicultural Student Center (3rd floor of the Student Services Building) by May 4, 2007.
  • Graduates are asked to gather at 5:30 pm backstage to prepare for the formal processional that will begin at 6:30 pm. The entry to the backstage area is located on the East side of the building facing Gammage Parkway down the service ramp.

  • Free parking will be available at ASU's Gammage Auditorium, located at the corner of Apache Avenue and Mill Avenue. Disabled parking is available in this lot as well.
