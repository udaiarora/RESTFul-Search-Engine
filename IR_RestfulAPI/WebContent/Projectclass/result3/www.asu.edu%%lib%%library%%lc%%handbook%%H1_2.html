H.1.2: Travel Fund Procedures

Effective 12/20/89 .. Revised 5/16/96

APPLICATION FOR FUNDS


Fund availability governs the deadline(s) for travel requests. Although there may be multiple deadlines, to ensure complete consideration applicants should project travel plans for the year as early as possible and submit them by the first deadline. Using the "Application for Travel Funds" form present requests in writing to the Research and Professional Development Committee, including appropriate documentation (travel dates, program information, estimated costs, etc.). Use ASU guidelines for the amount reimbursed for travel, lodging, and per diem expenses to estimate costs (see Travel Guide and Per Diem). If more supporting documentation is needed, the Committee reserves the right to withhold a decision and request further information. Because the Committee suggests early submission of travel requests each fiscal year, individuals may request reconsideration of their allocation if earlier airfares are no longer available or registration fees have increased. Although the Committee will consider these requests, the amount of funding is always dependent on available funds.


Once the Committee approves funding, a traveler may claim reimbursement up to the amount allocated, with original receipts showing proof of expenses (receipts must show expenditures up to or greater than the amount allocated; exception: the $26/day "per diem" and "Other" expenses under $26 do not require receipts). Hotel receipts must show a zero balance for reimbursement. Individuals who are allotted travel funds should follow the procedures outlined in this section for submitting the necessary reimbursement forms.


TRAVEL AUTHORIZATION/CLAIM FORM


Obtain a ASU Travel Authorization/Claim Form from Library Administration. Complete the travel authorization (TA) portion of the form before the trip, obtaining any departmental and authorizing travel officials (ATO) signatures necessary. Forward the entire TA (do not separate and retain any copies), along with a copy of the approved funding source (e.g. Application for Travel Funds) to the Library Business Office for further processing and signatures. An ATO must sign the TA side of the form before the trip, in order to be covered by the university's insurance. The ATO's for the University Libraries are the Dean, Associate Dean for Library Services and the Senior Business Operations Manager.


The TA/Claim form will be returned to the traveler after all necessary signatures are obtained. Forms will no longer be forwarded to the ASU Travel Office before the trip. Complete the Travel Claim portion of the form after returning from the trip, and forward to the Library Business Office for processing. The Business Office will forward the TA/Claim to the Travel Office for reimbursement.


CASH ADVANCES


To receive a cash advance for your trip, complete the appropriate portion of the form, choosing either ASU check or traveler's checks. Forward the entire form to the Library Business Office for processing. The Business Office will forward the TA/Claim to the Travel Office for approval/preparation of the cash advance. This is the only instance that the TA/Claim will be forwarded to the Travel Office prior to the trip. To receive a cash advance, Travel must be notified at least 2 weeks before the trip.


DIRECT PAY FOR AIRFARE


The Library Business Office can pay airfares directly, providing the traveler uses American Express Travel on campus. After making travel arrangements, forward the TA/Claim form to the Library Business Office for payment. The travel agency will either notify you to pick up your ticket, or deliver it directly.


LIBRARY CAR AND UNIVERSITY VEHICLES


Contact the Senior Business Operations Manager to schedule use of the library car or to requisition a university vehicle. University vehicles need to be requested at least 3 to 4 weeks in advance of any trip.
