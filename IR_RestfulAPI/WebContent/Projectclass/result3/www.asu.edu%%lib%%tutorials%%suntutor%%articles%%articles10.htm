How to select the appropriate indexes?

Here are some tips for choosing appropriate indexes:

  • Examine your assignment.
    • What types of articles/information do you need? Do they need to come from scholarly journals, or is it ok to use popular magazine articles?
    • How many articles do you need?

  • What topic have you chosen?
    • Is there a subject specific index that matches the topic?
    • If it is a broad topic, or one that it commonly discussed, you may be able to use a general index.

  • How much time do you have?
    • Is your deadline approaching quickly?
    • An index that provides the full text of articles may be most helpful.

If you have difficulty locating the correct index please Ask a Librarian.
