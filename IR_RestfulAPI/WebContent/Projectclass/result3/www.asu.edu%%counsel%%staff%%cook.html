Kathy Cook joined the Office of General Counsel in November 2004. Kathy is an Executive Assistant to Nancy Tribbensee, General Counsel, and to Cynthia Jewett, Associate General Counsel. Her duties include a broad range of general and legal assistance in personnel issues, grievances and employment disputes, intercollegiate athletics, student affairs, research, intellectual property, free speech issues, police services, and parking and transit services. Prior to joining the Office of General Counsel, Kathy worked as a legal assistant in the East Valley and Las Vegas for 12 years.
