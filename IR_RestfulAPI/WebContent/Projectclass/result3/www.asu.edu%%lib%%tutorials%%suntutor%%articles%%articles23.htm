Getting your articles: In the library

If the citation is not followed by, or provides a link to the full text, you will need to locate a paper copy of the article. To see if the library owns the journal or magazine, use the online catalog.

  • Select the search option labeled Journal Title
  • Type the words of the journal title (not the article title) into the search box
  • The search screen will look like this:

Do not use abbreviations when searching for journals or magazines in the online catalog. If you do your search will not work correctly.

If you do not find the journal listed, Ask a Librarian for help.
