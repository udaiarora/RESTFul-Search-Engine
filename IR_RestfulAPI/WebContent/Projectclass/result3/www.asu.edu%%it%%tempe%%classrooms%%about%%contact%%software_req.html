Computing Sites & Classroom Software Software Request Form

This form is for requesting the addition of a software package to every machine in the Information Technology (IT) Computing Sites and Classrooms on the Tempe Campus of ASU.

ASU’s IT sites and classrooms are for general-purpose use by the entire university community. Specialized software for use by a smaller group of students is generally better served through another service offering known as an Instructor Volume.

When a general software package is selected for inclusion in the IT sites, it may be possible to perform the installation during the next available academic break (May, August, or December-January) if funding is available. On the whole, qualifying software purchased through IT funds requires a lead-time of approximately six months prior to being made available. Funding for software packages is occasionally provided through other university or departmental budgets. These requests are considered on a case-by-case basis.

Please note: This form is not secure. If you wish to submit a question or comment securely, you may obtain a paper form at the front desk of any IT Computing Site.
