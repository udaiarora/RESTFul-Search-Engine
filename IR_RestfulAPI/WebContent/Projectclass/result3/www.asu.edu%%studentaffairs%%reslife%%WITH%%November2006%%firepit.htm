Don't get burned - Important dates

RESIDENTIAL LIFE- IMPORTANT DATES TO REMEMBER

November 23 and 24 – Thanksgiving Observed – Residence Halls open
November 24 – Last day to submit “Hall/Room Transfer Request” for Fall semester. Transfer request forms are available at your hall front desk and should be submitted to the hall Office Specialist.
November 30 – License Agreement/Contract Release requests due
December 1 –Winter Break housing applications are due if you will be staying with us over break.
December 6 - Every effort will be made to notify you in writing of our decision prior to Reading Day, December 6, 2006.

December 7 - 9, 11 – 13 - Final Exams

December 14 – Commencement

You must be completely checked out no later than Friday, Dec. 15, 2006 at 5p.m.

December 15 – Residence Halls close at 5 p.m. for students not signed up for Winter Break housing

December 20 – Spring housing payment due
December 20 – Spring Meal Plan payment due.
January 13 – Residence Halls open for Spring semester on Saturday, Jan. 13t, 2006 at 8 a.m. Starting Jan. 13, you may submit “Hall/Room Transfer Requests” for Spring semester
NO TRANSFER REQUESTS FOR SPRING WILL BE ACCEPTED BEFORE JAN. 13.
No transfers will take place during the first two weeks of the semester, so you must remain in your original room assignment until your request is approved.

License Agreements are for the full academic year

Please see the Schedule of Charges and Deadlines for valid reasons for release from your License Agreement.

If you have a valid reason to request a release from your License Agreement, you MUST submit the appropriate paperwork to your Community Coordinator by Thursday, November 30, 2006.

License Agreement Release materials, along with instructions, are available at your hall front desk.
If you will be moving out permanently at the end of the semester due to withdrawal from the university, graduation, military activation, ASU sponsored academic internship, or Student-for-Student Exchange, you must complete a “License Agreement Release” by Nov. 30, 2006 and submit it to your residence hall Community Coordinator for approval

Please be reminded that your license agreement is for the full academic year and there are financial consequences for checking out prior to the end of Spring semester. If your “License Agreement Release” is approved, make sure you check-out properly with a Resident Assistant to avoid charges.
