Hello Jerry!

(originally posted 10/10/06)

The ASU Music Library is pleased to announce its latest exhibit.


“Hello Jerry!” is a joint effort between Joann Yeoman-Tongret of the Lyric Opera Theater and Kathy Kryzs of the Department of Archives and Special Collections. The exhibit features vintage album covers, playbills and other Broadway memorabilia in honor of Jerry Herman, the Tony Award-winning composer/lyricist of some of Broadway’s biggest hits, including: Hello Dolly, Mame, and La Cage aux Folles.


Jerry Herman will be bringing a team of Broadway performers to ASU for a free public seminar on October 26th, and a ticketed performance on October 28th as a special benefit for the Lyric Opera Theater.


For more information, view the Herberger College of Fine Arts announcement.

The exhibit will be on display through the month of October.
