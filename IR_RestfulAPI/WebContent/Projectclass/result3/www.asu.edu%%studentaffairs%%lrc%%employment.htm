Writing Tutor
The Learning Resource Center is seeking capable writers with strong written and verbal communication skills who are interested in working with students to help them develop their own writing skills. Tutors will also work with participating First-Year Composition instructors to support student learning and to discuss instructor expectations for written assignments. Supplemental Instruction Leader
SI Leaders needed for COM 100 and PGS 101 for Fall 2006.

SI Leaders are trained like tutors to assist students with difficult courses; they also create and provide SI materials to students on course material, and work with faculty members to create meaningful SI sessions for students.

SI Leaders must meet the following requirements:
Sophomore standing (25 or more credit hours), with coursework at ASU
Be a full time degree seeking student Cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher
Subject area grade of 3.5 or higher in tutoring subject, prior enrollment in selected is preferred.
Confirmation of content knowledge from SI faculty in each content area
Attend and successfully complete 12-hours of training
Attend mandatory bi-weekly staff meetings
Attend class/lecture for your chosen course each week
Meet with participating faculty weekly

Skills/Experience Required:
Must have a cumulative GPA of 3.0 and a 3.5 GPA in tutoring area. Must have earned a minimum of 24 credit hours at ASU. Mandatory training provided. 
Employment Locations:
SI Leaders work in any of the following locations: Manzanita, Palo Verde West, Hassayampa Academic Village, and Sonora
