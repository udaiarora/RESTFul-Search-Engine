ASU COMMISSION ON THE STATUS OF WOMEN

The commission is directed by an Executive Committee consisting of three campus commission chairs, the president’s ex-officio to the CSW, and the academic associate for the commission. Each campus commission is directed by a campus commission chair appointed by the provost of that campus. Each campus commission includes five members, a chair, and four members from each employment category (Administrative/Service Professional, Academic Professional, Faculty, and Classified Staff). Members serve staggered two-year terms. One graduate and one undergraduate student are appointed for each campus and serve one-year terms. Each campus commission will work on issues specific to the campus under a central theme as directed by the Executive Committee.
