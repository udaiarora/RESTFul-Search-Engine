Obtain a Sun Card

The Sun Card is the official photo ID card of Arizona State University. All in-person transactions require a photo ID. You can obtain your Sun Card during on-campus ASU Orientation for incoming freshmen at the Polytechnic and Tempe campuses. For more information about how to obtain a Sun Card, including how much it costs, visit the Sun Card Web site.

To obtain your Sun Card in person:

ASU at the Polytechnic campus
