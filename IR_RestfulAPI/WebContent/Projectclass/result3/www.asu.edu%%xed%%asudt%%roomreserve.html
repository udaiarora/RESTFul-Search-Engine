How to Reserve a Room

Select the type of room and the room configuration based on the number of participants and type of event.
Phone in your order to verify availability, pricing and deposit requirements (480) 965-3046. Be sure to read the Facility Policy.

Download, complete and then fax or mail the appropriate Facility Use Application Form (see instructions below).

GENERAL, FACILITY
What kinds of events are appropriate for the Mercado?
ASU at the Mercado is a multipurpose facility available to the community for educational purposes, including meetings, seminars, training sessions, and classes.

What are the Mercado hours?
The Mercado is open Monday to Thursday from 7am to 10pm, Friday from 7am to 6pm, and Saturday from 8am to 5pm. Hours vary during the summer months. Special events may occur at other hours.

RESERVATIONS, PAYMENT
Can a room reservation be held without any paperwork?
No. Due to heavy demand, a classroom request form is required. Requests are handled in the order in which they are received. When a form is received, the best available space is set aside for that function and a tentative reservation is sent to the client for review. A reservation is confirmed when the form is signed by the client and faxed to the Mercado.

How far in advance should a room be reserved?
It’s best to reserve a room as far in advance as possible. Each room request is handled in the order in which it is received. A reservation is not final until a signed contract (and a deposit, if required) is received from the client.

Are deposits required?
Room deposits are required for those organizations holding a first-time event at the Mercado. Deposits are 50% of the event’s room rental charge. Balances are billed at the end of the month in which events occur.

Is a credit card a valid form of payment?
Yes. Visa or MasterCard are accepted for deposits and room charges.

What is the Mercado reservation cancellation policy?
The facility policy contains complete details of all Mercado policies. Requirements vary to cancel a room reservation without a fee. Please call with specific questions.

FOOD, ETC
How is food provided?
There are three options:
1. Contract directly with a caterer approved by the Mercado. It is important to provide the Mercado staff with a copy of the catering invoice once the event has concluded.
2. Contract directly with a caterer of your choice. There is a $50 fee for using a caterer not on the Mercado list of approved caterers.
3. Bring your own food. There is a $30 fee for bringing non-catered food.

How are beverages ordered?
See the beverage list for selection and prices, then contact the Mercado staff to place your order.

May alcohol be served at Mercado events?
Yes. Alcoholic beverages may be permitted in certain circumstances, although a permit is required. Please contact the Mercado staff to begin the process. (The event must be hosted by a caterer who is licensed to serve alcohol, and a copy of the caterer's license must be provided to the Mercado once the client is approved through the permitting process.)

OTHER SERVICES
How early is the room available for set-up?
Meeting rooms can be accessed one hour before the start of the event.

Is outside AV equipment allowed for an event?
Yes. Outside AV equipment is allowed. Before the event, please inform the Mercado staff of all equipment needs (including power strips, etc.) so the room may be prepared in advance.

What software is available in the computer lab?
Computers are equipped with Microsoft Office, including Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Access and FrontPage. If specialty software is required, please contact Mercado staff. For other information about the computer lab, visit the Web site.

Are there copy and fax services available at the Mercado?
Yes. Contact the 2nd floor receptionist in building C. The cost for photocopying is 10 cents per page and $1 per page for transparencies. These charges can be included the room bill or paid for in cash. Also, self-service copy machines are in building C and building A (10 cents per copy, dimes only). In addition, fax service is available. The cost to send a fax is $1 for the first five pages and $1 for each additional page.

How is Mercado staff to be contacted during the event?
Use the beige telephone on the wall in the meeting room to call the second floor receptionist, who will handle the request. Appropriate Mercado staff will respond to your needs.

PARKING
Where is parking available? Is there a fee associated with parking?
The Heritage and Science Park garage is the most convenient parking for Mercado event participants. The garage is at the southeast corner of Monroe and Fifth streets. There are two garage entrances; one is on Monroe Street and another on Fifth Street. Additional parking is available at parking meters along the street.

All reservations are subject to a $5 facility use/parking fee per person. This fee covers tables, chairs and room set-up, and one parking validation per person.

Over the years we have been in business we have been able to provide limited free parking for the users of the facility. As our costs have increased, this fee will help us continue to provide you with high quality service.

LODGING, NEARBY ATTRACTIONS
Are there restaurants and other things to do nearby?
Yes. Within walking distance are restaurants, museums, theatres, sports venues, and historic buildings. The Copper Square Web site has current and helpful information.

Is there convenient lodging for out-of-town participants?
Yes. Several hotels are within walking distance of Mercado and many more are nearby.
