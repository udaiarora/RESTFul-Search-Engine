CAMPUS RECREATION BOARD OF GOVERNORS

The board is advisory to the vice president for university and student initiatives on matters of general policies and budget for the Recreational Sports Program and the Student Recreation Complex. The board is appointed by and responsible to the vice president for university and student initiatives for multiyear terms. Student members are appointed by the president on the recommendation of the vice president for university and student initiatives. The board meets on the third Wednesday of each month, except in the summer. The first meeting is in September and the last meeting is in May unless more meetings are needed.
