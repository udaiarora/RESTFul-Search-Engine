Office of the President

In addition to President Crow’s administrative staff, there are several specialized program offices affiliated with the Office of the President. These offices include: the Office of University Initiatives; the Office of Sustainability Initiatives; and the Office of the Advisor to the President on American Indian Affairs.

The day-to-day operation of the university is managed by President Crow and his senior administrative team.

The President’s office is located on the fourth floor of the ASU Fulton Center. [Map & Directions]
