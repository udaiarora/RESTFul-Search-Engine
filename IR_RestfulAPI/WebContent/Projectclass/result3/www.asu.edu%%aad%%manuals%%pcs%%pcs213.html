PCS 213: Loaned Equipment

Purpose

To identify and record equipment loaned to ASU by external agencies

Sources

Property Control
Purchasing and Business Services

Policy

ASU assumes no responsibility for non-owned equipment on loan or demonstration to ASU except by written agreement.  The agreement must be signed by the appropriate person with signature authority. The Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form shall be submitted to Purchasing and Business Services to ensure proper insurance coverage through the Arizona Department of Administration, Risk Management Section.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
If equipment will be on campus for fewer than 30 days:
Department
  1. Obtain an Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form from Purchasing and Business Services and prepare it before accepting the equipment.

  2. Send a photocopy of the form to Purchasing and Business Services.
Purchasing and Business Services
  1. Send a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.
If equipment will be on campus for 30 days or more:
Department
  1. Prepare an Equipment on Loan or Demonstration form before accepting the equipment.

  2. Send a photocopy of the form to

    1. Purchasing and Business Services

      and

    2. Property Control.
Purchasing and Business Services
  1. Send a photocopy of the form to Insurance Services.
endtable

Cross-Reference

For more information on the university guidelines for accounting for equipment on loan or demonstration and not owned by the university, see the Purchasing and Business Services Policies and Procedures Manual—PUR 502–06, “Equipment on Loan or Demonstration.”
