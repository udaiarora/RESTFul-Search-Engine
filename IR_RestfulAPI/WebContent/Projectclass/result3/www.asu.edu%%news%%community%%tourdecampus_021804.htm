W. P. Carey MBA Volunteer Council Organizes Road Race; Fifth Annual Tour de Campus 5K Charity Run

What: Fifth Annual Tour de Campus 5K Charity Run
Where: Arizona State University Main Campus, Tempe
When: Sunday, February 29 at 8:00 am
Cost: $10 for students, $20 for public before Feb. 25.; $15 for students, $25 for public after Feb. 25.
Benefiting: The Valley's less fortunate children

The fifth annual Tour de Campus 5K Charity Run takes off at 8 a.m. from the Student Recreation Center at Arizona State University in Tempe on Sunday, Feb. 29. Organized by the W. P. Carey MBA Volunteer Council at the W. P. Carey School of Business, the race takes participants on a tour of the university's beautiful campus. All proceeds will benefit the children of the Phoenix Children's Hospital, the Thomas J. Pappas School, and the United Way.

Online registration is available at www.active.com until Feb. 25. Search under individual sports for W. P. Carey MBA Le Tour de Campus Charity Run, or enter the zip code 85287. Participants may also register in person on race day beginning at 7 a.m. at the Student Recreation Center, Apache Boulevard and Lemon Street on the southeast corner of campus. All entrants receive a T-shirt and post-race refreshments.

Sponsors include t he W. P. Carey MBA Volunteer Council, the W. P. Carey School of Business, the W. P. Carey MBA Association, the W. P. Carey MBA Alumni Council, Associated Students of Arizona State University (ASASU), and the Arizona State Savings & Credit Union.
