Our Goal

The mission of Adelante is to provide academic and social support and bring cultural awareness for undergraduate students determined to be successful in college.

Our goal is to inspire our members to be professional, scholarly, and culturally conscious leaders, while also experiencing the best of the college life.

? Assists students to achieve their goal of graduation

? Provide students with the resources to overcome barriers and be successful

? Give back to the community through service

? Provide a forum to exercise and develop as leaders

? Maintain pride in the Latino/Chicano/Hispanic culture and experience
