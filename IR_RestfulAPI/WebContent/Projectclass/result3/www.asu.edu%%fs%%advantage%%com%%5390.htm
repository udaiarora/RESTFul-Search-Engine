Alumni

5390

11

Gifts from former students who have graduated from ASU. Receipts may be in the form of cash or gifts-in-kind.

Individuals

5390

16

Gifts from all persons not classified as alumni or parents. Receipts may be in the form of cash or gifts-in-kind.

Parents

5390

21

Gifts from persons other than alumni who are parents, grandparents, or guardians of either currently enrolled or former students. Receipts may be in the form of cash or gifts-in-kind.

Religious Organizations

5390

26

Gifts from churches, synagogues, temples, and their denominational entities and service groups. Receipts may be in the form of cash or gifts-in-kind.

Fund-Raising Consortia

5390

31

Gifts from entities formed by a group of cooperating institutions or organizations to facilitate their fund-raising activities. Receipts may be in the form of cash or gifts-in-kind.

Donated Services

5390

40

Gifts in Kind of donated services.

Other Organizations

5390

99

Gifts from nonpublic organizations not described by another revenue source. Receipts may be in the form of cash or gifts-in-kind.
