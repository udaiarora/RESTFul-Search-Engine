Researching Non-Profit Organizations in Arizona


Nonprofit organizations must follow many of the general requirements as businesses in most states.  The following are our general guides to doing business research:

  1. Company & Industry Research especially:
      Researching a Company --- or use the Researching Small Businesses in Arizona
      Researching an Industry --- or use the Arizona Small Business Industry Analysis
  2. Cities
  3. Marketing Resources especially:
      Consumers, Polls, and Market Data
      Demographic, Economic, and Statitical Information
When you are ready to focus on Arizona, the best sources are
  1. Directories:
      Reference USA
      DexOnline (yellow pages)

    Specialized for Non Profit Organizations:

      Arizona Registered Charitable Organizations
      Associations Unlimited (print edition: Encyclopedia of Associations)
      BBB Wise Giving Alliance National Charity Reports Index
      Business Journal (Phoenix) special supplement: "Corporate Citizenship". 1997-
        HD 60.5 .U52 A73x HAYDEN REFERENCE
      Charitable Choices -- Index of All Charities
      Foundation Center Foundation Finder
      Guidestar Charity search
      IRS list of organizations eligible to receive tax-deductible charitable contributions
      Minnesota Attorney General's Office Charity search
      United Way Partner Agencies in Metro Phoenix, Arizona
       
  2. News:
      Arizona Newspapers web sites (Newslink)
        I prefer searching the newspaper's site directly and using the indexed and full text access from other providers:
          Access World News, Business NewsBank, CD News: the Arizona Republic, Arizona Business Gazette , Ethnic Newswatch, Lexis/Nexis Academic, Newspaper Abstracts, Wall Street Journal (Asian WSJ, WSJ Europe)
      Radio web sites (Newslink)
        I have found that the network sites rather than the local station sites easier to search for news.
      TV web sites (Newslink)
        I have found that the network sites rather than the local station sites easier to search for news.
       
  3. Arizona Government and Business Information
      Arizona Business Information
        Types of Business Information -- Where to Find it in Arizona
        Suggested public information available on what a non-profit is, what it owns, what it owes, what it is owed, etc. :
          - Asset/Liens & Deeds (Real property ownership, what they owe on it & to whom)
          - Bankruptcy Court Cases (Special Federal Court -- who has filed for protection or reorganization)
          - Charity Organization & Fundraiser (Has the organization registered with the Secretary of State as a Charity/Fundraiser?)
          - Incorporation (Has the organization registered with the state corporation commission?)
          - Licenses & Registrar of Contractors (List of the licensing agencies for professions & who is registered)
          - Patents & Trademarks both State & US (Do they have royalty rights and claims to a name, slogan, or invention?)
          - Plaintiff/Defendant Index to court cases (Who has sued them, whom did they sue, & criminal actions connected with them.)
          - Property Taxes (especially important if there is a TAX LIEN)
          - Tax Valuation/Assessments (What is their real property worth for tax purposes?)
      Arizona State & Local Governments on the Net
       
  4. Non Profit Financial Information, including IRS code 501(c)3 organizations Form 990:
      Arizona Corporation Commission forms
      BBB Wise Giving Alliance National Charity Reports Index
      Guidestar Charity search
      Minnesota Attorney General's Office Charity search

    To compare to statistics collected by the IRS and others:
