Co-Sponsored Events

For co-sponsor consideration please send the answers to the following questions via e-mail.

1-What's the name of your event?

2-How does the event advance the understanding of intergroup relations, social justice, and/or diversity?

3-Will the event be open to the campus? and free of charge?

4-How will the audience be engaged through dialogue or otherwise to achieve a deeper understanding of the subject matter presented, and/or to achieve a stronger connection to others on campus (beyond their typical circle/group)?

5-What is the overall budget and what are you requesting that the IRC cover? What other co-sponsors have you secured?

6-How will the IRC be noted as a co-sponsor?
