Dr. Daniel J. Canary

Professor

 

STA 335D

(480) 965-6650

dan.canary@asu.edu

Dan Canary (Ph.D., University of Southern California) is Professor of Human Communication at Arizona State University.  Dr. Canary has co-authored or edited 10 books and over 50 articles, book chapters, and essays.  Dan is a Past President of the International Network for Personal Relationships and recent Editor, Western Journal of Communication.  Also, Professor Canary serves as a member on several editorial boards of journals that publish research on relationships and communication.  Dan likes to write music, play guitar, and travel.
