Academic Senate and Assembly at the Tempe Campus

Academic Senate Standing Committees and Faculty Grievance Bodies

Personnel Committee
The Personnel Committee addresses policy and procedure matters dealing with faculty governance, hiring, affirmative action, academic freedom, grievance, tenure, promotion, sabbatical and other leaves of absence, faculty and academic professional development, insurance, retirement, and other faculty and academic professional perquisites. 
   Committee Roster
   Contact the Committee Chair

Student-Faculty Policy Committee
The SFPC serves a policy-forming and advisory capacity in matters governing student conduct, organizations, admissions, registration, grading, retention, graduation requirements, academic integrity, scheduling, advisement and counseling, undergraduate education, and all other student-faculty academic concerns. 
   Committee Roster
   Contact the Committee Chair

University Affairs Committee 
University Affairs serves in an advisory capacity in the study, clarification,and formulation of policies and procedures in areas affecting the university, including public relation initiatives at the legislature and legislative coffees, campus master planning, design review, policy review for special use of facilities on campus, allocation of university resources, university-wide information and communication services, parking, physical plant, athletic programs, library, computer services, auxiliary services, utilities, and other university facilities. 
    Committee Roster
    Contact the Committee Chair

Committee on Committees 
The Committee on Committees recruits and nominates faculty and academic professionals for election or appointment to senate committees, university committees, and faculty-student committees. It does this in part through the Academic Preference Surveys circulated to faculty and academic professionals. 
    Committee Roster
    Contact the Committee Chair

Curriculum and Academic Program Committee 
CAPC deals with the establishment, modification, and disestablishment of curricula and academic programs. This includes academic units at all levels, laboratories, centers and institutes, as well as research programs, extended education, and honorary degrees. Recommendations of the Committee typically come before the Senate, which makes recommendations to the University president and the Arizona Board of Regents. 

    Committee Roster (2005-06)

   Contact the Committee Chair

Committee on Academic Freedom and Tenure (CAFT)

The committee reports to the senate developments in the areas of academic freedom and tenure.  It investigates alleged infringements upon the academic freedom or tenure of faculty members and reports to the senate.  It hears cases and makes recommendations to the president involving faculty complaints of discrimination; procedural regularity in connection with a decision to deny tenure, promotion, or renewal of a contract; and denial of academic freedom. 

   Committee Roster

  Contact the Committee Chair

Governance Grievance Committee (GGC)

The committee hears complaints alleging unfair treatment because of absence or misinterpretation, misapplication, or violation of policies or procedures.  Annually, it reports its summary recommendations about policy and procedures to the Senate president and the university president.
