January 27, 2004
Academic Assembly Remarks
A comprehensive metropolitan research university

January 2004
Led by President Michael Crow a team of ASU faculty and ASU Foundation board members traveled to mainland China and Taiwan to follow up on a series of “scouting” visits in previous months. The broad goal of the enterprise was to connect with various potential academic, financial, and research partners and to discuss sustainability science, biosciences, science policy, business education, economic and trade related research, and technology transfer in the context of ASU’s vision for a “New American University.” Prospective partners and allies include top Chinese universities, ministries of the central government of the People’s Republic of China, municipal leaders of Shanghai, executives of state owned enterprises, and independent business entrepreneurs. Development was significantly advanced and plans have been set into motion as a result of this trip, the first stage of a new initiative to link ASU with China across several arenas.
