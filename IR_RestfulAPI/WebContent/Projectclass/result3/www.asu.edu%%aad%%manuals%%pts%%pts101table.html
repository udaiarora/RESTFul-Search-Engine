Vehicle owners may register vehicles and purchase decals (with associated gate-access keycard) at the Decal Sales Office, Parking and Transit Services, located on Fifth Street between Forest and College avenues or online. Owners must furnish an ASU ID card (or driver’s license), a completed decal application, and vehicle license plate number(s), and may pay by cash, check, or authorized credit card, or by payroll deduction for qualified faculty and staff.

The Decal Sales Office:

  1. verifies the eligibility and identity of applicants before accepting payment

  2. inputs the data into the parking system

    and

  3. issues a receipt and the ASU Parking and Vehicle Control Regulations map with each decal and gate-access keycard.

Vehicle owners must display the proper decal in registered vehicle(s) in accordance with the ASU Parking and Vehicle Control Regulations and ensure that each motor vehicle registered to the parking decal remains in compliance with State of Arizona emissions standards during the entire registration period.
