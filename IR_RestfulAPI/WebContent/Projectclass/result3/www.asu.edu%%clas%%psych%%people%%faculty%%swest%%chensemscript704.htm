Note. The four first-order factors are defined as endogenous latent variables (LE), and LYs are used to define the lower-order factor loadings; whereas the second-order factor are defined as an exogenous variable (LK), and GAs are used to defined the second-order factor loadings.

 

The first factor loading of each factor is set to 1, and the variance of that factor is estimated; if an observed variable?s factor loading is set to 1, that variable?s intercept is set to 0. The second-order factor mean is set to 0, and intercepts of the lower-order factors are estimated.

 

'diffeas' ? difficulty in reasoning; 'sloract' ? slow in reacting; 'confsed' ? confused; 'diffconc' ? difficulty in concentrating; 'enrgtic' ? energetic; 'wornout' ? worn out;

''frust? ? frustrated; 'hlthwry' ? worry about health.

 

QOL - Quality of Life ; Cogni ? Cognition ; Vitali ? Vitality ; MenHeal ? Mental Health ; DisWorry ? Disease Worry.
