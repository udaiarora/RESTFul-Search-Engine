ASU MUSEUMS, GALLERIES, AND COLLECTIONS COMMITTEE

The committee is a vital outreach service organized to promote awareness of the resources, programs, and common concerns of various museums, galleries and collections at ASU. There are a wealth of facilities throughout the university that manage collections and develop educational exhibitions in the sciences, humanities, and arts. The goals of the committee are:


  • To reach and attract diverse audiences on the ASU campus and the Arizona community.
  • To create and maintain a viable outreach program.
  • To seek opportunities for university museums, galleries, and collections professionals to work together to develop educational exhibits and programs.
