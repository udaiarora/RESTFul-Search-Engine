April 15, 2004

Herberger College School of Music and Washburn team up for substantial piano savings

WHAT: Annual Piano Sale at ASU's Herberger College School of Music

WHEN: May 14: ASU alumni preview, 9:30 a.m. - 6 p.m.

   May 15: Open to the public, 9:30 a.m. - 6 p.m.

   May 16: Open to the public, 12-5 p.m.               

WHERE: Music Building, 40 E. Gammage Parkway, Tempe

INFORMATION: 480-727-6770, beginning May 7 .

TEMPE, Ariz. - Enjoy substantial savings on the purchase of a used piano at the annual Herberger College School of Music Piano Sale at ASU.

The sale is Friday - Sunday, May 14-16 at the Music Building on ASU's main campus in Tempe. The Washburn Piano Company will offer a selection of Steinway, Yamaha, Kawai, Baldwin and Charles Walter pianos that have been on loan to the School of Music during the 2002-2003 academic year.

The sale commences with an ASU alumni preview day from 9:30 a.m. - 6 p.m. on Friday, May 14. It is open to the general public from 9:30 am - 6 p.m. on Saturday, May 15, and 12-5 p.m. on Sunday, May 16.

Those unable to attend the sale on the days listed above may call to schedule a preview appointment on Thursday, May 13, although not all pianos will be at the site that day due to set-up schedules.

All pianos for sale have received regular maintenance by professional technicians and many carry manufacturers' warranties. The selection of keyboard instruments includes grand pianos and vertical models in all finishes and sizes. Financing and delivery will be available.

Call 480-727-6770 to make a phone appointment, beginning May 7 . Please note that this phone number will not be operational prior to May 7.
