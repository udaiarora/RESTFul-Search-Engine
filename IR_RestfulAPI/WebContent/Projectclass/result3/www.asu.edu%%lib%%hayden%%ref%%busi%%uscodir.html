COMPANY INFORMATION SOURCES

U.S. Company Directories


Check also Telephone and E-Mail Directories (http://www.asu.edu/lib/ref/gen/phbks.html) to find basic directory information on companies by name, location, or product/service.
    When you see this button next to an entry, click on it to go to the ASU Libraries Indexes page. You may also get to this page by clicking on INDEXES on any of our web pages, including our Home Page: http://www.asu.edu/lib/


  1. America's Corporate Families and International Affiliates. 3 vols. Parsippany, NJ: Dun & Bradstreet Information Services, 1983- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4057 .A1563x Vol. 1 & 2 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)
      HG 4057 .A146 Vol. 3 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)

    Lists over 16,400 parent companies having $50 million or more annual sales and their 100,000 subsidiaries, divisions, and major branches. The first volume lists ultimate parents alphabetically with their respective subsidiaries, divisions, etc. arranged hierarchically under them. The second volume contains cross-indexing by subsidiary/division (to determine the parent company), by geographic location, and by SIC. The third volume covers multi-national companies and provides the same type of organization information on each parent and its dependent units with the same cross-indexing features found in the first two volumes.

  2. American Business Disc. Omaha, NE: American Business Directories, 19 - . Annual. (ASU owns latest year.)
      ONLINE ACCESS Click on Business

    Covers directory information for over 10 million business locations in the U.S. Searches by company name, type of business, geographic location, size, etc. Notes headquarters, subsidiary, and branch locations as well as public status and ticker symbol/stock exchange.
  3. Companies International. Detroit, MI: Gale Research, 1993- . Annual. (ASU owns current year.)
      HG 4009 .C66x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located in MARS Room)

    Provides a computer-searchable listing of private and public company directory information taken from Ward's Business Directory (see entry later in this subsection) for U.S. companies and from the World Business Directory for companies in more than 180 other countries. Can be searched by company name, location, SIC code, Harmonized System codes, size of company, names of officers, etc. to locate information on a specific company or produce lists of companies meeting particular criteria.
  4. CompaniesOnline
      http://www.companiesonline.com

    Dun & Bradstreet provides this searchable web directory of over 100,000 public and private companies. Search criteria are company name, location, industry, number ranges of annual sales and/or employees, ticker symbol, and URL. The company listings also include D-U-N-S number, immediate and ultimate parent, key contact, trade names, and ownership, as well as ticker symbol, exchange, and latest stock quote for public companies. Beware however, that many listings have left the industry field blank so searches by industry will be incomplete.
  5. CorpTech Database
      http://www.corptech.com

    Offers directory information on 45,000 high-tech U.S. companies searchable by company name, stock symbol, product/industry, and executive name. Capsule reports are free and give annual sales range, ownership, business description, CEO, and web address while profile reports offering additional information are available for a fee.
  6. Directory of Corporate Affiliations. 6 vols. New Providence, NJ: National Register, 1967- . Annual. (ASU owns 1967- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4057 .A219 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)

    A "who owns whom" for determining relationships between companies which is divided into separate volumes for the master index, public companies, private companies, and international companies. The first section in Vol. I is a master alphabetical index leading from divisions, subsidiaries, etc., to the parent company. Parent company entries provide basic directory information on the parent, followed by its subsidiaries, divisions, and affiliates and their reporting relationship. There are also cross indexes by brand name, geographic location, SIC code for industries, and personnel.
  7. Dun's Directory of Service Companies. Parsippany, NJ: Dun & Bradstreet Information Services, 1989- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HD 9981.3 .D86 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)

    Provides the same coverage for service companies as the Million Dollar Directory (see entry later in this subsection) does for non-service companies.
  8. Hoover's Handbook of American Business. Austin, TX: Reference Press, 1991- . Annual. (ASU owns 1991- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4057 .A28617 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)

    Profiles over 700 public and private companies. Each one-page profile gives a description of the company, a short history, a 10-year table of basic financials, directory information, product lines, brand names, subsidiaries, rankings, and competitors. A separate section reproduces company rankings from well-known sources like the Fortune 500. Indexes provide access by industry, corporate officers, brands, and subsidiaries.
  9. Hoover's Online
      http://www.hoovers.com
      ONLINE ACCESS (LEXIS-NEXIS Academic Universe)       Click on Business

    The web site provides free capsule information on 10,000 public and private companies. This may include a summary of the company business and current situation; a link to their corporate web page; employee, sales, and income numbers; key officer; 24-hour delayed SEC filings; mission statement; and job openings. In addition, for a subscription fee there is access to full-page profiles for 2600 companies, as seen in the various Hoover's printed company handbooks, as well as real-time SEC filings online. The full profiles can also be found electronically through the library's LEXIS-NEXIS Academic Universe subscription in their "Company Financial Information" section as one of the report types.
  10. Million Dollar Directory. 5 vols. Parsippany, NJ: Dun & Bradstreet Information Services, 1959- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HC 102 .D8 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)

    Provides basic directory information on approximately 160,000 firms with the greatest net worth, including some 30,000 private companies. The first three volumes list the companies alphabetically; two other volumes contain geographic (yellow pages) and industry (SIC) indexes. Entries for each company include approximate sales, number of employees, stock exchange where traded, ticker symbol, division names, etc. The Reference Book of Corporate Managements on the same table is a companion publication providing brief biographical sketches on corporate executives and Dun's Business Rankings, also in the same location, provides rankings by sales volume and by employee size within industries and states.
  11. Standard & Poor's Register of Corporations, Directors & Executives. 3 vols. New York: Standard & Poor's, 1928- . Annual. (ASU owns 1980- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4057 .A4 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)

    Lists more than 45,000 public and private companies. Company information is alphabetically arranged in vol. 1, with each entry including the address, telephone number, corporate officers, description of business lines, SICs, stock exchange(s) in the case of publicly-traded companies, and sometimes annual sales figures, number of employees, and company logo. Biographical information on corporate officers is in vol. 2, and indexes (geographical, SIC, and personal name) are in vol. 3.
  12. Ward's Business Directory of U.S. Private and Public Companies. 6 vols. Detroit, MI: Gale Research, 1961- . Annual. (ASU owns 1981, 1984, 1986- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4057 .A47x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)
      HG 4009 .C66x HAYDEN REFERENCE (Companies International located in MARS Room)

    Especially valuable for identifying privately-held companies, this gives address, CEO, estimated annual sales, number of employees, year established, and SIC of U.S. companies. An electronic version of the most current issue is incorporated in Companies International (see entry earlier in this subsection). In the paper version, vols. 1, 2, and 3 list company entries alphabetically; vol. 4 lists companies geographically by zip code within each state and also contains a "Special Features" section of rankings and analyses; and vol. 5 provides industry rankings (by SIC) of companies as well as an alphabetical listing of SIC codes. Note the rankings are by sales in most industries but in some instances they may be by total assets, gross billings, or operating revenues. See the "User's Guide" at the front of the volume to determine the financial basis for ranking your industry. A new addition is the last volume, printed in two parts, which provides state rankings of companies by sales within SIC code.
