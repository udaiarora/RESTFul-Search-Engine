When You Arrive

 

From Phoenix Sky Harbor Airport

All major airlines fly into Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport. ASU's Tempe campus is located in Tempe, Arizona, approximately 15 minutes from Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport.

  • Take Red Mountain Freeway Loop 202 East to the Scottsdale/Rural Rd. (exit 7).
  • Turn right onto Rural Rd. and drive south to Apache Blvd.
  • Turn right (west) onto Apache Blvd.
  • Turn left (south) on College Ave.
  • Make first left into Parking Lot 17 West. (Please note that this is a metered lot with the meter for all stalls located near the crosswalk.)

When You Arrive

Welcome to ASU's Tempe campus. Please arrive 30 minutes early to park and walk to the Student Services Building.

ASU Visitor Parking is available in Lot 17 West on the southeast corner of Apache Blvd. and College Ave. Limited visitor parking space may be available in Parking Structure 1 (accessible from Lemon St.) and Parking Structure 4 (accessible from Rural Rd.). Posted regulations and fees apply. Please observe and obey all of ASU's posted parking regulations. Parking fees are subject to change without notice. For more information, please visit the Parking and Transit Services web site at www.asu.edu/dps/pts or call 480.965.4527.

Once you have parked, proceed to the Student Services Building (SSV) located on the corner of Forest Ave. and Gammage Pkwy. A major landmark West of the SSV is Gammage Auditorium on the southwest corner of the Tempe campus. Once inside the SSV, please check in for your visit at the START General Information Desk in the center of the first floor of the SSV.
