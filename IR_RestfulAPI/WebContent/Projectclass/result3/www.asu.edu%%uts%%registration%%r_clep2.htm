CANCELLATION & TRANSFER POLICY : The $15.00 test registration fee is non-transferable and non-refundable .

RESTRICTIONS: You will not receive credit on a CLEP exam for a lower-level or prerequisite course when you have already received credit at that level or at a higher-level within the same field. If you have accumulated more than 100 credit hours, it is possible that you will not receive CLEP credit. Check with your advisor and the department in which you are majoring before you register. You must wait six months to retake the same CLEP exam.

IF YOU ARE TAKING ENGLISH COMPOSITION WITH ESSAY or FRESHMAN COLLEGE COMPOSITION: ASU will neither honor CLEP scores to fulfill the “First-Year Composition” requirement nor award elective credit for these tests. However, CLEP examinations in this area may qualify you to register for ENG 105. Alternatively, you may take the English Placement Test to qualify for ENG 105 provided you obtain an acceptable score. There is no charge for the English Placement test. However, you may take it only once.

SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS: (No additional fee.) We must have advance official written approval from ASU Disability Resource Services (DRS) to grant any special accommodations to a candidate. A physician’s note alone is not sufficient.
