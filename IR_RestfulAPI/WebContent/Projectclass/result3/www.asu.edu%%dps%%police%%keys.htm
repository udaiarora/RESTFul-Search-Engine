Key Control

ASU Police Services Key Control handles all requests for keys to ASU Main Campus buildings and is open from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday through Friday (excluding University holidays).  Police Services works in conjunction with the Facilities Management Key Shop to fill requests for keys.

Copies of the current Key Policy and Procedures, the Key Action Form, and the Approval Authority Form may be obtained by calling 965-6090. A copy of the Key Action Form can also be printed from the ASU DPS web site at http://www.asu.edu/dps/police/konline.htm The Key Policy is published in the Department of Public Safety Policies and Procedures Manual, as DPS 206, "Building Access Services."  The normal procedure for requesting ASU Main Campus keys is as follows:

  1. Complete a Key Action Form. Make sure all information is filled in correctly and that an Authorized Signer approves the request. Instructions for completing the Key Action form are included on the back side of the form.

    2.  
  2. The original form must either be brought to the front desk of the DPS Administration Building or be mailed to ASU DPS at mail code 0804.

    3.  
  3. Forms that have been completed correctly will generally be processed within two working days from the day the request is received. For example, if Key Action Forms are received by Key Control on Monday before 4:30 PM the key(s) will be ready after 10:00 AM on Wednesday of the same week.

    4.  
  4. The keyholder or their designee may pick up the requested keys at the front desk of the DPS Administration Building, from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Anyone picking up keys must know for whom and for what building the keys have been requested. Authorized pickup persons must also present a valid form of picture identification upon request.

    5.  
  5. All lost or stolen keys must be reported to ASU Police Services within 24 hours of the discovery of their loss. Please call Key Control at 965-6090 to file a loss or theft report during normal working hours. Keyholders are responsible for all keys issued to them and will be charged a fine if their keys are lost or stolen. Please refer to the Key Policy for more information.

    6.  
  6. Keys may be transferred from one keyholder to another as long as a Key Action Form is completed. Instructions for transferring keys are included on the back of the Key Action Form. Completed forms must be mailed to ASU DPS at mail code 0804.

    7.  
  7. Please call Key Control at 965-6090 if you have any questions regarding key request policies and procedures.

    8.
