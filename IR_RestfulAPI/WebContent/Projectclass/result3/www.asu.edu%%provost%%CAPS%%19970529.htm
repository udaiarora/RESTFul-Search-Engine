Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

May 29, 1997

  1. The minutes for the May 1 meeting were approved.

  2. Replacement for Cristina: Elliot Palais, Chairperson for the Committee on Committees will take care of finding a replacement for Cristina by the beginning of the new fiscal year.

  3. Officers for next year: The committee discussed the procedure for electing the next slate of officers. We also discussed the pros and cons of having co-chairs, or expanding the duties of the chair-elect. The actual election will take place after the beginning of the new fiscal year.

  4. Still no word on any progress concerning revisions to ACD 506-09.

  5. Post-continuing review: the PACAPS/CAPS combined committee has reviewed and commented on the document that Annette has revised; it is a straight-forward adaptation of the faculty document. The committee will continue working on the justification of the AP status; Gary and Marilyn Myers are compiling statistics, Alison is working on an initial draft that she will distribute via email for comments.

  6. New Business

  • Pat is taking care of weeding the old CAPS files from Darby Shaw, AS Office, and will consolidate them into the archives. Pat has also prepared an instruction sheet for maintaining the archives.

  • Text change to "Who are APs": instead of "àare the caretakers of the University Collectionsà", now reads "are responsible for the University Collectionsà".

  • Gary will invite the newly-elected CAPS members to the June 26 meeting.
