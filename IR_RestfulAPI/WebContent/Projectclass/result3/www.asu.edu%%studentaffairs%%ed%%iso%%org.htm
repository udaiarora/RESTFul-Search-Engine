International Student Organizations

These groups provide some or all the following services: transportation from the airport, temporary housing, help with permanent housing, initial grocery shopping, help with registration, and social activities.

For groups listed below without a direct link to their Web site, visit the
Student Organization Resource Center (SORC) Web site to search for currentcontact information. They are divided alphabetically and by
category. Specific groups may be found by selecting the first letter of the group's name in the left hand column.

For the names and telephone numbers of current organization officers and of individuals who will aid students not represented by formal organizations, contact us by e-mail at: iso@asu.edu.
