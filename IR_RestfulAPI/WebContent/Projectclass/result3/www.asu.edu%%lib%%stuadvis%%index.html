Students...Come Dine with the Dean!

Library Student Advisory Committee

 

The Library Student Advisory Committee (LSAC) is a new committee formed to open a channel of communication between students and Sherrie Schmidt, the Dean of Libraries & University Librarian. The library will use the committee to solicit advice from students in order to enhance library resources and services.

 

The Library Student Advisory Committee will meet once each semester in Hayden Library (with food provided!). Student members are asked to make at least a one-year commitment to serving on the committee. Students must be users of one or more libraries on the Tempe campus and/or they should be users of the library's online resources. Can't make one of the formal meetings? Don't worry, there will be other opportunities to participate through such things as focus groups, online surveys and other library events.

 

How will serving on the Library Student Advisory Committee benefit you, the student? Serving on the committee will provide an opportunity to share with the administration concerns of highest priority to students; the chance to meet new friends; it will enhance your resume; and, of course, will provide two free meals a year.

 

The next meeting will be held in the Fall 2006 semester, date to be announced.
