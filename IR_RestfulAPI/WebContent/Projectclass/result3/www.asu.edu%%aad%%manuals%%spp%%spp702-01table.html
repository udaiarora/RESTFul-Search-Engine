Accrual Schedule for Classified Employees (100% FTE)

Beginning with year 1 of service, maximum accrual hours per pay period is 3.67; maximum annual accrual in hours is 88; maximum accumulation hours (1 1/2 x annual accrual) is 132.

Beginning with year 3 of service, maximum accrual hours per pay period is 5.34; maximum annual accrual in hours is 128; maximum accumulation hours (1 1/2 x annual accrual) is 192.

Beginning with year 5 of service, maximum accrual hours per pay period is 7.34; maximum annual accrual in hours is 176; maximum accumulation hours (1 1/2 x annual accrual) is 264.

Accrual Schedule for Service Professionals and Administrative Employees (100% FTE)

Maximum accrual hours per pay period is 7.34; maximum annual accrual in hours is 176; maximum accumulation hours (1 1/2 x annual accrual) is 264.
