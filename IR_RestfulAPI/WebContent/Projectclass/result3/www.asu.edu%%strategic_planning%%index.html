Inaugural Address, November 2002

Strategic Planning and Policy Analysis is responsible for the development of campus and university strategic plans that are submitted to the Arizona Board of Regents, the Governor's Office of Strategic Planning and Budgeting, and the Joint Legislative Budget Committee. Vice Presidents, Deans, and other administrators are consulted throughout the development process. In addition, policy analysis is provided to university leadership on various higher education issues.
