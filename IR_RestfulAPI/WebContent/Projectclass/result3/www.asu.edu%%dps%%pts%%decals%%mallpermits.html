ASU Mall Permits

ASU Parking and Transit Services must have notification from an ASU department before issuing a mall pass. The change in policy is the due to safety concerns raised after an internal audit.

Requests for mall passes must be accompanied by approval from the sponsoring department. ASU departments who sponsor events on campus that require vehicles to load, unload or operate in an unrestricted mall area must provide written approval to Parking & Transit Services at least 48 hours prior to the desired date of issuance. Approval requests may be submitted via e-mail to parksvcs@asu.edu. The e-mail must include the project name, company name or name of contractor requesting mall passes, company representative, project start date, finish date and times of operation.

New - Beginning January of 2006, any company granted permission by an ASU department to park on the mall is now required to bring in a business card when coming into purchase their Mall Pass. If the ASU Department is picking up the pass they will need to bring in a business card for the company utilizing the mall space.

Companies and other customers may no longer call PTS Events directly to make arrangements for mall passes.

Mall passes should be picked up at the Decal Sales office located in the Towers Bldg. at Forest Ave. and 5th street. Be sure to have license plate number(s) and vehicle description(s) available when picking up Mall Permits.
