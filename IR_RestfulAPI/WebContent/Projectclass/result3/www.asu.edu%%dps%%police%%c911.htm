When to Make a 9-1-1 Call

What is 9-1-1?

9-1-1 is the number to dial for the fastest possible emergency response when you need Police, Fire, or Medical Assistance in a life or death situation.
 

When you make a 9-1-1 call:

  • Stay calm
  • Give your name
  • Give location of incident
  • Answer all pertinent questions that the 9-1-1 operator will ask
  • You will be asked to describe all suspects (weight, height, age, hair, eye color, and clothing description)
  • If a vehicle is involved, get a license plate number, color, and make of the vehicle, and direction of travel
PLEASE STAY ON THE LINE UNTIL YOU ARE TOLD TO HANG UP.
 

Remember to call 9-1-1 only to:

  • Save a life
  • Report a crime in progress
  • Report a fire
  • Prevent a crime
