Vegetation Mapping of the Pinaleno Mountains

ABSTRACT

The intent of this study is to determine the accuracy of a piece of the Arizona GAP Vegetation Map. The study area is located on the Pinaleno Mountains within Coronado National Forest. There are eight distinct vegetation types within the study area. The GAP map is currently an ArcView GIS shapefile and an area of interest (AOI) has been created for the study area. Random points (UTM) were generated and inserted into ArcView to ensure they were in the AOI. At each point location, an observation is made as to the vegetation type. The points on the ground will be located using a Garmin III GPS. The data collected in the field will be compared to the existing Arizona GAP Vegetation Map to determine the overall accuracy of the study area.
