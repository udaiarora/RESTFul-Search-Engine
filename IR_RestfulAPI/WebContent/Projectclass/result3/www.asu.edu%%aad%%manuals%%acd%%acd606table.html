Faculty member or academic professional:

  1. obtains a list of approved companies from Human Resources (HR)

  2. contacts an agent to arrange an annuity contract

    and

  3. executes a salary reduction agreement.

The agent sends the salary reduction agreement to HR.

HR:

  1. reviews the salary reduction agreement and arranges for deduction of premiums from the faculty member’s or academic professional’s salary.

    and

  2. transmits the individual’s premiums to the appropriate company.
