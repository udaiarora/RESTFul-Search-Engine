Technical Services Department

 

The Technical Services Department is responsible for providing the whole range of technical services to the Arizona State University Libraries Tempe campus: acquiring library materials in all formats and languages, paying for materials, cataloging monographic and serial materials and resources, preparing materials for binding, and physically processing materials.

 

Technical Services consists of 9 units: Accounting, Bibliographic Searching/Ordering, Monographic Receiving/Copy Cataloging, Catalog Management/Authority Control, Original Cataloging, Non Roman Acquisitions and Cataloging, Monographic Processing, Serial Holdings Maintenance and Serials Receiving. The department is headed by a management team comprised of three Academic Professionals, Betsy Redman, Ronda Ridenour, and Becky Uhl.
