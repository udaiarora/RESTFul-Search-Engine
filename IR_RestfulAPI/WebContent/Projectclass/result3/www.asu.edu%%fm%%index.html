If you have an emergency or require immediate assistance, please call our Service Center at (480) 965-3633. This number is monitored 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to serve your needs.

To request information or non-emergency services, please send written requests through e-mail (FACMAN) or use our online Service Request Form.
