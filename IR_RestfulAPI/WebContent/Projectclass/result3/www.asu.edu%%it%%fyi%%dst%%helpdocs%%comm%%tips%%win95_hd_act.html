How to Stop Windows95 Machine's HD Activity?

Question:
I have a win95 machine that periodically has minutes of hard drive activity without apparent cause what causes this activity? i'll have the machine on for the day, be on a different machine and all of a sudden i hear the hard drive running. The machine is ethernet connected i recently heard that others are having similar problems with hard drive activity...sometimes causing delays in user control until the hard drive activity stops.

Response:
Office 9X's Fast Find indexing will do this several times a day, unless you turn it off or reschedule it. We just remove it from the startup folder if it annoys a given user too much.
