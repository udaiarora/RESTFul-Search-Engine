Disabled Parking

Parking and Transit Services is committed to the disabled community and works closely with the Disability Resources Office to ensure equal access and compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act.

State Authorized Permanent Disabled Parking:

Individuals with a permanent disability, who show a valid Permanent Disabled State Placard or disabled vehicle registration issued in the applicant's name, may purchase an ASU Disabled decal. The placard or vehicle registration must be presented along with photo identification at the time of the decal purchase.

State Authorized Temporary Disabled Parking:

Individuals with a temporary disability, who show a valid Temporary State Disabled Placard issued in the applicant's name, will be sold an ASU Disabled decal OR ASU Disabled Placard depending on the expiration date of the State Authorized Temporary Disabled Parking Placard. Faculty/staff/students must have a current ASU Tempe Red parking decal or pay the cost difference between their decal and an ASU Tempe Red decal. Photo identification must be shown at the time of purchase. Individuals receiving an ASU Disabled Placard must pay a $25 refundable deposit to receive a gate access card allowing them admission into the parking structures.

Location Options:

  • Surface Lots 3, 11, 17, 21, 39, 41, 46, 49, 58 and 59
  • Parking Structures 1 (decal side), 2, 3, 5, 6, 7 and ASU Fulton Center

Physician Authorized Temporary Disabled Parking:

Faculty/staff/students must have a current ASU Access A, Striped, or Orange parking decal. Photo identification must be shown at the time of purchase. A medical doctor or Student Health must provide the following information on either Doctor's office letterhead or a prescription form:

  1. Patient's name
  2. Nature of disability
  3. Length of disability (not to exceed 6 weeks); and
  4. Specifically state that disabled parking is required (the term "closer parking" is not acceptable).

Location Options:

  • Disabled stalls in current decal location
  • Lot 59- Disability Resources for Students will provide cart service upon request (480) 965-3055

General Information

  • Visitors with disabled plates or State Placard may park at disabled meters or visitor lots and pay applicable fees.
  • When displaying a disabled decal or placard, you must park in properly marked disabled stalls only.
  • Your disabled decal or placard is for the exclusive use of the disabled driver. It will be revoked if used by a non-disabled person.
  • The number of disabled stalls on campus exceeds the number required by law.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact Jim Hemauer, Disability Resources for Students, at (480) 965-1362 or Parking and Transit Services, at (480) 965-6124.
