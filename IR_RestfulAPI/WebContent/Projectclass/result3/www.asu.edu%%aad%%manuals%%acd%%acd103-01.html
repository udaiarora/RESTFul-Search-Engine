ACD 103–01: Arizona Board of Regents—Introduction

Purpose

To explain the governing structure of the university

Source

Arizona Revised Statutes §§ 15–1621, –1625

Policy

Membership and Authority

The Arizona Board of Regents consists of eleven members. Eight citizens are appointed by the governor of the state for terms of eight years, and one student member is appointed each year for a one-year term. The governor and state superintendent of public instruction are ex officio members. This board, acting under the constitution and laws of the State of Arizona, is the governing body of the university.
