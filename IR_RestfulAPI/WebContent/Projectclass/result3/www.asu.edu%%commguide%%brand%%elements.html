The ASU Brand: Elements of the Brand

The five elements that comprise a successful brand identity include:  

  • Logo and standards

  • Color Palette

  • Typefaces or typography

  • Language

  • Imagery

Some of these elements are already in place at ASU; others need to be fully implemented in all of our communications.   The more consistently we apply these elements to our web pages, print pieces and PowerPoint presentations, the stronger the ASU brand will be identified by our audiences.

The logo, standards, color palette, and typography are described in detail in the Graphic Elements section of the Communications Guide.
