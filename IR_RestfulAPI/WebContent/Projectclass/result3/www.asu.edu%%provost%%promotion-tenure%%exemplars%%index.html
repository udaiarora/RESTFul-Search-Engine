Promotion and Tenure Exemplars

Exemplars are teacher/scholars whose work represents the ideals of the New American University. The achievements of these rising stars demonstrate both tremendous talent and hard work.

Each year, following review of all tenure and promotion cases university-wide, four or five faculty are selected to represent their cohort as exemplars. We are pleased to recognize these exceptional colleagues, and to share their vitae and personal statements.
