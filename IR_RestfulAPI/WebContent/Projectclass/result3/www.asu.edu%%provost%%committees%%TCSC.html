TEMPE CAMPUS STANDARDS COMMITTEE

The committee is advisory to the executive vice president and university provost regarding undergraduate student petitions that concern university-wide academic requirements. The committee is appointed by and responsible to the executive vice president and university provost. (Two-year terms.)
