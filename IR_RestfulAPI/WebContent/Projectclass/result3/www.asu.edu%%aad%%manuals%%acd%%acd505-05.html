ACD 505–05: Display Advertisements for Job Vacancies

Purpose

To bring consistency to display advertisements for job vacancies

Source

Office of the President

Applicability

All hiring officials

Policy

All display advertisements for ASU job vacancies will be drawn up to fit a specified format and to include an approved ASU logo. The standardized format and logo will demonstrate to the job seeker a consistent and professional image of the university. The department head will be responsible for all advertisements placed for jobs within his or her area.

Procedure

To place advertisements with this format and logo in the Arizona Republic, the department head or other campus manager will place the ad through Human Resources.

To place such advertisements in any other publication, the academic department head or other academic campus manager should access the Ad Placement Form on the Human Resources Web site.
