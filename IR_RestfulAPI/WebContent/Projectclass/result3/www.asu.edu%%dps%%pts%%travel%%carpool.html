Is Driving Your Car
a Waste of $$$?

Finding alternative ways to get to work is not only good for the air, it is good for you! Carpooling cuts down on congestion and pollution. Carpool to start saving now. If you reduce the miles put on your car, you will have fewer oil changes and you won't have to replace brakes and tires as often. YOU SAVE MONEY! You could be eligible for discounted car insurance, too! (Check with your insurance agent.)

If you are interested in carpooling but do not know anyone in your area, sign up for a free account with our very own ASU Rideshare Service. It's fast, easy and the best way to get in touch with other ASU employees and students that are looking to carpool. If you have any questions call (480) 965-1072.

ShareTheRide.com is aonther great carpool match site! ShareTheRide.com is a free ridematching service that provides you an easy way to find others in the Valley who are interested in sharing the ride to work in a carpool or vanpool. Or you can find somebody to bike with in to work. In a few quick and easy steps, you can register yourself in this secure, private carpool match database.

If you already have an ASU parking decal for Parking Structure 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, Lot 3 (Gammage) and Lot 59, then you have an added incentive to carpool; preferred carpool parking spaces! For more information to register for the carpool parking spaces in these lots, call (480) 965-1072 or e-mail ASU Commuter Options Program with your questions or comments.
