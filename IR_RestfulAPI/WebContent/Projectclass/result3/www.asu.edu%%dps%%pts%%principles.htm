Parking and Transit Services Principles

  1. PTS must provide customer-focused, world-class services and be fully integrated with all aspects of ASU planning and operations for ASU to be world-class.

  2. PTS must collaborate with surrounding communities for integrated programs that reflect our changing community and increased urbanizations.

  3. PTS is an auxiliary enterprise that shall fund its own expenses, including parking structure debt service.

  4. PTS are purchased commodities and not student, employee or department rights or benefits.

  5. PTS will not receive subsidies nor subsidize other programs.

  6. PTS shall be included in all long-range campus master and strategic planning.

  7. Departments that sponsor events/conference will pay the actual direct and indirect costs associated with requested parking services as determined by PTS.
