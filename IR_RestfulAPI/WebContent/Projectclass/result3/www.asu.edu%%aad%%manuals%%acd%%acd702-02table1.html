ACD 702–02 Table 1

Faculty and academic professionals employed 100 percent time (40 hours per week) accrue 4 hours per pay period, or 9 days per academic year and 12 days per fiscal year.

Faculty and academic professionals employed 50 percent time (20 hours per week) accrue 2 hours per pay period, or 4.5 days per academic year and 6 days per fiscal year.
