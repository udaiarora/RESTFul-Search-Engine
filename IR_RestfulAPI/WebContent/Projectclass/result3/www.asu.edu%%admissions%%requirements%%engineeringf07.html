Freshman Admission Requirements
 

Professional Program Status

•  SAT 1400 Reasoning or ACT 32

•  and no math or science competency deficiencies

•  and if you have taken college courses prior to high school graduation, you must have a 3.5 transfer GPA
 

Pre-professional Program Status

•  no math or science competency deficiencies

•  and 1140 SAT Reasoning or 24 ACT score or 3.0 ABOR (competency courses) GPA or top 25% of high school class

•  and if you have taken college courses prior to high school graduation, you must have a 3.0 transfer GPA

To become admitted to the Professional Program from the Pre-professional program, freshman applicants must meet performance levels in a set of required skill courses as set by each department in the school.

 

See also the ASU General Catalog.

Transfer Admission Requirements
 

Pre-professional Program Status

Students with fewer than 24 transferable college credit hours must have:

•  no math or science competency deficiencies

•  and 1140 SAT Reasoning or 24 ACT score or 3.0 ABOR (competency courses) GPA or top 25% of high school class

•  and 3.0 transfer GPA

Students with 24 or more transferable college credit hours must have:

•  a 3.0 cumulative transfer GPA

•  and no math or science competency deficiencies

Note: Transfer students who receive an AGEC, Associates, or higher degree, are exempt from the math/science competency requirement.

To become admitted to the Professional Program from the Pre-professional program, transfer applicants must meet performance levels in a set of required skill courses as set by each department in the school.

Transfer applicants will not be admitted directly into the Professional Program.
 

See also the ASU General Catalog.
