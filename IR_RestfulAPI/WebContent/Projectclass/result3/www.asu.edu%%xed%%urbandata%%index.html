The primary purpose of the Urban Data Center is to serve as a resource for analysis and implementation of public policy in metropolitan Phoenix. ASU at the Downtown Center works closely with ASU researchers and organizations such as the Morrison Institute for Public Policy, ASU Libraries, the Joint Urban Design program, local governments, state agencies, and other independent local and national organizations to develop and promote policy relevant urban studies and build comprehensive data bases on policy issues for scholars, residents, urban planners, and community leaders.
