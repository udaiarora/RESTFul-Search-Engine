Activating an ASURITE Account

Activating your ASURITE UserID is a self-service process. You can activate your account by visiting the ASURITE Activation and Service Management website. The Web site also allows you to add services to your account. Incoming students will receive an activation code from the Admissions Office which will allow them to activate an ASURITE UserID from off campus.

There are also special terminals set aside just for activating an ASURITE UserID and adding new services. The process takes about fifteen minutes. If you have problems finding the terminals, or activating your ASURITE UserID, the staff at the IT Information Desk or in the computing site will be happy to assist.

The ASURITE Activation Computers are publicly available at the following locations:

Tempe Campus
Computing Commons, First Floor Lobby
(between the Computer Store and Classroom Support)
Polytechnic Campus
Computing Commons, Rm 151
West Campus
Fletcher Library Technopolis Computing Center

Computers in any Computing Site on campus may also be used to activate an account.

The ASURITE Activation system will be down for maintenance every weekend beginning Saturday at 8:00 p.m. and ending Sunday at 8:00 a.m..
