What is ASU Forensics? College forensics is competitive speech and debate. As a team,  we travel to universities and colleges around the country competing against other schools for recognition and trophies. This academic sport is a great way to use the critical thinking skills acquired here at ASU.

ASU Forensics is now in its 110th year, and we here at ASU are proud to be part of such a longstanding tradition of excellence.
