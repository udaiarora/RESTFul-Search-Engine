If the crime has just occurred, the victim should dial 911 for an emergency police response and provide information about the suspect, including a physical profile, clothing description, the direction and method of travel, and the type of property stolen.

If the crime occurred previously and an emergency police response is not necessary, the victim should call 480/965–3456 to report the crime.

The ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) Communications Center dispatches an officer to complete a crime report.

The victim:

  1. checks with coworkers or roommates to determine whether suspicious individuals were observed in the area

  2. should have information about the suspect ready, along with an inventory of the stolen property, including any identifying numbers, marks, or characteristics

  3. supplies the title or license plate numbers if a vehicle has been stolen

    and

  4. should try not to disturb the crime scene.

The Police Services Division completes a crime report and conducts a preliminary investigation. The victim notifies the insurance company, banks, and credit card companies of the loss, if applicable.
