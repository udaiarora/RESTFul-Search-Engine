What to do when experiencing problems dialing-in?

Q. Why is it that I often have to dial in several times (3+) with my modem in order to get an active connection so that I can login?

If you are having trouble with the lines - any trouble - dropped connection, not getting a connection - please report it to the helpdesk (965-6500) - sometimes a modem goes bad - or other problems occur and that we are not aware of until someone reports it.
