Web & Application Development Group

 

The Web & Application Development Group supports approximately 400 custom applications and programs used by University Administration and Business and Finance Departments.

This group supports all Web development and site maintenance for University Administration and Business and Finance units, including:

- Auxiliary sales sites
- Monthly student statements for Sun Card usage
- Survey applications
- Interactive, database-driven Web forms
- Custom reports available online
- Facilities building programs

The group also supplies priority project management and development for technical projects such as requested by the units.
