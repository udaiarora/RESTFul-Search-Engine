Welcome to the International Student Office

The International Student Office (ISO) supports student success by engaging students to responsibly maintain their compliance to visa regulations and by providing programming and advisement that enrich their educational experience. 

ISO programs and services include:

Immigration matters
SEVIS reporting compliance

  • Visa and document administration required for F-1 and J-1 degree-seeking students and their dependents

ISO student success and community programming

  • Cultural immersion workshops
  • Fall and Spring New Student Orientation
  • International Friends Program
  • ISO Sun Devil Experience
  • Optional Practical Training (OPT) and Curricular Practical Training (CPT) Workshops
  • World Festival

ISO consultation

  • University administrative policies and procedures for international students
  • Liaison with administrative and academic units, sponsors and governmental agencies
  • Information, referral, and resource center
