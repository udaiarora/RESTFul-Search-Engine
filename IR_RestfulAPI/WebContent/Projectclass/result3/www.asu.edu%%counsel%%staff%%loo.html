Lisa S. Loo is Associate General Counsel at Arizona State University where she focuses on commercial transactions, procurement, real estate and immigration matters. Prior to joining Arizona State University in 1993, Lisa was in private practice for eight years. She is a founding board member of the Arizona Asian American Bar Association. The State Bar of Arizona recognized Lisa as the 1993 Outstanding Young Lawyer. She obtained her law degree from the University of Virginia in 1985 and her undergraduate degree from Fordham University in 1982. Lisa also serves as a judge pro tem for the City of Phoenix.
