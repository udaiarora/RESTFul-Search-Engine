BASIC COMMANDS

While logged in to voice mail, use the following commands to move through the system:

Help *4 or *H
Restart at Activity Menu *7 or *R
Wait *9 or *W
Transfer to an extension *8 or *T
Look up name/extension *6 or **N
Exit system **9 or **X
Transfer call to an operator *0
Delete *3 or *D
Undelete **8 or **U
Hold message in category **4 or **H
Relog in **7 or **R
Skip call answer greeting 1

Use while addressing:

Alternate addressing
(switch between name/ext.)		 *2 or *A
Use mailing list *5 or *L
