Co-Sponsored Events

The IRC partners with a number of campus and community units and organizations to create unique, innovative and engaging learning opportunities related to social justice, diversity and intergroup relations.

We are especially interested in co-sponsoring on-campus events that are open to all members of the ASU community at minimal or no cost. The Intergroup Relations Center offers publicity through IRC (web, e-mail, and events), limited funding, and event facilitation. For co-sponsor consideration please contact us.

This academic year we are co-sponsoring the following events:

Knowledge that Matters Conference
The Women and Gender Studies Program and the FEMMSS (the network of feminists involved in establishing a new epistemology, metaphysics, and science studies)
February 8-10th at the Memorial Union.
