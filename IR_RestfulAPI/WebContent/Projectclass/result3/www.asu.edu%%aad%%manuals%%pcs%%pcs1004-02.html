PCS 1004–02: Relocation of Research Equipment to Other Universities

Purpose

To ensure the successful transfer of sponsor-funded equipment to other entities or universities

Sources

Federal Acquisition Regulation § 845.6
Arizona Constitution Article IX, § 7
Property Control

Applicability

Faculty or principal investigators transferring equipment

Policy

Faculty or principal investigators desiring to relocate their research and property to another university may do so at no charge to the acquiring university except for shipping or freight. Faculty or staff wishing to purchase equipment for personal use must purchase the equipment from Surplus Property and Recycling (see PCS 1002–06, “University Employee Purchases”). Sponsor-funded equipment that has been cost shared with state or local funds may be subject to reimbursement for the state or local participation (see PCS 1004–01, “State/Local Funded Equipment”).

Procedure D

Responsibility
Action
Faculty or principal investigator (PI)
  1. Prepare Property Relocation Authorization Form (PRA) listing property to be transferred and forward to the sponsored property coordinator.
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Review PRA for title and for state and local participation.
If state and/or local participation:
  1. See PCS 1004–01, “State/Local Funded Equipment.”
If no state and/or local participation:
  1. Approve PRA form and forward original to faculty or principal investigator.
Faculty or PI
  1. Forward PRA to department chair or dean of college for approval.
Chair/Dean
  1. Approve or disapprove the PRA.
If approved:
  1. Contact the sponsored property coordinator to remove ASU identification from equipment.
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Remove ASU identification from equipment prior to equipment departure date.
  1. Remove the equipment from the property control system.
