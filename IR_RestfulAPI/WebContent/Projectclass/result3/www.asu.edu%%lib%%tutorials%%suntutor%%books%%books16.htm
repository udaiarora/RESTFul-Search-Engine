How to find books

Understanding your results

Saving your results

You are able to print, e-mail, or download your results from the catalog.

Printing

You can use the print function of your browser to print the catalog information screens of the books you are interested in. However, if you have a long list of titles you may want to e-mail or download the results.

E-mailing or Downloading

There are two ways to save the catalog information for the books you have found. From the catalog information screen for a particular book, you can click on the "Save for Export" button.

Or, if you have a list of titles that you have found in your search, you can select the titles you'd like to save by clicking in the box to "mark" the title. You then need to click on the "Save Marked Records" button before moving to the next page of your search results.

Once you have selected all the titles you'd like to save, click on the "View Saved " button.


To e-mail the results, select "E-mail" and type in your address in the "Mail To" box. To save to a disk, select "Local Disk" option. In addition, you can use the "Screen" option to print out the entire list of results. After any of these selections, you then click on "Submit".
