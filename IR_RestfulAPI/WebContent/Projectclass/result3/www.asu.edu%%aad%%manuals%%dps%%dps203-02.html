DPS 203–02: Reporting Property Crimes

Purpose

To provide guidelines for reporting property crimes on university property

Source

University policy

Policy

The Police Services Division (PSD) responds to every call received concerning a property crime on university property.

Reporting Property Crime Emergencies

If the crime has occurred within moments and information is available about a suspect, call 911 (ASU Department of Public Safety [ASU DPS] emergency number) immediately. PSD will begin an immediate and thorough search for the suspect and the stolen property.

Reporting Nonemergency Property Crimes

If an emergency response by the police is not required, call 480/965–3456 to report the crime and ask an ASU DPS officer to respond.

Note:The victim of a property crime will be asked to assist ASU DPS officers in apprehending the offender and recovering stolen property. Specifically, the victim will be asked to aid in the prosecution of an apprehended suspect. This means the victim must be willing, if necessary, to go to trial and testify. Should a victim not be willing to aid in prosecution, PSD still completes a crime report.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
If the crime has just occurred:
Victim
  1. Dial 911 for an emergency police response.

  2. Provide information about the suspect, including: a physical profile, clothing description, the direction and method of travel, and the type of property stolen.
If the crime occurred previously and an emergency police response is not necessary:
  1. Call 480/965–3456 to report the crime.
ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) Communications Center
  1. Dispatch an officer to complete a crime report.
Victim
  1. Check with coworkers or roommates to determine whether suspicious individuals were observed in the area.

  2. Have information about the suspect ready, along with an inventory of the stolen property, including any identifying numbers, marks, or characteristics: if a vehicle has been stolen, supply the title or license plate numbers. Try not to disturb the crime scene.
Police Services Division
  1. Complete a crime report and conduct a preliminary investigation.
Victim
  1. Notify the insurance company, banks, and credit card companies of the loss, if applicable.
endtable
