MLK Day of Service
Saturday, January 20, 2007
Time 8:00am-1:00pm

As a part of the larger University MLK celebration, students will be going UMOM New Day Centers to serve residents there and do some general clean-up. Breakfast, lunch, and transportation will be provided. UMOM New Day Centers is a homeless shelter for families. Please visit http://www.asu.edu/mlk for information on additional ASU events celebrating Dr. King.

Register for the MLK Day of Service. For more information, please contact the Community Service Program
