Leadership Breakfast Series

The Leadership Breakfast Series provides a dynamic forum for the open exchange of ideas. Each breakfast attracts a diverse group of leaders from the university and the community. Breakfasts are held throughout the year and are occasionally held in conjunction with an established college lecture series.

 


Relation to Design Imperatives

Design Imperative #3: ASU as Knowledge Entrepreneur
A Culture of Academic Enterprise

Breakfast speakers are nationally-known individuals with cutting-edge ideas. As a forum for knowledge sharing, the Leadership Breakfast Series can propagate new knowledge and new partnerships within and amongst the university and the community.

Design Imperative #6: Intellectual Fusion
Interdisciplinary/Multidisciplinary/Transdisciplinary/Postdisciplinary

Due to the dynamic participant list, university and community members who may not know each other are brought together around important issues, fusing disciplines and sectors.
