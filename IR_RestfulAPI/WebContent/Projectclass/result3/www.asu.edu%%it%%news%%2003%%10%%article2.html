IT Announces Staff Changes

Dr. Lewis has announced organizational changes in the IT department. The realignments are being made as a result of retiring staff, with groups being consolidated and some staff receiving new assignments. The changes include the following:

  • Telecommunication Infrastructure Design and Expansion, headed by Craig Trimble, now reports directly to Lewis. The group is responsible for information technology and planning on construction starts.
  • Data Communications Operations is now part of Instruction, Research and Infrastructure Support, and its staff reports to Assistant Vice Provost for Information Technology John Babb.
  • Telephone Services is now part of Computing Technology Centers, and its staff reports to Assistant Vice Provost for Information Technology Bob Nelson.
  • Joe Askins has been named Director of IT Business Continuity and Security Planning. His assignments will include representing IT in an inter-departmental planning group for business continuity.
  • Assistant Vice Provost for Information Technology Darel Eschbach of Telecommunications Services is retiring June 30, 2004.

The Information Technology department organizational chart is online as part of the IT department Web pages, at http://www.asu.edu/it/org/
