Wuhan

In the spirit of academic collaboration, ASU faculty is currently working in developing an accelerated BS/MSE educational program with Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Chinese students who elect to participate in this program will complete three years at HUST and then study at ASU in order to complete technical electives and senior design. Upon receipt of their  HUST BS degree, these students will be given the opportunity to spend an additional year at ASU working toward an MSE degree from ASU. This program will likely be launched in 2006.

ASU faculty and HUST are also currently in the midst of an exciting project on neural rehabilitation.
