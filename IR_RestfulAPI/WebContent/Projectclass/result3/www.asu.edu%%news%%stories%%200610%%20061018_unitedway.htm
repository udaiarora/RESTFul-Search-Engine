October 18, 2006

Campus community boosts United Way campaign

ASU's United Way campaign officially opened on Sept. 15, with a goal of $662,670.

As of Oct. 11, the campus community is more than halfway there, with $435,309 pledged so far, says Deanna Kilgore, ASU's United Way Loaned Executive.

More than 1,500 ASU employees sent their pledge cards back in time for the ?early bird? drawing, and the lucky winner, Barbara Anderson of University Administration, won a round-trip ticket on US Airways.

?The ASU community has shown great support for this year's United Way campaign,? Kilgore says. ?This year, our approach is simply educating people about the critical needs in our community and providing people with the opportunity to give back if they so choose.

?I am continually impressed by the generosity of the ASU faculty and staff, and am happy to report ASU has reached 66 percent of its goal well before our target date of Oct. 13.

?This success would not be possible without the hard work of the wonderful campaign managers and unit reps for organizing fun activities and information sessions around the Valley of the Sun United Way community-wide Results That Matter fund.?

Though the campaign ?officially? ends Oct. 13, United Way still will accept pledges, and they can be made online.

To give online, on ASU's secure pledging site, follow these steps:

? Go to the Web site (www.asu.edu/unitedway).

? Click on ?Donate Online!? on the left-hand menu

? Click on the link in No. 1 that says ? United Way online donor site.?

? Enter your ?Username: Your ID #? (no dashes).

? Enter the following password: Type ?ASU? and your ID# (no dashes). Example: ?ASU993123456.?

? Click ?Log In.?

Once you arrive at the home page, browse around the site for useful and meaningful information.

When you're ready to pledge, click on ?Pledge Now? to make your donation.
