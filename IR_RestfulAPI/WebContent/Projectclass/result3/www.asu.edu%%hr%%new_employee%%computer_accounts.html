Human Resources - New Employee

ASURITE Accounts & Services (Computer Accounts)

The University Technology Office (UTO) Help Desk is located in the Computing Commons Building, room 202. Call (480)-965-6500 with questions.  Walk-in Hours: Monday through Friday, 8:00 am to 6:00 pm.  Telephone/E-mail Hours: Everyday, 6:30 a.m. to 12:30 a.m.

GETTING STARTED

The University Technology Office provides a wide variety of computer access to the ASU community. These services are available to all students, faculty, and classified staff who need to use the computing systems for academic or administrative purposes.  To use these services you will first need to activate your ASURITE UserID and create a password (step 1); then you may add services as needed (steps 2 and 3).

STEP 1. ACTIVATE YOUR ASURITE USERID

ASURITE UserIDs may be activated at http://www.asu.edu/asurite/ from any on-campus computer. If you need to activate the account from an off-campus computer, you may contact the UTO Help Desk at 480-965-6500 to obtain an activation code. You may also activate the account without an activation code by using a dedicated terminal at one of the following locations:

Tempe campus - Computing Commons - First Floor Lobby

Downtown Phoenix campus – University Center – First Floor Lobby Polytechnic campus - Computing Commons - Room 151

West campus - Fletcher Library Technopolis Computing Center

The process takes about 15 minutes. If you have problems finding the terminals or activating your ASURITE UserID, the staff at each site will be happy to assist you. Please memorize your password and the answers to your Identity Verification questions.  Do NOT write them down.  Once you have activated your ASURITE UserID you may add ASURITE services to the account (step 2).

STEP 2. REQUEST ACCESS TO EXCHANGE/OUTLOOK E-MAIL Once you have completed the basic activation process through http://www.asu.edu/asurite/, click on the Subscribe to More Services button.  Check the box for ASU Main Campus Exchange (Outlook E-mail) and click on Subscribe.  This service is for ASU Tempe, ASU Downtown Phoenix, and ASU Polytechnic employees only. ASU West employees can view http://www.west.asu.edu/it/start/facstaffAccts.htm for information about Exchange/Outlook e-mail on the West Campus.  See your department computer support personnel for assistance configuring Outlook to access the mailbox once you have completed this process. 

You may also need to request services which are not available through self-subscription (step 3). 

STEP 3. SUBMIT A REQUEST FOR COMPUTER ACCESS FORM (IF NEEDED)

In addition to Outlook you may need access to Administrative systems such as SIS, Advantage, HRMS and DARS. If you need to obtain access to these services, complete and submit the attached Request for Computer Access form.   The form is also available at http://www.asu.edu/helpdesk/. Meet with your supervisor or manager to decide the specific access you will need.  Complete the request form, and obtain supervisor/manager signature authorization.  Submit the form to the Tempe Campus UTO Help Desk located in the Computing Commons Building,  room 202. You may also FAX the completed Request for Computer Access form to (480) 965-5171 or mail it to PO Box 870101, Tempe AZ 85287.  Sometimes additional signatures are needed for you to gain access to certain data. The UTO Help Desk will route your form for the appropriate signatures. Depending on the number of services requested and the number of signatures needed, it can take from 3 days to 3 weeks to process your request.  The UTO Help Desk will contact you by e-mail when the request has been completed.
