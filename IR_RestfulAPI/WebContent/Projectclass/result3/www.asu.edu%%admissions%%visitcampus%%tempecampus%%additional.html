Carson Student-Athlete Center Tours

Tour the 165,000-square-foot Carson Student-Athlete Center, located at the south end of Sun Devil Stadium. The Carson Student-Athlete Center houses academic and athletic training facilities for ASU's 22 sports and 550 student athletes.

While ICA tours are not offered in the summer, for more information about ICA tours, or to schedule a special tour for a school group, please contact Kim Edwards at 480.965.3482.

Tours are offered on Mondays and Fridays during the spring and fall semesters only (generally running from August 22nd to May 12th for each academic year).
