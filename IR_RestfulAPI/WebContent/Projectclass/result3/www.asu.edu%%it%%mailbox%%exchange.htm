Outlook/Exchange at ASU

A Microsoft product for messaging and collaboration, Outlook 2000, XP and 2003 combines support for Internet standards-based messaging systems, calendar, contact, and task-management features. Outlook is highly integrated with Microsoft Exchange Server and Internet Explorer.
What account will I need?
An account on the ASU Exchange server. You may obtain an Exchange account, if you are an employee, from the Computer Accounts Office (480-965-1211) or use the self-subscription service at http://asu.edu/asurite.

What software will I need?
Microsoft Outlook 2000, XP, or 2003. Outlook comes bundled with Microsoft Office. The version you have will depend on the version of Microsoft Office your department is using and supporting. Please contact your local support person for installation assistance. For home use, please go to the Customer Assistance Center (Computing Commons 202) and request the CD and installation instructions for home use.

How do I set up Outlook at work or at home?

Please see our Setting Up Outlook documents. If these documents don't answer your questions or you experience difficulties, please submit a question online at the Ask IT Web page or call the IT Help Desk.
