Book Donations to the Library


We welcome your donation of books and other library materials to the Arizona State University Libraries. We can accept those types of materials which support the curriculum and research needs of the University. Materials are particularly welcome which fall within the scope of the Libraries’ collection development policy and which are not already held within the Libraries’ collections.


Types of materials which can not usually be utilized by the University Libraries:
Materials which fall within the following categories generally can not be added to the University’s collections:

  • Textbooks
  • Popular magazines or periodicals
  • Scattered or single volumes of a multi-volume set
  • Material in poor condition
  • Pamphlets
  • Popular fiction, non-fiction, or trade books
  • Titles which the University Libraries already own
  • LP Sound Recordings
Since it is sometimes difficult to determine whether material falls into the categories above, please contact us about your collection in advance before delivering the materials.  Generally the library does not pick up materials which are being donated.  If your donation can not be utilized by the University Libraries, there are other organizations within the Phoenix area which may welcome your donation. These include:
  • VNSA Book Collection 602-265-6805 (a charity book sale with drop boxes and pickup available)
  • Your local public libraries including:
    • Apache Junction Public Library
    • Avondale Public Library
    • Chandler Public Library
    • Gilbert Public Library
    • Glendale Public Library
    • Mesa Public Library
    • Phoenix Public Library
    • Scottsdale Public Library
    • Tempe Public Library
  • Nursing homes and care centers
  • Homeless and other group shelters
  • Retirement homes
  • Prisons
How gifts are used within the University Libraries:
The University Libraries reserve the right to utilize donated materials in whatever way best benefits the collections and services. Gifts may be either added to the collection, donated to another institution, or sold with the proceeds to benefit the University Libraries. Gifts donated with restrictions or special conditions are accepted on a case by case basis, with a view towards making the material available to researchers within the parameters of the restrictions.

Appraisals:
The University Libraries and library staff are prohibited by law from appraising gifts for tax deduction purposes. The local telephone directory lists appraisers; also, the University Libraries can provide a list of appraisers in the Phoenix area.

Contact for Donations:
If you are interested in making a donation to the Arizona State University Libraries, please contact Debra Rose at  480-965-7039.
