Mission

The ASU Adult Re-Entry Program seeks to assist and support students age 25 and older who have been out of the educational system for a significant period of time.  We know that Adult Re-Entry students have similar needs and share many of the same concerns when returning to education.  We seek to meet those needs and address those concerns through our services.
