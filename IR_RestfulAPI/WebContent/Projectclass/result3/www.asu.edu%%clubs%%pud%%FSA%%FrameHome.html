Purpose of FSA

 

            Motivating students to complete mission possible, COLLEGE, Future Scholars of Arizona is a conference specifically set up to meet the needs of Phoenix Union High School District students (grades 9-12). Held on the ASU Main Campus and free to students, each individual takes part in a series of informational and fun workshops to discuss what they need to know about getting in and surviving college, hosted by current college students. Whether it?s finding information on admissions to out-of-state colleges, community college transfers, or how to fund a college education, our Future Scholars of Arizona conference strives to help all PUHSD students realize, yes, they can earn a college degree.

 

Who can participate:  Any high school student.
