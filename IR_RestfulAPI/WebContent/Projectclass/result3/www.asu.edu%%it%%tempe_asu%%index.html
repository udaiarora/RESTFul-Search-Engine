Downtown Tempe Public Wireless Service

Mission

Arizona State University and the City of Tempe have joined forces to provide the Downtown Tempe area with free Web Access. This community outreach project showcases the cooperation between the City of Tempe and ASU. This access provides free Internet access (http and https access only) for anyone in the coverage area.

Getting Started

After configuration of the SSID for the wireless network, open a web page to login. ASU users can use their ASURITE UserID and password to gain access, all other users can use the guest button.

Requirements

Any compatible 802.11b or 802.11g wireless card is required to use the wireless service.

SSID

The ssid of the network is asu_tempe.
