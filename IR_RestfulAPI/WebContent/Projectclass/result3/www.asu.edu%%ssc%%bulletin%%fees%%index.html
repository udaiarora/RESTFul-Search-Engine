FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FEE
All students are assessed a per session Financial Assistance Trust fee. The fee is $12 per session for Tempe and Downtown Phoenix campus students and $11 per session for West and Polytechnic campus students. This fee is matched by the State of Arizona. Student grants are awarded from this trust, under the usual financial assistance eligibility criteria.

STUDENT RECREATION COMPLEX FEE
All ASU Main summer students are assessed a $12 per session Student Recreation Complex Fee as approved by a 1985 student referendum. This fee permits full use of the Complex's many recreational opportunities during the Summer Sessions.

ARIZONA STUDENTS ASSOCIATION (ASA) FEE
The ASA is a non-profit lobbying organization that represents Arizona's public university students to the Arizona Board of Regents, State Legislature, and U.S. Congress. During 1996-1997, students at the state universities voted to change the mechanism for funding the ASA. A $1.00 fee will be charged to each student each session. Any refunds for this fee will be provided through the ASA Central Office at 1-877-294-6900.

LATE FEE
Beginning with the first day of each session, a $50 late fee is added.

FEE PAYMENT
By registrering for classes you agree to pay all applicable tuition and registration fees. Class registration will not be cancelled due to non-payment.

FEE PAYMENT DEADLINES
For registration processed on or before April 26, payment must be received by April 26 at 4:30 pm unless financial aid is awarded by the payment deadline date or you choose to enroll in the Tuition Installment Plan. A US Postal Service postmark of April 18 is suggested to meet the April 26 deadline. If tuition is not paid by the deadline, you will aautomatically be enrolled in the Tuition Installment Plan and charged a non-refundable per session $50.00 enrollment fee.

For registration processed after April 26, payment must be received the same business day by 4:30 pm. All fee payment deadlines are extended to 9:00 pm daily to students using ASU Interactive to pay by eCheck or credit card (no Visa accepted). If tuition is not paid by the deadline, or financial aid awarded by the deadline, you will automatically be enrolled in the Tuition Installment Plan and charged an non-refundable $50.00 enrollment fee.
