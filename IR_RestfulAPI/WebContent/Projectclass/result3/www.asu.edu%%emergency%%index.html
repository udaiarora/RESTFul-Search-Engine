ASU’s emergency preparedness procedures are continuously monitored, tested, and adjusted as needed. A critical part of any emergency plan is communication, and we will use various means of communication to get information to the ASU community as soon as possible. Following are several ways in which emergency information can be acquired:

  • Emergency Information Phone Line: 480-727-9911 or toll-free 877-278-2785 (877-ASU-ASU5) to get recorded information.
     
  • ASU Website: the Contact ASU page, www.asu.edu/contactasu, links directly to an emergency information website, www.asu.edu/emergency. It includes useful information about campus resources and emergency procedures and will be updated as needed with emergency information.
     
  • Emergency Alternate Website: the university has established an emergency alternate website, www.arizonauniversities.info, for use in the event our internal online resources are inaccessible in an emergency. Please refer to this web address if you are unable to access www.asu.edu.
     
  • Email: ASU will communicate emergency information and campus status updates via university email.
