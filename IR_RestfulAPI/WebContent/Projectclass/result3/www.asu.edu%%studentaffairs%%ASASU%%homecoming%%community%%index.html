Why Come to ASU Homecoming? Plenty of Reasons!

Arizona State University has been a major part of the greater Phoenix community since its founding in 1928 and currently with over 54,000 students there is no denying that ASU students, faculty, staff, and alumni are a large part of our community. The community has given so much to ASU and vice versa, and it is now that time of the year when ASU really opens up to the Tempe Community and invites them to celebrate with us our history and tradition â?? a history and tradition that they are very much a part of. Arizona State University urges all community members and their families to come and celebrate with us this October and see what ASU really has offer to offer the community.

Events for Families / Community Members

We have a week full of events and some that are aimed specifically at the community.

10/18 8pm-11pm AZ Ballroom - Night of a Thousand Laughs

The annual comedy night during Homecoming week features students who know how to make people laugh. Be wowed and entertained by comical skits of local comedians.

10/20 9am-2pm - ASU Classes Without Quizzes

Have you ever wished classes existed without quizzes? Well, join ASU's top professors as they present short lectures on their current studies at ASU. Whether you want to meet one of these top scholars, or could not get an override into their classes, this event will allow the ASU community and Alumni to experience their teaching.

10/20 8pm-9pm - Lantern Walk

TAs the oldest tradition at Arizona State, Lantern Walk is Homecoming's signature event, allowing students to retrace the steps of previous students up "A" Mountain in a ceremony celebrating ASU Pride. In the past, the ceremony has had surprises ranging from fireworks to marriage engagements!

10/21 10am-11am - Annual Homecoming Parade

The annual parade welcomes the ASU community to show their accomplishments to the community. This family event is great for parents and kids alike. Join Sparky, the ASU Cheerleaders, and the Marching Band as they get fans pumped up for the Game and Block Party. Location: University Drive from McAllister to Mill. Time: 10:00am - 11:00am.
Download the parade application (in .pdf format).

10/21 10am-12pm - Block Party

The biggest party of the year for the ASU community! Join students, alumni, and community members as they interact and reacquaint themselves with their Alma Mater. Current ASU students will also receive free food and drinks at the Block Party with a valid student ID!

10/21 12:30pm Kickoff - Sun Devil's Homecoming Football Game

Watch the Arizona State University Sun Devils Football team march on to victory!
