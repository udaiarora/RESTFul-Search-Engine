Where does the output give fewer indices in 5.1 than in 5.6?

Question:
When I run my EQS program in version 5.1, I receive fewer indices than in version 5.6. I am particularly interested in RMR or the Standardized RMR or the RMSEA. What might be the problem?

Response:
Since version 5.1 was developed before 5.6, these indices are not part of that version but were added in 5.6.
