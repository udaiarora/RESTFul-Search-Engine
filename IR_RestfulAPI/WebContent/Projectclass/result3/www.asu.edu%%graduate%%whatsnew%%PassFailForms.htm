New Protocol for Pass/Fail Forms
Monday, February 28, 2005

Currently, after a student has scheduled his/her oral defense of a thesis or dissertation with the Student Academic Services area of the Division of Graduate Studies, our staff prepares the pass/fail paperwork for the oral defense and sends the material via campus mail or courier.  The student’s committee chair, supervisory committee members, head of the academic unit and the dean of the college (for doctoral students only) receive paper copies of this material.

Student Academic Services initiated a pilot program in Fall 2003 to explore changing the delivery of pass/fail forms for oral defenses. Pass/fail forms were emailed to participating academic units as a PDF attachment. The pilot program generated a great deal of interest and positive feedback.

Based on the success of the pilot program, Student Academic Services will no longer be sending paper copies of pass/fail forms for oral defenses. Pass/fail forms will be sent via email with a PDF version of the pass/fail form attached. The PDF forms are official and will be accepted by the Division of Graduate Studies as originals.

Under the new email system, the procedure would be as follows:

  • Students should continue to schedule their oral defenses with Student Academic Services under existing policies and procedures.

  • Once the oral defense paperwork is produced, the committee chair, the supervisory committee members, the academic unit's designated graduate support staff member, and the dean’s office (if necessary) will receive an email with the pass/fail form included as a PDF attachment.

  • We will email the forms to the faculty and staff's ASU Outlook email address. FERPA guidelines require that this paperwork should only be sent to official (i.e., asu.edu) email addresses.

  • The head of the academic unit’s copy can be printed by the designated graduate support staff, if necessary.

  • The committee chair should print the form and bring it to the defense for the committee to sign. Only one form should be signed by the entire committee.

Please appoint a designee (e.g., graduate secretary or advisor) to receive a copy of each email and pass/fail form, if you have not already done so. The designee should send an email to grad-sas@asu.edu and confirm his/her email address and academic unit. We suggest notifying your faculty about the procedural change as well.

If you have questions, please don’t hesitate to contact Student Academic Services @ (480) 965-3521.
