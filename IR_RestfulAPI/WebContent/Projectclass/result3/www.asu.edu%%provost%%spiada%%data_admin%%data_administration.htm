Mission

Data Administration is responsible for developing policies, standards, definitions, and conventions governing the creation and use of enterprise administrative data. Enterprise administrative data are data needed by the university for reporting, management analysis, and essential operating activities.
Specifically, this office:

  • Identifies enterprise data required to support the information needs of ASU's management in collaboration with other offices.
  • Coordinates with other offices to ensure that administrative systems contain mission-critical administrative data.
  • Recommends, to senior executives and designated committees responsible for data management, policies controlling access to and management of data. (These policies ensure the security, privacy, and appropriate use of administrative data.)
  • Establishes definitions, standards, and naming conventions for enterprise administrative data. These conventions ensure the integrity, accuracy, and internal consistency of administrative data.)
  • Prepares and disseminates reference sources containing pertinent policies, definitions, and other information necessary to use administrative data.
  • Identifies official data for entry into the data warehouse and maintains the data warehouse. (Official data are the accepted reference points for management and analysis purposes.)
