The Jukebox

The Jukebox is the place where ASU Students, Faculty, and Staff can find out what they can do with the Web and Internet at ASU. This site offers Web news, solutions, and suggestions (some sections specifically tailored to the Web environment at ASU) to help everyone maximize their use of computers and the Internet.

Webpages @ ASU

If you are currently a student, faculty, or staff member at Arizona State University, you can publish your own webpages or manage a department's webpages. For information about a particular type of web space that ASU provides, select one from the list below.

  • Personal Sites
  • Instructional Sites
  • Departmental Sites

If you are seeking information on webspace for student organizations affiliated with ASU, please see the information supplied by SORC at http://www.asu.edu/clubs/sorc/webspace.htm.

If you are new to webpage design and would like to learn more, checkout our brief tutorial for Web Beginners.

Alternatively, if you would like detailed information about accessibility and usability guidlines, checkout the ASU Web Standards.
