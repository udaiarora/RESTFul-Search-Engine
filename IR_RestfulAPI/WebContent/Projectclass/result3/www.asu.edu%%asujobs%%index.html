Welcome and thank you for your interest in career opportunities with ARIZONA STATE UNIVERSITY!

EQUAL EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY/AFFIRMATIVE ACTION EMPLOYER

Before you apply:

We strongly recommend that you review the applicant tutorial. It will take approximately 10 minutes. Applicant Tutorial

You will be required to provide a valid email address. Much of the recruitment process will be done through email. You may obtain a free email account from www.gmail.com.

When applying for a position, you will be allowed to attach only ONE document.  Please combine any documents required (cover letter, resume, references and other relevant information) into ONE document to attach to your application. 

If you need assistance:


For Technical Assistance call the OASIS Helpdesk at: 480-965-6500.

For Application questions, please contact the ASU Human Resources Office at: 480-965-2701.

If you have difficulty accessing a computer, you can access a computer at your local community center, or library. The Office of Human Resources at the location below has a number of computer workstations available for public use.

New Users:

To search positions, click the Search Staff Postings link at the left.

You may view job openings without registering.

As a New User to the system, you must create a user name and a password. Once created, you may access your applications and apply for jobs at any time.

Returning Users:

Click Login, and enter your username and password.

This allows you to:

  • Edit your existing profile
  • Apply to new jobs
  • Review the status of positions you have applied to

Once you begin the application process it is recommended that you do not use your browser's "Back", "Forward", or "Refresh" buttons to navigate the site. Using the back or forward buttons will not work in this application and you may lose data that you have entered. Please use the navigational buttons within the site.
