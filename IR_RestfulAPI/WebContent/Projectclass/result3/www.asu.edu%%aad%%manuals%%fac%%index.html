The Department of Facilities Planning and Management is now Facilities Management. As such, this manual title, formerly the Department of Facilities Planning and Management Policies and Procedures Manual (FAC), has changed to the Facilities Management Policies and Procedures Manual (FAC), and policies have been updated accordingly.
