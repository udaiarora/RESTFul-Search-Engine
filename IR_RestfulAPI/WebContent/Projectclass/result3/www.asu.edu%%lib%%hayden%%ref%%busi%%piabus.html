BUSINESS
PERIODICAL INDEXES AND ABSTRACTS


    When you see this button next to an entry, click on it to go to the ASU Libraries Indexes page. You may also get to this page by clicking on INDEXES on any of our web pages, including our Home Page: http://www.asu.edu/lib/


  1. ABI/Inform Global Edition with Fulltext. Ann Arbor, MI: University Microfilms International, 1971- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1985- .)
      ONLINE ACCESS Click on Business

    Provides computer-searchable abstracts for business-related articles in over 1200 English-language scholarly journals and general business magazines worldwide. It can be searched by subject terms (descriptors), keywords, company name, geographic area, etc. The periodicals it covers makes it particularly well suited to finding information on business practices and management issues. About half the entries include the full text of the article.

  2. Business and Industry Database. Beachwood, OH: Responsive Database Services, 1994- . Daily. (ASU owns 1994- .)
      ONLINE ACCESS Click on Business

    Abstracts articles in over 1000 trade and business periodicals, newspapers, and newsletters from over 30 countries and includes the full text of over half. Indexing has a marketing orientation emphasizing facts, figures, and key events related to companies, industries, markets, and products. Can also be searched by enhanced SIC codes.

  3. Business Periodicals Index. New York: Wilson, 1958- . Monthly (except August). (ASU owns 1958- .)
      HF 5001 .B848x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10B)
      ONLINE ACCESS 1982- (Business Abstracts) Click on Business

    A basic index to more than 350 business periodicals with abstracts in the online version since 1992. This is mostly useful for researching older years that predate the other business indexes. For articles written from 1913 to 1957, use this index's previous title, Industrial Arts Index (T 1 .I52x SCIENCE REFERENCE).

  4. F & S Index. United States. Foster City, CA: Information Access Company, 1960- . Weekly. (ASU owns 1962- . Latest 10 years in Reference; earlier years in Journals; 1962-1978 in Microforms.)
      HG 4961 .F8 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    F & S Index. Europe. Foster City, CA: Information Access Company, 1978- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1978- . Latest 10 years in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HC 249 .A1 F2x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    F & S Index. International. Foster City, CA: Information Access Company, 1967- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1968- . Latest 10 years in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HC 10 .F2x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Excellent source for locating articles on various companies, industries, and economic conditions. Indexed by expanded SIC codes and company name (in separate sections), this source is especially valuable for its coverage of numerous trade journals as well as general business publications, the Wall Street Journal, and the business section of the New York Times. This coverage makes it particularly useful for finding information on smaller companies and industries. The Europe and International editions also provide indexing by country subdivided by SIC. Use the International edition for everything except United States and Europe.

  5. General Business File. Foster City, CA: Information Access Company, 1980- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1980- .)
      ONLINE ACCESS Click on Business

    This source provides comprehensive and up-to-date, computer-searchable subject indexing of more than 960 business periodicals. Coverage includes the Wall Street Journal and the business section of the New York Times for the last 90 days. It is especially useful for information on smaller companies found in trade journals and regional business newspapers. Full text is available for about half the sources since 1993. Also includes directory information for 150,000 companies and the latest issues of full text investment analysts' reports from Investext for companies and industries.

  6. PROMT: Predicasts Overview of Markets and Technology. Foster City, CA: Information Access Company, 1977- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1981- .)
      HD 9650.1 .P16 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Provides abstracts of selected articles from the F & S Index on companies and industries, as well as some market share information and other statistical data. Very useful for up-to-date information on trends in different industries and technologies.

  7. U.S. Government Periodicals Index. Bethesda, MD: CIS, 1992- . Quarterly. (ASU owns 1992- .)
      ONLINE ACCESS Click on Government Publications

    Indexes periodicals published by federal agencies including business titles such as Business America put out by the Department of Commerce and the Monthly Labor Review from the Department of Labor.
