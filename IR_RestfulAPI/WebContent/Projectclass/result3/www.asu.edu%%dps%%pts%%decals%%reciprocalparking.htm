ASU Reciprocal Parking

Most ASU decals, whether purchased at the Tempe, Polytechnic, Downtown Phoenix or West campus, are honored at the other campuses
in designated areas.

In order for the decal to be valid for reciprocal parking, it must be a purchased decal, meaning it was not included as part of a residence hall payment.

If you have a TEMPE CAMPUS DECAL, you may park...
Polytechnic West Downtown
Any green lot Lots 1, 2, 3, 10, 11, 20 DP5*
 
If you have a POLYTECHNIC DECAL
(with the exception of a Polytechnic Resident Hall Decal) you may park....
Tempe West Downtown
Lot 59E Lots 1, 2, 3, 10, 11, 20 DP5
 
If you have a WEST CAMPUS DECAL (excluding Lot 19), you may park...
Tempe Polytechnic Downtown
Lot 59E Any green lot DP5
 

If you have a DOWNTOWN CAMPUS DECAL
(excluding Student Civic Plaza East), you may park...
Tempe Polytechnic West
Lot 59E Any green lot Lots 1, 2, 3, 10, 11, 20

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

*DP5 is located at 320 N. Fourth Ave., on Fourth north of Van Buren Street

## As long as the decal is a PAID parking decal, meaning the decal was not included as part of a student’s residence hall payment or distributed for some other reason free of charge, it is valid in the aforementioned lots.
