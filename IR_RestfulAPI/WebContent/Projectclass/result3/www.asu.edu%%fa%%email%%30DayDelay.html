30-Day Delay Notice for Fall 2006

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this e-mail notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

Freshmen, first-time ASU Federal Direct Subsidized and/or Unsubsidized Student Loan borrowers are subject to a 30-day delay in the disbursement of these funds. Please note: if you have applied for a Parent Loan for Undergraduate Students (PLUS) there is no 30-day delay on disbursement of PLUS funds.

If all eligibility requirements are satisfied, these loan funds will credit to your student account no sooner than September 21, 2006. Any resulting refund owed to you will be mailed to your local address on file with the University or credited to your bank account (if you enrolled in direct deposit) two to five days after crediting to your student account. Please use ASU Interactive to verify your local mailing address.

The ASU Student Financial Assistance office strives to assist you in having funds available when they are needed, please plan accordingly as there will be no exceptions made for this requirement. Please feel free to call or visit one of our locations if you have questions.

Sincerely,

Arizona State University
