Chart Control: autoScale

Chart Control: autoScale


determines whether the chart is automatically scaled to fit within its borders

Type: Character
Valid Values: On Off

Details

Details

Off Turn off auto-scaling and use values specified for XScale, YScale, and ZScale. Available only for catagorical variables.
On Turn on auto-scaling and ignore values specified for XScale, YScale, and ZScale.
