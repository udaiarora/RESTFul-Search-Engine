Marketing Resources

 

This bibliography is designed to be a list of the most frequently used sources for advertising & marketing information, particularly for ASU class projects. It is to be used with the Company and Industry Research, whose sources are not usually repeated in this bibliography. I suggest starting with the Periodical Indexes to get an idea of what is going on in the industry, your company, and the world. Business cycles and the current cultural and economic world situation play an important role in the decisions and recommendations.
