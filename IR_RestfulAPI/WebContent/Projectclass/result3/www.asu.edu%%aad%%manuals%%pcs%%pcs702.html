PCS 702: Retention of Property Records

Purpose

To retain university property records for a sufficient time period

Sources

Arizona Revised Statutes § 41–1346
University policy

Policy

Property records are retained by Property Control for the periods listed below. D

Type of RecordRetention PeriodRemarks
Capital Transaction Reports—Equipment onlytwo yearsafter fiscal year created
Capital Transaction Reports—Land and buildingstwo yearsselect files transferred to Archives
Physical Inventory Reportsfive yearsafter fiscal year created
Property Relocation Authorizationsfive yearsafter fiscal year created
Purchase Orders/Journal Vouchersfive yearsafter fiscal year created
endtable

Cross-Reference

For information on the storage of records, see PCS 703, “Records Storage and Destruction.”
