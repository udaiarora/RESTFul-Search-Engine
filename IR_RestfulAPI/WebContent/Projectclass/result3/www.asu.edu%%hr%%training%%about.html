About The Learning and Performance Solutions Group

The Learning and Performance Solutions Group provides ongoing support for and responds to the developmental needs of the organization and employees. The group also serves as a clearinghouse for training and development opportunities.

Classes provide training and information to assist ASU employees in performing their roles effectively in support of the university. Professional trainers facilitate sixty percent of the classes. Experienced topic experts lead the operational classes.

Training is provided to employees at no cost. All employees, as well as student workers, are eligible to enroll in most classes. University policy allows each employee 16 hours of release time per year to participate in professional development activities. (See SPP 601 for more information on Training Release Time.)
