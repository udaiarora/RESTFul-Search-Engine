Reviewing your search results

Did you retrieve too few articles?

Use synonyms

Just as the ?and? connector is used to narrow a search topic and reduce the number of articles retrieved, the ?or? connector can be used to help you broaden your search topic and increase the number of articles retrieved. The ?or? connector is used to link synonymous keywords

Using the previous example, we might wish to develop a list of similar words for media:

television
movies
We can then put those synonymous keywords together to make a new search statement:
media OR television OR movies

The above search will retrieve more articles than would have been retrieved by searching only the keyword media.
