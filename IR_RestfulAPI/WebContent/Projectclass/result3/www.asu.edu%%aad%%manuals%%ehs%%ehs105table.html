American National Standards Institute (ANSI) Rating Requirements

Protective equipment purchased after July 5, 1994 must meet the following:

  1. eye and face protection: ANSI Z87.1 1989

  2. foot protection: ANSI Z41 1991

    and

  3. head protection: ANSI Z89.1 1986.

Protective equipment purchased before July 5, 1994 must meet the following:

  1. eye and face protection: ANSI Z87.1 1968

  2. foot protection: ANSI Z41.1 1967

    and

  3. head protection: ANSI Z89.1 1969.
