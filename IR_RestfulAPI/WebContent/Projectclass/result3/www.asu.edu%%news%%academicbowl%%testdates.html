| ACADEMIC BOWL


 

Colleges set dates for tests to select teams

Colleges planning to field tems in the Academic Bowl have set dates for testing that will help determine team make up.

Each participating school and college will select four team members and four alternates based on input that includes a competitive written test offered by each competing school or college. The final decision on team membership will be made by each college’s dean.

Following are the current dates and times for the tests. Times will be added as they are finalized. For additional information, contact the specific college.
