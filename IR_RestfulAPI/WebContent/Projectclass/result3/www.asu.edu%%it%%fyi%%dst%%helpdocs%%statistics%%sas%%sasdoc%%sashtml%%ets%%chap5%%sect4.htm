The SASEFAME Interface Engine

Reading and Converting FAME Database Time Series

The SASEFAME engine supports reading and converting time series stored in FAME databases. The SASEFAME engine uses the FAME WORK database to temporarily store the converted time series. Only the series specified by the FAME wildcard are written to the FAME WORK database.
