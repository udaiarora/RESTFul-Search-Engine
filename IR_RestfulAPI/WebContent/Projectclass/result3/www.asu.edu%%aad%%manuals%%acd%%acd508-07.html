ACD 508–07: Retirement Age

Purpose

To define the university’s policy on retirement

Source

The amendments to the Age Discrimination in Employment Act, 29 United States Code § 631 (1986)

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

To conform with federal law, the university specifies no retirement age for tenured or nontenured faculty nor for continuing or noncontinuing academic professionals.

Cross-Reference

For information on retirement benefits and programs, see the policies in ACD 607, Retirement Programs and Benefits.
