Introduction and Instructions

As an employer, Arizona State University is committed to its employees and is interested in identifying opportunities to improve the work environment. The Exit Interview provides a valuable source of information to measure the overall quality of worklife at ASU. Your responses are confidential to the Office of Human Resources and will not be shared with the department without your permission and will not become a part of your official personnel file. When appropriate, cumulative data in a tabulated format such as through statistics and/or graphs may be shared with Deans, Directors, and other Administrative Officials. Your re-employment with ASU will not be affected by your responses.

While the University does not require you to provide any of the following information, we value your responses and hope that your suggestions, comments and observations will help ASU in it's continued effort to make the best possible work environment for its staff. Your feedback is greatly appreciated.
