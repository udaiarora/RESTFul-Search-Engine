MSC Success Story


Katie Curiel

Katie Curiel is currently in her fifth and final year at ASU majoring with a B.A. in Human Communication and minors in Business and Spanish. Katie is a native of Phoenix, AZ. and contributes greatly to the Phoenix and ASU communities through her humanitarian efforts and social consciousness.

Throughout her college experience she has dedicated much of her time outside of the classroom to aiding specifically with the needs of the Phoenix Union High School District community through her involvement with the Phoenix Union High School District/ASU Partnership. During her time at ASU she has been a dedicated member of the Phoenix Union Devils community service based organization and has worked the last four years in the PUHSD/ASU Partnership office as a Peer Coach, Student Involvement Liaison, College Cousins Leader, ASU Summer Transition Academy Counselor and Intern, and a Success Link Workshop Presenter, among her service to the Big Brothers Big Sisters and the Student Speakers Bureau. Katie also feeds her passion for learning about other cultures through traveling throughout the U.S. and other countries. She has traveled much of the U.S. and Mexico, and has ventured into Switzerland and Italy.

She aspires to continue giving back to the community which enabled her to succeed, through the Teach for America program, volunteer efforts, and a possible career within the Phoenix Union High School District. She also plans on continuing her education and receiving a Master's degree in Higher Education to possibly return to Student Affairs at Arizona State University. Katie's experiences with serving and working with students at the secondary and higher education levels, coupled with the motivation and knowledge she was given throughout her education, have enlightened her to use her own knowledge and motivation to create social change and greater opportunities for under-represented individuals.
