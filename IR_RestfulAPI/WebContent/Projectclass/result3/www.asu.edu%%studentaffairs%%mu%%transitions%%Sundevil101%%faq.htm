Frequently Asked Questions
How can I make a reservation for Sun Devil 101: Welcome Week?
The reservation system will be available in May, 2007. Please check back for updates at later time.
Why attend Sun Devil 101?
Sun Devil 101 is a great time to get acquainted with ASU and its resources. You will have many opportunities to meet other new students and parents and interact with ASU Faculty and staff. Have fun making new friends at the many FREE activities.
What is Sun Devil 101?
Sun Devil 101 is a time to meet new friends, get involved, enjoy free food, attend program sessions, and relax before the school year begins.
Who should attend Sun Devil 101?
Sun Devil 101 is open to anyone. Freshmen students and their families are especially encouraged to attend. Friends are also welcome.
Is Sun Devil 101 free?
YES!
Should my parents come to Sun Devil 101?
Absolutely! There are activities and events scheduled for students and parents throughout the weekend. Review the ASU Sun Devil 101 Complete Schedule with your parents and make sure to attend our signature events.
Where can my parents stay during Sun Devil 101?
For information on hotels close to ASU, contact the Tempe Convention and Visitors Bureau at 800-283-6734. Or look for accommodations on the Greater Phoenix Convention & Visitors Bureau and Scottsdale.com web sites.
