Student Account Refunds by Direct Deposit

Direct Deposit to your bank account is available and is the most convenient way to receive student account refunds, including Financial Aid.

To enroll for Direct Deposit login to www.asu.edu/interactive using your ASURite User ID and Password.  Under Financial Services choose the option "Student Account Direct Deposit Enrollment".

Answers to frequently asked questions about Direct Deposit enrollment on the Internet and Direct Deposit in general, are available at www.asu.edu/sbs/DD_FAQ or in the Student Accounts Office on the 2nd floor of the Student Services Building.
