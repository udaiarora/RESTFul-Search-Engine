January 7, 2002

Children's dance, creative drama classes offered at ASU

The departments of Theatre and Dance in the Herberger College of Fine Arts at ASU invite children to explore the creative world of the performing arts through a series of Saturday morning classes specifically designed for children ages 5-11.

The Department of Theatre's program for young people, dubbed "Imagination Corporation," has been in existence for 23 years. For a seventh year, the Department of Dance is offering the "Discover Dance" program as well.

WHEN: The one-hour classes begin Saturday, Feb. 1 and meet for six successive weekends through March 8. For Imagination Corporation, children ages 5-7 meet 9 to 10 a.m.; ages 8-11 meet 10:15 to 11:15 a.m. For Discover Dance, children ages 8-11 meet 9 to 10 a.m.; ages 5-7 meet 10:15 to 11:15 a.m.

WHERE: Classes will be held in the Nelson Fine Arts Center on the ASU campus.

HOW MUCH: Tuition for the six-class series is $45 for each child in either dance or drama. If a child registers for both drama and dance courses, the total tuition is only $80 for 12 sessions.

CALL: ASU instructors supervise Imagination Corporation and Discover Dance. For registration information on Imagination Corporation, contact Pam Sterling at

(480) 727-6114; for Discover Dance, contact Kathy Lindholm Lane at (480) 965-0412.
