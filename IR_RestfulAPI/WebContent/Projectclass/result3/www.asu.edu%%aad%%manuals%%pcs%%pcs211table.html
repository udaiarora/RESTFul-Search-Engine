To prepare a requisition for the installment purchase, the department:

  1. records a description of the equipment and the payment due (e.g., 1st, 2nd, or 3rd payment) on the description line of the RX “L/P”

    and

  2. enters in the PTEX table the month the lease payments will begin, when they will end, and other information to assist the buyer in placing the purchase order (e.g., number of payments).

Purchasing and Business Services:

  1. processes the RX

  2. generates a purchase order

    and

  3. records the ASU property control number(s) (PC#s) in the PTEX table of the purchase order.
