Understanding your results

Recognizing materials

While you use an index to find journal articles, some indexes also include other types of publications. See the examples below for hints on how to recognize and find these materials.

    Journal articles
    Title: Children's Reactions to a Television News Story.
    Subject(s): TELEVISION & children; TELEVISION broadcasting of news
    Source: Communication Research, Oct2000, Vol. 27 Issue 5, p641, 33p, 3 graphs
    Author(s): Smith, Stacy L.; Wilson, Barbara J.

    Hint: Look for volume and issue number, and dates with months or seasons. See the next page in this lesson to learn how to find these in the library.

    Book chapters
    DT: Chapter
    TI: Media and television violence: Effects on violence, aggression, and antisocial behaviors in children.
    AU: Derksen,-Daniel-John; Strasburger,-Victor-C.
    BK: Hoffman, Allan M. (Ed); et-al. (1996). Schools, violence, and society. (pp. 61-77). Westport, CT, US: Praeger Publishers/Greenwood Publishing Group, Inc. xiii, 357 pp.SEE BOOK
    IB: 0275949788 (hardcover); 0275955060 (paperback)
    PY: 1996
    LA: English

    Hint: Look for names of editors, usually followed by the abbreviation "Ed" or "Eds"; the name of publishers or a place of publication; or the word in followed by the book title. To find these, search the catalog for the title of the book, not the chapter or article.

    Newspaper articles
    TI- Teen shootings and media hype.
    DE- VIOLENCE in mass media -- Social aspects -- United States
    DE- CHILDREN & violence -- United States
    JN- Christian Science Monitor
    PD- 05/27/98, Vol. 90 Issue 126, p19
    PG- 0p
    AU- Kubey, Robert W.

    Hint: Look for date with month, day and year. Sometimes the citation will include the abbreviation "col" for column, or will indicate a particular section. To find these, search the catalog for the newspaper title using the journal title option.

    Government documents
    SUA: Y 4.C 73/7:S.HRG.104-369
    CA: United States. Congress. Senate. Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation.
    TI: Television violence : hearing of the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation, United States Senate, One Hundred Fourth Congress, first session, July 12, 1995.
    SO: Washington : U.S. G.P.O. : For sale by the U.S. G.P.O., Supt. of Docs., Congressional Sales Office, 1996 [i.e. 1996].
    PY: 1996
    SE: United States. Congress. Senate. S. hrg. ; 104-369.
    IT: 1041-A 1041-B (MF)
    DE: Television-and-children-United-States. Violence-in-television-United-States.

    Hint: Look for government agency names; or the acronym GPO or phrase "Government Printing Office". To find these in the library, ask a librarian for help.

Other citations you find may include:
    Dissertations
    DT: Dissertation-Abstract
    TI: The immediate and cumulative effects of television mediation on children's aggression.
    AU: Nathanson,-Amy-I.
    SO: Dissertation-Abstracts-International-Section-A:-Humanities-and-Social-Sciences. 1999 Jan; Vol 59(7-A): 2236
    IS: 0419-4209
    PY: 1999
    UM: AAM9826434
    LA: English

    Hint: Look for the word dissertation or phrase "dissertation abstracts international"; the acronym UMI or phrase "University Microfilms International". Unless the dissertation was written for a degree at Arizona State Univesity, it is unlikely that the library owns it. However, dissertations can be requested through interlibrary loan. See the page What if the journal is not in the online catalog for more information on requesting from interlibrary loan.

    Conference papers
    AU: Singer,-Mark-I.; Miller,-David
    TI: Mental Health and Behavioral Sequelae of Children's Exposure to Violence.
    PY: 1998
    AV: Web site: .
    NT: In: Chapter 5, "Prevention & Early Intervention," of Proceedings of the Annual Research Conference, A System of Care for Children's Mental Health: Expanding the Research Base (11th, Tampa, FL, March 8-11, 1998).
    DT: Reports-Research (143); Speeches-or-Meeting-Papers (150)
    CP: U.S.; Ohio
    LA: English
    PG: 5
    DEM: *Aggression-; *Emotional-Problems; *Television-; *Violence-

    Hint: Look for the name of a conference or meeting; a place and date range indicating where and when the meeting took place; or the word "proceedings."
