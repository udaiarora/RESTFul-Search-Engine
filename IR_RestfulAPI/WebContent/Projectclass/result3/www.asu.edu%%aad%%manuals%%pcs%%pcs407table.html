The department receiving equipment verifies that the equipment is as specified and in good condition, and enters a receiver online for the equipment.

If the department does not have online capability, a memo may be sent to Payables and Reimbursements. The memo should state the purchase order number, line number, quantity received, and whether the shipment is full or partial.

Note: Departments must input an RC (receiver document) or notify Payables and Reimbursements within two working days of receipt of equipment.

The department notifies Purchasing and Business Services and Payables and Reimbursements immediately of any discrepancy between the order and equipment received.

Property Control tags the equipment and adds it to the property control system.

If the equipment is not identified and tagged within two weeks of performing an RC, the department receiving equipment contacts Property Control.
