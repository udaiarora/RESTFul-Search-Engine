ACD 601–08: Automobile Insurance

Purpose

To provide payroll-deducted premiums for university-sponsored automobile insurance

Sources

Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 6–604
University policy

Applicability

Faculty and academic professionals employed to work 20 hours per week (50 percent FTE) or more for a continuous period of six or more months

Policy

Eligible faculty and academic professionals may authorize payroll deductions for the university-sponsored automobile insurance plan.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Faculty member
or
academic professional
  1. Contact Human Resources (HR) for the name of the local agency.

  2. Contact the local agency for the sponsored automobile insurance program and arrange for coverage.
Local agent
  1. Bind coverage and send payroll authorization form to HR.
