Sparky's Den

Phone: 480.965.3642
Lower Level - Memorial Union
Semester Hours of Operation*
Monday, Wednesday, Friday 9:00 am - 10:45 pm
Tuesday and Thursday 10:00 am - 10:45 pm
Saturday 12:00 pm - 10:45 pm
Sunday 2:00 pm - 8:00 pm

Please note: Sparky's Den is closed during university holidays and has limited hours during semester breaks and summer. Please call ahead to ensure we can serve you.

Located at the north end lower level of the Memorial Union, Sparky's Den offers 8 bowling lanes, 15 billiards tables and numerous video games. It's a great place to unwind after a busy day or hold a group event. Sparky's Den is open to any community member with a valid photo ID.

Students Playing Billiards Sparky's Den also offers Cosmic Bowling which incorporates glow in the dark bowling balls, pins, lanes, and disco lighting to a variety of music.

So drop in between classes or reserve Sparky's Den for your department or organization to celebrate another successful semester or any occasion.

Students playing Video gamesFree Sundays! Registered student organizations may reserve the bowling lanes for one hour free of charge, once per semester on an available Sunday for a group event. Please call 480.965.5291 for more information.
