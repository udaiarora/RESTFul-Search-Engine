Primary Sources in History: Asia

13. CIA RESEARCH REPORTS. JAPAN, KOREA AND THE SECURITY OF ASIA, 1946-1976.   CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm DS 518.1 J36x NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 5 R    INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 518.1 J36x Guide      SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Reports from the U. S. Central Intelligence Agency.  Collection arranged by country, then chronologically.  Covers not only Japan and Korea, but also Australia, Burma, Indonesia, Malaysia, New Zealand and the Philippines.  Includes intelligence memos and biographical reports.  Covers communist methods and vulnerabilities; confrontation between Indonesia and Malaysia, 1950-1951; Korean War.  Reports emphasize U. S. security, particular relations between China an the Soviet Union. Also covers the Industrial development of Japan. SUBJECTS: Japan; Korea; Asia; Southeast Asia; Vietnam; 20c; 1940s-1970s

14. INDIA (REPUBLIC) OFFICE OF THE REGISTRAR GENERAL. CENSUS OF INDIA, 1872-1951.   CALL NUMBER: University of Arizona Microfiche 123  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:    INDEX OR GUIDE: Census of India, 1872-1951.  Microfiche Guide 123.  Follows the physical organization of the microfiche collection, by year, province and statistical categories.    SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Census reports for the early year consist largely of general population tables for the provinces, their principal towns and suburbs and other demographic statistics.  Later reports include caste and ethnographic indexes, economic and industrial tables and maps.  SUBJECTS: India; South Asia; 19c; 20c; Census.

15.MILITARY SITUATION IN THE FAR EAST AND THE RELIEF OF GENERAL MACARTHUR.    CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 6782    NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 8 R INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 919 G78x Stacks.  The guide is a list of the contents of each reel.  Reel 8 contains a detailed subject/name index to the printed edition of the hearings.  It can be used as an index to this transcript by using the conversion table, also located on reel 8. SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Transcript of 1951 Senate hearings on Pres. Truman's firing of the general and into developments in the Korean War following the introduction of Chinese troops.  There are over 8,000 pages of material, including information on U. S. foreign relations and military policy during the Korean War.  Includes passages previously marked "top secret."  SUBJECTS: MacArthur, Douglas; World War II, Asia; Korean War; China; 20c; 1940s; 1950s

16. OSS STATE DEPT. INTELLIGENCE & RESEARCH REPORT PT 3. CHINA AND INDIA, 1941-1949.      CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm DS 774 .C435x NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 6 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 774 .C435x Index.  A reel guide and subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Declassified research reports cover World War II; communism; economic, political, social and military conditions.  Some biographies of officials.  Noteworthy events during this period include the establishment of Pakistan and the rise of self-government in India.  The University of Arizona has the 1950-1961 supplement to this set (Microfilm 2952).   SUBJECTS: Asia; China; India; World War II; 20c; 1940s

17. OSS STATE DEPT. INTELLIGENCE & RESEARCH REPORT. PT 2.  POST WAR JAPAN, KOREA & SOUTHEAST ASIA, 1945-1949.  CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm DS 889 P83x   NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 6 R      INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 889 P83x Guide   SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Declassified reports cover Burma, Indochina, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Malaya, Philippines, Thailand.  Includes biographies and information on political and economic and social conditions, foreign policy.  Documents the industrial development of Japan, the spread of communism in Asia and the situation in Korea in the years preceeding the Korean War.  SUBJECTS: Asia, Japan; Korea; Southeast Asia; World War II; 20c; 1940s

18. OSS STATE DEPT. INTELLIGENCE & RESEARCH REPORT. PT. 8. JAPAN, KOREA, SOUTHEAST ASIA AND FAR EAST, 1950-1961.  CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm DS 889 J335x  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 7 R      INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 889 J335x Guide  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Declassified documents cover Burma, Indochina, Indonesia, Malaya, Philippines, Vietnam (North and South), Japan, Korea.  Information on foreign relations, politics, economic conditions; the political and economic revival of Japan; the Korean War; the outbreak of war in Indochina; and the general strategic position of the Far East.  SUBJECTS: Japan; Korea; Southeast Asia; Asia; 20c; 1950s; 1960s

19. SPECIAL STUDIES SERIES. JAPAN, KOREA AND THE SECURITY OF ASIA, 1970-1980.      CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 7772  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 4 R INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 518.1 J35x Guide Micro Ref. Provides author, title and subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Contains studies commissioned by the government from "think tanks." Topics include political, economic and military matters of concern to U. S. security.  Papers are divided into three sections: East Asia, Japan and Korea.  Notable events during this period include the end of the Vietnam War and the resumption of diplomatic relations between the U. S. and China. SUBJECTS: Japan; Korea; Asia; Southeast Asia; 20c; 1970s
