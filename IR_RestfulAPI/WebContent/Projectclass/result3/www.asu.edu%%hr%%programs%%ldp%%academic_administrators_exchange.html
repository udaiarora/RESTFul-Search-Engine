The Academic Administrators Exchange

Purpose

The Academic Administrators Exchange is the newest component of the Leadership Development Program. The Exchange provides a venue for sharing of ideas and expertise through small, self-select, colleague-facilitated workshops open to members of both the chairs/directors and the associate/assistant deans councils. As the role of academic administrators expands, the monthly plenary sessions of the councils have also evolved to address more global issues. The Exchange preserves the fundamental skill-development aims of the Program by responding to requests for a focused forum in which to address specific administrative skill development.

The Exchange is coordinated through Human Resources Programs by a designated coodinator. There is significant programming collaboration with the Office of the Provost and the Center for Learning and Teaching Excellence.
