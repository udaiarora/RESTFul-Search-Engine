Arizona Universities Classification/Compensation System (AUCCS) Job Descriptions

  • Search AUCCS Job Descriptions

ASU will have classified job classification descriptions available to the university community through the Human Resources web site: http://www.asu.edu/hr/compensation/. NOTE: The Human Resources web page will serve as the communication and official copy of the classified job classification descriptions. Colleges/departments should discard the contents of the 3-ring binders, which were provided in July 1990.

Human Resources-Compensation has elected not to place all AUCCS classified job classification descriptions on the Human Resources web site. Certain jobs are considered to be frozen, not available for current use at ASU, and others are considered not available for future use at ASU. The frozen job classifications are designated by "F" job classification status in the HRMS job classification table. The AUCCS system contains approximately 733 job classifications. ASU currently uses approximately 419 job classifications, and will have approximately 533 job classification descriptions defined on the Human Resources web site.
