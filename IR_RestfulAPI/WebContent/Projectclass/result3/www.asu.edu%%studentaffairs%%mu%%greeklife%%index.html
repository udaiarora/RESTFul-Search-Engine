Greek Life at Arizona State University
Fraternities and sororities have held a prominent place at Arizona State University for over 85 years. For our members the Greek community is a home away from home - a place that plays a critical role in enhancing the quality of life while at ASU, as well as long after graduation. Academic achievement, service to the community, leadership opportunities, social networks and overall successful student development are some of the benefits of membership in a Greek chapter. The men and women of ASU?s Greek community have been, and continue to be, a major influence on student activities and programs. Our Greek community offers a continuing tradition of leadership and service with each new membership class.
