Reporting from the Field: SAS Software Experts Present Real-World Report-Writing Applications

Have you ever gotten frustrated trying to do report writing with SAS software? Let the experts show you how. In this excellent reference book of stand-alone chapters, experienced SAS users share their wealth of experience and advice on report-writing strategies. Loaded with examples, the emphasis is on practical applications of the SAS System. Each chapter offers a fresh look at a report writing application. Products covered include base SAS, SAS/IML, SAS/GRAPH, and SAS/STAT software. Learn what the experts have to say about debugging report-writing programs, online reports using display manager, generating surveys, producing reports with PROC TABULATE, customizing reports using SCL, and creating custom reports using PROC SQL views.

329 pages.
