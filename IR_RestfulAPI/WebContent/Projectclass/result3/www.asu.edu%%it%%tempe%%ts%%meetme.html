Meet-Me Conference Calling

Scheduling The Call

The calendars for the Meet-Me conference call bridges are kept in Outlook. The process to schedule a Meet-Me conference bridge is very similar to scheduling a conference room. To schedule a call, follow these basic steps:

Outlook 2002

  1. Open the Calendar folder in Outlook.
  2. Under the Action menu, choose New Meeting Request.
  3. Under the Scheduling tab, click on the Add Others button and choose "Add from Address Book" from the drop-down list. The "Select Attendees and Resources" dialogue box will open. Make sure you are viewing names from the Global Address List.
  4. In the box titled "Type Name or Select From List", begin typing m.equip.it.VCEO.m. As you type, the list will jump to the entries that match your input. You will see 4 Meet-Me Conference bridges listed. Choose any one of the four by clicking once on it and then clicking the Resources button. Then click OK.
  5. You will now be back at the Scheduling tab in your meeting request box, where you can view the calendar of the Meet-Me Conference bridge you requested. Be sure to enter your meeting start and end times at the bottom of the box to view the availability of the resource.
  6. If the resource is available at the desired time, click Send. If not, you may need to go back to the Global Address List and choose another Meet-Me Conference number.
  7. Distribute the Meet-Me Conference phone number to the call participants and instruct them to call that number at the given time.

Outlook 2000

  1. Open the Calendar folder in Outlook.
  2. Under the Action menu, choose New Meeting Request.
  3. Under the Attendee Availability tab, click on the Invite Others button. The "Select Attendees and Resources" dialogue box will open. Make sure you are viewing names from the Global Address List.
  4. In the box titled "Type Name or Select From List", begin typing m.equip.it.VCEO.m. As you type, the list will jump to the entries that match your input. You will see 4 Meet-Me Conference bridges listed. Choose any one of the four by clicking once on it and then clicking the Resources button. Then click OK.
  5. You will now be back at the Attendee Availability tab in your meeting request box, where you can view the calendar of the Meet-Me Conference bridge you requested. Be sure to enter your meeting start and end times at the bottom of the box to view the availability of the resource.
  6. If the resource is available at the desired time, click Send. If not, you may need to go back to the Global Address List and choose another Meet-Me Conference number.
  7. Distribute the Meet-Me Conference phone number to the call participants and instruct them to call that number at the given time.

Making The Call

At the prearranged time, each call participant dials the Meet-Me Conference bridge number.

  • The first caller hears, "Welcome to the ASU Meet-Me Conferencing Service. You are the first party to join the call."
  • Each additional caller hears, "Welcome to the ASU Meet-Me Conferencing Service. Your conference call is already in progress."
  • A maximum of six parties can call the bridge simultaneously. If a seventh caller attempts to join the conference, the system will inform the caller that the conference is full and will disconnect the call.
