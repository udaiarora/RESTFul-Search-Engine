The HR Advisor can help ASU administrative staff understand and interpret ASU policies and procedures consistently as they apply to classified, service professional and administrative employment categories.

It does not replace the Staff Personnel (SPP) Policies and Procedures Manual or a consultation with an HR Partners team member. It should be used as a guide in the application of university policies and procedures.
