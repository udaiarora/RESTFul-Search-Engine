USI 304–01 Table

Students employed hourly have unlimited allowed earnings, paid 100 percent by the employing unit.

Students in the on-campus Federal Work-Study Program have limited allowed earnings that must not exceed the student’s determined need eligibility, paid by the employing unit at 30 percent.

Note: The Federal Work-Study Program (FWSP) pays up to 70 percent of wages.
