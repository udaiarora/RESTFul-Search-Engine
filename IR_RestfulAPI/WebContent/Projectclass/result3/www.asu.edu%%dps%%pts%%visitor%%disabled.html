Disabled Parking

Parking and Transit Services is committed to the disabled community and works closely with the Disability Resources Office to ensure equal access and compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act.

Visitors with disabled license plates or displayed State disabled placards may park at disabled meters or in the visitor lots and pay applicable fees. Meters are $1.50/hr. (1hr. max) and Visitor lots are $2/hr. with a $8 maximum exit fee.
