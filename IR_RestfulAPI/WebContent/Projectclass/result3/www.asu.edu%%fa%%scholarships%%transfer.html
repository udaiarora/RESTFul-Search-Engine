Transfer Scholarships

For Outstanding Transfer Students Entering ASU Fall 2007

Scholarship opportunities for ASU students are awarded based on merit, need, or a combination of both. You can also apply for institutional and private donor funds. Many of the scholarships are renewable.

Scholarships may be used towards your educational expenses including tuition and fees, housing, books and other costs of attendance. Please be aware that ASU does not combine institutional recruitment scholarships. In the event you qualify for more than one of these scholarships, ASU will award you the scholarship with the higher dollar value.
Maroon & Gold Scholarship
Award:
  • Institutional recruitment scholarship cash cash award currently valued at $4,591 (amount subject to change).
  • Limited renewal awards are available.
Eligibility:
  • Arizona high school students and college transfer applicants from targeted (but not restricted) student populations (ethnic minority, first generation, or low income students) are encouraged to apply.
  • Students must demonstrate financial need documented by the 2007/2008 Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).
Application:
  • 2007-08 Application
Conditions of Award & Renewal Criteria:
  • Admissible to ASU and full-time enrollment at the ASU Tempe or ASU East campus beginning Fall 2007.
  • Renewals are based on the completion of 24 ASU credits by the end of the spring semester, participation in the Maroon & Gold retention program and a competitive cumulative Grade Point Average.
  • Renewals are limited.
Contact:
  • School Relations Office at (480) 965-2601
Deadline:
  • February 1, 2007
Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship
Award:
  • $2000
Eligibility:
  • Students must be currently enrolled in classes at a Community College.
  • Students must apply to ASU.
  • Students must have a 3.5 transfer GPA and a minimum of 24 transfer credit hours.
  • Scholarship winners must enroll for classes for the fall semester immediately following the scholarship award to remain eligible.
  • Scholarship is renewable for three consecutive semesters beyond the initial award if a 3.25 ASU GPA is maintained. (Scholarship may be awarded for a total of four semesters).
  • Students must register for a minimum of 12 credit hours (full load) each semester.
  • Scholarship is awarded for spring and fall semesters only (not summer session).
Application:
  • 2007-08 Application
Deadline:
  • May 1, 2007
