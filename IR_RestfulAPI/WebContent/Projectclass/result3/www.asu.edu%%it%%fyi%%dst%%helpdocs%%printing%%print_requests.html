How to check the status of print jobs

The command:

npshow -S "owner=$USER"
Displays the details of all print jobs for the specified owner. Replace "$USER" with your userid to see only your print requests.

The command:

npshow -S "queue=$PRINTQUEUE"
Replace "$PRINTQUEUE" with "Qcc102pr1" to display the details of all print jobs for the queue "Qcc102pr1".

For more information, enter "man npshow" at your system logon prompt.
