Employee E-Mail and Resources at ASU

Employees are eligible for an Exchange account, accesssible using Microsoft Outlook. Employees can also have an IMAP account if they prefer. In addition, ASU supports Blackberry wireless handhelds. Employees can also sponsor listserv's on LISTS.ASU.EDU.

How do I sign up for my ASU e-mail account?

Outlook
Software client for PC's, MAC's and Internet access from any computer.

Blackberry, Windows Mobile, & Treo
Handheld wireless devices can synchronize to your ASU Exchange account.

Electronic Post Office
ASU's internal e-mail systems are connected to each other and to the Internet through an e-mail hub called the Electronic Post Office (EPO). Use it to configure an e-mail alias.

Usenet News, sometimes referred to as Netnews, offers a wide variety of information on subjects and topics. You have the option of just soaking up the information, or becoming an active participant in the News groups.
