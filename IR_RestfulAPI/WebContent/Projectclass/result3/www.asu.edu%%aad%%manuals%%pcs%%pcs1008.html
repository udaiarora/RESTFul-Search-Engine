PCS 1008: Building Retirements

Purpose

To ensure the retirement of university-owned buildings

Sources

Arizona Constitution Article IX, § 7
Property Control

Policy

Property Control is responsible for retiring buildings that have been demolished.

Procedure D

Responsibility
Action
Department of Facilities Management
  1. Notify Property Control by memo that a building is scheduled to be demolished.
When the building has been demolished:
  1. Forward a formal memo to Property Control.
Property Control
  1. Verify that there were no capital or noncapital assets in the building.
  1. Retire the building from the property control system.
endtable
