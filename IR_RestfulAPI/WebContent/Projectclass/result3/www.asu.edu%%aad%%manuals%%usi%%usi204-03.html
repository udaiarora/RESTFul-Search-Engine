USI 204–03: Application for Graduation for Bachelor’s and Master’s Candidates

Purpose

To notify the Graduation Section that a bachelor’s or master’s candidate intends to complete all course work and requirements for graduation at the end of a specific semester

Sources

University Registrar’s Office
Division of Graduate Studies

Policy

A bachelor’s or master’s degree candidate must apply for graduation by the deadline dates specified in the ASU General Catalog and the ASU Graduate Catalog or pay a late fee.

A degree candidate must pay the appropriate fees at the time of application.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Student
  1. Pay the graduation fees and obtain a receipt at Cashiering Services.

  2. Take the graduation fee receipt to the Graduation Section and provide a definite graduation date.
Graduation Section or Registration Services at the Polytechnic campus (undergraduate students only) or Records & Registration at the West campus
  1. Type the Undergraduate Application for Graduation or the Graduate Application for Graduation, as appropriate, for the student.

  2. Verify that the student is meeting all graduation requirements outlined in the Undergraduate Program of Study form, Degree Audit, or the Master’s Degree Program of Study form.

  3. Give a diploma case to the student.
Student
  1. Sign the graduation application.
Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus
  1. File a copy of the application for processing in the semester in which graduation is anticipated.
endtable
