October 29, 2003

East, West campuses gear up for FestDevil Festivities

As a part of the annual Homecoming FestDevil activities, East and West campuses have big plans to show their Sun Devil spirit.

ASU East is holding its annual Homecoming FestDevil from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m., Oct. 29 at the Williams Campus Union. All ASU faculty, staff, students and alumni are welcome to attend this free event, which will include free food off the grill, music with a live DJ, games, prizes and festival booths. ASU East student organizations and clubs also will have informational booths set up.

The winner of ASU East's "Kiss the Pig" contest will be announced and will be asked to pucker up for a good cause. This student organization fund-raiser is an annual favorite and allows students, faculty and staff to help pick their favorite ASU East faculty or staff member to kiss a pig.

Throughout the week, ASU East departments will be participating in the university-wide Homecoming FestDevil 2003 Office Decorating Contest.
For information, call (480) 727-1203.

ASU West is holding its ASU West Spirit & Cultural Day Oct. 28. Faculty, staff, and students are encouraged to dress up in their best maroon and gold outfits. A Spirit Contest will recognize individuals and groups who decorate their spaces - office door, office space, Las Casas door, or student club table display.

Spirit Contest winners will be announced at the Homecoming Spirit Concert, Oct. 28, from 11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. in the Delph Courtyard. In addition to enjoying entertainment from KNJR Energy 92.7 and the ASU West Hip Hop dancers, attendees will have the chance to fish for Homecoming prizes. ASU West Homecoming Royalty nominees will be announced.

The FestDevil Coffeehouse will be open from 4:30 to 6:30 p.m. in the Delph Courtyard and will feature live music, gourmet coffee, and brownies.
For information, call (602) 543-2586.
