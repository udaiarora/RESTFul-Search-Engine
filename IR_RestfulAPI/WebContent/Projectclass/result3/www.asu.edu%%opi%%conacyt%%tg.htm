Applying Students Granted Students at ASU Enrolled Students
 

June

  • Apply for DS-2019 (needed to process the J1 VISA).

July

  • Arrange appointment at the U.S. embassy to process the J1 visa:
    http://www.usembassy-mexico.gov/smxvisitar.html.
  • Plan and prepare travel arrangements to move to Arizona:
    http://www.arizonaguide.com/default.aspx.
  • Arrange housing and transportation for the duration of stay in Arizona:
    http://www.asu.edu/studentaffairs/reslife/index.htm.
    http://asasu.net/usg/services/off-campus-housing.php.
  • Pre-register for classes:
    http://www.asu.edu/registrar/registration/ways2reg.html.

August

  • Register for classes:
    http://www.asu.edu/interactive
  • Go to the International Student Office with your passport and immigration documents as soon as you arrive on campus.
  • Pay for health insurance.
  • Sign up for email account:
    http://www.asu.edu/mailbox/student.htm
  • Apply for a Social Security number (international student must have to be employed). Contact the International Student Office for more information.
  • Attend ASU International Student Office Orientation and other applicable orientations.
  • First day of classes in late August

September

  • Contact Pan American Initiatives if you have any questions or suggestions.
  • Contact your department CONACYT representative.
  • Brochure in Spanish
  • Poster in Spanish
