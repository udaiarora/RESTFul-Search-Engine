Standards for Monographic Claiming

 

Monographic firm orders are claimed monthly, as well as "on demand." 


Claims for monographic materials are triggered by the claim cycle code in the vendor record. The "a" code is used for domestic vendors, and the "f" code is used for foreign vendors. The intervals between claims are greater for foreign vendors than for domestic vendors.


The number of days from the time orders are created until they are claimable is:


  Rush 1st 2nd 3rd
CODE   Claim Claim Review
1>a 15 60 120 180
2>f 45 100 180 260

 

The claim cycle code allows for "rush" claims as well as 1st, 2nd, and 3rd claims to be generated. When the material is claimable for the third time, acquisitions staff will make an informed decision about redirecting orders, canceling them, or claiming them. Selectors will be informed of cancellation decisions.


Selectors will be sent a list of cancelled orders monthly, and may request that orders be reinstated or redirected if they feel it is appropriate.
