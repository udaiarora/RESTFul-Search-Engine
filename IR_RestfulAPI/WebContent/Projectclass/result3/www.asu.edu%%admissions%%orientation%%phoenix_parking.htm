ASU at the Downtown Phoenix campus: Phoenix, AZ

Check-In Information
All Orientation programs at ASU's Downtown Phoenix campus are from 8:00 a.m. to approximately 3:00 p.m. Check in begins at 8:00 a.m. Optional campus tours will be offered in the afternoon.

Important Notes

All students planning to take the Calculus Placement exam should arrive at 7:15 a.m. with their photo ID to have enough time to be checked-in for the exam.

All students admitted to the Barrett Honors College need to attend the 8:30 a.m. Barrett Honors College Welcome.

Students must participate in the full day on-campus Orientation program to complete the registration process.

Accommodations
For information on hotels close to ASU, contact the Greater Phoenix Convention and Visitors Bureau: http://phoenixcvb.com. For more information about Phoenix, visit the City of Phoenix Web site at www.ci.phoenix.az.us.

Parking, Directions and Maps
Parking for Orientation participants is available in the Heritage and Science Park Garage. The Downtown campus ASU Orientation programs will take place in the Mercado Building C. For maps of ASU's Downtown Phoenix campus and driving directions visit www.asu.edu/map.

ASU Orientation provides parking validation/pass only for one vehicle per party in Heritage and Science Park Garage (student and his/her guests constitute a party). Therefore, the party is responsible for parking fees for any additional vehicles or if the party decides to park in another location besides Heritage and Science Park Garage. Note: you must bring the ticket that you receive upon entering the parking structure to your Orientation program for parking validation.

Note: Please be aware that some information contained within this page may be subject to change or may need to be updated. For the most up-to-date information, please return to this page before attending your ASU Orientation program for the latest details, or contact New Student Orientation with any questions you may have.
