Student Office Hours

The following dates have been set aside for students who would like a chance to discuss ASU-related issues related with President Crow and other members of the university administration in a small group setting.  Space is limited, so students are asked to register for any single session by completing an online registration form.  A representative of the Office of the President will contact you to confirm your registration.  Tempe campus office hours will be held in the Office of the President, 4th Floor - ASU Fulton Center (NE corner of College Avenue and University Drive).  Locations for office hours at the Downtown Phoenix, Polytechnic, and West campuses will be announced shortly.Â 

Please note that, in order to accommodate everyone in attendance, issues/problems of a personal nature should continue to be addressed in private appointments with the appropriate university officials.  You are welcome to contact the Office of the President at 480/965-8972 for additional information.
