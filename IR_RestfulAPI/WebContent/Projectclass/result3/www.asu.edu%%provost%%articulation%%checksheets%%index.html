Curriculum Check Sheets

The check sheet is a document that breaks down the course requirements for a particular degree program. It shows core course requirements, General Studies requirements, and possible electives or related fields. This tool helps the student and his/her advisor easily determine which requirements have been satisfied and which have yet to be satisfied.
