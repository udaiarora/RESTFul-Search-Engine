The department forwards to the Development Office all gift information:

  1. donor name and address

  2. list of equipment donated

  3. approximate or appraised value of each item

    and

  4. originals of all correspondence, the postmarked envelope in which a gift is mailed, and all other relevant paperwork.
The Development Office:

  1. issues an official ASU gift receipt acknowledging the gift

  2. forwards a photocopy of the official ASU gift receipt to Property Control

    and

  3. records the gift on Advantage.

Property Control tags the equipment and adds it to the property control system.
