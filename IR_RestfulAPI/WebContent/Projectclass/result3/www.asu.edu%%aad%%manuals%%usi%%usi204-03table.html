The student

  1. pays the graduation fees

  2. obtains a receipt at Cashiering Services

    and

  3. takes the graduation fee receipt to the Graduation Section and provides a definite graduation date.

The Graduation Section, Registration Services at the Polytechnic campus (undergraduate students only), or Records & Registration at the West campus:

  1. types the Undergraduate Application for Graduation or the Graduate Application for Graduation, as appropriate, for the student

  2. verifies that the student is meeting all graduation requirements outlined in the Undergraduate Program of Study form, Degree Audit, or the Master’s Degree Program of Study form

    and

  3. gives a diploma case to the student.

The student signs the graduation application.

The Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus files a copy of the application for processing in the semester in which graduation is anticipated.
