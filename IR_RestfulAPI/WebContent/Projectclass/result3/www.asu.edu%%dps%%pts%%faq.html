Why do we pay for parking? The Arizona Board of Regents has directed that Parking and Transit Services (PTS) be self-supporting.  No state money, tuition, or student fees go to PTS.  We are completely self-funded through the receipt of parking fees, event support, and citations.

Why is parking so expensive? Actually, ASU has the lowest structure parking rates in the PAC-10 and the second lowest surface parking prices.

What does PTS do with the revenues? We pay the bond debt on the structures, maintain the lots and structures, lighting, emergency call boxes in the structures, free FLASH service, and provide motorist assistance services.

When are decals required? 7:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m., Monday-Friday. Decal holders may cross-park after 4:00 p.m. except in PS-1, PS-6, ASU Foundation Parking Structure, Residential or Restricted lots which require proper decal at all times as posted. (Cross parking in PS-3 and Lot 16 is not permitted until 6 p.m., Mon. – Thurs. Resident Decal Holders may cross park in other resident decal lots after 4 p.m., except in Lot 50 and 63.)

Where is my decal valid on other ASU campuses? Most ASU decals, whether purchased at the Tempe, Polytechnic, Downtown Phoenix or West campus, are honored at the other campuses in designated areas. In order for the decal to be valid for reciprocal parking, it must be a purchased decal, meaning it was not included as part of a residence hall payment. Please click here for a list of reciprocal-eligible decals and a list of the ASU reciprocal parking lots

Why isn?t there enough parking for everyone? Actually, ASU Tempe experiences a 90% utilization of all of its parking lots at peak demand.  Due to pedestrian traffic, parking is located at the perimeter of campus so it may not be as convenient as all our patrons would like.  Additionally, we experience increased demand the first two weeks of each semester.

My friend is leaving campus.  Can I use his parking decal? No.  Decals are not transferable.  Please notify our office if anyone attempts to sell you a parking permit. The permit is invalid, regardless of the price you paid for it. Such transactions are improper, and may result in your vehicle being towed.

I forgot my  decal.  What do I do? You may pick up a free temporary at PTS, 525 S. Forest, (5th Street between Forest and College) Monday-Friday beginning at 7:30 a.m.

Help!  I?ve locked my keys in the car/my battery is dead. Call PTS Dispatch at 965-0641.  A field officer will respond as quickly as possible.

Why do we have to have gate arms? Gate arms act as a deterrent to unauthorized parking, and help lower the number of annual citations that must be issued.

May I park in visitor parking when I am late or forget my key card? You may park in visitor parking if space is available and you are willing to pay the fee.  Otherwise, you may park in Lot 59.

My schedule is too tight to park in Lot 59.  Why can?t I get closer parking? All decals are sold on a first-come, first-served basis.  New decals for the next academic year go on sale in July.  The number of people on campus prevents us from making exceptions for individual schedules.  Lot 59 is a 15-20 minute walk from the center of campus.  Free bus service is also provided on the FLASH.

Why do I have to park south of University Drive during events? Arizona State University is a very diverse community.  The many cultural and sports activities provided supports that diversity and may result in a temporary change in your parking assignment.  We make every effort to provide prior notification and alternative parking.

What if I am attending an event?

ASU Tempe Campus decal holders may park for free south of University Drive during events.  A small parking surcharge is included in the ticket price for Gammage events.  For event parking information, please call 965-6406.
