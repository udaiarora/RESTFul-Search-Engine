The department selling property completes a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form and sends it to Surplus Property and Recycling. One PRA form may be submitted for many items with an Excel spreadsheet attached stating: item #/lot #, item description, ASU property control #, and minimum bid amount (established by the department), if applicable. If desired, the department arranges and pays for newspaper advertising.

Surplus Property and Recycling:

  1. sends letters to prospective customers announcing the sealed bid sale and includes copies of the Excel spreadsheet, the bid guidelines, the closing date, and successful bidder payment instructions

  2. invites the department hosting the sale to submit names of prospective bidders

    and

  3. advertises the sealed bid sale on the Surplus Property and Recycling Web page with bid submission instructions, physical location of property for viewing, and viewing hours.

The department selling property provides viewing hours for prospective bidders and answers questions regarding property available for bid.

Interested parties send bids in sealed envelopes marked “Bid Enclosed” to:

Arizona State University
Surplus Property and Recycling
P.O. Box 970508
Tempe, AZ 85287-0508

or

fax the bid to Surplus Property and Recycling at 480/965–8519.

Bids are opened in the Surplus Property and Recycling manager’s office at 8:00 am the day after the bid closes.

Surplus Property and Recycling faxes or mails invoices (including all necessary information regarding acceptable forms of payment and payment and pickup schedule) to all successful bidders, identifying item #/lot # and successful bid amount, including sales tax when appropriate. In order to be exempt from Arizona sales tax, a bidder must have the appropriate forms on file with Surplus Property and Recycling prior to the close of the bid sale. These forms are available from the Surplus Property Warehouse sales office, 480/965–7630.
