Description of Image

This map shows the location of the four computing sites. At the center is CC Atrium (also called CPCOM), on the south side of Orange Street, between the Physical Education Building West, and the ASU Bookstore. To the southwest of CC Atrium is BAC 016, between and to the south of Physical Education Building West, and Business Administration. To the north of CC Atrium is ECG 150, at the northeastern end of the Engineering Center. Northeast from there, across Tyler Mall from Noble Library, is GWC 185, in the Goldwater Research Center.
