Admission

Admission to the University is required and must be completed prior to registration. ASU students enrolled during the 2007 Spring Semester are admitted for Summer Session 2007, unless they graduated in May 2007. The preferred method of applying is on-line. Complete and submit application at http://www.asu.edu/apply. If you are unable to apply on-line, admission can be accomplished with a mail-in application or in person.

GRADUATE STUDENTS
Direct inquiries to the Division of Graduate Studies (DGS) Admissions, Administration B Wing, Room 170 (480) 965-6113. You may apply on the WEB at: www.asu.edu/graduate.

Summer Session applications are processed for non-degree admission only.  For admission or readmission to a graduate degree program refer to the DGS website at www.asu.edu/graduate.

ADVISEMENT
The registration process can not be initiated if advisement is required. Each college establishes categories of students within that college who are required to meet with an advisor before registering.

Undergraduate students without an advisor should visit the www.asu.edu/duas/cas/ directory to determine the appropriate advising contact information. Students being readmitted or requiring a registration approval should contact the college advising office of their major.

Graduate students should contact the graduate studies personnel in their academic unit.

ASU West students without an advisor should contact the West Campus Advising Center, UCB 201 at (602) 543-4222.

AUDIT OPTION
The audit option must be completed before the close of Drop/Add and is included in the student credit load. The audit option is available in all colleges.
