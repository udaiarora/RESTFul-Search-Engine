Memorial Union Golden Anniversary Dinner

  • Download Forms:
    • Alumni, External Companies, and Community Members - Table Sponsorship or Individual Seat(s)
    • ASU Departments, Faculty, Staff and Students - Table Sponsorship or Individual Seat(s)
    • Fund Transfer to ASU Foundation Account

ASU students, faculty, staff, alumni, and community members are cordially invited to attend:

Memorial Union Golden Anniversary Dinner
Friday, February 24, 2006
Arizona & Ventana Ballrooms, Memorial Union

5:30 pm (invitation only events)
VIP Reception - Alumni #202
ASASU Reception - Kaibab #208E

6:00 pm - SOLD OUT (RSVP required)
Cocktail Reception - Ventana Ballroom #226

7:00 pm - SOLD OUT (RSVP required)
Dinner - Arizona Ballroom #207

Mistress of Ceremonies & Honorary Chair:
Dr. Christine K. Wilkinson
Senior Vice President and Secretary of the University
President, ASU Alumni Association

Dress is business attire.

Table Sponsorship:

  • Golden Level = $5,000
  • Maroon Level = $2,500
  • Sponsor Level = $1,000

Individual seating is also available at $125.

All proceeds from the Golden Anniversary Dinner remain in the Memorial Union Initiatives Foundation account. Funds will be deposited with the ASU Foundation, a separate non-profit that exists primarily to support ASU. This account is used for student scholarships, student travel, conference attendance, and other initiatives that directly benefit students.

A special thank you to Sodexho, Distinctive Catering and the ASU Alumni Association.

MU Golden Awards

The MU recognized prominent members of our community for their contributions to the ASU community as former student leaders, dedicated members of society, and individuals of great moral character. Awards were presented to:

  1. Ed Carson, '51
  2. Alfredo Gutierrez
  3. Michael Monti, '90
  4. John Fees, '89

Keynote Speaker

The keynote speaker at the Golden Anniversary Dinner was Marsha Herman-Betzen, Executive Director of the Association of College Unions International (ACUI), who has traveled the world as an ambassador of college unions and spreading the message of campus community.
