Meet Your Officers: Ashley Marton, President
 
 
Ashley was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona but has traveled all over the world since her childhood. She is currently a senior studying political science and history. When not in class, she spends her time volunteering at Body Positive, helping the American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU), Community Outreach and Advocacy for Refugees (COAR), Amnesty International, Ryan White Planning Council (2005 HIV/AIDS Care Assessment Project) and, of course, Pi Sigma Alpha. She has been a junior fellow for Professor Valerie Hoekstra and is a member of the National Scholars Honor Society. Ashley also will be teaching a political science freshman seminar in the spring of 2007. During the summer of 2005 Ashley had the opportunity to travel to Greece (Athens and Mykonos), Italy (Rome, Florence, Venice and Ischia), Croatia (Split and Dubrovnik) and Bosnia (Mostar). During her travels she development an interest in the nations of the former Yugoslavia, particularly Bosnia. She plans to return to Croatia and Bosnia in the summer of 2006.  After graduation Ashley wants to attend law school to study international law or civil rights law. As for now, she is looking forward to a great year with Pi Sigma Alpha.
