Resetting DCE Authentication Password

DCE Authentication passwords can be reset online at the ASURITE Activation and Service Management website if you know your current ASURITE/Kerberos password or have setup the identity verification questions available at that website. Otherwise, you will need to come to the IT Help Desk (CPCOM 202) with valid photo ID in order to have the password reset.

ASU West customers can go to the IT Helpline in the FAB or to the IT Help Desk in Technopolis in the Fletcher Library for password assistance.

ASU East customers can go to the Computing Commons on the East Campus for password assistance.

If you have a working ASU Exchange/Outlook email account, we can reset the DCE Authentication password to something generic and email it to that mailbox along with instructions for setting it to something more personal and secure.
