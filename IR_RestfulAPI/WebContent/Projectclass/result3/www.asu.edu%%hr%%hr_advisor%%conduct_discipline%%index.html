Discipline
This policy (SPP 809) applies to regular, non-probationary classified employees who need to improve their job performance and/or have not complied with departmental and university policies and procedures. ASU follows a progressive discipline process to provide sufficient feedback through written reprimands before an employee is terminated. There are a few exceptions that may bypass the discipline process. An HR Consultant can provide guidance and general assistance if necessary.

Violence in the Workplace
The university has a zero-tolerance policy (SPP 814) with regard to acts of intimidation, threats of violence and acts of violence in the workplace. Any ASU supervisor, manager or individual who receives a report of a suspected violation of this policy should document and report the suspected violation to the ASU DPS and/or the appropriate authority.
