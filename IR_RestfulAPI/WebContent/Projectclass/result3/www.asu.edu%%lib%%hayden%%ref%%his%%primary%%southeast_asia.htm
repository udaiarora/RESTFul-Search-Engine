Southeast Asia

297. BLAIR, EMMA HELEN. THE PHILIPPINE ISLANDS, 1493-1803.  CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 3x5 5115  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 222 F (55 VE)  INDEX OR GUIDE: The index to the collection is found in Vols. 54-55.  SCOCPE AND CONTENTS: Microcopy of Spanish documents translated from the original manuscripts.  Consists of reports of explorations of early navigators, descriptions of the islands and their peoples, their history and records of the Catholic missions. Information derived from contemporary books and manuscripts showing the political, economic, commercial and religious conditions from the earliest relations with European nations to the beginning of the 19th century.  SUBJECTS: Spain – Colonies; Philippines – History; 16c; 17c; 18c; 19c; Southeast Asia

298. CIA RESEARH REPORTS. VIETNAM AND SOUTHEAST ASIA, 1946-1976 and supplement  CALL NUMBER: Doc.Serv. Microfilm DS 557.7 V544x  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 7 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 557.7 V544x Guide. Reel/subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Records are arranged geographically and chronologically. Intended to provide the president with accurate information upon which to base decisions. Includes examples of cables, memos, biographical and situation reports covering the final years of French rule through the end of U. S. involvement. Covers Southeast Asia as a region plus the following countries: North Vietnam, South Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Thailand.  SUBJECTS: Vietnam, Southeast Asia; 20c

299. CORNELL UNIVERSITY. SOUTHEAST ASIAN CATALOG.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 8868  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 13 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: See Online Catalog for description of contents.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS:  SUBJECTS: Southeast Asia – Bibliography; Asia

300. PHILIPPINE CULTURE SERIES.  CALL NUMBER: University of Arizona Microcard 991.4  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:  INDEX OR GUIDE: All items in the collection are cataloged in the U of A card catalog under the series title, author, title subject.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: A collection of scarce books, journals and government documents dealing with the history, geography, ethnology, languages and culture of the Philippine Islands.  SUBJECTS: Philippines; Southeast Asia; 19c; 20c

301. PHILIPPINES (REPUBLIC). OFFICIAL GAZETTE OF THE PHILIPPINES, 1974-1984.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm DS 651 .P57  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 54 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: No index. There is a table of contents for each issue.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Contains text (in English) of laws; supreme court decisions; orders and regulations of administrative offices; and legal and official notices.  SUBJECTS: Philippines – Laws; 20c; Documents; Southeast Asia

302. PRESS CUTINGS ON ASIAN AFFAIRS: NORTH VIETNAM, 1963-1970.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 9106  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 11 R  INDEX OR GUIDE:  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Newspaper clippings, mostly in Asian languages. Includes occasional English and French articles. Filmed in monthly segments.  SUBJECTS: Vietnam – History; 20c; 1960s; Southeast Asia

303. SPECIAL STUDIES SERIES. VIETNAM AND SOUTHEAST ASIA, 1960-1980.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 7820  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 13 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 557.7 V545x Micro Service. Reel/subject index. Contains abstracts of reports.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Reports from various universities and “think tanks” on Southeast Asia as a region and on Cambodia, Laos and Vietnam. Deals primarily with the Vietnam War and the politics of both North and South Vietnam.  SUBJECTS: Vietnam; Southeast Asia; Laos; Cambodia; 20c

304. TRANSCRIPTS AND FILES OF THE PARIS PEACE TALKS ON VIETNAM, 1968-1973.  CALL NUMBER: Doc.Serv. Microfilm DS 559.7 T7x  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 12 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 559.7 T7x Guide. Reel/subject index. Separate index for topics raised by 1: U.S.-South Vietnam held peace talks in Paris in an attempt to end the Vietnam War. This collection contains complete verbatim transcripts and documents released by the State Department covering official conversations and plenary sessions. Topics include halt to bombing, handling of prisoners of war, withdrawal of forces, elections, etc.  SUBJECTS: Vietnam; Southeast Asia; 20c; 1960s; 1970s

305. U. S. ARMED FORCE IN VIETNAM, 1954-1975.  CALL NUMBER: Doc.Serv. DS 558 U54x 1983  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 21 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 558 U54x Guide (4 vols.). Each has table of contents and reel guide but no subject indexes.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Film is in 4 individually numbered parts: Pt. 1: Indochina Studies; Pt. 2: Vietnam, Lessons Learned; Pt. 3: Reports of U. S. Army Operations; Pt. 4: U. S. Army Senior Officer Debriefing Reports. Collection filmed from holdings of the Army Center for Military History and the National Technical Information Service. Covers military and political developments in Laos and Cambodia as well. Includes military strategic lessons, troop morale, Khmer Republic, details of specific military operations and campaigns and information on Vietnamese society.  SUBJECTS: Vietnam, Laos; Cambodia; Southeast Asia; 20c

306. U. S. LIBRARY OF CONGRESS SOUTHEAST ASIA SUBJECT CATALOG.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 8869  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 9 R  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Have same catalog in hard copy: Z 3221 U525 Folio (in sorting room on level 4). Separate country section for each nation in the area plus a section for the region.  SUBJECTS: Southeast Asia – Bibliography.

307. VIETNAM MICROFORM: WAR IN VIETNAM. CLASSIFIED HISTORIES BY THE NATIONAL SECURITY COUNCIL.  CALL NUMBER: Doc.Serv. Microfilm DS 558.2 V5x  NUMBER OR REELS/FICHE: 8 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 558.2 V5x Guide. Reel guide, no subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Covers Gulf of tonkin attacks; Honolulu Conference; Manila Conference; deployment of major U. S. forces. Based on 2,000 top-secret documents from the upper levels of the executive branch, including White House Situation Room reports, daily reports from Gen. Westmoreland, reports from U. S. ambassadors to Vietnam. Filmed from the holdings of the Lyndon Johnson Library.  SUBJECTS: Vietnam; Southeast Asia; 20c

308. VIETNAM: A DOCUMENTARY HISTORY. WESTMORELAND VS. CBS  CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 6246  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 1,010 F  INDEX OR GUIDE: KF 228 W42 W47 1987 Micro Ref. Lists the documents; contains tables of witnesses, exhibits, and court cases cited.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: General Westmoreland brought a libel suit against the CBS television network for its special “The Uncounted Enemy: A Vietnam Deception,” which accused the US military of conspiring to exaggerate enemy casualties. Contains 10,000 pages of trial transcripts; more than 30,000 pages of exhibits, including declassified government documents; depositions and affidavits from Vietnam era officials such as Robert McNamara, Dean Rusk and William Bundy; and pre-trial pleadings. The documents reveal information on military strategy, results of specific battles, and the political environment in which the war was fought. Includes the complete script of the program.  SUBJECTS: Vietnam; Southeast Asia; 20c
