SPACE SURVEY

Space Survey Website

The annual space survey is used in the University's annual 7-11 report to the Arizona Board of Regents, and for determining campus space management requirements. This report helps justify new building and renovation needs for the campus. The survey updates and verifies space inventory information which includes building and room number, room function, square footage, and occupancy information (both personnel and departmental).

In addition, the space survey is used for allocating Building Depreciation, Interest, Equipment Depreciation, and Operations & Maintenance expenses when preparing the University's Facilities & Administration Cost Rate (indirect Cost) proposal to the Federal Government. The negotiated F&A rate applied to sponsored projects results in the recovery and redistribution of F&A related costs to various university units. The survey collects the type of activity that takes place in each room by allocating the space on a percentage basis.

The information provided in this survey is also used for responding to other space related inquiries from within ASU and externally from the Federal Government, State Legislature, other universities, organizations, and the general public.
