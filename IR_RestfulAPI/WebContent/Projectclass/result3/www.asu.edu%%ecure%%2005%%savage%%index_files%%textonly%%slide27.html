Survey Results

Inclusion of supplementary materials

Almost 75% of authors who submitted

manuscripts did not respond to the question.

We assume that these authors did not submit

additional electronic materials. We might then

want to ask, when they began writing the

dissertation, did those authors envision the

document as a digital document with all its

attendant functionality or as a simple paper

document in digital form?
