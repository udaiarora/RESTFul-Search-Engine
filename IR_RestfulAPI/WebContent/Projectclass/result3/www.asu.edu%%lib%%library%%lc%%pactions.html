Emeritus Status


Academic professionals with continuing status who retire are automatically recommended for emeritus status which must be approved by the President. Written notification of the retirement date to the Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost initiates this process. For academic professionals without continuing status, or posthumous emeritus recommendations, please send a formal request from the chair and dean to the Executive Vice President and Provost. These must also be approved by the President.
