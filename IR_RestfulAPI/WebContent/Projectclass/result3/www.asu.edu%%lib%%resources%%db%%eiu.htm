Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU)

   

Database Information

Description:
Provides data and forecasts about political, economic, and business climates of various regions and up to 200 countries as well as related news, analysis, and risk factor assessments. Includes the online version of the Country Report series, the Country Profile series, Country Commerce, Country Finance, the Business Briefings series, the Economist, Global Outlook, and selected Country Forecast series.
Date Range:
News and analysis includes 1996 +, statistical data includes 1980 +, and forecasts start from the present and extend up to 5 years into the future.
Updated:
Daily
Includes:
Full text and data sets
Subject Coverage:
Advertising; Area Studies; Asian Studies; Business; Country Information; East European Studies; Economics; Latin American Studies; Marketing; Political Science; Russian Studies; Slavic Studies; Statistical Information
Types of Materials Covered:
Country analysis and risk factor assessments, data series and forecasts, newsletters, economist magazine, and business conditions advisories.
Geographic Scope:
United States and International
Content Provider:
Economist Intelligence Unit Limited
Alternate Methods of Access:
Print Equivalent:
  • Country Report for selected countries in print; see the ASU Libraries' Catalog for volume availability and location.
  • Country Profile for selected countries in print; see the ASU Libraries' Catalog for volume availability and location.
  • Country Commerce; see the ASU Libraries' Catalog for volume availability and location.
  • Country Finance; see the ASU Libraries' Catalog for volume availability and location.
  • Business Briefings newsletters for Asia (see the ASU Library Catalog for volume availability and location); Europe (see the ASU Library Catalog for volume availability and location); and Latin America (see the ASU Library Catalog for volume availability and location).
  • The Economist; see the ASU Library Catalog for volume availability and location.
  • Country Forecast for selected regions and countries; see the ASU Library Catalog for volume availability and location.
    Microform Equivalent:
  • E.I.U. Country Reports; see the ASU Library Catalog for volume availability and location.
    Software Requirements:
    Web browser
    Tips:
    When the links down the middle are not functioning properly (namely, asking for ID + password instead of allowing access), use the links in the navigation bar instead. Example: Use "Data Services" in the navigation bar instead of "CountryData" down the middle and then select "EIU Country Data" under "Data Services."
