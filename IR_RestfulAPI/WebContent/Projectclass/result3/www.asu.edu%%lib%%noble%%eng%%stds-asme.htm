ASME - American Society of Mechanical Engineers

  • ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Codes. New York, NY: ASME, 1989- .
    TJ 291 .A583x Science Reference


Standard rules for the construction of steam boilers and other pressure vessels. ASU has the 1995 edition.

 

ASTM - American Society for Testing and Materials

  • Annual Book of ASTM Standards. Philadelphia, PA: ASTM, 1970- .
    TA 401 .B6 Science Reference


ASTM publishes standard test methods, specifications, practices, guides, classifications and terminology. These are collected annually into a multi-volume set. Use the index volume (00.01) to locate your standard. Since the index is the last volume to be published, use the previous year's index to find what volume your standard is published in. ASU keeps the latest five years.

 

Building Codes


Online:

  • FirstSource Building Code Library
    Requires free registration to browse. Search by city or browse by state to find information on codes adopted by specific jurisdictions across the U.S. Provides contact information for each jurisdiction and links to the city web sites and to code organizations.

 

  • Arizona City Codes
    This list created by ASU's Architecture and Environmental Design Library provides links to all Arizona cities who have their codes available online. Scroll to the bottom of the "Arizona Links" section to see which cities are available.

IEEE - Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers

  • IEEE Xplore: IEEE/IEE Electronic Library (IEL)


Provides full-text to the most current editions of IEEE standards. Use "Browse" link to Standards to locate known standards. Search by title keywords or standard number in "Basic Search".
