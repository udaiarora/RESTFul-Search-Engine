Primary Sources in History: General


1.  

CBS NEWS DAILY NEWS BROADCASTS, 1975-
CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 S322
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:
INDEX OR GUIDE: Columbia Broadcasting System News Index (D 839.3 .C17x Ref. Table 2B).  A subject index to the news.
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Verbatim transcripts from television news broadcasts from 1980-1986.  Includes CBS Evening News, CBS Newsbreak, CBS Sunday Night New, 60 Minutes, CBS Reports and CBS News Special Reports.  Covers special events, such as assassination of President Kennedy.
SUBJECTS: Current Events; Modern History; 1970s; 20c; Television Broadcasting

2.  

ETHEL PERCY ANDRUS GERONTOLOGY CENTER. LIBRARY. CATALOGS.
CALL NUMBER: Microfilm HQ 1061 .E835x Ref.
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 4 R
INDEX OR GUIDE:
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: A film edition of the card catalog of a specialized library in the field of the aged and aging.  In effect, this is a comprehensive bibliography of monographs, research reports, conference proceedings, state and federal documents, agency publications and serials.  Coverage is interdisciplinary, including psychology, sociology, social work, biology, housing, environmental planning, health care and geriatric nursing.  Primary emphasis is on social gerontology.  Vol. 1: Author/title catalog; Vol. 2: Subject catalog.
SUBJECTS: Aging - Bibliography - Catalogs; Gerontology; Aged; Old Age

3.  

NAMES IN THE NEWS, 1988-
CALL NUMBER: CT 100 N36x
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:
INDEX OR GUIDE: CT 100 N36x Ref.  Index by name and vocation/profession.  Also indexed in a CD-ROM machine in reference.
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Contains biographical information from newspapers in 450 U. S. cities.
SUBJECTS: Biography; 20c

4.  

NATIONAL NEWSPAPER INDEX, 1979-
CALL NUMBER: Microfiche AI 3 .N22x Ref.
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Annual indexes to the following newspapers: New York Times, Wall Street Journal and Christian Science Monitor.  (Ask at Reference desk for additional indexes to these and other newspapers).
SUBJECTS: United States - Newspapers - Indexes; 20c.

5.  

NEWSBANK, 1977-
CALL NUMBER: Microform Service (see Online Catalog for call number for each series).
INDEX OR GUIDE: Newsbank Index. H 1 N53x Ref. Table 2B. There is also a CD-ROM index to recent years of Newsbank in the Reference Room.
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Covers wide range of current affairs: government and politics, welfare, consumers, business and economic development, arts, literature, transportation, urban affairs.  Consists of microfiche selections or clippings from newspapers around the country.  ASU has fiche for Names in the News section from 1988 on.  The other series start with 1977.
SUBJECTS: Current Events; Urban Affairs; Business Arts; 20c
