Letter of the Week

Dear President Crow:

Why is the change in the graduation fee structure going into effect as of February 1?  Why change things in mid-stream and why not wait until after the fee deadline for spring commencement?

Thank you for your e-mail regarding Arizona State University graduation fees.  Since commencement ceremonies are held in both the spring and the fall, there was unfortunately no single, ideal opportunity to implement the new fee structure.  It is my understanding that the new fee was originally going to be put into effect as of the first day of this semester, but it was pushed back in order to give the students who are scheduled to graduate this spring additional notice regarding the change.  I appreciate your advocacy on behalf of your fellow students and hope that you understand the factors that were involved in this change.

Again, thank you for taking the time to write and I wish you well with your studies this semester.
