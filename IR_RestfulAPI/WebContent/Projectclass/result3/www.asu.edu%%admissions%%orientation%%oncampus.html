Is ASU Orientation Required?
All incoming freshmen are expected to attend Orientation.

About the Program
ASU Orientation is designed to get you on track and keep you on track for graduation!

  • Learn about your major's critical tracking requirements.
  • Meet with an academic advisor and learn about your college's academic expectations.
  • Plan for and enroll in appropriate courses in the correct sequence for your major.
  • Learn about academic support programs to help you meet your academic goals.
  • Meet other students in your major.

Your Major: In order to provide you with the appropriate academic advising, it is important that we have the correct information about the major you have chosen. If the major displayed in your reservation confirmation is incorrect, or has changed, please contact New Student Orientation or submit an Orientation Change Request to update your major information at least 5 business days before your scheduled ASU Orientation program. Note: Requests for changes to the major are updated every 24 hours. If you have not already made your Orientation reservation, please contact New Student Orientation to change your major before making a reservation.

Check-In: Arrive early for your program to check in. Review and print the Parking and Directions information before attending your program.

Agenda:

  • Learn about your major's critical tracking requirements.
  • Meet with an academic advisor and learn about your college's academic expectations.
  • Plan for and enroll in appropriate courses in the correct sequence for your major.
  • Explore opportunities to get involved on campus.
  • Enjoy lunch with other students, parents and University staff.
  • Meet campus representatives and learn about university resources.

What Your Program Fee Includes: As part of your reservation for the on-campus ASU Orientation program:

  • You, the student, will receive group academic advising, an Orientation Student Handbook, an ASU General Catalog , Schedule of Classes , lunch, parking in designated lots and a Campus Tour.
  • Your guest reservation includes a special family session, an Orientation Family Guide, lunch, parking in designated lots and a campus tour.

AP, IB and CLEP Test Scores: In order to receive college credit for Advanced Placement (AP) courses, the International Baccalaureate (IB) program and/or College-Level Examination Program (CLEP), request that your official test scores be sent to ASU. Results from these exams will also be used by your academic advisor to place you in appropriate courses.

Program Dates and Fees
Dates: Orientation program availability varies by ASU campus and major. When you log into the Orientation Reservation System, you will see the list of available dates for your major.

Fees: ASU Orientation fees:

  • $60.00 per student
  • $40.00 per guest

Reservation Information and Reminders
Please be aware of the following important information for students and guests attending ASU Orientation:

  • Please do not secure travel arrangements and accommodations until you have received the online confirmation of your reservation.
  • Reservations will be accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • For the best opportunity to receive a preferred program date, be sure to make your reservation online three or more weeks before your earliest preferred date.
  • Reservations will be accepted beginning January 16, 2007.
  • In order for you to be provided with the appropriate academic advisement, it is important that ASU has the correct information about the major you have chosen. If your major has changed, please immediately contact New Student Orientation.
Upon successfully reserving online, you will receive an online confirmation. Be sure to print out your Orientation program confirmation, as well as Parking and Directions information upon completing your reservation. You will not be mailed confirmation information.

Program Payment
Students requesting the check payment option have 10 business days to remit their payment to New Student Orientation. If payment is not received in 10 business days, your ASU Orientation reservation will be forfeited. Your balance will still be due and your ASU Orientation reservation will need to be rescheduled.

Cancellations or Changes

  • If you would like to change your program date or cancel your ASU Orientation session, you must notify New Student Orientation in writing (by mail, fax or online) so that the cancellation or Orientation Change Request is received at least five business days before your scheduled Orientation date.
  • Each change to your program date or cancellation will be assessed a $15 processing fee.
  • A $15 processing fee will be charged for all returned checks and denied credit cards.
  • If you wish to add a guest or substitute a guest, you must submit your Orientation Change Request in writing or online .
  • Any Orientation refund requests will be processed in July 2007 upon conclusion of the ASU Orientation season with required documentation. Please note that in order to receive a refund for a cancellation you must submit your request in writing (by mail, fax or online) 5 business days before the date of the program you wish to cancel.

Special Needs
If you have special needs, call 480.965.2880 or e-mail orientation@asu.edu at least 10 business days before your scheduled program. Special needs include: disabled parking, assistance with tours, language interpretation and/or a special meal.
