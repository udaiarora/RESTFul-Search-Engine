WHAT'S NEW AND NEWS


To keep informed all those that are interested in new and exciting activities and new resources at the ASU University Libraries, I have created this page, specifically tailored, for the Political Science Department and our patrons interested in the Libraries' resources in political science.  Until I get a handle on just what is of interest to you, this may be a somewhat diffuse collection of news and new items.  If there are items you would like to see displayed here, please let me know.  I look forward to hearing from you.  --  Ed Oetting 


New ( and New-ish) Databases and Other Resources:


Berkeley Electronic Press Journals  Berkeley Electronic Press Journals is a database which includes scholarly articles in the fields of economics, business, marketing, law, science, technology, medicine, political science, current affairs, etc.  Of particular interest are the working papers, specifically working papers from a wide variety of institutes in the UC system.  Well worth checking out.


El Pais.es  This database contains the online version of the newspaper: El Pais.es (Daily and Sunday editions) in Spanish.


Global Views and Voices    (Articles, Debates, and Personal Testimony on the World Today)
The database includes more than 2,500 articles and 200 photo essays on more than 100 key topics of global relevance, written by more than 600 authors from 58 countries. Global Views and Voices juxtaposes the views of leading thinkers with local and personal perspectives on current events, globalization, media, development, culture, and the arts, from writers around the world. 


International Index to Black Periodicals Full Text (1910+) (Full text 1998+)  Covers cultural, economic, historical, religious, social, & political issues of relevant to the Black Studies discipline. Includes citations from scholarly and popular journals, newspapers and newsletters from the U.S., Africa & the Caribbean.


Russian Academy of Sciences Bibliographies  (1992+) Describes material on the social sciences and humanities published in the Commonwealth of Independent States and Eastern Europe including books, manuscripts, dissertations, and articles from more than 10,000 periodicals.


Statistical Theory and Methods Abstracts  Statistical Theory & Method Abstracts (STMA) provides worldwide coverage of published articles on mathematical statistics and probability. These abstracts provide valuable information on new developments in all fields of probability theory, estimation, linear models, stochastic processes, operations research, testing of hypothesis, experimental design and time series analysis.


Universal Database of Social Sciences and Humanities  Includes materials from newspapers, journals, government documents, dissertations, etc. from Russia. It covers not only Humanities and Social Sciences but also Science and Technology, Medicine, and Agriculture.

Sage Full-Text Collections (On Trial through 10/31/04)

The SAGE Full–Text Collections developed by CSA (Cambridge Scientific Abstracts) offer full-text access to the most popular journals in Communication Studies, Criminology, Politics and International Relations, and Sociology published by SAGE and participating societies. This includes more than 20,000 articles, book reviews, and editorials, with all the original graphics and tables.
