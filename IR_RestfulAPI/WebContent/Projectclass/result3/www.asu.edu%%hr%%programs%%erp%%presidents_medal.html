Eligibility

The President's Medal for Team Excellence recognized ASU classified and administrative staff, academic and service professionals, and faculty groups that demonstrated excellence in working together to improve the quality of university life, research, instruction, and/or partnership building.

This award recognized team efforts demonstrating a customer/stakeholder focus and utilizing a structured approach to problem solving. Any committee, task force, process improvement team, or other organized group sharing a common goal could apply for the President's Medal for Team Excellence. Teams could be permanent or ad hoc, and functional or cross-functional.
