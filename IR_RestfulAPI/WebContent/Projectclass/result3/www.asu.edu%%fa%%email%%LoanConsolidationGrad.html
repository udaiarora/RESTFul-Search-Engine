Save Money - Consolidate Your Loans Today

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this e-mail notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

Paying off your student loans may seem light years away, however, it is not too early to do something to reduce your loan debt before you ever make a payment. Apply through the Federal Family Education Loan Program (FFELP) for a consolidation loan with a FFELP lender.

What is consolidation? It is the combining of multiple student loans into one new loan with a lower fixed interest rate.

To apply for a consolidation loan we recommend you contact one of our preferred lenders:
