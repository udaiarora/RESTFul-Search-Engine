    • Importing References from GeoRef into RefWorks

    1. Go to "Favorites" and click on GeoRef

      If you have not yet saved GeoRef as a favorite:
      • Go to the Libraries' home page at http://www.asu.edu/lib/
      • Under the FIND category, click on ARTICLES
      • On the FIND ARTICLES page, click on G (for GeoRef)
      • Locate GeoRef in the list and click on it
      • Save the next page to your favorites


    2. Click on the GeoRef link to open database





    3. Do the Search




    4. Review Results




    5. "Mark" the Records of Interest by Click in the Box to Left of Record




    6. Click on the "Save" Icon




    7. Change from "Save to Disk" to "Export to RefWorks"



    8. If Needed, Make Changes in the Options




    9. Click on OK




    10. Sign-In to RefWorks




    11. Click on "View Last Imported Folder"




    12. Mark Records




    13. Move Reference(s) to Appropriate Folder





    14. If finished importing records to RefWorks, Log Out of RefWorks.
      If you will be doing more searching, leave RefWorks' window open, and return to GeoRef.
