USI 201–05: Registration and Drop/Add after the Published Deadline

Purpose

To allow an eligible student to register for or drop/add classes after the deadline for such transactions, but before the final transaction deadline for the semester, as published in the online University Registrar’s Office Semester Calendar

Source

University Registrar’s Office

Applicability

This policy applies to students who are:

  1. continuing from the previous semester

    or

  2. admitted or readmitted to ASU.

Policy

An eligible student may register for or drop/add classes after the published deadline with the appropriate approval.

A student who wishes to register for a semester for the first time after the deadline must:

  1. obtain advising sign-off if required by the university, college, or department

  2. obtain approval from the authorized designee of the college of the student’s major

  3. have the registration processed at any registrar site (the Records Information counter at the Tempe campus, Records & Registration at the West campus, or Registration Services at the Polytechnic or Downtown Phoenix campus) within five business days of the date signed

    and

  4. pay a $50 late fee for initial registration, along with the normal tuition and fees for the semester.

A student who wishes to drop/add classes after the deadline must:

  1. obtain advising sign-off, if required by the university, college, or department

  2. obtain approval from the authorized designee of the college offering the class(es)

    and

  3. have the drop/add processed at any registrar site (the Records Information counter at the Tempe campus, Records & Registration at the West campus, or Registration Services at the Polytechnic or Downtown Phoenix campus) within five business days of the date signed.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Student
  1. Complete an Initial Registration Request form or a Drop/Add Request form.

  2. Obtain approval to register for or drop/add a class from the appropriate college.
If approved:
Dean or designee
  1. Sign and date the form in the space provided.
Student
  1. Take the form and a photo ID to any registrar site (the Records Information counter at the Tempe campus, Records & Registration at the West campus, or Registration Services at the Polytechnic or Downtown Phoenix campus) for processing within five business days of obtaining the dean’s approval.
University Registrar’s Office staff
  1. Process the Initial Registration Request form or the Drop/Add Request form. Print the schedule/billing statement.
Student
  1. Remain at the site to receive the schedule/billing statement. Review the statement for accuracy.

  2. Pay any fees by using the ASU Interactive Web site.

    Note:For specific information on fee payment in person, contact the Tuition Payment Office on the second floor of the Student Services Building.

endtable
