Site Home >Summer Conference Assistants

Summer Conference Assistants (SCAs) are experienced, involved ASU students who live throughout the residence halls during the summer. They maintain safety and security in the halls as well as assist summer conference groups during their stay. SCAs also facilitate check-in and check-out for conference guests. An SCA is confident, enthusiastic, friendly and dedicated to making a guest’s visit to ASU enjoyable.

What do Summer Conference Assistants do?

Build a community where guests feel comfortable and welcome
Check-in and Check-out paperwork
Room inspections
Address policy violations
Crisis intervention
Night/weekend community walks
Weekly desk shifts
Attend required staff meetings and training sessions

Serve as a welcoming presence as well as resource for a diverse group of guests

What are the requirements?

Have a minimum GPA of 2.5 at time of application and while holding the position.
Be a current/returning student of Arizona State University or taking summer classes at the Tempe campus

Not exceed more than 10 hours per week of outside activities including but not limited to: classes, internships, practicum, clubs, or part-time jobs

Be in good standing with Residential Life, Arizona State University and Student Life

Must live in assigned residence hall for the entirety of employment
Who is an ideal candidate?

An ideal candidate:

  • Can take charge in situations that may arise
  • Is highly personable, motivated, and organized
  • Possess exception communication skills
  • Is familiar with University resources
  • Is professional
  • Works well individually and in team environments
  • Is accomplished in time management and multi-tasking

What’s in it for you?

Summer Conference Assistants:

  • Gain a greater level of confidence and professionalism by working with people of various backgrounds
  • Grow professionally and socially in their interaction with guests and staff
  • Develop leadership and communication skills
  • Are compensated a single room (may have to share bath with suite/floor mates) in their assigned residence hall
  • Up to 5 hours per week compensation for administrative duties and 15 hours per week at the desk at the following pay rates:
  • $5.15/hr: 8 a.m. to 10 p.m.
    $6.15/hr: 10 p.m. to 2 a.m.
    $7.15/hr: 2 a.m. to 8 a.m.

PLEASE NOTE: Total compensation package may affect your financial aid status, please check with your financial aid counselor.

Recruitment Process:

Interested applicants need to complete and submit a Summer Conference Assistant application (including attached questions) and submit to the Residential Life office located in the Student Services Building, room 170. Applicants should fully review and complete application to be considered. Please see application for deadline.
