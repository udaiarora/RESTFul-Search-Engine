Chronological History of BBA


Current titles are in bold; dates are in parentheses

Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (1947-1963)

Split into 6 sections:

  1. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta - General Subjects (1964-)

  2. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Specialized Section on Nucleic Acids and Related Subjects (1962-1964)
    Changed title to:
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Nucleic Acids and Protein Synthesis (1963-1981)
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Gene Structure and Expression (1982-)

  3. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Specialized Section on Lipids and Related Subjects (1963-1964)
    Changed title to:
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Lipids and Lipid Metabolism (1965-1998)
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Molecular and Cell Biology of Lipids (1998-)

  4. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Specialized Section on Enzymological Subjects (1963-1964)
    Changed title to:
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Enzymology and Biological Oxidation (1965-1966)
    Split into:
    • Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Enzymology (1967-1981)
    • Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Protein Structure (1967-1981)
    Recombined into:
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Protein Structure and Molecular Enzymology (1982-2002)
    Changed title to:
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Proteins and Proteomics (2002-)

  5. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Specialized Section on Biophysical Subjects (1964)
    Changed title to:
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Biophysics including Photosynthesis (1965-1966)
    Split into
    • Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Bioenergetics (1967-)
    • Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Biomembranes (1967-)

  6. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Specialized Section on Mucoproteins and Mucopolysaccharides (1964)
    Changed title to:
    Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Mucoproteins and Mucopolysaccharides (1965)
    Ceased


New Sections Added:

  1. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Reviews on Biomembranes (1972-2002)
    Ceased

  2. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Reviews on Bioenergetics (1973-1987)
    Ceased

  3. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Reviews on Cancer (1974-)

  4. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Molecular Cell Research (1982-)

  5. Biochimica et Biophysica Acta (BBA) - Molecular Basis of Disease (1990-)
