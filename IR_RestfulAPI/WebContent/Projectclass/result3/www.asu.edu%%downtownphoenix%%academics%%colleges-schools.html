Colleges and Schools

at the Downtown Phoenix campus

ASU at the Downtown Phoenix campus is a unique blend of learning opportunities that include:

  • Basic and exploratory undergraduate education, through University College
  • Nursing and research, at the College of Nursing and Health Innovation
  • Public administration and social work, through the College of Public Programs
  • Journalism and broadcastin,g through the Walter Cronkite School of Journalism & Mass Communication (coming in 2008).

These programs share an important common purpose; they emphasize a connection between knowledge, theory and practical application, infusing intellectual energy into a new creative learning environment.

This environment, in turn, will serve as an incubator for proactive community involvement and civic participation. It brings the intellectual laboratory of an urban campus to the streets and avenues of an emerging downtown community.

For detailed information on the anchor Downtown colleges, schools, specific departments and centers, visit their individual websites.
