Time/Mileage Schedule

Motor pool vehicles (daily rentals) may be replaced every 4 years or 75,000 miles, whichever comes first.

Service vehicles (pickup trucks and vans) may be replaced every 8 years or 75,000 miles, whichever comes first.

Other passenger vehicles may be replaced every 5 years or 75,000 miles, whichever comes first.

Emergency Vehicles may be replaced every 3 years or 75,000 miles, whichever comes first.
