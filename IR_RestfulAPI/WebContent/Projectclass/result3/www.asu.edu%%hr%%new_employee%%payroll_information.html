Human Resources - New Employee

Payroll Information

Payroll forms are handled by the ASU HR Employee Service Center located in the University Services Building. Call (480) 965-2701 with questions. HR Employee Service Center Hours: Monday - Friday 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Your Hiring Department must submit your Hiring Process Report (HPR) and you must complete and submit the payroll paperwork before you can be paid.

Please complete the New Employee Payroll Packet forms and gather the I-9 verification/ identification documents you will need. (A list of acceptable documents is listed on the back of the I-9 form.) You must submit these forms and complete the verification of your Employment Eligibility Verification Form (I-9) within 72 hours of your employment start date. You cannot be paid until your I-9 is received and verified.

You may submit your completed payroll forms to the HR Customer Service Center before your start date. Submit your payroll forms in one of three ways:

1)  Bring the completed forms and acceptable I-9 documentation with you to your scheduled session of Orientation, or

2)  Bring the completed forms and acceptable I-9 documentation with you to the HR Employee Service Center located in the University Services Building, or

3)  You may mail notarized I-9 form, along with copies of verification/indentification documents and completed payroll forms to HR Employee Service Center, ASU, P.O. Box 875612, Tempe, AZ 85287-5612.

Payroll Experts at Orientation!  During the first 30 minutes of Orientation, representatives from the ASU HR Payroll office will be on hand to expedite the payroll forms process and answer any questions new employees may have regarding I-9 verification. Forms for optional direct deposit of your paycheck will be available.
