ASU Libraries' Document Delivery Service
Support Services for ASU Students and Staff with Disabilities

  • Materials are requested on-line.
  • Materials are typically ready in 2 to 3 days.

Book Retrieval

  • No charge for retrieval of books owned by ASU Libraries, including Hayden, Noble, Music, Law, Architecture, West, and Polytechnic.
  • Books can be picked up at the Library Express window or delivered to any Tempe campus address free of charge.
  • Book Requests may be made online.
    There are two ways to request books online:
    1. Fill out the online Book Request Form.
    2. Request the book directly from the book's record in the ASU Library catalog by using the "Request" button, located at the top and bottom of each book record.
    To receive the ADA rate, type "DRS student" or "ADA student" in the note/delivery address field.

Photocopy Requests

  • Photocopies of journal articles are available at a reduced rate.
  • $1.00 for each 1-10 pages per article
  • 2 articles of 2 pages each = $2.00
  • Individuals with print disabilities must clearly mark photocopy requests with desired format of the photocopy. (For example, "One page per copy" or "For use on Kurzweil.")
  • Photocopies can be picked up and paid for at the Library Express window, or delivered to any Tempe campus address for individuals who have established an account.
  • To establish a Library Express account:
    Deposit money, cash or check, at the Library Express window. Self-identify as individual with disability to receive the ADA rate.
  • Photocopy Requests may be made online: Photocopy Request Form
    To receive the ADA rate, type "DRS student" or "ADA student" in the note/delivery address field.

Hours for The Library Express window

Monday to Friday 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Materials can be picked up at the Circulation Desk during other library hours.

Location of The Library Express window

Hayden Library, Concourse level at the Interlibrary Loan/Library Express window - the third window on the left before the stairs to Reference.
