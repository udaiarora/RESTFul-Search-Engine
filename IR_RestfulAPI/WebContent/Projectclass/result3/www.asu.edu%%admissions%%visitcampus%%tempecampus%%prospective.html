Apply to ASU
Applying to ASU is an important step for your future. If you have not already done so, please review ASU's undergraduate admission requirements and application timelines for incoming freshmen and apply!

Students who have applied to the university should hear about their admission status in 4-6 weeks. You may check your admission status through ASU Interactive, ASU's online system allowing you access to student services.
