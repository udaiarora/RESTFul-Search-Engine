USI 301–07: Summer Financial Aid

Purpose

To determine aid availability for summer sessions and to set eligibility criteria

Sources

34 Code of Federal Regulations §§ 668, 668.32(f), 674, 675.25 (a)(b), 676, 682, 685, and 690

Federal Student Aid Handbook

Policy

For purposes of awarding summer financial aid, the summer semester is considered a trailer to the previous academic year. Students will be considered for the following aid programs (until such time as funding for these programs is exhausted) on a first-come, first-served basis:

  1. Federal Pell Grants

  2. ASU grants

  3. university grants

  4. Student Aid Trust Grants

  5. Federal Work-Study

  6. Federal Perkins Loans

  7. Federal Parent PLUS Loans

    and

  8. tribal scholarships.

Summer financial aid information is available on the Summer Aid Web site.

Cross-References

For more information on specific kinds of financial aid, see USI 302–01, “Emergency Short-Term Loans.”

Also see:

  1. USI 303, Scholarships

    and

  2. USI 304 through 305, the Student Employment policies.
