Fee Structure

Team Challenge is FREE to student organizations and academic
classes (Certain situations may warrant a small fee being assessed, due to
resources used. This can be discussed at the time of registration.

  • Student Organizations / Academic Classes: None
  • Faculty/staff : $40/hour
  • ASU Program (student outreach) : $25/hour
  • ASU Program (professional development) : $40/hour
  • Costs do not reflect if the Team Challenge is off campus; there is travel for facilitators involved.

Team Challenge Equipment may be rented by anyone who has been facilitator trained. Costs are included on the inventory checkout sheet. If you are interested in renting equipment, please fax the sheet to Stefani at 965-2210 or call her at 965-8909.

Cancelation Policy

We are excited for the opportunity to work with your group. There are circumstances where Team Challenges must be rescheduled and we are committed to working with your organization to do the best we can to serve you. In an effort to cover costs of our facilitators, reservations, scheduling and administrative services, there is a $20 cancellation fee (or 10% of the entire fee, whichever is greater) associated to Team Challenges that:

  • Are a “no show” by participants
  • Have less than anticipated amount of attendees by 50%
  • Cancel within 24 hours of the scheduled day/time
  • Rescheduled Team Challenges within 24 hours of the scheduled day/time

Cancellation fees will not be incurred if there is a rescheduling of the Team Challenge prior to 24 hours before the scheduled Team Challenge.

Multiple cancellations will result in a higher cancellation fee. We reserve the right to refuse service.

Cancellations can be paid at the 1st floor SRC Administration Office by:

  • Cash
  • Check (made payable to: ASU)
  • Credit card (visa and MasterCard only)
  • Purchase order (acct ID: studentrec).

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact Stefani at 480-965-8909
