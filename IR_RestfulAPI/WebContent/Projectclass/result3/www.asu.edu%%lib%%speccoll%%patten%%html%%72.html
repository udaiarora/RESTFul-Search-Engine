Mattioli, Pietro Andrea

1500-1577.

[Compendium de plantis omnibus].

De plantis epitome utilissima, Petri Andreae Matthioli,
senensis, medici excellentissimi, &c..

Novis iconibus et descriptionibus pluribus nunc primum diligenter aucta,
a D. Joachimo Camerario. . .; accessit catalogus plantarum, quae in hoc
compendio continentur, exactiss. Francofurti ad Moenum: Sigmund Feyerabend,
1586. First editing by Camerarius of the author's Compendium de plantis
omnibus (Venice: Valgrisi, 1571). The German STC assigns responsibility
to Sigmund Feyerabend, the Hunt Botanical Catalogue to Johnann Feyerabend.
"Iter Baldi civitatis Veronae montis" by F. Calzolari, with separate t.p.:
p. [1]- [17] (4th group). Another issue (?), described in the Hunt Botanical
Catalogue (no. 153), has t.p. with more lengthy other title information,
including mention of Calzolari's work. Signatures Q and R bound in reverse
order. References: BM STC German, 1455-1600; Hunt 153 (variant).

The manuscript annotations throughout are in at least two hands. This
1536 edition of PAT-71 includes the section on the plants of Monte Baldo,
missing from PAT-71.  This section on Monte Baldo does not have any
illustrations and is written in essay form, rather than individual plant
entries as are found in the main work.

Subjects:
Botany--Pre-Linnean works; Medicine--Early works to 1800; Italy
