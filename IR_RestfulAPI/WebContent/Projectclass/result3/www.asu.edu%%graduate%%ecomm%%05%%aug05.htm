Congratulations to Associate Dean Marjorie Zatz!

Associate Dean Marjorie Zatz has accepted the position of Vice Provost for Academic Personnel in the Provost’s Office and left the Division of Graduate Studies (DGS) on August 1, 2005. During her four plus years at DGS, her work on behalf of graduate education and graduate students at ASU has been outstanding and her accomplishments many. A search is currently underway for her replacement.

Check out more of the latest DGS news at www.asu.edu/graduate/aboutus/news.

— REMINDERS —

ASU Health Insurance. The deadlines to register for ASU Health Insurance via SunDial or the Interactive website are September 2 for the fall semester and February 3 for the spring semester. For more information, go to: www.asu.edu/health/insurance.html.

New Graduate Student Reception. DGS is hosting an ice cream social on Wednesday, August 17, at 5:00 p.m. on the Student Services Lawn to welcome new graduate students to ASU. Representatives from a variety of departments and student organizations will be in attendance to provide information. All new graduate students are encouraged to attend.

TA/RA Information on gPortal.
  • New and continuing TAs and RAs for 2005-2006 AY must be entered through our gPortal system to receive their tuition remission and health insurance (if applicable).

  • If you are offering an assistantship to a student enrolled in an academic unit other than your own, you will need to contact his/her "home" department to enter the assistantship information.

  • Remember to fill out the Hiring Process Report (HPR), available at www.asu.edu/hr/forms/hpr.doc for any new TAs and RAs that you are hiring.

  • If you do not currently have access to manage TA and RA benefits, please have your direct supervisor send an email request to grad-access@asu.edu.
Social and Academic Mentoring Program (SAM). There are still research assistantships (8%) available for advanced graduate students who are able to mentor new incoming graduate students during their first year of graduate school. For more information, go to: www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/SAM.

UGEM Research Assistantships. UGEM research assistantships are still available for first-year competitive, underrepresented graduate students. UGEM grants are nomination-based and DGS matches the academic unit's contribution to make it a 50%-time research assistantship. For more information, go to: www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/ugem.

Graduate Tuition Fellowships. All fellowships (GTFs) have been committed for academic year 2005-06.
