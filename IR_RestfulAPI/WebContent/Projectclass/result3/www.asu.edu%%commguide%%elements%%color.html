PRIMARY COLORS
ASU’s primary colors are maroon and gold. These two colors should be present in all applications, since they are the principle color-based elements of the ASU brand. ASU gray (along with black and white) complete the ASU logo color palette. These may be used as appropriate either in representing the logo or as secondary colors (see below).
