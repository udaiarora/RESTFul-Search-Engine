Bosnian/Croatian/Serbian Language Courses

Courses

The 2007 CLI will offer Introductory and Intermediate BCS courses (subject to funding and sufficient enrollment). Following the courses we offer a three-week overseas practicum at Azbukum in Novi Sad, Serbia & Montenegro. There is also an academic-year program available.  The eight-week introductory course is comprised of two classes: BCS 101: Elementary Serbo-Croatian I and BCS 102: Elementary Serbo-Croatian II, for a total of 8 semester credit hours.  Elementary Serbo-Croatian I (or its equivalent) is a pre-requisite for Elementary Serbo-Croatian II.  The nine-week intermediate course is comprised of two classes: BCS 201: Intermediate Serbo-Croatian I and BCS 202: Intermediate Serbo-Croatian II, for a total of 8 semester credit hours.  Elementary Serbo-Croatian (or its equivalent) is a pre-requisite for Intermediate Serbo-Croatian.

Course Schedule: 4-July 27, 2007, Monday to Friday, 8:30 am to 12:30 pm., ASU Main Campus.

Practicum Schedule: July 30-August 17, 2007, Novi Sad, Serbia
