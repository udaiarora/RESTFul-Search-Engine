The ASU Brand: Brand Essence

Understanding the ASU brand means understanding all of the elements that make up the brand. To define ASU’s brand, we referred to two definitive ASU documents, A New American University and One University in Many Places. These white papers describe who ASU is and how we approach our mission. Distilling these documents down into our aggregate promise as an institution, through research, focus groups, and testing helped determine ASU’s Brand Essence or promise in its simplest form:

Arizona State University is a vanguard.

While we may never use the word “vanguard” in our communications, we do need to convey the essence of a vanguard. This can be done by using synonyms and language that instills the same message.

Some examples include:

Leader
Entrepreneurial
Thought leader

Risk taker
Bench marker 
Groundbreaker

Swift
Flagship
Leading the charge   

Change agent
Cutting edge
Edgy
