USI 602–01: Mental Health Emergencies

Purpose

To provide assistance to students experiencing mental health emergencies

Sources

University policy

Policy

Mental Health Emergencies Occurring during Working Hours (8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday)

Members of the university community may call any of the counseling centers and request emergency assistance. See the counseling centers’ Web pages for detailed information specific to Counseling and Consultation at the Tempe campus, Student Counseling & Health Services at the West campus, and Student Counseling Services at the Polytechnic campus.

Please note that callers may be referred to EMPACT at 480/921–1006.

Mental Health Emergencies Occurring after Hours (5:00 p.m. to 8:00 a.m., Monday through Friday, or Any Time on Weekends or Holidays)

Residence hall students should contact a member of the Residential Life staff, who will assist them in accessing campus services. ASU students living off campus may call the EMPACT crisis hotline at 480/921–1006.

After-hours emergency assistance is available for all campuses. For information specific to each counseling center, see Counseling and Consultation at the Tempe campus, Student Counseling & Health Services at the West campus, and Student Counseling Services at the Polytechnic campus. Emergency assistance may also be accessed by calling 911.

Cross-Reference

For more information about services offered by ASU counseling centers, see USI 602–02, “Eligibility for Services.”
