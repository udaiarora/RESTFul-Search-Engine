Questions and Answers

Q: How do entering 2007-08 freshmen apply for the ASU Advantage?

A: Students only need to:
1) Complete the ASU Admissions Application, and
2) Complete the 2007-08 Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).

Q: What is the general eligibility criteria to participate in this program?

A: If you meet the following criteria, you will be eligible to participate:

  • Arizona resident.
  • 2007 Arizona high school graduate.
  • Admission to ASU as a degree-seeking freshman for the fall 2007 semester.
  • Demonstration of total family income of $25,000 or less on your FAFSA.
  • Full-time enrollment at any of ASUâ??s campuses in fall 2007 and full-time enrollment for seven consecutive semesters thereafter (fall and spring).

Q: How is the $25,000 total family income determined?

A: A student's total family income must be equal to or less than $25,000, as documented on the student's FAFSA for that award year.

Q: What is the maximum number of semesters a student can participate in the program?

A: Program participation extends for eight (8) full-time, consecutive semesters.

Q: Do I have to be enrolled full-time to be eligible for this program?

A: Yes. Full-time enrollment as of census for each fall and spring semester is required.

Q: How will I be notified if I am eligible for this program?

A: Participation and Renewal Criteria will be sent to eligible students with their 2007-08 Financial Aid Notification.
