Commencement Memories
Photos, Videos, Flowers & Announcements

PHOTOGRAPHER
Chappell Graduation Images
1-800-424-3686

Chappell Graduation Images will be available in the east tunnel for Master and JD candidate portraits and in the west tunnel for Doctoral and Bachelor candidate portraits 75 minutes prior to the start of the ceremony.

Chappell offers graduates the opportunity to have quality portraits taken with absolutely no obligation to purchase. Within one week, a complimentary color proof will be mailed to you. You may then choose to purchase enlargements.

Videos
Videos and DVDs of the University Commencement Ceremony may be purchased through Great Video Productions. Please check with the dean's office of your college about video and DVDs for college convocation ceremonies.

Flowers
Congratulatory bouquets of roses and carnations will be available at commencement from the ASU Student Foundation. All proceeds from these sales are used for ASU student scholarships and leadership development.

Celebrate the graduation of your friends as well as yourself and help raise funds for student scholarships. Booths are located inside the four main entrances to the arena.

Annoucements
Personalized graduation announcements may be purchased online through CB Graduation Announcements.
