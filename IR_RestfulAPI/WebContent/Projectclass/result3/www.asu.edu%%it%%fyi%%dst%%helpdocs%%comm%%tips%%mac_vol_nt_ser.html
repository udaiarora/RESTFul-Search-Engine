MAC Volume and NT Problems

I'd like to thank all who offered suggestions to the solution of my problem with MAC users not being able to see their files on an NT server. Problem began with NT 3.51; I loaded the latest patches and went through the workaround offered with Q147909 - to no avail. I upgraded the server to NT 4.0 (service patch 3 was supposed to automatically take care of the problem - it didn't). BUT, upon going through the workaround mentioned above, the problem was taken care of after step two.

  1. Stop services for Macintosh.
  2. From a DOS prompt type Dir > D:"macshare:AFP_IdIndex (if macshare is the directory name).
  3. Restart services for Macintosh.

Works for me!!

Ted Richardson
