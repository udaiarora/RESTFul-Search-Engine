E-Communiqué
Vol. 2.4, November 9, 2004

The E-Communiqué is published monthly with notices of upcoming deadlines, clinics and seminars, and other matters related to graduate education at ASU. Please visit the Graduate Calendar or the What's New page for information on events not included in this bulletin.

DGS LAUNCHES NEW FELLOWSHIP PROGRAM!

DGS is pleased to announce the launching of a university fellowship program. We are able to provide a limited number of awards that will help ASU recruit, retain, and graduate top graduate students.
  • DGS Dissertation Fellowships: competitive university-wide fellowships for highly meritorious doctoral students in the final stages of post-candidacy doctoral work. www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/DGSdissfellowship

    DGS Doctoral Enrichment Fellowships: competitive university-wide fellowships designed to enhance recruitment of high quality doctoral students who will contribute to the diversity of ASU's graduate student population. www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/DGSenrichfellowship

  • Reach for the Stars Fellowships: competitive university-wide fellowships designed to promote the diversity of the graduate student population by providing ASU's most promising undergraduates with a pathway to graduate school and the tools and resources for their success. www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/ReachForStars
— UPDATES —
Graduate Admissions policy change: Doctoral students who have an approved Leave of Absence prior to their departure and are re-applying to the same program are exempt for the application fee. They will need to re-apply online, indicate the fee will be paid by check, submit the application, and notify Graduate Admissions at (480) 965-6113. www.asu.edu/graduate/admissions/domestic.html

Student Academic Services (SAS) has revised the following forms:

  • Report of Doctoral Comprehensive Exams & Approval of the Ph.D. Dissertation Prospectus
  • Master’s Defense Schedule Form
    (formerly, Authorization for Scheduling a Master’s Defense)
  • Doctoral Defense Schedule Form (formerly, Doctoral Dissertation Defense Recommendation Form)
Please make sure that your faculty and students use these new versions. www.asu.edu/graduate/forms
