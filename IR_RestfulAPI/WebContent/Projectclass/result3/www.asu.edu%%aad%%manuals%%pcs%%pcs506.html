PCS 506: Off-Campus Loans of Equipment to Agencies

Purpose

To control off-campus loans to agencies of university- and sponsor-owned equipment

Source

Property Control

Applicability

All moveable university- and sponsor-owned equipment having been assigned an ASU property control number (ASU PC#)

Policy

Equipment may be loaned off campus to state or non-state agencies only if it is to be used at the agency for university or sponsored purposes (unless it can be demonstrated that the university would benefit from the loan) or used by a subcontractor.

Removal of Equipment from Campus

Equipment may be removed from the university premises only for university or sponsored project use. It may not be removed for personal use. There are no exceptions. Temporary off-campus loans may not exceed two years in duration and must be renewed biannually.
