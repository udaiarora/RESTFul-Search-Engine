Announcements

Walker speaks on Phoenix's "Faces of Diversity" series

Asst. Director Thomas Walker was invited to speak January 19th, as part of the "Faces of Diversity" brown bag series, sponsored by the Phoenix Human Relations Commission and the Equal Opportunity Dept. The ongoing series asks Valley residents to share their stories about diversity in the community. Walker's story, focused on his experience around gender, was titled "Mama's Boy meets Man of the House: The make-up of one modern man," and will be broadcast on Phoenix Channel 11, the city's government access station.

Murphy facilitates at South African conference

Program Coordinator, Sr., Teri Murphy is two evening dialogues as part of the Memory, Narrative & Forgiveness conference in Cape Town, South Africa. The conference brings together international scholars and activists for 'Reflecting on 10 Years of South Africa's Truth and Reconciliation Commission' and ongoing work around the world in intergroup justice, healing and peace-building.
