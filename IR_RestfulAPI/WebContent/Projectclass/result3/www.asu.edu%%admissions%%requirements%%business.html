Freshman Admission Requirements
 

Pre-business Program

•  Meet ASU's general freshman admission requirements

•  and submit your SAT Reasoning or ACT score by August 31, 2007

Pre-business students apply to the Professional Program in the sophomore year for the junior/senior-level courses.
 

Dean's Select

Students who meet the following criteria are admitted as Dean's Select students and are guaranteed admission to the Professional Program in their first choice of major when they apply to the Professional Program in their sophomore year. To maintain this guarantee, students must achieve a 3.5 cumulative GPA and a 3.5 skill course GPA at ASU when they apply to the Professional Program in the sophomore year.

•  3.5 GPA or top 25% of high school class

•  and 1200 SAT Reasoning or 26 ACT score

•  and 3.0 transfer GPA in college courses taken prior to high school graduation
 

Carey Direct

Business freshmen who meet the following criteria are admitted directly to the professional program in the major of their choice in the W. P. Carey School of Business. These students do not have to apply to the Professional Program in their sophomore year.

•  3.6 GPA

•  and 1250 SAT Reasoning or 28 ACT score

•  and 3.5 transfer GPA in college courses taken prior to high school graduation
 

See also the ASU General Catalog.

Transfer Admission Requirements
 

Transfer students with 30 or more transferable college credits after high school graduation who meet the following criteria will be admitted to the Pre-business program:

•  3.0 transfer GPA

•  and 1040 SAT Reasoning or 22 ACT

With fewer than 30 transferable college credits earned after high school graduation:

•  3.0 transfer GPA

•  and 1040 SAT Reasoning or 22 ACT

•  and meet freshman admission requirements

Transfer students apply to the Professional Program in the sophomore year for the junior/senior-level courses.

 

See also the ASU General Catalog.
