Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

December 4, 2002

Members Present: Patricia Feldman, Sue Hunter, Stefan Myhajlenko, Barbara Trapido-Lurie
Members Absent: Carol Behl, Victoria Trotta 

1. The Minutes from the November 13, 2002 meeting were formally approved. They will appear on the CAPS website shortly.  

2. The minutes from Gaye Murphy?s November Spotlight Speaker presentation were reviewed and 3 minor edits were added and then approved.  They will appear on the CAPS website shortly.

 3. OLD BUSINESS

There has been no response to the letter forwarded by CAPS to the Academic Senate President regarding Academic Associate representation.

 4. NEW BUSINESS

(a) The faculty promotion and tenure (P&T) process is undergoing a review by the University at President Crow?s request.  Patty will inquire as to the applicability of these changes to the AP community and whether there ought to be an AP representative on the University-wide Task Force Committee being constituted by the Vice Provost?s office to explore this matter.

(b) Patty alerted CAPS that the committee would be asked to assist with ACD manual, AP policy section revisions, sometime next year.

(c) Spring-03 Spotlight Speaker: It was suggested that we might ask Gail Hackett to lead an AP Open Forum once the direction of several new University initiatives have become clearer.

The meeting was adjourned at 10:45 a.m.

Respectfully submitted, Stefan Myhajlenko

Minutes were approved 12/5/2002
