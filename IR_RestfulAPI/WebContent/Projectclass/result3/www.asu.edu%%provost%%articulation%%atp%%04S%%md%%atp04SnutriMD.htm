    ATP NOTES

    1. Six credit hours taken in three Awareness areas are required for the four-year degree. One course may satisfy more than one Awareness requirement simultaneously. It is strongly recommended that at least one of the three Awareness area requirements be met before transfer. See the current CEG or an advisor for recommended coursework to meet these requirements.
    2. It is strongly recommended that students see the current CEG or an advisor for recommended courses that concurrently satisfy Humanities and Fine Arts [HU] requirements, or Social Behavioral Sciences [SB] requirements and at least one of the three Awareness requirements.
