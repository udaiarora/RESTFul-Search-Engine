Get ready Sun Devils, because you're invited to Homecoming 2007!
ASU Homecoming Festdevil

Arizona State University is entering a new era; filled with school spirit and tradition. As you bring your student to ASU for the first time, you return as an Alumni, or visit ASU as a supportive member of our ASU Community; Homecoming is a time for us all to join together and celebrate our common bond: ASU school spirit. From our traditional Lantern Walk to our amazing Parade, Homecoming is full of activities for the entire family. So clear your calendar, invite the entire family out, make any necessary travel plans, and get ready for the amazing week of October 22-27th, 2007.

In Sun Devil Spirit,

Neal Stephenson
