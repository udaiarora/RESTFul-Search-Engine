Increasing Your Quotas


AFS

Requests for up to 50 MB (total) can be made in person at the IT Help Desk, over the phone by calling 480-965-6500, or by submitting a question.

Requests for more than 50 MB must be submitted in writing to the IT Help Desk using the Request for Computer Access form. Students must get a Faculty or Classified Staff sponsor signature on all requests over 50 MB.

IMAP Mailbox (WebMail)

The default IMAP mailbox quota for students, faculty and staff is 10 MB. They may self-select their own quotas by logging into myASU, clicking on EMMA, clicking on Quota Check, selecting the desired quota, then clicking the Change button. Students can select quotas of 10 MB, 20 MB, 30 MB or 50 MB. Faculty and staff can select up to 250 MB.

Exchange Mailbox (Outlook)

The default Exchange mailbox quota is 250 MB. Requests are reviewed on an individual basis by the Web and Messaging Applications Team . For further information, submit a question.
