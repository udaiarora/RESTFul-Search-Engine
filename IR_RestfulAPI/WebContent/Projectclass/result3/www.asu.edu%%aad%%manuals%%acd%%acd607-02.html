ACD 607–02: Contribution to Social Security

Purpose

To specify mandatory Social Security contributions and requirements for obtaining Social Security numbers

Source

Arizona Revised Statutes §§ 38–702 to –703

Applicability

All faculty and academic professionals, except nonresident aliens with “F” or “J” visas

Policy

All eligible faculty and academic professionals are required to contribute to Social Security and to obtain Social Security numbers, as indicated in the chart below.

ProcedureD

IfThen

Faculty member or academic professional has no Social Security number and is not exempt from federal and state taxesHuman Resources:
