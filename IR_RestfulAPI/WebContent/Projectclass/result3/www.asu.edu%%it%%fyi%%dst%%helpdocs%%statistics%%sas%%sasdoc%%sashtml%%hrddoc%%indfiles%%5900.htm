SAS Technical Report A-102, SAS Regression Applications

A survey of regression techniques, this report is illustrated with examples of SAS regression procedures. It is designed to supplement regression textbooks by providing examples that work through the computations. Much of the material assumes that the reader is acquainted with matrix algebra, basic statistics, and calculus.

158 pages.
