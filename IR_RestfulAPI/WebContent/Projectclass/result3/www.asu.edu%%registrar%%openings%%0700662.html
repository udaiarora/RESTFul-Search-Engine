Coordinates and supervises the staff and activities of a University Registrar's Office section (Records Information). Provides a full range of supervision of staff, including selection, training, performance evaluation, discipline, schedule development and work prioritization. Provides directions and information to diverse populations, including students, parents, departments, and general public. Coordinates special activities and projects. Develops and recommends process improvements to supervisors. Recommends and manages wage budget, audit payroll reports to include monitoring of hourly employees, which include student workers. Initiates and/or coordinates the preparation and/or maintenance of reports, forms, procedures, manuals, and other written materials. Directs and participates in cross-training activities including procedures such as customer service, academic transactions, usage of ASU Interactive, and data entry, to assist with variable peak processing demands. Serves on committees and projects as assigned. Liaison for new student orientation and University Registrar's Office policies and procedures. Coordinates system changes, testing and implementation for section.  
Six years of office/clerical experience which includes two years of lead experience; OR, Associate's degree in Office Administration, Business Administration or a related field AND four years of clerical/administrative experience which includes two years of lead experience.  
OR, any equivalent combination of education and/or experience from which comparable knowledge, skills and abilities have been achieved.  
 
Experience with: generating correspondence and report writing; working with various university departments and advising units; word processing, spreadsheets, E-Mail, and reporting software (e.g. MS Word, MS Excel, MS Outlook and BrioQuery); user level experience on a mainframe computer (e.g. ASU's Student Information System and/or PeopleSoft). Evidence of: direct customer service experience and leadership skills; effective verbal and written communication skills. Experience with university registration policies and procedures.  
This position offers an excellent opportunity for an individual who is looking for a challenging position and the opportunity to contribute to enhancing services to students, as a member of a team that has high exposure within the University. ASU offers a tuition waiver (to include the employee, spouse and dependents), paid vacation and holidays, excellent benefit package, employee training and development opportunities, and an employee assistance program.  
ASU conducts pre-employment screening for all positions which includes a criminal background check, verification of work history, academic credentials, licenses, and certifications.
