Compatible Wireless Network Card List

The following list of 802.11b and 802.11g wireless network cards are known to have 802.1x compatibility and should work on the ASU Wireless network. However, ASU IT does not guarantee that all of the cards listed will work on the network.

The table below provides the following information:

  • Card Name/Model: Lists the wireless card name and model
  • Tested By: Identifies whether the card is known to work on the network.
    • ASU IT: Indicates that the card is known to work and is supported by ASU IT.
    • User-tested: Indicates that an ASU Wireless user has reported that the card works on the network, but ASU IT does not support the card.
    • No entry: Indicates that the card is 802.1x compatible but has not been tested by ASU IT or an ASU Wireless user.
