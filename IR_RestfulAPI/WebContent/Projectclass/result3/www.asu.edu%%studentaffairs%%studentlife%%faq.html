Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ’s) community outreach site.  This site offers a revolving series of questions that the community outreach program and the office of student life routinely respond to.  Many of the questions that are posed and answered on this site are applicable to students that reside on or off campus.  Be aware that the responses here in no way are intended to offer legal advice or are offered as definitive outcomes to any described situation.  Please contact student life, community outreach at 480-965-6547 for information.  We hope you find the information helpful!

QUESTION:  What should I do if I am having problems with my roommate and I live off campus?

ANSWER:  If you are experiencing issues/problems/concerns with a roommate and you live off campus, you can contact the ASU Student Legal Assistance Office which is located in room 329 of the Memorial Union. Student Legal Assistance is open Monday through Thursday from 8:00am-6:00pm, and Friday from 8:00am-12:00pm.  You can call for an appointment or stop in and make an appointment.  Student Legal Assistance employs a full time attorney that can assist you.  Another option is to contact your landlord and review options for relocating or obtaining a new roommate.  If you reside on campus at ASU, you should speak to your community coordinator of your residential hall.  You can also make an appointment and speak with Advocacy & Assistance within the Dean of Students office.  You can reach that office at 480-965-5852.  You can also refer to the following web site that provides information about conflict mediation at http://www.asu.edu/studentaffairs/mu/bestpractices/conflict.htm.
