Academic Program Profile

The Academic Program Profile (APP) is a web-based application maintained by the University Office of Institutional Analysis that is designed to provide a comprehensive set of program-level data on students, staff, and finance.  The primary purpose of the APP is to enhance the accessibility of critical information for academic units for program improvement by integrating data from many sources into one location.  The APP also supports the academic program review process coordinated by the Division of Graduate Studies.


For security purposes, access to the APP is managed by the Office of Institutional Analysis.  Forms requesting access must be completed and signed by the head of the academic unit or senior university administrator.  Approved account holders may then use their ASURITE User Id and password to log onto the system once they receive notification that their account has been activated. When an approved account holder moves to another department, the person's access will be automatically removed and the person will have to resubmit a new APP access form.
