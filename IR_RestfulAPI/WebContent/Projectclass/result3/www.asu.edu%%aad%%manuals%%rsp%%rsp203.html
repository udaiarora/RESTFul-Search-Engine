RSP 203: Radiation Safety in Research and Teaching

The ASU policies and procedures ensuring that radiation-producing materials and equipment are used in a manner that minimizes radiation hazards and that research and teaching activities involving the use of radiation comply with state, federal, and university standards appear in the Environmental Health & Safety Policies and Procedures Manual at EHS 113, “Radiation Safety in Research and Teaching.” For more information, contact the Environmental Health & Safety Office at 480/965–1823.
