"The Capitol Scholars Program was a wonderful introduction to national politics and policy making. Like many of the other students who participated, I had a keen interest in these areas but little idea how to get started. This program helped us get our foot in the door. It plays an incredibly important role in encouraging young people, particularly from distant Western states, to engage in national politics and policy." Brian Lutz, 1999 Capitol Scholar Research analyst, United Nations Millennium Project
