A Proposed Canadian Model

  • Establish by legislative mandate an agency reporting to Parliament through the Ministry of Industry or Heritage or a combination of both;

  • Fund centrally through Parliament;

  • Grant authority to act on behalf of the Government of Canada in international negotiations related to research data and its management standards and practices;

  • Structure as a network of distributed service points with a central service facility;
