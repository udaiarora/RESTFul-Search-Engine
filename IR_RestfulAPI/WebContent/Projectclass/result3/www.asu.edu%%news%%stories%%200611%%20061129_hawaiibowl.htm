Nov. 29, 2006

Sun Devils go bowling in Hawaii

The Arizona State University football team has accepted an invitation to play in the 2006 Sheraton Hawaii Bowl on Dec. 24 at Aloha Stadium in Honolulu.

Take a look back at the 2006 season

The Sun Devils will face the University of Hawaii Warriors (10-2) on Dec. 24. Kickoff is scheduled for 6 p.m., Arizona time.

This is will be the 23rd Bowl game appearance for the Sun Devils, and the fourth in the past five seasons. Arizona State holds a 12-9-1 all-time record in Bowl games, including two straight victories.

“We are extremely excited to accept this invitation to participate in the 2006 Sheraton Hawaii Bowl,” says Vice President for University Athletics Lisa Love. “Our student-athletes have played their hearts out all year, and this is a great reward for their season of hard work.”
The Hawaii Bowl will mark the third straight postseason appearance for ASU, as the Devils will attempt to win their third consecutive Bowl after victories over Purdue in the 2004 Sun Bowl and Rutgers in the 2005 Insight Bowl.

The match-up with Hawaii will be the seventh all-time meeting between the two schools and the first since 1979. ASU holds a 5-1 all-time edge in the series. ESPN will televise the game nationally.

Ticket information for the 2006 Sheraton Hawaii Bowl will be released once it becomes available.

Arizona State will begin preparations for the game, when it begins practice on Tuesday, Dec. 5.
