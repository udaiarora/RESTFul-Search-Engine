PTS News and Updates

Veterans Way Partial Closure Extended through February 1st

Westbound Veterans Way between Sixth Street and College Avenue will be closed daily from 6 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Friday, Jan. 26, and again during this same time frame beginning Monday, Jan. 29, through Thursday, Feb. 1. In addition, Veterans between University Drive and Sixth will be closed in both directions from 3 a.m. until 6 a.m. Tuesday, Jan. 30. These closures are to allow for light-rail construction work. For more information, please click on the City of Tempe Street Closures & Restrictions link to the right under Items of Interest.

Brief Closure on Terrace Road at Rural Road

Terrace Road west of Rural Road will be closed Monday, Jan. 29, through Tuesday morning, Jan. 30, for repaving. Lot 44 decal holders can access their lot by taking either Apache Boulevard or Tyler Street to McAllister Avenue. Visitors wishing to park in Parking Structure 4 may still enter this structure via Terrace, but must do so by way of turning east onto Terrace off McAllister.

During these two days, the following bus detours will be in effect:

Neighborhood Flash Eastbound - Regular route east on University Drive to McAllister; south on McAllister to Apache Boulevard; east on Apache to Rural; north on Rural to Lemon Street; east on Lemon; resuming regular route.

Neighborhood Flash Westbound - Regular route west on Lemon to Rural; south on Rural; west on Apache; north on McAllister; resuming regular route north on McAllister at Terrace.

Intersection of Mill Avenue and University Drive
Restricted Jan. 15 - March 15

Mill Avenue at University Drive will be restricted to one lane in each direction from Monday, Jan. 15, through Thursday, March 15, 2007. This two-month restriction is being implemented to allow for water and sewer line installation. No right or left turns will be permitted in any direction. As a result, FLASH to University will adhere to the following detour:

Regular route south on Mill to Fifth Street; west on Fifth to Ash Avenue; south on Ash to University; east on University to resume regular route at Mill.

For a complete list of bus detours in effect due to this construction, please click here.

NOTICE TO THE ASU COMMUNITY

College Avenue south of Apache is no longer open to southbound traffic.  All traffic must turn east or west on Apache from College heading southbound.  Therefore if you are exiting Parking Structure 1 onto College and come to the intersection of College and Apache you must turn east or west. This was not an ASU decision to make. This was approved by the City upon request of the neighborhood which lives south of Apache along College. Please plan your commute accordingly.

South Campus Residential Housing Development
to Affect Parking

In an effort to create new and improved facilities for ASU residence hall students, the university is moving forward with campus development plans south of Apache Boulevard. As a result, construction of a new South Campus Residential Housing development will impact parking in this area beginning January 2007.

Parking Lots 38, 39, Motorcycle Lots 18, 30, 31, and 33, along with a portion of Lot 37 will no longer be available after Dec. 31. Please click here for more parking information on this project, a list of frequently asked questions and a map of the affected area.
