More Tsunami Information


(posted 1/19/05)


Soon after the devastating Tsunami on December 26, 2004, four disaster experts from Iran, Russia and the United States developed a Supercourse Just-in-Time lecture on Tsunamis, found at http://www.pitt.edu/~super1/lecture/lec18071/index.htm.


The authors are now providing a second lecture, "Earthquake & Tsunami South Asia, 26 Dec 2004," which can be found at http://www.pitt.edu/~super1/lecture/lec18091/index.htm.


The mission of the Supercourse is to translate the best possible scholarly information from leading researchers to educators. The Supercourse represents a global scientific effort of over 20,000 scholars to improve sharing of scientific information from research to the classroom.


For more information, visit the Supercourse web site at http://www.pitt.edu/~super1/index.htm or contact Ronald E. LaPorte, Ph.D., Director and Professor of Epidemiology, Disease Monitoring and Telecommunications at the WHO Collaborating Centre, Graduate School of Public Health University of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. E-mail: ronlaporte@aol.com or ghnetron@gmail.com.
