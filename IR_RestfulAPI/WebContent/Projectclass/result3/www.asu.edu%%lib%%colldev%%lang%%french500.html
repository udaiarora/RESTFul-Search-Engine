I. INTRODUCTION

This page presents library resources in support of French 500: Bibliography and Research Methods, a seminar taught by Dr. Deborah N. Losse Spring Semester, 2004. More details on the class as well as titles studied are given in the Syllabus below. Selected Library guides and Internet resources are also listed below as a complement to the Syllabus. All of the titles are available in the ASU Libraries.

II. SYLLABUS

Français 500. Bibliographie et Théories critiques ( Bibliography and Research Methods)

Deborah N. Losse
Department of Languages and Literatures
LL440A
Heures de permanence: de 8h à 9h lundi; de 11h à midi mardi
Téléphone : 480-965-3762
dlosse@asu.edu


Objectifs:
  • Maîtriser la forme bibliographique
  • Connaître les sources bibliographiques pertinentes à la recherche culturelle/littéraire
  • Connaître une variété de théories critiques modernes
  • Choisir un domaine: genre/période/mouvement/ phénomène culturel et formuler un projet de thèse préliminaire; choisir deux ou trois théories critiques les plus appropriées au domaine

IV. RESOURCES FOR LITERARY RESEARCH
GUIDES TO LIBRARY RESEARCH AND RESEARCH METHODS

Z 675 .R45 B42 1988 HAYDEN REF
Beasley, David. How to use a research library. New York: Oxford University Press, 1988.
Discusses library catalogs and how to use them, as well as research tools and advanced research methods.

Z1001 .B42x HAYDEN STACKS
Beaudiquez, Marcelle. Guide de bibliographie générale : méthodologie et pratique.
München ; New York : Saur, 1983.
Provides in depth explanations of how to use bibliographies, books, periodicals, government publications, theses, etc. Emphasis is on French publications.

PN 146 .C33 1993 HAYDEN REF
Cantor, Jeffrey A. A guide to academic writing. Westport, Conn.: Greenwood Press, 1993.
Offers advice on scholarly publishing, and how to prepare books, journal articles, conference reports, etc. for publication.

LB2369 .G53 1995 HAYDEN REF
Gibaldi, Joseph. MLA handbook for writers of research papers. 4th ed. New York : Modern
Language Association of America, 1995.
Contents: Research and writing -- The mechanics of writing -- The format of the research paper -- Documentation: preparing the list of works cited -- Documentation: citing sources in the text --
Abbreviations -- Appendix.

Z710 .M23 1987 HAYDEN RESERVE
Mann, Thomas.
A guide to library research methods. New York : Oxford University Press, 1987.

GUIDES TO GENERAL AND LITERARY RESEARCH

PR56 .A68 1993 HAYDEN STACKS
Altick, Richard D. and John Fenstermaker. The art of literary research. 4th ed. New York : Norton, c1993.
Explains techiques common to all research and applies them to literary investigation.
PN523 .M3x HAYDEN REF
Magill, Frank N. Magill's bibliography of literary criticism : selected sources for the study of more than 2,500 outstanding works of Western literature. Englewood Cliffs, N.J. : Salem Press, c1979.
An alphabetically arranged four volume set. Contents: v. 1. Abe to Eliot.--v. 2. Ellison to Longinus.--v. 3. Longus to Sexton.--v. 4. Shakespeare to Zola.

Z 1002 .M28 1985 HAYDEN REF
Malclés, Louise-Noëlle . Manuel de bibliographie. 4th ed. Paris: PUF, 1984.
and
Z 1002 .M4 v. 1-3 HAYDEN STACKS
Malclés,Louise-Noëlle . Les sources du travail bibliographique. Genève: Droz, 1950-1958.
Both titles serve as guides to general reference books.

PN 43 .P3x 1983 HAYDEN REF
Patterson, Margaret C. Literary research guide. 2nd ed. New York: Modern Language Association of America, 1983.

PN 523 .T47x 1982 HAYDEN REF
Thompson, George. Key sources in comparative and world literature: an annotated guide to reference materials. New York: F. Unger, 1982.
Has a 28 page section devoted to reference sources in all periods of French literature. Includes bibliographies, handbooks and studies of individual authors.

PN 695 .W67x 1995 HAYDEN REF
Wortman, William A. A guide to serial bibliographies for modern literatures. 2nd. ed. New York: Modern Language Association of America, 1995.
Includes a section on French literature of all periods and Francophone literature of Africa, Canada and the Caribbean.

An annual bibliography of journal articles, and books relating to French literary subjects and authors of all periods. Chapter headings and most of the entries are in French. Said to contain more European sources for French Literature studies than the MLA International Bibliography.

PQ 51 .B6 1968 HAYDEN STACKS
Bouvier, Émile and Pierre Jourda. Guide de l'étudiant en littérature française. Paris: PUF, 1968. Primarily a guide for the French University student but the description of French literary research methods should be helpful.

PQ 45 .G35 1990 HAYDEN STACKS
Galand, Rene Stratégie de la lecture. New York: Peter Lang, 1990. (Reading Plus. vol. 7)
Emphasized reading of French literature using a critical approach.

PQ 103 .K4 HAYDEN REF
Kempton, Richard. French literature: an annotated guide to selected bibliographies. New York: Modern Language Association of America, 1981.
Covers major bibliographies and guides to references books in French literature of all periods.

Z 2171 .L17 1970 HAYDEN REF
Langlois, Pierre and André Mareuil. Guide bibliographiques des études littéraires. 5th ed. Paris: Hachette, 1970.
A selective listing of French authors and critical studies.

PQ 103 .M32x HAYDEN REF
Mahaffey, Denis. Concise bibliography of French literature. New York: Bowker, 1975.
Covers critical works and biographies of authors. Arranged by century.

PQ 53 .M44 1993 HAYDEN STACKS
Mermier, Guy R. and Yvette Boilly-Widmer. Explication de texte: théorie et pratique. Lewiston, N.Y.: Edwin Mellen Press, 1993.

PC 2071 .O8x HAYDEN REF

VII. INTERNET RESOURCES

ARTFL (Project for American and French Research on the Treasury of the French Language)

The database includes nearly 2000 texts, ranging from classic works of French literature to various kinds of non-fiction prose and technical writing. The eighteenth, nineteenth and twentieth centuries are about equally represented, with a smaller selection of seventeenth century texts as well as some medieval and Renaissance texts.

Bibliographie Nationale Francaise

The French national bibliography. Available only from library computers.

Dictionnaires des XVIe et XVIIe siècles

Contains the texts of ten 16th and 17th century dictionaries. Includes a thesaurus for all the entries with their variants and spellings, along with the languages of etymology such as Hebrew, Arabic and Armenian. A set of dictionaries on cd-rom located in the Reference Department's MARS Room..

French Studies Website (Hayden Library)

Offers links to a variety of Internet resources on French studies including French culture, language, literature, news sources, as well as library catalogs in Canada and France.

Internationale Bibliographie der Zeitschriftenliteratur: International Bibliography of Periodical Literature (1987+)

The IBZ Online an international, interdisciplinary bibliography with particular emphasis on the social sciences, arts and humanities. It is a multilingual database, international in scope and indexes over 8700 periodicals.with a significant number of them published on the European continent.

Iter: Gateway to the Renaissance

Present bibliographies of articles and reviews drawn from 518 medieval and renaissance journal titles. Updated daily and with thousands of new records added annually. Indexes overs Indexes articles; reviews; bibliographies; catalogues; editions; abstracts; discographies; and notes.

Literature Resource Center: LRC

The Literature Resource Center provides access to biographies, bibliographies and critical analysis of authors from every age and literary discipline. It covers more than 90,000 novelists, poets, essayists, journalists and other writers, with in-depth coverage of 2400 of the most-studied authors. Search by Author Name, Title, Genre, Literary Movement or Literary Themes. Users can access current, full-text critical essays on major authors via the Literature Resource Center's link to more than 130 prominent literary journals, incluing French Forum and Nineteenth-Century French Studies, 2000-2001.

MLA Bibliography (1963+)

The online version of the multivolume MLA International Bibliography produced by the Modern Language Association of America. Provides bibliographic records pertaining to literature, language, linguistics, and folklore. Indexes monographs, working papers, proceedings, bibliographies, and other formats. Covers over 3,000 journals and series from 1963. The bound volumes are available in Hayden Reference.
