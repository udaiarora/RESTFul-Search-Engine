PCS 1004–03 Table

The department/principal investigator requests an equipment transfer authorization from the sponsored projects officer.

The sponsored projects officer requests, in writing, the equipment transfer from the sponsor, and copies the sponsored property coordinator on the memo.

If an equipment transfer is approved, the sponsored projects officer forwards the authorization letter to the sponsored property coordinator.

The sponsored property coordinator:

  1. determines from the department the equipment transfer departure date

  2. removes ASU property control numbers from equipment prior to the equipment departure date

    and

  3. removes the equipment from the property control system.
