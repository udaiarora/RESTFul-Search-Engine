Human Resources - Employee Resource Center

The Employee Resource Center is located in the University Services Building, on the southeast corner of Rural Road and Spence.

Hours of Operation: Monday - Friday 8:00 - 5:00

Explore the many resources and services available in the Employee Resource Center.

Resource Library
The resource library houses current books, video and audio tapes available for check out for ASU employees. Topics include communication, change, performance management, career development and wellness. The Resource Library is located within the Employee Resource Center.

Resume / Cover Letter assistance
Are you looking for assistance with your resume and cover letter? There are many resources to help you create your resume in an eye-catching format. For on line assistance please see Resume/Cover Letter. A qualified staff member is also available to assist you - please contact HR Staffing Services to schedule an appointment.

Technology
There are 2 computer stations located in the ERC, including a printer for your convenience. These computers are available for use by all staff members to log-on to the Learning and Performance Solutions e-Learning site, prepare your resume or cruise the web for information.
