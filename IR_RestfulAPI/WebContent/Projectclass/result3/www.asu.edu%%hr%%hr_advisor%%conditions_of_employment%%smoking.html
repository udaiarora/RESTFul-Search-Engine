FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Where is smoking allowed on the ASU campus?
Smoking is allowed only in designated areas as a courtesy to the members of the university community who do not smoke. Areas such as doorways, stairways, walkways—any common area where there is pedestrian traffic—should not be used as a smoking area.

Is smoking allowed in University-owned vehicles?
No, smoking is prohibited in any University vehicle.

Who do I call if I see someone smoking in an ASU vehicle or in an undesignated area?
You should report this information to your supervisor or to:
Risk Management
480.965.1823
risk@asu.edu.

What happens if an employee chooses to smoke in an undesignated area?
Smoking by an ASU employee in an undesignated area is addressed as inappropriate conduct that may result in disciplinary action.
