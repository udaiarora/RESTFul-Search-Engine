ACD 114: University Standing Committees and Boards

Purpose

To describe the membership and responsibilities of faculty and academic professional committees involved in university governance

Sources

Office of the President
Academic Constitution and Bylaws

Applicability

Faculty

Academic professionals

Policy

University standing committees and boards are university-wide committees and boards that are continuous and that are so designated by the president. A list of these committees and their membership can be obtained from the current ASU Roster of Boards, Committees and Councils.
