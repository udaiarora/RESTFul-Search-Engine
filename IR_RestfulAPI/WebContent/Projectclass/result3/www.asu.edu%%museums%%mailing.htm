MGCC Mailing List

 

To join the MGCC Mailing List, please follow these instructions:

ASU Subscribers:

Click on the URL below and select the menu item "Login" under "ASU Affiliated". This will take you to the WebAuth login screen. Enter your ASURITE UserID and password and click "login". After you have logged in, you will be taken back to the Announcement Groups Web page. Click on the menu item "Modify my Subscriptions". From the Subscriptions page you can subscribe to the Museums, Galleries and Collections List along with any of the existing Announcement Groups by clicking the Subscribe box.

http://announce.asu.edu/

Non-ASU Subscribers:

Click on the URL below and select the menu item "Create Account". Enter your name, and choose a User ID and password, then enter your e-mail address. The system will acknowledge the creation of your account by responding with an e-mail confirmation that must be accessed within 24 hours. You may then subscribe to the Museums, Galleries and Collections List or any other announcement groups available to non-ASU affiliates by clicking the Subscribe box.

http://announce.asu.edu/
