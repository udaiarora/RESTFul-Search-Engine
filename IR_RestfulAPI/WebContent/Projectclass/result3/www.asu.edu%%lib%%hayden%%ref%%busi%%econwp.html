Metasites & Indexes to Working Papers
    Abstracts of Working Papers in Economics is part of the electronic index to the Journal of Economic Literature called EconLit
    EconPapers - RePEc: Research Papers in Economics Archives (http://econpapers.repec.org/)
      This is a list of web sites for economic working papers. Most are the economic research bureaus of universitites.
    The Scout Report for Business & Economics Metapage links for Working Papers (1997-2001)
    (http://scout.wisc.edu/Reports/BusEcon/archive/)
      This link leads to the working papers of organizations such as OECD, IMF, World Bank, the Federal Reserve Banks of the U.S., etc.
    IDEAS Working Paper Information
    (http://ideas.repec.org/)
      This is another Metasite that leads to many other sites with research papers in economics.
