2006-07 Financial Aid Notification (FAN)

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this e-mail notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

Congratulations! ASU has prepared your 2006-07 academic year online Financial Aid Notification (FAN) which details your financial aid offer. If you are a new student, a paper FAN has also been mailed to you. The FAN includes known scholarships awarded to you. Please view your online FAN using ASU Interactive. Once you have logged in, choose "Access Financial Aid Services" from the main menu. In order to maximize funding opportunities available to all students, ASU asks that you accept, reduce and/or decline your offered award(s) without delay. Quick response allows ASU to reallocate declined funds to other deserving students.

ASU also wants to take this opportunity to remind you to only borrow loans that you truly need to cover your actual educational expenses. Excessive borrowing can create undue financial burden once repayment begins. Also, if you receive additional types of financial aid (or financial resources) your award package will be adjusted according to federal, state, and/or institutional guidelines.

If you are unable to access your online FAN or you have questions, please call or visit one of our locations.

Best wishes for a successful year,

Arizona State University
