State Library

  • Full service Public Library for state employees

  • Over 8 million objects, books, serials, Audio/Video, CDs, DVDs, maps

  • Provide access to electronic databases through KY Virtual Library

  • Also, provide technical assistance, grant funding, and support for public libraries across the state.

State Archives

  • --- cubic feet of holdings

State Records Management Authority

  • Provide records management advice and service for state executive branch agencies, and local governments

  • Generate records retention schedules

  • Serve as the working staff of the State Archives and Records Commission
