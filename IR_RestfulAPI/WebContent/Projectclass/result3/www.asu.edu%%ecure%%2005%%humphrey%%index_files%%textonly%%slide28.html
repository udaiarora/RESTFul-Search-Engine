National Consultation on Access to Scientific Research Data

  • A Task Force of experts was assembled to organize a two-day National Forum to investigate issues regarding access to research data in Canada and to formulate recommendations.

  • Experts from the natural and medical sciences were engaged to complement the work of the NDAC.

  • The Task Force developed a 'mind-map' of ideal achievements reached in 2010 as a result of improved data access.
