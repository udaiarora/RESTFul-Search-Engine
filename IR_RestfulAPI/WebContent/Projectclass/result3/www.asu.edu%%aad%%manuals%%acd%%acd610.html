ACD 610: Flexible Spending Accounts

Purpose

To provide eligible staff the opportunity to participate in a dependent care and/or medical care tax-savings program through payroll reduction

Sources

Internal Revenue Code § 125
Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 6–604

Applicability

Faculty and academic professionals who are employed to work 20 hours per week (50 percent FTE) or more for a continuous period of six or more months

Policy

Eligible faculty or academic professionals who meet the requirements can participate in the Dependent Care and/or the Medical Expense section of the Flexible Spending Account program through payroll reduction, by enrolling during the annual open enrollment period.
