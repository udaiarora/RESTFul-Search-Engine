Benefits Information

Optional benefits offered by ASU include medical, pharmacy, dental, vision, short-term disability, supplemental life insurance, and flexible spending accounts.  These coverages are effective the first of the month following your date of hire AND receipt of enrollment papework.  Although you have 31 days from your date of hire to make your elections, your effective date will be determined by when your forms are received by the HR Employee Service Center.

   E.g.:     Date of Hire:                         10-21

               Enrollment Period:                 10-21 through 11-20

               Paperwork received:              Benefits are effective:

               On or before 10-31               11-01

               Between 11-01 and 11-20     12-01

If benefits are not elected within the thirty-one days, you will have NO benefits until the next Open Enrollment or until you have a qualifying family status change.

You may obtain assistance on Benefits issues by contacting the HR Employee Service Center located in the University Services Building.   Call (480) 965-2701 with questions or visit the Employee Service Center at 1551 S. Rural Rd, Tempe; hours are Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

You or your department should call (480) 965-4751 to register you for HR Employee Orientation to receive benefits information. You may also register by e-mail at lps@asu.edu. Please state your name, phone number, department, and position name (faculty, post-doctorate, academic professional, service professional, or administrative or classified staff ).

Before You Attend Orientation:
Read the Benefits Enrollment Guide included with your New Employee Packet.  It contains a description and comparison of the insurance plans that you are eligible to choose, along with other important information to help you make your coverge elections.
