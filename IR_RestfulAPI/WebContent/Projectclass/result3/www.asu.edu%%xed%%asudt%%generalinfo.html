Driving Directions - MAP

The Mercado is conveniently located at Fifth and Monroe streets in downtown Phoenix.

From the east valley, take I-10 west (Los Angeles) and exit 7th Street south.
From the west valley, take I-10 east (Tucson) and exit 7th Street south.
From north Phoenix, take SR-51 south to I-10 west and exit 7th Street south.

Parking

Parking is readily accessible and conveniently located in the Heritage and Science Park Garage, situated at the southeast corner of Fifth and Monroe streets.

Effective July 17, 2006 all reservations will be subject to a $5 facility use/parking fee per person. This fee covers tables, chairs and room set-up, and one parking validation per person for the Heritage and Science Park garage at Fifth and Monroe streets.

Over the years we have been in business we have been able to provide limited free parking for the users of the facility. As our costs have increased, this fee will help us continue to provide you with high quality service.

Public Transit

The Mercado is only a few steps away from the DASH and Valley Metro bus routes.
