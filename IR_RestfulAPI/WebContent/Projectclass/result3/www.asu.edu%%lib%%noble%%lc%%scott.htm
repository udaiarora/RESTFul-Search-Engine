Presentations / Publications / Exhibits

Scott, M. & Vogus, B. (December 2004). “The Fast and the Furious: Online Paths to Government Information." Arizona Library Association Annual Conference. Presentation. Session was designed with the public librarian in mind, and examined five government websites and their effectiveness in searching government information.

 

Scott, M. (November/December 2004). “Answering State Government Reference Questions” Arizona Library Association Newsletter. <http://www.azla.org/associations/2837/november%2004%2Epdf>

 

Scott, M. & Stanton, D. (September - November 2004). "I Do Solemnly Swear . . .". Exhibit at the Hayden Library. The exhibit contained presidential government documents centered around three themes: Leadership, Power, and Elections & Transitions. The display coincided with the Presidential Debate held at Arizona State University, October 13, 2004.

 

Scott, M. & Vogus, B. (September 2004). “Fast Access to Online Government Info!” Area Reference Librarian’s monthly meeting. Online presentation.
