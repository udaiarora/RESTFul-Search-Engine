STAFFING SERVICES: RECRUITMENT

HR Professionals serving your needs with confidentiality and efficiency

The Staffing Services unit within the HR Partners' Group assists departments with consulting, advising and services to recruit qualified candidates to meet your staffing needs.

To accomplish this, we can provide you with the following:

  • Assist with writing customized staff requests to best reach your target audience
  • Recommend a variety of recruitment methods
  • Consult on advertising media for your job vacancy and process your advertising requests
  • Promote your open job vacancies to Staffing Services Community Partners
  • Assist with high volume seasonal hiring
  • Consult on special recruiting projects
  • Analyze attrition in your department upon request
  • Assist with developing department retention programs
  • Recommend short-term staffing options
  • Provide creative recruitment and advertising brainstorming
