Inter-religious Dialogue on Truth

Does your faith define your interaction in the world ?

Fishbowl conversation, followed by small group conversation and refreshments

Monday, November 20th
6.30-8.30pm


Hillel Jewish Center
1012 S. Mill Avenue
Tempe, AZ 85281
( just South of 10th and Mill)

Discussion: How have our religious traditions influenced the ways we look at the "Other"? What kind of stereotypes have we learned? What prejudices do we hold? Do we treat people differently who share our faith to those who ascribe to a different set of beliefs? Why? What kind of social controls and power differentials do our groups exhibit towards insiders vs. outsiders?

No RSVP needed; just join us, ready to dialogue!
