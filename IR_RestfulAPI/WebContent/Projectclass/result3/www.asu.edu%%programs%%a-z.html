Baccalaureate Degree Programs (Undergraduate Majors)

ASU’s baccalaureate degrees (B.A., B.S., B.S.E., B.S.D., etc.) and concentrations are listed alphabetically in the table below.

Go to: A | B | C | D | E | F | G | H | I | J | K | L | M | N | O | P | R | S | T | U | W | Minor and Certificates | Graduate Degrees

Major Information Sheetsare available for all undergraduate degrees offered on ASU’s Tempe, West, Polytechnic, and Downtown Phoenix campuses. These describe the requirements for majors and suggested programs of study for undergraduate degrees.

Check Sheets are available for undergraduate degrees offered at all campuses. Check sheets break down the course requirements for a particular degree program. It shows core course requirements, General Studies requirements, and possible electives or related fields. This tool helps a student and his/her advisor easily determine which requirements have been satisfied and which have yet to be satisfied.
