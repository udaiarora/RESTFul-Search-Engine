Graduate Travel Awards

The Center for Asian Research is pleased to offer limited funding on a semi-annual basis to support graduate student travel related to the presentation of scholarly research or for doctoral dissertation research. There are two application deadlines per calendar year: April 15 and November 15.

First priority is given to graduate students who are presenting their research at juried international ($500) and national ($250) conferences. Second priority to masters or doctoral students presenting their research at juried regional conferences ($150), and third priority to doctoral students who are planning dissertation research trips.

Applicants for travel to conferences should submit the following:


1) a letter of request with the name, dates and location of the conference;
2) title and abstract of paper to be presented;
3) proof of juried acceptance need not be furnished at the time of application, but is due before funds will be distributed.

Applicants for travel to conduct pre-dissertation or dissertation research should submit the following:

1) a letter of request including the intended destination(s) and a summary of the work to be conducted, including a list of the specific library or museum resources to be consulted;
2) a letter from the student’s committee chair attesting to the importance of the student’s research, the necessity of travel for research and the appropriateness of the selected site, and the student’s academic standing.

Applications should be addressed to the Center for Asian Research Executive Committee and forwarded to the attention of Center for Asian Research’s Academic Advisor, Sherry Harlacher P.O. Box 871702 Tempe, AZ 85287-1702.
