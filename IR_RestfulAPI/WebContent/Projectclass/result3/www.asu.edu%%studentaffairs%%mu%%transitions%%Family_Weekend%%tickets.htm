Football Ticket Information
Does my registration to Family Weekend include football tickets?
No. The registration fee for Family Weekend does NOT include tickets to the football game. However registering for Family Weekend will allow you to purchase football tickets at a discounted price. A discount code will be available at the end of the registration process.
If my student has season tickets for the football game, will we be able to sit with them?
Not unless you have purchased an extra ticket for your student. Students sit in the student section and those tickets are not transferable to general seating areas.
When is the last day to purchase football tickets at the discounted Family Weekend rate?
As a registered Family Weekend participant you will be able to purchase the discounted Family Weekend football tickets through the Sun Devil Ticket Office until Wednesday, September 27, 2006.
In what section will we be sitting during the Family Weekend football game?
There is no special section reserved for Family Weekend guests. You will pick your section when you order online. Please contact the Ticket Office at 480-965-2381 for more information.
Can I receive discounted Family Weekend football tickets by contacting the Ticket Office directly?
No. In order to receive the discount you must complete the Family Weekend Registration.
How will I receive my football tickets?
If you purchase football tickets through the Sun Devil Ticket Office before September 21 the tickets will be mailed directly to you. Tickets ordered after Thursday, September 21 will be placed in Will Call in the Sun Devil Ticket Office, located at the South end of Sun Devil Stadium. Photo ID will be required for pick up. Will call tickets will be available from 9:00 am- 5:00 pm Monday through Friday, and on game day from 10:00 am through half time of the football game.
Who can I contact for more information about Family Weekend Football Tickets?
If you have additional questions regarding the purchase of football tickets please contact the Sun Devil Ticket Office at 480-965-2381.
How do I order football tickets?
  • web site - thesundevils.com
  • Click on "Tickets."
  • Click on “Right Here” link located under Purchase Tickets.
  • Enter the promotion code then select “Go”. The code is good between July 27 and September 27.
  • On the next screen, click the red underlined letters “ Oregon”.
  • Enter quantity of tickets.
  • Click on desired section in upper endzone. Best available seats will be selected.
  • On the next screen, click on “Check Out” or read options provided below the order.
  • Click on “Register Now” to enter your contact and mailing information.
  • Click on “Next Step” to go to Payment Screen.
  • In the Payment Screen, enter payment.
  • Click “Submit.”
  • Once your order is submitted, close the ticket order window and return to the Family Weekend registration screen.
  • Tickets will be mailed if ordered on or before September 21. Tickets ordered after September 21 will be placed in Will Call. The deadline for using the promotional code is September 27. A photo ID is required to pick up tickets in Will Call, located at the south end of Sun Devil Stadium. Hours of operation are 9-5 Monday through Friday and on game day from 10:00 am through half time of the game.
  • Questions – email the Sun Devil Ticket Office at sundeviltickets@asu.edu or call 480-965-2381.
  • No refunds/no exchanges on football tickets.
