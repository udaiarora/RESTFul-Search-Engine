Campaign for Leadership

LSP Alumni Support Future LSP Scholars

ASU has embarked on its largest fundraising campaign in history--one that will provide a foundation of support for academic and student programs that will endure for ASU generations to come. The Leadership Scholarship Program is proud to be part of ASU's Campaign for Leadership, and alumni are actively developing support for current and future LSP scholars.

The LSP Alumni Council has set a goal of raising $250,000 with participation from all LSP alumni.

Remember, it's not how much you give that is important -- it's that you do give.

As of January of 2000, nearly half of the monetary goal has been met, but we need more participation from LSP alumni for us to reach our participation goal.

How Do I Contribute?

There are several ways to contribute to the Leadership Scholarship Program. Contributions to the campaign are managed by the ASU Foundation, a separate non-profit organization that exists to support ASU.

  • You may use this electronic form to submit a pledge to the campaign. ASU Foundation staff will send you follow-up information.
  • You may print this form, complete it, and submit your contribution with it to the ASU Foundation. PLEASE MAKE CHECKS PAYABLE TO THE ASU FOUNDATION AND MAIL TO: ASU CAMPAIGN FOR LEADERSHIP, ASU OFFICE OF SCHOLARSHIP DEVELOPMENT, PO BOX 873611, TEMPE, AZ 85287-3611

Campaign Q&A

What is the ASU Campaign for Leadership?

The ASU Campaign for Leadership is a window of opportunity for innovative ideas that advance the goals of both the institution and the community it serves. Investments made by ASU alumni, friends and community partners during this campaign will ensure that ASU is capitalized appropriately for its mission in the 21st century.

ASU?s goal is to complete the campaign by raising $400 million by 2001.

Why endow a public university?

The nation?s best universities are also its best-endowed universities, and building ASU?s endowment is the highest priority of the campaign. A well-endowed university is financially strong, able to make strategic investments in people and to undertake important new initiatives that are responsive to the community it serves.

Endowed funds provide:

  • the resources to attract world class scholars to enhance the academic contributions of the university,
  • distinguished teachers and researchers to meet the specific needs of local employers, and
  • talented students with the potential to lead our community in the next century.

What will the campaign mean to students?

The campaign will invest $75 million in students - dramatically increasing the amount of financial support available through scholarships and fellowships. Increasing student support will help reduce the indebtedness that so many students and their families incur in financing a college education. It will keep more of Arizona?s most promising students in-state and also help attract outstanding talent from around the county.

Why should I contribute to the Leadership Scholarship Program?

As an alumnus of the LSP program, you have experienced first-hand the benefits of participation in the program. Your gift will help support current students and expand the program to more students.

Is my gift tax-deductible?

Gifts to the ASU Campaign for Leadership are deductible from federal income taxes within the limits prescribed by law. In addition, some giving options offer capital gains or estate tax benefits.

What is a matching gift?

The impact of your gift can be doubled if you or your spouse work for a company or organization that matches charitable contributions made by employees. Please check with your company/organization regarding its participation.
