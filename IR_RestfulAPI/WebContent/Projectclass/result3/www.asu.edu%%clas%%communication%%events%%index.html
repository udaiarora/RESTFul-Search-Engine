The Hugh Downs School of Human Communication sponsors a variety of events designed to further the reach of our scholarship as well as bring into our School scholars, artists, and expert practitioners from around the world who share our commitment to use-inspired research and the advancement of theory.

Our newsletter link provides a bi-annual summary of these events and happenings in the HDSHC.

If you are an alum and have news you would like to share with the members of our worldwide learning community, please use the link "Tell Us Your News" to update us.

And, if you are a friend of The Hugh Downs School of Human Communication, please consider a gift through the ASU Foundation to one of our student scholarship funds or exciting Strategic Initiatives.  Thank you!
