Important Notice Regarding Financial Aid For Flight Students

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

As a student enrolled in Aeronautical Management Technology (AMT), we understand the additional cost involved with participating in the extraordinary flight program here at the Polytechnic campus. Because of this, we have worked with Sallie Mae to provide the Sallie Mae Signature Loan for ASU Flight Students. Details regarding this loan program include:

  • Apply beginning April 17, 2006.
  • To apply visit www.salliemae.com/signature, click "Apply Now" and list ASU as your school and aeronautical major as your course of study.
  • Interest rates as low as prime rate for students who qualify independently.
  • Interest rates as low as prime minus 0.5% for students who qualify with a credit-worthy cosigner.
  • Loan payments are deferred while the student is in school (Student must maintain half-time enrollment during the fall/spring semester).
  • Six month grace period after graduation or when student enrollment is below half-time credit hours before repayment begins.
  • No payments required during school. Interest may be capitalized at repayment.
  • Student may borrow each academic year up to their full cost of attendance (minus other financial aid received).
  • Maximum aggregate limit of $100,000 for undergraduates.
  • 15 year repayment term.
  • Students can borrow on their own credit history. No minimum income required.
  • Credit is good for 180 days. Students can borrow additional funds during that time without having their credit report run twice.
  • Cosigner release option after 24 scheduled monthly payments.
  • Student may borrow funds during the summer sessions for flight, even if the student is below half-time (Students must notify the Polytechnic Student Financial Assistance office).

This loan is intended to help AMT students acquire the funds to complete the flight module portions of the AMT professional flight degree program. You may apply beginning April 17th, 2006, by accessing www.salliemae.com/signature. Be sure to list ASU as your school and aeronautical major as your course of study. If you have difficulty qualifying for the loan or have unusual circumstances, please contact us.

Please call us at (480) 727-1041 if you have any questions.

Sincerely,

Student Financial Assistance
