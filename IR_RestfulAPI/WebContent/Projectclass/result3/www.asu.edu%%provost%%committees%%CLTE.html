COMMITTEE ON LEARNING AND TEACHING EXCELLENCE

This committee is advisory to the executive vice president and university provost and the director of the Center for Learning and Teaching Excellence on faculty needs; services and priorities for offering them; adequacy of existing services; and organization, staffing, and resource needs. Members serve for three years and include three administrators and six representatives appointed by the executive vice president and university provost from nominations provided by the Academic Senate Committee on Committees. Each college is to be represented over time.
