SPANISH MINOR or BIS EMPHASIS AREA

 

 

The Spanish minor or Spanish BIS Emphasis Area requires a minimum of 18 hours of upper-division SPA courses.

 

REQUIRED COURSES:

Course                                                                                     General Studies            Credits

SPA 313 or 315*  Conversation and Composition                    G                                 (3)

SPA 314 or 316*  Conversation and Composition                    G                                 (3)

*SPA 315 and 316 are designed for bilingual students. 

SPA 325 Introduction to Hispanic Literature                             HU                               (3)

SPA 412 Advanced Conversation and Composition                  G                                 (3)

Three semester hours from the following four courses:                                          (3)

SPA 471  Civilization of the Spanish Southwest             HU                              

SPA 472  Spanish American Civilization                       HU, G, H                    

SPA 473  Spanish Civilization                                       HU or SB, G

Three semester hours of an SPA elective:                          

Chose any SPA course, SPA 319 and above                see catalog                   (3)

 

TOTAL                                                                                                                       (18)

 

 

 

 

For Catalog Years 1999-00, 2000-01 and 2001-02, the requirements for the minor are the same as above, except that students may substitute SPA 413 Advanced Spanish Grammar for SPA 412 Advanced Conversation and Composition. 

 

 

Please note:

 

·        A minimum of 6 upper-division hours in the minor or BIS emphasis area must be taken in residence at ASU Main.

·        A grade of C or better must be achieved in all courses for the minor and BIS emphasis area. 

  • Pass/Fail courses cannot be used for the minor or BIS emphasis area.

·        SPA 311 and SPA 312 do not count towards the minor or BIS emphasis area in any catalog year.

·        SPA 313 and SPA 314 (like SPA 315 and 316) must be taken in sequence. 

·        Course substitutions for SPA 313 and 314 (or 315 and 316) are permitted only for native speakers and more advanced students.

·        You may not receive the minor or BIS emphasis area in a catalog year for which you are not eligible.  If you are unsure of your catalog year, check with your major advisor or the DLL academic advisor.
