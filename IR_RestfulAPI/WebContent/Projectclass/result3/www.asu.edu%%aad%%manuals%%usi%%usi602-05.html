USI 602–05: Counseling Fees

Purpose

To charge students for applicable clinical services

Source

University policy

Policy

Service Fees

ASU counseling centers may charge fees for services after an initial period of services offered at no charge. Many ASU students are covered by health insurance, either student health insurance offered by ASU or other insurance plans. At this time only Counseling and Consultation at the Tempe campus is able to accept health insurance and will bill insurance companies. See the counseling centers’ Web pages for detailed information on fee policies and insurance coverage at Counseling and Consultation at the Tempe campus, Student Counseling & Health Services at the West campus, and Student Counseling Services at the Polytechnic campus.
