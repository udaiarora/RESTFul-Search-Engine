ASU: A great place to work

If you want to be part of an ivy-covered, traditional university where your only colleagues are in your department and change takes years, don't consider a position at Arizona State University.

If you are inspired when working with colleagues from other disciplines, energized by mentoring undergraduate research, intrigued by the vision of the New American University, and want to see advancements happen quickly, ASU is definitely interested in you!

Want to know more about ASU? Grab a cup of coffee and read on!

  • ASU is on a dynamic mission to change the model of the institution of higher learning.
  • Rather than base our reputation on the quality of our incoming students, we rate our success on the quality of our students upon graduation. We are open to all qualified students and attract a high quality, diverse, and high achieving student body.
  • We believe that there is value in research which responds to problems in society.
  • ASU takes responsibility for the economic, cultural, and environmental wellness of the region and is directly involved in its communities.
  • This is a relatively young university, having been named a university less than 50 years ago.
  • ASU has the largest enrollment of any university in the country. That makes sense; we're the only research university in one of the fastest growing metropolitan areas in the country.
  • ASU's four campuses are different sizes and have different missions - one will meet your intellectual desires and suit your personal comfort level.
  • We field the second largest number of NCAA competitive teams in the Pac-10 (22). Go Devils!


If you work at ASU, you...

  • have a generous benefits package including medical insurance, vacation and sick leave, retirement options, and a host of other choices.
  • have access to first rate cultural and entertainment venues at discounted prices.
  • work in an energized atmosphere, helping the next generation of thought leaders, scientists and creative talents achieve their goals.
  • and your family members can enroll at one of the three state universities (Arizona State University, University of Arizona, and Northern Arizona University) at a reduced tuition rate.
  • work within a culture of caring and service. Volunteering, mentoring, and assisting opportunities are readily available.
  • stay fit and healthy with state-of-the-art recreational facilities and wellness programs.
  • can be a Sun Devil fan at all the intercollegiate sporting events at lower ticket prices. Go Devils!
  • can take advantage of numerous opportunities to grow intellectually through lectures and symposia, and professionally through training, leadership positions, workshops and more.
  • work in a beautifully landscaped environment. Each campus is carefully maintained, and the Tempe campus is an official Arboretum with over 300 species of plants from diverse geographic regions and miles of pathways.
