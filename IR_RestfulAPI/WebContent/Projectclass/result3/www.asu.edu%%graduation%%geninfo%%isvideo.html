Internet Broadcast on ASU Live!

ASU Commencement will be broadcast live over the Internet! The broadcast will be available to all, particularly for those family and friends who cannot attend the event in person. The software needed to view the event is available for download from a link on the ASU Live! website (http://dmit.asu.edu/live). An archive of the commencement video will be made available at the same web address following the event.

ASU Live! is a collaboration between ASU’s Information Technology DMIT and the Office of University Ceremonies.
