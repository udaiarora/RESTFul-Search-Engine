Authenticity Questions

  • How do you save attachments?

  • When you save an attachment, do you save the email along with it?

  • If you store important messages electronically outside of your email application, does the header information stay with the messages?

In order to ensure the authenticity of email and other electronic documents, certain actions must happen and particular information must be created and preserved. We asked the following questions in order to gauge individuals' knowledge of these functions:

  • [click] How do you save attachments? (i.e., do you save them outside your email program, if so, where and how?

  • [click] When you save an attachment, do you save the email along with it?

  • [click] If you store important messages electronically outside of your email application, does the header information stay with the messages?
