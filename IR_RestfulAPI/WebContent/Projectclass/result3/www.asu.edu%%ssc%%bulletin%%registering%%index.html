Registering

Student must be admitted, and have appropriate immunization records posted at Student Health Services prior to registering. Registration for Summer classes is available on line through ASU Interactivel or in person on a first-come, first-served basis and must be processed within the limits of course loads, class size, course restrictions and deadlines.   To complete your registration, fees must be paid by fee deadline dates or your registration will be cancelled. A Schedule/Billing Statement can be printed at any Registrar's site, or you may confirm your schedule and billing through ASU Interactive.

IMMUNIZATION REQUIREMENT
Students born after December 31, 1956, must provide the Student Health and Wellness Center  with documentation of measles (rubeola) immunization before registering.  At least one of the immunizations must have been received after 1979.  If submitting by U.S. Mail, allow two weeks for delivery and processing.

CREDIT LOAD
The Summer Sessions Credit load limit is 7 semester hours for each 5-week session, 9 semester hours for the 8-week session, and may not exceed a total of 14 semester hours for any combination of sessions.

NONDEGREE GRADUATE CREDIT
A maximum of 9 semester hours of graduate nondegree credits completed prior to admission to a graduate program at ASU may be applied toward a master's degree.

NONDEGREE UNDERGRADUATE CREDIT
A maximum of 15 semester hours of completed nondegree credits may be applied to a bachelor's degree program.
