Retirement Payments or Return of Contributions at Termination

The department:

  1. submits a termination Personnel Action form (PAF) to Human Resources (HR)

    and

  2. schedules an exit interview with the HR partner for the faculty member or academic professional.

If the terminating faculty member or academic professional elects to withdraw contributions or to retire, the HR partner refers the individual to the Arizona State Retirement Office.

The Arizona State Retirement Office provides for the return of faculty member’s or academic professional’s contribution (normally within six weeks) or for counseling regarding application for retirement payments.

Death Payments

The beneficiary contacts the Arizona State Retirement Office, which provides for payment of the benefit.
