May 30, 2006

11:00pm

Today was the second day of classes. I was relieved to see that we aren't going to be covering a chapter a day. It looks like a chapter every two days. That is still ridiculously intense, but it is better than a chapter a day.

Today we went to the Summer Palace, which is a big palace thing that covers 2.5 sq km and was built in 1750. Parts of it have been destroyed and rebuilt in a few wars, but it was amazing. After that, I went to eat at a Muslim restaurant ran by one of the smaller ethnic groups here, and the food was amaaaazing. It was so nice to have a meal that didn't have pork everything. The food was spicy and filling and just so good.

Picked up a Mao Zedong trinket today and the pollution was horrible. You could see the haze really well.

By the way, the Chinese may do a lot of things well, but breakfast is not one of them. I picked up some instant oatmeal at the market today. I'll let you know how Chinese oatmeal is tomorrow.
