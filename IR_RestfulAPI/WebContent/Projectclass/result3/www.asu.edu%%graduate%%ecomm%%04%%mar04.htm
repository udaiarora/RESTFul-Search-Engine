REMINDERS
Graduate College Awards for Tuition (GCAT) for Spring 2004—We still have funds to cover students with tuition waivers, spring 2004. We recommend focusing on nonresident waivers. Please email your GCAT form NO LATER THAN MARCH 5 to Catherine Lebihan at clebihan@asu.edu. www.asu.edu/graduate/resources/generalinfo/GCAT/

STEP-UP. While funds remain, the Graduate College may assist incoming first-year graduate students academically and financially. While reviewing applications for Fall 2004, please consider nominating students for research assistantships through STEP-UP. The Graduate College will match funds for nominations of promising new graduate students from underrepresented populations in their chosen field with a research assistantship under faculty supervision. Visit www.asu.edu/graduate/resources/generalinfo/stepup or contact Debra Crusoe (dcrusoe@asu.edu) or 5-9347.

SAM. For Fall 2004, academic units are advised to nominate a peer mentor who may be identified to help incoming graduate students succeed at ASU. The peer mentor position is a 3%-time research assistantship at the full expense (100%) of the Graduate College. Visit www.asu.edu/graduate/resources/generalinfo/SAM or contact Debra Crusoe at dcrusoe@asu.edu or 5-9347.
