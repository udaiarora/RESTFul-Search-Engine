Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

October 3, 2001

Present: Patty Feldman, Stefan Myhajlenko, Annie Platoff, Linda Shackle, Santos Vega
Absent: Carol Behl

The group edited and approved the minutes of September 5, 2001. A revised edition of the minutes will be distributed.

Chair's Report on Committee on Committees Meeting:

They discussed the preference survey that is used to find out on which committees people would like to serve. They are talking about revising the process.

Old Business:

The team decided to pursue the topic of the Arizona Regents University for our first speaker. Patty will make the arrangements.

We will select topics and invite speakers for other meetings as the year progresses.

The group decided to invite Gail Hackett to meet with CAPS members. The aim is to get a clearer understanding of the relationships between academic professionals and academic associates. This meeting will likely occur in November.

New Business:

The group discussed the possibility of offering workshops for APs on topics such as the promotion process. Should we get Gail Hackett's office to work with on this or do it on our own?

There was also a brief discussion of the current budgetary situation. How will affect APs?

Web Site:

The CAPS web site is located at http://www.asu.edu/provost/CAPS. The site has not been updated to include all of last year's meeting minutes. It will be updated in the future.

CAPS regular business meetings will be on the first Wednesday of each month. The next one will be Wednesday, November 7 at 10 a.m. in Room 214 of the Memorial Union. Santos volunteered to be the substitute secretary for the meeting in November. There will not be a CAPS meeting in December.

Annie Platoff, Secretary
