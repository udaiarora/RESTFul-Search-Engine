This group designs and administers a comprehensive benefits and retirement package. Teams are available to assist employees with service inquiries and transactions and to provide consultation on benefits, leaves and retirement programs and systems. Their purpose is:

  • To deliver a comprehensive and competitive benefits and retirement package to recruit and retain diverse, high-quality ASU faculty and staff.
  • To improve customer satisfaction through high-quality benefits and retirement counseling, leaves and disability management, and plan design and administration while continuously improving programs, processes and technology.

BENEFITS SERVICES
The HR Employee Service Center (ESC) assists employees with benefits service inquiries and transactions. See Your HR Service Team for all HR services provided by this team.

BENEFITS CONSULTING
The HR Benefits Consulting Unit administers the benefits programs/systems and provides consultation to departments on benefits, retirement, disability and leave programs. Employees also can make appointments to discuss complex benefit issues.

The HR Benefits Design Unit designs and communicates benefits, leaves and retirement programs and systems. This team ensures state and federal legislative and plan compliance, conducts research and analysis to improve programs, and designs and documents new plans, systems, employee communications, websites and presentations.
