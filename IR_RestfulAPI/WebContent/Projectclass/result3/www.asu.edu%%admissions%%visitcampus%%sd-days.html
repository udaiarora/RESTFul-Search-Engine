Sun Devil Days: ASU Visit for Prospective Undergraduate Students

Spend a day exploring our beautiful university and picture yourself at ASU! Sun Devil Days are information-packed open-house programs offering you and your family a more comprehensive view of ASU life. Sun Devil Days provide a comprehensive campus visit for prospective undergraduate students.

Sun Devil Days generally run from 8:30 a.m. to mid-afternoon and include, academic information sessions facilitated by representatives from ASU's Colleges and Schools as well as information sessions where you can learn about ASU's housing options, financial aid and scholarships and student resources. You may also take tours of ASU's campuses and residence halls.

All students who are interested in attending ASU are welcome to attend a Sun Devil Day. Choose from the following dates and campus locations:

  • October 7, 2006 at ASU's West campus.
  • October 9, 2006 at ASU's Tempe campus.
  • October 23, 2006 at ASU's Tempe campus.
  • November 3, 2006 at ASU's West campus.
  • November 13, 2006 at ASU's Tempe campus.
  • November 18, 2006 at ASU's West campus.
  • February 10, 2007 at ASU's West campus.
  • February 16, 2007 at ASU's Tempe campus.
  • February 23, 2007 at ASU's Polytechnic campus.
  • March 10, 2007 at ASU's West campus.
  • March 19, 2007 at ASU's Tempe campus.
  • March 23, 2007 at ASU's Tempe campus.
  • April 9, 2007 at ASU's Tempe campus.
  • April 13, 2007 at ASU's West campus.
