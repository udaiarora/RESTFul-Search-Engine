ASU Police Records

ASU Police Records is open Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., excluding holidays. In order to receive a copy of a releaseable police report the following is required:
    • Request a copy by calling 965-5645, sending an e-mail to Karon Zimpfer, filling out a request at the front desk of the DPS Administration Building, or, sending a written request by U.S. Mail to:

      Karon Zimpfer, Police Records Supervisor
      ASU DPS
      Box 0804
      Tempe, AZ 85287-0804
       
    • Please identify the report number when requesting a report.
    • There is a processing period of 72 business hours on all requests.
    • Reports can be picked up at the front desk in the Department of Public Safety Police Services building during business hours.
               

    Police reports are available for purchase at the following rates:

    Police Reports $5.00 first 30 pages
    $0.25 each page over 30
    Audio Tapes $10.00 each
    Video Tapes $25.00 each
    Photos $2.50 per photo
    $10.00 per contact sheet
    Clearance Letter $5.00

    Any person listed on a police report as a "victim" of a criminal offense is entitled to ONE FREE COPY.

    Accident reports will have the standard fees applied.

    Requests for statistical information will be filled when possible.  Please direct all requests to Karon Zimpfer, Police Records Supervisor, at 965-5645 or by e-mail to Karon Zimpfer.
