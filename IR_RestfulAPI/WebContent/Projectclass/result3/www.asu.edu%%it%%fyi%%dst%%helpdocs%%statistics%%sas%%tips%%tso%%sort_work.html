How to Increase Default Temporary Space?

I am running a SAS program on TSO and I am getting the following error message.

ERROR: Sort failed during output after writing 32510 observations successfully.
ERROR: Insufficient space in file WORK.DEMO1.DATA.

It seems that the temporary work space is not large enough. I keep increasing the region and time but it still fails.

The default WORK space available for SAS files is "SPACE=(6144,(500,200),,,ROUND)" which is approximately 525 tracks. However, you can add the following JCL statement after your EXEC statement to request more space.

//WORK DD UNIT=SYSDA,SPACE=(CYL,(p,s))

Where 'p' is the number of cylinders for primary allocation and 's' for secondary allocation. Furthermore, there are 15 tracks in one cylinder.
