The New American University is ASU’s vision for a university that is responsible for the economic, social, and cultural vitality of our region.

 

As a New American University, we seek to:

  • Provide quality education that is accessible to a broad population
  • Create a highly educated workforce
  • Generate economic growth
  • Conduct transdisciplinary research for the public good
  • Maintain a global perspective in our endeavors
