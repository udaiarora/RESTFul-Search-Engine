Payment Procedures

Be sure to watch your e-mail account for invoices and due dates!

Financial Aid

If you wish to use spring financial aid cash awards for Winter Session charges, you may authorize release of funds with your online registration request.

Credit Card

To pay with Visa, MasterCard or Discover, call the Winter Session office at (480) 727-9900, 8am – 4pm, Monday – Friday.

Check

To pay by check, enclose a copy of the invoice or indicate your name and ASU ID number on the check and make payable to Arizona State University.

Mail to Winter Session Business Office
PO Box 874001
Tempe, AZ 85287-4001

Cash

To pay by cash, visit the Winter Session registration office, Ritter B-132, 8am – 4pm, Monday – Friday.

Third Party

Winter Session accepts third party payers only if the student provides written authorization from the third party prior to registration request. No verbal authorizations will be accepted. Please note that the student is responsible for payment if the third party does not pay.

DO NOT make payments to your student account.
All payments must be received by the Winter Session registration office.

Payment Receipts

If you paid with financial aid funds and your registration is successful and funds are verified, you will receive a mailed registration confirmation indicating "held in FA/Student Account." If you paid for your registration by credit card, check or cash, once funds are verified, you will receive a registration confirmation and receipt.
