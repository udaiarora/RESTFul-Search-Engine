About Business and Finance

Welcome to the Office of the Executive Vice President
and CFO, Business and Finance

The Office of the Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer provides many services to the University, including Facilities Management, Purchasing, Financial Services, Mail, Parking, and central management and coordination of new construction projects through the Capital Programs Management Group.

The Office of the Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer strives to provide quality service and fiscal responsibility to this wide range of activities.
