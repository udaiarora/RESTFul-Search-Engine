If Federal Participation in the Equipment Is Less than $5,000

The department:

  1. prepares a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form for the property being salvaged

  2. sends the PRA to Surplus Property and Recycling

    and

  3. proceeds with PCS 1002–02, “Disposal of Equipment to Surplus Property and Recycling.”

If Federal Participation in the Equipment Is $5,000 or More

The department prepares a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form for the property being salvaged and sends the PRA to Surplus Property and Recycling.

Surplus Property and Recycling contacts the sponsored property coordinator for disposition instructions.

The sponsored property coordinator requests disposition instructions from the sponsoring agency.

Surplus Property and Recycling:

  1. processes the PRA form

  2. moves the equipment to Surplus Property and Recycling, if disposition instructions are not received within 120 calendar days.

  3. coordinates the sale of equipment

  4. deducts $500 or 10 percent of the proceeds, whichever is less, for administrative expenses

    and

  5. notifies the sponsored property coordinator of the proceeds remaining to be credited to the federal agency.

The sponsored property coordinator informs Surplus Property and Recycling of the agency/org to be credited.
