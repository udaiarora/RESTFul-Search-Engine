ASU Voice Mail Transition

The voice mail system upgrade previously scheduled for Fall of 2005 has been postponed until early 2006 when the next release of software will be available. The latest version of software will provide new enhancements and will improve some key features that will make ASUâ??s transition more seamless for the end users.

We would like to encourage you to take advantage of this additional time and confirm your ASU telephone extension number* and your ASU e-mail address using the online Extension Registration form (Requires ASURITE login).

Also, users are encouraged to subscribe to the Telephone Services announcement group at http://announce.asu.edu. Telephone Services uses this announcement group to notify its end users of outages, planned service upgrades, and other pertinent information, which will include updates regarding the voice mail transition.

* Note: Most voice mailbox numbers match the telephone extension number. If you are responsible for a voice mailbox not associated with your extension, please register that voice mailbox on the form.
