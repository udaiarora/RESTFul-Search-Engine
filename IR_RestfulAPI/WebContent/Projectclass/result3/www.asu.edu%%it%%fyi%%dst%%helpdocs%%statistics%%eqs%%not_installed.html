Why does EQS 5.1 state that "EQS is not installed or unauthorized copy"?

Question:
The output from EQS 5.1 states that "EQS is not installed or unauthorized copy". This message appears at the beginning of each page of output. What does it mean?

Response:
EQS 5.1 is producing the license error message because that part of the program is not Y2K compliant. Simply ignore the message. This will be fixed in later releases.
