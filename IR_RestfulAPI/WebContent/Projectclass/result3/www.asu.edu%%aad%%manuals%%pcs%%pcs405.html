PCS 405: Receiving Gifts and Donations

Purpose

To define a procedure for the receiving of gifts and donations to the university

Sources

Property Control
Financial Services
Development Office
Purchasing and Business Services

Policy

Gifts or donations received by the university must be reported to the Development Office for the purposes of acknowledging the gift and issuing an official ASU gift receipt.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Department
  1. Forward all gift information to the Development Office:

    1. donor name and address

    2. list of equipment donated

    3. approximate or appraised value of each item

      and

    4. originals of all correspondence, the postmarked envelope in which a gift is mailed, and all other relevant paperwork.
Development Office
  1. Issue an official ASU gift receipt acknowledging the gift.

  2. Forward a photocopy of the official ASU gift receipt to Property Control.

  3. Record the gift on Advantage.
Property Control
  1. Tag the equipment and add it to the property control system.
endtable

Cross-References

For more information on gifts to the university, see:

  1. the Financial Services Policies and Procedures Manual—FIN 303, “Gift Deposits”

    and

  2. the Purchasing and Business Services Policies and Procedures Manual—PUR 104, “Gifts and Gratuities.”
