Additional International Admission Requirements for Undergraduates

Beginning fall 2006, the following ASU Colleges/Schools have established freshman and transfer admission requirements beyond ASU's general university admission requirements and also have admission standards for the Professional Program status as well.

Before you apply, please ensure that you meet the following requirements if the major of your choice is administered by one of the following Colleges/Schools. When you apply, you have the option to apply to up to three majors. If your academic records or test scores do not meet your first choice, your application will be processed for your second or third choice. To search for undergraduate degree programs, use the Major Menu Tool.

W. P. Carey School of Business

 

Freshman International Students

Admission to the Professional Program

To be admitted directly to the Professional Program, freshman international applicants must meet the following requirements.

NOTE: You may apply to the Pre-professional Program or Pre-Business program if you do not meet requirements for direct admission to the Professional Program. Once you are a student at ASU, you will be required to apply to the Professional Program.

  • 3.6 GPA (GPA will be calculated by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • 1250 SAT or 28 ACT score and
  • Math requirement (to be determined by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • TOEFL 550 (paper-based), or 213 (computer-based), or iBT 79 (Internet-based), or IELTS 6.5. Exceptions to the TOEFL requirements are stated in the Admission Requirements and Application Procedures for international undergraduates.

Admission to the Pre-professional Program

To be admitted to the Pre-Professional Program, freshmen international applicants must meet the following requirements.

NOTE: Once you are a student at ASU, you will be required to apply to the Professional Program. Admission to the Professional Program is competitive, and, at a minimum will be based on overall GPA and performance in a set of skill set courses defined by each program.

  • 3.5 GPA on a scale of 4.0 (3.5 = B+ average; 4.0 = A average; GPA will be calculated by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • 1200 SAT or 25 ACT score and
  • Math requirement (to be determined by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • TOEFL 500 (paper-based), or 173 (computer-based), or iBT 61 (Internet-based), or IELTS 6.0. Exceptions to the TOEFL requirements are stated in the Admission Requirements and Application Procedures for international undergraduates.

Admission to the Pre-business Program

To be admitted to the Pre-business Program, freshman international applicants must meet the following requirements.

NOTE: Once you are a student at ASU, you will be required to apply to the Professional Program. Admission to the Professional Program is competitive, and, at a minimum will be based on overall GPA and performance in a set of skill set courses defined by each program.

  • 3.0 GPA on a scale of 4.0 (3.0 = B average; 4.0 = A average; GPA will be calculated by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • Submit your SAT or ACT score (can be taken at the end of the first semester at ASU) and
  • TOEFL500 (paper-based), or 173 (computer-based), or iBT 61 (Internet-based) or IELTS 6.0. Exceptions to the TOEFL requirements are stated in the Admission Requirements and Application Procedures for international undergraduates.

Transfer International Students

All qualified transfer international students will be admitted into Pre-business and must meet the following requirements.

NOTE: Once you are a student at ASU, you will be required to apply to the Professional Program. Admission to the Professional Program is competitive, and, at a minimum will be based on overall GPA and performance in a set of skill set courses defined by each program.

  • 30 transferable college credit hours and
  • 3.0 transfer GPA on a scale of 4.0 (3.0 = B average; 4.0 = A average; GPA will be calculated by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • 1040 SAT or 22 ACT (SAT or ACT test scores need to be submitted by the end of the first semester at ASU)
  • TOEFL500 (paper-based), or 173(computer-based), or iBT 61(Internet-based), or IELTS 6.0. Exceptions to the TOEFL requirements are stated in the Admission Requirements and Application Procedures for international undergraduates.

NOTE: An international transfer applicant who has fewer than 30 transferable college credit hours will be subject to the Pre-business Program requirements for freshman applicants (listed above).

Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering

 

Freshman International Students

Admission to the Professional Program

To be admitted directly to the Professional Program, freshman international applicants must meet the following requirements.

NOTE: You may apply to the Pre-professional Program if you do not meet requirements for direct admission to the Professional Program. Once you are a student at ASU, you will be required to apply to the Professional Program.

  • 3.5 transfer GPA (in U.S. college courses taken prior to high school graduation) and
  • 1400 SAT (combined Verbal and Math) or 32 ACT (composite) score and
  • No math or science deficiencies (to be determined by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • TOEFL 550 (paper-based), or 213(computer-based), or iBT 79 (Internet-based), or IELTS 6.5. Exceptions to the TOEFL requirements are stated in the Admission Requirements and Application Procedures for international undergraduates. 

Admission to the Pre-professional Program

To be admitted to the Pre-professional Program, freshmen international applicants must meet the following requirements.

NOTE: Once you are a student at ASU, you will be required to apply to the Professional Program. Admission to the Professional Program is competitive, and, at a minimum will be based on performance in a set of skill set courses defined by each program.

  • 3.0 GPA on a scale of 4.0 (3.0 = B average; 4.0 = A average; GPA will be calculated by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) or
  • 1140 SAT (combined Verbal and Math) or 24 ACT (composite) score and
  • No math or science deficiencies (to be determined by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • TOEFL 550 (paper-based), or 213(computer-based), or iBT 79 (Internet-based) or IELTS 6.50. Exceptions to the TOEFL requirements are stated in the Admission Requirements and Application Procedures for international undergraduates.

Transfer International Students

Admission to the Pre-professional Program

All qualified transfer international students will be admitted into the Pre-professional Program and must meet the following requirements.

NOTE: Once you are a student at ASU, you will be required to apply to the Professional Program. Admission to the Professional Program is competitive, and, at a minimum will be based on performance in a set of skill set courses defined by each program.

  • 24 transferable college credit hours and
  • 3.0 transfer GPA on a scale of 4.0 (3.0 = B average; 4.0 = A average; GPA will be calculated by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • No math and science deficiencies (determined by an international admission counselor within Undergraduate Admissions) and
  • TOEFL 550 (paper-based), or 213 (computer-based), or iBT 79on (Internet-based), or IELTS 6.50. Exceptions to the TOEFL requirements are stated in the Admission Requirements and Application Procedures for international undergraduates.

NOTE: An international transfer applicant who has fewer than 24 transferable college credit hours will be subject to the Pre-professional Program requirements for freshman applicants (listed above).
