 LISREL ESTIMATES (MAXIMUM LIKELIHOOD)
 
         LAMBDA-Y
 
               ETA 1      ETA 2
            --------   --------
    VAR 1       1.00        - -
 
    VAR 2        .92        - -
               (.04)
               25.99
 
    VAR 3        - -       1.00
 
    VAR 4        - -       1.14
                          (.03)
                          38.74
.92 is the value for Correlation Coefficient
(.04) is the value for Standard Error
25.99 is the t-value
