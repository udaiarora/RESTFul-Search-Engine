Biographical Sketch

[ CIO > Biographical Sketch >

Dr. Lewis received his B.S.E. degree from The Johns Hopkins University and his M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Northwestern University.

Dr. Lewis joined the ASU faculty in December of 1965 as an assistant professor of industrial engineering. He was promoted to associate professor in the fall of 1970 and full professor in 1981.

In the fall of 1980 he became the founding chair of the Department of Computer Science and assumed the role of associate dean of the College of Engineering and Applied Sciences in the fall of 1985. In July of 1993 he accepted the position of vice provost for information technology. Dr. Lewis assumed his current position of chief information officer and vice provost in January of 2003 and continues to hold the rank of tenured full professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering.
