ACD 603 Table

Faculty member or academic professional:

  1. contacts Human Resources (HR) to determine if the chosen financial institution is a member of the Arizona Clearinghouse Association

  2. obtains a Request for Direct Deposit form from HR

  3. completes the Request for Direct Deposit form and attaches a deposit slip

    and

  4. returns the Request for Direct Deposit form and deposit slip to HR.

HR:

  1. transfers electronically the faculty member’s or academic professional’s payroll funds to the financial institution

    and

  2. sends the faculty member’s or academic professional’s payroll stub to the individual through the employing department.
