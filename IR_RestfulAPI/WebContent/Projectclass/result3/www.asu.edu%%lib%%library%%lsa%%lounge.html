Lounge Policies

Hayden Concourse and 4th floor and Noble staff lounges are for the use of ALL Library employees.

  1. The newspaper is purchased with LSA monies and should always remain in the staff lounges. Please DO NOT remove the newspaper as it is put there to be enjoyed by all employees.
  2. The dish soap in the lounges is also supplied by LSA and it should also stay in the staff lounges.
  3. If you are planning a Department function or anything that requires any of the lounges to be used specifically for a few hours please put up a note that you are reserving the staff lounge on whatever date and the time you will be using it. Also specify which area of the lounge you want to reserve. If anyone needs to use LSA equipment such as a crock pot, punch bowl, etc., please contact an LSA board member.
  4. Major appliances will be repaired and/or purchased on approval by the LSA board.
  5. Cleaning of the appliances and general cleaning of the lounges is done on a volunteer basis so PLEASE, if you have made a mess, be mindful of their contributions and clean up after yourself. If you have dirty dishes please wash and put them away. DO NOT leave dishes in the drain tray or sink.
  6. In Hayden there are codes on the Concourse and 4th floor lounge doors. For the codes to these doors, please contact your Supervisor. You may also contact Deirdre Irvine for the Concourse floor code and the current LSA president for the 4th floor door.
  7. Please keep lounge doors closed at all times.
