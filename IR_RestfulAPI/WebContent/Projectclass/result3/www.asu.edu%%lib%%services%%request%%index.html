Items from another ASU campus library

Books

  • Click the "Request" button on at the top of the catalog information screen for the item you need.
  • To cancel a hold/request, please call the Circulation desk at any ASU Library: Downtown (602-496-0300); Polytechnic (480-727-1037); Tempe (480-965-3605); West (602-543-8520).

Articles

  • Tempe campus: Library Express/Document Delivery

  • Downtown Phoenix campus: Interlibrary Loan

  • West campus: Document Delivery

  • Polytechnic campus: Document Delivery/ILL

Items from Storage

  • To request materials housed in Storage, click the "Request" button on at the top of the catalog information screen if the volume you need is listed on the record.

If the volume is not listed, please contact circulation at 480-965-3605 or 480-965-2600, and a request will be made for you. Normal delivery time is 1-2 business days.

  • ASU Affiliates (Students, Faculty, Staff) may request articles from journals held in Storage through Interlibrary Loan (complete citation information is required). Articles will be scanned and delivered electronically.

Items to be delivered

  • Tempe campus: Library Express/Document Delivery
    Library Express provides books and photocopies of articles from any of the libraries in the ASU system for a fee. Faculty and Academic Professionals at the Tempe Campus are offered this service free of charge.

  • Polytechnic campus: Document Delivery/ILL
