 Employee Assistance Office

  

This team delivers high-quality HR customer service to applicants and employees. HR professionals assist employees with benefits, employment and payroll inquiries and transactions at one location or with a single telephone call. Their purpose is:

  • To provide the most cost-effective and efficient means of delivering HR services while simultaneously capturing data that allows the Office of Human Resources to improve its processes.
  • To improve internal and external HR customer satisfaction by providing timely and accurate assistance with HR services and transactions in one location or with a single call.
  • To free up other HR resources to provide administrative, consulting and strategic services and solutions to the ASU community.
