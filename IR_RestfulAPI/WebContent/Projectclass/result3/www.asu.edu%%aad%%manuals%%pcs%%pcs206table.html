When Property Is Donated to ASU as a Gift-in-Kind

The department notifies the college development officer (if one exists) or the Development Office.

The Development Office:

  1. acquires all documentation transferring title of the equipment to the university

  2. obtains any required signatures accepting the equipment on behalf of the university (e.g., general counsel, vice president)

  3. prepares a gift transmittal with the value, description of the equipment, name of donor, and department receiving the gift

  4. records the gift on the Official Gift Receipt form and sends a photocopy to Property Control

    and

  5. sends a letter of acknowledgment to the donor.

Property Control records the gift on Advantage.

When Equipment Is Donated to ASU through the ASU Foundation

The faculty member or student:

  1. prepares an ASU Foundation Request for Disbursement form

  2. attaches all receipts for the purchased equipment

    and

  3. sends the request and attachments to the Development Office.

The Development Office:

  1. prepares a check to reimburse the faculty member or student for the purchase

  2. makes a photocopy of the ASU Foundation check and forwards it, with all essential documents, to Property Control

    and

  3. records the gift on Advantage.

Note: When the ASU Foundation donates cash to university departments, the entry must be credited to a noncapital object code (e.g., revenue code).
