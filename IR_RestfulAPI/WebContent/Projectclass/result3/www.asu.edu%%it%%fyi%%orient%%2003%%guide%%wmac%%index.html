Web & E-Mail

[ Connecting > Guide > Web & E-Mail >

  • ASU is a connected campus, and has many on-line services, including myASU, ASU’s Web portal.
  • ASU offers e-mail services to all students, faculty, and staff.
  • EMMA is the Web application you can use to change your e-mail preferences.

myASU

ASU’s portal, myASU, is a customizable and personalizable Web page that gives users access to a mix of news, services, resources, and on-line courses. To use myASU, all you have to do is log in with your ASURITE UserID and password.

ASU on the Web

ASU’s Web site has a wealth of information useful to students, faculty, and staff. Several services are available online, including checking your grades, viewing your class schedules, and checking financial aid status.

ASU E-mail

Every student is automatically given an ASU e-mail address alias, usually firstname.lastname@asu.edu, which can be directed to any e-mail account you wish. It is the responsibility of the student to provide ASU with a working e-mail account address in order to receive official ASU correspondence.

As an enrolled student you may also subscribe to the ASU Student E-mail Service. You can add e-mail service by going to the ASURITE Web site. To access your ASU e-mail and manage changes to your e-mail service go to the EMMA Web site.

myASU

myASU is the Web-based system used to access online classes and course information. ASU also has a Web portal, which you can personalize and view news and services. To access either, just log in with your UserID and password.

EMMA

EMMA provides a common place for students, faculty, and staff at Arizona State University to access various electronic messaging services and information. With EMMA, users can read and manage their IMAP e-mail and configure the delivery of e-mail sent to their ASU e-mail addresses, among other services.


Faculty/Staff E-Mail

Faculty and staff may use Microsoft Outlook/Exchange instead of EMMA. Those wishing to participate in university-wide calendaring and scheduling should choose this option. Contact Computer Accounts to get an Exchange account. Contact your departmental support person for assistance with installing Outlook.

Web access to faculty and staff Exchange accounts is available at <http://exchange.asu.edu>.
