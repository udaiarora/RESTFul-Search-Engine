Resetting ASURITE (Kerberos) Password

ASURITE/Kerberos passwords can be reset online at the ASURITE Activation and Service Management website if you know your current ASURITE/Kerberos password or have setup the identity verification questions available at that Web site. Otherwise, you will need to come to the IT Help Desk (CPCOM 202) with valid photo ID in order to have the password reset.

Customers at ASU's West campus may go the IT Help Desk (CLCC 296) or to Technopolis (lower level of Fletcher Library) for password assistance.

Customers at ASU's Polytechnic campus may go to the Computing Commons (CNTR 150) for password assistance.

If you have a working ASU Exchange/Outlook e-mail account, we can reset the ASURITE/Kerberos password to something generic and e-mail it to that mailbox along with instructions for setting it to something more personal and secure.

To change a password other than your ASURITE (Kerberos) password, please see the Resetting Passwords page.
