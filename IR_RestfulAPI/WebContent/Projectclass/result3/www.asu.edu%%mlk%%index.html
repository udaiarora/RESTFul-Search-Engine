Tributes and recognition of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. and the accomplishments of the Civil Rights Movement have been celebrated at Arizona State University long before the creation of a state holiday in Arizona. Marches on the campus malls, speakers at the College of Law, and distinguished visitors to the University are all part of the legacy of leadership efforts ASU has made over the past twenty years. This site provides links to a variety of ways YOU may become involved in helping ASU to "Keep the Dream Alive!"
