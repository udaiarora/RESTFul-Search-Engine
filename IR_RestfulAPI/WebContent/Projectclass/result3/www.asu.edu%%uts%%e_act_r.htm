ACT Residual

Walk-ins are not permitted for this test.

Information
ACT Residual testing at ASU is used to help determine eligibility for undergraduate admission as well as admission to the W.P. Carey School of Business. Unlike the ACT National test, ACT Residual test scores are valid only at ASU. Study guides are available for pickup in our office.
Schedule
Available Dates for Exam
  • Friday, January 26 at 9:00 am
  • Monday, January 29 at 1:00 pm
  • Monday, February 5, 19 at 1:00 pm
  • Friday, February 9, 16, 23 at 9:00 am
Cost
The ACT Residual costs $35.00 and must be paid with a check or money order made out to Arizona State University..
Time
The exam consists of four multiple choice sections: English, math, reading and science reasoning. The total appointment time is four hours.
Register
In Person: Complete the ACT Residual registration form and submit it along with payment to an UTS staff member in 301 Payne Hall. At that time you will be given an admission ticket.
By Mail: Complete the ACT Residual registration form and submit it along with payment and a self addressed stamped envelope to:
Arizona State University
University Testing Services
PO Box 870511
Tempe, AZ 85287-0511
UTS must receive your registration and payment by 4:30 pm the day before your scheduled test date. Walk-ins are not allowed.
Reschedule
You must cancel or reschedule no later than seven days after your original test date.
  • To transfer the test date, submit your admission ticket along with a $5.00 check or money order payable to Arizona State University.
  • To cancel a test date and receive a refund of $17.50, you must fill out a refund request form available at our office.
Retake
You must wait 60 days since your last ACT Residual Test before you can retake the exam.
Admission
Exam entrance requires an admission ticket along with an original, valid, photo ID. The name on your photo ID must match the name on your admission ticket. You must report to the Testing Center at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time. No one will be admitted to the testing room once test booklets are distributed. No cell phones are allowed.
Calculators
Most four-function, scientific and graphing calculators may be used on the Mathematics test. The following types of calculators are prohibited:
  • calculators with a typwriter keypad (qwerty format)
  • calculators with built-in computer algebra systems such as:
    CASIO Algebra fx 2.0 Class Pad 300 and all model numbers that begin with CFX-9970G
    Texas Instruments model numbers that begin with TI-89 or TI-92
    Hewlett-Packard hp48GII and all model numbers that begin with hp40G or hp49G
You may go to www.actstudent.org or call 1-800-498-6481 to find out if your calculator is acceptable.
Scores
Test scores should be available for pickup in our office one day after the test date. Test scores are valid at ASU only and no additional score reports will be generated.
