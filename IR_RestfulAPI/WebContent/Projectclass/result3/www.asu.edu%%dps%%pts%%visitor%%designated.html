Attended Visitor Parking Lots

Access to the visitor parking lots is available from 6 a.m. to 10;30 p.m. Monday-Friday. Visitors should leave the visitor parking lot no later than 10:30 p.m. and pay the cashier upon exit. No overnight parking is allowed. The rate is $2.00 per hour, or portion thereof, with a $8.00 maximum exit fee.

Pay by Space Visitor Parking Lots

There are also 2 Pay by Space Visitor lots on campus. There are no attendants at these lots and each visitor must follow the instructions located at the pay machine. The rate is $2.00 per hour, or portion thereof, for up to 12 hours. Pay Machine visitor parking hours are from 7 a.m. to 10 p.m., Monday-Saturday (except at the University Services Building Lot 45 which is free on Saturdays). No overnight parking is allowed. Please keep in mind that all visitor lots are intended for short-term visitor parking, and are available to people who are NOT ASU students, faculty, or staff members. ASU students, staff, and faculty members should purchase an annual ASU parking decal.

Visitor Lot reservations can only be made by ASU Departments for their guests in PS-1 Visitor, PS 4 Visitor and ASU Foundation Structure. Validations are available only from those departments that purchase validation stamps, and it is up to each individual department to decide if they will sponsor visitor parking. ASU Parking and Transit Services will not validate parking. Please note that the Pay & Display lots do not accommodate reservations, validations and are first-come, first-serve.

The campus visitor lots accept cash only. Personal checks, business checks, travelers checks, and credit/debit cards are not accepted.

The visitor lots are VERY busy during the morning and afternoon hours, so please keep in mind that space is limited and space is available on a first-come-first-serve basis. If the lot you would like to park in is full, you may purchase a temporary parking permit at ASU Parking and Transit Services (Please go back to the main menu and select Temporary Permits).
