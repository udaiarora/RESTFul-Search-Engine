"Hairspray" fills Gammage air in April

Broadway's new phenomenon takes you back to 1962 Baltimore as Tracy Turnblad sets out to dance her way onto TV's most popular show. Can a big girl with big dreams - and even bigger hair - change the world and still have time to win the boy she loves?

As the New York Times says, "If life were everything it should be, it would be more like 'Hairspray.' It's irresistible!"

"Hairspray" will run at Gammage, April 13 - 25. Tickets are $77.50-$22.25. ASU's Gammage Box office hours are Monday-Friday, 10 am - 6 pm.

Groups receive discounts on select performances. For group services call (480) 965-6678. Children and students receive discounts up to 50 percent off select performances, American Express cardholders receive $3 off American Express Beyond Broadway performances. For information, contact ASU's Gammage Box Office at (480) 9654-3434, Ticketmaster at 480-784-4444 or visit (asugammage.com).

All M&I Bank Broadway in Arizona performances are presented by arrangement with ASU Public Events and Clear Channel Entertainment, Inc. Media support is provided by The Arizona Republic, KTVK-TV 3 and KEZ 99.9 FM.

Medina, with ASU Public Events, can be reached at (480) 965-1884 or (kariann.medina@asu.edu).
