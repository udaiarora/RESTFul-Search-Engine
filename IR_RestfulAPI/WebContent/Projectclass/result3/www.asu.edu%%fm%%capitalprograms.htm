CAPITAL PROGRAMS MANAGEMENT GROUP
provides the following services to the University community:

Capital Programs

  • Capital Improvement Plan
  • Capital Development Plan
  • Capital Program development
  • Capital Project tracking and reporting
  • Regent and University Reports
  • Annual call for minor capital projects

Design/Construction Management

  • Design implementation
  • Construction services
  • Warranty and post construction
  • Job order contracting
  • ADA project coordination
  • Asbestos and Lead-based Paint Programs

Engineering Services

  • Engineering Support
  • Campus Infrastructure
  • Utility Analysis
  • Building Permits
  • Construction Inspections
  • Deferred Maintenance Inspections & Reporting
  • MS4s-Stormwater Management & Reporting
  • Drywell Management
For on-going information regarding construction projects on campus, the ASU Construction Updates page provides a listing of Capital Projects and a major construction map, updated monthly.
