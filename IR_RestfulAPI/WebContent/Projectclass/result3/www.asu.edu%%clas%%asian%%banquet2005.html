The ASU University Club is located at 425 East University Drive on the ASU Main Campus in Tempe. Parking will be available.

Directions by car: if traveling east on University Drive, take the first right turn after the light at College Avenue; if traveling west, take the left turn immediately after passing under the ASU bridge. This small road is called Normal Lane, and it leads directly to the University Club parking lot.
