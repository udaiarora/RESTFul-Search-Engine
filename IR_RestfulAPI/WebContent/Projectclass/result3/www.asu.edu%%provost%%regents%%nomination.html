Nomination Form
Arizona State University Regents' Professor

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Any tenured or tenure-track member of the Arizona State University faculty may nominate individuals who are full professors at ASU and have made exceptional contributions as indicated in the guidelines (see attached memo). Self-nominations are prohibited.
  2. Nominations are made by completing and submitting this nomination form, together with a complete curriculum vitae for the candidate by January 27, 2006, to the Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University. Nominations may also be completed online at www.asu.edu/provost/regents/nomination.html. The nomination form and CV may be hand delivered to the Foundation Building, Room 420, addressed to the Regents' Professor Nomination Committee or faxed to the attention of the Regents' Professor Nomination Committee at 480-965-0785
  3. All nominations will be sent to the candidate's department and college for review prior to the selection committee's initial screening.
  4. The selection committee reviews the submissions and selects a group of finalists. In evaluating the candidate?s research, scholarship, or other creative activity, the committee relies on the nomination form and vitae to demonstrate a pioneering contribution and distinguished stature in the relevant field, as indicated, for example, by recognition by broadly based professional societies, publications in first-rank journals, or critical acclaim by other academics or professionals. The committee also looks for indications in the form and vitae of significant contributions in teaching and professional service.
  5. The committee collects additional information on faculty it designates as semifinalists. It makes inquiries of deans, department chairs, and leading individuals in the semifinalists' fields. A semifinalist's nominator is expected to assist the committee in the collection of this information.
