Hotspot: _hideHotspots

Hotspot: _hideHotspots


Removes highlighting from all objects intersected by a hotspot

Syntax
Details

Syntax

CALL NOTIFY (hotspot-name, '_hideHotspots');

Details

When a hotspot's highlighting is removed, the highlighted area reverts to the object's color, and the hotspot outline is removed from the display.
