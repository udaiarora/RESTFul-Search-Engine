Field Training Officer:

You may fill out the FTO forms online.  Click on the form name below:

Officer's Daily Evaluation

Phase Summary Report

Instructions:
  • To move forward from field to field, use the TAB key.
  • To select one of the multiple choice options (1 - 7, NRT, or NO), put the mouse pointer over the answer you want to chose and then click the left mouse button. An "X" will appear over the answer you have chosen.
  • To clear the form after you have entered information, wave the mouse pointer over the badge image in the upper left-hand corner of page 1 of the form.  This action will clear the entire form; to remove information in individual fields on the form, use the delete or backspace keys.
  • To print the form after you have completed it, click once in any blank white space on the form and then click the Netscape or Internet Explorer Print button.  (Be careful, however, not to click near the badge image as this may clear the form before you intend to.)
    • Note:  it is not possible to save the form after you have completed it.

    If you have any questions or problems completing these forms, contact  FTO Forms Online
