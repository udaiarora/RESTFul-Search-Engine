Setting Up Your Active Sync Up To Date Notifications

These instructions will create a constant communication between your Windows Device and your Outlook Exchange Account. 

When you receive an e-mail in your Outlook, the Exchange Server will send an SMS message to your phone using this information.  This SMS message will tell your handheld to retrieve messages from the server.  This creates a process similar to the Blackberry "push" technology, keeping your handheld up to date with the most recent items in your inbox.

 

Complete these steps AFTER you have synced for the first time and your wireless synchronization is working properly.

1.  Using your handheld, go to Active Sync > Options

2.  On the Server tab, click on the Options Menu.

3.  You should see the screen where you entered the server login credentials (your ASURite User ID), click on the Device Address button.  If you do not see a Device Address button, you will need to upgrade your firmware with the most recent version of your handheld software.  This is available from the product manufacturer.

4.  In the field marked "test messaging address" enter your SMS address.  This address is generally your 10 digit phone number@"the carrier's text domain.com".  An example of a sprint address would be 6025559988@messaging.sprintpcs.com; the same number on Verizons network is 6025559988@vtext.com.  Check with your carrier to verify the address your phone would use.

5.  Click OK and change the tabs to the Moble Schedule tab to change any options to "As items arrive".
