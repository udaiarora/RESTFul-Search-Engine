DGS Awarded Grant to Take Part in National Doctoral Completion Project

DGS has been awarded a grant to study how to increase doctoral degree completion rates, including those of minorities and women in fields where they have been traditionally underrepresented or experience disproportionately high attrition. ASU is one of 21 institutions selected from a highly competitive pool to receive the grant to participate as a research partner in the Ph.D. Completion Project led by the Council of Graduate Schools. The three–year project is designed to address attrition issues in the areas of the sciences, engineering, and mathematics, as well the humanities and social science disciplines. www.asu.edu/graduate/aboutus/news/2004/112204.htm
