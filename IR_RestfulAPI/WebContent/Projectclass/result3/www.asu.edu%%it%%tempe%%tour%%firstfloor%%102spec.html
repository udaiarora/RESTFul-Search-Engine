Room Specifications - Atrium Computing Site

Service Description

Phone: 965-4459
Email: compsites@asu.edu
Web: http://www.asu.edu/it/fyi/student/compsite.html

The Atrium computing site encompasses most of the first floor in the Computing Commons. This site is equipped with approximately 230 high-end PC, and Macintosh workstations.

Infrastructure

  • Telecommunications
    • Data connections (1 per workstation/printer)
    • Voice connections (at PC and Mac Helpdesks)
  • Lighting
    • Skylight generated
    • Fluorescent zumtobel Indirect/Direct Luminaire suspended 18" from ceiling (ceiling ht @ 13'0") throughout

Equipment

  • Various. See computing site equipment charts

Special Amenities

  • Furniture
    • Task chairs
    • 11 Workstation Pods (capacity per/pod 16) which include:
      • Maximum freestanding plastic laminate straight worksurface 60"W x 28"H x 30"D
      • Maximum plastic laminate corner worksurface 48"W x 28"H x 48"D
      • Maximum plastic laminate curved worksurface 60"W x 28"H x 30"D
      • CPU Holder 10"W x 22"H x 19"D (mounted to underside of worksurface)
      • Acoustical/tackable fabric dividers
    • 10 Workstation Clusters (capacity per/cluster 6) which include:
      • Maximum plastic laminate worksurface 48"W x 28"H x 48"D
      • Acoustical/tackable fabric dividers
      • 2 Helpdesk Areas (capacity per/helpdesk 2)
  • ADA
    • Electric adjustable plastic laminate worksurface 48"W x ( 26" - 40.25"H ) x 48"D
    • All students, not just those who use a wheelchair, enter the first floor computing site via ramps.
    • Adaptive technology hardware and software are provided. Click here for current information
