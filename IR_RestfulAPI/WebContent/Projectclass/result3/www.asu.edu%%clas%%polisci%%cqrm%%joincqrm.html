JOIN CQRM

        

        

 

  

Institutions wishing to join the Consortium can take up one of three levels of institutional sponsorship. Premium sponsors pay an annual membership fee of $4,150, and have three institute slots reserved for their students or junior faculty. Regular sponsors pay  $3,100, and  have two spaces reserved for their nominees. Affiliates pay an annual membership fee of $1,550, and have one institute slot reserved for their students or junior faculty.

 

To join the Consortium please email consortium@asu.edu to request a membership form.
