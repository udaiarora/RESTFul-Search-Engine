Legislative and Government Internship Program
County Supervisors Association of Arizona

COUNTY SUPERVISORS ASSOCIATION OF ARIZONA
1905 W. Washington, Suite 100
Phoenix, Arizona 85009
Contact: Todd Madeksza
(602) 452-4504

The County Supervisors Association of Arizona represents the 15 county elected boards of supervisors in Arizona. The boards of supervisors are the primary policy making body in each county.

The County Supervisors Association is hiring one full-time paid intern for the months of January ? May. There are no holiday or semester breaks. The intern will be registered as a paid lobbyist with the Arizona Secretary of State. Undergraduate and Graduate students are encouraged to apply.

Duties include: bill tracking, legislative research, attending caucus and floor sessions, attending committee hearings, contributing articles for the Legislative Update (published weekly), presenting legislative information to the Legislative Policy Committee, and communicating with county Supervisors and staff on a regular basis.

To apply, complete the "Arizona Legislative and Government Internship Program" application. Applications should also be accompanied by a resume and a brief writing sample. For additional questions, please contact Todd Madeksza directly at (602) 452-4504.
