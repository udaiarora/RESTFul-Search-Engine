Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

March 6, 2002

Present: Carol Behl, Patty Feldman, Stefan Myhajlenko, Annie Platoff, Linda Shackle, Absent: Santos Vega

The group approved the minutes of February 6, 2002 with changes.

CAPS Speakers:

Barbara Mawhiney, Director of Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Office, has agreed to speak on the topic "Rationality of the Job Classification System for Academic Professionals." The presentation will be on Wed. April 3, 2002 from 10:30-11:30 AM in the Pinal Room (Rm. 215) of the Memorial Union. We will publicize the event through the AP e-mail distribution list and the Insight.

Terms for CAPS members and election of new members:

Below is a list of members and when their term expires. Anne Platoff notified the committee that she is resigning from her position at ASU as of the end of April. The Committee on Committees has recruited a slate of candidates for the upcoming election.

· Carol Behl (2004)
· Patricia Feldman (2004)
· Stefan Myhajlenko (2003)
· Anne Platoff (2004)
· Linda Shackle (2002)
· Santos Vega (2002)

Old Business:

The group has not received a response to the letter we sent to Dr. Morton Munk, President of the Academic Senate, about voting rights for Academic Associates.

New Business: none

CAPS regular business meetings will be on the first Wednesday of each month. The next one will be Wednesday, February 6 at 10 a.m. in Room 214 of the Memorial Union. Santos volunteered to be the substitute secretary for the meeting in February. There will not be a CAPS meeting in January.

Annie Platoff, Secretary
