The department relinquishing equipment:

  1. completes a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form (see PCS 501A)

    Note: Forms are available in Property Control.

  2. records the ASU property control number (ASU PC#), if one exists

  3. obtains the approval of both the releasing and receiving departments

    and

  4. sends the PRA to Property Control.

If Department of Facilities Management movers are not needed, Property Control updates its records to reflect the new custodial unit, location, and other pertinent data.

If Department of Facilities Management movers are needed, Property Control retains its copy for departmental files and forwards the original and the remaining copies to the Department of Facilities Management.

The Department of Facilities Management verifies that the numbers are on the PRA or writes them on the original.

If ASU PC#s are affixed to the equipment, the Department of Facilities Management:

  1. relocates the equipment for the requesting department

  2. signs the PRA upon completion of the move

    and

  3. forwards the original to Property Control.

Property Control:

  1. matches the original with the copy previously signed by the department

  2. reviews the original and the copy for changes

  3. reconciles discrepancies with the Department of Facilities Management

    and

  4. updates Property Control records to reflect the new custodial unit, location, and other pertinent data.
