Human Resources - Professional Development

Enrollment Eligibility

Eligibility for classes, workshops, and general online services offered through the Learning and Performance Solutions Group of the Office of Human Resources is based on several factors. Below is the official policy, followed by related resources.

  • Effective January 1, 2003 employees are eligible to register for workshops and seminars through the Learning and Performance Solutions Group after an ASU ID number is assigned by Human Resources (Payroll) and entered into HRMS.
  • Human Resources Payroll can be reached at (480) 965-2701 for ASU ID# verification.

Exceptions to this policy are listed below. These exceptions allow new employees or non-ASU employees to register for a limited number of offerings.

  • New Employee Orientation: To register for New Employee Orientation please see the following page for more information: http://www.asu.edu/hr/new_employee.html.
  • Information sessions for prospective ASU employees wishing to learn about the ASU application process. See the following page for more information: http://www.asu.edu/hr/jobs/faq_about_jobs.html.
  • ASU Foundation employees, ASU Volunteers, and others who are not and will not be paid through the ASU Human Resources payroll system.
