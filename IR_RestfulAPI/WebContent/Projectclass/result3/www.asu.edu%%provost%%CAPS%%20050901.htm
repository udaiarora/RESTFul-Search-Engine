Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

September 1, 2005 ? Called to order 10:00 am

Members Present:  Beth DiFelice, Judy Grace, Lydia LaFaro, Brenda Shears, Dan Stanton, Barbara Trapido-Lurie

Committee members introduced themselves.

Old business

ACD-507:  Committee members discussed the most recent draft of ACD-507, Academic Professional Personnel Actions.  It was agreed that the document should be circulated among all APs within the next few days, in order to allow time for their comments before the planned first and second readings of the document at the Academic Senate meetings in September and October.

 

Marjorie Zatz:  New Vice Provost for Academic Personnel, Marjorie Zatz, introduced herself to the committee.  She and the committee discussed ACD-507 and other issues likely to warrant CAPS? attention in the year ahead.

 

Academic Senate:  Barbara Trapido-Lurie presented highlights of the August Senate meeting.

 

New Business

 

Selection of committee roles:  Committee members accepted the following assignments for 2005-2006:

  • Chair ? Barbara Trapido-Lurie
  • Academic Senate representative ? Dan Stanton
  • Committee on Committees representative ? Judy Grace
  • PAC-20 representative ? Brenda Shears
  • Secretary ? Beth DiFelice
  • Archivist / Webmaster ? Lydia LaFaro

 

Future activities:  The committee discussed the possibility of holding a Fall Spotlight Session focusing on the policies currently being developed for Postdoctoral Associates.  Barbara Trapido-Lurie will present this idea to Marjorie Zatz.

 

Meeting adjourned at 11:40 am
