Human Resources - Payroll

Payroll Information

Salaried Employees

Payment is semi-monthly, 24 time a year, on the 15th of the month and at the end of the month. The payroll is run three to four days prior to the actual payday. Therefore, any changes to pay, such as tax deferred deductions, and the like need to be in the Service Center no later than the paperwork due date listed in the payroll calendar.

Note: Faculty are paid on an 18 pay cycle (August 16 through May 15) unless they elect the 24 pay cycle. For Election Form see HR web site.

Temporary and Student Employees

Payment is every two weeks, 26 times a year according to the Biweekly schedule listed under "Related Links".

Related Payroll Items

  • If you do not have direct deposit of your paychecks, you can arrange for that by bringing a blank, voided check to the Service Center and completing a Direct Deposit form - also available on the web.
  • Changes in your tax withholding for State (A-4) and Federal (W-4) Tax purposes can be made anytime. Forms are available on the web and in the Service Center.
  • Earned Income Credit Advance Payment Certificates (W-5) are available in the Service Center and on the web.
  • FEE Waivers are obtained at the FEE Payment Office located on the second floor of the Student Services Building.
  • Tuition Waiver forms are available on the web and at the Service Center. Waivers for classes at NAU, the U of A, and for retirees should be brought to the Service Center for processing. Waivers for classes at ASU should be taken to the Student Fee Payment Office in the Student Services Building. Tuition Waivers for Graduate level courses 500 and above are taxable for spouses and dependents.
  • W-2 Forms will be sent between January 1, and January 31, each calendar year. Be sure the Center always has your current home address.
