SUN DEVIL FITNESS

Sun Devil Fitness - Instructors

The 2006-2007 Sun Devil Fit instructors are geared up for an action packed spring schedule. Although you will recognize many of our returning instructors, some new faces will be leading classes this year! All instructors are trained to provide current fitness instruction that will contribute to your health, well-being, and fitness goals in a fun and motivational manner.
