ECUADOR: The Amazon and the Andes Field School

Summer Teacher Fellowships
Second Session: July 4-28, 2006


An Andean quichua
lady displays her
corn crop

K-12 and community college teachers with an interest in Latin America are eligible to receive one of five $1,000.00 teacher fellowships to participate in the second session (July 4-28, 2006) of the Andes and the Amazon Summer Field School in Ecuador.

Recipients must:

  • be U.S. citizens or permanent resident
  • enroll in IED/REL 494/591: Teaching Indigenous Cultures for 1-3 credits
  • submit a letter of recommendation on University letterhead.
  • must take coursework for a grade and cannot audit classes covered by the award.

***Preference is given to applicants who can communicate in Spanish***


Quichua students in salasaca classroom

To apply: Please send a current résumé and a letter of interest written on school letterhead, describing your Spanish speaking abilities and plans for incorporating the knowledge and experience gained from the field school into your classroom curriculum.
