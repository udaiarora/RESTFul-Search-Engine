Catalog Coordinator Checklists

Catalog coordinators receive selected checklists to complete with each General Catalog draft. The purpose is to find and resolve discrepancies before they’re printed.

If you have questions about the role of the catalog coordinator or checking steps required on each catalog draft, contact Steve Gobbell by e-mail at sgobbell@asu.edu or by phone (5-1568).

Curricular Processing Flow Calendar

Find out when your proposal will receive its final approval. The Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University provides a “Curricular Processing Flow Calendar” (Word file) for actions requiring Arizona Board of Regents (ABOR) approval and actions not requiring ABOR approval. See the corresponding page numbers for approval levels and applicable dates.

Actions requiring ABOR approval or approval of the ABOR executive director include:

•degree program: planning (page 1)
•degree program: implementation (page 2)
•degree program: disestablishment, name change, or merger (page 3)
•academic unit: establishment, disestablishment, name change, or merger (page 3)
•centers, bureaus, institutes, laboratories: establishment, disestablishment, name change, or merger (page 4)

Actions not requiring ABOR approval include:

•concentration or minor: establishment, disestablishment, name change (page 5);
•Academic minors in a subject area for which there is no degree program must be approved by CAOs; ABOR Executive Director approves and reports to Board for consideration. See ACD 305-12.
•certificate programs: establishment, disestablishment, name change, or merger (page 6)
•new courses and special cases of course deletions and changes (page 7)
