Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

August 2, 2000

Present: Maggie Mangini, Helen Seaton, and Santos Vega

The Committee on Academic Professionals (CAPS) met in the Conference Room of Noble Science and Engineering Library on August 2. Three of the members were unable to attend but Maggie, Helen and Santos conducted the business of electing new officers and selecting a meeting time and place.

Chair: Santos Vega
Vice Chair: Maggie Mangini
Secretary: Helen Seaton
Archivist/WebMaster: Linda Shackle

Meetings will be held on the first Wednesday of each month from 10:00-11:30 a.m. at the Memorial Union. The meeting dates for the coming year are:

September 6; October 4; November 1; December 6; February 7; March 7; April 4; May 2; August 1 (transition meeting of old and new committee).

Note that no neetings are scheduled for January, June or July 2001.

At the next meeting, we will set our agenda for the coming year and discuss what we think should be our goals or focus this year. Some ideas were: invite the new vice provost to meet with our committee at a later date; offer a workshop for APs on how to prepare a promotion packet; develop a networking directory; have some no-host luncheons and feature the work of APs in various departments.

The next meeting will be on Wednesday, September 6. This one meeting will begin at 9:00 a.m. so that Sanots can attend. It will be in Room 204 (Copper) at the MU.

Helen Seaton, Secretary
