The Music Index Tutorial
    The Music Index Tutorial
"Go to the Arizona State..."
    Go to the Arizona State University Music Library Webpage (http://www.asu.edu/lib/music) and click on Reference.
"Scroll down to Electronic Indexes..."
    Scroll down to Electronic Indexes/Abstracts for Music and click on Music Index Online.
"Click on ?Music Index..."
    Click on ?Music Index Online? when on campus or sign in when off campus.
"In order to view the..."
    In order to view the help menu, go the the basic search screen and click on ?Instructions & Help.?
"You may resize the two..."
    You may resize the two windows by dragging them as desired.  The Help Topics appear in the bottom window.
"More information here about the..."
More information here about the help topics.
"To do an expert search"
    To do an expert search, click on ?Go to expert search.?
"The important fields for most..."
   The important fields for most searches include keywords, document type, language, and range of publication dates .
"The search engine within this..."
The search engine within this database is NOT case sensitive.

"To save a full citation"
To save a full citation, you may print from your computer, copy it and paste it into a word processing document, or write it down.  Music Index does not offer any save or email functions.
