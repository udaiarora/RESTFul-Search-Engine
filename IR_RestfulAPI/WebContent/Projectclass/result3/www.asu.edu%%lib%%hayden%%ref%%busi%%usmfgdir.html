COMPANY INFORMATION SOURCES

U.S. Manufacturer's Directories


Check also Telephone and E-Mail Directories (http://www.asu.edu/lib/ref/gen/phbks.html) to find basic directory information on companies by name, location, or product/service.
  1. Colt Microfiche Library of State Directories. Red Bank, NJ: Colt Microfiche, 1980- . Annual. (ASU owns 1980- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Microforms.)
      HC 102.2 .C6 Fiche HAYDEN REFERENCE (Reference Microforms Cabinet)

    This microfiche set contains individual state manufacturing or industrial directories. These can sometimes be the only place to find information on small private companies. The information given may be only name and address or may include a total sales figure or number of employees.

  2. MacRae's Blue Book. 2 vols. New York: MacRae's Blue Book, 1893- . Annual. (ASU owns latest year.)
      T 12 .M3 HAYDEN REFERENCE & SCIENCE REFERENCE

    The "original" industrial directory, emphasis is on identifying suppliers of original equipment. It includes corporate, trade name, and product classification indexes. Information is very basic.

  3. Thomas Register of American Manufacturers also Thomas Register Catalog File. 27 vols. New York: Thomas Publishing, 1905- . Annual. (ASU owns latest year.)
      T 12 .T6 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A) & SCIENCE REFERENCE
      http://www.thomasregister.com (registration required online)

    The most comprehensive list available of manufacturing firms and specific products in the U.S. and Canada. Companies are arranged by type of product (vols. 1-17) and indexes are included by company name and brand name (vols. 18-19, "Company Profiles"). Actual product catalogs from many companies comprise the rest of the set (vols. 19-27). The Internet version offers many of the same features in an online searchable format but covers fewer companies. For the food industry, see the Thomas Food Industry Register in paper (HD 9321.3 .T5 Hayden Reference) and online (http://www.tfir.com).

  4. U.S. Industrial Directory. 2 vols. Stamford, CT: Cahners, 1940- . Annual. (ASU owns latest year.)
      T 12 .C769 HAYDEN REFERENCE & SCIENCE REFERENCE

    Similar to Thomas Register (above) but not as comprehensive. Vol. 1 is the telephone-address directory and Vol. 2 is the product directory.
