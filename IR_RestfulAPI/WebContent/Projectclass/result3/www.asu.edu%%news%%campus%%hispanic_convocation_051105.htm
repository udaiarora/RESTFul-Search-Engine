May 11, 2005

ASU celebrates tradition with Hispanic Convocation

ASU’s Hispanic community will celebrate 20 years of tradition and student achievement with its Hispanic Convocation at 5 p.m., May 14.

“We anticipate about 250 graduates participating in this convocation,” says Misty Cisneros, program coordinator senior of ASU’s Hispanic Convocation.

The convocation is scheduled to take place at Wells Fargo Arena, with doors opening at 4 p.m. featuring entertainment from Mariachi de ASU.

Cisneros says this year’s convocation will honor graduates and alumni who have “paved the way for our younger generations,” including the original ASU Hispanic Convocation Committee.

“We have invited Latino alumni from the years of 1984 – 1995, 1994 – 1995 and 2004 to participate in this year’s convocation,” Cisneros says. “Congressmen Ed Pastor will be providing the keynote address on how the Hispanic Convocation was created and how it has evolved over the years.”

ASU President Michael Crow will also speak at the convocation that will also feature the participation of ASU’s faculty and staff who are members of the university’s Hispanic community.

This year’s emcee is Ricardo Torres, chief executive officer of Latino Insight. Torres is involved in the community and is one of the first to donate to the Una Promesa Campaign. The Una Promesa Campaign is a marketing strategy being used to collect more funds for Hispanic Community Scholarship Program (HCSP). Graduates who have received financial assistance from the HCSP also will be recognized at this year’s convocation.

“This convocation is so full of life and spirit,” Cisneros says. “It’s an amazing feeling to walk across the platform, look out in to the audience and see how proud your family and friends are of you – and know that all your hardwork has paid off.”

Food City is sponsoring a reception immediately following the event, open to all graduates, family, friends, faculty, staff and alumni.

For more information about ASU’s Hispanic Convocation, contact Cisneros at (480) 965-8890 or (misty.cisneros@asu.edu).

Romero, with Marketing & Strategic Communications, can be reached at (480) 727-3116 or (mlromero@asu.edu).
