Videos for Classes


Video Courses Online are now being provided by Distance Learning and Technology.

For more information contact Distance Learning at:

Email: distance@asu.edu

Phone: (480) 965-6738


Media from the Libraries' collection placed on Course Reserves are available for checkout at the Circulation/Reserve desk at Hayden Library. Photo ID is required for checkout. Most media items on Course Reserves have a 4 hour, in library use only loan.



For more information, call (480) 965-3605.


Searching for Videos in the Online Catalog


The online catalog can be used to locate videos in all formats in the ASU Libraries including videos located in branch libraries, library departments, and at West campus.

To find a specific video, search by title.  You can then choose the "Limit /Sort Search" button .

 

 

and choose "Video/Film" from the Material Type dropdown menu:

 


 


To find a video on a topic, search by keyword , and choose "Video/Film" from the Material Type dropdown menu.

 


Requesting Videos


From another campus

You can request videos from the West campus or Polytechnic campus to be sent to you through our online catalog by clicking on the "Request" button at the top of the catalog information screen . Please note: you cannot use the "Request" button to place videos on hold for you that are available (the status will say "SHELF") at your campus location.

 


This will bring you to a screen that will ask for your ASURITE ID and password (unless you have already entered them) . You then need to select the library where you want to pick up the video when it becomes available. (Because of their short term loan period, videos may not be requested for delivery by Library Express). Then click on "Submit".


Community Card patrons may borrow media with shelf status by going to the owning library and checking the item out. Community Card patrons may not place a request on media that is checked out or have media paged from another ASU Library.


Instructors who need items for in class viewing may still use the online booking form (see below). Bookings for class use will take precedence over requests and may delay fulfilling requests made online.


That are Checked Out

If the video you want is currently checked out, follow the same steps outlined above to request it through the online catalog. It will place the video on hold for you as soon as it is returned. Please note: you cannot use the "Request" button to place videos on hold for you that are available (the status will say "SHELF") at your campus location.


Community Card patrons may borrow media with shelf status by going to the owning library and checking the item out. Community Card patrons may not place a request on media that is checked out or have media paged from another ASU Library.


For Classroom/Seminar Use

Use the online media booking form to request videos that are required for a specific time period for a course presentation or seminar use. Current ASU faculty, staff and students may reserve videos for a specific date.
