Suggested Timeline for Retiring Employees

       Arizona State Retirement System Participants

       •  2-5 years prior to retirement:

          Attend an ASRS Pre-Retirement Seminar held at ASRS

       •  1 year prior to retirement:

          Attend an ASRS Retirement Group Meeting held at ASU or

          Attend an ASRS Retirement Group Meeting held at ASRS

       •  3 months prior to retirement:

          Contact Social Security to apply for Medicare, if applicable

       •  30 days prior to retirement:

          Attend an ASU Retirement Meeting conducted by a Benefits

          Representative to discuss benefit options and to complete any

          necessary paperwork.  Call (480) 965-2701 to schedule a date.

          Benefit Office Retirement Meetings held at ASU

         

       Optional Retirement Plan Participants

        •  6 months prior to retirement:

           Contact your ORP provider:

                    AIG VALIC              602.674.2614

                    Fidelity                 1.800.343.0860
                    TIAA-CREF           1.866.548.3705

           Contact Social Security to apply for Medicare, if applicable

        •  30 days prior to retirement:

           Representative to discuss benefit options and to complete any

           necessary paperwork.  Call (480) 965-2701 to schedule a date.
