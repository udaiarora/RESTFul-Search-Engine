Charles Trumbull Hayden Library
Audio Tour

Presented by The Library Channel


Welcome to the Hayden Library audio tour. The tour begins on the State Seal inside the Hayden Library entrance. The tour will provide location and background information on all levels of the building, helping you become more familiar with library services and the locations of materials.


To take the tour, download the audio file into your portable MP3 player, start at the seal and follow the directions of the announcer. You may also print out map pages for each level. For iPod users we are providing a chapter enabled MP4 version, which contains chapter stops and images for each location you are visiting. We are also providing a standard MP3 audio only version for everyone else. You can view the chapter version via iTunes or Quicktime on your computer if you do not own an iPod.


The audio tour may be downloaded by clicking on one of the two links provided below:

  • Chapter Enabled Hayden Library Audio Tour (MP4): Download Podcast in MP4 format This version includes location images and requires iTunes or Quicktime. Right Click and choose "Save Target As" for Internet Explorer.
