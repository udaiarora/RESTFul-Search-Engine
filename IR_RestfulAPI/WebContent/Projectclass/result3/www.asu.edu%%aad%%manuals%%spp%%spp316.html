SPP 316: “Whistle-Blowing” and Personnel Actions

SPP 316, “Whistle-Blowing and Personnel Actions,” is an Arizona Board of Regents’ policy. It is maintained outside of the ASU system of administrative manuals.

The Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual is available only in PDF format. An Adobe Acrobat Reader plug-in is required to view these PDF files. See our main policies and procedures page if you require this plug-in.

To enter the Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual, go to http://www.abor.asu.edu/1_the_regents/policymanual/index.html#6. This link accesses Chapter VI. Click on item 6-914, “Protection of Employees from Reprisal for Whistleblowing.”

To return to this manual, you may wish to set a bookmark to http://www.asu.edu/aad/manuals/spp.
