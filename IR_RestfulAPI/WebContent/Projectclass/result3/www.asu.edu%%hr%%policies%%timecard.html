Time Cards

Do you know what generates a time card?

When you pay an employee through HRMS a pre-printed timecard is generated and sent to your department.

If you look in the HRMS system and find that your employee has been entered for the job in your department and you have not received a time card, this is because the employee data was entered after the previous payroll was run. To turn in hours for this employee, you will need to complete a blank time card. Blank time cards can be found at the following link: Time Card Form.

The UNDERLINED and BOLDED information should match the information on the time card.

The pay line START and STOP dates also must be open. (This means the dates are reflecting the current pay cycle.)

If all these match what is on the HRMS system the time card is correct and will pay correctly. Please remember the employee must be entered into the HRMS system in order to be paid and for the time card to process. This can be check by looking on line in the inquiry function of HRMS. Search by ID number or by name. (Some employees have chosen to use a number other than their social security number.)

 

Please remember to sign all time cards and gray bar reports and write a phone number where you can be reached should any questions arise regarding their processing. Blank time cards should only be used when an employee is on HRMS but no pre-printed time card was generated.
