TOEFL and TOEIC

new feature: UNOFFICIAL TOEFL RESULTS - Fall 2 2006

AECP administers an institutional TOEFL exam (paper and pencil) five times a year, in March, May, July, October and December. Scores from the institutional TOEFL are accepted only by ASU. Registration information is provided to you in your classes four weeks before the test. The cost to take this TOEFL is $90.

AECP also administers the TOEIC (Test of English for International Communication) each class cycle. Scores from this exam can be used by corporations, governments, and other organizations to assess your English language proficiency. The cost to take the TOEIC is $70.

TOEFL Waiver Policy for ASU
Students who complete AECP's Advanced 2 level with grades of "A" or "B" may enter an ASU graduate or undergraduate degree program without TOEFL scores unless the degree program has a special or higher requirement. AECP has a full-time student advisor to help students with admission to ASU.
