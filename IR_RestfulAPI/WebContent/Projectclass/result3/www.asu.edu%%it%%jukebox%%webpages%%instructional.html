Instructional Web Sites (Instructor Volume Space)

How does Class Webspace work?

Essentially, we point the web server to serve up the files located in an Instructor Volume you have previously requested be set up for your class. Once you have requested an Instructor Volume at http://www.asu.edu/instructorvolumes and received confirmation that it has been created, send a note to web-q@asu.edu requesting a URL for your class. Be sure to include your ASURITE UserID and the course number and prefix of your class in this note. The URL for your class webspace will be similar in format to http://www.asu.edu/courses/eng241.

Are there other options for obtaining webspace for my class?

Yes. Some of these options are described under Frequently Asked Questions.
