If the faculty member or academic professional has a valid Social Security number, Human Resources (HR) proceeds to process forms for employment.

If the faculty member or academic professional has no Social Security number and is not exempt from federal and state taxes, HR:

  1. requests that the faculty member or academic professional apply for a Social Security number immediately

    and

  2. may assign a temporary number before proceeding with employment processing.

If the faculty member or academic professional has no Social Security number and is exempt from federal and state taxes, HR proceeds to process forms for employment.
