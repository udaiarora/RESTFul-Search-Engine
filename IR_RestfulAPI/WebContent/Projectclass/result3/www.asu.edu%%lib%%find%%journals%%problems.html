GENERAL INFORMATION:

  • This report is generated from information supplied by e-journal vendors and aggregators.  Some vendors report monthly, some quarterly, and some less regularly.  Accuracy and currency depends on the quality and schedule of the data submitted by the vendor.
     
  • Some titles appear multiple times in this list in different forms because of the way the vendor lists the title.
     
  • Look for alternative variations in titles. If you cannot locate a title, try alternative forms of that title. For example:
    • IEEE International Pulsed Power Conference appears under the title, Pulsed Power Conference, IEEE Int'l.
    • The Baltimore Sun, appears under the title, The Sun (Baltimore, Md: 1837).
    • Science Magazine can also be found under the title, Science (Washington, D.C.)
