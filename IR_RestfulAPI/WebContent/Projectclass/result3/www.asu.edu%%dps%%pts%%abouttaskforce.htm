About the Parking and Transit Task Force

The Parking and Transit Task Force was established in October 2005 under President Crow’s direction. Carol Campbell, ASU Chief Financial Officer, and Dean Patricia White, College of Law, chaired the committee with the goal of designing a parking and transportation plan for ASU’s future that was inclusive, creative, fiscally responsible and forward thinking. The Task Force had a very open and collaborative process which included representatives of classified staff, faculty, student government groups, campus events, student affairs, residential life and athletics ( ICA).
