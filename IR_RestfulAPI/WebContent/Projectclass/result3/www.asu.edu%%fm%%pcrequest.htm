Request for Services - Capital Programs Management Group  

All requests for Capital Programs Management Group (CPMG) estimate and design services should be made on the "Capital Project Request for Estimate" form. This form is used for facility remodeling, renovation, and construction projects. Each form sent to CPMG must be signed by the responsible Dean or Director, and by the responsible Vice President.

Each fall, the Provost issues a University-wide call for Capital Projects to the Vice Presidents. This call is for capital projects that require special funding by the University. Requests responding to this call are also made on the "Capital Project Request for Estimate" form.

Requests for Capital Project estimates which may require special funding by the University do not have to wait for the Provost's annual call for projects. Capital Project estimates may be requested at any time, and should be made throughout the year.

The Capital Project Request for Estimate form is now available for download and printing from the University Services web site. This form is available in Microsoft Word format.
