DPS 101–02: Emergency Call Boxes

Purpose

To provide instructions on how to locate and operate an emergency call box

Source

ASU Department of Public Safety

Policy

Emergency call boxes at various locations at each campus provide a direct, immediate communications link to the ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) Communications Center. Emergency call boxes are identified by a bright blue light above the box, and are located in parking structures and high traffic areas throughout campus.

Call boxes are to be used in emergency situations only. Nonemergency situations should be reported to ASU DPS by dialing 480/965–3456 from any of the three campuses.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Person reporting an emergency situation
  1. In case of emergency, locate a call box in a parking lot, structure, or high traffic area by looking for a bright blue light.

  2. Follow the instructions on the box.
ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) Communications Center
  1. Acknowledge the activated call box alarm and give the caller instructions on how to speak into the box.
Person reporting an emergency situation
  1. Push the speaker button and speak into the speaker/microphone, using a normal tone of voice. Tell the dispatcher the nature of the emergency, the location, any injuries, and other information as requested.
ASU DPS Communications Center
  1. Dispatch the necessary emergency assistance.
endtable

Cross-Reference
