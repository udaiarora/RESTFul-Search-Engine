September 2, 2005

Fire departments to visit ASU as part of campus fire safety events

The ASU fire marshal’s office, the Environmental Health and Safety Department and the local fire departments that provide service to ASU will visit all of the university’s campuses this month as part of an annual promotion of campus fire safety.

Many colleges, universities, and state and federal officials hope to reduce the loss of life and property damage, especially in light of the fact that 75 lives have been lost because of fires at American colleges and universities since Jan. 1, 2000.

To this end, ASU and the Glendale, Mesa, Phoenix and Tempe fire departments are working together to help promote campus fire safety by providing information about the programs each agency has to offer.

Here is a schedule of fire safety events planned at the Polytechnic, Tempe and West campuses:

Polytechnic campus

Every Tuesday and Wednesday, from 10 a.m. – 2 p.m., there will be a fire safety booth set up in the Student Union near the bookstore. In addition, every Wednesday in September, from 11 a.m. – 1 p.m., the Mesa Fire Department will be on campus just south of the student union on Sutton Road to display fire trucks and a fire sprinkler trailer. The schedule is as follows:

• Mesa Fire Department: Sept. 7 – Transportation safety (helmet safety, driver safety, seat belt safety and more); Sept 14 – fire engine company and fire sprinkler trailer, which provides a demonstration of fire sprinkler system and component activation; Sept. 21 – fire sprinkler trailer and educational handouts; Sept. 28 –home safety display and fire engine company.

Tempe campus

Every Wednesday and Thursday, from 10 a.m. – 2 p.m., there will be a fire safety booth set up, with the first week set up in front of the Student Recreational Center (SRC) and the remaining Wednesdays and Thursdays on the first floor of the Memorial Union, near the information desk. In addition, every Thursday in September, from 10 a.m. – 2 p.m., Tempe Fire Department officials will be on campus, with the first week near the booth in front of SRC, and the remaining Thursdays outside the southeast area of the Memorial Union.

The Tempe Fire Department will have engine companies and their fire prevention representatives available from 10 a.m. – 2 p.m. each Thursday.

West campus

Every Tuesday and Wednesday, from 10 a.m. – 2 p.m., there will be a fire safety booth set up in the Sands Classroom area. Also, Phoenix Fire Department or Glendale Fire Department officials will be on campus every Tuesday in September, southwest of Fletcher Library, just north of Lot 2. The fire department officials will display their fire trucks and their safety houses, a structure that provides a realistic experience of being in a fire or smoky condition. The schedule is as follows:

• Phoenix Fire Department: Sept. 6 – fire engine company and safety house; Sept. 13 – fire engine company.

• Glendale Fire Department: Sept. 20 – fire engine company and safety house; Sept. 27 – fire engine company.

For more information, call ASU Fire Marshal Jim Gibbs at (480) 965-0536.
