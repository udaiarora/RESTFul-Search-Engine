The TRANS Procedure

Objective Value

If the problem is infeasible (see the "Reasons for Infeasibility" section), a note to that effect is written to the SAS log. Otherwise, the value of the objective function, the total cost (the multiple of flow and cost, summed for all arcs) at optimality, is reported on the SAS log.
