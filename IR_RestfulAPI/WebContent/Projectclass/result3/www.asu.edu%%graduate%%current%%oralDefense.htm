Scheduling and Holding an Oral Defense

If you have a thesis, dissertation, research paper or practicum, you will need to schedule an oral defense with your department and Student Academic Services (SAS) in the Division of Graduate Studies. Please be mindful of the end-of-semester deadlines for submitting your document and scheduling your oral defense. For exact dates, please see Graduation Deadlines and Procedures, available in Administration Building, B-wing, Suite 170 or online at www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/GradDdlns.

It is also important that you meet the 10 working day deadline for submitting your thesis, dissertation, research paper, bound document, or practicum for format evaluation. All documents must be submitted at least 10 working days before the scheduled defense date. A chart on the back of the defense schedule form lists these dates. The chart is also available online at: www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/GradDdlns.

To prepare for your defense you will need to:
  • Verify that you are registered for at least one credit hour of appropriate coursework for the semester in which you defend.

  • Complete a defense schedule form which officially schedules your defense with the Division of Graduate Studies. For doctoral students, this is the Dissertation Defense Recommendation form. For master's students, this is the Application for Authorization to Schedule a Defense form. These forms are available in the Administration Building, B-wing, Suite 170 or online at www.asu.edu/graduate/forms.

  • Make sure that you include the building and room number where you will be holding your defense. SAS cannot schedule your defense without location information.

  • Verify that your committee composition is correct. If any of your members have changed, you will need to submit a Graduate Supervisory Committee Change form, available in Administration Building, B-wing, Suite 170, center lobby or online at www.asu.edu/graduate/forms.

  • If you have not already turned in your document for format review, you will need to submit a copy of it (printed on one side) when scheduling your defense with the following materials:
    • Format Approval Sheet
    • Required extra copies of specific pages:
      • Dissertation — 3 additional copies of the title page, 2 additional copies of the abstract
      • Thesis — 2 additional copies of the title page, 1 additional copy of the abstract
Questions? If you have questions about any of the information on this page, please contact Student Academic Services (SAS) at (480) 965-3521.
