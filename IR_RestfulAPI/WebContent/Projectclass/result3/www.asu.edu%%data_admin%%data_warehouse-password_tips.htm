Password Tips

DO NOT WRITE DOWN YOUR PASSWORD. Instead, choose something that you can remember. Try using one of the methods below to help you come up with a secure password which you can remember.
Your password must be between 6 and 8 characters long.
You should use a mixture of UPPER and lower case letters and include at least one number or special character.

The special characters available are:

! % * - _ + = : , . / ?
A password should be easy to remember, but hard to guess.
Do not use your name, phone number, birthday, or other easily obtained personal information
Do not use your significant other's name or nickname, a pet's name, or your children's names
Do not use any word that can be found in a dictionary
Do not use any simple geometric pattern on the keyboard (i.e. qwerty)
Do not use any obvious permutation of the above (i.e. your name spelled backwards)

Suggested Methods:

Think of a phrase or sentence, take the first letter of each word, and insert punctuation and/or a digit.
"Friday the 13th was a scary movie" - Ft13wasm
"quick, Superman, to the phone booth" - q,S,2tpb
Choose a word and break it up with digits and punctuation and add capitals.
"virtuosity" - vir2osiT
"homework" - h.Work!
