Music On Hold and Music in Queue

Music on hold is available to all ASU departments on Main Campus only.
(Departments at other ASU campuses, please contact Telephone Services at 965-2011 if interested.)

Music on hold refers to the music heard by a party when they are placed on hold by a station. Two types of music are offered: general and custom.

General

For general music on hold, there is a one-time charge of $5 for programming to set it up (per station), and no monthly charge for the feature. The music source is supplied and maintained by Telephone Services, and consists of instrumental music suitable for general business application.

To add music on hold to a phone, simply submit a Telephone Services Request (TSR) to the Telephone Services department (mail code 0502) with the extension(s) to which music should be added.

Custom

Custom music on hold is available to departments who wish to play unique, specialized recordings while customers are on hold. An installation charge of $1,500 will be charged to the department to cover the cost of necessary equipment and custom programming. Ongoing maintenance will also be charged at $10 per month.

As with general music on hold, there is a one-time charge of $5 to activate the feature on a station and no monthly charge.

The music source for custom music is supplied and maintained by the requesting department, and the department is responsible for observing all copyright laws and regulations, and any royalty fees associated with audio played on the device. Telephone Services can provide information on several types of playback devices that are available.

Music in Queue

To have the University’s general music source heard by callers who are waiting to be answered, the music source must be added to each Vector Directory Number (VDN), vector, and ACD group involved. A one-time charge of $5 will be charged to add music in queue to each VDN, vector or group. In most cases, this will result in a total one-time charge of $15.00. There is no monthly charge for the feature.

To request music in queue for your department, submit a TSR to Telephone Services (mail code 0502).

For more information about Music on Hold or Music in Queue, contact us at (480) 965-2011.
