Broadcasting & Publishing Directories

This list of broadcasting and publishing directories are all arranged by geography. Most are by country or state and then by city. A more comprehensive list is Advertising/Media Directories.

  1. Editor & Publisher International Yearbook. New York: Editor & Publisher, 1959- . Annual. (ASU owns 1959-. Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    PN 4700 .E42 HAYDEN REFERENCE
    PN 4700 .E42 WEST REFERENCE

    This work does NOT provide any information about the city or region the newspaper is published in, but the existence of a newspaper at all in some cities of the world can be important for finding out more about the area. Many will subscribe to the newspaper of the locale to find out more about the area. This does give the language of the newspaper. The least information provided for each newspaper is the address and telephone number. The most information will be the names of all department/section editors and managers, news wire services, equipment, specifications, advertising rates, special sections, fax number, price and circulation figures.

  2. Editor and Publisher Market Guide. New York: Editor & Publisher, 1927- . Annual. (ASU owns 1964- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    HF 5905 .E38 HAYDEN REFERENCE
    HF 5905 .E38 WEST REFERENCE

    Analyzes over 1,500 U.S. and Canadian communities where a daily newspaper is published. Information included covers location, principal industries, climate, retail outlets, daily newspaper circulation, market rankings, forecasts, population growth, disposable personal income, and retail sales.

  3. Gale Directory of Publications and Broadcast Media. Detroit, MI: Gale Research, 1869- . Annual. (ASU owns 1932- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    PN 4867 .A48x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located at Reference Desk)
    PN 4867 .A48x SCIENCE REFERENCE (located at Reference Desk)
    PN 4867 .A48x WEST INFO

    Although this is a directory to publications and broadcasting, it is arranged by state and city of publication/broadcast with a snippet of information on the city. It is useful for locating a newspaper to subscribe to before moving to a location. Good coverage of the weeklies and more rural area newspapers.

  4. Newslink. Champaign, IL : NewsLink Associates, 2004

    This web site links to all the newspaers, radio, & television web sites from around the world.

  5. Newspaper Advertising Source. Wilmette, IL: Standard Rate & Data Service, 1919- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1990- . Latest issue in Reference; earlier issues in Journals.)
    HF 5905 .S73 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Radio Advertising Source. Wilmette, IL: Standard Rate & Data Service, 1929- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1990- . Latest issue in Reference; earlier issues in Journals.)
    HF 5905 .S74 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    TV and Cable Source. Wilmette, IL: Standard Rate & Data Service, 1947- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1990- . Latest issue in Reference; earlier issues in Journals.)
    HF 5905 .S745 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    These three SRDS services each have an identical map and a "State, County, City, Metro Area Data" table at the beginning of each state's entries. Data include population totals plus separate black and Hispanic figures, household income, household expenditures by type, and number of automobiles.

  6. Television and Cable Factbook. 3 vols. Washington, D.C.: Television Digest, 1982/83- . Annual. (The Tempe campus owns 1986-1997 in Science Journals. The West campus has current only.)
    TK 6630 .A1 T4538x SCIENCE REFERENCE
    TK 6630 .A1 T4538x WEST REFERENCE

    Now published in 3 volumes over a two year time span, the Cable and Services volumes are published in the same year and the Stations volume is published the next year. The Cable volume is probably the most useful to those who plan to relocate a family within the U.S. This volume is arranged by state and city. Each cable system listed under each city includes a list of channels offered and pay options. Cross-references are provided for some cities. Be sure to check all the suburb names in the larger metropolitan areas. In the last section of the Service volume are all the cable industry statistics. Some of these tables do provide city level statistics. The Stations volume includes Arbitron ratings information with each station listing and for the metropolitan areas.

  7. Working Press of the Nation. 4 vols. Burlington, IA: National Research Bureau, 1949- . Annual. (ASU owns 1963- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    PN 4867 .W62x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located at Reference Desk)

    This work is arranged by city within each volume, except for Vol. 4. The volumes are Newspaper Directory, Magazine & Internal Publications Directory, TV & Radio Directory, and Feature Writers, Photographers & Professional Speakers. The directories include the name, address, and telephone and fax numbers, brief area information, year established, frequency of publication, rates, personnel, deadlines, and owners. Other specialized information is provided, as appropriate, for each medium. Useful specialized indexes are included in each volume.
