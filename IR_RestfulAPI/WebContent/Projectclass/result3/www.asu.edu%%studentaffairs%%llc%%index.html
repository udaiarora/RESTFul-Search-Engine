What are Living and Learning Communities?

Living and Learning Communities @ ASU

The Living and Learning Communities (LLC) at ASU provide you with the opportunity to connect with others who share your academic and personal interests. As part of your First-Year Experience, the LLC can help you connect with resources to support your success in the first year and beyond.  In your LLC you will find: 

  • Opportunities to live and study with students who share your interests.
  • Upper class tutors and mentors to show you the ropes and connect you to ASU.
  • Workshops and programs to promote your academic and personal success.
  • On-site tutoring, classes, and success workshops.
  • Activities and programs that integrate academic and social experiences.
