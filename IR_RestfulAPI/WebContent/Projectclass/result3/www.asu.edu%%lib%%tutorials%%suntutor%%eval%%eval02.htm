Types of resources

There are many different sources that you might use for a paper or assignment, including some that you may not think of as research sources. Each needs to be evaluated in a different way. Below are some types of sources.

  • Books
  • Articles in journals, magazines, or trade publications
  • Newspaper articles
  • Web pages
  • Broadcast media - television shows, radio, video
  • People (friends, instructors, lecturers, interviewees, etc.)

Caution: Be sure to check with your professor to see which types of sources are acceptable for the assignment.

If you need more information on how to find sources, try some of the other modules in SunTutor.
