The Center for Asian Research maintains its own library
of Asian videos (including many feature films), available for free checkout by all students and faculty.

Students may check out videos for up to five days, by leaving a valid student ID card or a $20 deposit.
The Center's video collection is located on the 6th Floor, east wing of the Lattie F. Coor Hall Room 6677.
