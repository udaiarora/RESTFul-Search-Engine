Complete Your Master Promissory Note (MPN)

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this e-mail notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

According to our records, you have accepted a Federal Direct Subsidized and/or Unsubsidized Student Loan for spring 2007 but have not completed a Master Promissory Note (MPN). Disbursement of this loan is dependent upon completion of this requirement. You can easily complete this requirement online at dlenote.ed.gov. The Direct Loan MPN is a legal document you sign the first time you secure a Federal Direct Student Loan. The MPN lists your borrowing conditions and repayment terms.

To view the electronic MPN Website effectively, click here for browser instructions. If you are using an older browser that is not supported, your interaction with this site may be impacted. You will need your U.S. Department of Education issued Personal Identification Number (PIN).

Please contact us or visit one of our locations if you have questions about this notice. We look forward to serving you.

Sincerely,

Arizona State University
