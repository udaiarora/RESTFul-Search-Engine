Information for New Employees

Welcome to ASU!!

We are pleased to introduce you to our university community. To access essential information and necessary forms, please use the links located below and on the right-hand side of this web page. If you have any questions please call the HR Employee Service Center at (480) 965-2701 or email Ask HR.

This website describes the formal orientation sessions that are available. There are numerous types of employees at Arizona State University; therefore, we provide a variety of orientation sessions to acquaint new employees with the University.

Benefits-Eligible New Employee Orientation

This university-wide orientation is open to all new benefits-eligible

employees - those employees who will be working at least 20 hours per week (or 50%) and who are expected to be employed for six months or more.

Payroll Orientation
Departments may request that a Payroll Specialist come to their department to process payroll forms when they are hiring large groups of employees who are not benefits-eligible. To arrange this, contact Sandy Badilla at (480) 965-4820 or sandy.badilla@asu.edu.  New employees should bring appropriate I-9 verification/identification documents to this session. Payroll forms can be found in the New Employee Payroll Packet.

Department Orientation
Administrators and colleagues may provide new employees in their academic or operational work units with a departmental orientation that acquaints them with their particular departmental policies and procedures. Ask your Supervisor or Manager if your unit has a formal departmental orientation program.  Click on checklist to see a list of work issues that you may want to cover. 

New Faculty Orientation
This orientation program is geared specifically for new faculty members and is held once each fall.
