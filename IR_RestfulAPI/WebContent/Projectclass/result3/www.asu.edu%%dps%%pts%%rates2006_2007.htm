Parking Decal Rates for 2006-2007
and Beyond

2006-07 ASU Parking Decals, for all four campuses, go on sale June 1, 2006. Decals may be purchased via Online Parking Services or in person at the Decal Sales office at each campus specific location (Downtown Phoenix campus decals may be purchased online or at the Tempe campus Decal Sales office).

Resident Hall Decals are not available for purchase or renewal online. Click here for more info

If you plan to renew your current parking decal or downgrade (purchase a decal of lower value), please visit the Renewing Your Decal for 2006-07 section.

+ Note: Rates for 2008 and 2009 are only estimates and are subject to change.
