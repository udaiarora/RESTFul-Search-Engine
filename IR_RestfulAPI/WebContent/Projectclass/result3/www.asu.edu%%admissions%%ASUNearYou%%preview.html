Sun Devil Sneak Previews: Off Campus

Can't make it to ASU for a campus visit? We'll come to you! Each year, ASU holds informational sessions for prospective students and parents in various locations around the country.

For additional information about the Sun Devil Sneak Preview, or other ASU off-campus events, please call 480.965.3065.
