Bachelor Degree Candidates

Bachelor degree candidates should enter the Wells Fargo Arena through the lower west ramp 30 minutes prior to the ceremony. Graduate seating is designated by college and volunteers on the arena floor will help you find your seating area. Click here for a general Commencement seating chart (PDF).

Students will indicate their participation at the University Commencement Ceremony when completing the Cap and Gown Order Form through the ASU Bookstore at the ASU's Tempe campus, ASU's West campus or ASU's Polytechnic campus. Most college convocations and all special interest convocation events require an RSVP for paticipation. Please contact the dean's office of your college to ask about the RSVP policy for your college convocation event. For information about special interest convocation activities, please contact the University Ceremonies Office at (480)965-3565.
