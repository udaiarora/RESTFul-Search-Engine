Welcome to Arizona State University!

ASU has a rich and vibrant educational environment due, in large part, to its international student body, of which the majority is graduate students. There are over 3,000 international students studying at ASU, representing more than 140 nations and nationalities. ASU and the Division of Graduate Studies are committed to working in partnership with academic programs to insure you have a quality educational experience. To that end the university has established a wide range of programs and support networks designed to serve our international students. These include the International Student Office (www.asu.edu/studentaffairs/ed/iso), numerous international student organizations (www.asu.edu/studentaffairs/ed/iso/student_org.htm), as well as financial support for international students (www.asu.edu/graduate/financial/international.htm).


International Admissions
This site contains information and resources for international students who would like to begin, or have already begun, the application process. Included here are the international student application and Financial Guarantee form.

International TAs
Here you can find information for international students who have received teaching assistantship/associateships, including information about the SPEAK test.

International Student Office (ISO)
www.asu.edu/studentaffairs/ed/iso
The ISO conducts orientation programs for new students and offers assistance with administrative concerns, immigration advisement, and personal counseling, including providing information on student visas, F-1 and J-1 visas, international student organizations, and international student traditions.
