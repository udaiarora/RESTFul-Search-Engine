Legislative and Government Internship Program
2007 Schedule

Schedule begins August 2006 and ends May 2007

August 1 Notification to eligible ASU students
September 6 Agency Open House for interested students, 11 a.m.- 3:00 p.m. ASU Tempe Campus, Memorial Union, Room 202, Alumni Lounge: Legislative and government agency representatives, guest legislators
September 22 Application filing deadline - 5:00 pm
October 4 Screening committee's recommendations for interview: Office of the Provost begins contacting students for interview dates and times
October 11 - 13 Student interviews with ASU committee, 8 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. (interview is approximately 15 minutes)
October 17 ASU Committee determines top candidates to represent ASU
October 17 Students notified by e-mail of internship status

October 23

 

 Preinterview Orientation, 2:40-4:30 p.m. REQUIRED
Location: ASU Tempe Campus Agriculture Building (AG), Room 105
October 26, 27, 30, 31 Student interviews at Senate, House, Governor's Office and Arizona Supreme Court
November 15 Offers extended by Senate, House, Governor's Office and Arizona Supreme Court
November 15-22 Student interviews at all remaining legislative and government agencies
November 22 Offers extended by all remaining legislative and government agencies
November 22 Students notify government agencies of acceptance/declination
January 2-5 Orientation at government offices
January 8 Legislative session begins
February (TBA) ASU Alumni Statehood Picnic, Noon - 1:30 p.m., Wesley Bolin Park
March 5 Midterm evaluations due at ASU from intern supervisors
March 7-9 Mid-term evaluation with each intern at the agency locations (approximately 15 minutes)
March 16 Midterm evaluations sent to ASU faculty
May 4 Final evaluations due to ASU by intern supervisors
May 4 Internship ends

Questions? Call Heather Hoffart at 480.727.8051 or email legislative.internship@asu.edu.
