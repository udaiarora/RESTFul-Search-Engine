The I-9 form (included in the New Employee Packet) must be completed NO LATER THAN three (3) working days after the start of employment, but it is preferable to complete the paperwork before your start date.

Return the form and all attachments plus the completed New Employee Packet to:
Arizona State University
Human Resources

Tempe, AZ 85287-5612

NOTE: If the I-9 is signed by an authorized representative not employed within ASU's Human Resources Department, the submission of the I-9 is subject to review and approval by the Human Resources Department.

Employees must complete all areas contained in section 1 of the I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification form and then sign and date in designated area. Authorized representatives, such as a notary public, must review documents from list A or list B and list C and fill in the proper information requested in the proper information requested in section 2.

Form I-9, if signed by a notary public, must be dated at the time of the document review and must have the notary seal affixed and include the notary's printed name and title.

Specific Instructions for the Notary Public
It is the sole decision of the employee as to which documents they want to submit for I-9 verification. If the employee submits a document listed in list A, only one document is required.

If the employee submits a document listed in list B, they must also submit a document listed in list C (that is, two documents are required, one from List B and one from list C).

In either case:

  • please check the submitted documents for authenticity and their expiration date
  • copy the documents submitted (federal law permits the copying of documents for I-9 purposes even if the document itself states that copying is prohibited)
  • notarize (sign, date and seal) the copies
  • complete section 2 and
  • sign, date and seal the certification following section 2.

As an alternative for the final bullet point above, you may indicate the following on a separate page:

“The attached copies are true and correct copies of the originals presented to me and they appear to be genuine.
I have examined such documents and the person listed on such documents personally appeared before me and is the person listed on such documents.”

Please sign, date and seal this alternative statement in the same manner, as you would notarize any other document.
