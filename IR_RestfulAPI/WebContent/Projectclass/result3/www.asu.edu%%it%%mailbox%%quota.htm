E-Mail Quota

Students, faculty and staff can subscribe to an ASU IMAP mailbox. This e-mail mailbox has a default quota of 10MB but the users can increase the size of the mailbox based on their affiliation (students can increase it up to 50mb and staff can increase it to 250mb).

Steps to increase your IMAP Quota

Once your ASURITE UserID has been activated (and for students-- registered and paid for classes) you can increase your mailbox size. To do so, follow these instructions:

  1. Go to http://my.asu.edu
  2. Log in using your ASURITE UserID and passphrase
  3. Choose the emma from the black navigation bar
  4. Choose the Quota Check from the grey navigation bar that will appear in the new window
  5. Choose a new quota amount by clicking in the radio button next to the desired size
  6. Be sure to save any changes by choosing the Change button
  7. Use the logout button when you are finished
