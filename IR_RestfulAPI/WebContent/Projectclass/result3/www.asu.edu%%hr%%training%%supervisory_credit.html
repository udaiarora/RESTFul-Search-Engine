Supervisory Experience Credit for Management Training Series

Visit The Supervisory Academy Web Page

Upon successful completion of Supervisory Academy, a participant will earn one year of employment credit as a supervisor at ASU.

Successful completion includes:

  • Full attendance and participation
  • Completion of all homework assignments
  • Completion of the final exam with a 70% or better
