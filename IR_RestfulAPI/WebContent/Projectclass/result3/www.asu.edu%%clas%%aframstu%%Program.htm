MAJOR REQUIREMENTS from FALL 2002 (45 hrs)

  • A minor or a certificate of a minimum of 18 hours in other academic field is required.

1. Core Courses (21 hrs)

    AFR 210   Introduction to African American Studies (SB, C, H)

    AFH 353   African American Literature before Harlem Renaissance (L2, HU, C)

    AFH 354   African American Literature since Harlem Renaissance (L2, HU, C)

    AFS 363   African American History before 1865 (SB, C, H)

    AFS 364   African American History since 1865 (SB, C, H)

    AFR 429   African American Studies Theory and Methods

    AFR 490   Field Study or Pro-Seminar

     (or)  498   

 

2. Concentration Courses (12 hrs)

    12 Hours must be taken from one of the following concentrations.

    AFH:   Humanities/ Arts

    AFR:   Politics and Society

    AFS:   Social and Behavioral Sciences

 

3. Related Courses (12 hrs)

    6 Hours from each concentration other than the student’s own concentration.

 

 

MAJOR REQUIREMENTS before FALL 2002 (45 hrs)

  • A minor or a certificate of a minimum of 18 hours in other academic field is required.

1. Core Courses (18 hrs)

    AFR 210   Introduction to African American Studies (SB, C, H)

    AFH 353   African American Literature before, or since Harlem (L2, HU, C)

     (or)  354   

    AFS 363   African American History before 1865 (SB, C, H)

    AFS 364   African American History since 1865 (SB, C, H)

    AFR 429   African American Studies Theory and Methods

    AFR 490   Field Study, or Pro-Seminar 

     (or)  498   

 

2. Concentration Courses (12 hrs)

    12 Hours must be taken in one of the following concentrations.

    AFH:   Humanities/ Arts

    AFR:   Politics and Society

    AFS:   Social and Behavioral Sciences

 

3. Related Courses (15 hrs)

At least one course must be taken from each concentration, regardless of the particular concentration you have selected. The courses must be taken under the prefixes of unit offering. (Ex. If you want to take Ethnic Relations in the United States for this requirement, you should register for ASB 202, instead AFS 294). You may also choose other related courses outside AAS Concentrations under permission of your advisor. Please note that Related Courses cannot be counted simultaneously as Core or Concentration Courses.

 

 

MINOR/ BIS REQUIREMENTS (18 hrs)

  • A minimum of 12 upper division course work is required. 

1. Core Courses (9 hrs)

    AFR 210  |  AFH 353 or 354  |  AFS 363 or 364

 

2. Concentration Courses (9 hrs)

    One course (3 hrs) must be taken from each concentration.

    AFH:   Humanities/ Arts

    AFR:   Politics and Society

    AFS:   Social and Behavioral Sciences

 

 

CERTIFICATE REQUIREMENTS (24 hrs)

 

1. Core Courses (15 hrs)

    AFR 210  |  AFH 353 or 354  |  AFS 363  |  AFS 364  |  AFR 429

 

2. Concentration Courses (9 hrs)

    One course (3 hrs) must be taken from each concentration.

    AFH:   Humanities/ Arts

    AFR:   Politics and Society

    AFS:   Social and Behavioral Sciences
