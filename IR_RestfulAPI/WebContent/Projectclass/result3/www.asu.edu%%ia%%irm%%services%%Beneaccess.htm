Benefactor is the campus wide alumni/development system. The system contains records for all of the University's alumni, donors, and friends. By sharing a common database, authorized campus units and individuals can easily access consistent data and have centralized security features to protect confidential information.

Access to Benefactor is granted only to ASU and ASU Foundation employees who are approved by their department and who have successfully completed training. IRM provides this training in our classroom facility, at no cost to the unit.

Colleague is the financial application utilized by the ASU Foundation. Access to the Colleague database is restricted to Foundation Finance employees. Several Colleague reports are available on the ASU Foundation's web site to authorized Colleague account holders.

The process to request access begins with establishing an ASURITE ID. ASURITE ID's are administered by the Office of Information Technology. ASU employees create their own ASURITE ID through the Information Technology web site. Foundation employees coordinate with the Foundation Human Resources department to obtain an ASURITE ID.

Once an ID is active, the employee and their supervisor must complete the IRM Request for Benefactor/Colleague Access form. Detailed assistance in completing the form is available. The completed form should be faxed to the IRM Help Desk at 965-6686. A confirmation e-mail will be sent to the requester once access has been established.

Finally, detailed information on Benefactor training is available and a schedule of Benefactor training classes can be viewed on the IRM Master Calendar.
