Instructions for Downloading General Studies Forms

The criteria checklists are available in three formats: (1) HTML, (2) Adobe PDF, and (3) Microsoft Word. The General Studies Course Proposal cover form is available in Adobe PDF and Microsoft Word formats.

The HTML format is available for users' convenience; no extra software or plug-ins are required to view this text. It can also be printed and used as a working copy to submit new courses, but it is recommended to download either the Adobe PDF or Microso ft Word files when submitting proposals for new General Studies courses.

Adobe PDF format is also available for downloading. You must have a copy of Adobe Acrobat Reader to view these files. Click on the following to get a free copy of Acrobat Reader.

The Microsoft Word files will download to your computer and allow you to word process the file and print it. You must have Microsoft Word version 97 or later to use these files.

The forms can also be obtained by contacting:

  • ASU Main and ASU East: the General Studies Office at 965-0739 or 965-5657.
  • ASU West: the Curriculum Office at 543-4509.
