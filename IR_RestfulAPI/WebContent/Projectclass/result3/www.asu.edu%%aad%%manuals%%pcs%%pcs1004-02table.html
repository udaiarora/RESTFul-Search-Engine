PCS 1004–02 Table

The faculty or principal investigator (PI):

  1. prepares the Property Relocation Authorization Form (PRA) listing property to be transferred

    and

  2. forwards the listing to the sponsored property coordinator.

The sponsored property coordinator:

  1. reviews the PRA for title and for state and local participation

  2. if there is state and/or local participation, proceeds as in PCS 1004–01, “State/Local Funded Equipment.”

    and

  3. if there is no state and/or local participation, approves the PRA form and forwards the original to faculty or to the PI.

The faculty or PI forwards the PRA to the department chair or dean of college for approval. The chair/dean approves or disapproves the PRA.

If approved, the chair/dean contacts the sponsored property coordinator.

The sponsored property coordinator:

  1. removes ASU identification from equipment prior to the equipment departure date

    and

  2. removes the equipment from the property control system.
