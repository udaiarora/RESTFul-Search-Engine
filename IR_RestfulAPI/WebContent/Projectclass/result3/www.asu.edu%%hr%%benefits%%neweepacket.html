Welcome to ASU!

The Benefits Office of Human Resources is pleased to offer you a comprehensive university-offered benefits program. You have 30 days (for retirement) or 31 days (for health, life and disability) from your eligibility date to enroll in the retirement and benefits programs.

Within 31 days, you may elect your benefits by completing:

  • Insurance Election Forms

Within 30 days, you must enroll in a retirement plan:

  • For Classified Staff
  • For Other Personnel

You may choose other benefits and take advantage of our wellness program:

  • Other Benefits for home, auto or long-term care insurance
  • Additional financial security with a 403(B) Plan or a Deferred Compensation plan or Savings Bonds
  • Wellness for work/life balance and good health

IMPORTANT: You MUST complete your Benefits Enrollment within 31 days. Health (medical, dental and vision) coverage, short-term disability and supplemental life insurances, and the flexible spending account plans are effective the first of the month following both eligibility AND receipt of your enrollment forms, provided your elections are made within 31 days of eligibility.
