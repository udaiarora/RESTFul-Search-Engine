The sponsored property coordinator prepares and forwards the inventory listing to all facilities in custody of government-owned property.

The facility with government property:

  1. reviews the ASU Property Control inventory listing

  2. locates property

  3. marks property as either Found, Not Found, or Deleted on report

    and

  4. forwards the report to the sponsored property coordinator.

The sponsored property coordinator:

  1. reviews, reports, and resolves inventory discrepancies with the facility

    and

  2. reports the results to the appropriate government agency and/or the cognizant government property administrator.
