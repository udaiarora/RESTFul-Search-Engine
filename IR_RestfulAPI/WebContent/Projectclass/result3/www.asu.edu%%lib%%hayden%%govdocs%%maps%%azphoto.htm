Photo Index of Arizona Cities, Towns & Locales

About the Photo Index


The tables below list the cities or parts of cities shown on aerial photographs in the Arizona State University Libraries' Map Collection. This table does not include all of the Collection's aerial photos.  Additional photo sets will be added to the Photo Index in the future.


Photo sets included in the Photo Index:

  1. Fairchild aerial photos
    Project ID: Fairchild
    Date: 1930s

  2. Arizona Highway Department aerial photos
    Project ID: By Interstate, Highway or State Route number e.g. US-60
    Date: Late 1950s through early 1960s

  3. United States Geological Survey (USGS) aerial photos
    Project ID: By USGS project ID e.g. GS-VAOB
    Date: Early 1960s

    4. New!
  4. United States Geological Survey (USGS) Orthophoto Quadrangles
    Project ID: Orthophoto
    Date: Early 1970s

More Information on Aerial Photos

Consult the Map Collection's Aerial Photos Page, or contact the Map Collection at (480) 965-3582.
