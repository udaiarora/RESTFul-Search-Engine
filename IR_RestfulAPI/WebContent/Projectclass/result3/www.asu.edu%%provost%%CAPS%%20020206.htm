Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

February 6, 2002

Members Present: Patricia Feldman, Stefan Myhajlenko, Carol Behl, Santos C. Vega.
Members Absent/Excused: Linda Shackle, Annie Platoff

The Agenda included: Review and approval of minutes from 12/5/01 There having been no meeting in January; Continue discussion of CAPs speakers for Spring 2002; Old business; New business; Plan next meeting, and Adjourn.

Patricia Feldman, Chair, called the meeting to order.

Review and Approve Minutes. Members present reviewed the minutes of the previous meeting. It was decided that the minutes were to be corrected at a later time by Annie Platoff, CAPs secretary, and sent out later. Carol Behl moved to approve the minutes with the changes Annie will make. Motion passed.

Discussion of CAPs speakers. Discussion on CAPs speakers for spring 2002 ensued. Ms. Barbara Mawhiney, Director of Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Office, ASU agreed to speak on April 3rd, 2002 on Job classification system. The time scheduled is 10:30 - 11:30. Carol Behl pointed out that Pre-registration was on the same day and that she would not attend and possibly others needing the information would also not be able to attend. Discussion followed with the result that April 3rd was not approved as a feasible date. Other dates were proposed. Patty Feldman agreed to investigate room availability with a possible change to Pinal room, which is larger and holds more persons. She would approach Ms. Mawhiney for availability on a different date. Committee members agreed to allow Patty to probe and make the appropriate decision. Carol Behl will approach Linda Shackle on use of name rosters for publicizing the Speaker's event.

New Business. It was discussed that Annie Platoff would move to UCSB in May. In addition, new CAPs members would be needed for next year's elections. It was recommended that CAPs members search for possible APs for the elections.
The next CAPs meeting was planned. It was decided to ascertain the remaining times of the CAPs members' terms. It was decided to draw up an executive summary of Barbara Mawhiney's presentation and place it on the CAPs web-site.

The next CAPs meetiings will be March 6th, April on Speakers Day, May 1st, and June 5th.

Old Business. Under Old business: Ms. Feldman presented a copy of the memorandum she had mailed to Dr. Morton Munk, President of the Academic Senate. The subject of the memorandum was the "Request for Academic Associate Voting Rights. Dr. Munk had not yet responded to the memorandum. In regards to the letter sent out to Dr. Munk and his failure to respond, it was decided that Patty would attend the Committee on Committees meeting on Feb. 8, 02 and bring up the issue, again. Also, Patty planned to bring up the pending replacement of Annie Platoff, if necessary, with Darby Shaw, secretary of Committee on Committees.

Adjournment

Santos C. Vega
