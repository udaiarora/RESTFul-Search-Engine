NEW GRADUATE COLLEGE DEFERRAL POLICY
Students may request a deferral of the year and term to which they were admitted to the next start time of their program. The request should be made to the department. If the department approves the request, the department should notify Graduate Admissions by email. Deferrals will not occur without departmental approval. Departments may request a deferral of a student's application to the next start time of the student's program. Department may do this through AUI at the time they recommend admitting the student or through an email request to the Graduate College via their Graduate College contact person.
