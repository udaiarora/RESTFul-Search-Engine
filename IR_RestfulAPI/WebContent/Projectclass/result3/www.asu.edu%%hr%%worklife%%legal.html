LAWYER REFERRAL SERVICE
Lawyer Referral Service is a service of theMaricopa County Bar Association. Legal problems can be puzzling when you don't know where to turn for answers. The Lawyer Referral Service can
  • Find the lawyer that best fits your needs.
  • Provide you with a 30-minute legal consultation with an attorney for a $35.00 administrative fee. (Fee is non-refundable once the appointment is scheduled.)
  • Refer you to a lawyer that has professional malpractice insurance and is in good standing with the State Bar of Arizona and the Maricopa County Bar Association.
  • Schedule an appointment with a lawyer located near your work or home.
