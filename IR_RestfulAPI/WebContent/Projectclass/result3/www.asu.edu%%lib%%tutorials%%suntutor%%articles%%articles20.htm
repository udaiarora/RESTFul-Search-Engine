Saving your results

In every index there will be options for saving your search results. The most common options are:

  • Print ? to make a paper copy of the search results (citations and/or full text articles)
  • E-mail ? to send the search results to your e-mail account
  • Download ? to save the search results to a floppy disk
Not every index will offer all three options. To find out which options are available in an index, look for menu choices such as:
  • Download
  • Print
  • Save
  • Export
  • E-mail
  • Output
Follow the instructions on the screen. If you have any questions about saving your results, Ask a Librarian.
