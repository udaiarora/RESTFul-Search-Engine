Nancy McGovern
Digital Preservation Officer
Cornell University
Cornell’s Project Prism: Developing a Preservation Risk Management Program


Nancy Y. McGovern is the Digital Preservation Officer and heads the new Research Department in Instruction, Research, and Information Services (IRIS) at Cornell University Library. She is the co-editor of RLG DigiNews. She has over 15 years of experience with digital preservation initiatives, including a decade working on electronic records at the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA), and is working on her PhD in digital preservation at University College London.
