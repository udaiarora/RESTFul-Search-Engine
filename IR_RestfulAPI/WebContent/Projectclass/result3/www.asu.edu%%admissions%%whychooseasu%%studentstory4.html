Genevieve - Global Business

The atmosphere at ASU really eased my transition from high school to college.

The ASU Living and Learning Communities helped me meet other students, faculty members, and the academic advisors were really helpful when it came time to plan my classes. At first, I didn't know which classes to take, but academic advising helped me choose classes that counted towards my Global Business - Marketing major, so I was never wasting my time or resources.

It is also through the academic advisors that I learned about ways to get involved on campus. Currently, I am a Devils' Advocate, co-treasurer of the International Students Association, and a senator in the Associated Students of Arizona State University at the West campus, ASU's student government organization.

ASU is really a good value, and ASU's West campus is the perfect fit for me.
