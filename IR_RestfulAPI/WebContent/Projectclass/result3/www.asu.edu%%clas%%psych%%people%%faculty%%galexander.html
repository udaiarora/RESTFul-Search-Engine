research interests:

My research focuses on the effects of aging and Alzheimer's disease on brain-behavior relationships. I use neuroimaging techniques, including positron emission tomography (PET) and structural and functional magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), in combination with measures of cognition and behavior to study the effects of aging and neurodegenerative disease on the brain in humans. I also have interests in applying these techniques to animal models of aging and disease.
I participate in the state funded Arizona Alzheimer's Research Center, the NIA funded Arizona Alzheimer's Disease Center, and the NIA Alzheimer’s Disease Neuroimaging Initiative (ADNI).
