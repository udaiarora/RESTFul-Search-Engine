HALO Animal Rescue

Halo is looking for interns who are interested in fundraising. They are currently seeking funds to continue their work and mission which is to end the pet overpopulation epidemic by providing temporary shelter to abandoned cats and dogs until permanent homes can be found. Each year, HALO places over 2,000 abandoned animals in loving and forever homes. HALO was started in 1994 by a mother and daughter who wanted to make a difference. With the help of a grant writer and someone who is able to raise funds for their organization, they can do even more. Their hope is to build a shelter for our animals and start a spay/neuter clinic so they can go out to the low income neighborhoods and get the animals spayed/neutered so they can reduce the number of unwanted animals in another capacity.

I encourage anyone who is interested to visit our HALO website. Also, for more information, contact Danielle at 602-410-8514.

In Touch Mission International

In Touch Mission International is a non-profit organization that raises American's awareness of the great needs in other countries. This is done through our newsletter and local public speaking. We have many projects overseas which we work to enable by raising US awareness and funds on their behalf. The following are internships which we are currently offering:
Grant Writing Internship
These individuals will conduct necessary research and write grants for funds for ITMI projects. Multiple interns are needed for this job. Of course, experience in writing corporate communication and research is a plus, but we are willing to train individuals who are interested. The hours are flexible and grant writing is a highly valued resume skill.
Non-profit Office Intern
This individual will help with filling book orders, filing, general office tasks, mailings, etc.
Video and Web Internship
This individual will edit videos taken by our director and put them on our website. Must have website skills as well as video editing skills. As of right now, we have many videos that we would like to put to use on our website, but this internship could include opportunities to take some videos of events or speakers as well.
Bibles for Africa Program Internship
This individual would start and coordinate an ongoing effort to recruit people in churches, beginning locally and expanding from there, to buy used Bibles at garage sales and used book stores, then mail them to Africa. This would require finding good deals on mailing, possibly through FedEx employees, raising money for mailing, recruiting individuals in churches to buy Bibles, designing publications for the program and scheduling speaking opportunities to promote it.
General Internships
ITMI's normal business includes a wide variety of activities and opportunities. Although we cannot guarantee to have something for everyone, we are able to create a variety of types of positions for visionary individuals with an idea of what they want to do in an internship assuming it fits with our mission statement and purpose.
Liaison Internship
This individual will facilitate interaction between Project CURE and ITMI. Project CURE and ITMI partner to send shipments of medical supplies to needy places around the world, in exchange for help for our ministries from the government. There are so many people that benefit from these partnerships. However, each shipment requires a great deal of explicit communication, negotiating and general communication. This intern would be entrusted with this. Will requires odd hours because communicating with the other side of the world can sometimes only be done on their time zone.
Marketing/Public Relations
This individual would learn as much as possible about ITMI, then contact local churches and arrange and carry out promotion opportunities, such as speaking in Sunday School classes, a short interview in the main service, setting up a booth with information in the foyer, recruiting classes for service projects, etc. Must be available on Sundays for promotion opportunities. Other hours are flexible.
Internship candidates should be aware of and in agreement with our organization's statement of faith and purposes. Please visit In Touch Mission website to learn more, then contact Summer Kelley at 480-968-4100 between 9am and 5pm for more details or to express interest.
