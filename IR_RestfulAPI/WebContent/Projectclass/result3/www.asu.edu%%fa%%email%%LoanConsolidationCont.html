Save Money - Consolidate Your Loans Today

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this e-mail notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

Paying off your student loans may seem light years away, however, it is not too early to do something to reduce your loan debt before you ever make a payment. You may apply for a Direct Consolidation Loan now. This loan is available to most Direct Loan borrowers and certain other borrowers even before graduation.

What is consolidation? It is the combining of multiple student loans into one new loan with a lower fixed interest rate.

Applying is easy and can take as little as 8 minutes online at www.loanconsolidation.ed.gov. You do not have to fill out forms at the Student Financial Assistance office.

Interest rates may continue to rise so don't hesitate to call and consolidate today. This is the last opportunity to consolidate your loans while you are in school. As of July 1, 2006 in-school consolidation will no longer be an option.

Visit www.loanconsolidation.ed.gov or call 1-800-557-7392 for more information.
