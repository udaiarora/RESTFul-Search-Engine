What if the journal is not in the online catalog

If the online catalog does not list the journal you need, or the library does not own the issue you want, ASU Libraries can request a copy of the article from another library. This service is called Interlibrary Loan (or ILL).

To request an article through interlibrary loan, you will need the full citation for the article:

  • author, article title, journal title, volume, date, and page numbers
You can request the article through the libraries? website by filling out an online form and submitting it to the library. A completed ILL form for an article would look like this:

It may take one day to two weeks to obtain an item through Interlibrary Loan.
