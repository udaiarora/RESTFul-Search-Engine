Social Work

INTRODUCTION

This subject guide lists resources in the area of Social Work available in Arizona State University’s Libraries. This is not a comprehensive bibliography, but rather a selective list of resources useful for beginning research on a topic in Social Work. Covered sources include dictionaries & encyclopedias, guides & handbooks and indexes & abstracts, including online services. Sources on children & youth, death and dying, disabilities, families, gerontology, statistics, and substance abuse are also covered in this guide.
