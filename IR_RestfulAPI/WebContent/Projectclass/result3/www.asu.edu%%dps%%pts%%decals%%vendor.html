VENDOR DECAL INFORMATION

Companies with annual contracts to conduct business at Arizona State University may purchase vendor decals. The cost per year is $720.00. The decal is valid from August 16 through August 15 of the following year. The cost is pro-rated monthly.

Every vehicle must display an ASU main decal when parking on campus. If you have more than one vehicle, every vehicle must have a decal or only one vehicle may be on campus at a time. For infrequent visits, loading zones, meters, and visitor lots may be a more economical option.

If you have any questions, please contact Decal Sales at (480) 965-6124, 7:30-4:30MST, Monday - Friday.

PARKING OPTIONS

Frequency of trips to campus, length of visits, and equipment involved should be considered when making a decision to purchase a vendor decal.

Yellow Loading Zones - 20 minute parking for loading/unloading only (FREE)

Meters - $1.50/hr. (one hour maximum)

Visitor Lots - $2/hr. ($8 maximum exit fee)

RESTRICTIONS

Parking is not permitted in:
  • Reserved spaces
  • Disabled spaces
  • Green curbs
  • Red curbs
  • Malls without a mall permit

Parking in a visitor lot or at meters requires you to pay the applicable fee.

The decal does not entitle vehicles to drive on the mall. A mall permit supplements, but does not replace, the parking decal. Due to congestion, driving on the mall is not recommended. If you need a mall permit, please contact Mr. John Carver, Facilities Management, at (480) 965-6489.
