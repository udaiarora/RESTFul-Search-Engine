Search Strategy:

  1. Click on the link for the specific book above. Be patient, this book may take several minutes to load.

  2. Either enter the compound's name in the search box OR scroll through the table of contents, expand the appropriate section by clicking on the + sign, and then click on the text icon on the right for the compound.

  3. To find the original articles in which the toxicity information was reported, look for the link to the references. In this example the link is "28ZPAK - 43, 72."   The "28ZPAK" part of the reference refers to the information at the bottom of the record - this identifies the book or journal; the "43, 72" identifies the volumes, issue, and/or page numbers within that book/journal.
