About Histogram Controls

With a Histogram control, you can create:

  • Two-dimensional histograms with either one or two variables

  • Three-dimensional histograms of either two or three variables

The simplest histogram displays a two-dimensional visualization of a single variable. For example, the following histogram shows the frequency for the single variable Age, specified for the X axis.
