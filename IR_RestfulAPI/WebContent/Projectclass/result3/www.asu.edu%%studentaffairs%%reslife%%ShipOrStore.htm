Site Home > The UPS Store @ ASU

The UPS Store offers storage for the summer. So plan ahead and store for the summer items that don't need to be shipped home. The UPS Store has two convenient locations on campus. North campus at Palo Verde East and South campus at the Sonora Center. Both locations offer full service packaging and shipping services and are open Mon-Fri 9:00am-6:00pm and Sat 10:00am-2:00pm. Closed Sunday.

If you are returning in the fall, consider storage as an affordable option. Shipping large items may be more expensive than the value of the item. For a reasonable price, The UPS Store can store your items until you return in the fall. Our knowledgeable staff can help you determine which option is best to suit your needs.

Choose the perfect service for your needs:

  • Storage
  • UPS Ground
  • UPS 3 Day Select
  • UPS 2nd Day Air
  • UPS Next Day Air

All UPS shipping services are trackable with insurance and guaranteed delivery dates.

It’s simple. We provide the box; you fill it up and return it to one of our conveniently located stores on the Campus. We seal it, store it for the summer, and have it awaiting your return for pick-up.

Please see the following for sizes and prices:

22x22x22 Box $40.00/summer Up to 70lbs*
18x18x18 Box $25.00/summer Up to 50lbs*
24x18x12 Box $25.00/summer Up to 50lbs*
Bike Box $65.00/summer
Mini-Fridge $55.00/summer No box necessary

Bring your items to The UPS StoreTM on campus. We’ll label and store them off-site in a climate controlled, secured warehouse. The items will be available at your choice of The UPS StoreTM locations on campus at a convenient time during move-in**.

Visit us at one of the following conveniently located stores:

The UPS StoreTM at Palo Verde East The UPS StoreTM at South Campus
350 East University Drive 725 East Adelphi Drive
Tempe, Arizona 85281 Tempe, Arizona 85281

*Items weighing over the allowable weight will be accessed an additional fee of $10.00/box. Other items may be priced and allowed for storage at The UPS Stores discretion.
** There may be additional charges for deliveries outside the designated delivery time window.
