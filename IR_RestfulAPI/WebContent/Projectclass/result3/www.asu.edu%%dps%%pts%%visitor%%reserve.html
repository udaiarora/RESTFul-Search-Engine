Visitor Parking

Click here for ASU Foundation Visitor Parking Information

Reservations may only be made by ASU Departments. Arrival times must be between 7am - 5 pm and Monday thru Friday ONLY. Reservations are not for ASU Main students, staff or faculty members. Requests need to be received by our office two business days prior to your guests' arrival date.

Requests for larger groups (20 or more guests) need to be made at least two weeks prior to the reservation date and should be submitted online. Reservations for groups require an additional lot attendant and/or alternate location which will be determined by PTS. The lot attendant is necessary to allow for a proper and speedy check-in, as well as to help monitor parking availability within the lot. There is a one-hour minimum, $16 per hour, per attendant. An internal PO # will be required once reservation request are confirmed

Validation Stamps may be purchased at the Towers Administrative Office. They are sold only to ASU Departments. You may purchase a book of one hundred or a sheet of ten all day/and 1 hour stamps. Please have a copy of your Purchase Order and ASU ID.
