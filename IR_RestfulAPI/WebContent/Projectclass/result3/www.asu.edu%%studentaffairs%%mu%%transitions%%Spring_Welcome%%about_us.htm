About Us

The mission of Transition and Parent Programs is to coordinate and direct transition activities and parents programs and services for new students and their families on behalf of the Division of Student Affairs. Transition and Parent Programs aims to assimilate new students and their families to the services, resources, and campus culture at Arizona State University, and to provide resources, programs, and information for parents and families.
