Future Commencement Dates

Future commencement dates are determined by the University Academic Calendar, which is set by the ASU Registrar’s office. Dates for 2006 and 2007 Commencements are confirmed. Dates for 2008 Commencement Ceremonies are still tentative.

These dates are for University Commencement Only. Please contact the dean’s office of your college to request convocation information.
