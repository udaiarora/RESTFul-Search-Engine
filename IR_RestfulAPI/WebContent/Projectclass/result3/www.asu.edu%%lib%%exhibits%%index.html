Exhibits at ASU Libraries

Hayden and Noble Libraries’ Exhibits

The ASU Libraries’ Exhibits Committee facilitates the exhibits located in the Hayden Library Lobby and in the Noble Library Atrium. These exhibits reflect the scholarly, historical, social, and cultural concerns of Arizona State University and its community. They are viewable during normal library hours.


Archives and Special Collections

Archives and Special Collections has exhibits in the Luhrs Gallery, which is located on the 4th floor of Hayden Library. For information about these exhibits, please visit the Luhrs Gallery.


Archives and Special Collections also has online collections and exhibits.


Arizona Historical Foundation

Located on the 4th floor of the Hayden Library, the Arizona Historical Foundation supports two exhibit areas. Saints and Shady Ladies is a permanent exhibit featuring black and white photos of some of Arizona's wild and not so wild women. Other exhibits are mounted on a rotational basis.
