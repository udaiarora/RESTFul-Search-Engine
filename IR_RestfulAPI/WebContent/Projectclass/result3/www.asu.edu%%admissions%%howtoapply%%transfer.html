What should I do if I miss the priority application dates?
Apply as soon as possible. Students admitted are expected to complete Orientation, so the sooner you apply for admission, the sooner you are on track for Orientation and other steps toward completing your enrollment.

When is the best time to transfer to ASU?
You should transfer to ASU as soon as you are admissible so you are on the right track for earning your university degree.

Do my transcripts need to be at ASU by the priority date?
Yes. Your application is considered complete when ASU has received your "Application for Undergraduate Admission," your transcripts and test scores, and your application fee.

I went to college while in high school and have college credit, but I have not gone to college since I graduated from high school. Am I a freshman or transfer student?
You will be considered to be a freshman. A freshman is a student who is applying to ASU while enrolled in high school or a student who has not attended college since high school graduation. Please read the freshman information.

I graduated from high school and went to college, but I do not have many college credits. Am I a transfer student or a freshman?
You will be considered a transfer student. A transfer student is one who has graduated from high school and has attended college after graduation from high school.

I previously attended ASU as a transfer student. Am I a transfer student or a readmission student?
If you have previously attended ASU as a degree-seeking student, you will be considered a readmission student. Please read the readmission information.

For more information about transferring to ASU, visit www.asu.edu/admissions/transfercenter.
