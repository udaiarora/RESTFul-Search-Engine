Organization Open House

ASU Organization Open House
Wednesday, January 31
11 a.m. - 1:30 p.m.
Hayden Lawn and North MU

Registration is now closed. All ASU students are encouraged to stop by and check out the variety of student organizations and activities.

Use this opportunity to recruit new members, promote your club and advertise your upcoming events. Bring as many of your members as possible to your table to show your ASU and club pride.

Your club is encouraged to bring an interactive activity. PRIZES will be awarded for the Best Display, Most Engaged and Best Overall!
