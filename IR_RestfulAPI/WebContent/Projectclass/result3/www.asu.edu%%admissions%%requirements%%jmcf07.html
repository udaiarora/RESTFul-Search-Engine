Freshman Admission Requirements
 

•  3.0 (4.0 = A) ABOR GPA

•  and have no English competency deficiencies

•  and meet one of the following two requirements:

      •  top 25% of high school class

      •  or 1040 SAT Reasoning or 22 ACT

•  and if you have taken college courses prior to high school graduation, you must have a 3.0 transfer GPA.

 

See also the ASU General Catalog.

Transfer Admission Requirements
 

•  a minimum of 12 transferable college credit hours earned after high school graduation; with fewer than 12 transferable college credit hours, students must also meet freshman admission requirements above.

•  and 3.0 cumulative transfer GPA

•  and no English competency deficiencies

Transfer applicants must apply for admission to the Cronkite School. The admissions committee considers a variety of criteria, including major and cumulative GPA, media experience, writing ability, and commitment to the field.

 

See also the ASU General Catalog.
