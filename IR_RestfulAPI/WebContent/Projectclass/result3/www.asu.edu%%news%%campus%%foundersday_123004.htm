ASU seeks nominees for Founders’ Day awards

The ASU Alumni Association is seeking nominations through Jan. 6 for the university’s Founders’ Day awards, given annually to recognize excellence among ASU’s alumni, faculty and staff.

The awards, among the university’s highest honors, will be presented at an April 7 ceremony at ASU’s Memorial Union.

“Founders’ Day recognizes the foresight and determination of those who were instrumental in establishing this university,” says Alumni Association President Diane McCarthy.

Two awards will be given to alumni in recognition of their contributions to ASU and the community. The Alumni Achievement Award, open to all ASU alumni, and the Young Alumni Award, given to a graduate of the past 15 years, also honor alumni for excellence in their professions.

The staff achievement award recognizes an ASU staff member whose outstanding contributions to ASU have significantly enhanced the well-being and reputation of ASU within the community at large.

Faculty members are recognized in three categories: recognizing excellence in teaching, research and service.

Nomination forms and award information can be found online at (www.asu.edu/alumni/programs/awards/founders). Inquiries can be directed to Jill Andrews at (480) 965-7754.

Holeman, with the ASU Alumni Association, can be reached at (480) 965-8150 or (jholeman@asu.edu).
