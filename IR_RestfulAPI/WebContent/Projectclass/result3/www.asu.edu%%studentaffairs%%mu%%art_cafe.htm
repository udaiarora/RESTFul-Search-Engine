Art Café

The Art Café featuring Einstein Bros. Bagels is an informal eatery and community gathering space that provides an avenue for ASU students, faculty and staff alumni to demonstrate their talents in the area of performing and visual arts. The café includes not only delicious bagels, sandwiches and beverages, but works of art by ASU community members during the semester.

Throughout the semester, scheduled performances and events will occur. Performers are ASU students, faculty, staff, and alumni. In addition to the performances, the Art Café is home to Gallery.

Gallery at the Art Café features an assortment of abstract art, mixed media, photography prints, drawings, and paintings. The exhibitions typically run for four weeks before being rotated. Below is the schedule of upcoming performances and Gallery exhibitions.

If you are interested in exhibiting your art, or for more information about the Art Café please contact Gabriel Escontrias, at (480) 965-9023.
