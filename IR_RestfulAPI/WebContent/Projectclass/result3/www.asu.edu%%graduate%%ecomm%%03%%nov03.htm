The due date for tuition and fees for RAs/TAs will now be automatically delayed until Mar 25 (spring) and Oct 25 (fall) as long as:

  • The academic unit listed the student on the RA/TA Tuition Offset Form submitted to the Graduate College financial support office, and

RAs and TAs employed 50% time are reminded to specify that they are using financial aid to pay for health insurance when they register (via SunDial or ASU Interactive) in order to receive their health insurance award.

Graduate College Awards for Tuition (GCAT) are still available for the spring semester. www.asu.edu/graduate/resources/generalinfo/GCAT

Family Educational Rights & Privacy (FERPA) reminder: A student's information can only be released to the student. To release a student's information to a third party, you must have written authorization from the student. You may only mail documents (including express mail such as Federal Express or Airborne) to the ASU address on record (i.e., one of the addresses on the SIS) unless you have written authorization from the student to send his/her mail to a different address. Questions concerning these policies should be directed to the director of graduate admissions, Michael Dickson, at Dickson@asu.edu or 5-4885.
