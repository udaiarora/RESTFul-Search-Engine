Get the Latest News

To keep you up-to-date, you can subscribe to NEWDOCNEWS, a monthly electronic newsletter. This newsletter provides SAS software users with information about new books published during the previous month, notice of new Observations articles on our web page, announcements of online documentation, notice of documentation updates, and general news items about SAS documentation.

For USA customers to automatically receive this electronic newsletter via e-mail, here is how you can subscribe:

    1. Send an e-mail message to listserv@vm.sas.com.
    2. Leave the Subject line blank.
    3. The text of the message should be

      4. Subscribe NEWDOCNEWS-L John Doe (replace John Doe with your name)

    4. Send the message.

You will receive a message confirming that you have been added to the NEWDOCNEWS LISTSERV. The message will also contain instructions on how to unsubscribe from the listserv in the future, if you choose.

For International customers to automatically receive this electronic newsletter via e-mail, here is how you can subscribe:

    1. Send an e-mail message to pubs@unx.sas.com.
    2. The subject line should be ? Add to NEWDOCNEWS .
    3. The text of the message should be

      4. Subscribe NEWDOCNEWS John Doe (replace John Doe with your name)

    4. Send the message.

We will forward your name and address to your local SAS office and they will add you to their electronic newsletter listserv.
