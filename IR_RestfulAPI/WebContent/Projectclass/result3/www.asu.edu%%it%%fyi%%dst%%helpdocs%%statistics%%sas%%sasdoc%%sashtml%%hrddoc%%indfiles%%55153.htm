The Cartoon Guide to Statistics

by Larry Gonick and Woollcott Smith

All statisticians, from beginners to the experienced, will delight in this humorous approach to the central ideas of modern statistics. Topics explained in simple and funny illustrations include the summary and display of data, probability in medicine and gambling, Bernoulli Trials, the Central Limit Theorem, and confidence interval estimation.

240 pages.
