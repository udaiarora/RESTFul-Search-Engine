December 4, 2003

AzTE takes on new vice president of marketing and managing director of Technology Venture Clinic

Tempe, Arizona - Arizona Technology Enterprises, LLC (AzTE), the technology licensing and business development company responsible for the commercialization of university research from Arizona State University (ASU) and Northern Arizona University (NAU) announced today the addition of Charlie Lewis as Vice President of Marketing and Managing Director of the Technology Venture Clinic. As Vice President, Mr. Lewis will be responsible for the development of the sales and marketing plans for AzTE as well as managing the new Technology Venture Clinic.

Mr. Lewis will be creating brand awareness, establishing corporate identity and designing marketing strategies for inventions born out of ASU. Chief Executive Officer of Arizona Technology Enterprises, Peter Slate stated, "Marketing is crucial to the commercialization of intellectual property, and with the amount of technology being generated at ASU, Charlie will be a valuable addition to the team."

The Technology Venture Clinic, funded by Rogers & Theobald law firm and Wasatch Venture Fund, will function as a teaching laboratory for top students attending ASU. The clinic will draw from several disciplines including law, business, science and engineering, where students will gain first hand knowledge of marketing innovations developed at ASU. Mr. Lewis will be coordinating the activities of the students involved in the clinic to help accelerate the success of AzTE. Slate said, "The Venture Clinic is something that will benefit everyone involved. Students will contribute new ideas and solutions in marketing innovations, and will also gain valuable skills that will be useful throughout their professional lives."

Prior to working with Arizona Technology Enterprises, Mr. Lewis was a Managing Partner at Arris Ventures, where he led the team of partners focused on early stage technology investing. Before joining the team at Arris Ventures, Mr. Lewis worked as Vice President of Sales and Marketing at Paradise 94. While there he co-managed $25 million of private equity.

"With the current momentum that Arizona has generated in the fields of technology and life sciences," stated Mr. Lewis, "it is exciting to be part of an organization like AzTE that will help further innovation and economic development in our state."

Peter Slate added, "Charlie has an enormous amount of talent and expertise in the field of venture capital and marketing. With his talents we will be able to commercialize technology more efficiently and market in a more effective manner."

About Arizona Technology Enterprises

Arizona Technology Enterprises, developed in March of 2003, is the technology commercialization and business development company working with Arizona State University (ASU) and Northern Arizona University (NAU). The mission of the company is to build strong long lasting relationships between the universities and the business industry. These relationships will help strengthen research and create more investment opportunities in Arizona.
