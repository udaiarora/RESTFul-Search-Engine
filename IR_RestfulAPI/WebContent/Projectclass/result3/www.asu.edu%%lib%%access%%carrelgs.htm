Graduate Study Carrels

Guidelines and Regulations

Assignment


Studies will be assigned according to the following priorities:

  1. Doctoral students writing dissertations
  2. Doctoral students studying for comprehensives

Applicants may only apply to either Hayden Library or Noble Science & Engineering Library. A carrel may not be kept at both libraries.


Assignments will be made shortly following the start of the Fall semester. Notification will be by email, phone or mail. Failure to pick up by designated date will result in reassignment of the carrel.


Use of Studies


Carrels may be assigned to two graduate students, who may or may not know each other. Please be courteous, clean, orderly, and accommodating. If a serious conflict of schedule occurs, please see the Administrative Assistant for Access Services at Hayden’s Circulation Desk.


Library policy requires that there be no food or drink in the studies. Any such items will be removed.


The key given to you should not be passed to another person. There is a replacement charge, currently $10.00, for a lost key.

 

Important: In order that all patrons have equal access to the library collection, no library material may be kept in studies overnight unless those materials are checked out to your ID. Studies (and study desks) are opened and examined by library personnel on a regular basis. Unchecked material will be removed immediately and placed back in the stacks.

 

Repeated violations of any of the above guidelines will result in the revocation of the study privilege.
