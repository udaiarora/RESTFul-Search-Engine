08. Global Engagement

North American Center for Transborder Studies

 

Regional competitiveness can help North America succeed in the world market. The North American Center for Transborder Studies is a trinational research alliance that pairs governmental partners with academic institutions in order to tackle real problems located both within North American border regions and continent-wide.

 

The center is unique in that it has direct access to policy makers at the federal level—where border policy is drafted. Housed at academic institutions in Canada, Mexico, and the United States, the center will utilize its research capabilities to explore issues related to North America and provide policy recommendations for technology, environment, migration, and economic development.
