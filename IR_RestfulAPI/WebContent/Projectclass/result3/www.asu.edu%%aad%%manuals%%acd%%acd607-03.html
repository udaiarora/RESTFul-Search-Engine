ACD 607–03: Participation in a Retirement Plan

Purpose

To specify participation in the Arizona State Retirement System or the Optional Retirement Plan

Sources

Arizona Revised Statutes §§ 38–781.01 to –.03; –.07 to –.10; –841 to –855; 15–1628
Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 6–602

Applicability

Faculty or academic professionals who:

  1. are employed to work 20 hours per week (50 percent FTE) or more for any 20 weeks during the fiscal year

    and

  2. are contributing to Social Security.

Policy

Faculty and academic professionals are required to contribute to one of the following retirement plans: the Arizona State Retirement System (ASRS) or the Optional Retirement Plan (ORP).

Faculty members or academic professionals and the state will contribute equally to the retirement plan chosen. The contribution rate to the ASRS and the ORP are subject to change July 1 each year as approved by the legislature.
