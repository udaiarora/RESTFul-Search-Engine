Journals

  • Deutschen Akademie der Wissenschaften zu Berlin List of Journals
    http://www.bbaw.de/bibliothek/digital/
    Page images from over 10 early science periodicals.

  • Gallica (Bibliotheque Nationale de France) List of Journals
    http://gallica.bnf.fr/PeriosListe.htm
    Page Images from over 25 early science, engineering or medical periodicals.

  • Internet Library of Early Journals
    http://www.bodley.ox.ac.uk/ilej/
    "A digital library of 18th and 19th Century journals." Includes: Annual Register, Blackwood's Edinburgh Magazine, Gentleman's Magazine, Notes and Queries, Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society, and The Builder.

  • JSTOR
    Library Network Database
    Access to thousands of journal archives.

  • Online Book Page: Serials
    http://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/serials.html
    This page lists freely accessible archives of serials (such as magazines, journals, newspapers, and other periodicals).

  • Selected Classical Papers from the History of Chemistry (Carmen Giunta, Le Moyne College)
    http://webserver.lemoyne.edu/faculty/giunta/papers.html
    "papers from the history of chemistry ... Many are seminal papers in their fields. Some are interesting curiosities."
