With an ever-increasing reliance on personal computing, the desktop is becoming a focal point for both academic and research activities. As such it is primarily the desktop, which invokes or connects to services in order to fulfill an individual's academic or research needs.

In support of the Academic and Research missions of the university, the Desktop Systems Technology (DST) group facilitates connectivity to, and the use of, ASURITE services for not only IT computing sites and classrooms but also for desktop systems accessing IT provided resources.
