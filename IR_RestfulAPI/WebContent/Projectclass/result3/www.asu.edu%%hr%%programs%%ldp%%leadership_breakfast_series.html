The Leadership Breakfast Series

Purpose

The Leadership Breakfast Series is a new endeavor, bringing together academic and community leaders to share ideas and encourage progress. Two breakfast sessions are scheduled each semester. The format reflects the University's belief that two elements are necessary for effective leadership/management of large universities--the learning of cutting-edge information to improve decision making, and the networking among faculty, students, and community members. The series brings people together to hear well-known experts on such topics as the environment and the media. Previous lecturers have included environmental activist and attorney Robert F. Kennedy Jr. and Pulitzer-prize winning author David Halberstam.

Contact Information

The Leadership Breakfast Series is planned by the Office of the President and coordinated by a designated Program Coordinator through the Learning and Performance Solutions Group.
