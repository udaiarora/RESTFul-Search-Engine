RSP 402: Patents

ASU’s policies and procedures regarding intellectual property are controlled by the Board of Regents’ Intellectual Property Policy (ABOR 6-908) adopted at the June 24-25, 1999 meeting. To view ABOR 6-908, please visit the Board of Regents’ Web site, which is maintained outside of the ASU system of administrative manuals, at Arizona Board of Regents’ Policy Manual.

To return to this manual, you may wish to set a bookmark to http://www.asu.edu/aad/manuals/rsp/index.html.

For implementation of ABOR 6-908, see RSP 604, “Intellectual Property Management Implementation Policy.”
