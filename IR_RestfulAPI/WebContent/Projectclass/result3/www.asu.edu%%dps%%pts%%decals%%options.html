PARKING HOURS

7 AM to 4 PM WEEKDAYS: Parking lots restricted for decal holders with the specific lot number on their decal.

4 PM to 7 PM WEEKDAYS: All ASU Tempe campus decals (excluding motorcycles) may cross-park in any decal lot ( EXCEPT Residence and Restricted Lots). Click here for a list of Restricted Lots & Structures.

AFTER 7 PM WEEKDAYS, AND ANY TIME ON NON-EVENT WEEKENDS: No decal is required EXCEPT in Residence and Restricted Lots. Click here for a list of Restricted Lots & Structures.

PARKING OPTIONS

Disabled - Signed parking stalls for disabled parking are available in most lots. Vehicles displaying a valid ASU Tempe Disabled Parking decal may park only in the appropriate signed Disabled stall in the parking lots. Visitors displaying a disabled placard or plate may park in disabled visitor lot stalls and pay the appropriate parking fees. Please notify ASU Parking and Transit Dispatch Office at 965-0641 when a disabled stall is occupied by an unauthorized vehicle.

Motorcycle - Motorcycles over 50cc must display a valid motorcycle or conversion decal. Conversion decal hangers must be retained from year to year to be reused. Conversion hangers cost an additional $10. Motorcycles under 50cc are not authorized for use on public streets, but must use bike lanes and park in bike racks on Campus.
