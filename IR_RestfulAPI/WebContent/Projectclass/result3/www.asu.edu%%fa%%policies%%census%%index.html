Census & Proration Policy

Your Credit Load Affects Your Aid

Your offered awards were based on full-time enrollment in both the fall and spring semesters. If you register for less than full-time in either the fall or spring terms, your aid may be prorated based on your lower enrollment status.

If your enrollment changes prior to Census (see right column for definition), your awards may fluctuate according to the schedule below. Dropping credits after your awards have been disbursed (including direct deposit) may result in a bill. Census enrollment figures will determine your final semester disbursement amounts.

Student Financial Assistance does not count audited courses toward students' enrollment status.

Definition: Census

The point in time that ASU's SFAO evaluates your official enrollment status for financial aid purposes. The actual time that Census occurs is at 9PM [Mountain Standard Time] on the 21st calendar day of each semester.
