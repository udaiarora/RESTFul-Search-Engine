March 10, 2004

Sun Devil Football Ticket Giveaway Under way

Sun Devil Ticket Office Manager Jason Butikofer and his staff are preparing for brisk sales this spring as ASU fans take advantage of an ultra generous football ticket giveaway.

Current season ticket holders who renew their 2004 season tickets in full by April 7 will receive two complimentary season tickets on top of those purchased, and can also purchase single game tickets to home games at approximately half off the public rate through July 1.

In addition to this incentive, Sun Devil football season and single-game ticket prices will remain the same as last year and in some instances be reduced even further for the 2004 season.

Butikofer says this promotion is the latest effort to reward loyal Sun Devil fans.

"People are excited to take extra people with them to Sun Devil Stadium on Saturdays, and at the same time are excited to see increased attendance and in turn an improved atmosphere at Sun Devil Stadium," he says.

Butikofer says ticket season holders will get a lot for their money including ticket priority status for bowl games and the opportunity to purchase a parking pass.

Those who renew online or in person by March 17 will be entered in the Early Renewal Drawing for prizes including a trip for two to the ASU-California game in Berkeley Oct. 30, a group party package of 20 upper-level sideline tickets for either the ASU-Stanford game Nov. 6 or the ASU-Washington State game Nov. 13, six loge tickets to the ASU-UTEP game Sept. 2, four loge tickets to the ASU-Oregon State game Sept. 25, a foursome of golf at The Legacy Golf Course, a foursome of golf at Karsten Golf Course at ASU, as well as an assortment of ASU merchandise such as helmets, jerseys and autographed footballs.

To order new season tickets or single game tickets, including the $5 tickets in the expanded Koetter's Korners sections, call (480) 965-2381. New season tickets go on sale May 1. Single game tickets go on sale Aug. 2. Single game tickets can be purchased online at (www.thesundevils.com). Fans can also buy tickets at the Sun Devil ticket office, located at Sun Devil Stadium, Monday through Friday between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m.

Zajac, with Media Relations & Public Information, can be reached at (480) 965-8145 or (izajac@asu.edu).
