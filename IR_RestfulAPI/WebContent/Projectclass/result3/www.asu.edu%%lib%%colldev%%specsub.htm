Subject Websites


French Studies Web Site
  • Offers links to a variety of Internet resources on French studies including French culture, language, literature, news sources, as well as library catalogs in Canada and France.  Please refer to Hayden Reference Humanities Sources page for additional subject information.
     
  • FRENCH 500: BIBLIOGRAPHY AND RESEARCH METHODS
    This page presents library resources related to French 500: Bibliography and Research Methods, a graduate seminar taught at ASU, Fall Semester 1999.  Includes sections for library help online, resources for literary research, bibliographies by period, selected Internet resources and course syllabus.
     
  • FRENCH 535: FRENCH LITERATURE OF THE 16TH CENTURY
    This page presents library resources related to French 535: French Literature of the 16th Century, a graduate course taught at ASU, Fall Semester 2000.  Includes sections for library help online, resources for literary research, bibliographies by period, selected electronic and Internet resources, including Renaissance links, and course syllabus.

German Studies Web Site
  • Provides links to German Studies resources on the Internet including links to resources on German language, dictionaries, electronic publications, and libraries in Austria, Germany and Switzerland.  For additional subject or other resources, refer to Hayden Reference Humanities Sources.
     
  • GERMAN 500: BIBLIOGRAPHY AND RESEARCH METHODS
    This page presents library resources related to German 500: Bibliography and Research Methods, a graduate seminar taught at ASU, Fall Semester 1999.  Includes sections for library research guides, library help online, selected Internet resources and a syllabus which contains a listing of all bibliographies and reference works discussed in the course and  help by ASU Libraries.
History Studies Web Site
  • Provides links to Medieval, and U.S. History subject pages, and provides a list of historical web sites available on the internet.  For further History subject or resource pages, please refer to the Hayden Reference History Sources.
Russia and East Europe Web Site
  • Provides information about our Russian and East European collection and connects to Internet resources on campus, around the nation and the world.  For further History subject pages or European History pages, please refer to the Hayden Reference History Sources.
Southeast Asian Studies Web Site
  • Provides a description of the Southeast Asian studies collection, information to ASU Southeast Asian resources, and a connection to other university libraries, institutions, and newspapers.  Also see Hayden Reference Humanities Sources.
