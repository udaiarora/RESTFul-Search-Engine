Identity Examples

Applicability: Identity bars are must be included on all web sites that do not have an ASU logo at the top of the page.

Purpose: To improve overall site navigation by providing a link back to ASU home page and provide consistent branding across all ASU web sites.

Related Links:

  • Identity Bar Color Combinations
  • Download Identity Bar Graphics
  • Download HTML Code for Identity Bar

Guidelines for use:

  • A 25-pixel bar contains the words “Arizona State University” in 12 point Frutiger, The words “Arizona State University” provide a link to the ASU Home Page (www.asu.edu).
  • The text in the identity bar must be left justified (aligned with the upper left corner of the page) or content justified (aligned with the left side of the page content).
  • The text and background colors of the identity bar must be approved ASU colors.
  • The identity bar’s background color should contrast with the background color of the page it is on (or adjacent elements), so that the bar is visibly distinct from the rest of the page.
