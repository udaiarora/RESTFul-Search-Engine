CLAS Advisement and Registration Program Summary

Thank you very much for your interest in the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences Advisement Program on August 5, 2005.

Students attending the CLAS Advisement and Registration Program will learn ASU academic requirements, meet with academic advisors, and register for fall semester classes. All students should complete Pre-Orientation Homework before attending the program.

Make a Reservation:
Before making a reservation, you will need your ASURITE UserID and Password to complete the reservation process. These were provided to you in the letter acknowledging ASU's receipt of your admission application. You must first activate your ASURITE UserID and set a password. If you need more information about, or help with, your ASURITE ID and Password, please refer to the instructions in Steps to Attending ASU online.

Make a Reservation

Check-In and Program Details:
Check-In begins in the Memorial Union Ventana AB Ballroom at 7 a.m. The program concludes at Noon.

Contact Information:
If you have any questions regarding the CLAS Advisement and Registration Program, please call 480.965.6506 for further information.

Parking:
Arrive 30 minutes early to park and walk to the Memorial Union. ASU Visitor Parking is available in Lot 17 West on the southeast corner of Apache Blvd. and College Ave. (Lot 17 West is a meter lot that charges $1.00 per hour) and Structure 4 on the southwest corner of Terrace Rd. and Rural Rd. Limited visitor parking space may be available in Parking Structure 1 on the northwest corner of Apache Blvd. And Normal Ave. Posted restrictions and fees apply.

Please observe and obey all of ASU's posted parking regulations. Parking fees are subject to change without notice. For more information, please visit the Parking and Transit Services web site at www.asu.edu/dps/pts or call 480.965.4527
