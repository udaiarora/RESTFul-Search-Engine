Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

December 5, 2001

Present: Carol Behl, Patty Feldman, Stefan Myhajlenko, Annie Platoff, Linda Shackle, Santos Vega

The group approved the minutes of October 3, 2001 and November 7, 2001.

Draft Letter Requesting Voting Rights for Academic Associates:

The group discussed drafting a new letter to request that Academic Associates be allowed to vote for their CAPS representative. Based on advice from Gail Hackett, the group decided that the letter would be addressed to Dr. Morton Munk, President of the Academic Senate. Carol will draft the letter, which will be circulated and approved via e-mail.

CAPS Speakers:

The group discussed possible speakers for the Spring semester. Patty will schedule a speaker for February and another for later in the semester. We will publicize the event through the AP e-mail distribution list and the Insight.

Old Business:

none

New Business:

none

CAPS regular business meetings will be on the first Wednesday of each month. The next one will be Wednesday, February 6 at 10 a.m. in Room 214 of the Memorial Union. Santos volunteered to be the substitute secretary for the meeting in February. There will not be a CAPS meeting in January.

Annie Platoff, Secretary
