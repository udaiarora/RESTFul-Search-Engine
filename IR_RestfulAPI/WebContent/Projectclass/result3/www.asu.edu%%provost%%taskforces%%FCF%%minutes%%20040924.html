Rolling Appointments

Development of Policies and Procedures is moving slowly. A draft of the policies will go to the Personnel Advisory Council in October. After that it will be presented to the Deans. Implementation is not expected until Fall, 2005.

FCF will continue to keep the issue active.

Benefits

FCF are eligible for accrual of sick leave and for cash payment for accrued sick leave at retirement as long as they satisfy the requirements for retirement. See http://www.asu.edu/aad/manuals/spp/spp701-01.html

Need clarification of the requirements for retirement. Information in the ACD manual refers to "faculty member or academic professional."

Donation of Sick Time

Can sick time be donated to a pool for colleagues who have exhausted their own time? Staff may donate vacation time. Donating vacation time is acceptable, but it is not usually desirable to donate sick time that the employee might need.

The Vice Provost will discuss with David Butler

Salary issues and Salary Adjustments

FCF salaries are varied from unit to unit based on such factors as market value, availability of qualified applicants for the positions, and directives from the legislature, ABOR, and others. Salaries are public information and can be found in Hadyn Library by checking at the reference desk.

Salary adjustments may be based on rules voted in departments about 10 years ago. Since then the balance of Tenure/tenure track and FCF has been changing, especially in math and English. There are wide variations in who is eligible and who is considered continuing faculty.

An approach to these variations would be to urge the Provost to establish "Principles" to guide decisions, for example, "Continuing employees are eligible for salary adjustments."
