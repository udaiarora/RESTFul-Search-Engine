ACD 810: Registration of Faculty and Academic Professional Organizations or Groups

Purpose

To register faculty and academic professional organizations operating on campus and within the structure of the university

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

Every faculty and academic professional organization which operates within the structure and on the campus of the university, except as an official academic unit, division, committee, or board, shall register with the university. Registration shall be necessary before the facilities of the university are available to the organization.

The Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University administers the registration of faculty and academic professional organizations. (Forms are available in that office.)

Registration shall be permitted unless the university finds that an organization seeks to accomplish its local or national objectives, goals, purposes, or activities by the use of violence and advocates and incites immediate action which will probably materially and substantially interfere with the discipline and normal activities of the university and the rights of others, constituting a clear and present danger to the property, personnel, and orderly function of the university.

Registration of an organization by the university does not constitute university endorsement or approval of the policies and activities of the organization.

An organization shall neither represent nor imply, in any manner, in its contacts with the public, that it speaks for or in the name of the university.

The university shall assist registered faculty and academic professional organizations by scheduling facilities for group use under the policies of the university and the Board of Regents.
