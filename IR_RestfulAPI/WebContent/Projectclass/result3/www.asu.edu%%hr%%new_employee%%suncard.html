Sun Card - Permanent ASU Photo ID

The Sun Card is your permanent ASU photo identification card and is required for all full-time, permanent employees. This I.D. may be used to obtain university parking decals, bookstore discounts, library privileges, events tickets, and access to certain buildings on campus.

After an employee's hiring information has been entered into the HRMS payroll system and the employee has an ASU ID number, they are eligible to receive an ASU Sun Card Photo ID. You will be asked to fill out a card with your name, ASU ID number, and classification (Faculty, Staff, or Other). You may receive your Sun Card at your scheduled session of Benefits-Eligible New Employee Orientation in the University Services Building, room 1302 or at the Sun Card Office.

Sun Card Office Locations:

ASU at the Tempe campus: Sun Cards can be obtained in the Sun Card Office in the Memorial Union on the Lower Level on the south side of the building (inside Union Station).  Call 480-965-2273 with Sun Card questions. Sun Card Office Hours are Monday - Friday 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.

ASU at the West campus: Sun Cards can be obtained the Bookstore in the University Center Building during regular office hours. Call 602-543-5000 for more information.

ASU at the Polytechnic Campus: Sun Cards can be obtained at the Bookstore in the Student Union. Call 480-727-1762 for more information.

ASU Downtown Phoenix: Sun Card Office is located in the Bookstore location at 411 N. Central Ave.  Call 602-496-0348 for more information and hours of operation.

While visiting the Sun Card Office, pick up information on special services like Sun Dollars and Long Distance Calling Card.
