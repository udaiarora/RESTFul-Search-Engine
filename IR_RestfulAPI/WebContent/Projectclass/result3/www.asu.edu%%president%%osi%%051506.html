Green Loans Touted as Profit Opportunity
American Banker

In theory, the savings from energy-efficient building features should give both residential and commercial mortgage borrowers extra income to service their debt, helping to qualify them for larger loans.  But even though ready-made home loan products have made the process of valuing those savings relatively straightforward, such products have found few takers, partly because few consumers are aware of them, industry experts said.
