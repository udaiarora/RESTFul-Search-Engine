DIRECTORY INFORMATION

This slide emphasizes the importance of not releasing an item of information if it has not been identified as directory information.

It also reinforces one of the two statements in the slide titled "The Essence of the Act) (slide 12).

The exceptions to students' written permission are found in 34 CFR 99.31 (Appendix 2 of the 2001 AACRAO FERPA Guide) and at slide 44 of this presentation.
