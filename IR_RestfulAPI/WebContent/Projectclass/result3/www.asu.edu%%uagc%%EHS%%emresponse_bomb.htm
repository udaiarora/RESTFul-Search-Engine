BOMB THREAT


BY TELEPHONE:

DO NOT HANG UP!! Remain calm.

  • Take the caller seriously.
  • Ask a lot of questions, using the checklist below as a guide.
  • Have a co-worker call 9-1-1 on another line or call 9-1-1 yourself immediately after hanging up.
