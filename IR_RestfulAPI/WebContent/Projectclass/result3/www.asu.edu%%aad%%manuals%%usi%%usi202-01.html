USI 202–01: Readmission of Undergraduate Students

Purpose

To allow readmission to the university of an undergraduate student for the purpose of registration

Sources

University Registrar’s Office
University Undergraduate Admissions Board
Individual colleges within the university

Applicability

This policy applies to undergraduate students who:

  1. have not enrolled in ASU courses during the semester immediately preceding the application for readmission

  2. have not been academically disqualified

    and

  3. meet the requirements of the college in which they wish to enroll.

Note:This policy does not apply to undergraduate students who have previously been enrolled as nondegree undergraduates at the university and wish to enroll in degree programs upon returning to the university.

Under these circumstances, undergraduates must apply for admission as new degree-seeking students.

Policy

To be readmitted to the university, an undergraduate student must:

  1. apply for readmission at the University Registrar’s Office, Admission Services at the West campus, or on the Web for the specific semester for which he or she intends to reenroll

  2. submit transcripts for all academic work completed at an accredited college or university after leaving ASU (these transcripts must be sent directly from the transfer institution)

    and

  3. pay readmission application fee.

Action

  1. Skip to step 14.

  1. Forward the student’s application to:

    1. the dean’s office in the college, if the student wishes to remain in that college
