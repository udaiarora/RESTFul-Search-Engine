French Senior Lecturer

Three year renewable Senior Lecturer position with PhD with specialty in Applied French Linguistics to begin August 2007. Duties include teaching two French language courses per semester and coordinating and supervising multiple sections of the Lower-division language courses. Native or near native proficiency in French, a minimum of five years of experience teaching and supervising at the College level highly desirable. Evidence of familiarity with the use of technology in language instruction is required. Demonstrated knowledge of Italian a plus. Salary is competitive with full benefits. For consideration, please send letter of application, full curriculum vitae and three current letters of recommendation (written within the last year) addressing teaching and coordinating abilities to Dr. Robert Joe Cutter, Department of Languages and Literatures, Arizona State University, Tempe, AZ 85287-0202. Review of applications to begin December 15, 2006, if not filled bi-monthly there after until search is closed. Electronic and fax applications cannot be considered. Arizona State University is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer. Background check is required for employment.
