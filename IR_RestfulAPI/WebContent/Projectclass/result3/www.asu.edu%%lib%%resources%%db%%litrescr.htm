Literature Resource Center

LRC 		  

Database Information

Description:
Includes biographies, bibliographies & critical analysis of authors from every age & literary discipline. Covers novelists, poets, essayists, journalists & other writers. Includes full text of journals on literature and literary criticism.
Updated:
Irregularly
Includes:
full text
Subject Coverage:
Biography; Drama; Electronic Journals; English Literature; Humanities; Languages; Literature; Literary Criticism; Literature in Spanish & Portuguese; Medieval Studies; Renaissance Studies
Types of Materials Covered:
journal articles on literature and literary criticism; reference works; covers more than 90,000 novelists, poets, essayists, journalists and other writers, with in-depth coverage of 2,000 of the most-studied authors
Content Provider:
The Gale Group
Software Requirements:
Web browser
Tips:
Literature Resource Center includes the Encyclopedia of Literature and Gale's Literary Index. Gale's Literary Index is a master index to the major literature series published by The Gale Group. It combines and cross references more than 130,000 author names, including pseudonyms and variant names, and more than 140,000 titles into one source. Gale's Literary Index provides quick and easy access to 40 of Gale's literary series. These series contain complete biographies on authors and critical essays on their writings. You can use Gale's Literary Index to locate authors and titles covered in any of the following series:
