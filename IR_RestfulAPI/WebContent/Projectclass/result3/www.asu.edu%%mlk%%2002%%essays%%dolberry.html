2002 Essay/Poetry Contest 		Cailee Dolberry, Grade 2
Teacher: Ms. Connie Spencer
Franklin Northeast School
Mesa Unified District

Second Place
Primary (Grades K-2)


     My dad helps other people by making artificial limbs. An artificial limb is a brace made of plaster. Say somebody lost their leg or arm; he makes them an artificial limb. I have a cousin who needed an artificial brace. My dad also makes plastic braces for many other people.

     My dad's name is Allen Lord Dolberry. He helps my mom by washing the dishes and helps the whole family by protecting me. He's wonderful.
