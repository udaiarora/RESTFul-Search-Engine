Finder Tool Tips

When the name field isn't extended far enough to reveal long file names in Finder's column view, you can rely on the yellow highlighted "tool tip" text to let you know the full name of a file or application. Hovering your mouse pointer over the text will ultimately reveal the name, although there is sometimes a lag in waiting for Finder to "wake up" and show you. To speed up the process of revealing the file name, hold down the Option key as you hover, which immediately pops up the full text of the name. Alternatively, if you never want to look at the "tool tips," just hold down Command as you are pointing at files in the Finder; the tips will be suppressed.
