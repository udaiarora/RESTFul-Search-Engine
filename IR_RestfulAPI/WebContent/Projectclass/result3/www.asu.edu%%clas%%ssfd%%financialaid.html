Scholarships and Fellowships

Through the generosity of special contributors, the School of Social and Family Dynamics is able to provide our students with funding. Students can apply for these funds each semester by picking up the appropriate paperwork from the department secretary (Social Science 144) or by downloading the application online (Word file).

Students majoring in Family/Human Development or Sociology are encouraged to apply for these scholarships:
.

  • Janet and Brad Wilde Family Undergraduate Research Scholarship (Due November 15) - Each scholarship recipient shall identify a research project that involves alcoholism and drugs and their effects on the family as well as solutions, with the goal of discovering productive and healthy solutions that will result in positive outcomes, thus sustaining families and positioning them for success. Download details on the Janet and Brad Wilde Research Family Undergraduate Scholarship (Word file).
    .
  • Anna and Don Kirkman Family Scholarship (Due February 15)
    Eligibility Guidelines:
    (1) Junior/Senior Undergraduate Majors in Family Studies/Child Development or Sociology
    (2) Outstanding academic record, minimum 3.0 GPA
    (3) Demonstrated interest in studying families.
    .
  • The Cowden Fellowship (Due February 15)
    Eligibility Guidelines:
    (1) Undergraduate Majors in Family Studies/Child Development or Sociology
    (2) Completed at least 24 semester credit hours at ASU by the end of the Spring semester of the year applying.
    (3) Outstanding academic record, minimum 3.0 GPA
    .
  • The Fitch-Craig Scholarship (Due February 15)
    Eligibility Guidelines:
    (1) Junior/Senior Majors in Family Studies/Child Development or Sociology
    (2) Arizona Resident
    (3) Good academic standing
    (4) Demonstrates financial need
    .
  • The Monts Scholarship (Due February 15)
    Eligibility Guidelines:
    (1) Undergraduate Majors in Family Studies/Child Development or Sociology
    (2) Outstanding academic record, minimum 3.0 GPA
    (3) Evidence of involvement and leadership on campus and in the community
    (4) Demonstrates financial need
    .
  • Dean’s Circle Scholarship (Due February 15) - The award is $1000
    (1) Undergraduate students at any level, majoring in Family Studies/Child Development or Sociology
    (2) Enrolled full time (at least 12 hours) and maintain full time enrollment
    (3) Outstanding academic record, minimum 3.5 GPA
    Download details and application form for the Dean's Circle Scholarship (Word file).

Download the SSFD Scholarship Application (Word file)

Students are encourage to apply for those scholarships that are open to degree-seeking undergraduates in the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences.
