General Studies Advising Guides (GSAG)

for Arizona Public Community Colleges


These guides show how Arizona Public Community College coursework applies to General Studies course requirements at Arizona State University (ASU). General Studies value of a course is "year specific", meaning that in order to see if coursework will apply for General Studies, you must review the General Studies guide for the year in which you completed the course at the community college.

General Studies Advising Guides are available for the following Arizona Public Community Colleges:

  • Arizona Western College
  • Central Arizona College
  • Cochise College
  • Coconino Community College
  • Diné College
  • Eastern Arizona College
  • Maricopa Community Colleges
  • Mohave Community College
  • Northland Pioneer College
  • Pima Community College
  • Yavapai Community College
