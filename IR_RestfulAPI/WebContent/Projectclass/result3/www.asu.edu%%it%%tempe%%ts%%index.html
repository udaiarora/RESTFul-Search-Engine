Telephone Services is responsible for maintaining the telephone system and all telecommunications services as they relate to the phone systems on the four ASU campuses. Our scope of services ranges from installation of telephone sets to development and design of complex call handling solutions, such as interactive voice response and call center applications.

Jun 22, 2006
Attention all AT&T Long Distance Calling Card Holders:
Effective immediately ASU will be transitioning all long distance services over from AT&T to Qwest. As of September 1, 2006, all AT&T Long Distance Calling Cards will be disconnected. Read More...

News Update: The ASU voice mail system upgrade previously scheduled for Fall of 2005 has been postponed until early 2006 when the next release of software will be available. Read more details about this upgrade on the Voice Mail Transition page.
