HOW TO PURCHASE A DECAL

Decals may be purchased through online services (with the exception of Residence Hall decals) or in person at Parking and Transit Services, Decal Sales (Room 105, The Towers, 525 S. Forest Ave.), between the hours of 7:30 AM and 4:30 PM (MST) weekdays.

ASU Tempe resident hall students will receive an ASU residential parking permit application and instructions during the month of June. The applications should be completed, payment attached and returned to the ASU Decal Sales office as soon as possible as orders are processed on a first come, first served basis. Decals will then be distributed during the official move-in process. Photo ID and check-in sheet or resident hall validation stamp on your suncar are required to pick up your decal.

Students that are assigned or move-in to a ASU Resident Hall after the summer mailing can only purchase a parking decal in person at the Decal Sales office. You must present a residence hall check-in sheet, lease agreement or resident hall validation stamp on your ASU ID (Sun Card) to purchase a decal.

If you have any questions please call the Decal Sales office at (480) 965-6124.

Only ASU Resident Life students may purchase and display a resident permit.

Please have all of the following with you at time of purchase:

  • Correct License Plate Information (If temporary plate for new vehicle, please have VIN #)
  • Completed Parking Permit Application
  • Photo Identification
  • Acceptable Form of Payment (Cash, Check, MasterCard, Visa, or if faculty/staff, may pay by payroll deduction)
  • Proxy along with a photo ID for both parties - if making a transaction for someone else.
