THE UNIVERSITY OF CHICAGO

Department of Political Science

Political Science 509 Winter 1996

Seminar on Comparative Case Study Method

Stephen Walt


 



Course Description: This course examines the case study method for doing social science. The strengths and weaknesses of this approach will be considered. Emphasis will also be placed on the mechanics of doing case studies.

Course Requirements: It is expected that every student will do all the reading and attend all the class meetings. Each student will be expected to participate in the seminar discussion. The grade will be based on classroom participation (25%), and three five-page papers (25% each).

The papers will be due at the start of class on February 21, February 28, and March 6, and will deal with the specific book assigned for those class meetings. Each paper will be double-spaced, have at least 1-inch margins on all four sides, and have a standard-sized font.
