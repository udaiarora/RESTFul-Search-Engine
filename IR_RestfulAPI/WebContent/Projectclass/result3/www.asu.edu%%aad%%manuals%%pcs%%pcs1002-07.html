PCS 1002–07: Temporary Warehouse Storage

Purpose

To keep track of capital equipment being stored or removed from storage

Sources

Property Control
Surplus Property and Recycling

Policy

Capital equipment or other property not currently being used may be placed in warehouse storage at Surplus Property and Recycling. Effective January 1, 2005 storage fees will be charged quarterly and departments are required to provide an agency/org on the Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form at the time property is delivered for storage.

Procedures

Moving Equipment to Temporary Warehouse Storage D

Responsibility
Action
Department with equipment to be stored
  1. Complete a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form (see PCS 501A), checking the Temporary Warehouse Storage box in the “Activity” section. If an ASU property control number (ASU PC#) exists, record the number.

    Note:Forms are available in Property Control.

  2. Provide an agency/org in the “codes” area. One PRA form is required for each agency/org code.

  3. Send the signed PRA to Surplus Property and Recycling for verification signatures and retain the originating department copy.
Surplus Property and Recycling
  1. Sign the PRA, verifying equipment storage.

  2. Verify that an agency/org code is printed on the form for quarterly billing purposes.

    Note:Departments will be billed the first month of each quarter by the 15th for all property in storage on the first day of the current quarter. Billing months will be July, October, January, and April. Storage fees are assessed in advance; the current fee is $.80 per sq. ft. These rates are subject to change with the approval of the university Budget Office. Departments will be notified by memorandum of charges to be billed prior to the billing action.

Removing Equipment from Temporary Warehouse Storage

To remove equipment from storage, use the procedure in PCS 501, “Relocating Equipment within a Department.”
