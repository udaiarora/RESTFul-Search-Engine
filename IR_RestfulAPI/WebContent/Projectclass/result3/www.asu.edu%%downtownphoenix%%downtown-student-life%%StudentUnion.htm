The ASU-Wells Fargo

Student Center
at the

Arizona Center
is opening soon!

The ASU Wells Fargo Student Center will be opening very soon.  It is a space available to all ASU students to relax, have fun, take a break and get involved.  The center is equipped with computer games, board games, pool-hall type games, TVs, and lots of information – such as off-campus housing, jobs/internships/volunteer opportunities, how to get involved and local/ASU events.  It is also the home to the Student Government Advisory Board and Student Organizations – where student leaders can utilize resources, reserve a meeting room, and get advising.  Don’t miss these signature events:

  • WOW – What’s On Wednesdays – at 7pm every Wednesday expect a very cool event.

  • TNK – Thursday Night Karaoke – every Thursday Night – you get to showcase your singing talent – and 1/month (about every 3rd Thursday) you get to showcase ANY talent at OPEN MIC – spoken word poetry, dance, step, instruments – anything goes.  Open Mic is hosted by Divine Essence who schedules a local or national spoken word artist each time as well.

  • Game Nights – every Friday – come check it out!  From Board Games to computer games, it’s never boring!

  • Movie Nights – Most Saturdays!

About the Arizona Center

Go to any major city's downtown area and you'll find one spot that seems to give the whole place its power, its electricity. In downtown Phoenix, that place is Arizona Center.  Here you’ll find great dining, unique shopping at a variety of specialty retailers and above all, natural beauty to enjoy.  Check out the Grotto at Arizona Center three acres of quaint meandering gardens, illuminated trees and waterfalls.

Located in both the heart of Copper Square and the ASU Downtown Phoenix campus, the Arizona Center is an open-air oasis of dining, shopping, ponds and gardens in a unique urban setting, and is now the home of ASU's new Student Union for the Downtown Phoenix campus.  For more information and vendors, visit the Arizona Center.  -
