ASU Transfer Events and Visit Options

Picture yourself graduating from ASU with your 4-year degree. Begin by exploring ASU at one of these programs especially designed to serve transfer students.

Events Calendar

 

Transfer Thursdays
Students who plan to transfer to ASU are invited to to visit ASU's Tempe campus for Transfer Thursdays! Take advantage of various campus services including, admission and advising presentations, meetings with academic advisors, and a campus tour. For more information, visit the Transfer Thursdays Web site.

 

More Campus Visit Options

You are welcome to explore ASU through our campus visit options. However, if you have specific questions related to transferring to ASU, the transfer events and visits may be the most beneficial.

TOP

 

ASU Days - Events at the Community Colleges
These events are for all students planning to transfer to programs at any of the ASU campuses. ASU Days, typically held at Maricopa Community College sites in the Phoenix area, allow you to meet representatives from ASU's academic programs and student support services.
