Home > Events > Empty Space Theater > Directions

The Empty Space Theater at Arizona State University Main is a black box theatre for Performance Studies projects from students and faculty in the Hugh Downs School of Human Communication.  It houses a fully equipped, state-of-the-art sound, light and projection system, a large, dynamic performance space and handicapped-accessible seating facilities for upwards of 75 people.

Our new location is in the ASU Performing and Media Arts building at 970 E. University, NE corner of University and Rural.   For more information, call (480) 965-5095.
