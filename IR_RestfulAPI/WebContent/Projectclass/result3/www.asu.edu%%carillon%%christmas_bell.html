ORDER A Christmas Bell

Designer Barbara Atkinson has completed the limited-edition sterling silver Christmas bells to benefit the ASU Carillon Society.

The Christmas tree bell, which includes a chain, sells for $55. The Snowman, Which also comes with a chain, is $60.

To purchase a bell, call Judith Smith at 965-4821, or e-mail jps@asu.edu.

The Arizona State Savings and Credit Union, ASU office, is raffling a Snowman bell. Tickets are available at the Credit Union.
