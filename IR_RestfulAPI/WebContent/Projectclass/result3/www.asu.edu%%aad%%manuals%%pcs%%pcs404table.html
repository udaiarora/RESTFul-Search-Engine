Fleet Service:

  1. receives the vehicle and verifies that it is as specified and in good condition

  2. notifies Purchasing and Business Services and Payables and Reimbursements immediately if a discrepancy exists between the order and the vehicle received

    and

  3. forwards the invoice to the department.

The department enters a receiver online for the vehicle that was received. If the department does not have online capability, a memo may be sent to Payables and Reimbursements. The memo should state the purchase order number, line number, quantity received, and whether the shipment is full or partial.

Note: Departments must input an RC or notify Payables and Reimbursements within two working days of receipt of a vehicle.

Property Control tags the vehicle and adds it to the property control system. If the vehicle is not tagged and identified within two weeks of the department performing an RC (receiver document), Fleet Service contacts Property Control.

Fleet Service applies for title, registration, and plates with the Department of Motor Vehicles and marks the vehicle with all necessary ASU logos.

When title, registration, and license plates are received, Fleet Service:

  1. applies license plates to the vehicle

  2. places a photocopy of the registration in the vehicle

  3. files the original registration in the Fleet Service office

  4. forwards the original title to Surplus Property and Recycling

    and

  5. notifies the department that the vehicle is ready for service.
