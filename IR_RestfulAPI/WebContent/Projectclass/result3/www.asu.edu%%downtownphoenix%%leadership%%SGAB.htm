Student Government Advisory Board (SGAB)

on the ASU Downtown Phoenix Campus!

 

Join us for our general meetings every other Monday from 7pm to 9pm, in the Plaza Room, at the Residential Commons starting

January 22, 2007!

 

Our primary goals this semester include:

  1. Establish the Downtown Phoenix student government constitution

  2. Engage students in the development of the SGAB

  3. Inform the student body of SGABs existence.
