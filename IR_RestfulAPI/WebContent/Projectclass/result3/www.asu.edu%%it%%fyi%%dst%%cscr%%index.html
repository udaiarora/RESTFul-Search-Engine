DST provided services are instrumental in the configuration and on-going operation of the IT Sites and Classrooms. From the installation of the operating systems and select applications on various Macintosh and Windows workstations to the configuration and testing of a large collection of applications on server farms, DST is both an architect and implementer. In order to insure the site and classroom environments meet the needs of the user community, DST staff maintains a continuing dialogue with students, faculty, and researchers to identify and make available an up to date and diverse collection of applications utilized in research activities and academic related studies
