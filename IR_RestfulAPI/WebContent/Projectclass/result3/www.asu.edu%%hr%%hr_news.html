President’s Awards Application Forms Available on Web

 

Application forms for the President’s Award for Innovation and the President’s Medal for Social Embeddedness are now available.  These awards recognize extraordinary employee contributions in the areas of innovation, collaboration among departments and disciplines, use-inspired outcomes, social embeddedness, and community partnerships.  The submission deadline is January 18, 2007.  Descriptions of the awards, criteria, and downloadable applications can be found on the web at http://www.asu.edu/hr/programs/erp/employee_rec_programs.html. 

 

Benefits-Eligible New Employee Orientation

ASU’s New Employee Orientation has been expanded to a full day to offer new benefits-eligible faculty and staff more essential services and more in-depth information about ASU.  Weekly sessions are held on Mondays (except holidays) in USB 1302 from 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM, with a lunch break around noon.  To register a newly-hired benefits-eligible employee for New Employee Orientation, please call (480) 965-4751 or e-mail lps@asu.edu.

 

For more information about Benefits-Eligible New Employee Orientation, visit http://www.asu.edu/hr/new_employee.html or http://www.asu.edu/hr/new_employee/benee_orientation.html or contact the Learning and Performance Solutions Group at 965-4751 or lps@asu.edu.

ASU-FULTON HOMES Promotes Home Ownership

ASU and Fulton Homes are teaming to make new home ownership more affordable for ASU Students, Faculty and Staff.  Philanthropist Ira A. Fulton has come up with an extra way in which ASU can receive even more support from Fulton Homes. Fulton Homes is donating an additional 1% back to the University for each home purchased.  Full time Faculty, Full time staff, and Full time students at Arizona State University receive 3% off base price without using a Realtor.  In addition, 1% of the base price of the home will be donated to ASU and where the donation is directed, is up to the buyer.  For example, if a faculty member in the English department purchases a new Fulton home, he or she can direct that additional 1% to the English department, and also receive 3% off the base price of the new home.  Verification is needed in order to secure the discount.  Staff and faculty need to submit a copy of their last pay stub.  Students will need to print out their current class schedule out of MyASU.com.  This offer is also valid to graduates of ASU for a period of four years after a full time student graduates.  This will be verified through the ASU Alumni office.  These offers cannot be combined with any other offers. 

“This is another manner in which Fulton Homes and ASU students, faculty and staff  can team up and help shape the future of this great learning institution. ” reports Ira A. Fulton, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Fulton Homes.    Fulton Homes is the largest family owned home building business and is based in Tempe.  Ira A. Fulton has invested $160 million in ASU, and is the schools largest Philanthropist.   For more information on Fulton Homes go to www.fultonhomes.com.
