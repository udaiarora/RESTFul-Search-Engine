Barrett Political Science Scholarship

Application Form

2006-2007


 
 

Thanks to the inspiration and generosity of Barbara and Craig Barrett, the Department of Political Science at Arizona State University is able annually to name a Barrett Political Science Scholar -- a designation that brings not only special distinction but also an award of $2,800.

To be considered in the competition for the award, you must (a) be an undergraduate major in political science, (b) have a GPA of at least 2.5 both overall and in Political Science, (c) have achieved at least 60 credit hours toward graduation, and (d) demonstrate financial need. In order to demonstrate financial need, you are asked to submit along with this form a copy of your Statement of Need and a copy of your Financial Aid Notification. If these are not available to you, you may alternatively complete and submit a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) form (available from a Political Science secretary or from the Student Financial Aid Office).

In filling out this application form, please be as thorough in your replies as possible. Typed responses are encouraged. You may provide your responses on a separate sheet, but please take care to proceed in the sequence shown below.
