Chief Information Officer Advisory Committee

[ CIO > Committees > CIOAC >

Purpose

To assist and advise the Provost on information technology–related issues within the University.

(The former Communications Advisory Committee and its UNCEL working group are a subcommittee of the CIOAC).

Reporting Structure

Advisory to the University CIO and Vice Provost for Information Technology.

Membership

  • University Chief Information Officer, Chair
  • Representative of the Vice President for Research and Economic Affairs
  • Lead information technologist from each of:
    • Barrett Honors College
    • College of Architecture and Environmental Design
    • W. P. Carey School of Business
    • College of Education
    • College of Engineering and Applied Sciences
    • Graduate College
    • Katherine K. Herberger College of Fine Arts
    • College of Law
    • College of Liberal Arts and Sciences
    • College of Nursing
    • College of Public Programs
    • University Libraries
  • Representative from each of:
    • ASU East
    • ASU West
    • Extended Campus
  • Chair, Communications Advisory Committee
  • Senior technical leadership from the central University Information Technology unit, representing each of:
    • Adminstrative Information Systems
    • Enterprise Infrastructure Design and Support
    • Digital Media and Instructional Technologies
    • Office of Classroom Management
