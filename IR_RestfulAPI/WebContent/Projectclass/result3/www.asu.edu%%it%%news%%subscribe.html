IT News Subscription Groups


IT News Updates

The IT News Updates announcement list keeps you informed of the latest changes to services and facilities for Information Technology. Announcements are sent as the changes occur. For a monthly summary of IT News and events, subscribe to IT News Magazine announcement group.

IT News Magazine - Inside IT

The Inside IT announcement list keeps you informed of new releases to Inside IT, a monthly on-line magazine published by Information Technology Information Dissemination Team. Announcements are sent on a monthly basis, and include a summary of all articles and announcements published that month. To receive updates and changes to IT services and facilities as they occur, subscribe to IT News Updates announcement group.

How to Subscribe

To subscribe to either of these lists, please go to Announcement Groups Web site and do the following, based on your affiliation with ASU:

INSTRUCTIONS FOR ASU SUBSCRIBERS:

  1. Click on the URL below and select the menu item "Login" under "ASU Affiliated". This will take you to the WebAuth login screen.
  2. Enter your ASURITE UserID and password and click "login".
  3. After you have logged in, you will be taken back to the Announcement Groups Web page. Click on the menu item "Modify my Subscriptions".
  4. From the Subscriptions page you can subscribe to any or all of the existing Announcement Groups by clicking the Subscribe box.

URL - http://announce.asu.edu/


INSTRUCTIONS FOR NON-ASU SUBSCRIBERS:

  1. Click on the URL below and select the menu item "Create Account".
  2. Enter your name, and choose a User ID and password, then enter your e-mail address.

The system will acknowledge the creation of your account by responding with an e-mail confirmation that must be accessed within 24 hours. You may then subscribe to Inside IT or other announcement groups available to non-ASU affiliates.

URL - http://announce.asu.edu/
