INQUIRY ACCESS
Inquiry access will be open to all university employees with a need for HRMS information to do their jobs. This will allow those employees with HRMS access to view all job assignments and other employee information of any ASU employee regardless of what department(s) they currently or have previously worked in.

UPDATE ACCESS
Update access will be restricted to PAF and PDF information by home department.

TERMINATION OR TRANSFER OF EMPLOYEES WITH UDPATE ACCESS
With decentralization of PAF and PDF activity, it becomes critical to manage update security in a timely manner. To provide for effective security management, when an employee no longer required update access, a REQUEST FOR ACCESS from should be submitted immediately to HR systems at mail code 4903, so access can be deleted. If the employee is terminating employment at the university, all access will be deleted. If the employee is transferring from one department to another only update access will be deleted unless inquiry access is requested as well.

EMPLOYEES WILL NOT BE ABLE TO UPDATE THEIR OWN RECORDS
Human Resources will be available to assist departments in processing PAF and PDF changes when an employee must update their own record.

TURNAROUND PAF'S WILL INCLUDE USERID OF THE PERSON UPDATING THE RECORD
