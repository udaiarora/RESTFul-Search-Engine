January 2007

Student Spotlight


What is your name?

Brian Mychal Fleming

Residence hall?

Manzanita Hall

Position on campus?

Manzanita Hall Council President

Major?

Journalism Mass Communications/ Theatre

Hometown?

Los Angeles , California/ Kingman, Arizona

What made you decide to attend ASU?

They have exceptional rates for tuition and exceptional programs as well.

What has been your favorite class?

So far my favorite class has definitely been Women's Studies 300.

What has been your most challenging class?

CSE 180 (Computer Literacy)

What is your favorite place on the ASU campus?

I would have to say the fountain by the FAC building.

Where do you see yourself in five years?

Definitely working somewhere in the media field. My dream is to work for either MTV News or a Public Relations Company.

If you won $1 million dollars, what would you do with it?

I would definitely pay off my tuition and with the rest I would save until I was old enough to finance a house and car.

What is one thing most people don’t know about you?

That I am in love with J-Pop music its short for Japanese Pop music and I love to sing in Japanese, but unfortunately I can't speak it.
