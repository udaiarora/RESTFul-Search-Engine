Maricopa County Pre-Law Workshop for College Freshman, Sophomores, and Juniors

The Sandra Day O’Connor College of Law at Arizona State University will conduct a pre-law workshop October 25, 2006. Sponsored by a grant from the Law School Admissions Council, this workshop is open to college-level freshman and sophomores in Maricopa County (juniors may register after October 15, space permitting). Please go to www.law.asu.edu/prelaw to register for the program. The conference is free but limited to the first 300 participants who register at the website.  Because the educational program in most law schools is enhanced for all students when a variety of perspectives and experiences are brought to bear on legal issues, we hope to attract a diverse group of registrants to this workshop.

Location: The Sandra Day O’Connor College of Law at Arizona State University in Tempe, AZ, with free parking in PS-4, entrance on west side of Rural Rd., just north of Lemon; or on Lemon, just west of Rural Rd.  Decals not needed that day.  Carpooling is recommended.

Date: October 25, 2006

Goals: To help college pre-law students prepare academically for law school, prepare to take the LSAT, and apply effectively to law schools, and to help undecided college students determine whether law school is an attractive option.

Costs: This workshop is free to online registrants and will include a free lunch, all sponsored by the Law School Admissions Council.

Open to: All freshman and sophomores in colleges, universities, or community colleges in Maricopa County, but registration is limited to the first 300 who register online.  Subject to space available, high school seniors and college juniors, including students outside of Maricopa County, may attempt to register after October 15.

Online Registration: Go to the ASU main prelaw website at www.law.asu.edu/prelaw   From there, click on the line “Register for the Maricopa County Prelaw Workshop,” just below the main title on the page.  Then, fill in the blanks in the boxes at that site.  This is the only means for registering.  If you register and then must cancel, go back to the registration form and fill in the final box to notify us so that we can give your seat to another student.
