Available to Graduate Assistants, Faculty, and Staff

Service Description
ASU Polytechnic Campus Services (ASU Polytechnic Campus ASURITE Windows Domain)
This service is available via self-subscription to faculty and staff on the Polytechnic Campus.
Others who need access should contact the ASU Polytechnic IT Helpline at 480-727-1118.
ASU Tempe Campus Exchange (Outlook E-mail) (ASU Tempe Campus ASURITE Windows Domain and ASU Tempe Campus Exchange)
E-mail and calendar service on the Tempe Campus MS Exchange Server (exchange.asu.edu)
This service is available via self-subscription to faculty and staff.
Others who need access should have their supervisor or faculty/staff sponsor contact the IT Help Desk or submit a Request for Computer Access form. Include an expiration date of no longer than one year.
ASU MBA Student Exchange (Outlook E-mail) (ASU Tempe Campus ASURITE Windows Domain and ASU MBA Exchange)
E-mail and calendar service on the MBA MS Exchange Server (mainexstu1.asu.edu)
This service is available via self-subscription to full-time and executive MBA students only.
ASU West Campus Services (ASU West Campus ASURITE Windows Domain and ASU West Campus Exchange)
E-mail and calendar service on the West Campus MS Exchange Server (westex1.west.asu.edu)
This service is available via self-subscription to faculty and staff on the West Campus.
Others who need access should contact the ASU West IT Helpline at 602-543-4357.
ASURITE Windows Domain (faculty/employee) (ASU Tempe Campus ASURITE Domain)
Provides a login for computers on ASU's ASURITE Windows Domain (usually office computers) and all Exchange mailboxes except those on the West Campus server.
This service is available via self-subscription to faculty, staff and MBA students.
Others who need access should have their supervisor or faculty/staff sponsor contact the IT Help Desk or submit a Request for Computer Access form. Include an expiration date of no longer than one year.
Breeze Presentation Creation Environment Enables users to create narrated, synchronized Powerpoint presentations for web-based delivery. This service is available via self-subscription to faculty, staff, GAs and TAs. Others who need access should send email to breeze-q@asu.edu.
PPP Point-to-Point Protocol off-campus Internet access.
This service is available via self-subscription to faculty, staff, graduate assistants (TAs and RAs) and MBA students.
Others who need access should have their supervisor or faculty/staff sponsor contact the IT Help Desk or submit a Request for Computer Access form. Include an expiration date of no longer than one year.
