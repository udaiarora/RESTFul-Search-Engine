OPTIONAL RETIREMENT PLAN

  • A defined contribution plan.
  • Qualified under Internal Revenue Code 401(a).
  • Offers a choice of three retirement companies.
  • Participation in the retirement program begins on your first day of eligible employment.
  • Employees currently contribute 7% of gross salary (through payroll deduction) The University matches this contribution.
  • Employees select the investment company and determine the investment allocations.
  • University pays for long-term disability premiums.
  • Retirement benefit is based on performance of investments selected by the employee. Ultimate account balance is a result of the amount of contributions deposited to the ORP account and the performance of the chosen investments.
  • Employees are immediately vested in their own retirement contributions.
  • Employees are fully vested after five years of ORP participation, or immediately when an owner of a qualified retirement plan at time of employment.*

COMPLETE YOUR ORP ENROLLMENT NOW
NOTE: You MUST complete your Retirement Plan Enrollment Within 30 Days. Your retirement plan election at time of employment is irrevocable. This will be your retirement plan for the duration of your employment at the University.

ORP COMPANIES
Fidelity Investments Tax-Exempt Services Company
Founded in 1946, Fidelity is America’s largest privately held investment company. Fidelity offers a variety of investment options covering a range of risk and return characteristics to help participants build a retirement portfolio.

TIAA-CREF
TIAA-CREF has provided retirement options to people working in the academic, medical and cultural fields for more than 85 years. TIAA-CREF, with its non-profit heritage, has long subscribed to a different set of guiding principles directly influenced by the people it serves.

VALIC
 VALIC exclusively markets and services tax-deferred retirement plans to the employees of educational, healthcare, governmental, public sector and other organizations. VALIC has more than 45 years of experience specializing in tax deferred retirement programs.
