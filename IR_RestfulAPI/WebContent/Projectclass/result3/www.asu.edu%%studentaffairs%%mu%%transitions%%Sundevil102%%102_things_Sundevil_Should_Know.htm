More to Explore-102 Things Every Sun Devil Should Experience

1 Get your student ID, Sun Card.
2 Go to class.
3 Add maroon & gold to your wardrobe!
4 Visit the Sun Devil Involvement Center located on the 3rd floor of the Memorial Union and get involved on campus!
5 Become an ASU season ticket holder.
6 See a Broadway show at Gammage Auditorium.
7 Laugh at ASU's comedy troupes- Farce Side and Barren Mind Improv - who perform every Thursday and Friday on Union Stage, lower level of the Memorial Union.
8 Go Greek! Participate in fraternity/sorority recruitment.
9 Take advantage of the great discounts on ASU apparel at the ASU Bookstore.
10 Go tubing on the Salt River located in Mesa.
11 Go biking or running in Tempe Beach Park.
12 Visit the cool exhibits like the 7th Annual Dia de los Muertos Festival Exhibit at the Anthropology museum.
13 Visit the ASU Writing Center for help with a term paper.
14 Sit up front during your big lectures and get to know the professor.
15 Take the FLASH to and from class.
16 Get free tutoring at the Polytechnic campus at the Center for Academic Support.
17 Find ASU's Secret Garden.
18 Get your friends together to attend an Arizona Diamondbacks or Arizona Cardinals game.
19 Get free legal advice at the Student Legal Assistance office located on the 3rd level of the Memorial Union.
20 Attend the annual ASU Art Museum Film Festival in the spring.
21 Take a self-guided tour of Old Main.
22 Join an ASU Intramural team!
23 Read the student newspaper, The State Press, and know what's going on around campus and in the community.
24 Visit the ASU Planetarium.
25 Visit all the ASU's libraries.
26 Get a ride from Safety Escort Service.
27 Expand your appreciation for diversity - participate in Voices of Discovery sponsored by the Intergroup Relations Center.
28 Enjoy a smoothie from Surf City Squeeze located in the Student Recreation Complex.
29 Visit ALL of ASU's campuses -- Downtown Phoenix, Polytechnic, Tempe, and West.
30 Catch a movie at the Valley Art Theatre on Mill Avenue in Tempe.
31 Learn about Campus Safety resources by visiting the DPS website.
32 Learn the ASU Fight Song and Alma Mater.
33 Tailgate with friends before a home football game.
34 E-mail or call home at least once a week.
35 Take a leisurely stroll along Palm Walk.
36 Visit the student union at the Polytechnic campus.
37 Paint the "A" gold on A Mountain.
38 Enjoy fresh sushi at Southern Tsunami Sushi located in the Memorial Union.
39 Get involved in a club or student organization -- there are over 500 groups to choose from at ASU!
40 Go boating on Tempe Town Lake.
41 Invite your family to Family Weekend.
42 Hang out around the Memorial Union during lunch and check out the vendors on the mall or concert on Hayden Lawn.
43 Grab a cup of java with your professor at one of the Starbucks locations on the Tempe campus.
44 Go to the Tempe Arts Festival on Mill Avenue in the fall and spring.
45 Watch The Nutty Professor which was filmed at ASU.
46 Eat breakfast at Cereality in the Memorial Union.
47 Jump in the Student Recreation Complex pool to cool off.
48 Make regular appointments to meet with your academic advisor.
49 Enjoy a flavored tea at Bubble Teaze located in the Memorial Union.
50 Stay around after a Sun Devil victory at the stadium to hear the victory bell ring!
51 Visit Career Services to help you start on your resume and get a job or an internship.
52 Go to Islands restaurant on Mill Avenue for fries.
53 Go hiking at Camelback Mountain.
54 Apply for every scholarship you can -- you just might get one.
55 Find a mentor and be their friend, not just a student.
56 Attend as many Homecoming Festdevil events as you can -- you will be glad you did!
57 Find your own little study place.
58 Get free tutoring at the Learning Resource Center in the Memorial Union or Palo Verde West.
59 Serve others and participate in ASU's Community Service Program or Service Learning Program.
60 Enjoy a Jamba Juice, lower level of the Memorial Union.
61 Save your money and open a checking/savings account at Wells Fargo or Arizona State Savings & Credit Union -- both located in the Memorial Union.
62 Get a team together for the annual Oozeball tournament in the spring.
63 Visit all of the computer labs on campus.
64 Take a walking tour of the ASU Arboretum.
65 Take a walk down Mill Avenue
66 Donate blood -- Bleed maroon and gold!
67 Visit the Nelson Fine Arts Museum.
68 Buy a day planner -- schedule time for your classes and fun!
69 Participate in "A" Week during spring semester.
70 Participate in ASU Cares in March.
71 Play frisbee or hacky-sack on Hayden Lawn.
72 Find great CDs and DVDs at Hoodlums Music, lower level of the Memorial Union.
73 Hold late night study sessions at the IHOP on Apache Blvd.
74 Enjoy lunch at the Maricopa Café, 2nd level of the Memorial Union.
75 Form a study group.
76 Get ice cream at Cold Stone Creamery, located on the first floor of the Memorial Union.
77 Sell your books back after the semester to buy a loved one a gift!
78 Become a student worker.
79 Go to the Phoenix Zoo.
80 Take a trip to the Grand Canyon.
81 Go shopping at the many outlet malls in the Phoenix area.
82 Do an internship and get on-the-job experience.
83 Exercise! The Student Recreation Complex is a great place to work out.
84 Get to know your faculty by going to your professor's office hours.
85 Attend World Festival in the spring.
86 Check out all the student activities at the West campus.
87 Wear Sparky's horns to a game.
88 Meet your student government officers - Tempe campus, West campus and Polytechnic campus.
89 Watch the movie Jerry Maguire -- "I'm a Sun Devil, Jerry!"
90 Talk to the person next to you in class.
91 Hike to the top of "A" Mountain and watch a sunrise/sunset.
92 Check out an event at the Gammage Auditorium.
93 Visit Parking and Transit Services to get a free Valley Metro bus pass.
94 Attend a Freshman Year Experience workshop.
95 Be an active part of the ASU community by volunteering with ASU's Community Service Program.
96 Play billiards, video games, or bowl in Sparky's Den, lower level of the Memorial Union.
97 Attend commencement.
98 Get free pancakes at "MU Late Night: Feed Your Brain for Finals" the evening of Reading Day in the Memorial Union.
99 Attend a spring training baseball game during March and April.
100 Go to class!
101 Check out the ASU Visitor Information Center and learn about ASU and the surrounding communities.
102 Love being an ASU Sun Devil!
