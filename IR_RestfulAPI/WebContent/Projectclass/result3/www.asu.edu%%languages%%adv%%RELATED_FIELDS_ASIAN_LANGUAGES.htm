ASIAN LANGUAGES MAJOR (CHINESE & JAPANESE):  POSSIBLE RELATED FIELDS  [15 credits]

 I.  JAPANESE

1. Chinese or Korean Language or Literature, must have minimum 6 credit hours:

CHI - all with prefix (except CHI 194 Intro to East Asian Culture), such as CHI 101 First-Year Chinese I and CHI 205 Chinese Caligraphy; or CHI 321 Chinese Literature I, or CHI 322 Chinese Literature II, or FLA 420 Chinese Literature in Translation

Or

KOR - all with prefix (except KOR 194 Intro to East Asian Culture), such as KOR 101 First-Year Korean I and KOR 102 First-Year Korean II, or KOR 250 Korean Culture and Society, or KOR 347 Korean Film and Literature. or KOR 350 Women of Korea.

 2. Historical Overview Requirement - 3 credit hours from approved courses giving an historic overview of Japan.

·          ARS 201 (Art of Asia)

·          ARS 473 (Art of Japan)

·          HST 108 (Introduction to Japan)

·          HST 303 (Studies in Asian History, when focus of the course is Japan)

·          HST 387 (Japan)

·          HST 388 (Japan)

·          HST 389 (Japanese Society and Values: Pre-Modern)

·          REL 355 (Japanese Cities and Cultures to 1800)

·          REL 444 (Religion in Japan)

 II.  CHINESE

1. Japanese or Korean Language or Literature, must have minimum 6 credit hours:

JPN - all with prefix (except JPN 194 Intro to East Asian Culture), such as JPN 101 First-Year Japanese I and JPN 206 Japanese Caligraphy or JPN 102 First-Year Japanese II, or FLA 421 Japanese Literature in Translation

Or

KOR - all with prefix (except KOR 194 Intro to East Asian Culture), such as KOR 101 First-Year Korean I and KOR 102 First-Year Korean II, or KOR 250 Korean Culture and Society, or KOR 347 Korean Film and Literature. or KOR 350 Women of Korea.

 2. Historical Overview Requirement: 3 credit hours from approved courses giving an historic overview of China.

·          ARS 201 (Art of Asia),

·          ARS 472 (Art of China)

·          ARS 475 (Chinese Painting)

·          HST 383 (China)

·          HST 384 (China)

·          HST 451 (Chinese Cultural History)

·          HST 452 (Chinese Cultural History)
