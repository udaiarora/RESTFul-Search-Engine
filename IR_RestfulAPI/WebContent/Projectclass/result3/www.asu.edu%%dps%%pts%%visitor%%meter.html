METERED PARKING

Visitors to the ASU Tempe Campus needing parking for 1 hour or less can use one of our metered parking areas. There are fourteen metered parking areas on campus, and the rate is $1.50 per hour. All meters have a one hour maximum time limit (except lot 52 and disabled meter stalls which have a two hour time limit). Please keep in mind that these areas are intended for short-term visitor parking, and are available to people who are NOT ASU students, faculty, or staff members. ASU students, staff, and faculty members should purchase an annual ASU parking decal.

The meters in these lots accept nickels, dimes, and quarters. Pennies will be accepted by the meters, but NO time will be given. If the meter is damaged, jammed, or otherwise not working, please do not park there (you can be cited) and report the problem to ASU Parking and Transit Services at (480) 965-0641 as soon as possible. Meters are in effect Monday - Saturday 7 am - 10 pm unless otherwise posted.
