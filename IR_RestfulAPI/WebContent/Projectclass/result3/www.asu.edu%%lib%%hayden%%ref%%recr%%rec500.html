Community Resources & Development

Research Sources

 

Community resources and development encompasses a wide range of broad subject areas. There is business and non-profit business, recreation, public administration, travel, tourism, planning and development and the environment, to name a few of the topics that can be part of every research project in this field. The major difficulty for most is knowing where to look for information on a specific area of the field. Below is a list of resources available at ASU Libraries. Many of the resources are available electronically, but not everything.
