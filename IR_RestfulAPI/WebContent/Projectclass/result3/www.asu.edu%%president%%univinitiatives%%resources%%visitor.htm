Parking
Visitor parking is located in Parking Structure 1 at Lemon Street and Normal Avenue. Visitors with parking reservations should give their name to the parking attendant upon arrival and then bring their parking ticket to their meeting for validation. Visitors without parking reservations must pay the cashier upon exit. The rate is $2.00 per hour, or portion thereof, with an $8.00 maximum exit fee.


Visitor parking is also available in Lot 42 at Terrace Road and McAllister Ave. Lot 42 is a Pay by Space Visitor lot. There are no attendants at this lot and each visitor must follow the instructions located at the pay machine. The rate is $1.00 per hour, or portion thereof, for up to 12 hours ($12).

Metered parking is available for up to one hour along Forest Avenue and Gammage parkway for up to one hour ($1 per hour).
