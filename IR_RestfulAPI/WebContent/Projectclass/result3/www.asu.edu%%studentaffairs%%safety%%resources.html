Student Assistance Coordination Committee (SACC) – Tempe Campus
The SACC coordinates the delivery of appropriate resources to students with behavioral and/or mental health problems in order to reduce the occurrence, duration and intensity of crisis related trauma.

Critical Incident Response Guidelines for the Tempe Campus

The guidelines outlines critical incidents related to students to which faculty or staff may need to respond and list departments that provide public safety, university, psychological, and medical support and which may assist faculty and staff when responding to a specific incident involving a student.
