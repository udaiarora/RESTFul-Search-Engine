Parking

Please NOTE: Be sure to review this important information regarding traffic delays and road closures in Tempe.

Free parking is available at most areas on campus the day of commencement and convocations. Parking will be restricted in residence halls and locations where special permits are required. Regular fees will be collected at meters and visitor lots. Lot 58 will be open for disabled parking. A drop-off site for elderly guests will be located at the northeast corner of Wells Fargo Arena. For more parking information, please call Parking and Transit Services at (480) 965-6406. The doors to the arena open one hour prior to commencement. Please arrive as early as possible. Car pooling will save energy and help alleviate traffic congestion for this event. Be advised - College convocations will take place immediately following University Commencement. Multiple convocation ceremonies will take place on Friday following commencement. If you plan to enjoy campus or to visit downtown Tempe after Commencement or any convocation ceremony, please move your car to a location outside of Lot 59 (Wells Fargo Arena) or Lot 3 (Gammage Auditorium). This will allow other families and friends attending other events at these locations to find convenient parking.
