Retirement

Guest: Cynthia Jewett, Associate General Counsel

Cindy Jewett provided background information concerning retirement issues that have arisen in previous FCF meetings.

Employees who are benefits eligible may retire even if employed on year to year contracts. The requirements are different depending on whether the employee selected the Arizona State Retirement System (ASRS), or the Optional plans (Valic, TIAA-CREF, ING, Fidelity). In addition, at retirement they are eligible for reimbursement for accumulated sick hours.

Sources of retirement information include:

Academic Assembly Membership

Guests:

Barbara Kerr, President, Academic Senate

George Watson, Chair, Executive Committee

Presently Academic Assembly membership is limited to Tenure and Tenure Track faculty, Academic Professionals, Lecturers and Senior Lecturers on multiyear contacts. Instructors and clinical faculty are not eligible.

The FCF Advisory Committee is asked to draft a motion to the Academic Senate's University Affairs Committee concerning representation of currently-unrepresented full-time contract faculty to the Academic Assembly.

The subcommittee members include Christine Helfers, Martha Cocchiarella, and Mirna Lattouf . George Watson offered assistance as needed.
