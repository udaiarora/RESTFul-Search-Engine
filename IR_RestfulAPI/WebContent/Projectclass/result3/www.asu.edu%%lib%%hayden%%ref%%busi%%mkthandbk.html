Marketing Resources
HANDBOOKS & MANUALS
  1. Dutka, Alan. AMA Handbook for Customer Satisfaction. Lincolnwood, IL : NTC Business Books, 1994.
    HF 5415.3 .D88 1994 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Using the case study method, action plans, and lists of advantages and disadvantages, this work takes you step by step in analyzing the satisfaction levels of your customers. The appendix is a copy of the Malcom Baldrige National Quality Award Customer Satisfaction Components.

  2. Bly, Robert W. Advertising Manager's Handbook. Englewood Cliffs, NJ: Prentice-Hall, 1993.
    HF 5823 .B634 1993 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    This is a technical manual for those who have not done much in advertisement management, but are responsible for managing the company's advertising budget. A very practical, down to earth guide through the maze of annual planning.

  3. Advertising Manager's Handbook. 4th ed. Chicago: Dartnell, 1985.
    HF 5823 .S78 1982 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Direct Marketing Handbook. New York: McGraw-Hill, 1984.
    HF 5438.25 .D555 1984 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Marketing Manager's Handbook. 2nd ed. Chicago: Dartnell, 1983.
    HF 5415.13 .D35x HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Sales Manager's Handbook. Chicago; Dartnell, 1989.
    HF 5415 .D32 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Sales Promotion Handbook. 7th ed. Chicago: Dartnell, 1979.
    HF 5438.5 .D325x 1979 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Published by Dartnell, these comprehensive handbooks explain chapter-by-chapter all aspects of marketing and sales. Chapters range from the broad, such as the scope of marketing management, to the specific, such as telephone marketing. Practical applications and detailed discussions make these extremely useful guides. Each chapter ends with suggested sources for further reading.

  4. Boddewyn, J. J. Barriers to Trade and Investment in Advertising: Government Regulation and Industry Self-Regulation in 53 Countries. New York: International Advertising Association, 1989.
    HF 5834 .B64 1989 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Boddewyn, J. J. Global Perspectives on Advertising Self-Regulation. Westport CT: Quorum Books, 1992.
    HF 5834 .B65 1992 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Boddewyn, J. J. Premiums, Gifts, Competitions and Other Sales Promotions: Regulation and Self-Regulation in 42 Countries. New York: International Advertising Association, 1988.
    HF 6161 .P7B6x HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Boddewyn, J. J. Sexism and Decency in Advertising: Government Regulation and Industry Self-Regulation in 47 Countries. New York: International Advertising Association, 1989.
    HF 5827 .B5787x 1989 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    These works are very useful in checking the acceptability of advertising and how advertising is done in other countries. It is particularly helpful for those who have never experienced another culture and its regulations.

  5. Kremer, John. The Complete Direct Marketing Sourcebook: A Step-by-Step Guide to Organizing and Managing a Successful Direct Marketing Program. New York: Wiley, 1992.
    HF 5466 .F665 1992 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Just as the title suggests, this is a very thorough guide to Direct Marketing. A very useful supplement to a how to write a marketing plan, because of the level of detail included in this work.

  6. Do-It-Yourself Busines Promotions Kit. Englewood Cliffs, NJ: Prentice-Hall, 1995.
    HF 5823 .G72 1995 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Experienced marketers will not need this book. This is designed for the small business people trying to do their own advertising and marketing. This is a good handbook for the basics of marketing.

  7. Do's and Don'ts in Advertising Copy. New York: National Better Business Bureau, 1949- . Looseleaf. (ASU owns latest year only.)
    HF 5831 .N28 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    This handbook is a guide to what can cause trouble if included in advertisements. Guidelines and rules issued by the various government agencies are illustrated with the instances that triggered the ruling or as a key example. The work is divided into subject sections, but the best way to find the needed information is to use the index in the front of Vol. 1.

  8. Cohen, William A. The Entrepreneur and Small Business Marketing Problem Solver. New York: John Wiley, 1991.
    HF 5415.13 .C634 1991 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    This is really a handbook that many marketers would like to have on their desks. It provides and overview of all the key areas of marketing. Chapters cover market research, new products, pricing products and services, publicity, selling, trade shows, mail order marketing, selling to the government and marketing/business plans. Helpful charts and forms are found throughout the book. Also included are directories of Small Business Administration field offices, Better Business Bureaus, and trade associations. The last appendix is a sample marketing plan.

  9. Parmerlee, David. Evaluating Marketing Strengths and Weaknesses. Lincilnwood, IL: NTC Books, 1993.
    HF 5415.13 .P3246 1993 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Arranged in a workbook format, this American Marketing Association book guides you through the process of evaluating your company's marketing program. This work can also be used to analyze the programs and campaigns of the competition.

  10. Keup, Erwin J. Franchise Bible: A Comprehensive Guide. Grants Pass, OR: Oasis Press, 1990.
    HF 5429.23 .K48 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    This is the "everything you wanted to know" type of manual on both starting a new franchise and on buying an existing franchise.

  11. MacDonald, Jack and Curtis R. Holsopple. Handbook of Radio Publicity and Promotion. 3rd ed. Blue Ridge Summit, PA: TAB Books, 1991.
    HF 6146 .R3 M173 1991 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    A very detailed presentation of specific types of promotions and how to do them. This is the type of handbook that helps those just starting understand the terminology and know how to do a particular type of radio advertising.

  12. International Marketing Handbook. Detroit, MI: Gale Research, 1981- . Irregular. (ASU owns 1985- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
    HF 1009.5 .I538 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    Designed to be a handbook to developing import and export marketing, this has detailed profiles and lists of government agencies and trade associations for 141 of the countries of the world. This is less technical than a legal book, but very readable and detailed for the non-lawyer. Volume 3 also has some interesting overviews on such topics as financing overseas, area guides, metrics in international trade, and world commercial holidays.

  13. Smith, Jeanette. Publicity Kit: a Complete Guide for Entrepreneurs, Small Businesses, and Nonprofit Organizations. New York: Wiley, 1991.
    HD 59 .S52 1991 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    What a wonderful guide to doing your own publicity for those who have no training in this area! This small book leads you through all the media mazes for getting the word out about your business. It's small size and helpful examples make this a very useful manual.

  14. Researching Business Markets: The IMRA Handbook of Business-to-Business Marketing Research. London: Kogan Page, 1991.
    HF 5415.2 .M295 1991 HAYDEN REFERENCE

    This handbook covers basic marketing research with a focus on the specific problems of business-to-business markets.

  15. Sales Force Compensation. Chicago, Dartnell, 1982- . Biennial. (ASU owns 1986- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Stacks.)
    HF 5439.7 .C65x HAYDEN REFERENCE

    This is a survey of the salaries and compensation paid to the sales forces of companies. Whether intending to seek employment in the field or currently managing a sales force, this is an extremely important tool. The first sections of the book are the survey statistics arranged by compensation and then by industry. The second portion of the book is really a handbook on how to manage a sales force for peak performance.
