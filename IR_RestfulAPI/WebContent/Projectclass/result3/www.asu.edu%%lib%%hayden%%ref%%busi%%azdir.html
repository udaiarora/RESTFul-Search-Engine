COMPANY INFORMATION SOURCES


Arizona Directories



Check also Telephone and E-Mail Directories (http://www.asu.edu/lib/ref/gen/phbks.html) to find basic directory information on companies by name, location, or product/service.


  1. Arizona Business Directory. Omaha, NE: American Directory Publishing, [1988/89]- . Annual. (ASU owns 1989/90- . Latest year on Reserve, earlier years in Journals.)
      HF 5065 .A6 A75x HAYDEN RESERVE

    Basically a typical "telephone book" for all of Arizona business. The yellow pages provide broad subject/product access. Other lists are manufacturers listed by city and SIC, and businesses by city. This information can be searched online as a part of the Americian Business Disc by limiting the location to Arizona (see under "U.S. Company Directories").

  2. Arizona Directory of Exporters. Phoenix, AZ: Business and Trade Program, Arizona Department of Commerce, 1985- . Biennial. (ASU owns 1985/86- . All years in Document Services; latest year only in Reserve.)
      COM 4.8:I 58 HAYDEN AZ DOCUMENTS
      HF 3161 .A7 A6x HAYDEN RESERVE

    This is an alphabetical directory of Arizona companies currently exporting or interested in exporting their products. There is a cross-index by product or service and a listing of companies by city.

  3. Arizona High Tech Directory. Tempe, AZ: CS Communications, [1988]- . Annual. (ASU owns 1990- .)
      COM 4.8:H 43 HAYDEN AZ DOCUMENTS

    Company entries are listed alphabetically under separate sections for manufacturers, software, support services, and sales. Also includes indexes by company, city, and product/service.

  4. Arizona Hispanic Business Directory. Phoenix, AZ: Arizona Hispanic Chamber of Commerce, 1984- . Irregular. (ASU owns 1984- .)
      F809.7 .A75x HAYDEN CHICANO

    The directory lists companies by product or service. An alphabetical listing of members at the back repeats the company information but does not refer back to the classified section.

  5. Arizona Industrial Directory. Phoenix, AZ: Phoenix Metropolitan Chamber of Commerce, 1954- . Annual. (ASU owns 1956- . Latest year on Reserve; earlier years in Journals and Arizona Collection.)
      HD 9727 .A6 D5x HAYDEN RESERVE, HAYDEN ARIZONA, & SCIENCE RESERVE

    The manufacturing section of this directory has an SIC listing and a city listing. The wholesaling section is arranged similarly. A list of professionals and services is included. The directory is also available as a part of the Colt Microfiche Library of State Directories (see under "U.S. Manufacturer's Directories").

  6. Black Commerce Directory. Phoenix, AZ: Targeted Media Communications, 1986- . Annual. (ASU owns 1992/93and 1995/96. Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HF 5065 .A6 B5x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8B)

    This is a yellow-pages style directory to black-owned business mostly in the Phoenix area. The directory does not claim to cover all black-owned business but only the ones that have listed themselves with the directory.

  7. Corporate Arizona. Phoenix, AZ: Business Journal, 1989- . Annual. (ASU owns 1989- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4057 .A7 T35x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located at Reference Desk)

    Profiles publicly owned companies either headquartered in Arizona or having a significant presence in the state. Includes brief financial information. Previously published as two separate publications, Taking Stock and Taking Stock II.

  8. Dun's Regional Business Directory: Phoenix/Tucson. Parsippany, NJ: Dun & Bradstreet Information Services, 1989- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4058 .P545 D86 HAYDEN RESERVE

    This directory is typical of the ones by this publisher. It purports to list all the branch/factory/office locations of major companies in Phoenix, Tucson, and surrounding counties. The fullest information is given in the geographic listing with only cross-references given in the SIC section and the alphabetic listing.

  9. Incorporating Monthly Microform. Phoenix, AZ: Arizona Corporation Commission, [1989]- . Monthly. (ASU owns 1989- .)
      CC 1.3:I 52 FICHE HAYDEN AZ DOCUMENTS

    Provides a cumulative list of companies that have filed for incorporation with the Arizona Corporation Commission. The filing information required by the Commission includes the identity of officers and a short balance sheet listing assets and liabilities. This information is not shown on the microfiche but is available at the Commission offices for a nominal service fee. The Library's copy is received second hand with several months delay.

  10. Survey of Foreign-Owned Firms
      http://www.cob.asu.edu/ieic/ieic3.html

    Database of over 200 foreign-owned companies in Arizona which can be searched by industry, location, or country of foreign ownership. This data is the result of survey work done by the International Economic Information Center which is a joint project of ASU's College of Business and the Greater Phoenix Economic Council. Statistical findings derived from the survey are also available at this site.

  11. Top 25 Lists: Greater Phoenix Business Journal. Phoenix, AZ: Business Journal, 1986- . Annual. (ASU owns 1988- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HF 5068 .P55 B66x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located at the Reference Desk & on Table 8B)
      http://www.amcity.com/phoenix

    Previously called The Book of Lists, this is a very current compilation of business rankings. The top 25 or 50 companies of the major industries in Arizona are included. Since the information is voluntarily supplied, the private companies may have very little information beyond the address, telephone number, and key contact person. The web version shows only the top five companies in each category from the most recent issue of the rankings but may be useful if a more complete listing is not needed.
