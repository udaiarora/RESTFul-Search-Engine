Student Employment with Tempe Campus Support

Tempe Campus Support currently recruits student workers to fulfill a variety of roles on our team through a single applicant process. Roles include classroom support, sites support, call center staffing, and administrative support.

Duties of student workers in Tempe Campus Support involve a high degree of complexity and may include any combination of the following: assisting customers with computer and multimedia technology; providing support to high-level customers of Tempe Campus classroom and meeting facilities; demonstrating new and emerging technologies to customers; effectively communicating with diverse customers via telephone, in person, and in writing; delivery and setup of computer and multimedia equipment weighing up to 50 pounds.

Tempe Campus Support employees need to be good problem solvers who are genuinely interested in helping people. They are responsible for assisting all customers with their questions and/or problems concerning our computing facilities, classrooms, multimedia equipment, policies, and operations. Each customer must be assisted to the best of each employee's ability in a professional and courteous manner. Patience, tact, and the ability to handle pressure are important as well as being able to work independently and as part of a team.

Applicants must have a flexible schedule and be willing to work early morning, late evening, and/or weekends. Applicants should expect to work a fixed schedule for the duration of any given academic session. Work shifts are 4-6+ hours in length. The nature of our work requires us to staff various customer support centers throughout the day and we will likely not be able to offer you an ideal work schedule.

Only ASU students currently enrolled as half-time or greater will be considered. Tempe Campus Support employees work a minimum of 12 hours per week and the starting wage is $8.00 per hour. Applicants should also familiarize themselves with the following policies regarding student employment:

  • Student Employment Eligibility Criteria
  • Employment of Nonimmigrant International Students

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Previous customer service experience
  • Fluency in both spoken and written English
  • Effective verbal and written communication skills
  • Computer and Microsoft Office literacy
  • Ability to complete work under minimal supervision

Desired Qualifications:

  • Experience working in an academic environment
  • Previous knowledge of the Tempe Campus
  • Valid Arizona driver's license
  • Professional appearance and approach
  • Applicant should be a self-starter and highly motivated
  • Experience troubleshooting computer and multimedia technology

If you are interested in working with us, please submit a fully completed application using the on-line form below. Take care in completing this application as the information provided is considered in detail and taken as a sample of your communication skills. We receive a large number of applications and do not contact each applicant. You will be contacted via Email if your application is chosen for an interview.
