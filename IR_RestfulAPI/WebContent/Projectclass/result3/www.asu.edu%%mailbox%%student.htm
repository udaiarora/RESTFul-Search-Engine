Student E-Mail Accounts at ASU

All applicants of ASU are given an asu.edu e-mail address upon acceptance. Students must choose a location for mail sent to their ASU e-mail address to be delivered as both Instructors and Administration personnel will send e-mail correspondence often.

If you would like an ASU mailbox for this address to be delivered to, you can subscribe to it once you have registered and paid for your first semesters' classes. If you already have an e-mail account (such as aol, hotmail or yahoo) and would like to direct mail sent to your @asu.edu to this address, we can do that too. Regardless of your choice, students must designate a destination address for their asu.edu e-mail address.
