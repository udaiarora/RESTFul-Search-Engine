J A M E S P A T S I M M O N S


Patricia Marie Eisele was born in Phoenix, Arizona, May 24th, 1927. Clark Rorbach was born in Duluth, Minnesota, March 11th, 1926. They were married in Phoenix, Arizona in Trinity Episcopal Cathedral, 114 West Roosevelt, Phoenix, Arizona on April 15th, 1950. Patricia and Clark had four children: Elizabeth (Betsy) Rorbach, Sally Ann Rorbach, Clark Edward Rorbach and Katherine Rorbach. Patricia and Clark were divorced. James Pat Simmons was born November 9th, 1924. Patricia Rorbach and James Pat Simmons were married March 4th, 1973. Jim Simmons is head of the United Bank. Patricia and Jim live in Paradise Valley, Arizona, adjoining Phoenix.
