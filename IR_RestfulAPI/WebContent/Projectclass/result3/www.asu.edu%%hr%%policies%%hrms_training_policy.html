HRMS Decentralized Entry Training Policy

To receive permanent sign-on capability, successful completion of the following levels of training is required depending on the level of access requested:

HRMS On-line Inquiry - The user learns how to navigate in HRMS.

HRMS Employee Changes - Assumes familiarity On-line Inquiry. Training for updating pay information is required before access will be granted.

At the department's option, temporary sign-on capability will be made available for the new employee for up to four weeks with approval of a unit administrator. The ID will expire after four weeks unless the required training is completed. The employee must have authorization and a trained person to oversee their work. Attending HRMS Training sessions conducted may fulfill required training by HR Staff. Contact the HRMS Helpline @ 965-0467 for more information. HRMS Training will be made available for completion within a four-week period, based on demand. When demand is not sufficient to conduct a class, HR will assist departments by providing one-on-one training, which will allow them to receive permanent sign-on capability.
