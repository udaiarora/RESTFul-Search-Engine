List of Compiler Diagnostic Messages

You can generate a complete listing of compiler diagnostic messages, along with their explanations, by attempting to compile a program with the program-name of ERRMSG specified in the PROGRAM-ID paragraph.

The diagnostics are not listed or explained in any manual. Most messages are self-explanatory, but if you need help interpreting messages please contact consulting.
