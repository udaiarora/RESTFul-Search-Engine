Southern Arizona: Tucson, AZ

Hotel Information
Double Tree Hotel Tucson at Reid Park
445 S. Alvernon Way ,
Tucson ,  AZ 85711-4198

Phone: 520.881.4200
Fax: 520.323.5225 
dtreidpark.com

Check-In Information
The program runs from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Check-in begins at 9:00 a.m.

Make sure to review the Checklist to ensure you are thoroughly prepared for your ASU Orientation program.

Students must participate in the full Off-Campus Orientation program to complete the registration process.

Hotel Accommodations
If overnight accommodations are desired, please call the hotel for reservations. Accommodations are not included in the program fee.

Parking
Hotel parking is available for program participants at a reduced rate. Parking is not included in the program fee.

Map and Directions

From Phoenix - Take I-10 until you come to the 22nd/29th Street exit. Go left/East at the intersection. Remain on 22nd Street until you come to Alvernon Way . This is approximately 5 miles. Go left/North onto Alvernon Way . We are about 9 blocks down on the right/east side.

From Benson/Sierra Vista - Take I-10 to the Palo Verde North Exit. Take Palo Verde North, which turns into Alvernon Way , pass over the 22nd Street heading north and we are located about 9 blocks on the right/east side of the road.

From Nogales - Take I-19 till it joins I-10 west, the first exit is 22nd/29th street. Exit go right/east onto 22nd Street . Take 22nd Street until you reach Alvernon Way approximately 5 miles. Go north/left onto Alvernon Way . We are on the right/east side about 9 blocks.
