Lab Details

The Disability Resources for Students Lab is currently equipped with 16 IBM Pentium 4 Computers operating Windows NT/2000 and 2 Power Macintosh G3s with Operating System 8.5.

The Lab is open Monday through Friday, 8am - 5pm. It is staffed by a Lab-aide to answer any general questions about the software and hardware used in the Lab.

Map of the Lab can be downloaded (Right-Click Text or Braille and select SaveTargetAs) in Text or Braille versions.
