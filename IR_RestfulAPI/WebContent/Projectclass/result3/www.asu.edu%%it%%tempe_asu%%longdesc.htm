Downtown Tempe Public Wireless Service

Coverage Map

Long Description

Tempe Wireless Access is available north of ASU. The coverage area is
an L-shaped section approximately 5 blocks wide by 2 blocks long. The boundaries are:

North perimeter: Fifth Street
South perimeter: Seventh Avenue
West perimeter: Ash Avenue
East perimeter: College Avenue

There is some additional coverage in these areas:

North of Fifth Street and University Towers, extending to Rio Salado

South of Seventh Avenue on either side of Mill Avenue, extending south to University Drive, west to Harkins Theatre and east to Myrtle Avenue.
