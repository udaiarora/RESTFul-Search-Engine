Probationary or Conditional Reviews for Faculty and Academic Professionals

Material submitted shall include (see guidelines for Promotion & Tenure for Faculty or for Promotion & Continuing Status for APs for more detail):

  1. The Request for Academic Personnel Action form (available online at www.asu.edu/provost/forms ) , along with any additional forms used by the academic unit.
  2. Table of Contents
  3. Evaluation(s) by personnel committee(s).   Transmittal letters of the chair/director and dean.
  4. A job description for the academic professional.
  5. Summary evidence of teaching and/or job effectiveness.
  6. Self-assessment - goal statement (up to 4 pages in length)
  7. A current curriculum vitae.
  8. A current copy of the unit's/department's and college's approved probationary review process criteria.

A cover list from the dean should be provided of all probationary or conditional reviews of faculty and/or academic professionals in the college, listed by academic unit, who are currently being reviewed and for what action.
