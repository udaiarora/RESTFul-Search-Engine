The ASU Brand: Position Statements

The more often we use consistent statements about the university, the more likely our audiences will remember them and be impacted by them.   Statements such as those below succinctly and effectively describe ASU using language that supports the brand.

  • We are a force for discovery, turning students into thought leaders who will shape the world of their future.

  • Our work has impact, improving the economic, social and cultural health of the communities it serves.

  • We teach in a context that encourages the creation of new knowledge.

Arizona State University is leading the transformation of higher education to a model that is both academically rigorous and broadly inclusive. It is founded on the belief that an institution must serve the needs of all segments of society, not just an elite few who are mathematically or verbally gifted.
