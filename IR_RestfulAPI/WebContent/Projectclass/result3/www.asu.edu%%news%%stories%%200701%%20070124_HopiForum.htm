January 25, 2007

Hopi tribal candidate forum cancelled

The Hopi tribal chairman candidate forum, to be held Jan. 26 at ASU, has been cancelled.

The event was being planned by ASU's American Indian Policy and Leadership Development Center and The Phoenix Indian Center, Inc.

The Hopi tribe is in the process of holding a special election to elect a new tribal chairman. The primary election, with 14 candidates, was held Jan. 24 and a general election is planned for Feb. 7.

For more information, call Cyndee Coin with ASU's American Indian Policy and Leadership Development Center at (480) 965-5869 or conact Bonnie Secakuku with H.O.P.I. at info@hpoipolitics.org.
