Business & Marketing Plans

The works listed below are the ones we consider to be the best for reference or manual use. Other works on writing marketing or business plans are in these same call numbers in the Hayden Stacks. Web sites are also becoming more available.

  1. American Express Small Business Services. Small Business Exchange. Creating an Effective Business Plan
      http://home3.americanexpress.com/smallbusiness/tool/biz_plan/index.asp

    This is a web page with step by step instructions and additional help.

  2. Arizona Department of Commerce. Entrepreneur's Edge 2004.
    Arizona Department of Commerce. Guia del Empresario 2004.
    Arizona Department of Commerce. Arziona Business Connection
    The first two are the "How to start a business in Arizona" guides in English and Spanish versions. The Third item is an online step by step instructions and help with setting up a business.
  3. Bplans.com: The Small Business Planning Destination.
      http://www.bplans.com/

    A really well organized web site with sample business and marketing plans. The Planning Spreadsheet Glossary allows you to get explanations of any part of a business plan - what it is and how to write the section. It also allows you to develop a mini plan on the web site. It is a web site designed to sell you their business plan software.
  4. Bizplans.com: the Planning People
      http://www.bizplans.com/

    This is a business plans software company web site. The value of this site is the sample plans that you can view and the free books you can read online. The graphs included in the samples are extremely helpful in showing how to layout and illustrate your plan.
  5. Edward Lowe Foundation
      http://edwardlowe.org/
    Edward Lowe PEERspectives.
      http://peerspectives.org/

    The Edward Lowe Foundation is dedicated to "Championing the Entrepreneurial Spirit". The Foundation web site has pages designed for BUSINESS OWNERS, particularly "Thinking about starting a company" that are helpful. The PEERspectives pages allow you to do a keyword search for BUSINESS PLANS. This will get you into the very thorough information they have on how to do business plans and related topics.
