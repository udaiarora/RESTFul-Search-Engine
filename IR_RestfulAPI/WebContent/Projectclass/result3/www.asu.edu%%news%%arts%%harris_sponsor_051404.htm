May 14, 2004

Performance partner: Group signs on as sponsor for Lyric Opera

The Harris, a wealth management group, has signed on as the first-ever corporate sponsor of the ASU Herberger Mainstage Lyric Opera Theatre 2004 - 05 season.

The Lyric Opera Theatre will stage four productions in the upcoming season, beginning in October with the musical "Side by Side by Sondheim." Lyric Opera Theatre is a signature performance program in ASU's Katherine K. Herberger College of Fine Arts School of Music.

"The ASU Herberger College of Fine Arts is thrilled to receive support from The Harris for our Lyric Opera Theatre season," says J. Robert Wills, dean of the Herberger College . "ASU is known for presenting quality arts programming to the community. The Harris sponsorship invigorates our efforts and helps us to continue providing quality musical theatre experiences for local audiences."

The Harris is a long-time supporter of arts in the community and has used its offices to showcase works by local artists. This is its first opportunity to extend that support to the Lyric Opera Theatre and to recognize the exceptional performances staged by the ASU Herberger College of Fine Arts.

"We are pleased to be able to lend our support as the first corporate sponsor for the Lyric Opera Theatre season," says Harley Barnes, Arizona regional president, The Harris. "We believe strongly in the importance of the arts as a way to enrich our lives and we are proud to support the School of Music's efforts to bring the drama and spectacle of the opera to our community."

The Harris has been a part of the Arizona community since 1976 and has eight locations in Phoenix, Carefree, Scottsdale, West Valley and Tucson.

The Harris organization, founded in Chicago in 1882, provides private banking, trust administration, investment management, wealth advisory services, direct investing and full-service investing to clients nationwide.
