December 22, 2005

Looking back at 2005

As the year comes to a close it is a good time to reflect on the accomplishments and highlights for ASU in 2005. It was a busy year — from researchers seeking new discoveries, to students earning the highest accolades, to ASU playing a key role in helping the recovery efforts of one of the largest natural disasters in the nation's history — ASU spent a great deal of time in the international spotlight.

Here is a look at some of the year's highlights:

   
  ASU steps forward for Katrina relief   University academics, enrollment soars
     
   
  University reaches out to China   Year brings accolades for ASU individuals
     
   
  New initiatives spur academic creative efforts   Interdisciplinary research yields strong results
     
   
  Campus adds to core of downtown Phoenix   Nationally recognized figures join ASU
