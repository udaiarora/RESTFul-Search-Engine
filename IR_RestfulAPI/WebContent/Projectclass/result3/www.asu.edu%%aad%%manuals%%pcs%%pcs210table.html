The department selling equipment:

  1. negotiates a selling price with the acquiring department

  2. completes a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form, checking the “Interdepartmental” section and obtaining approvals of both the releasing and receiving departments

  3. prepares an expense transfer (IX) document

    Note:Use object code 7810 95, crediting and debiting the agency/orgs for the releasing and receiving departments respectively.

  4. records the ASU property control number(s) in the description field of the IX

    and

  5. sends the PRA and a photocopy of the IX to Property Control.

Property Control changes the asset record to reflect the new custodial unit.
