Miscellaneous:

Foreign Language Articles

The best or most well known syntheses for some compounds may not be written in English. Translations of foreign language articles are not available. General science and chemistry translation dictionaries are available in the Science Reference shelves under Q123 and QD5, respectively. A Beilstein Dictionary for translating chemical terms from German into English is available online. Another online translation dictionary (not necessarily science or chemistry related) may be found at: AltaVista (Babel Fish).
