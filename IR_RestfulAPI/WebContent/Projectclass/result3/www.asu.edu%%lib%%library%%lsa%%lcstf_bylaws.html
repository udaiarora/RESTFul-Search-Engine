Article 1. Name
This Organization shall be known as the Arizona State University Library Classified Staff Task Force (CSTF).
Article 2. Statement of Function
  1. CSTF is subject to and validated by the University Libraries' Dean.
  2. The Task Force shall seek to represent classified staff by:
    1. Assisting to further communication channels and mutual support networks inside/outside the university libraries.
    2. Providing a forum for classified staff to present issues of concern and to actively participate in their resolution.
    3. Collaborating with the Dean of the University Libraries on general staff issues and/or concerns.
Article 3. Eligibility/Service
  1. Eligibility for service or activity is open to all classified staff not on probation (or under progressive discipline).
  2. Election/service shall require the approval of the individual's department head.
  3. Eligibility for offices of Chair and Chair-Elect is open to all 100 % classified staff not on probation (or under progressive discipline).
  4. The term of service for the offices of Chair, Chair-Elect and Representatives shall be for one year, from July 1st to June 30th.
Article 4. Officer's and Representative's Responsibilities
  • Section 1. Chair
    1. Duties of the Chair shall include but not be limited to:
      1. Attend all LSA Board meetings as a Board member.
      2. Arrange meetings with Chair-Elect and/or Representatives as needed.
      3. Act as an intermediary with Library Administration and other organizations as required.
      4. Attend Administrative Council meetings as an Ex-Officio member.
      5. Write and submit an end-of-year report to the Council and Library Administration.
      6. Organize the Annual Meeting between the Library Administration and Classified Staff.
      7. Fulfill such other responsibilities as are given to this office through direction from the University Libraries' Dean.
    2. If the office of Chair should become vacant, the Chair-Elect shall assume the Chair position for the balance of the term as well as for the following year.
  • Section 2. Chair-Elect
    Duties of the Chair-Elect shall include but not be limited to:
    1. Attend all meetings arranged by the Chair.
    2. Assume the responsibilities of the Chair in his/her absence.
    3. Succeed to the office of Chair the following year, or in the event the position should become vacant.
    4. Oversee the Election Process.
    5. Maintain contact with department representatives.
    6. Fulfill other such responsibilities as are given by the Chair.
  • Section 3. Representatives
    1. There shall be a Representative from each department in the library. This Representative is appointed by the appropriate Department Head.
    2. Duties of the Representatives shall include but not be limited to:
      1. Attend all meetings arranged by the Chair.
      2. Solicit comments, concerns, and proposed solutions to the Chair and Chair-Elect.
      3. Serve on temporary committees established by the CSTF Chair.
    3. A new Representative will be appointed by the appropriate Department Head should a position become vacant.
Article 5. Election Process
  1. Ensure adequate departmental representation.
  2. Oversee eligibility, nomination, and the election of Chair-Elect.
Article 6. Committees
  1. There shall be committees as needed.
  2. These committees are subject to the CSTF Chair.
Article 7. Bylaws
  1. The Bylaws shall be considered in effect at all times.
  2. All Classified Staff shall have the opportunity to review and vote on any proposed changes to the bylaws.

Amended June 2004
