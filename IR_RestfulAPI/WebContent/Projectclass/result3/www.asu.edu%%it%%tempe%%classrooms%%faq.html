Getting Assistance

Q: How do I get assistance in the classroom?

A: Dial 5-3342 using the campus phone (if available) in each of the classrooms. Technical support staff will be dispatched right away to fix your problem. This includes software, hardware, and facilities related difficulties.

Q: How do I contact classroom support outside of class time?

A: General classroom questions can be directed to classroom.support @asu.edu or by calling (480) 965-3342.

Q: What time can I get into a classroom?

A: Any open access classrooms is open during normal building hours. The student mediation classrooms are open one-half hour prior to a scheduled class or event and should be scheduled through Classroom Scheduling. Depending on preceding classes, we can make arrangements for you to have access to your classroom even earlier. Please contact us ahead of time to make these arrangements.

Q: How do I learn to use the multimedia equipment available in the classroom?

A: You can schedule training with a staff member anytime. For more information and to fill out a form to request training, visit the Classroom Training page. From this page, you can fill out a form to schedule a training session with one of our staff members.

Q: I'm an instructor for a special event. Where can I find out more specifics about campus and the building that the class is in?

A: Classroom Support would be happy to answer as many questions about campus as we can. If we don't know the answers we can probably direct you to a better source of information. It's always a good idea to contact us as early as possible to get details ironed out.

Printing Questions

Q: What do I do if I don't have any money on my Sun Card?

A: A Value Transfer Station (VTS) is available in the Computing Commons Atrium next to the ASU Computer Store. You can increase the balance of Sun Dollars on your Sun Card by inserting your card and $1, $5, and $10 bills into the VTS. You may also add funds to your Sun Dollars account at the Sun Card office in the Memorial Union or online at the Sun Card Web page.

Q: Since I'm paying for my printing, what level of quality should I expect?

A: We will offer print quality sufficient to submit work to a professor at ASU free of streaks, wrinkles and other blemishes. If the print quality is below this standard, we will reprint your job free of additional charges.

Q: There is a problem with my print job. Can you reimburse my Sun Card account?

A: No. We are unable to reimburse Sun Card accounts. If there is a problem with a print job due to printer malfunction, we will reprint the job at no cost to you. Please see a classroom support staff person for assistance.

Q: I accidentally printed the wrong file. Can you print the correct file for me?

A: No. Once you release a print job from your workstation, you are charged for the printing. If you send the wrong file, simply delete the job from the queue instead of releasing it, or do nothing, and the job will be deleted automatically in 2 hours.

Q: I want to print on paper other than that provided in the classrooms. Can I do this? What will I be charged?

A: We provide 20lb. stock 8.5" x 11" white paper. If you would like to print to a different size paper, transparency film or other media, please ask for assistance from a classroom support staff person. User supplied media must be verified against the specifications of our printers to avoid damage. You will be charged the same per sheet fee as if you were using the supplied paper.

Classroom Information

Q: What type of equipment is available in my classroom?

A: Since equipment varies from classroom to classroom, you can find a complete list of equipment in your classroom by visiting the Classroom List page. There, you can search for your classroom by building or by classroom type.

Q: How do I get more equipment for my classroom?

A: To reserve more equipment, visit one of our Equipment Circulation offices, call (480) 965-3342, or fill out an online request form on the Equipment Request page.

Q: How can I schedule a class?

A: To schedule a class, contact Classroom Scheduling at (480) 965-3342.

Equipment Circulation Questions

Q: What equipment can I reserve and for how long?

A: For a complete list of equipment available for check out, please visit our Equipment List page.

Q: Where can I return equipment that I checked out?

A: You can return equipment to any Equipment Circulation office, but we ask that you return the equipment to the office where you received it if at all possible.

Q: What are your office hours and where are they located?

A: For a complete list of office hours and locations, please visit our Office Hours and Locations page.

Additional Information

Q: When do the student mediation classrooms close?

A: Typically, these rooms close 15 minutes after the class is over. If an instructor would like to stay later in the room, they may feel free to do so, assuming there isn't another class. Students are not allowed to remain in the room without an instructor present.

Q: Do all of the classrooms have the same schedule during finals week?

A: The classrooms on campus do not have the same schedule during finals week. Your classroom may be in use during your normal instructional period by another class. Your room designee can provide you with the location of your final exam.

Q: What if someone tries to use a computer in the room while I'm teaching a class?

A: Just like any other class on campus, only students enrolled in your class are permitted to attend the lecture. Student mediation classrooms are not open access labs. If you have difficulties with a student who is not enrolled in your class, call 965-3342 and a support staff will escort that person out of the room.

Q: Why can't my students and I have food or drink in the classroom?

A: We ask that you cooperate with us and not allow food or drink in the classroom in order to keep the room presentable and equipment functioning for all users.
