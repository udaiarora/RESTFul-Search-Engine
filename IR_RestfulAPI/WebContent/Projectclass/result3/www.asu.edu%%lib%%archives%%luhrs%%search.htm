Search the Luhrs Family Manuscript


Enter a word or words in the box for a simple keyword search:

 


 
       

The Luhrs manuscript is searchable by words in the document. Type a word in the blank and the search engine will point you to the pages which contain that term. Note that enclosing multiple words in quotation marks will cause the search engine to search the words as a phrase, rather than looking for the individual words anywhere in the document. Without quotation marks, multiple words will be searched as if they were connected with "and," so any pages that contain all the search terms (whether or not they are in close proximity on the page) will result as a hit.


Note: Due to some misspellings in the original text, if your search does not return results for a term you are confident is likely to be mentioned in the manuscript, try searching some possible misspellings as well. For example, the name "Duppa" appears in the manuscript as "Duppe."
