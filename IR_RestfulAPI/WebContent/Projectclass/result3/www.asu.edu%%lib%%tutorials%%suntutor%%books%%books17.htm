How to find books

Understanding your results

Locating the book

Once you have the call number and location information for a book, you can find it on the shelf in the appropriate library. Be sure to check the "Status" area to make sure it is on the shelf.

You can refer to the library's online locator to help direct you to the appropriate library, floor and/or area.
