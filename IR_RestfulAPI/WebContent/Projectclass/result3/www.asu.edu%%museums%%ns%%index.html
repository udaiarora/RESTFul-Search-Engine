Natural Sciences Collections

ASU is famous for the Mars Space Flight Facility, known for its photographs of Mars. The R.S. Dietz Museum of Geology houses minerals, gems and fossils from around the world. Our Living Collections contains Arizona's only complete collection of all eighteen sub-species of Arizona native rattlesnakes, and the Center for Meteorite Studies has one of the world's largest meteorite collections.
