ASU Performance* Duplication Services

The Music Library provides duplication of in-house performance recordings (ASU performances* made by the School of Music) to compact disc. Compact discs will be provided by the Music Library. In order to request a recording of an ASU performance:


Search the ASU Performance Index

(Note: Recent performances may not appear in index)

  • Completely fill out request form online.

You will be notified by e-mail when your request is complete. All finished recordings will be available at the Music Library, unless a post-paid envelope has been furnished for mailing.

Cost:


Payment of cash upon completion (no checks, credit, departmental charge, etc.) in exact change for the total amount **:

Performance to CD ..................................................................$5/performance

(Strict duplication of master copy. Note: Master copy may not have tracking)


CD of excerpts, added tracking, applause/gap elimination) ….$10/disc


*Non-copyrighted performances
**Duplication charges are waived for ASU faculty and staff
