ASU faculty and staff may order publications using this order form. PDF versions of all publications are available below. Individuals not affiliated with ASU may request copies of a publication by contacting ASU Public Affairs.

ASU 2004-2005 Annual Report
The ASU 2004-2005 Annual Report summarizes the major accomplishments of the university in fiscal year 2005.
(Download PDF version)

Comprehensive Development Plan
The Comprehensive Development Plan describes the university's long-term infrastructure plan, including maps that cover projected growth for each campus.
(Download PDF version)

Essence of ASU
The Essence of ASU is a pocket-sized booklet that provides brief definitions for the major concepts behind the New American University vision, including each of the eight design imperatives.
(Download PDF version)

The New American University
The New American University presents the vision that is driving the university along with ASU exemplars that embody its principles.
(Download PDF version)

One University in Many Places
One University in Many Places is a presidential white paper that defines the principles for the organization of each campus under a single Arizona State University identity.
(Download PDF version)

Tempe Campus Guide
The Tempe campus guide offers an informative look at the university's largest campus. The guide features a map and specific details on campus offerings and locations.
(Download PDF version)

Polytechnic Campus Guide
The Polytechnic campus guide offers an informative look at the university's east Mesa campus. The guide features a map and specific details on campus offerings and locations.
(Download PDF version)
