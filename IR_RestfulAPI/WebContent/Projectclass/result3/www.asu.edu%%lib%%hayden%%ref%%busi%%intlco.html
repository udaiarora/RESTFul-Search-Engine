COMPANY INFORMATION SOURCES

International Company Directories and Financials


International volumes of company directories described in earlier sections, such as Moody's Manuals (see under "U.S. Public Company Financials") as well as America's Corporate Families and International Affiliates, and Directory of Corporate Affiliations, and the Companies International database (see under "U.S. Company Directories") can be consulted in addition to the sources below.

Check also Telephone and E-Mail Directories (http://www.asu.edu/lib/ref/gen/phbks.html) to find basic directory information on companies by name, location, or product/service.
    When you see this button next to an entry, click on it to go to the ASU Libraries Indexes page. You may also get to this page by clicking on INDEXES on any of our web pages, including our Home Page: http://www.asu.edu/lib/


  1. Asian Company Handbook. Tokyo: Toyo Keizai, 1990- . Annual. (ASU owns 1992- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HC 411 .A753x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Profiles over a 1,000 companies on the exchanges in Hong Kong, Indonesia, Malaysia, Republic of Korea, Singapore, Taiwan, and Thailand. The brief one-page entries include directory information, company description and recent trends, basic financial data, subsidiaries and affiliates, foreign partners, capitalization, major shareholders, and business results.

  2. Asia's 7,500 Largest Companies. London: ELC Publishing, 1985- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 5770.5 .A2 A7x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Europe's 15,000 Largest Companies. London: ELC Publishing, [1975]- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HD 2356 .E9 E93 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    These directories rank companies by type in a tabular format listing the corporate address and data on sales, profitability, employees, capital structure, and year established with a cross-index alphabetically by company name. The publication on Asia covers the Asia Pacific region (i.e., Hong Kong, Japan, Malaysia, Philippines, Taiwan, Indonesia, South Korea, Singapore, Thailand, and China) while the publication on Europe covers Western European countries only.

  3. Directory of American Firms Operating in Foreign Countries. 3 vols. New York: Uniworld Business Publications, 1956- .Irregular. (ASU owns 1961, 1966- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4538 .A1 D5 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Directory of Foreign Firms Operating in the United States. New York: Uniworld Business Publications, 1969- . Irregular. (ASU owns 1971, 1978- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4056 .A21943 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    The first directory lists U.S. companies alphabetically in Volume 1 and gives the foreign countries in which each company operates. Volumes 2 and 3 are arranged by country and then by U.S. company operating there citing the foreign affiliate associated with each. The second directory is arranged by country of origin and then by foreign company with the American affiliate appearing next to it. There are also alphabetical cross indexes by foreign company and by U.S. company for the second directory.

  4. Directory of Foreign Investment in the U.S.: Real Estate and Business. Detroit, MI: Gale Research, 1991- . Irregular. (ASU owns 1991-1994. Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4057 .A21944 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 8A)

    Arranged geographically for the real estate ownership and by SIC code for the businesses, this source indicates even partial ownership by foreign companies. Basic addresses and phone numbers are included for the U.S. company and the foreign company. Whenever possible, valuation, business description, revenues, number of employees, purchase date, and price are included. There are additional geographic and alphabetical indexes.

  5. Disclosure/Worldscope Global. Bethesda, MD: Disclosure, 1992- . Quarterly. (ASU owns 1993- .)
      HG 4050 .D57x HAYDEN REFERENCE MARS
      ONLINE ACCESS latest update (Global Access) Click on Business

    This database provides basic directory and financial information on public companies from around the world. Up to ten years of standardized financial data in the currency of the country of origin is presented with a few key financial figures given in US dollars for comparability across companies. Each company's accounting policies are also listed to assist in judging comparability of the data. Financial ratios for companies, countries, and industries are included as well as monthly foreign exchange rates and rankings by country and industry. A list of current news articles completes each company entry. The latest update is also available on the library web as Global Access offering both increased access and searchability.

  6. European 5000. 4 vols. London: Euromoney, 1990- . Annual. (ASU owns 1990- . Latest in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4132 .Z5 P75x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Covers the top 5,000 public companies based on capitalization from 18 European countries broken out into three volumes by region (Central and Southern Europe, Northern Europe, and Western Europe). There is a separate index volume by sector (industry), by country and sector, and by company name which also includes selected rankings. Company entries provide brief, half-page overviews with basic finance and stock data provided by Worldscope/Disclosure Partners.

  7. French Company Handbook. Paris: International Business Development, 1981- . Annual. (ASU owns 1983- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HD 2853 .F73 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Analyzes over 100 publicly traded and state owned French companies by industry. Includes directory information, directors, major activities, company background, major shareholders, principal activities, subsidiaries and holdings, strategy and trends, important developments, and financial highlights. An alphabetic index by company name is at the front.

  8. Hoover's Handbook of World Business. Austin, TX: Reference Press, 1992- . Annual. (ASU owns 1992- . Latest year in Reference; earlier in Journals.)
      HG 4009 .H66 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    "Everything you need to know about the most important European and Asian companies." This work includes regional, country, and company profiles. The company profiles have a two-page format which includes an overview, brief history, important executives and offices, statistics, subsidiary and affiliates list, and a list of competitors. There is also a section for lists of rankings.

  9. Japan Company Handbook--First Section. Tokyo: Toyo Keizai, 1974- . Semiannual. (ASU owns 1974- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HC 461 .J35 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Japan Company Handbook--Second Section. Tokyo: Toyo Keizai, [1983]- . Semiannual. (ASU owns 1985- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HC 461 .S45x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Japan has three major exchanges (Tokyo, Osaka, and Nagoya) and five local ones. First section companies are those listed on the major exchanges which, among other requirements, have at least 20 million shares outstanding. Second section companies are roughly those listed on the major exchanges with less than 20 million shares outstanding. The second section handbook also profiles separately companies listed on the local exchanges and those sold over-the-counter as well as a selection of unlisted companies. The one-page profiles arranged by industry provide brief overviews including basic financial and stock data. An alphabetic index by company name at the front of each section handbook locates companies in that handbook while the one at the back locates companies in the other section's handbook.

  10. Latin America 25,000. Parsippany, NJ: Dun & Bradstreet Information Services, 1984- . Annual. ASU owns 1992- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4091.5 .A25x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Formerly called Dun's Latin America top 25,000, this provides the usual type of directory information found in D&B business directories. Entries are listed alphabetically by country with a cross-index by company name. The second volume lists companies by industry classification (SIC) and gives a ranking of the top 1,000 businesses by employee size.

  11. Latin American Companies Handbook. New York: Moody's Investor's Service, 1994- . Annual. (ASU owns 1994- . Latest in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4091.5 .L356 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Covers companies in ten Latin American countries. Each country has its own section with an introductory profile and stock exchange information followed by one-page company summaries arranged alphabetically. The summary includes performance ratios, earnings, balance statement, capitalization information, and latest dividend paid.

  12. Principal International Businesses. Parsippany, NJ: Dun & Bradstreet Information Services, 1974- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HF 54 .U5 D8x HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    A directory of over 50,000 leading companies outside the U.S. Arranged in three sections: geographic, product classification, and alphabetical. Company descriptions in Part I provide the usual directory information as well as the line of business, number of employees, and chief executive's name.

  13. Survey of Industrials. Toronto: Financial Post Information Service, 1927- . Annual. (ASU owns 1968-1987, 1989- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 5151 .F53 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Summarizes the financial performance of over 2,000 companies publicly traded in Canada. The entries are reminiscent of those in the comparable Moody's and Standard & Poor's publications. They entries are arranged alphabetically by principal company with an index at the front connecting parent companies, subsidiaries, and affiliates to the appropriate page and alphanumeric company designation. There is also an index at the back by 2-digit SIC major groups.

  14. Thomas Register of European Manufacturers
      http://www.tipcoeurope.com (registration required online)

    Provides access to directory information on manufacturers in 17 Western European countries by product/service and by company name.

  15. Who Owns Whom: Continental Europe. 2 vols. High Wycombe, England: Dun & Bradstreet Ltd., 1977- . Annual. (ASU owns 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4132 .Z5 W5 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    Who Owns Whom: United Kingdom & Republic of Ireland. 2 vols. High Wycombe, England: Dun & Bradstreet Ltd., 1962- . Annual. (ASU owns 1967, 1970, 1974, 1993- . Latest year in Reference; earlier years in Journals.)
      HG 4135 .Z5 W5 HAYDEN REFERENCE (located on Table 10A)

    The first volume of each set lists parent companies by country showing the headquarters address and the names of its subsidiaries and associates. The second volume of each set provides cross references from the subsidiaries and associates to the parent company's name and country.
