Dr. William Lewis,
Chief Information Officer
and Vice Provost

The Arizona State University Chief Information Officer (CIO), Dr. William Lewis, works with the university community to develop a vision for the role of information technologies in support of the university mission and strategic plan. The university CIO identifies strategies and implements standards, infrastructure, and services to fulfill that vision in a diverse, distributed university technology environment. Responsibilities include university-wide coordination and prioritization of planning, budget requests, resource allocation, implementation, and oversight related to ongoing operation of information and educational technologies. The university CIO oversees development of common architecture, standards, and approaches, and coordinates distributed information technology efforts by the colleges, departments, campus units, and university as a whole.

The university CIO advises the president, members of the administration, other universities, and the Arizona Board of Regents on technology initiatives, state-of-the-art practices in emerging technologies, and appropriate technology solutions to support operations, and serves as primary university contact for IT matters with state and national IT organizations.
