Ask a Question

Ware-q@asu.edu Email Support

WARE-Q is an email distribution list that goes to approximately 25 Warehouse consultants. The consultants cover every aspect of the Data Warehouse and are approved to access all databases on the Warehouse. The first available consultant with an answer will contact you. When contacting ware-q, it is helpful to attach your query to your email. WARE-Q is more efficient than calling individual consultants and you often get good ideas from more than one consultant.

Examples of topics that are handled on ware-q include:

User ID's and passwords.
How to get access to the Warehouse or status of warehouse access requests.
Software questions - BrioQuery and Sybase. (We will answer Access questions as best we can, though we do not provide official technical support for Access against the data warehouse.)
Data definitions and use questions - Student, Financial, HR and Sponsored Research data.
Error message troubleshooting.
How To questions of all types.
Warehouse performance questions. (i.e., why is it slow today?)

Please check out Frequently Asked Questions first. Your question might be answered there, or by a link provided there.

The only no-no: Don't Send Passwords in email, please!
