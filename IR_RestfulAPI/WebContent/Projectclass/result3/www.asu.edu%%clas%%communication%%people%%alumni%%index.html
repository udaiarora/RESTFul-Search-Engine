Welcome Alumni!

We invite you to read current and past editions of our newsletter, "Communication Matters" and to "Tell Us Your News".

"Your education is your finest treasure.  Join me in our opportunity to extend the meaning of philanthropy through your continued association with the students and alumni of HDSHC.  By visiting  http://asu.edu/alumni/involve/, you can subscribe to a variety of listservs, maintaining your connection to ASU, providing the information you need to make informed and educated decisions based on your desired level of involvement.  You can also assist us in contacting you, keeping you apprised of developments and opportunities through your continued involvement with HDSHC and ASU, by maintaining current contact information at the Devils' Den (http://www.asu.edu/alumni/devils-den/).

"More importantly, visit the HDSHC website at http://www.asu.edu/clas/communication and challenge yourself to find ways to continue your association with HDSHC by committing time, financial contributions, or both.  For additional information on how you can help build a stronger community for current and future students of HDSHC, please visit http://www.asu.edu/clas/communication/people/alumni/giving.

"Explore ... commit ... attach ... involve ... develop."


                                                                          -- Milissa Hutloff Koehler

Milissa is a double alumna of HDSHC.  She completed her BA in 1999 and her MA in 2005.
