Site Home > Halls >Sahuaro Hall

Sahuaro Hall provides co-ed housing for freshman and upperclass students. Sahuaro offers community style bath and a limited number of single rooms for upperclass students. Amenities include: 24-hour campus security and front desk services, computer lab, laundry facilities, vending machines, community kitchen, air conditioning, convenience store, cafeteria, volleyball court and basic cable.

Sahuaro Hall Floor Plan.

Amenities include:

Twin beds (36x80)
Window & Curtains
Desk & chairs
Waste/Recycle basket
Dresser & Closets
Ethernet port for each student
Modified rooms for students with disabilities
