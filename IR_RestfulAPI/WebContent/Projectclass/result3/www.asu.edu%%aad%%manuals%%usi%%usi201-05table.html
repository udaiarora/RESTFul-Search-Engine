The student completes an Initial Registration Request form or a Drop/Add Request form and obtains approval to register for or drop/add a class from the appropriate college.

If approved, the dean or designee signs and dates the form in the space provided. The student takes the form and a photo ID to any registrar site (the Records Information counter at the Tempe campus, Records & Registration at the West campus, or Registration Services at the Polytechnic or Downtown Phoenix campus) for processing within five business days of obtaining the dean’s approval.

University Registrar’s Office staff processes the Initial Registration Request form or the Drop/Add Request form and prints the schedule/billing statement. The student remains at the site to review the statement for accuracy. The student then pays any fees by using the ASU Interactive Web site

For specific information on fee payment in person, contact the Tuition Payment Office on the second floor of the Student Services Building.
