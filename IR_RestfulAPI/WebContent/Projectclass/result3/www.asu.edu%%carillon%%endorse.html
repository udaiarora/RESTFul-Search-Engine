The SDSU carillon is an important musical addition to our major campus activities, including Commencement, Homecoming, Founder's Day, campus memorials, holiday events, and a variety of special-event programs. The daily ringing of the carillon continues to be one of SDSU's oldest continuing traditions.”

–Dr. Terry O'Donnell
Professor of Music
