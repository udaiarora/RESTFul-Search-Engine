Putting it in action

Imagine that you've been given the assignment below. What type of index would you select to find the articles you need to fulfill the assignment?

Write a short paper on the effect of television violence on children. Please use several articles from journals or magazines to support your claims. The paper is due in one week.
For this topic, television violence and children, a good choice to start with would be a general index, that will have information from a variety of publications. Also, since your deadline is a short time away, choosing one that provides the full-text of articles would also be wise. So a good choice for this assignment would be either Academic Search Premier/Ebscohost, a general, full-text index.
