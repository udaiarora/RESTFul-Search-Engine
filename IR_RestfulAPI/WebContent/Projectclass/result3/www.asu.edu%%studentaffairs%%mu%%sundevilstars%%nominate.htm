What is a Sun Devil Star?
Sun Devil Star (n.) /sun dev·il star/
a current Arizona State University student who has exemplified one or all of the following characteristics: 1) made a positive contribution to the ASU community; 2) demonstrated leadership skills; 3) served as a role model to other Sun Devils.

See also: leader, role model, volunteer, student employee. - Sun Devil Stars (pl.)
