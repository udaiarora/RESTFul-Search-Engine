Hyperion

Hyperion is the desktop client software for Data Warehouse users.

Hyperion Licenses

System: Server

Availability: See setup instructions below

Restrictions: Faculty & Staff Only

Cost: $330 licenses (one time cost)
$120 yearly maintenance (prorated the first year) on annual renewal (July – June)

Payment Method: Purchase Order (vendor code: UTOBIQ)

Form: Hyperion Site License Agreement

Setting Up Hyperion Service

  1. You must complete a Hyperion Site License Agreement form.
  2. You must have a PO for the correct dollar amount.
  3. Fax both forms to 5-5171 ATTN: Patti Dawson.
  4. You will be notified when your license and software are ready to be picked up. They are not placed in campus mail.
