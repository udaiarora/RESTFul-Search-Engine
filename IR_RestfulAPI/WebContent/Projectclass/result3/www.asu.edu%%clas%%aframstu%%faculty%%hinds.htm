David Hinds(PhD, Howard University,1998, Political Science) is an Assistant Professor of African and African American Studies with a concentration on the Caribbean and African Diaspora Studies. His major areas of teaching and research are Governance and Politics in the African Diaspora , Race and Ethnicity in Plural Societies ,Black Political Thought and Political Protest and Resistance.
