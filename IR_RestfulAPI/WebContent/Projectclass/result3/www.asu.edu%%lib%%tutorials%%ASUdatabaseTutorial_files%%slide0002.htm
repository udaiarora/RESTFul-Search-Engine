The Song Index contains popular, folk, and art songs found in the collection owned by the Arizona State University Music Library.  Entries provide the song title, composer, lyricist, first line, and the collection in which it is found.
The Performance Tape Index provides access to performances in the Arizona State University School of Music from 1985 to the present.  Included are recitals and concerts given by graduate students, faculty, and large ensembles.  The tapes are housed in the Music Library.  Each entry contains the performer, date of performance, medium of performance, and the music performed.
