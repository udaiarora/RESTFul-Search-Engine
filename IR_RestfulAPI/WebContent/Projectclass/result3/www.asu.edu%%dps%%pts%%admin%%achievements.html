NOTABLE ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Introduced the customer service oriented Online Parking Services that allows customers to view an account, purchase a permit, appeal a citation and pay a citation over the internet.
  • Initiated direct e-mail notices to advise the campus community of construction and event activities.
  • Created a Parking Structure 1 evening permit to improve service for faculty, staff and students who require evening specific access to campus.
  • Attended every Faculty, Staff and Student orientation to communicate parking policies/procedures and address any parking and transit related questions.
  • Actively participated in improvement sessions for New Employee and Student orientations to enhance the introduction to ASU.
  • Aided support and funding to the ASU Clean and Beautiful campaign.
  • Implemented Phase 1 of action plan to address in-depth peer institution Program Review recommendations.
  • Implemented internal management reviews of PTS sections to streamline operations and identify areas for improvement.
  • Implemented numerous projects to enhance campus safety and security e.g. Partnership with Undergraduate Student Government to help fund improvements to the Safety Escort Service including doubling the SES annual budget to increase service hours from 7p.m. to 3 a.m. seven nights a week and procuring a van to help the additional campus coverage.
  • Partnered with various campus entities to review transit alternatives involving South Campus and, on a separate project, Light Rail.
  • In the fall of 2004, opened Parking Structure Seven which features 1,600 spaces.
  • Opened 1,200 spaces in the ASU Foundation Parking Structure in January 2005. The structure provides permit parking and restores much needed visitor parking spaces for the north campus.
  • Improved the process for acquiring a temporary disabled placard by working with Disability Resources to create on form that both departments will validate.
  • Funded the purchase of additional golf carts for the services offered by the Disability Resource Center.
  • Improved parking stalls in Parking Structure 5 to accommodate compact cars and larger vehicles by creating vehicle specific spaces in designated areas.
