PCS 701: Records Maintained by Property Control

Purpose

To maintain accurate audit records for internal and external audit agencies and to provide departments with information about equipment

Sources

48 Code of Federal Regulations § 45
United States. Office of Management and Budget. OMB circulars A-21 and A-110
Property Control

Policy

ASU is required to keep and maintain property control records for all active, missing, and retired capital equipment on the property control system.

The following records and databases are kept and maintained by Property Control. These lists represent the primary records databases and are not all-inclusive.

Property Control Records

  1. active sponsor-owned equipment files

  2. assets “Not Found” reports for physical inventories

  3. ASU Foundation gifts for capital equipment

  4. equipment screening certifications

  5. “final” sponsored inventories

  6. gift-in-kind records for capital equipment

  7. interdepartmental transfers (IXs) for capital equipment

  8. journal entries for capital equipment

  9. Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) forms

  10. purchase orders for capital equipment

  11. Temporary Off-Campus Loan of ASU Equipment forms

Property Control Databases

  1. Asset

  2. Betterments

  3. Capital Asset

  4. Facility Betterments
