WEST CAMPUS CLASSIFIED STAFF COUNCIL

The purpose of the West Campus Classified Staff Council (WCCSC) is to encourage definition of the role of classified staff; to promote and enhance communication among classified staff and, through the provost of the West campus, with other campus and university communities; and to advise and make recommendations on all issues relating to classified staff.
