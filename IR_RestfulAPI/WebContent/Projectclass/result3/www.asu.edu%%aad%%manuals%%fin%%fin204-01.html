FIN 204–01: Additions/Changes/Deletions in Org Managers/Authorized Signers

Purpose

To discuss the procedure for:

  1. establishing the org manager and authorized signers for a new agency/org

  2. changing an org manager

    and

  3. adding/deleting authorized signers

Source

University policy

Policy

University departments establish org managers and authorized signers for new agency/orgs, change existing org managers, or add/delete authorized signers from existing agency/orgs by completing the appropriate section(s) on Establishing/Changing Org Manager/Authorized Signers on Agency/Org(s), Advantage Form A.D

Advantage Approval Matrix
SituationRequired Approval Signature, Including Advantage User ID1
Establishing a new agency/org2New org manager or higher-level authority
Change in org manager3Department chair or higher-level authority over the agency/org
Change in org signer(s), other than org managerPresent org manager or higher-level authority

1The Advantage User ID for each approval signature must be included on Form A so Advantage Security can ensure that the person signing has the authority to approve the addition/change/deletion.

Note:For org manager changes on sponsored project agency/orgs, certain additional approvals may be required. Consult your Office for Research and Sponsored Projects Administration site officer.

2Also complete a New Account Application Form (see also FIN 202, “Establishment of Agency/Orgs”).
3Indicate whether the previous org manager will remain an authorized signer on the agency/org(s) and retained, if warranted, on the Advantage security tables.

Procedure

Responsibility
Action
Requesting department
  1. Complete an Advantage Form A to initiate org manager or authorized signer change(s) and obtain all required approval signatures. Refer to the Advantage Approval Matrix above.
If adding, changing, or deleting an org manager and/or authorized signer for an established agency/org:
  1. Forward the ASU Advantage Form A document to the Advantage Security section of Financial Services, for updating of Advantage tables.
If establishing an org manager and/or authorized signer(s) for a new agency/org:
  1. Forward the completed document(s), including all required signatures, to Financial Services.
Financial Services
or
Office for Research and Sponsored Projects Administration
  1. Review the completed document(s) for completeness and accuracy.

  2. Update Advantage tables as deemed necessary.

  3. File ASU Advantage Form A document for updating/record retention purposes.
endtable
