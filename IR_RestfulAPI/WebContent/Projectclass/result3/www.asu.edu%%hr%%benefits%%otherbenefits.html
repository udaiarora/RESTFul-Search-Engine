LONG-TERM CARE INSURANCE

A long-term care plan is offered by UNUM/Provident Insurance.
The plan offers choices regarding coverage type and duration. Choices include provisions for comprehensive coverage for Nursing Home, Adult Day Care and In-Home care, if you are unable to perform some daily living tasks without assistance. Coverage is available through payroll deduction for you, your spouse, parents and parent-in-laws.
