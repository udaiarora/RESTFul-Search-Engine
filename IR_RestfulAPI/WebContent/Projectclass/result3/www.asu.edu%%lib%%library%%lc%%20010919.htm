Minutes of Meeting


September 19, 2001

 

Meeting called to order at 3:02 PM


Chair: John Barnard
Secretary: Mimmo Bonanni
A quorum was present.


I. Approval of Minutes

 

Minutes of April 18, 2001 meeting were approved as written.

 

II. Chair & Executive Committee Report

 

The Chair thanked Dean Schmidt for providing refreshments for this first meeting of the new Council. JoAnn Mulvihill and Annie Platoff were also thanked for continuing their respective appointments as Kenneth P. Knepp Parliamentarian and as Librarians' Council Page Master.


It was announced that the Research and Professional Development Committee has appointed Ginny Sylvester to fill the vacant position created by Tom Turner’s resignation. The chair also introduced some recent library hires at ASU Libraries: Jody Foote, Qunying Li, Jeanne Miller, Sheila Young, and Smita Parkhe.


III. Dean's Report

 

The Dean thanked Pat Etter for her contributions as Chair last year and presented her with a ceremonial gavel.


The Dean made several announcements: (a) The ASU Libraries Remote Storage Facility project is underway and slated for the ASU EAST campus. (b) ASU Libraries MAIN acquired the symbolic three millionth volume for the libraries collection. (c) The Virtual Reference Service software for remote reference has been purchased and a pilot project is underway by the Remote Reference Task Force. (d) The third stage (AP midrange salaries) in librarian salary adjustments will be addressed this year (effective January 1, 2002). (e) The Dean praised all of the new initiatives and improvements in library services that we undertook this year. As an example, she stressed the value of having the new document delivery service available during the 2001 serials cancellation.


The Dean wished the council assembly good luck for the upcoming year. A question and answer session followed.


IV. Committee Reports

 

Personnel Committee - Scott Herrington

The Committee is in the process of placing the "Marion the Librarian" packets on the Librarians Council Web site.

Review Committee - Annette Voth

The Committee has finalized its calendar and will begin work on sabbatical requests.

Research and Professional Development Committee - Ginny Sylvester

The committee reminded council of this year’s deadline for submitting application request for travel funds. This year’s budget request has been approved and signed by the Dean. A $3000 research fund was also announced.

Compensation Committee - Lois Schneberger

The committee has appointed a chair and finalized a meeting calendar for this year.

Academic Senate

No report

Committee on Academic Professional Status - Annie Platoff

CAPS includes six committee members this year, including one academic associate. Librarians that are on the committee include: Annie Platoff and Linda Shackle. Some CAPS agenda items include presentations from Arizona Regents University, a presentation on the grievance process, and a discussion on the role of academic associates in the university community.

Page Master - Annie Platoff

The Page Master noted that she has updated the online Librarians' Handbook, including all revisions for 2001. This year the Page Master will be working with the Personnel Committee to upload the, “Marion the Librarian,” Web pages to the online Librarians’ Handbook. This year the Page Master hopes to create a manual or procedural guide for future Page Masters.


V. Old Business

 

None.


VI. New Business

 

Nomination and Election of the Nominations Committee

Sherrie Schmidt nominated Greg Green for the Nominations and Elections Committee, and Annie Platoff nominated JoAnn Mulvihill. Both accepted the nomination. Ginny Sylvester was also nominated but she declined the nomination. Nominations were closed. The motion was seconded and approved. It was unanimously approved that Greg and JoAnn serve on the Nominations and Elections Committee, along with the past chair of Librarians’ Council, Pat Etter. As past Chair of Librarians’ Council, Pat Etter will chair the Nominations and Elections Committee.


Meeting Adjourned at 3:29


Mimmo Bonanni, Secretary
