POLYTECHNIC CAMPUS CLASSIFIED STAFF COUNCIL

The council is advisory to the vice president and provost on policies and programs to promote and enhance the classified staff of the Polytechnic campus. The council works to improve the employment environment, policies and procedures affecting classified staff, and the general welfare and equal opportunity of classified staff. It promotes organizational and personal development and enhances internal communication among classified staff, other campuses, universities, and local communities. The council consists of 11 members, ten of whom are elected from campus units and one appointed by the vice president and provost. (Three-year term.)
