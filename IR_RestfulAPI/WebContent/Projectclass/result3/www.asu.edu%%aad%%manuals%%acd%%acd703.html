ACD 703: Report of Illness, Accident, or Death

Purpose

To provide for timely notification of appropriate persons in the event of illness, accident, or death

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

Illness, Accident, or Serious Injury

In case of illness, accident or serious injury of a faculty member or academic professional, the chair/director of a unit should be notified immediately. If an injury is serious, the President’s Office also should be notified at once. If the serious injury occurs on campus, the chair/director of a unit should notify the ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) immediately. If the condition results in extended absence, the chair/director should notify the dean of the college and the executive vice president and provost of the university.

Death

If a member of the faculty or an academic professional dies, the chair/director of a unit and the President’s Office should be notified at once. If the death occurs on campus, the chair/director of a unit should notify ASU DPS and Human Resources. The Human Resources Department will expedite the final payroll check and insurance claim by assisting the family and department in the preparation of necessary forms. To reduce confusion, units are requested to coordinate all assistance with the Human Resources Department. For advice on matters such as life insurance and retirement benefits, the family should be referred to the Human Resources Department.

If death occurs as a result of an accident during the course of employment, the unit chair/director should confer with the director of Human Resources and file an Industrial Commission Accident Report form for workers’ compensation.

Death of a Family Member

In the event of the death of a member of the immediate family of a faculty member or academic professional, the unit chair/director or division head should notify the President’s Office.
