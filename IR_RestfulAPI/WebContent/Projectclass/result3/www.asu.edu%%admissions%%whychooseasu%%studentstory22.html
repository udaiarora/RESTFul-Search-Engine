Jenelle - Communication

"ASU provides students every academic tool and connection, so we can succeed in the best possible way."

Jenelle is currently pursuing her degree in Communication in the Hugh Downs School of Communication. She chose ASU "because of its outstanding communication school and research facilities within the communication field." One of her favorite ASU memories was being able to meet Hugh Downs and ask him personal questions.

The piece of advice Jenelle would most like to give to prospective students is to become involved within a community at ASU right away. She has followed her own advice by joining Public Relations Student Society of America, Undergraduate Student Government and becoming a Resident Assistant to name a few. Jenelle is also a public relations intern at Orca Communications Unlimited in Phoenix, Arizona.

The most important thing for Jenelle is the immersion of culture into the workforce, and she was recently presented an opportunity to work for MTV - Tres, the new MTV Spanish channel. Jenelle's professors and teaching assistants have helped her evaluate her personal and career goals to shape her learning experience into a life experience.

"The faculty at ASU are so passionate!"
