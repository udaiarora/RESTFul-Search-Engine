SAT

Scholastic Aptitude Test


Information
The Scholastic Aptitude Test is widely used to help determine eligibility for undergraduate college admission. SAT bulletins are available for pickup in our office or visit www.collegeboard.com for more information.
Schedule
Available Dates for Exam
  • January 27, 2007
  • March 10, 2007
Cost
The Scholastic Aptitude Test costs $41.50.
Time
To find out the new format for the SAT, please visit www.collegeboard.com.
Register
Registration deadlines are typically five weeks prior to the test. To register online with a credit card, visit www.collegeboard.com or obtain a registration bulletin which is available for pickup in our office.
Reschedule
You must complete a worksheet in the SAT bulletin and contact customer service by the Wednesday 2 1/2 weeks before the test date to reschedule. There is a rescheduling fee of $20.50.
Retake
You must wait 60 days before retaking the exam.
Admission
Walk in testing is permitted, however there is an additional $36.50 fee and space is not guaranteed. Regular exam entrance requires an admission ticket along with a photo ID.
