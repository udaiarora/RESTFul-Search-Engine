Greetings!

Welcome to the Arizona State University (ASU) Department of Public Safety web site. Thank you for visiting us. My name is John L. Pickens. I serve as your Director and Chief of Police with great pride. This is a great place to learn, work, or just visit. It is our role to help you enjoy a safe and friendly environment.

We are an internationally accredited police agency and provide police services for all ASU campuses. ASU Police Officers are State Certified and have the same powers as any police officer in the State of Arizona. The Arizona Board of Regents and the President of ASU are empowered to organize, provide for, and operate a police department. (ARS 1-215)

ASU Police is responsible for and has agency jurisdiction for law enforcement matters occurring on property that is under the control of the Arizona Board of Regents and on the main campus of ASU. ASU Police has primary jurisdictional responsibility for property within the main campus core boundaries. Mutual aid agreements with the City of Tempe allow officers of ASU Police to give appropriate secondary attention to traffic control and enforcement on the streets that run through or are contiguous with ASU properties. The authority of police officers of ASU may extend to any place within the state under state statute. (ARS 13-3871)

The next time you see ASU Police Officers, say hello. We are being proactive and attempting to build partnerships. We treat everyone with respect and dignity and we need your support, cooperation and trust to create and maintain a partnership for a safe community.

Professionally Yours,

John L. Pickens
Director/Chief of Police
