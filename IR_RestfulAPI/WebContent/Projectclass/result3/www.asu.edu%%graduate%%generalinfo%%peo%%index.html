P.E.O. Scholar Awards


DEADLINES
October 31, 2006. Late nominations will be accepted, but the DGS cannot guarantee that they will be reviewed by the local P.E.O. chapter.

PURPOSE
P.E.O. Scholar Awards are one-time competitive awards for women of the United States or Canada who are either pursuing a doctoral degree or engaged in advanced study or research (master's applications are not being considered this year). In addition to recognizing and encouraging excellence in higher education, these awards provide partial support for study and research for women who will make significant contributions in their varied fields of endeavor. Priority is given to women who are well established in their graduate programs or advanced study or research. www.peointernational.org/projects/policies-psa.php

CONTACT
Pat Saldivar, Division of Graduate Studies, at psaldivar@asu.edu or 965-5906.

AWARD
The P.E.O. Scholar Awards are given once each year for the ensuing academic year. This year, $10,000 will be awarded to provide partial support for a year's study. The number and amount of the awards are determined each year in accordance with the funds available.

ELIGIBILITY
An eligible applicant must:
  • be a citizen of the United States or Canada at the time of nomination;
  • have at least one academic year remaining to complete her doctoral degree or research from the date the awards are granted (Aug. 1);
  • be within two years of completing her doctoral degree or advanced study or research at the time the awards are granted (Aug.1; graduation may not take place before May of 2008 and no later than May 2009);
  • be a full time graduate student;
  • demonstrate academic excellence, maturity, and seriousness of purpose; and
  • have clearly defined and realistically obtainable career objectives.
Applicants from Law, Education, Sciences, and the Arts are especially encouraged to apply.


APPLICATION PROCESS
Eligible students first complete the pre-application (below) and submit it electronically by email to Pat Saldivar (psaldivar@asu.edu) by October 31, 2006. The pre-applications will be forwarded to the local P.E.O. chapter, who will then invite selected individuals to submit a full application to the national organization.
