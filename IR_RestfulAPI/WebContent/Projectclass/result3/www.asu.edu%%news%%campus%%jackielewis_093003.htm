September 30, 2003

Foundation names new vice president

Jackie Lewis will be joining the ASU Foundation senior management team as vice president, constituent development, beginning Oct. 13. An effective leader and accomplished development professional, Lewis is currently president of the Provena Saint Joseph Medical Center Foundation in Joliet, Ill. Under her leadership, the Foundation quadrupled its assets in just two years.

At the ASU Foundation, Lewis will oversee and directly supervise the fund-raising activities and staff on all three ASU campuses and in all colleges, intercollegiate athletics, extended education and public events.

"Jackie will provide the focus, leadership and mentoring necessary to support highly effective college-based fund-raising teams," says Jeff Schemmel, senior vice president and executive director of development for ASU. "She is experienced in all aspects of fund-raising and brings great energy and expertise."

"Jackie's senior management experience will enhance the team we already have in place to lead the Foundation and support President Crow's goals for ASU as a new American university," says Lonnie Ostrom, ASU Foundation president.

As president of the Provena Saint Joseph Medical Center Foundation, Lewis also has been serving on the six-member senior management team for the Medical Center, providing leadership and direction for the 2,200-employee, $250 million annual enterprise.

Over the past 15 years, she has provided leadership for multiple statewide and regional fund-raising campaigns, primarily for non-profit enterprises. She has expertise in developing major gift solicitation and cultivation strategies, securing lead gifts and organizing record-breaking annual giving campaigns and fund-raising events.

Prior to her success with Provena Saint Joseph, she was director of major gifts for Mercy Foundation in Des Moines, Iowa. There, she served on the team that produced the top three years in the Foundation's fund-raising history. As development director for the American Diabetes Association in Texas, she designed and implemented a campaign that increased annual support by 150 percent and the donor base by 200 percent. She recorded similar fund-raising achievements for the Iowa affiliate of the American Diabetes Association, the Iowa Family Policy Center, the Republican Party of Iowa and various political campaigns.

Bruce, with the ASU Foundation, can be reached at (480) 965-4429 or (maggie.bruce@asu.edu).
