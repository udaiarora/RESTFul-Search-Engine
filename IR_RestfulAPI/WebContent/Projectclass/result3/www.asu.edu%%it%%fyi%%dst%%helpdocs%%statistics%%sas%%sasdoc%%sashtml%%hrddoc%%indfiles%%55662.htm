SAS Today! A Year of Terrific Tips

by Helen Carey and Ginger Carey

Learn day-by-day and have fun doing it! This perpetual calendar will carry you through the year with a tip every day, some providing useful SAS System information and some just helpful advice on planning your time and using your skills to their best advantage. SAS users at all levels will benefit from the tips included in this calendar; tips cover such areas as inputting data, debugging, using functions and formats, making the most of the SAS display manager, finding SAS information on the web, ordering SAS documentation, and keeping up with SUGI and the regional conferences. Use it as a reference tool with the help of its extensive index, or just take it a day at a time. The calendar stands on your desk, pages flip easily because of colorful spiral binding, and Oscar, the cartoon character, illustrates the tips with humor that will keep you smiling.

396 pages.
