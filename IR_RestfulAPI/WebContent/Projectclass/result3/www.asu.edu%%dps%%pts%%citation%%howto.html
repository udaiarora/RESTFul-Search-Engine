How to Make an Appeal

IN PERSON

Please bring the citation to the Appeals Office located in the Towers, Room 107, at 525 S. Forest Avenue. Office hours extend from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM (MST), Monday through Friday, excluding University holidays.

QUESTIONS?

Any questions regarding a citation can be directed to ASU Parking and Transit Services, Appeals Office: (480) 965-4527
