DPS 101–01: Reporting an Emergency

Purpose

To define appropriate action in the event of an emergency

Source

Arizona State University Emergency Operations Manual

Policy

Anyone discovering or having information related to an emergency situation, regardless of type or size, should immediately notify the ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) by dialing 911, or by activating one of the many emergency call boxes located throughout each campus. If directed by ASU DPS, the person discovering the emergency will notify others in the area who may be affected, through word of mouth or by activating the nearest fire alarm.

Note:Dialing 911 from an on-campus phone or activating an emergency call box will connect the caller directly to the ASU DPS Communications Center. There is no need to dial the campus prefixes 3, 5, or 7 before dialing 911, including on-campus pay telephones.

The ASU DPS Communications Center is responsible for notifying other departments and appropriate university personnel, and for providing assistance as needed. ASU DPS will respond to the incident, secure the area to prevent injury, block unauthorized access, and preserve evidence.

Cross-Reference

For more information on how to report emergencies, see DPS 101–02, “Emergency Call Boxes.”
