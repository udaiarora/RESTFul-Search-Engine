ACD 204–05: Sales by Faculty or Academic Professionals to the University

Purpose

To ensure fair purchasing procedures for materials, services, and equipment

Sources

Arizona Revised Statutes § 38–503(C)
University policy

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

The university will not purchase any supplies, materials, equipment, and/or contractor-type services from a faculty member or academic professional unless the purchase:

  1. results from a competitive bidding procedure

    and

  2. has been approved by the appropriate provost or vice president(s).

Cross-References

For the university’s complete and definitive policy on conflict of interest, see ACD 204–08, “Conflict of Interest.”

For detailed information about bidding requirements, see the Purchasing and Business Services Policies and Procedures Manual (PUR).
