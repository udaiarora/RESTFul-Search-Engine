---. The Pre-Raphaelites. London: Studio Vista, 1970. This work provides a general introduction to both the art and the philosophy of the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood, attempting to focus on the uniqueness of the group while still placing it within the context of the historical period. The paintings of Rossetti, Hunt and Millais are examined in detail, and the contributions of other less well-known members and associates of the Brotherhood are also considered. The last chapter of the work examines the successors to the Pre-Raphaelites, relating them to the Aesthetic Movement and Art Nouveau. This work has numerous illustrations, including some in color.
