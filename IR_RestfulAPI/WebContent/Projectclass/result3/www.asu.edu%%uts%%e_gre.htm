GRE

Graduate Record Examination - General Test

Information
The Graduate Record Examinations general test is taken by individuals applying to graduate schools. GRE testing policies and procedures, bulletins as well as free test prep software are available at www.gre.org. Registration, rescheduling and cancellation can now be done online at www.ets.org/register.html.
Schedule
The GRE general test is a computer based test given year round. Test appointments are usually scheduled in the mornings, Wednesday through Saturday. Occasionally, afternoon appointments may also be available. The reporting time for admission is always 30 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. Dates and times may vary at different times of the year.
Cost
The current fee for the GRE general test is $130.00. 
Time
The exam consists of analytical, verbal and quantitative sections. The total appointment time is four hours.
Register
GRE registration is now available online at www.ets.org/register.html. You can also register by calling the GRE national registration line at 1-800-473-2255.
There are two ways to register through our office:
In Person with a check, money order, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover or JCB.
By Phone with Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover or JCB.
Reschedule
Once the appointment is made, notification must be given three full days prior to your test date to reschedule for a $40.00 fee or to cancel for a partial refund of $60.00. The three full days does not include the day of notification or the day of the appointment. For example, you must give notification on Monday to reschedule or cancel a Friday appointment.
Retake
You can take the exam once per calendar month, but no more than five times in any 12 month period.
Admission
The reporting time for admission is always 30 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. You must present a valid ID containing name, a recent photo and signature to be admitted to test. If you are testing outside of your country of citizenship, you must present a valid passport. Please see specific ID requirements in the GRE bulletin.

Graduate Record Examination - Subject Test

Information
The GRE Subject Tests gauge undergraduate achievement in specific disciplines. Each Subject Test in intended for students who have majored in or have extensive background in that specific area. The disciplines include: Biochemistry; Cell and Molecular Biology; Biology; Chemistry; Computer Science; Literature in English; Mathematics; Physics; and Psychology.
