The Cognition, Action, Perception program at ASU offers a unique blend of expertise in a variety of arenas, ranging from traditional perspectives and research topics in Experimental Psychology to unique, cutting-edge approaches and specializations. Moreover, our area fosters extensive interdisciplinary collaboration among the faculty and students both within our group and across other departments. Students interested in joining our program are encouraged to learn more about faculty research and to contact  faculty directly with any questions or comments.
