Maps and Directions

The Office of the Executive Vice President and CFO, Business and Finace, is located on the third floor of the Fulton Building, Suite 320. The Fulton Building is located at 300 E. University Drive in Tempe, on the northeast corner of University Drive and College Street.

Parking Information

Visitor parking is available in the parking structure directly north of the Fulton Building. The rate is $2/hour with a maximum exit fee of $8. Please take your ticket with you.

Additional parking information and maps are available from Parking and Transit Services.
