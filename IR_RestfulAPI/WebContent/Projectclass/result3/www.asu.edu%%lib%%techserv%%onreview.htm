Materials on Review

 


Materials go on review each Monday at 2:30 p.m. in the review area in Collection Development.  All materials are on review for one week, except materials that need reference location decisions.


Moving materials to and from the review area takes approximately 3.75 staff hours per week (3 people, 1.25 hours).
