The Science & Engineering of Materials Program is an interdisciplinary Graduate Program administered by the Graduate College and in cooperation with the College of Engineering and the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences. The program offers an MS in Material Science and Ph.D. in Science and Engineering of Materials
