Privacy Policy

The Learning and Performance Solutions Group creates and maintains a training transcript for each employee who enrolls in any of the offered courses. The training transcript may be accessed only by the individual employee and the systems administrator. The training transcript is intended to serve as an aid in helping employees keep track of courses taken and in identifying future training opportunities. Supervisors will not be provided with access to an individual employee's training transcript, absent an employee's written consent. However, if an employee is given release time to attend a training course, HR can confirm to a supervisor the fact that the employee attended or did not attend a training session on a given date and time. Additionally, if an employee is reporting completed training to a supervisor in an annual self-evaluation or other personnel related matter, HR can confirm to the supervisor the fact that the employee did or did not complete each of the training courses listed.

For additional information on exactly what supervisors are eligible to review, please see SPP 1101.

For additional information, please contact the Learning and Performance Solutions Group.
