Human Resources - Leadership Development Program

The Associate and Assistant Deans Council

Purpose

The Associate and Assistant Deans Council hosts forums in which these administrators may meet to share ideas and expertise. Because they must problem solve across disciplines, colleges and populations, it is especially valuable for this very diverse group to build collegiality and mutual trust. Their steering committee proposes topics and speakers for monthly meetings during the academic year and plans their annual retreat. The council also hosts internal presenters to discuss current University initiatives as well as outside speakers who can add to their knowledge about issues of higher education.

Activities of the Associate and Assistant Deans Council are planned by an eight member committee with support from the Office of the Provost and are coordinated by a designated Human Resource coordinator through HR Programs.
