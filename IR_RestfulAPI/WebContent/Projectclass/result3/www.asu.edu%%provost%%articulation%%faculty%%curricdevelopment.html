ASU Tools and Information

  • ASU Online Course Search
          ASU online catalogue offers a complete list of course at all ASU campuses
  • New! Curriculum and Academic Programs Committee (CAPC)
          Access to meeting minutes and approvals regarding Academic Programs (CAPC) and new courses (TCCS): Tempe and Downtown campuses
  • CEG Advanced Query
           Course Equivalency Guide Advanced Query provides a list of current direct equivalencies to the ASU course. Enter the ASU prefix and number (separated by a space) in the ASU Equivalency field to search for all direct equivalencies in the current CEG. Use the drop down box to search past CEG's or narrow your search to a specific community college using the Source Institution field.
  • Common Course Search Tool
          This tool can alert you if an ASU course about to be modified or deleted is a statewide "common course". Used when processing ASU course modifications/deletions via ACRES.
  • Criteria for Upper/Lower-Division Courses
          Approved by the Academic Program Articulation Steering Committee (APASC)
  • Curriculum Development

  • General and Graduate Catalog

  • General Studies Course Query (ASU Courses)
