Getting Started: An Unofficial Checklist
  • I know the ASU Campus and am able to find my way around easily.
    If you don't know the campus well, take a FREE campus tour: Student Services Building C118 or call 480.965.2677.

  • I have had my official transcripts sent to ASU and have an unofficial copy to show my academic advisor.
    If you haven't already done so, you must request--in writing or in person-- that your official transcript be sent directly to ASU from each college or university you have attended.  Also request that an unofficial copy of your transcripts be sent to you, and keep it for your own records.  You may also need to submit a high school transcript, test scores, or other documents. Check with the Undergraduate Admissions Office at 480.965.7788.

  • I have provided proof of measles immunization / immunity.
    If you were born after December 31, 1956, you need to show proof of measles (rubeola) immunity or immunization given after January 1, 1980 BEFORE YOU CAN REGISTER for classes. Call Student Health for more information at 965-1358.

  • I have met with an academic advisor.
    You may need to meet with an academic advisor even before you are admitted to the university.  If you have been accepted into a college, contact your department or college to make an appointment.  If you are unsure or want to explore different areas of interest, contact the University College Center for Academic Advising at 480.965.4464.
    • College of Architecture and Environmental Design
    • College of Business
    • College of Education
    • College of Engineering and Applied Sciences
    • College of Fine Arts
    • College of Liberal Arts and Sciences
    • College of Nursing
    • College of Public Programs
    • School of Social Work
    • University Honors College

  • I have visited the ASU Adult Re-Entry Center.
    Visit the Adult Re-Entry Center in the Memorial Union, Lower Level, to find out what we can do to help you be successful at ASU.
