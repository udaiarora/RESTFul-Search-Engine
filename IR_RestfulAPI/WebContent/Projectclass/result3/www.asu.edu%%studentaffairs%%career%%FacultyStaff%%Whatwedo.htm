What We Do . . .

  • Assist students to systematically analyze college and non-college experiences and incorporate this information into career-related decisions
  • Provide career guidance services that help students select major fields of study and relate these to career fields
  • Provide Career Life Planning information that focuses on factors that influence career choices over the life span
  • Assist students employment through a wide range of services, including: projected job markets, overall employment statistics, job search strategies, interview skills training, job fairs, and on-campus interviewing
