The individual must provide an ASU ID card (or driver's license), license plate number, and vehicle make, model, and color when requesting a temporary parking permit from Decal Sales, Parking and Transit Services (PTS).

The Decal Sales Office verifies the requestor’s ownership of a valid ASU decal and issues a temporary parking permit for the authorized lot, providing a duplicate of the temporary permit for the Appeals Section, PTS.

Note: Non-decal holders will be charged.

The individual displays the temporary parking permit in the vehicle as directed.
