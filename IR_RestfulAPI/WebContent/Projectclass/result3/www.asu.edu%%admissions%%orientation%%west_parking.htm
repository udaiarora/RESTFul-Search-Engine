ASU at the West campus: Glendale, AZ

Check-In Information
All Orientation programs at ASU's West campus are from 8:00 a.m. to approximately 3:00 p.m. Check-in begins at 8:00 a.m. Optional campus tours will be offered in the afternoon.

Important Notes
All students planning to take the Calculus Placement exam should arrive at 7:15 a.m. with their photo ID to have enough time to be checked-in for the exam.

All students admitted to the Barrett Honors College need to attend the 8:30 a.m. Barrett Honors College Welcome.

Students must participate in the full day on-campus Orientation program to complete the registration process.

Accommodations
For information on hotels close to ASU, contact the Glendale Office of Tourism & Visitor Center 877.225.5749 or visit www.visitglendale.com.

Directions to ASU's West campus
ASU's West campus is located on West Thunderbird Road between 43rd and 51st Avenues.

From Tempe : Drive north on Rural Road across the Salt River bridge. Take the Loop 202 (Red Mountain Freeway) west to I-10 (Maricopa Freeway). Stay west on I-10 through downtown Phoenix and take I-17 (Black Canyon Freeway) north (about 10 miles) to the Thunderbird Road exit. Proceed west on Thunderbird Road to the 47th Avenue entrance to campus. Follow the signs for visitor parking.

From Scottsdale : Take Cactus Road west to the 47th Avenue entrance to campus. ( Cactus Road changes its name to Thunderbird Road when it crosses Cave Creek Road ). Follow the signs for visitor parking.

Alternatively, take the Loop 101 (Pima and Agua Fria freeways) north and west to I-17 (Black Canyon Freeway). Go south on I-17 to the Thunderbird Road exit. Proceed west on Thunderbird Road to the 47th Avenue entrance to campus. Follow the signs for visitor parking.

From Sun City : Take Bell Road east to 51st Avenue . Turn right (south) and proceed to Thunderbird Road . Turn left (east) on Thunderbird Road and proceed to the 47th Avenue entrance to campus. Follow the signs for visitor parking.

From the North : Take I-17 (Black Canyon Freeway) south to the Thunderbird Road exit. Proceed west on Thunderbird Road to the 47th Avenue entrance to campus. Follow the signs for visitor parking.

From the South : Take I-10 (Maricopa Freeway) west through downtown Phoenix and take I-17 (Black Canyon Freeway) north to the Thunderbird Road exit. Proceed west on Thunderbird Road to the 47th Avenue entrance to campus. Follow the signs for visitor parking.

If you're coming from ASU's Tempe campus, you may be interested in taking the Express Shuttle Service .
