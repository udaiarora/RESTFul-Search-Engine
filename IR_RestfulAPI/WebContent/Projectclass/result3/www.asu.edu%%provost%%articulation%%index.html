Articulation agreements specify how transfer credit will be accepted from one institution to another and may be written in terms of specific courses, or even partnerships involving entire programs. These agreements offer the student an opportunity to more effectively plan their academic career, minimize loss of credit, and avoid repeated coursework at the receiving institution.
