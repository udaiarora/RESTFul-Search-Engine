Published a review of Ecology Abstracts for the committee Bibliography committee of ANSS. Review was published in the ANSS Newsletter spring 2001 edition, and reprinted on the Cambridge Scientific Abstracts Web site (CSA).

 

Bennett, D.B., et al. (2001). RUSA review underway: your survey responses. Reference and User Services Quarterly, 40, 352-360.
Member of the Organization Committee for RUSA, two-year term (2001-2002). Published a survey article on RUSA and its role within ALA, "RUSA Review Underway," in the Summer 2001 issue of Reference User Services Quarterly. The article presented the groups completed survey on RUSA and RUSA's role as a member of ALA.

Exhibit focused on the Southeast Asian textile collection of anthropologist Jane Richardson Hanks, April-August 2000.
