Herb Journal.

[Vol. 1] no. 1 (Oct. 1936)-Vol. 3, no. 12 (Dec. 1939). New Rochelle, N.Y.: Rosetta E. Clarkson, 1936-1939. 3 v. Monthly serial. Distinctive title: Magic Fragrance, v. 1; Sachets & Seeds, v. 2; Witches' Garden, v. 3. Place of publication varies: Milford, Conn., -Dec. 1939. Issues also sold bound into volumes with distinctive titles.

Patten collection has loose issues: v. 3: 8-12.

Each issue is devoted to a specific subject, such as salads or medicinal herbs or flowers in food.  Practical information is given on the particular topic, as well as historical details and literary quotations.  The final issue deals with the role of herbs in modern health and it includes a chart listing the importance of herbs beginning with the Chaldeans in 5000 B.C.

Subjects: Herbs--Periodicals.
