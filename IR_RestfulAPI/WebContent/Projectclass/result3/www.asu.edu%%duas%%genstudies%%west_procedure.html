Procedure for Requesting a General Studies Designation at ASU West

In order for an ASU West course to receive a General Studies designation, a proposal must be submitted to the ASU West Senate Curriculum and Programs / General Studies Committee (SCP/GSC). SCP/GSC meetings are held once a month during the academic year. 

In order for a course to be considered by the SCP/GSC, appropriate materials must be submitted to the ASU West Curriculum Office one month prior to a scheduled SCP/GSC meeting. The following paperwork is required for each new proposal:

  1. Course Proposal Cover Form
  2. Criteria Checklist for General Studies area(s) being requested.
  3. Course catalog description
  4. Course syllabus - mandatory Generic Syllabus required
  5. Table of contents from any required textbook(s) and/or list of required readings/books
  6. Description of how the course meets the specific criteria in the area for which the course is being proposed (included on the generic syllabus or completed on a separate sheet).

Submit the completed paperwork to:

Julie Ramsden
Curriculum Office
Faculty and Administration Building (FAB) N301-1
Mail Code 1251
(602)543-4509
Email: ramsden@asu.edu

The Course Proposal Cover Forms and Criteria Checklists can be printed from the Web using the links in items 1 and 2 above. They are also available by contacting the Curriculum Office.

In general, the effective dates for approved courses are the first listing of those courses in the spring or fall Schedule of Classes. Therefore, courses approved before May 1 will be effective the following spring semester. Courses approved before Februa ry 1 will be effective the following fall semester.
