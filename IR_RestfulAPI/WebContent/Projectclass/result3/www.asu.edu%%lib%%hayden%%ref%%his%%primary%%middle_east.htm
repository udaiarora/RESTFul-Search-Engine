Primary Sources in History: Middle East

293. ISLAM FICHE.  CALL NUMBER: Microfiche DS 36.8 I84x  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:  INDEX OR GUIDE:  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Readings from Islamic primary sources in English translation. Includes history, religion and culture from origins to 20th century. Have first series, which comprises 1/5 of the total collection. Fiche is in a binder.  SUBJECTS: Islam; Middle East; 7c; 8c; 9c; 10c; 11c; 13c; 14c; 15c; 16c; 17c; 18c; 19c; 20c

294. MIDDLE EAST DEVELOPMENT DOCUMENTS ON MICROFICHE.  CALL NUMBER: University of Arizona Microfiche 895  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:  INDEX OR GUIDE: Microfiche Guide 895. A series of cumulative catalogs, updated periodically. Arranged alphabetically by country. Provides document number needed to locate the microfiche.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Consists of officical almanacs, statistical compilations, economic and geographic surveys, as well as annual reports and developmental studies published by domestic and foreign banks, investment houses and industrial concerns. Information on the principal states, emirates and sheikdoms of North Africa and the Near East provides documentation for comprehensive comparative studies of regional developments in the region.  SUBJECTS: Middle East; Near East; 20c

295. OSS. STATE DEPT. INTELLIGENCE AND RESEARCH REPORTS, PT. 7. THE MIDDLE EAST, 1941-1949.  CALL NUMBER: Doc.Serv. Microfilm DS 63 M52x  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 3 R  INDEX OR GUIDE; DS 63 M52x Guide. Reel/subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Declassified reports on economic, political and military situation. Covers Middle East as a region plus the following countries: Afghanistan, Egypt, Iran, Kuwait, Levant States, Libya, Palestine, Saudi Arabia, Syria, Transjordan, Turkey. Documents the role of international oil companies in the region and provides information on the conflicts between Arabs and Jews. The University of Arizona has the 1950-1961 supplement to this set (Microfilm 2956).  SUBJECTS: Middle East; World War II; Near East; 20c; 1940s

296. SPECIAL STUDIES SERIES. THE MIDDLE EAST, 1970-1980.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 8813  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 18 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 63 M516x Guide Micro Ref. Reel/subject index. Contains abstracts of reports.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Studies commissioned by government from “think tanks,” on problems of the region and individual countries. Time period is 1945-1980. Topics include: Arab-Israeli conflict, the military situation and internal politics, economic development, foreign policy, superpower involvement, Persian Gulf region, Palestinian problem, terrorism and petroleum. Covers Middle East as a region plus the following countries: Egypt, Iran, Iraq, Israel, Jordan, Lebanon, Oman, Palestine, Saudi Arabia, Syria, Turkey, and Yemen.  SUBJECTS: Middle East; Near East; 20c
