Mapping FAME Frequencies to SAS Time Intervals

The following table summarizes the mapping of Fame frequencies to SAS time intervals. It is important to note that FAME frequencies often have a sample unit in parentheses following the keyword frequency. This sample unit is an end-of-interval unit. SAS dates are represented using begin-of-interval notation. For more on SAS time intervals, see "Date Intervals, Formats, and Functions" in your SAS/ETS User's Guide. For more on FAME frequencies, see "Understanding Frequencies" in your USER'S GUIDE TO FAME.
