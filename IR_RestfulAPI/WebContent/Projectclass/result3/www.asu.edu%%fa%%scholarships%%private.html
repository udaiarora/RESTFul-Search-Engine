Private Donor Scholarships

Employers, private individuals, organizations and corporations are all contributors to private scholarships. These scholarships are awarded through the Scholarship Office and through departments within the colleges. Some private scholarships are noted below, plus additional scholarships can be found on the departmental/college scholarship web page. A student seeking to apply for a private scholarship must complete the proper scholarship application(s).

Private Donor Scholarship Listing The following list of scholarships is maintained by the ASU Foundation. Some require the ASU General Scholarship Application to be considered for, while others do not (list is grouped by this requirement).

Carefully read the criteria for each scholarship to prevent being eliminated from the selection process.
