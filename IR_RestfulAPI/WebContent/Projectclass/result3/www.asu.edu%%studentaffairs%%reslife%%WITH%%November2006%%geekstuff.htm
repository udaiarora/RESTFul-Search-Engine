Why Clean Access Agent?


Many students often call the Reslife Computer Support Helpdesk with the question “Why do we have to have Clean Access Agent installed to get on the internet?” The answer is simple -- for security. You wouldn’t sleep with your door open at night, or leave your room open when you go to class. Clean Access Agent is just that, a doorway to the network or internet. Clean Access Again simply is a check tool. It performs network validation, in simple terms it looks at your computer to see if the computer has the minimum security standards (antivirus updates) and windows updates to access ASU’s residential network. In no way does the Clean Access Agent harm your computer or delete files. Daily student computers are introduced to the campus environment which may have harmful viruses. The Reslife Helpdesk uses “Clean Access Agent” as a tool to help protect your computer as well as all ASU’s network. If you have question or problem with connectivity please feel free to contact the Helpdesk at 480-965-8565 or email us at rl.helpdesk@asu.edu

Thanks for your support,
