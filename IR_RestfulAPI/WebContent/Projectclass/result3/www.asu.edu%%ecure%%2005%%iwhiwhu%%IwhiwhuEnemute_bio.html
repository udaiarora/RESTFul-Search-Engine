Enemute Iwhiwhu
Assistant Lecturer
Delta State University, Abraka Nigeria
Records and Information Resource Management in A Private Organization: A Case Study of Shell Petroleum Development Company

Bio

Mr. Iwhiwhu, Enemute Basil is an Assistant Lecturer with the Department of Library and Information Science in Delta State University, Abraka Nigeria. He obtained a B.Sc. (Ed) Honours Degree in Chemistry from the defunct Bendel State University, Ekpoma, 1991 and Mater Degree in Archives and Records management (MAS) from the University of Ibadan, 1998. He worked as a practicing Librarian for ten years before moving to the Department of Library and Information Science in the same University.
