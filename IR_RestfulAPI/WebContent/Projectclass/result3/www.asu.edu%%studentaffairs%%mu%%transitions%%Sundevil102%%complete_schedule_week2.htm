New Danceworks I
7:30 p.m.
Dance Studio Theatre, PE East 132
Martha Howe takes an interdisciplinary approach to dance setting choreography influenced by prose and poetry, including some of her own literary work. Sara Parish includes video and other media in her exploration of human relationships through motifs on a structure. The inspiration for Ying Yang's work spans the most progressive to the most traditional dance of her native China. Event runs from January 25 through January 28.

Tribal Scholarship Fair
2:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
