Services

ADA Accommodations  

If you have special accommodation needs, please contact us and we will be happy to work with you.

 

  
Beverages

You may request coffee, juice, soda, bottled tea and bottled water for your function. Please call (480) 965-3046 at least 72 hours in advance of your meeting to make arrangements for service. Two lounges are equipped with vending and soda machines. These lounges are located in Building C on the second and third floors.

 
Beverage Items Available

Lipton bottled flavored teas – 16 oz. bottles – $1.25 each
Soda – 12 oz. cans – $1.00 each (Pepsi-branded and distributed products)
Premium bottled water – 20 oz. bottles – $1.00 each
Ocean Spray juices – 16 oz. bottles – $1.50 each cranberry, apple, orange
Starbucks coffee – initial order, $.95 per cup/refills, $.75 per cup

Catering

Your meeting may be catered by any of the Mercado-approved caterers and vendors.

Link to the list of approved caterers (HTML)

If you choose to supply your own food and beverages, your event will be subject to a $30 facility fee. If your event is run by caterers or vendors who are not on the approved list, it will be subject to a $50 facility fee.

 
Ethernet/Wireless

Ethernet connections are available in all classrooms; the IP address, gateway and subnet for the room will be provided. The cost to use an Ethernet connection in the classroom is $125 per day. In addition, technical support is available to set up Ethernet connectivity. Our technical support team will set up 10baseT connections (converters for BNC coaxial cables are not available). The cost for technical support is $60 per half hour and should be scheduled at least two weeks prior to your event. For your convenience, we honor late requests for technical support. The cost for late requests is $80 per half hour.

 
Parking

Effective July 17, 2006 all reservations will be subject to a $5 facility use/parking fee per person. This fee covers tables, chairs and room set-up, and one parking validation per person for the Heritage and Science Park garage at Fifth and Monroe streets.

Over the years we have been in business we have been able to provide limited free parking for the users of the facility. As our costs have increased, this fee will help us continue to provide you with high quality service.

 
Phone Conferencing

Phone conferencing is available. A Polycom conference phone is available for your needs. Requests need to be made 10 days in advance. Call for additional conferencing details.

 
Photocopy and Fax Services

If you require the use of a copy machine see the receptionist on the second floor in Building C. The cost for photocopying is 10 cents per page and $1 per page for transparencies. For your convenience, we will include these charges in your rental bill or you may pay cash to the receptionist. Fax service is also available upon request. Please see the receptionist for assistance. The cost to send a fax is $1 for the first five pages and $1 for each page thereafter. The cost to receive faxes is 20 cents per page.

 
Public Pay Phones

Public pay phones are located in the lounge areas on the second and third floors of Building C. In addition, meeting rooms are equipped with intercom phones that connect to Building C's second floor receptionist desk. For your convenience, a courtesy phone is located in Building C at the second floor receptionist area.

 
Satellite Downlinks

All classrooms have the ability to receive satellite downlinks. Costs vary depending on your needs and requirements. Call (480) 965-3046 for details.

 
Standard Equipment and Supplies

Each meeting room is equipped with white marker boards; dry-erase markers and projection screens are provided at no cost. Overhead projectors, slide projectors and lecterns also are available upon request. Additional audio/visual equipment is available upon request for a nominal fee.

 
Technical Support for the Computer Lab

Two hours of technical support is provided with a full-day rental to help load software. If additional technical support is needed, the cost is $60 per half-hour and should be scheduled at least two weeks prior to your event. The cost for late requests is $85 per half hour.

 
Web Access Stations  
Web access stations are located on the second and third floors and allow users of the facility a convenient area to check e-mail and access the Internet through a terminal or personal laptop. There is no access to a printer.
