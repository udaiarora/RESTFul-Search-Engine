PCS 1301: Property Closeout for a Completed Contract/Grant

Purpose

To properly handle the property in a contract termination

Source

Property Control

Policy

Property Control is responsible for performing a final equipment inventory for completed contracts and grants that require a final report.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Grant and Contract Accounting
  1. Forward to the sponsored property coordinator an expense budget summary noting the expenditures on the contract or grant.
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Review the expenditures for capital equipment purchases.
If zero dollars were spent in the ASU financial system and zero assets appear on the property system:
  1. Prepare a Negative Property Report and forward to Grant and Contract Accounting.
If dollars were spent on capital assets:
  1. Prepare a Property Control Report for the contract or grant.
  1. Reconcile the Expense Budget Summary and the Property Control Report.
  1. Submit the final property report to Grant and Contract Accounting.
Grant and Contract Accounting
  1. Submit the final property report to the appropriate government/sponsor agencies.
If government-owned property is on a final property report:
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Schedule an appointment with the PI for a physical inventory of the equipment.
  1. Verify property control numbers, model and serial numbers, location, and condition of equipment.
  1. Update the property control system.
  1. Reconcile the account with both the property control system and the university financial system.
When disposition instructions are received from the contracting officer:
  1. Dispose of the property in accordance with the contracting officer’s letter.
