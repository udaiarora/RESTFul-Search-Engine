Advising
 
Our office was established to advise ASU's No Preference students.  Our nine advisors all have advanced degrees (meaning, we know how to graduate from college) and a wide range of experiences in higher education (including teaching, counseling and career development).  You will be assigned an advisor when you schedule your initial advising appointment.  That person will advise you until you choose a major.  

If you know what major you want, visit ASU's Advising Directory to find an advisor in that major.
