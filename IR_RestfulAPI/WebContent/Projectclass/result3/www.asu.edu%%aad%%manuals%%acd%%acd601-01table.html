Health/Dental Selection

Faculty member or academic professional selects and completes the appropriate application for selected coverage at orientation. Human Resources (HR) submits the health insurance application to the State Department of Administration to be forwarded to the company.

Health/Dental Cancellation or Changes

Health and dental coverage cancellation or change is subject to qualifying family status change or open enrollment. The faculty member or academic professional visits HR to complete the necessary documentation. HR stops payroll deduction of the premium if appropriate and notifies the State Department of Administration of the cancellation or change.

Health/Dental Claims (Indemnity)

The faculty member or academic professional completes the appropriate section of the claim form and attaches the original copy of the physician’s or pharmacy’s bill and forwards to carrier.
