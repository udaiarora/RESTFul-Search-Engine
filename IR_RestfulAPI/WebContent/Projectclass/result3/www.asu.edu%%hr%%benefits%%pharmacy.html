Pharmacy
Walgreens Health Initiatives (WHI) is the pharmacy provider
for all Benefit Options medical networks. It is a large, national

network of pharmacies not only in Walgreens stores, but also pharmacies in most other drug store chains, supermarkets and

discount stores. You can look on-line for the location of participating pharmacies anywhere in the country.

Personalized prescription history, drug information and rates can be found at www.mywhi.com or you can call Customer Service at

1.866.722.2141.
