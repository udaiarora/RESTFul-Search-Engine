8th Annual Sun Devil Stars

Nominate a Sun Devil Star
View 2006 and Past Recipients

What is a Sun Devil Star?
They are an undergraduate student at ASU who has exemplified one or all of the following characteristics: makes a positive contribution to the ASU community; demonstrates leadership skills; and serves as a role model to other Sun Devils.

The Cermony
Sun Devil Stars ceremony is held in the spring semester and recognizes and celebrates the contributions of ASU students who are not necessarily in a visible leadership role but who have made a positive impact on the ASU community as leaders and role models.

Procedure
All students who are nominated by ASU faculty and staff are honored at a ceremony held in February. Students are eligible to receive the Sun Devil Star award only once. Please view Sun Devil Star Past Recipients to make sure the student being nominated has not already been recognized. Students will be notified of their nomination and will receive an invitation to the award ceremony. The ASU faculty or staff who nominated the student will also be invited to the event.

Questions?
Please contact Kim Miller at 480-965-9600 with any questions. Sun Devil Stars is sponsored by ASU's Memorial Union.
