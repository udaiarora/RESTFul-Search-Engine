My Account: Email Notifications

 

Why provide your email address?

Providing the library with your email address will allow you to receive library notifications such as overdue, recall and request notices as quickly as possible. When this option is chosen, notices generated by the Libraries’ online system will be sent to you by email. Mail that is not generated by the system will continue to be sent by post or campus mail.

As with printed notices, you are responsible for monitoring your account, which may be done online through the "My Account" feature. Failure to receive a notice, for whatever reason, does not remove your responsibility for meeting the due date assigned at check out or for honoring recall notices sent to an email address.

 

How to set your email address

1. Go to the main library web page at http://www.asu.edu/lib and click on "My Account"

 

 

2. Enter your ASURITE ID and password if prompted.

 

3. When the screen comes up with your name and address, click on the "Add/Modify Your Email" button on the left hand side of the screen. This will pop up a new window.

 

 

4. In the new window, enter your desired email address. Please note: be sure this email address is correct and contains no errors!

 

 

5. You will then see a message letting you know your email has been successfully updated.

 

 

Some things to keep in mind

  • Additional email addresses may be used provided each email address is separated only by a comma with no spaces between addresses. For example:

    • Email1@asu.edu,email2@yahoo.com,email3@gmail.com

  • You may add or edit your email address at any time through the “My Account” function in the Library’s webpage, http://www.asu.edu/lib/. It is your responsibility to ensure that the email address is current and accurate.
  • SPAM filtering software may interfere with the delivery of Library notices. If you do use SPAM filters, be sure to customize your profile to accept mail from the library where the address includes @asu.edu and the subject line includes [ASULIB]. For ILL/Interlibrary Loan notices, customize your profile to accept the library's ILL address: ill@asu.edu

Please note: Do not reply to notices. They are informational only. For immediate service, please direct your questions regarding library notifications to the library circulation staff at 480/965-3605.

  • If you neglect to update your email address or your email account is over quota, notices sent to you will bounce. Bounced email will be deleted and all subsequent notices will be deleted until you have updated your email address through “My Account”.

Any questions?

Please call the circulation desk at 480/965-3605 or 480/965-2600 for assistance with this process.
