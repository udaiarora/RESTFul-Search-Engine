VIS Lab/GIS Lab


Service Description

Visualization Center Phone: 965-9699
E-mail: visteam@asu.edu
Web: http://www.asu.edu/it/fyi/research/vis/vc

The VIS Center offers high-end graphic workstations (Silicon Graphics, PC and Mac), software resources and services for video capture, animation, digital drawing, virtual reality, 3D graphics and modeling used in research and creative/artistic endeavors.

Geographic Information Systems
Phone: 965-4007
E-mail: gislab@asu.edu
Web: http://www.asu.edu/it/irs/rs/GIS/

The GIS Lab provides hardware, software and staff support services to store, modify, analyze and display geospatially-referenced information. GIS can be used to create custom maps, perform data base queries, generate models and perform site and market analyses. The lab is extensively involved in GIS user support, multi-disciplinary projects and externally sponsored research projects, using cartography, data integration and visualization.

The VIS Lab and GIS Lab are available to current ASU faculty, staff, graduate assistants and teaching assistants, or for special projects by arrangement.
