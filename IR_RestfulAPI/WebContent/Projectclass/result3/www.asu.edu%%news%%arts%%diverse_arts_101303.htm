College offers diverse arts at ASU

The Katherine K. Herberger College of Fine Arts annually presents more than 1,000 public performances and exhibitions and entertains half-a-million audience members in its top-notch venues on campus. Now it's offering online ticketing, a weekly e-mail update and special bargains.

Online ticketing is the most convenient way to purchase tickets to ASU arts events. Go to (http://herbergercollege.asu.edu) and click Online Ticketing to use your credit card to purchase tickets to any of the 24 Herberger Mainstage productions in four award-winning areas: lyric opera theater, theater, dance and organ music. Once purchased, tickets will be waiting at the venue.

Several new deals are now available for Mainstage season subscribers. The first is called First Fridays. Buy one Mainstage subscription and receive a second companion subscription absolutely free for First Friday performances only.

Another option that offers maximum flexibility in attending Mainstage performances is the new Freedom Pack. Just $100 buys 10 vouchers for any of the 24 productions. The Cheap Seats Card is the best deal for students. For only $30, students can attend any 20 performances of the Herberger Mainstage season and treat their friends, too.

It's easy to be informed about Herberger College arts events through subscription to E-vents, a weekly e-mailed calendar of events on campus. Get the scoop on everything from art exhibitions and faculty recitals to award-winning dance performances and musicals. Send an e-mail to (events-subscribe@asu.edu) or go to (http://herbergercollege.asu.edu/) and click E-vents to start receiving ASU arts news .

For more information about Herberger College events and tickets, call (480) 965-6536. For a complete calendar of Mainstage events, visit (http://herbergercollege.asu.edu/calendar/mainstage).

Tanguay, with the Herberger College of Fine Arts, can be reached at (480) 965-7144 or (denise.tanguay@asu.edu).
