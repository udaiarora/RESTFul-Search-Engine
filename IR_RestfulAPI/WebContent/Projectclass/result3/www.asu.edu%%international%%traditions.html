International Traditions at ASU

ASU is a global leader in research, teaching, and service.  As a student at ASU, you will have opportunities to work with faculty who are recognized globally for their scholarship and creative endeavors.  

Learn more about ASU global engagement:

  • General information
  • ASU global engagement in the press

ASU provides numerous exciting opportunities to participate in cultural programming throughout the year.  ASU students come from over 140 different countries which contribute to a diversity of perspectives and experiences that enrich the university.

Learn more about ASU international activities:

  • ISO Activities at Tempe campus
  • ISS Activities at West campus

Find an international student organization to get involved or become a student leader in the International Student Coalition.
