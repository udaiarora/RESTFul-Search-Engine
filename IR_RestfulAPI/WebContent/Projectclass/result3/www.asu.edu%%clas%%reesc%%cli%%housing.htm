Housing

CLI Housing Information

On- and off-campus housing options are available. ASU has air-conditioned residence hall options. See the Residential Life web site or call 480-965-1531 for additional information or an application. Off-campus housing information can be obtained from a number of sources including the ASU Web Devil classified ads or the Arizona Republic, the state's largest daily newspaper.

 

On-campus housing options

ASU has air-conditioned residence halls with communal kitchen facilities. See the Residential Life web site or call 480-965-3515 or 480-965-1531 for additional information. Students can download the residential life application form from the website.

Apartments

There are apartment complexes near the university that offer furnished apartments with short-term leases.  One option within walking distance is the Bali Lanai, 1137 East Orange Street, Tempe, AZ 85281, (480) 966-9531.  Another option on a bus line directly to ASU is Granada Lakes, 5701 S Rural Rd, Tempe, AZ 85283, 480-838-4528.
