Feedback on content of policies and procedures

If you have comments and suggestions regarding the content of the policies and procedures within a particular manual, please contact the sponsoring office, department, or vice presidential area. Academic and Administrative Documents (AAD) does not determine the content of the policies and procedures that it publishes. Contact information for each manual is listed in the manual’s Introduction, policy number 001.

Feedback on online format and usability

AAD works with several university departments to publish the online manuals with the intent of making them accessible to the entire university community. Nonetheless, various browsers and computer configurations may retrieve information differently.

We would be pleased to receive your comments and suggestions regarding the format and functionality of the online manuals. Please send your comments to AAD (aad@asu.edu). Every comment will be logged in, reviewed, and addressed.

Browser configuration to send e-mail

To send e-mail through your browser, the Preferences setting on the browser must reflect your identity. If necessary, consult your browser manual or its help feature to make this adjustment. You may also wish to contact your department’s computer consultant or the Information Technology Help Desk at 965–6500.
