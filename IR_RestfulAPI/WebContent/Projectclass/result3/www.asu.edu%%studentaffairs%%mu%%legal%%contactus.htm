Visit Our Office

The Student Legal Assistance office is located in Room 329 on the 3rd level of the Memorial Union. The office hours are from 8:30 am - 5:30 pm, Monday - Thursday and from 8:30 am - 12:30 pm on Friday. E-mail lawinfo@asu.edu to schedule an appointment.

Please note: Legal questions cannot be answered by e-mail.
