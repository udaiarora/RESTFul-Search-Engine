Cost

Program Fees:
Are $1,440.00 for one session and $2,858.00 for both sessions. (Both of these totals include the program deposit fee that you are requested to send in with your application form.) These costs include ground transportation in Ecuador, lodging and three meals a day.Tuition per Session.

Other Student Fees Due with Tuition:
All students pay the Arizona Students Association fee of $1.00.
All students pay the Financial Aid Trust Fund fee of $12.00.

Total from above per Session : $13.00

Costs Not Covered by the Program: Airfare (which is approximately $800.00 with a group rate) , passport fees if you need to apply for a new passport, health and travel insurance while abroad (some insurance is already provided under the International Student Identity Card which each participant will receive prior to travel, but supplemental insurance is available at the student's own cost), a $32.00 airport exit fee which is due when you depart Ecuador, and personal expenses (money for souvenirs, snacks, etc.). at least an additional $200.00 (or more) to cover this last category. So, the minimum total for this section would be $1,000.00.
These prices are subject to change.

FLAS Fellowships: FLAS Fellows must enroll for 7 credits of Kichwa Language. The FLAS Fellowship pays for the tuition of Kichwa language classes. Other optional courses may be taken at the student's expense.
