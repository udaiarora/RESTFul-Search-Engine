ASU in the Community

ASU in the Community is an online resource of Arizona State University’s community outreach engagement activities. The site demonstrates ASU’s commitment to the region, while also serving as a tool for identifying specific programs and linking to a network of people engaged in the community.
 
With information about more than 400 programs being conducted at over 500 locations throughout the state, the web site is a key university asset, enabling ASU to assess its efforts to become socially embedded in the community, to enhance outreach program awareness, to plan and collaborate, and leverage resources. The web site compiles basic information about hundreds of programs and activities that benefit the community, from campus-wide community service projects to legal clinics, health services, tutoring and mentoring, summer camps, and college readiness programs. An interactive mapping tool enables users to map ASU's community outreach programs against a back drop of census data, school districts, legislative districts, congressional districts or zip codes.


Use ASU in the Community to search, map and connect:

  • Search for community outreach programs by interest area, keyword, city, audience, campus, zip code, college or any combination of these criteria
  • Map ASU’s community outreach programs using an interactive mapping tool over a dynamic backdrop of census data, school districts, legislative districts, congressional districts, city council districts or zip codes
  • Connect with ASU’s community outreach programs, collaborate with others who engage with the community, and share community outreach news and events in the Community Camera e-newsletter

Relationship to Design Imperatives

Design Imperative #2: Societal Transformation
ASU Must Become a Force, and Not Only a Place

Playing an active role in our community is an important way that ASU can be more than just a place. The programs listed on the ASU in the Community website represent community outreach activities that touch the lives of Arizona?s citizens almost every day. By being involved in the lives of community members at many different levels, from education to healthcare, and social and legal services, ASU is using its human and knowledge resources to transform the community one person at a time.

Design Imperative #7: Social Embeddedness
Public Service/Community Engagement/Outreach

ASU in the Community demonstrates our commitment to public service, community engagement, and outreach. By compiling a comprehensive listing of our outreach activities, we can assess ASU?s efforts to contribute to the economic, social and cultural vitality of the region. The information will enable us to better leverage limited resources and determine if we are where we are needed in the community.
