FAQ for Staff

  1. New to ASU?
    Check out the New Staff Guide—this will hopefully answer many of your questions.
  2. What’s an ASURITE ID?
    ASURITE is the name of ASU’s common User ID for computing systems (like email and course management). Three days after your payroll forms have been processed, you may visit from an on-campus computer www.asu.edu/selfsub/ to self-subscribe a computer account.
    Information Technology also has an online guide to computing resources at ASU: Connecting with IT.
