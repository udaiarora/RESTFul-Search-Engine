Librarians' Council

C.2.5: Compensation Committee

Effective 12/20/89 .. Revised 4/2/99

POLICY AND PROCEDURES STATEMENT

 

 

The Compensation Committee is one of the Standing Committees of the Librarians' Council. It consists of five (5) members elected for terms of two years. The terms of the elected members are staggered with two or three members to be elected each year. All Council members are eligible for this committee.

 

 

RESPONSIBILITIES


  1. To study compensation issues as they relate to salaries for academic professionals at ASU and recommend appropriate action as necessary.


    2.  

  2. To compile and revise criteria and procedures relating to compensation matters such as merit pay, market adjustments, pay equity and any other salary issues.


    3.  

  3. To study compensation issues not directly related to salaries and recommend appropriate action as necessary.


    4.  

  4. To take under consideration any relevant item of interest to the academic professionals upon the request of the Librarians' Council.

 

PROCEDURES

 

 

At the first meeting after the election of new members the Committee will choose a chairperson and a secretary.

 

 

All members of the Compensation Committee are voting members. All decisions require the presence of at least three voting members.

 

 

All compensation policies compiled by the Committee must be approved by the Librarians' Council.
