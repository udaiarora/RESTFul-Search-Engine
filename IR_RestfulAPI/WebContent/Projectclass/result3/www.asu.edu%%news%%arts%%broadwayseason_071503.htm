July 15, 2003

Broadway season tickets on sale this month

Single tickets for the M&I Bank Broadway in Arizona 2003 - 04 season at ASU's Gammage Auditorium, are going on sale July 21. This Broadway season is presented by ASU Public Events and Clear Channel Entertainment and includes three weeks of the smash hit musical, "Mamma Mía!," Sept. 9 - 28, and two weeks of Broadway's newest phenomenon and Tony Award-winner, "Hairspray!," April 13 - 25, 2004.

Single tickets go on sale at 10 a.m., July 21. Ticket prices are: $22.25 - $77.50 and are available at ASU's Gammage Box Office, all Ticketmaster outlets and (www.ticketmaster.com). To order by phone, call (480) 784-4444; for groups of 20 or more call (480) 965-6678.

In addition to "Mamma Mía" and "Hairspray," the seven-show M&I Bank Broadway in Arizona subscription series will feature the following all-new productions:

. "42nd Street," Nov. 11 - 16;
. "Starlight Express," Jan. 6 - 11, 2004;
. "The Exonerated," Jan. 27 - Feb. 1, 2004;
. "Urinetown," May 4 - 9, 2004; and
. "Oklahoma," June 8 - 13, 2004;

And special Broadway engagements:
. "Radio City Christmas Spectacular," Dec. 12 - 31;
. "Def Poetry Jam," Feb. 12 and 13, 2004; and
. "Riverdance," March 30 - April 4, 2004.

In its 14th season, M&I Bank Broadway in Arizona is the largest theatrical subscription series in the state, and one of the most successful Broadway series in the country. ASU's Gammage Auditorium is the home theater of Broadway in Arizona.
For information about the 2003 - 04 M&I Bank Broadway in Arizona season call (480) 784-4444 or visit (www.asugammage.com). ASU's Gammage Box office hours are from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., Monday - Thursday.

All M&I Bank Broadway in Arizona performances are presented by arrangement with ASU Public Events and Clear Channel Entertainment, Inc.

Medina, with ASU Public Events, can be reached at (480) 965-1884 or (kariann.medina@asu.edu).
