Sun Devil Fitness - Class Descriptions
Format offerings vary each semester based on instructor availability.

Step Classes

Water Classes

Athletic Workouts Classes

Other Cardio Options

Strength, Flexibility and Balance

STEP

Cardiovascular workouts using the step with movements choreographed to music

Intro to Step-30: New to group exercise or step? This class is for you! Learn
the basic moves and terminology needed to get you started on the step. The focus
is instructional; you may or may not break a sweat. This class meets the first
six weeks of the semester only.

Step Basics-45: This low-to- moderate intensity class will focus on basic step
choreography needed to get you going on the step – and keep you coming back for
more!

Step-30: Get a quick cardio fix! Thirty minutes of simple, powerful & FUN step
moves. Personalize it by choosing your own intensity level.

Step-45: 45-minutes combining strong simple movements for an effective workout.

Step-60: A full hour to condition your cardiovascular system. A great place to
work on your cardio endurance!

WATER
Little or non-impact cardiovascular and strength workouts using buoyancy belts,
buoyancy bells, noodles, etc.

AquaFit-45: A low-impact, total body workout in the shallow pool including
cardio, interval training, and resistance training using equipment and water
resistance.

AquaFit-60: A longer water workout that may include shallow and deep water
routines (buoyancy devices provided for deep water).

ATHLETIC WORKOUTS
Athletic cardiovascular and strength workouts that are not
choreography-intensive

Cardio KickBox-60: This fitness-based kickboxing class is designed to introduce
technique and basic cardio kickboxing movements such as jabs, crosses, snap
kicks and sidekicks.

Hard Core Box-60: Gloves and kick pads are used in this intense class
integrating self defense, sport drills and plyometric training.

Boot Camp-30: Want an intense workout but don’t have much time? We’ll get your
heart rate up with callisthenic drills…it’s a cardio quickie!

Boot Camp-60: Test your body's limits with this straight forward, no-frills
workout modeled after military basic training. Work your entire body with
various strength, cardio, agility, and flexibility drills designed to get your
body into the ultimate training condition. Drop and give me 20!

Sports Conditioning-45: This class is a dynamic, moderate-to-high intensity
athletic training session utilizing sports-based drills that emphasize total
body movement and engage multiple energy systems. You will challenge your speed,
agility, balance and coordination through an array of heart-pumping exercises!

Sports Conditioning-60: The same as our exciting 45-min. format, with an added
focus on core functional strength.

OTHER CARDIO OPTIONS
Cardiovascular workouts based on movements choreographed to music

Hi/Lo-60: You don’t need equipment to sweat! This moderate-to-high intensity
workout increases your heart rate by using range of motion and low impact/high
impact choreography for a fun exercise experience. You can stay low impact if
you prefer!

Turbo KickBox-60: A pre-choreographed cardiovasular workout utilizing movements
from kickboxing. Expect high energy intervals that will motivate you from
beginning to end!

Latin Moves-60: Get your heart rate up to the spicy grooves of latin dance!

Hip Hop-60: A high energy, fun, cardio workout! Learn basic hip hop dance moves
and dance progressions to fresh, fast-paced music. This class is hot!

ZUMBA-60: A latin-dance inspired cardio workout that uses a combination of fast
and slow dance rhythms to tone the whole body! Use the basic step of salsa,
meringue and the box step to swing, dip, shuffle and spin! Caution: Laughing,
hollering, clapping and all-out party-mania may occur!

STRENGTH, FLEXIBILITY AND BALANCE
Core-30: Strengthen your abs and back, improve your posture and leave feeling
great.

Strength-30: A quick, full-body workout to tone your muscles.

Strength-45: Build strength and coordination targeting the major muscle groups
in a shorter, efficient workout.

Strength-60: A full hour to strengthen and tone the major muscle groups using
your own body weight and all our “toys”…handheld weights, body bars, resistance
tubing, stability balls, etc.

BOSU: BOSU stands for “both sides up”, which is how you can use this apparatus.
The BOSU will always enhance your balance and coordination, and will challenge
other physiological systems as well. The class title on the schedule let’s you
know what we’ll focus on (cardio, strength, core) and how long we’ll go (-30,
-45, -60 minutes). You’ll love the challenge!

Stretch-30: Increase your flexibility by moving your joints through a range of
motion with controlled and concentrated effort. You will finish relaxed and
rejuvenated!

Pilates Sampler-30: Experience the benefits of Pilates in this 30-minute sampler
session. Experienced instructors from throughout the Phoenix valley will lead
you through basic Pilates movements and answer any questions you may have. For a
more in-depth Pilates experience, register for the Sun Devil mindBODY program.

Yoga Sampler-30: Experience the benefits of yoga in this 30-minute sampler
session. Experienced instructors from throughout the Phoenix valley will lead
you through basic yoga movements and answer any questions you may have. For a
more in-depth yoga experience, register for the Sun Devil mindBODY program.

Belly Dance Sampler-30: Learn basic belly dance technique. While the focus is on
learning correct technique, the goal is enjoyment! For a more in-depth
experience with veils, finger cymbals and choreography, register for the Sun
Devil mindBODY program.
