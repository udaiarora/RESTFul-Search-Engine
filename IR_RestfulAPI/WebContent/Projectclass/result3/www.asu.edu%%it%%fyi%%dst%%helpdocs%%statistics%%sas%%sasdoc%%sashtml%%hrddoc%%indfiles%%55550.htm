Quick Start to Data Analysis with SAS

by Frank C. DiIorio and Kenneth A. Hardy

Everything you need to get started with SAS software is right here, from understanding program syntax to managing data and performing statistical analyses. First-time users of SAS will benefit from Quick Start's no-nonsense approach to statistical analysis. The book explains the fundamentals of SAS software and gives readers the basic SAS skills needed to begin immediately managing and analyzing data. A special "Statistical Roadmap" chapter helps readers find the appropriate analytical method to best serve their research needs. The book's straightforward style emphasizes the practical use of SAS over obscure programming theory. Readers who need to analyze large, complicated data in the social sciences, life sciences, public administration, or engineering will find this book particularly useful.

301 pages.
