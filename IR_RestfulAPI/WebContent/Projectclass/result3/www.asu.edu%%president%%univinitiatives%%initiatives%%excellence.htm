President’s Medal of Excellence

The inaugural ASU President’s Medal of Excellence was awarded in May 2006 to Jerry Bisgrove, President and CEO of the Stardust Companies, for his commitment to social and economic development in the Valley. Awarded by ASU President Michael Crow, the new award honors innovative leaders who have advanced awareness and action on issues that affect the well-being and positive development of their communities

 

Mr. Bisgrove is a noted philanthropist, businessman, and local leader on issues of affordable housing and nonprofit and business leadership that furthers social and economic development. His work has helped to bring the need for high quality, culturally responsive, and sustainable affordable housing to focus at ASU, in the Arizona legislature, and with local city officials and organizations.

 

Three major events marked Mr. Bisgrove ’s receipt of the President’s Medal of Excellence: a leadership celebration breakfast, participation in the honorary degrees dinner, and presentation of the medal at Spring 2006 commencement.
