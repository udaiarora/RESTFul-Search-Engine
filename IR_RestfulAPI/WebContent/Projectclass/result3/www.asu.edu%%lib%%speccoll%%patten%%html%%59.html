Jaskiewicz, Johann

b. 1750.

Dissertatio inauguralis medica sistens pharmaca regni
vegetabilis. . .

Vindobonae: Typis Joan. Thomae nob. de Trattnern, 1775. Includes index.

Thesis--Universitat Wien, 1775.

The author begins with an explanatory preface followed by a list of
14 propositions outlining his work.  The major portion of the work
is a listing arranged by class of the plants discussed.  There is
an index of plants by Latin name at the end of the work, and those plants
native to Austria are marked with an asterix.  The author cites many
medieval and ancient authors, including Matthioli, Dioscorides, Gesner,
and Galenus.

Subjects:
Medicinal plants.
