Learning Outcomes: Evaluate Sources

Why Evaluate Sources?

  • The student can recognize why they should evaluate sources.

Elements for Evaluation

  • The student will be able to identify elements that will be useful for evaluating a resource.

Evaluation Based on Author Information

  • The student will identify the elements that indicate the author's expertise and/or bias.

Evaluation Based on Date of Publication

  • The student can use publication date information to evaluate a resource.

Evaluation Based on Author Intent

  • The student will determine the author's intent.

Evaluation Based on Appropriateness of Source for Research

  • The student will determine the appropriateness of source for research.

Books

  • The student will identify elements for evaluating books.

Web Pages

  • The student will identify elements for evaluating web pages.

People

  • The student will identify elements for evaluating people as a source.

Broadcast Media

  • The student will identify elements for evaluating broadcast media.

Article Sources

  • The student will identify elements for evaluating article sources.

Who Can Help?

  • The student will know to ask a librarian for help when needed.
