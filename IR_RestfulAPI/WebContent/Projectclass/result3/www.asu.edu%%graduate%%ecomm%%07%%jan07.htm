Strategies for Success: Maximize Your Presentation, Communication and Leadership Skills

A Strategies for Success (SFS) seminar on Wednesday January 31, 2007 will feature speaker Ellen Kaye, author of the best selling business book Maximize Your Presentation Skills: How to Speak, Look and Act on Your Way to The Top. Graduate students as well as ASU juniors and seniors can learn to build self-confidence, make a great first impression, and to speak, look, and act more professionally. This event is co-sponsored by the Division of Graduate Studies, the Graduate and Professional Student Association, and the Memorial Union. Location: Neeb Hall. 3:30-5:30 p.m.
