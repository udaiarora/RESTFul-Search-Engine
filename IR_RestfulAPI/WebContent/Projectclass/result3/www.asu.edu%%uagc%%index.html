Welcome to the Office of the Vice President for University Administration and Legal Affairs

Under the leadership of Paul Ward, the Office of the Vice President for University Administration is charged by the President with overseeing certain administrative services including the Department of Public Safety, Environmental Health & Safety,  the Office of Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action, Internal Audit and Management Services, the Office of Human Resources, and the Office of General Counsel.

Our principal purpose is to meet the needs of the university community while operating within the legal and regulatory compliance requirements of the State of Arizona and the Arizona Board of Regents.

We are striving to continuously improve the administrative and legal services we provide in support of ASU's vision as a New American University.

 

 

Core Principles

  • treat all members of the university community with respect and dignity;
  • promote diversity and equal opportunity;
  • promote open, honest communication where each department’s contributions are valued and respected;
  • work as a team with shared responsibility, accountability, co-ownership, ethical integrity, and professionalism;
  • collaborate with the university community, providing value-added administrative services;
  • provide an environment that supports work-life balance; and
  • continuously improve everything we do.
