Do you need...

  • A download of names and addresses for a mailing?
  • A list of your current donors?
  • A roster of alumni for your college from the class of 1956?

Benefactor Users have access to reports in three ways:

  • General (XGEN) and department specific (XUSR) reports can be run from the Benefactor menu
  • Benefactor Users who have completed the data retrieval class can develop their own, custom reports.
  • IRM report writers, with two weeks lead time, can create ad hoc reports.

All requests to IRM for ad hoc reports are assigned a unique number which is e-mailed to the requester along with an acknowledgement of the request. Although report requests are accepted by fax (965-6686) and via campus mail (mail code 5905), e-mail is the submission method preferred by IRM for speed and reliability.
