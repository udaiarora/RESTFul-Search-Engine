Useful Privacy Law Resources on the Internet

Cyberspace Law for Non-Lawyers
http://www.ssrn.com/update/lsn/cyberspace/csl_lessons.html

An excellent, plain-English discussion of basic privacy (and other) law as it applies in the online world

Lost and Found in Cyberspace
http://www.info-law.com/lost.html

A more detailed law review article about online privacy law

Cyberspace Law Index: Privacy
http://www.jmls.edu/cyber/index/privacy.html

A collection of important cyberspace-related privacy cases, law review articles, and other materials, in full text

Department of Justice Computer Crime and Intellectual Property Section
http://www.usdoj.gov/criminal/cybercrime/

Information on search and seizure in cyberspace
