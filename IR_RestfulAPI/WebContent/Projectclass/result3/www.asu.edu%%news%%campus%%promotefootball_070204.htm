July 2, 2004

ASU and the Cardinals join to promote football

With past legal issues resolved, ASU and the Arizona Cardinals held a joint press conference June 28 to announce a new era of cooperation to promote football in Arizona.

"I'm exceedingly happy that we are here today with a focus on the future," said ASU President Michael Crow. "We have reached the conclusion that it is to the benefit of our community, to the benefit of the sport of football and all that it means here in the United States to have programs that are working together, linked together, evolving together and building together, and we're very excited about the opportunity to move in that direction."

Michael Bidwill, Cardinals vice president and general counsel, said, "Dr. Crow and I began talking six or seven months ago about our shared vision for football in the community and about finding common ground to reach agreement and move forward in a very positive way. We're moving forward in a spirit of cooperation that reestablishes our focus back on our football programs to return ASU and the Cardinals to some great football in the Valley for all the fans."

The new partnership includes a number of collaborative initiatives between ASU and the Cardinals for the future. These include elements related to academic, marketing and community relations activities involving the two groups.
