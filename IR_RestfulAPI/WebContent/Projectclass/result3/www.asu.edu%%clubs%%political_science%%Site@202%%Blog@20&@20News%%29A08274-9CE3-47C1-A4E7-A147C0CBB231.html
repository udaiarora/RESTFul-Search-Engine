Meet Your Officers: Doug Lowden, VP
 
Douglas was born the son of two Federal Workers in the busy world of Washington, DC.   His family quickly moved to Phoenix to escape the humidity and harsh winters of Northern Virginia.  Doug then went on to coast through elementary, middle, and high school in North Scottsdale.  Not knowing what to do after graduation, he decided to pursue an interest in film at what was then the only film school in Arizona, Scottsdale Community College.  After a year of waning interest and a mediocre performance at best, Douglas realized that film school was not for him.  After attending a POS 101 course, he decided to pursue his interest in politics instead.  He promptly enrolled in Arizona State where he excelled over the next two years in the Political Science program.  Douglas has traveled across Europe, and was a finalist for the NSEP award to study in Russia.  Recently, he dropped his Russian minor to study Economics.  Douglas graduates in May 2007, and faces the options of pursuing graduate studies, law school, or joining the full time workforce.  Currently, law school is leading, yet that could be altered depending on LSAT score.  
 
 
Pi Sigma Alpha Delta Theta
