I-20 DEADLINES
The deadline for issuing an I-20 to a student is June 30 for the Fall semester and Nov. 15 for the Spring semester. Departments willing to send an I-20 to a student via overnight mail/courier after the deadline should email Graduate Admissions requesting the I-20 be produced and stating that the department will send it to the student. Departments are cautioned that in accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act the I-20 can be sent only to the address of record on SIS unless the student has provided written signed authorization to send it to a different address.
