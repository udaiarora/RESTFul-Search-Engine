PCS 601–01: Identifying Inventoriable Equipment

Purpose

To explain methods for identifying inventoriable equipment

Sources

48 Code of Federal Regulations § 45
United States. Office of Management and Budget. OMB circulars A-21 and A-110
Property Control

Policy

ASU Property Control Numbers

Most inventoriable equipment is tagged with an ASU property control number (ASU PC#).

Dummy Numbers

Inventoriable equipment that cannot be tagged (e.g., technical interference, defacing) may be assigned a “dummy ASU PC#” beginning with a “9” (e.g., 9003564).
