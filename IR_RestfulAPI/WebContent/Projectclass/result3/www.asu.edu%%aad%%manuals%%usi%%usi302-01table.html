The student obtains the short-term loan application in person at the Student Financial Assistance Office (SFAO) or at Financial Aid Services (FAS) at the West campus and submits the appropriate documentation and application to the SFAO or FAS.

SFAO or FAS staff reviews the application and all documentation and approves or denies the loan.

If the loan is approved, the student obtains the check in person at Student Business Services or at Student Accounts at the West campus the next business day (24-hour turn-around time).

Note: For Buros loans, the student must obtain the application from his or her college dean’s office.
