Only complete applications will be considered. A complete application includes:

  1. Completed and signed application form.
  2. School record of the 2004/2005 standardized test scores for reading, mathematics, and language.
  3. Two completed teacher recommendation forms, which are provided.
  4. Personal statement form, which is provided.
  5. Non-refundable application fee of $25 (check or money order payable to ASU)

We are still accepting applications for all of the courses offered for 6th and 7th grade students.

Forms

All forms are available for download.

 

Admission

Students will be admitted to the program based on an evaluation of their standardized test scores, teacher recommendations, and personal statement.

 

Notification of Acceptance

Students who are accepted into the program will receive a packet that contains a letter of acceptance, class information (syllabus, location, time, etc.), a student/parent handbook, health history forms, lunch information, and payment information. Notification of acceptance will be mailed by May 5, 2006.

 

Tuition Payment

A tuition payment of $100 is due upon acceptance into the program. The remaining balance is due by Friday, June 9. Students with an unpaid balance after June 9 will be withdrawn from classes. Checks, money orders, or credit cards (Visa or MasterCard) will be accepted.

 

Refunds

The $25 application fee is non-refundable under any circumstances. Tuition refunds will be made only if a student is unable to attend because of a medical emergency or serious medical problem documented by a doctor.
