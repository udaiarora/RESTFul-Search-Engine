Meeting and Conference Room Rentals
 Services Room Descriptions Equipment Rates How to Reserve a Room

 

As one of the premier rental spaces in the downtown area, the Mercado provides many services and amenities that make using these facilities a personal and first-class experience.

Promise of Success
As part of our quality service, we offer the following to ensure that each event is successful:
Experienced, professional staff
Meeting rooms that are set up on time and to specification
Reliable audiovisual equipment
A dedicated facility representative available on the day of the event for assistance
Accurate and timely billing documentation

Finding the Perfect Space
Mercado, building C can house nearly 1,100 visitors in 19 meeting rooms of a variety of sizes and functions. Available for rental are conference rooms and boardrooms which can be configured in a variety of ways, two highly technical computer classrooms and a wireless technology classroom. In addition, breakout areas are conveniently located throughout the facility.

Technology Rooms
A technology classroom, which accommodates 60 in a tiered setting, has the capability for each student to plug in or wirelessly connect. This room, in addition to all other rooms, has the capacity for satellite downlinks. Two state-of-the-art multimedia computer classrooms are also available with up to 20 Pentium IV 3.2Ghz computers with 1 GB RAM and MS Windows XP OS. Special software needs can be accommodated by special arrangement.

Competitive Rates
As a nonprofit, state-run organization, ASU understands that competitive rates are an important factor. Our competitive rates and amenities are some of the best in town and we would enjoy the opportunity to host your event or function.

Touring our Facility
Customized tours of the facility are offered so that potential customers can view the variety of rooms available and their amenities. Call us to schedule a tour appointment.
