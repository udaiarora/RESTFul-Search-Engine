Accessing ASU Library indexes and electronic journals from off-campus

Most of the ASU electronic indexes and electronic journals are available from other computing sites on campus, or from your computer at home. You need to sign in before accessing these resources from off campus locations.

You will be prompted to sign in with your ASURITE ID and password.
