Press Release:
ECURE 2004 Call for Proposals Extended

October 2003

Dynamic speakers and presentations are sought for the ECURE 2004 conference, an international, interdisciplinary and interactive event taking place March 1-3 at Arizona State University. ECURE is the only North American conference for wide-ranging discussion of the full spectrum of issues impacting academic electronic records today and tomorrow.

ECURE 2004 will focus on technical, policy and training issues related to electronic records of university research. Research projects, policy reviews and case studies on management of data sets, research documentation requirements, public accessibility, institutional repositories, and electronic publication are sought for this year's conference. Technical topics may include but are not limited to: metadata and cataloging, storage, migration, security, e-mail, portal sites, disaster planning and recovery/business continuity, system security and data integrity, multimedia retention and digital preservation costs. Proposals will be accepted through November 14th, 2003.

ECURE is attended by college and university administrators, information professionals, attorneys and faculty from across the United States and Canada. ECURE 2004 will be held on the beautiful Arizona State University Main Campus at Tempe, Arizona in the Phoenix metropolitan area. For submission instructions, presentations from previous conferences, and further information visit the ECURE website, http://www.asu.edu/ecure.
