Eligibility Requirements

Faculty/Staff

Faculty and staff can reserve any of our equipment with a valid ASU Sun Card. The equipment inventory list indicates the maximum checkout period for each piece of equipment.

Teaching/Graduate Assistants

Teaching and graduate assistants can reserve any of our equipment with a valid TA/GA Temporary ASU ID and Sun Card. The equipment inventory list indicates the maximum checkout period for each piece of equipment.

Students

Students must present a valid student ASU Sun Card and must present an authorization form to reserve equipment. Students can receive the equipment only after Classroom Support has confirmed the signature of the faculty or staff member on the authorization form. The equipment inventory list indicates what equipment can be checked out and the maximum checkout period for each item.
