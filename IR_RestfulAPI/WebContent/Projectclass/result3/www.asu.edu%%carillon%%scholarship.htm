Scholarship Fund Established

The Arizona State Credit Union has established a $1,000 scholarship for a student carillonneur for the 2006-07 academic year.

We hope to match that fund and offer a $2,000 scholarship, to be called the Arizona State Credit Union & Friends scholarship.

The University Carillonneur will give regular concerts and help other students learn to play and appreciate our carillon.

To donate to the scholarship, send checks, payable to the ASU Carillon Society, to:

Arizona State Credit Union, PO Box 870901, Tempe, AZ 85287-0901. Cash donations also are welcome.

You can also send checks to the Credit Union for new bells!

Thanks, Arizona State Credit Union, for your support.

For more information, call Judith Smith, Chair, ASU Carillon Society, at (480) 965-4821, or send an e-mail to carillon@asu.edu
