Room Specifications - CC 227 - Training Classroom

Service Description

This room is designed to accommodate computer based training for ASU faculty and staff.


Infrastructure

  • Telecommnications
    • 18 Data connections
    • 1 Voice connection
  • Lighting
    • Fluorescent - recessed non specular direct parabolic (three tubes per fixture with multiple switching) throughout
    • Dimmable incandescent recessed downlights at rear of classroom

Equipment

  • Computer
    • 17 - Pentium class PCs
    • 1 - Laser quality printer
  • Multimedia
    • 1 - Proxima 5100 video projector (ceiling mounted)
    • 1 - Overhead projector (on mobile cart)

Special Amenities

  • Visual Communications
    • Egan Visual "Egan System" Track with three reversible panels (markerboard/tackboard), and one flipchart panel, all 36"W x 48"H
    • 1 - Ceiling mounted, electric projection screen 84"W x 84"H
  • Furniture
    • 1 - Instructor lectern (Egan Visual "Mobile Instruction Centre")
    • 9 - Tables with grommet and wire management channel 84"W x 29"H x 30"D
    • 21 - Task chairs
    • 1 - Stool
    • 2 - Wall mounted coat racks
  • Millwork
    • 1 - Lockable cabinet 109"W x 36"H x 25"D to support shared printer, provide storage, etc. Power and data connections within millwork, accessible via grommets on top.
  • ADA
    • 2 - A/C powered adjustable height tables 42"W x 29"H to 36"H x 30"D
