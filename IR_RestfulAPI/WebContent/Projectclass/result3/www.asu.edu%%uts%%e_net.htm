NET

Nurse Entrance Test


Information
The Nurse Entrance Test evaluates areas that are essential for academic success in nursing programs including basic math skills, science reading comprehension, and learning styles. Questions regarding admission, requirements, scoring or retesting should be addressed to the College of Nursing and Healthcare Innovation .
Schedule
  • January 25, 31 at 9:00 am
  • January 25, 31 at 1:30 pm
Cost
The NET costs $40.00 and must be paid with a check or money order made payable to Arizona State University.
Time
The exam consists of a 30 minute handwritten essay and a computer based multiple choice test. The essay portion is administered first, followed by a 10 minute break before the computer portion of the test. Candidates should allow up to 3 hours for the total appointment time.
Register
Prior to registering for the NET exam, please read the Information Sheet.
In Person: Complete the NET registration form and submit it along with payment to a UTS staff member in 301 Payne Hall. At that time you will be given an admission ticket.
By Mail: Complete the NET registration form and submit it along with a check or money order payable to Arizona State University and a self addressed stamped envelope to:

Arizona State University
University Testing Services
PO Box 870511
Tempe, AZ 85287-0511


Your CON ID is required on the registration form. UTS will not process your registration form without it. UTS must receive your registration and payment by 4:30 pm one working day before your requested test date. Early registration is highly recommended, as seating is limited for each testing session and spaces often fill up quicly towards the end of the testing cycle.
Reschedule
You must cancel or reschedule no later than 7 days after your original test date.
  • To transfer your test date, submit your admission ticket along with a $5.00 check or money order payable to Arizona State University.
  • To cancel a test date and receive a 50% refund, you must fill out a refund request form available only at our office.
Retake
If your scores on the NET do not meet the minimum required, you may retest only once. Your retest date must be no less than 30 days after your first test date. However, if your Reading Comprehension score is .60 or higher and your Math score is .65 or higher, you may not retest for a higher score.
Admission
Exam entrance requires an admission ticket along with valid photo ID. The name on your photo ID must match the name on your admission ticket. Make sure you arrive at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time.
