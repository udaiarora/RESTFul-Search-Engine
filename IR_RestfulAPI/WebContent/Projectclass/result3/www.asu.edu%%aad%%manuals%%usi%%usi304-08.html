USI 304–08: Employment of Nonimmigrant International Students

Purpose

To outline the requirements for the hiring of nonimmigrant international students

Source

8 Code of Federal Regulations § 214

Policy

Student Employment requires nonimmigrant international students to submit an employment clearance letter from the International Student Office (ISO) or International Student Services Program at the West campus before starting on-campus employment if they:

  1. are enrolled less than full time with a visa classification of F1

    or

  2. have a visa classification other than F1.

The employment clearance letter must state that the student is eligible for on-campus employment.

Cross-References

For more information on student eligibility, see:

  1. USI 301–01, “Financial Aid Eligibility”

  2. USI 304–07, “Student Employment Eligibility Criteria”

    and

  3. the Academic Affairs Policies and Procedures Manual—ACD 517–02, “Employment of Nonimmigrant International Students.”
