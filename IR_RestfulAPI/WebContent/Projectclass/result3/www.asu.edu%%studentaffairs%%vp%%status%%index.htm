STATUS (Student Affairs Tribute to University Staff)

STATUS (Student Affairs Tribute to University Staff) recognizes administrative, classified and service professional staff across the campuses who make a difference by providing continuous, outstanding service to students at Arizona State University.

Each day university professionals work to improve efficiency and effectiveness of student services through the pursuit of excellence. They work to enhance the student experience and support student success by working directly with students, meeting over the lunch hour, advising student groups and lending a hand to a student in need. Staff also contribute to the student experience behind the scenes by developing new programs to serve students more effectively, working to make student processes easier, mentoring and advocating on a student's behalf. In each of these instances, staff dedicate themselves to helping students achieve success.

Nominations for STATUS are typically collected in the spring.
