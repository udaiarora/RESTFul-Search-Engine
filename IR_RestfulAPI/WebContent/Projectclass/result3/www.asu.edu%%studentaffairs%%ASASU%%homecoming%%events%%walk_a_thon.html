Homecoming - Walkathon 2006

Walk/Run for charity alongside fellow ASU community members!

Date: October 22nd, 2006

Time: 12pm - 4pm

Location: Sun Angel Stadium

Description: All proceeds go to the Phoenix Children's Hospital and the Center for Cancer and Blood Disorders. Registration is $10 for adults, $8 for students, and $5 for children. Food, entertainment, and giveaways will be at the event. The organization that brings the most people will receive a prize!
