How to Apply

Read about how to apply for the position of ASU New Student Orientation leader. Then, download and print the application, or pick up an application in SSV Rm 110, complete it, and turn it in as instructed.

Downloadable application instructions

Contact Dale Pelz, Jr. at 480.965.8144 or dale.pelz@asu.edu with any questions.

Thank you for reviewing and considering the application today! I'm excited to see that you have an interest in becoming a New Student Orientation Leader here at ASU!

Here are some things you need to know as you prepare your application:

  1. Read the entire application packet and/or this Web site! The job description is very important and you should be familiar with the various dates for the selection process and the Summer SOL employment period.
  2. Type your application! Or at the very least, type your essay questions and resume/activities list.
  3. Interviews will be held April 25-26, 2006 every half-hour from 9:00 ? 5:00pm. You must sign up for an interview time when you turn in your application.
  4. I must be able to reach you via email or phone during the selection process. Preferably by phone. Please make sure you type your email address and phone number or print them neatly on your application.
  5. Please ask questions if there is anything not clear on the application or job description. It will be important that you understand the job expectations and the specifics of the application process.

Thanks again for your interest! I look forward to meeting you during the interview process!!
