    What are Sun Dollars?

    Sun Dollars is a pre-paid services account managed by the ASU Sun Card Office. It works similar to a bank debit card except that cash cannot be withdrawn (however, refunds are available when you leave ASU). You simply put money in your account and then present the card to any of the participating merchants to make purchases. We have found that Sun Dollars are popular with students and parents alike. In short, Sun Dollars is a service provided by the university to make your life easier.


    Why should I use Sun Dollars?

    1. They are FREE! There is no cost to use Sun Dollars - You put $100 in and you get $100 to spend.

    2. You SAVE MONEY! Click here to find out how.

    3. You simply cannot beat the CONVENIENCE. At ASU your Sun Dollars can be used all over the place (see our complete list) including some of the vending and copy machines on campus. Imagine not have to dig for change, write checks, or worry about keeping track of your spending (you can get an account statement whenever you want one from our office). With Sun Dollars that's all in the past! Many campus locations do not even accept credit cards or personal checks.

    4. Sun Dollars are SAFE. You don't have to worry about carrying around cash all the time. If you loose your card, just report it to us and we'll deactivate it.

    5. There is NO RISK. Sun Dollars do not expire and they are fully refundable when you leave the university. You can open an account and try it for as little as $20!

    Why Not Just Use Credit Cards?

    Sun Dollars are faster, safer, and more convenient than cash or credit cards. Since they can be used for both small and large purchases - a sandwich, textbooks, a coke or candy bar from a vending machine - they are designed for the realities of campus life. Many places on campus do not accept credit cards. Since Sun Dollars are pre-paid you don't have to worry about running up large bills that you can't pay. Finally, Sun Dollars are administered by ASU and we really do try to design the system to meet your needs.

    Important Rules & Responsibilities

    1. Lost, Stolen or misplaced cards must be reported immediately to the 24 hour hot-line at 965-CARD. If you don't have your card, someone else might. Don't take a chance on losing your Sun Dollars.

    2. The cardholder may be responsible for $50 or more resulting from unauthorized use of the card. Please refer to the Initial Account Disclosure Form for complete details about cardholder liability.

    3. Sun Dollars are non-transferable. Only the person pictured on the Sun Card will be allowed to spend Sun Dollars from that account. The cardholder may be required to sign a receipt for goods received.

    4. The minimum contribution to your Sun Dollars account is $20.

    5. Cash cannot be withdrawn from Sun Dollars accounts, however, refunds are available when you officially leave the university (by graduating, withdrawing from the university, or terminating employment). Refunds for students will be credited to the participants University Student Account; refunds for all others will be mailed to the last known address. Balances left in the account will carry over to the next semester and from year to year.

    6. The cardholder is responsible for maintaining a valid Sun Card which is in proper working condition. The Sun Card is the access device for the Sun Dollars account. The card must be presented at the time of purchase and shall be the only means of accessing the participants account. The Sun Card is available from the Sun Card Office in the Memorial Union.

    7. Accounts which remain inactive for more than 18 months may be subject to service fees. Refer to the Sun Dollars Initial Account Disclosures for more details.

    8. Additional details may be found by referring to the Initial Account Disclosure Form.
