ACD 704–01: Vacation Leave—Academic-Year Appointments

Purpose

To provide vacation leave for faculty and academic professionals on academic-year appointments

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

Faculty and academic professionals employed on an academic-year basis

Policy

Faculty and academic professionals on academic-year appointments follow the academic calendar, which specifies interim holidays. Faculty and academic professionals employed on an academic-year basis do not accrue vacation time.
