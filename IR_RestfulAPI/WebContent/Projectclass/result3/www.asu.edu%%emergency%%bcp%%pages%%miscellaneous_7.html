STATEMENT OF WORK DESCRIPTION - SOW # 1

PHASE I - MODIFY EMERGENCY OPERATIONS PLAN

GOAL:

To align ASU Emergency Operations Plan with local plans of City, County, and other agencies.

OVERVIEW:

Arizona State University (ASU) has requested the services of MH & Associates Consulting (MHA) to review its ASU Emergency Operations Plan (EOP) and convert it to mirror the current City of Tempe EOP in its format and operating structure. The converted ASU EOP will require updated data (e.g. personnel, campus, EOC, etc.) to be collected and inputted to the new EOP format and structure.

ASSUMPTIONS

  • MHA will use the current ASU EOP as the baseline for evaluation and conversion to the current City of Tempe EOP;
  • MHA in coordination with ASU will coordinate internal and external department resources to support the timely review and data gathering required to complete the EOP conversion;
  • MHA will use Project Management and Emergency Management best practices throughout implementation of the business continuity planning program;
  • MHA will work with Maricopa County Emergency Management (MCDEM) throughout the project to ensure the final document meets all government standards;
  • MHA will work collaboratively with all areas of the ASU organization.


SCOPE

The scope of the project in Phase I encompasses the review and modification of the ASU EOP to mirror the City of Tempe EOP. Scope changes during the project may affect date of project completion and increase/decrease the number of hours required. Any and all scope changes must be documented and reviewed with and approved by MHA, the assigned Project Manager and Project Sponsor.
