Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

July 10, 1997

Present: Ken Furuta, Gary Hembree, Tom Keating, Molly McCartney, Annette Voth, Karl Weiss

The minutes from the June 26th meeting were accepted. It was also noted Larry Mankin is a Special Assistant to the President in regards to a message Gary sent out.

Karl distributed the working copy of the Bylaws for the Committee for review.

The Annual Report is tabled until the next meeting.

There was more discussion about the Ingenhousz Industrial Liaison. Of concern is that the person may not have a chance to meet the Continuing Status and Promotion requirements. (Annette will look into whether they have hired the Scenic Designer in Music where there are similar concerns).

Discussion then turned to orientation for new Academic Professionals and whether we can provide information at that point. Ken noted that the new faculty orientation before the fall semester could be an appropriate place. Tom will call George Watson about getting a session there.

The Committee wondered how we could be kept abreast of new AP announcements of vacancy. Gary currently receives those for Classified Staff and will call Human Resources to be removed from that list and added to an AP one.

Discussion turned to pay raises. The ACD states that the percentage needs to be the same for all groups for appropriated raises. The Committee will monitor the next rounds of raises.

It was noted that some APs are disenfranchised from voting for Academic Senators. Karl and Annette will talk with Academic Senate President, Darwin Linder.

Karl urged APs to attend the Legislative Coffees.

We discussed having an open meeting in September or October to see if there are any issues out there.

ACD 507-05 is at the lawyers for review. After the meeting, Gary reported that ACD 507-05 has come out of the lawyers' office and has been approved by President Coor.

For the next meeting, think about what should be included in an AP orientation session.

Submitted by Ken Furuta
