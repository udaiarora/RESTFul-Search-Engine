Tools for Selectors



YBP?s Gobi: Search slips and place orders from YBP/L&C (password required).


Harrassowitz OttoEditions: Search the Harrassowitz catalog (password required).


Monographs order form: Firm orders that are not available through YBP/L&C.


Print serials order form: Requests for serials that are only available in print.


Electronic resources suggestion form: Requests for all electronic resources, including e-books, journals available online, free resources, databases, etc.


Web Millennium: For viewing fund information ? updated daily (password required).


Important Ordering Dates:

  • July 1: Begin submitting orders (can order up to 20% of previous fiscal year's allocation prior to receiving firm order line allocations)


  • February 1: A minimum of 65% of allocation (encumbered plus expended)


  • April 1: A minimum of 90% of allocation (encumbered plus expended)


  • May 15: 100% of allocation (encumbered plus expended)


Online Catalog Order Status Glossary:

  • ?Ordered? ? an order has been placed for this item


  • ?Under Consideration? ? this item has arrived on approval, but has not actually been purchased.


  • ?In Process? ? the invoice has been paid, this item is being cataloged and physically processed.
