Inside Out: Sustainability Communication Begins in the Workplace
GreenBiz
This article makes a case for working "inside out," that is, engaging employees in the design, adoption and promotion of a worthwhile sustainability program before significantly communicating the commitment to the outside world.

 

Fannie Mae Cuts Ribbon on Green Data Center
Earth Vision
The Fannie Mae Urbana Technology Center (UTC), a 247,000 square-foot data center and office building, has earned a Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) certification from the U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC), making the data center the first of this type of facility in the country to obtain a LEED rating.

 

Green For Good Launches Website' Featuring Nearly 1400 Green, Organic and Eco-Friendly Products
Environmental News Network
Addressing the growing popularity of ecologically friendly products in the marketplace, Green for Good, LLC today launched its website, www.greenforgood.com.

 

New Report Links Industrial Business Performance, Environmental Compliance

GreenBiz

Industrial competitiveness can be improved by boosting waste management capabilities that also make it easier for companies to comply with environmental regulations, says a new report from the Environment and Business Roundtable.

 

Pitt to Teach Green Building in the Tropics
Earth Vision
Starting this fall, University of Pittsburgh students will be able to travel to the University at Campinas (UNICAMP) in Sao Paulo , Brazil , to conduct research in green construction and sustainable water use technology under a new Pitt program funded by a $3.2 million Integrative Graduate Education and Research Traineeship (IGERT) grant from the National Science Foundation.

A sustainable world needs a sustainable China
Sustain Online
The continuing growth needed to satisfy the aspirations of its 1.3 billion people and the trend of multi-national companies moving their production facilities to China pose a serious threat to the environment. Due to the scale of their environmental impact, the consequences are felt worldwide.

Chamber of Deputies approves sustainable development project for ...
Agencia Brasil
The idea is to combat the deforestation of the Amazon while taking advantage of the region's economic potential. With that objective, the Chamber of Deputies has passed a law that will permit the private sector to obtain land concessions in public forests in the Amazon region for sustainable projects.
