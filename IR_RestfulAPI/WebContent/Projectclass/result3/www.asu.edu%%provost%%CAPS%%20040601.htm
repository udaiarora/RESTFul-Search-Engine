Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

June 1, 2004

Members Present:  Sue Hunter (Chair), Beth Luey, Barbara Trapido-Lurie and Victoria Trotta

 

Excused:  Patricia Feldman

Old business

Revisions to ACD 508-05 and 509-03:  Sue Hunter has changed the text of these documents as requested by Mary Stevens, ASU Deputy General Counsel. The revised documents are presently in the Provost?s office.

 

New business

Visit by Nancy Gutierrez: In response to a request by CAPS, Nancy Gutierrez, Vice Provost, visited the meeting to respond to questions about current AP personnel actions. She also responded to questions concerning several other topics related to APS.

Concerning this year?s AP personnel actions, Dr. Gutierrez stated that all promotion-only cases were approved. Concerning the continuing status cases, President Crow determined that APs in research-related positions would not be granted continuing status, but would be granted promotion. Discussions are underway with these individuals to determine the nature of their new contracts and compensation.

Dr. Gutierrez also discussed the upcoming process of developing new personnel policies and procedures concerning APs. She compared the process to that carried out in developing new faculty personnel policies and procedures. In that case, an ad hoc committee, including ASU legal counsel Mary Stevens, developed the new policy and procedures language that was eventually presented to the Academic Assembly for approval. It is likely that a comparable committee will be formed to develop new AP policies and procedures language.

CAPS Committee membership: With this meeting, Patricia Feldman completes her 3-year term of membership on CAPS. Sue Hunter will be leaving her position at ASU, and thus is also completing her term on CAPS. The committee thanked both for their service on the committee, and especially for the contribution both made as committee chairs.

2004-2005 CAPS officers: The committee selected Barbara Trapido-Lurie as next year?s chair. A new secretary and webmaster will be selected at the September meeting.

Meeting adjourned at 11:00 am.
