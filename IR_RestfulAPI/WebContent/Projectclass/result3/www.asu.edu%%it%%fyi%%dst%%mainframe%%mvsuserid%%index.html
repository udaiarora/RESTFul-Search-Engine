Suspended UserIDs on Academic and Administrative MVS

Information Technology (IT) is merging the Access Management system (which keeps track of MVS UserIDs) with the ASURITE self-subscription system.

This process requires that unaffiliated UserIDs (i.e., UserIDs whose owners are no longer at ASU) be suspended; however, some of these UserIDs are still in use. For example, some UserIDs have been "passed down" from one employee or researcher to another as work assignments changed, but the ownership information has never been updated.

All of the files associated with these UserIDs will be retained; however, no one will be able to access the files after suspension without first contacting IT. You are encouraged to review the list, and if one or more of the UserIDs are being used by you or your group, please call Eldon Norton at (480) 965-5617 or send email to eldon.norton@asu.edu.
