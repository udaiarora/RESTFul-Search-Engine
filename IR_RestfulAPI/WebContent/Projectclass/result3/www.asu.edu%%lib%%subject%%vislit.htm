Visual Literacy

 

 Biblio-File

            The following is a short bibliography containing information that gives introductory information into the research of visual literacy.  For more detailed information such as ephemera or personal papers, consult the Arizona and Southwest Index.

 

SUBJECT HEADINGS

  1. Library of Congress Subject Headings.  14th ed.  Washington, D.C.:  Library of Congress, 1991.

Z695.U4749 HAYDEN REF & ARIZ

Gives exact subject headings used in the ASU Libraries Catalog.  Items related to visual literacy can be found in the Arizona and Southwest Index under such terms as visual perception, visual communication, and visual education.

 

JOURNAL INDEXES

  1. Journal of Visual Verbal Languaging.  1980-1988 / Journal of Visual Literacy. 1988- . Bi-Annual.

LB1068.J68 VISLIT

The Journal of Visual Verbal Languaging was first published by the International Visual Literacy Association (IVLA) in 1981.  The name of the journal was changed to the Journal of Visual Literacy in 1988 to coincide with a change in format.
  1. Visual Literacy Review.  1989- .  Quarterly.

    VL EPH-67, 129-141

 

The Visual Literacy Review is a quarterly newsletter published by the IVLA.  It contains articles on visual literacy and information on the IVLA.
