Welcome Week Events
Must-Attend Events
Join us in our traditional signature events intended to welcome you as a new Sun Devil family. There are events for parents and students and everyone in the family is welcome to attend. Please join us in celebrating the new school year and enjoy the fun!
Workshops
Choose from over 60 workshops to learn more about ASU and its resources. These workshops will provide you and your parents information about academic resources, campus safety, involvement opportunities, faith-based programs, multicultural groups and more!
Department Sponsored Events
Join various departments and organizations on campus for great events to welcome parents and students to ASU. Learn information from the different colleges, enjoy everything from luncheons to tours, and much more.
