The Mercado is the place for people who want to know more.

Whether they are students earning a degree, researchers working to improve Arizona's future, or corporate trainers expanding employee job skills, this is a place where people learn, discover and create. The tenants of this six-building complex (anchored by Arizona State University) generate a synergistic force in downtown Phoenix that enhances the knowledge of the workforce and metropolitan community.

The property's full-service, Class B office space offers views of unique southwestern architecture, lush landscapes, balconies and bricked walkways. Convenient covered parking is available. Tenants can take advantage of state-of-the-art conference facilities throughout Building C. The building specializes in servicing small- to mid-sized events in three stories of computer-mediated classrooms and two computer labs.

Location

The Mercado is the emerging knowledge hub of the thriving Phoenix urban community. Located in the heart of Copper Square, just south of the downtown Phoenix bioscience/education campus (home to TGEN), it is within walking distance of several retail amenities and cultural venues including the Arizona Center, Arizona Science Center, Civic Plaza, Bank One Ballpark, America West Arena, and Heritage Square Historic District.
