The Coaltion of International Students:

The International Student Coalition was created during the Spring semester 2004 and is comprised of all the international clubs and associations of Arizona State University. CIS was born out of the need to unite the voices of all international organizations at Arizona State. Therefore, CIS mission is to promote the interests of the international community of ASU and provide support and representation to its members.
