Timeout - Print Jobs Purged After Two Hours

Print jobs sent to IT-provided printers in the public computing sites can only remain in the print queue for two hours before being automatically purged. This timeout does not apply to other printers.

Print jobs are kept on hold in the print queue and not actually printed until you pay for the print job with your Sun Card. Once payment is made, the print job is released and printed. If you wait longer than two hours to make payment, the print job is automatically purged from the print queue.

A purged print job cannot be recovered. If you need the printed output from a purged job, you have to print the file again.

RECOMMENDATION:

Do not delete a disk file that you're trying to print until after you have obtained the printed output. If the print job is created by a program spooling directly to the printer, you should consider modifying the program to write to a disk file instead of the printer.
