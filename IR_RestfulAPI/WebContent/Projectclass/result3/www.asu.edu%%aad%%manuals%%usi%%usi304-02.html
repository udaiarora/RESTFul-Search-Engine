USI 304–02: Continuing Student Employment

Purpose

To facilitate continued employment of student employees between specific academic periods

Source

Student Financial Assistance Office/Student Employment

Policy

Participants in a student employment program who wish to continue working for the same employer from fall to spring, spring to summer, spring to fall, or summer to fall, must notify their supervisors.

If the employers wish to continue the employment of specific students from fall to spring, spring to summer, spring to fall, or summer to fall, re-advertisement of those jobs is not necessary.

For work-study employment, the department must prepare and submit appropriate paperwork. Student Employment will notify employers of the time frame in which continuation requests should be submitted.

Procedure
  1. For students employed through the Federal Work-Study Program (FWSP):

    1. departments prepare a Personnel Action Form (PAF) requesting a continuation and submit it to Student Employment. If the continuation is processed directly online in the Human Resources Management System, departments send a screenprint of the transaction to Student Employment

      and

    2. Student Employment Office staff will determine eligibility and award FWSP for the next employment period.

  1. For students employed through the hourly program:

    for hourly employment with a stop date of “99/99/9999,” no continuation request is necessary, because the budget roll-over automatically creates a continuing pay line for the next fiscal year.
