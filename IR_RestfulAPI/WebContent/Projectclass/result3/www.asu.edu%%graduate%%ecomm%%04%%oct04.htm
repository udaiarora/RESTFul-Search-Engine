Small Amount of Funding Still Available
A limited number of Special Tuition
Fellowships for Continuing Students are
still available on a first-come, first-serve basis. Graduate students who meet the full eligibility requirements (enrolled in 9 or more credit hours per semester, not an RA or TA, entering second or later year of study, meritorious according to your criteria, and likely in financial need) will be considered. DGS will provide a $1,500 tuition fellowship, half to be paid in the fall and the remaining in the spring. Students enrolled in 6, 7, or 8 credit hours and who otherwise meet the criteria also will be considered. To the extent possible, DGS will provide them with $1,000, paid out in equal amounts in the fall and spring. Academic units nominate students by sending their names and student IDs to Catherine Lebihan at clebihan@asu.edu.
www.asu.edu/graduate/whatsnew/specialTuitionFelProg.htm
