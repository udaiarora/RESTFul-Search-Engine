The Arizona Sash Door and Glass Company furnished all the birch trim and doors for the ninth and tenth floors for $4,032.87. The floor in Robert Renaud's office was Parquet and Mr. Joseph Miller had it under his desk chair, all at a cost of $1,516.80. Entz-White Lumber Supply, Inc. installed the wooden shutters for several of the large arched windows on the tenth floor for $7,037.00.
