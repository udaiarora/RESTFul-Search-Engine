D.3: Initial Employment Procedures

Effective 8/22/91 .. Revised 8/22/91

INITIAL EMPLOYMENT PROCEDURES


After considering the recommendation of a selection advisory committee, the Dean of University Libraries and University Librarian makes an initial appointment offer to a candidate. The terms and conditions of the offer are subject to the discretion of the Dean of University Libraries and University Librarian, who is provided with a recommendation from the Review Committee for rank. The formal offer of an appointment is made by letter from the Dean of University Libraries and University Librarian, setting out the terms and conditions of the position (See ACD 503).


Final, formal acceptance of an offer of appointment is also made by letter and should include specific acceptance or rejection of terms.
