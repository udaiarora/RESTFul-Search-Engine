Service & Safety

In addition to parking safety recommendations and a variety of improvement initiatives, one of the many services that ASU Parking and Transit offers to vehicles parked on the ASU Main Campus is motorist assistance. This free service includes vehicle battery jump starts and unlocking vehicles when the keys are locked inside.

For service, you may contact ASU Main Parking Dispatch at:

(480) 965-0641

Monday - Thursday: 7 AM to 10:30 PM
Friday: 7 AM to 8:30 PM

Hours may vary during school breaks and holidays.

You may also contact ASU Parking Dispatch from:

  • Any ASU Main Campus visitor lot booth
  • Through ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS) at: (480) 965-3456

When you call, you will be asked:

  • The make, model, and color of your vehicle
  • Which parking lot you are in
  • Where you are parked in that lot
  • If you have a photo I.D. and proof of ownership or authorization as a driver.
