Human Resources - New Employee

Benefits-Eligible New Employee Envelope

What's Included?
The Benefits-Eligible New Employee Envelope includes important forms and information sheets on the following new employee services:
  • Welcome Letter
  • Benefits-Eligible New Employee Orientation Checklist
  • Payroll Information
  • Benefits Information
  • Parking Information
  • Computer Accounts Information
  • Sun Card /ASU I.D Information

How do I obtain a Benefits-Eligible New Employee Envelope?
From Your Hiring Department - Once you have accepted a position at ASU, you will most likely receive this envelope of information from your hiring department.

In Person at the ASU Employee Service Center located in the University Services Building, 1551 S. Rural Rd, Tempe. (see Campus MAP).

Through the Mail - Call (480) 965-2701 and a Benefits-Eligible New Employee Envelope (BENEE) can be mailed to you.
