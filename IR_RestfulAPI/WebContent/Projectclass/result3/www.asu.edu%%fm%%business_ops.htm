Business Operations

Business Operations provides centralized support for all Capital Project/Plant Fund, State, Local, and Foundation accounting within University Services. It is comprised of two sub-units: Capital Project/Plant Fund Accounting and State/Local Accounting. 

Capital Project/Plant Fund Accounting directly assists the Capital Programs Management Group regarding the various construction initiatives at ASU. Capital Project/Plant Fund Accounting supports the budget monitoring function and the financial processing steps taken for the various ASU capital project/plant fund initiatives.

State/Local Accounting directly supports all of the major departments (Facilities Management, Capital Programs Management Group, Administrative Services, University Real Estate Development Group, and University Architect Office) within University Services. State/Local Accounting oversees all of the budgetary matters for all state, local, and Foundation accounts for the aforementioned areas within University Services.
