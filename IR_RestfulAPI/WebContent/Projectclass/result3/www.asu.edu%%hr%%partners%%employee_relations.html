What if...? Call your HR Partner to discuss...
I'm concerned with the level of performance of a probationary employee Coaching strategies, progressive discipline policy and process. If needed, discuss how to release, assist with the required letter and payroll process.
I'm concerned with the level of performance of a regular employee Coaching strategies, progressive discipline process and procedures. If needed, assist with required letters.
I have a sick or injured employee needing leave time The appropriate referral and leave process. If needed, assist with required letters.
I'm concerned with an employee's personal issues that are affecting the workplace Options - EAP, coaching/counseling, or progressive discipline when appropriate
There is a feel of disharmony in the workplace Options - EAP, ombudsperson mediation, or employee development classes
I'm concerned about possible discrimination, harassment or inequity Details, policy interpretation and EO/AA referal.
I have a policy question Details and policy interpretation.
