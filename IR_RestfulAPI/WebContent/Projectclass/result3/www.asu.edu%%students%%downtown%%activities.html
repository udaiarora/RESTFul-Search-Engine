Activities & Events

ASU at the Downtown Phoenix campus provides students with a unique opportunity to become involved in the Downtown Phoenix community while pursuing their college degree.  Our campus provides intentional experiences for students to develop personally, socially and academically through integrated activities on-campus and throughout the City of Phoenix. By being embedded in such a diverse community, opportunities to get involved in experiences that interest you are unlimited.

Welcome Week

Thursday, August 17 – Sunday, August 20, 2006

Welcome Week at ASU on the NEW Downtown Phoenix campus

Whether you are new to the college experience or new to ASU's Downtown Phoenix campus, Welcome Week is for you and your family! Members of your College faculty and staff, as well as the student body, have joined together with the Downtown Phoenix campus Provost Office to create a Welcome Week program that is certain to be memorable.  We will provide you with the information and tools you need to make your college transition and experience a successful one.  The events planned will allow you and your family to discover all that our new, vibrant urban campus has to offer. Opportunities to meet new people, learn ASU traditions, have fun, and enjoy the community unique to our campus. Participate, discover, and take advantage of all ASU at the Downtown Phoenix campus has to offer!

See the Full Calendar of events and details below.

First 100 Days

Celebrate with us the First 100 Days of our brand new campus!
The Downtown Phoenix campus and City of Phoenix are celebrating the First 100 Days of the new ASU campus in downtown Phoenix. Programs throughout the First 100 Days will provide ASU students the opportunity to learn more about ASU traditions, services, community leaders, organizations, cultural programs, arts, sports, restaurants and more resources that their new campus and community have to offer them.

Click on the Downtown Devil Calendar button above and mark your calendar early for:

  • City Employment Fair - Thursday, Aug. 31, 4:00 - 7:00 p.m.
  • Downtown Phoenix campus Family Weekend, Sept. 29th – Oct. 1st 
  • Homecoming, Oct. 15th-21st

Celebrate by "Paving a Legacy" at the ASU Downtown Phoenix Campus!

By attending a certain number of events during the First 100 Days, you will be inducted into the "Legacy Circle!"  Legacy Circle members will get a free T-shirt and will be invited to a banquet on December 5th. Get your passport to Paving the Legacy at Welcome Week or online!
