Eugene Zimmerman Academic Scholarship

Application

 

Description:

The Department of Languages & Literatures annually selects a student to receive this scholarship established in honor of Eugene Zimmerman.  The purpose of the scholarship is to support an outstanding German major (junior or senior) who intends to continue his/her studies in German at ASU for at least one more year.

 

The amount of the scholarship for 2007-08 is $1000, and will be awarded in the fall semester.   

Criteria:

1.      Must be a full-time undergraduate student with a junior or senior class standing (having earned 56 or more credit hours) by time of enrollment in Fall 2007 semester.

2.      Must have a declared major in German, pursuing the Bachelor of Arts, College of Liberal Arts and Sciences, at ASU Tempe campus. 

3.      Minimum overall cumulative grade point average required is 3.50, with a minimum 3.50 GPA required in German.

4.      Must have completed German 202, its equivalent; or higher level German course

5.      Must register for full-time status to continue the study of German at ASU, Fall 2007.

6.      Financial need may be considered. Student should have a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and the resulting Student Aid Report (SAR) on file at the Student Financial Assistance Office at ASU and submit a copy of his/her Statement of Need and a copy of his/her Financial Aid Notification with the scholarship application. 

Selection Process: It is the responsibility of the Department of Languages & Literatures to select the scholarship recipient. 

 

·         Students must submit: (1) the scholarship application, (2) transcripts and/or DARS report, (3) financial need documents, and (4) a brief statement addressing the following questions: What motivated you to choose German as your major?  Please describe how your study of German will contribute to your plans for a career or profession? How is the study of German going to help you in the future, either in attaining an advanced degree or in your profession/career?

·         The application and brief statement must be typed.
