Books

Books are on the 3rd floor of Noble Science and Engineering Library in the TK call number area. Books on Applied Optics and Photonics are in TA1501-TA1820. Books on Telecommunications are in TK4001-TK7725.  Government Documents are in Hayden and some are online. Some important reference books are kept in the Science Reference Collection on the first floor.

To find books in Noble Library use the ASU Online Catalog.

  • To find a specific book, use the TITLE or AUTHOR search.
  • To find books about, electrical engineering use the SUBJECT HEADINGS search:
                Electric Engineering
  • To find books on a particular topic use a KEYWORD search:
                Signal Processing Digital Techniques
                Noise Control Engineering
                Electronic Circuits design
                Video Compression
               Wireless communication systems.
                Frequency Synthesizers

Journals and Journal Articles

    journals are on the 2nd floor of Noble Science and Engineering Library, mainly in the call number.
  • Current issues are on the first floor in the Current Periodicals  Room.
  • Microforms are in the northeast corner on the first floor.
  • Older volumes are kept on the east side of the floor. Some older volumes are kept in storage; request these items from either the Noble Library Circulation Desk or Noble Library Information Desk.
  • For online availability, search for the journal title(s) in Find - Journals .

To find journal articles about use one or more of the following journal indexes:

  • Applied Science and Technology Abstracts
  • Inspec
     Physics, Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. Also Includes Biotechnology;  Design; Electronics;  Energy; Geography; Industrial Design; Information Science; Materials Engineering;
  • Ei Compendex Web 
  • Web of Science/Science Citation Index (1955-)
