Definition of Classroom Types

  • Traditional - classroom is equipped with an overhead projector and screen.
  • Basic Mediation - classroom is equipped with a laptop connection, video projector, overhead projector, screen, VCR (or DVD/VCR player), slide projector (in some rooms), and audio system.
  • Instructor Mediation - classroom is equipped with an instructor computer workstation, video projector, overhead projector, screen, VCR (or DVD/VCR player), and audio system.
  • Student Mediation - classroom is equipped with an instructor computer workstation, student computer workstations, video projector, overhead projector, screen, VCR (or DVD/VCR player), slide projector (in some rooms), and audio system.

    Note: Some rooms have more specialized equipment than others.
