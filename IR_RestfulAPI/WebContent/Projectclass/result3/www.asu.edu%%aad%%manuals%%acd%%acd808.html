ACD 808: Lost, Stolen, or Found Property

Purpose

To provide procedures for reporting lost and stolen property and for handling found property

Sources

Arizona Revised Statutes §§ 12–941 to –946
ASU Department of Public Safety
University policy

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

University Property

Lost or Stolen Items

When university property is lost or stolen, a faculty member or academic professional aware of the missing item must:

  1. promptly report the loss or theft to the ASU Department of Public Safety (ASU DPS)

    and

  2. provide ASU DPS with a complete description of the item.

If assistance is required in furnishing the description to ASU DPS, the faculty member or academic professional reporting the loss should contact Property Control for property control numbers and related information.

ASU DPS will be responsible for:

  1. notifying other university departments of the missing items, as appropriate

    and

  2. investigating the loss or theft.

Found Items

University property found by a faculty member or academic professional must be turned in or reported to ASU DPS.

Personal Property

Lost or Stolen Items

When personal property is lost or stolen while on campus, the owner may report it to:

  1. the Memorial Union (MU) Information Desk staff

    and

  2. ASU DPS, if theft is suspected.

Unclaimed personal items are held at the MU Information Desk for up to 60 days.

Found Items

Personal property found by a faculty member or academic professional must be turned in to the lost and found operating in the building in which the items were found, e.g., Hayden Library or the MU Information Desk. Property found by ASU DPS employees must be handled and impounded in accordance with ASU DPS internal policies and procedures.

All university departments operating a lost and found, e.g., the MU or ASU DPS, temporarily store such items as a service to the community. Items not claimed within 60 days will be donated to a charitable organization, sold, or otherwise disposed of in accordance with Arizona Revised Statutes §§ 12–941 to –946.

Cross-References

For additional information on reporting lost or stolen items, see:

  1. the Department of Public Safety Policies and Procedures Manual: DPS 203–02, “Reporting Property Crimes”

    and

  2. the Parking and Transit Services Policies and Procedures Manual: PTS 105, “Replacement of Lost, Damaged, or Stolen Decals or Keycards.”
