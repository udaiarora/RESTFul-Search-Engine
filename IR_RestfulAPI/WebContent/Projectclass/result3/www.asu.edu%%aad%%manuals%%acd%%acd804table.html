The employee discusses the complaint or concern about smoking with the area supervisor.

The area supervisor tries to reach a mutually satisfactory agreement and to determine if smoke can be contained in a smoking area

If the complaint or concern cannot be resolved to mutual satisfaction, the employee addresses the concern through each succeeding supervisor up to the responsible administrative official.

If the complaint or concern is still unresolved, the employee contacts Human Resources for assistance.

The area supervisor consults with Human Resources on a resolution.

If no resolution acceptable to the complainant can be reached, the area is designated a nonsmoking area.
