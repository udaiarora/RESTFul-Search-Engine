    ATP NOTES

    1. MCCCD's COM230 also satisfies [L] requirements.
    2. Applicants for the social work major must have a minimum of 240 hours of work or volunteer experience in a social work related setting. SWU291 includes a component of 40 volunteer hours, which may be applied to the 240 hour requirement.
    3. No [HU] courses are required, if two [HU] courses from previous pages were selected.
