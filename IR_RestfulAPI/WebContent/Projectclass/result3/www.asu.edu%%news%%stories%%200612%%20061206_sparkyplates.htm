December 6, 2006

Sparky license plates provide perfect gift for Sun Devil fanatics

In many ways, it's the perfect gift: one size fits all, there's no complicated assembly process and gift wrap is unnecessary. An ASU college license plate perks up any car, and the money spent on the gift goes to a good cause: ASU students!

ASU's college plate, also known as a ?Sparky plate,? is available through the Arizona Department of Transportation's Motor Vehicle Division at the Web site (www.servicearizona.com). The plate, which costs $25 (in addition to a car's normal registration fees), is the perfect gift for the Sun Devil fan in your family, or a great gift to yourself to show pride as an ASU faculty or staff member. Seventeen dollars of the $25 fee for the plate is tax-deductible and is donated to the Medallion Scholarship, an ASU Alumni Association scholarship fund.

Another reason to give the gift of Sparky plates this season: ASU is getting clobbered in the statewide battle for college license plate dominance. With only a few weeks left in 2006, the University of Arizona is leading ASU in total numbers of license plates, boasting 11,337 plates to ASU's 8,143.

Those who are interested in buying a Sparky plate should drop by the event promotion desk at the Memorial Union on the Tempe campus Dec. 5. Alumni Association staff will be at the desk from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. to offer assistance with the ordering process, and those ordering plates can receive a free ASU license plate holder for doing so. The event promotion desk is located across from the union's information desk, located on the ground floor of the MU. All buyers should bring their current vehicle plate or registration information.

For additional information about the Sparky plate program, visit the Web site (www.asu.edu/alumni/involve/plates.html).
