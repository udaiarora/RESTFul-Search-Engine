After the 1964 Christmas holiday, Emma Luhrs Stroud flew up to Palo Alto, California to visit her daughter, Jean, and her family. On a weekend early in February, 1965, they all drove down to Carmel, California. Emma liked to take off her shoes and stockings and go wading on the beach along the water's edge. She was doing just that, at Carmel, when she saw a little larger braker coming toward her. She turned to get away from the breaker, but one foot had sunken into the wet sand. In turning, she fell twisting and breaking her hip. An ambulance took her to the hospital in Palo Alto. Jean immediately telephoned Ella Luhrs Taylor and George Luhrs Jr.


Being shortly after the end of the year of 1964, George Luhrs Jr. was deep in bookwork and the affairs of the Luhrs' Properties and did not feel he could get away at that time. He was doing the work of several people and was busy from early morning until late at night. He was sacrificing everything for the successful operation of the Luhrs' Properties. Later, and since, he deeply regretted that he did not just stop everything he was doing and with his sister, Ella, fly to Palo Alto to see his sister, Emma.
