SAS/SPECTRAVIEW Software User's Guide

Introduction

This appendix gives information about the two SAS data sets that are used for the book's examples.

If your site has installed the sample data sets, they are found in the sample subdirectory for the product. For example, for a UNIX environment, the location may be !SASROOT/spectraview/sample. Please note that this location is the default and is not necessarily the location of the sample programs at your site. Contact your SAS Software Representative for information on how to access the sample library files.
