Departmental Web Sites

How does Department Webspace work?

Webspace requests for department or college webspace on www.asu.edu are handled through the administrating department or college for your area. Webpages are stored and accessed through AFS space already assigned to a department. As with Personal Webspace, you will need to have an ASURITE account as this is how you will be granted permission to use department web space. Once you've received your confirmation after emailing the appropriate webmaster, you should return to the Jukebox and continue to learn more on how to create and upload your pages.

My Department already has Webspace. How do I get access to make updates?

Similar to getting new space, you need to contact the Webmaster/Pagemaster in your area. They are the people responsible for providing write-access to their department's or college's webpages.

Requesting Department/College Webspace

To setup your department or college unit's web space, please find your administrating campus department and contact the webmaster for that area using the email link provided. Be sure to explain your intended usage of the space and include the following information:

  • Name of Department or College Unit
  • Name of person who will administer the webpages
  • Phone number of future-administrator
  • ASURITE UserID of future-administrator
  • Requested AFS Directory Name (this will correspond with your site's web address, for example, if a department in the College of Liberal Arts & Sciences requests a directory "gofarther", their web address would be http://www.asu.edu/clas/gofarther if such directory did not already exist)
  • List of ASU Web site URLs with corresponding Webmaster/Pagemaster (ASU Webmaster Directory)

Exceptions

Of course, there are exceptions to the process this webpage describes. Many ASU departments maintain web servers independent from www.asu.edu. Contact the Webmaster/Pagemaster in your area for details and information on department-specific web environments.
