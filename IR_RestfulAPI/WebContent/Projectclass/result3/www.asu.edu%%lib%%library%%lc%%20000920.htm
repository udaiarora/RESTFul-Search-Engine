Librarians' Council


Minutes of Meeting


September 20, 2000

Meeting called to order at 3:04 p.m.


Chair: Pat Etter
Secretary: Gordon Dutrisac
A quorum was present.

I. Approval of Minutes

 

Minutes of April 2000 meeting were approved as written.

 

II. Chair & Executive Committee Report


The Chair thanked JoAnn Mulvihill and Annie Platoff for accepting their respective appointments as Kenneth P. Knepp Parliamentarian and as Librarians' Council Web Master. Dean Sherrie Schmidt was also thanked for providing refreshments for this first meeting of the new Council. It was announced that the deans approved changes to the following sections of the Librarians’ Handbook: D.2, E.3, G.2.2 and G.2.4.

III. Dean's Report


The Dean presented Marilyn Wurzburger with a gavel and thanked her for working as Chair last year.


The Dean asked librarians to support President Coor's "Learner-Centered Education" initiative in all that we do. The Dean echoed President Coor's support of Proposition 301 and asked that we help spread the word about the value of education. The Dean also addressed the need to carefully look at our serials' expenditure in the coming year.


The Dean wished the assembled good luck the upcoming year. A question and answer session followed.

IV. Committee Reports


Personnel Committee - Helen Seaton


The Committee continued work on "Marion the Librarian", fine tuning the 2nd and 4th year Marions. The Committee is continuing to revise the Librarians' Handbook.

 

Review Committee - Mary Swaty


The Committee has finalized its calendar and has begun work on personnel actions.

 

Research and Professional Development Committee


No report.


Compensation Committee


No report.


Academic Senate


No report.

 

Committee on Academic Professional Status - Helen Seaton

CAPS set its agenda for the upcoming year. CAPS will meet with a representative of the Academic Associates to discuss their status on campus and any similarities with the Academic Professionals. CAPS is also planning a spring workshop to help APs prepare promotional packets. It was noted that the librarians are unique among ASU APs in terms of support from peers for their promotional process. Other CAPS agenda items include an invitation to the new Vice-Provost Gail Hackett and a potential brown bag series highlighting the achievements of campus APs.

 

Web Master - Annie Platoff

The Web Master noted that she has normalized the procedure for updating the online Librarians' Handbook, and has created links on the Librarians' Council web site to assist members to track the progress of revisions. This year the Web Master hopes to create a job description highlighting the position's roles and responsibilities.

 

V. Old Business

 

None

 

VI. New Business

  1. Nomination and Election of the Nominations Committee.

    Annie Platoff nominated Jennie Duvernay for the Nominations and Elections Committee, and JoAnn Mulvihill nominated Linda DeFato. Both accepted the nomination (Linda in absentia through JoAnn). Marilyn Wurzburger moved to close the nominations. The motion was seconded and approved. It was unanimously approved that Jennie and Linda serve on the Nominations and Elections Committee, along with the past chair, Marilyn Wurzburger.

  2. Personnel Committee

    • Revisions to Librarians' Handbook Section G.2.1 (Personnel Reviews - Probationary)

      Changes requiring the addition of an updated job description to probationary review packets were read. There was no discussion and the Council accepted the changes unanimously.

    • Revisions to Librarians' Handbook Section G.2.3 (Personnel Reviews - Notification Process)

      Helen Seaton read the proposed changes that direct the Assistant Dean for Personnel to act as moderator in cases of negative reviews. A short discussion followed and the motion was passed unanimously.

  3. Executive Committee

    Pat Etter read the revisions to the bylaws and Librarians' Handbook C.2.1 (Standing Rules and Committees - Executive Committee). The revisions outline the duties of Parliamentarian and Web Master as appointed non-voting members of the Executive Council. These changes will be voted on at the next meeting.

 

 

Meeting Adjourned at 3:49

 

Gordon Dutrisac, Secretary
