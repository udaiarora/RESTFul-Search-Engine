"How to Mouse-Click Yourself to a Lawsuit in 5 Easy Steps"
Chris Pierson

Presentation

Students in attendance will understand the legal ramifications for their on-line actions. We will cover everything from copyright lawsuits involving RIAA and MPAA for music and movie downloading; inappropriate use/misuse of student IDs and passwords; how your actions on a wireless network can land you in jail; how failing to watch what you say online can land you in court; and give an update on the top malicious code attacks that are stealing your identity.
