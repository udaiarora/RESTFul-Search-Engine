Курс по македонски јазик

Macedonian Language Course

Course

The 2007 CLI will offer Introductory and Intermediate Macedonian  (subject to funding and sufficient enrollment). Following the courses we will offer a three-week overseas practicum in Ohrid, Macedonia. There is an academic-year program available.  The eight-week introductory course is comprised of two classes: MAK 101: Elementary Macedonian I and MAK 102: Elementary Macedonian II, for a total of 8 semester credit hours.  Elementary Macedonian I (or its equivalent) is a pre-requisite for Elementary Macedonian II.  The eight-week intermediate course is comprised of two classes: MAK 201: Intermediate Macedonian I and MAK 202: Intermediate Macedonian II, for a total of 8 semester credit hours.  Elementary Macedonian (or its equivalent) is a pre-requisite for Intermediate Macedonian.

Course Schedule: June 4-July 27, 2007, Monday to Friday, 8:30 am to 12:30 pm., ASU Main Campus.

Practicum Schedule: July 30-August 17, 2007, Ohrid, Macedonia
