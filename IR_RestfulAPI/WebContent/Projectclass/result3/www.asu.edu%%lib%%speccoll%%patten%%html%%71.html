Mattioli, Pietro Andrea

1500-1577.

Petri Andreae Matthioli . . . Compendium de plantis
omnibus: una cum earum iconibus, de quibus scripsit suis in commentariis
in Dioscoridem editis, in eorum studiosorum commodum, atque usum: qui plantis
conquirendis, ac indagandis student.

Accessit praeterea ad calcem Opusculum de itinere, quo e Verona in Baldum
montem plantarum refertissimum itur. Venetiis: In Officinia Valgrisiana,
1571. First work: an abridgment of the botanical portion of Mattioli's
Commentaries without the text of Dioscorides. Second work: One of
the earliest local floras, describing the plants on "Monte Baldo" in Verona
Italy, much visited by naturalists at the time is missing in our copy.
Includes 921 woodcuts. References: Johnston, S. H. Cleveland coll. 105.
Defective copy. Final 14 p. (3M5-3N4) wanting. Title page damaged, partly
illegible.

For each of the plants in this work, Mattioli gives its name in a variety
of languages, followed by the genus when known, the form of the plant,
the locations where it is found, its qualities, its powers, and any cautions. 
Each plant also has an illustration.  The marginal notes give the
sources for the information provided.  The work has a complete index
of Latin plant names.

Subjects:
Botany; Medicine.
