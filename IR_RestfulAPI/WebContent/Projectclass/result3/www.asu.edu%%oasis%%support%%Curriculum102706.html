OASIS Training Curriculum
Click the course title to view detail, click the title again to close. To enroll, click the Enroll link and follow the instructions. Use the Send Request link to send your request to the OASIS training team.
Note:This curriculum page can only be viewd by using Internet Explore.

Instructions for enrolling instructor led courses:

  • Select OASIS from drop down window
  • Type OASIS in the search field
  • Click the Search button
  • Find the course you want to enroll, click the Select button to view description
  • Click See Schedule for time, date and location
  • Click the Register link to enroll or get on the waiting list

Instructions for enrolling courses in Blackboard:

  • Log in to Blackboard
  • Click on the Classes tab
  • Type OASIS into the search field and click Go
  • Find the course you want to enroll, click the Enroll button
  • Click the course link to start training
