What levels of information can you find in an index?

Bibliographic indexes:

Bibliographic indexes include information that will help you find an article, but not the article text itself. These indexes contain the following elements:
  • Title
  • Author
  • Magazine or journal title in which it was published
  • Date
  • Page numbers
  • May include abstracts (summaries of the article?s contents)
Examples of bibliographic indexes include:
  • Inspec
  • MLA Bibliography
  • Sociological Abstracts
