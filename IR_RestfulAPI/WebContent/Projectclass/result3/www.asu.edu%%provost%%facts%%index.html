University Evaluation Surveys

Graduating Senior Report Card
Over 5,000 graduating seniors complete this exit survey each year when they apply for graduation. Questions focus on overall experiences at ASU, experiences in their academic major and general education courses, university services, and post-graduation employment and education plans.
Graduate & Law Student Report Card
Approximately 2,000 graduating graduate and law students complete this exit survey each year when they apply for graduation. Questions focus on overall experiences at ASU, experiences in their academic major, and post-graduation plans.
First Year Student Survey
First-year, first-time ASU students receive a survey with items focusing on reasons for attending ASU, early experiences, expectations, and background demographics. Students receive the survey mid-way through their first semester. It is administered in composition courses with the invaluable assistance of the Department of Composition and course instructors.
Undergraduate Alumni Survey
Undergraduate alumni are sent a survey approximately 3 years after graduation from ASU. They are asked about their perceptions of ASU, how well ASU prepared them for subsequent employment and education, as well as employment and post-education activities.
Graduate Alumni Survey
Graduate alumni receive a survey approximately 3 years after graduation from ASU. They are asked about their perceptions of ASU, how well ASU prepared them for subsequent employment and education, and their employment.
