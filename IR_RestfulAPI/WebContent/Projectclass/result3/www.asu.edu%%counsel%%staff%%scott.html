Kathy Scott has been with the ASU Office of General Counsel since 1986. As Assistant to the Vice President for University Administration and Legal Affairs she provides a broad range of support to the Vice President and serves as the office manager and business manager. Ms. Scott also manages the outside legal consultant appointments and accounts on behalf of various university departments. Prior to moving to Arizona, she was the office manager of several Washington, DC law firms. In her spare time Kathy takes continuing education classes to maintain her Arizona real estate license.
