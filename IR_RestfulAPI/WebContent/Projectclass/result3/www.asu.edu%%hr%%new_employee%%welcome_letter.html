Human Resources - New Employee

Welcome Letter

Dear New Employee:

Congratulations on your employment at Arizona State University. We look forward to helping make your transition into the ASU community as pleasant as possible.

The Benefits Eligible New Employee Envelope contains instructions and forms necessary to enter you into our Human Resources Management System (HRMS) and to insure that ASU complies with a federal law requiring all of our employees to verify their employment eligibility. Entry in HRMS is the key to your being paid and receiving all other services. You may submit your payroll paperwork in person when you arrive on campus at the HR Employee Service Center located in the University Services Building, or during your HR Employee Orientation. You also have the option to mail notarized verification and payroll forms prior to your arrival to: ASU HR Employee Service Center, P.O. Box 875612, Tempe, Arizona, 85287-5612. Early submission of these papers expedites new employee processes.

The Benefits-Eligible New Employee Envelope also contains a map that is color-coded to the location of services; a checklist of tasks necessary to obtain parking, computing and other services; and information about orientation. HR Employee Orientation is designed to acquaint new benefits-eligible faculty and staff with ASU, provide onsite sign-on for services, and provide information about major benefits such as health insurance and retirement plans. The Benefits Guide can be read prior to attending an orientation to get a general overview of the benefits available to employees, and specific questions can be answered by the benefits experts during the orientation session.  For additional information you may visit the Benefits web site.

HR Employee Orientation sessions are scheduled every Monday. You must be pre-registered to attend. You may ask your department to register you on a day you will be able to attend, or you may register yourself by calling (480) 965-4751 or e-mail lps@asu.edu. When registering please include your name, ASU ID # (if issued), contact phone number and your employment category, i.e., faculty, post-doctorate, academic professional, service professional, or administrative or classified staff. More Orientation details can be found at the new employee web page.

Attention to these details along with your department's submission of the Hiring Process Report (HPR) before the start of your employment will ensure that your paychecks and your insurance benefits begin without delay and will expedite delivery of other services.

Welcome to ASU!
