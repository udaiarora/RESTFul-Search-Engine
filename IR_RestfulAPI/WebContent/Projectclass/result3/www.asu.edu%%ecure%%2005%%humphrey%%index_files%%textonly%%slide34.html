Future Directions

  • A field of funded research is needed to study issues about the preservation of and access to research data.

  • Areas for research:

    • The Data Economy: "who gets what data, when and how";

    • The Life Cycle of Data: the course of data and its corresponding metadata from the earliest stages of planning through to the secondary uses of data;
