Just Transition Coalition Secures First Step towards a Sustainable Economic Future for Navajo and Hopi People

UN Observer

Since the closing of Mohave Generating Station, the Black Mesa coal slurry line and the Black Mesa coal mine, both Navajo and Hopi Tribal economies have been hit hard by the loss of revenues from coal and water royalties. The Just Transition for Southern California Edison Company (SCE) plan to establish an account to track and accumulate revenues from the sale of sulfur credits was developed to help the tribes offset the loss of revenues.

Competition Challenges Students to Develop Environmentally-Friendly Technology
Voice of America – USA

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency holds an annual nationwide competition, the P-3: the People, Prosperity and the Planet Student Design Competition for Sustainability, to encourage college students to research, develop and design scientific and technical solutions to sustainability challenges. Semi-finalists exhibit their entries on the National Mall, and winners receive small development grants.

 

UCLA Adopts Sustainability Charter
UC Los Angeles - Los Angeles, CA, USA

Chancellor Albert Carnesale has chartered a permanent committee to advise his office on advancing sustainability. The purpose of the Campus Sustainability Committee is to promote sustainability in planning, development and operation of the campus environment and facilities, as well as in the development of sustainability initiatives in education, research and community engagement.

USAID Releases 2005 NGO Sustainability Index for Central and Eastern Europe and Eurasia Region
USAID (press release) - Washington, DC, USA

The principal U.S. agency extending assistance to countries recovering from disaster, trying to escape poverty, and engaging in democratic reforms, the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) released its ninth edition of the non-governmental organizations (NGO) Sustainability Index for the Europe and Eurasia (E&E) region. “USAID is a leader that is finding solutions and tactics for civil society sustainability, and the NGO Sustainability Index allows us to continue to track progress and report on creative, new strategies," Drew Luten, USAID Acting Assistant Administrator for Europe & Eurasia, said.
