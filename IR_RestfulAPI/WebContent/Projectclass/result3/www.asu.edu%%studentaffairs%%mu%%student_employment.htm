Student Employment

A variety of student employment opportunities are available through Memorial Union. The department offers flexible work schedules to accommodate class schedules and promotion from within the various work groups is encouraged.

Search for jobs

Student employees are also encouraged to participate in SELECT (Student Employees Learning, Experiencing, and Communicating Together) which offers professional development workshops and discussions during the semester. Past topics have included time management, leadership development and resume writing.
