IT : Mainframe MVS/TSO —   IBM Assembler



The IBM assembler is used when no other reasonable alternative (i.e., a higher level language) is available to achieve the desired instructional or research purposes.

WARNING
If you use a REGION size larger than 8M, your results will be unpredictable. Please refer to the Programming Guide for Assembler H for more information.

Listed below are the reference manuals for assembler:

  • OS/VS-VM/370 Assembler: Programmer's Guide (IBM No. SC26-4036)
  • IBM System/370: Principles of Operation (IBM No. SA22-7085)

There are four cataloged procedures available for assembler. Sample JCL for the four procedures is contained in the following HELP topics.

ASMHC
JCL used to invoke the H-level IBM Assembler
ASMHCG
JCL used to invoke Assembler and the Loader
ASMHCL
JCL used to invoke Assembler and the Linkage Editor
ASMHCLG
JCL used to invoke Assembler, the Linkage Editor, and execute the resulting Load Module.
