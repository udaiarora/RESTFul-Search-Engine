ACD 805: Eating and Drinking in Academic Areas

Purpose

To protect the health and safety of persons in the university community

Source

University policy

Policy

Eating or drinking in university theaters, concert or lecture halls, laboratories, and libraries is prohibited. Under ordinary circumstances eating or drinking is also prohibited in classrooms unless authorized by the faculty member assigned to the class.

Drinking alcoholic beverages on campus is allowed only in designated areas and at events when permission has been obtained at least seven days in advance through the ASU Department of Public Safety.

All litter must be properly discarded.

Cross-References

For additional information about alcohol on campus, see:

    and

  2. The University Student Initiatives Policies and Procedures Manual—USI 106–03, “Alcohol and Other Drugs on Campus. ”
