Lot Closures During Event Parking

Sometimes, spaces in specific parking lots are needed to facilitate event parking. When the need arises to either partially or completely close lots, ASU Parking and Transit Services will endeavor to notify all those people affected as soon as possible. ASU Parking and Transit Services will notify parking decal holders in one (or all) of the following ways:
  • Notices in the ASU Insight.
  • Notices on the ASU Parking and Transit Services phone information system (480) 965-6209
  • Signs posted in the lots that are affected
