PCS 1002–05: Disposal of Hazardous Materials

Purpose

To ensure proper disposal of hazardous materials

Sources

Arizona Constitution Article IX, § 7
University policy

Applicability

University employees who wish to dispose of hazardous materials

Procedure

Employees who wish to dispose of hazardous materials should consult the Environmental Health & Safety Policies and Procedures Manual (EHS)—EHS 401, “Hazardous Waste Management.”
