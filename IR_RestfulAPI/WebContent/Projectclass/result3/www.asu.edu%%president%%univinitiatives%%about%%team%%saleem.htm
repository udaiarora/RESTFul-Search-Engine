Safwat Saleem
Graphic Designer


Safwat joined UI in February 2004. He has worked on many major projects, including design of the UI website, development of the Consortium for Science, Policy and Outcomes website, the redesign of the ASU Experience web portal, the creation of a graphic identity for the University Public Schools initiative and version two of the New American University.

Originally from Pakistan, Safwat moved to the United States in 1998 to attend the University of Southern Mississippi where he went on to receive his undergraduate degree in Computer Science. He has now received his Master's degree in Graphic Information Technology at ASU.

 

Safwat is also the editor of an online rock-music magazine called Bandbaja that focuses on independent rock bands from Pakistan. Safwat's future aspirations include directing his own feature film someday.
