Carpool Parking Policy and Procedures

Car Pool Policy & Application - MS Word

CARPOOL :

(Defined to register for a reserved carpool parking space). Two or more ASU faculty/staff employees commuting to the work place together a minimum of two days per week or full-time students (i.e., undergraduate, 12 credit hours per semester or graduate, 9 credit hours per semester) commuting to ASU a minimum of 3 days per week.

PARKING SPACES

Reserved Carpool Parking spaces are available in Parking Structures 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, Lot 3, Lot 72 and Lot 59. These spaces are clearly signed for carpool permits only.

These spaces are available on a first-come-first served basis and are reserved for carpools from 7:00 AM to 4:00 PM , Monday – Friday.

When carpoolers drive alone, they are not allowed to park in the designated carpool spaces.

PARKING DECALS AND CARPOOL PERMITS :

At least one member of the carpool must have a parking decal for PS-1, PS-2, PS-3, PS‑4, PS-5, Lot 3, Lot 72 or Lot 59. The license plate number of each vehicle using the carpool permit must be listed under the decal number as well.The vehicle parked in the reserved carpool space must display the lot specific parking decal as well as the carpool permit. Carpool permits may be used in other carpool areas when cross-parking after 4:00 p.m.

OBTAINING A CARPOOL PERMIT :( There is no charge for initial carpool permits.)

1. A parking decal must first be purchased from the Decal Sales office. All parking decals are sold on a space available basis.

After the ASU parking decal has been purchased, a carpool registration form needs to be completed and returned to the ASU Travel Reduction Office, Towers 103 at Fifth Street and Forest Avenue .

Registered carpools will receive one carpool permit to each carpool group.

4. Carpools must be renewed each year with the Travel Reduction Program. Renewal of carpool parking space use is not automatic with parking decal renewal. The carpool permit is regulated by the parking decal year.

5. All carpool registration information must be kept current.

NOTE: THERE IS A $25.00 REPLACEMENT FEE ON ALL LOST/STOLEN/DESTROYED CARPOOL PERMITS

*If you need assistance in finding a carpool partner, please call our office for a list of possible matches.
