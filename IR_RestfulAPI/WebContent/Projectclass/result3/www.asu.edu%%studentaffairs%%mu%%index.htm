New Look for the MU website!

The Memorial Union website is getting a new look and feel. Along with the updated design navigation menus are being upgraded for better ease of use. A "rolling update" is being performed, which means pages will be upgraded one at a time without taking the entire site down. Thank you for your patience during this update.

Sparky's Den

Relax with friends and squeeze in a few frames at Sparky's Den between classes or after a long day of studying. Located on the lower level of the Memorial Union, Sparky's Den has 8 lanes of bowling, 15 billiard tables and a large arcade.
