Access

The OASIS functional teams are currently determining the ASU business requirements and necessary security authorizations for employees to perform their jobs in the new system.  Specific Data Stewards/Trustees have been identified from each functional unit to work with the OASIS Security Team.  Since the current administrative systems display data in different ways than the new Student Administration or Human Capital Management systems, access approvals will not automatically transfer.  

 

To request access, or to check on the status of a request, click on this link: https:\\ep.oasis.asu.edu

 

Click on the links in the right-hand menu for step-by-step instructions -----> .

General questions about security/access can be sent to the OASIS Security Team.

Problems once you have received your access should be reported through the ASU UTO Help Desk 480-965-6500.
