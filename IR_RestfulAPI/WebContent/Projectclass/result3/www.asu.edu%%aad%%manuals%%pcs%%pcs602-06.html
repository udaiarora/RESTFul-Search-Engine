PCS 602–06: Off-Site Inventories of Government-Owned Property

Purpose

To establish a policy to schedule off-site property inventories for government-owned property

Source

Federal Acquisition Regulation § 45.508

Property Control

Applicability

All government-owned property

Policy

Government-owned property controlled by ASU Property Control and existing at subcontractor facilities is inventoried once every two years.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Prepare and forward the inventory listing to all facilities in custody of government-owned property.
Facility with government property
  1. Review the ASU Property Control inventory listing and locate property.
  1. Mark property as either Found, Not Found, or Deleted on report.
  1. Forward report to sponsored property coordinator.
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Review, report, and resolve inventory discrepancies with the facility.
  1. Report the results to the appropriate government agency and/or the cognizant government property administrator.
endtable
