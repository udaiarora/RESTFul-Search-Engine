Computer Workstation Printing

When printing from computer workstations in the libraries the computer will prompt for an input of a name and password. Any name and password is acceptable and it is associated with the print job list on the print stations.

 

When printing documents from MyASU, right click on the links to the documents and choose "Open in New Window". Then click on the printer icon in the new window.

 

Print stations are located in the following locations:

Public print stations charge a cost of $0.08 per page and require the use of a Sun Card or library copy card to pay for printing.

 

Wireless Laptop Printing

Important!
Your wireless connection must be working before running this installation. If it isn't, the install will fail. (Please see Disclaimer).

  1. Download ASUL-WiFiPrint.zip file, and then Unzip it to a folder.
  2. Depending on what library you are in, double click on the appropriate file to run:

    3.  
    • Architecture and Environmental Design Library: arch.bat

    • Hayden Library: hayden.bat

    • Music Library: music.bat

    • Noble Science and Engineering Library: noble.bat

  3. When the installation is complete, you will be prompted to reboot. Hit Cancel (it is actually not necessary).

You should now be ready to print. Choose the printer named "lp" in your printer list.

 

If you change libraries, re-run the proper .bat file associated with that library to re-install the corresponding driver.

Disclaimer: This feature is not officially supported by our printing vendor, and while every effort has been taken to test it, there may be some laptops that are incompatible with this service. If you do not feel comfortable installing this device, please do not do so. If you have any troubles with this "unsupported" feature, please feel free to stop by the Library Systems Help Desk (in the Hayden Library on the Upper Concourse level) for assistance. We will do our best to help you.

 

Photocopying

There are self service photocopies located throughout all the libraries on campus. In general there is usually at least one on each floor of the library. Photocopiers charge a cost of $0.10 per page if paying with cash, or $0.08 per page with Sun Card or library copy card.

 

Microfilm Printing

Printing from microfilm costs $0.15 per page if using cash, or $0.12 per page with Sun Card or library copy card.

 

Scanning

The Copy Center at the Noble Library now has a scanner that can convert your copy to a PDF file that can be saved to a disk, printed or emailed (if not too large). There is a. $2.50 minimum cost for 1 - 10 pages. Please call 480-965-7497 for more information.

 

Copy Centers

Full service Copy Centers are available in the Hayden and Noble Libraries. These centers provide:

  • On demand photocopies ($0.13 per page)
  • On demand microform copies ($0.20 per page)
  • Binding (call for prices)
  • Color photocopies (call for prices)
  • Lamination (call for prices)
  • B/W digital transfer (call for prices)
  • Faxing (call for prices)

You can contact the copy centers directly:

  • Hayden Library Copy Center: 480/965-7496
  • Noble Library Copy Center: 480/965-7497

 

Adding money to your Sun Card/Library Copy Card

Library Copy Cards can be purchased and money can be added to a Library Copy Card or Sun Card using the Cash to Card machine in the Hayden and Noble libraries. The Cash to Card machine accepts bills up to $20.00. It does not give change.

 

To purchase a card or add money to a card, follow the instructions on the machine display. Ask at the Reference or Circulation Desks if you need assistance.

 

Money can also be added to your Sun Card over the web.
