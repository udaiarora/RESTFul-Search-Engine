NewTowN's mission is to promote community stability and viability and to enhance the quality of life for Tempe residents through comprehensive, community-based development. Have you always wanted to own a home, but thought you couldn't?
Let NewTowN help you prepare a home buyer's action plan.

NewTowN can help first-time home buyers or existing home owners:

  • Assess their buying power
  • Obtain a credit report
  • Understand credit reports
  • Develop an action plan for "cleaning up" credit and maintaining a clean credit report
  • Negotiate with lenders to obtain the lowest interest rate available
  • Understanding refinancing option
  • Create a budget

 

The Home Buyer's Assistance Site
 A Smart Place to Start

The Home Buyer's Assistance site is an innovative, consumer-friendly way to learn about purchasing a home or refinancing an existing home mortgage. It is brought to you by Cendant Mortgage and is "powered" by Fannie Mae, the nation's largest provider of funds to lenders for home financing.

The website offers these features:

  • A clear, concise description of the mortgage process that helps take the mystery out of home buying and refinancing.
  • Easy-to-use calculators that help you figure out what you can afford, the likelihood of being approved for the loan of your choice and your true loan costs.
  • Access to a lender that offers:
    Many loan options to choose from with discounts exclusively for Arizona State University employees;
    Loans approved on-line in minutes;* and
    An approval decision for all the products for which you qualify at no cost to you.*

The Home Buyer's Assistance site gives you all the information, tools and access required to be a savvy mortgage shopper. Your path to home ownership and the tools to help you select a loan that's right for you could be just a "click" away.

*On-line approval of a loan application will be subject to your submitting an application to the lender; verifying your income, employment, assets and certain other information; and providing the lender with an acceptable property.

ASU is not affiliated with the lender that is linked to the Home Buyer's Assistance site. Cendant Mortgage is the only entity providing mortgage financing through this website. ASU is not acting as a lender or mortgage broker, nor operating in any other capacity in connection with the mortgage financing.
