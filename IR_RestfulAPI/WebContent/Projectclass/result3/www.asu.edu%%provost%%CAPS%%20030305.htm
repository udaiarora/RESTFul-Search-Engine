Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

March 5, 2003

Members Present: Patricia Feldman, Carol Behl, Sue Hunter, Stefan Myhajlenko, Barbara Trapido-Lurie, Victoria Trotta

1. Minutes from 02-05-03 were previously approved (via e-mail) and posted to the Web.

2. OLD BUSINESS

Spring Spotlight Series - Thanks to Barbara for drafting the flyer for April 28th
Spotlight Series presentation starring Vice Provost Gail Hackett. Patty Feldman will 
send the draft to Dr. Hackett for her review. Once edited and approved, Carol will 
distribute it to all APs via the electronic mailing list. The email announcement will also 
solicit questions for Dr Hackett and ask that those questions be transmitted to either 
Carol or Sue. If no questions from the floor are forthcoming, CAPS may compile 
some questions in advance of the presentation.  

 3. NEW BUSINESS

The Academic Assembly Committee on Committees is developing their slate of nominations for the Spring election. CAPS needs nominations for the two alternate positions. CAPS members who have APs to nominate should get those names to Patricia Feldman right away.

Due to the Spotlight Series, the regularly scheduled April CAPS meeting is cancelled. CAPS will communicate electronically if necessary.

There was some general discussion as to whether other issues should be addressed by CAPS at this time. 

 Respectfully submitted, Victoria K. Trotta (Secretary)

Minutes were approved 3/07/2003
