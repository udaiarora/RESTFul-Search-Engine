ACD 601–07: Unemployment Insurance

Purpose

To provide unemployment compensation for terminated faculty and academic professionals

Source

Arizona Revised Statutes §§ 23-601 to -799

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

Faculty and academic professionals who terminate employment with the university may be eligible for unemployment insurance benefits.

Faculty members and academic professionals who become unemployed and believe they are entitled to unemployment insurance should file a claim at the nearest Unemployment Office.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Faculty member
or
academic professional
  1. Assemble the following documentation/information (which may be required by the Unemployment Office):

    1. a Social Security card

    2. the name under which the employer does business

      and

    3. the employer’s business address.

  1. Take the documentation/information to the nearest Unemployment Office.

  2. File a claim.
