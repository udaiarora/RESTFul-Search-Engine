What is sexual assault?
    Sexual assault is knowingly engaging in sexual intercourse or oral sexual contact with any person without consent of that person. Sexual assault is a felony. (A.R.S 13-1406)
What is acquaintance rape?
    Acquaintance rape occurs when someone you know compels you to have sexual intercourse against your will. Acquaintance rape can occur if you are passed out, too drunk to refuse, too frightened to argue, or when you do not give consent.

    Acquaintance rape is sexual assault. It is unlawful conduct. Types of sexual assault include forced penetration of genital or anal openings with an object. Sexual abuse is sexual contact with any person without consent of that person.

    National surveys report that:

    • one in four college women has experienced rape or attempted rape.
    • nine in ten acquaintance rapes are not reported.
    • one in twelve college men admits to acts that meet legal definitions of rape. Yet, few of these men identify themselves as rapists.
