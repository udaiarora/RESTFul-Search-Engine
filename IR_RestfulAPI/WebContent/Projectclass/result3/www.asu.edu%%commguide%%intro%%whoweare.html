Who we are

“This is an exciting time to be at Arizona State University. We are doing something here that no other university is doing. We are growing to accommodate the demand for the services we offer, while enhancing academic quality and addressing the needs of the greater community at the same time. ASU is on a path to greatness.”

-ASU President Michael M. Crow

ASU is leading the transformation of American higher education toward a new model for the 21st Century. ASU is available to all who seek higher education; we are not exclusive to only the brightest and wealthiest. ASU researchers pursue scholarly interests that also consider the public good. ASU does not just engage in community service, but rather takes on major responsibility for the vitality of its community and the surrounding region.

ASU is accomplishing these lofty sounding goals by approaching its mission and future under the guiding philosophy of eight design imperatives and four definitive statements. These twelve essential attributes direct everything we do and initiate.
