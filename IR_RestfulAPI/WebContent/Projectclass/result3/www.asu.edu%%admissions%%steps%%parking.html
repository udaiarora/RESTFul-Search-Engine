Arizona State University provides free Valley Metro bus passes to all students. Pick up your free Valley Metro bus pass after June 15.

Visit the Web site of Parking and Transit Services for information on public transit, inter-campus shuttle services and free shuttle service (FLASH) available from outlying parking areas. ASU is committed to improving our air quality and encourages alternate modes of transportation.
