Indexes to Libraries Worldwide



OCLC WorldCat

    OCLC's (Online Computer Library Center)WorldCat is a Union Catalog or database that contains around forty+ million records in over 400 languages of items held by libraries around the world. These items include a broad range of materials including books, journals, musical scores, computer data files, magazines, newspapewrs, computer programs, manuscripts, sound recordings, films, slides, maps, videotapes and other forms of information. Items recorded in this database date from around 1000 A.D to the present. New records are added daily.

    Online Access

RLG Union Catalog

    RLG's (Research Library Group) Union Catalog contains around one hundred million items from one hundred plus member libraries around the world. These items include various formats including books, serials, archives, ,manuscripts, maps, musical scores, sound recordings, films, photographs, posters, computer files and more.

    Online Access

IFLA - International Federation of Library Associations/International Directory of Art Libraries

    IFLA has a database of about "...3000 libraries and library departments with specialized holdings in art, architecture, and archaeology throughout the world." These resources are searchable by the name of the library, librarian, city and nation.

Libweb

    Index to libraries worldwide maintained by Tom Dowling at the University of California at Berkeley.
     

Web CATS

    Web CATS is an index to libraries' web based online public access catalogs (OPACs). It is maintained by the compiler, Peter Scott, and the programmer, Doug Macdonald, at the University of Saskatchewan Libraries.
