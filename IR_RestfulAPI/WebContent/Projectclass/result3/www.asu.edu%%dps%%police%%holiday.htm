ASU Police Holiday Community Service Program

In 1998, members of the ASU Police contributed nonperishable food items for an "in-house" food drive for the Holiday season. Hams and other perishable items were purchased with donated funds to assist in this effort. This same effort was undertaken in 1999 and 2000, but included a partnership with the community. The unique thing about our Holiday Community Service Program is that all food and gifts are given to members of the ASU community. Families in need of assistance are identified through ASU's Employee Assistance office.

Additionally, ASU Police participated in the W. Steven Martin Police Toy Drive. Mr. Martin is a local radio celebrity who receives toys through corporate sponsors and other donations. All collected items are taken by police officers to the families in need of assistance.

For more information about the ASU Police Holiday Community Service program, contact Sgt. Allen Clark at (480) 965-6328.
