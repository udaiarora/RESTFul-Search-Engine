The department desiring property:

  1. acquires the hold tag from the warehouse staff

    Note: An ASU photo ID is required for hold tags.

  2. tags property for distribution back to campus

  3. completes the required information on the hold tag

    and

  4. completes a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form, obtains the immediate supervisor’s signature, and sends the PRA to Surplus Property and Recycling within 15 calendar days of applying the holding tag.

    Note: If the PRA form is not received within 15 calendar days, property will be returned to the warehouse floor for public sale.

If the Department of Facilities Management movers are not needed, the department desiring property forwards the completed original to Surplus Property and Recycling. Surplus Property and Recycling permits the department employee to remove equipment from Surplus Property and Recycling and updates the property control records to indicate the new custodial unit. Property must be transported in a state vehicle.

Property Control randomly verifies that equipment was delivered to campus.

If the Department of Facilities Management movers are needed, Surplus Property and Recycling:

  1. moves the property listed on the form to the holding area upon receiving the completed PRA form

    and

  2. forwards the original PRA form to the Department of Facilities Management.

The Department of Facilities Management:

  1. picks up the equipment from Surplus Property and Recycling

  2. verifies that the ASU property control numbers are on the PRA or writes them on the original

    Note: Obtain the Surplus Property and Recycling signature before moving the equipment.

    and

  3. coordinates movement of the equipment with the department acquiring the equipment.

Surplus Property and Recycling updates its records to reflect the new custodial unit, location, and other pertinent data.
