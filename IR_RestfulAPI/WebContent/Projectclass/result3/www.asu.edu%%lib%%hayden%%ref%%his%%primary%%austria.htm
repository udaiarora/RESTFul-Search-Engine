Austria

20. ALLIED COMMISSION FOR AUSTRIA. MINUTES OF THE COUNCIL, 1945-1955. CALL NUMBER: Microfilm DB 99.1 A325x. NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 23 R INDEX OR GUIDE: DB 99.1 A32 1960 Micro Ref.  Index to the Microfilmed Minutes.  Table of contents; reel/subject index (subject index is in two parts: 1945-1953 and 1953-1955).  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Includes agendas, unofficial US minutes of meetings of the four Commanders in Chief of the Allied Forces (Soviet Union, United Kingdom, France and the U. S.).  Problems considered: food supply, establishment of the Commission, provisional government, compilation of lists of Nazis, reconstruction, the monetary system, problems caused by activities of the Socialist Party, the People's Party and the Communist Party. SUBJECTS: Austria; Europe; 20c; 1940s; 1950s.

21. ALLIED COMMISSION FOR AUSTRIA. MINUTES OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE, 1945-1955.   CALL NUMBER: Microfilm DB 99.1 .A33x  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 15 R      INDEX OR GUIDE: DB 99.1 A32 1960 Micro Ref.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Chronicles the meetings of the deputies of the Commanders in Chief of the forces of occupation (Soviet Union, US, United Kingdom and France).  Discussed administrative and economic problems, including food supply, political activities, freedom of press, unions, confiscation of Nazi property, extradition of war criminals. SUBJECTS: Austria - History; Europe; 20c; 1940s; 1950s

22. AUSTRIA. REPUBLIK. STATISTISCHES HANDBUCH, 1920-1938; 1950-1965. CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 S320    NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 34 VE  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Particularly strong in economic, commercial and industrial statistics.  Includes an international section.     SUBJECTS: Austria - Statistics; 20c

23. AUSTRIAN NATIONAL LIBRARY CARD CATALOG, 1501-1929.  CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 3802  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 808 F  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Austrian National Library originated as the Hapsburg Court Library, and has been supplemented with many major acquisitions.  This fiche edition reproduces over one million typeset catalog cards, based on the "Old Catalog."      SUBJECTS: Austria; Europe

25. STATISTISCHES JAHRBUCH (HANDBUCH) FUR OSTERREICH, 1938. CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 S321      SCOPE AND CONTENTS:  SUBJECTS: Austria - Statistics; 1930s; 20c.

26. U. S. ALLIED COMMISSION FOR AUSTRIA. REPORTS OF THE U. S. MILITARY GOVERNMENT OF AUSTRIA, 1945-1950.  CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm DB 99.1 .A9x  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 7 R    INDEX OR GUIDE: Most of the reports on the film have their own index and contents tables.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Includes the Military Government Handbook containing the basic policies governing the administration of Austria.  Followed by periodical reports of the U. S. Commissioner, which cover developments in all areas of Austrian society: politics, education, displaced persons, economy, transportation, etc.  Includes many statistical tables and graphs.  Also includes the report: Rehabilitation of Austria, 1945-1947 and the Final Report of the U. S. High Commissioner. SUBJECTS: Austria - History; Economic Conditions; United States - Foreign Relations; 1940s
