AIMS Sample Practice Questions

The AIMS Practice Questions are a product of the Arizona Board of Regents for Arizona State University and its Office of Youth Preparation. These practice questions are provided to your school district compliments of the Office of Youth Preparation/Project PRIME (OYP).

 

OYP created a bank of language arts and mathematics questions in ten-question drills addressing each of the standards assessed in the Arizona’s Instrument to Measure Standards for grades 3, 5, and 8. The content of the practice questions is based on a comprehensive coverage of the standards at the performance objective level.

 

Below are links to sample practice questions for each grade level for Language Arts and Mathematics. The full set of practice questions and accompanying Teachers Manual are available upon request for Arizona schools and school districts. There will be a fee to schools and districts outside of Arizona
