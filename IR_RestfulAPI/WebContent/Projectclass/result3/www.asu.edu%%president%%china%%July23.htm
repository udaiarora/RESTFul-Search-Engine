July 23rd, 2006

11:38am


It's my last night here in China. I had a great day, but as I pack right now, the mood is slightly somber. On one hand, I feel so happy to be making progress to come home, but on the other, I love this place. I feel so comfortable here and I know I will miss it here very much. This has been one of, if not the greatest experience of my life. I really can't even describe everything I have done and experienced and realized and thought and learned, but I'm so glad this all happened and has all worked out. I'm so thankful for all of this, and for everyone that has helped me with it.


I leave for Hong Kong tomorrow, then for Thailand, then Taiwan, then home.


I'm resizing all my pictures and stuff and will try to have them posted soon. There are a lot since its over this whole travel portion of my trip.
