Location

We are located at the Palm Walk footbridge and University Drive on the southwest corner.
Show Map



Hours

  
Monday - Wednesday and Friday 9:00AM to 5:30PM
Thursday 10:00AM to 5:30 PM
Summer Hours 8:00AM to 4:30PM

Parking

The Health Service Building parking lot is reserved for sick or injured patients. Since parking is limited, please plan ahead to park in your regular decal parking area as a consideration to those who are ill. Your respect for the needs of others will be appreciated. Please remember to sign-in at the West entrance.

Visitor parking is also available behind the ASU Foundation building at the northeast corner of College and University Drive. Parking costs are $2.00 per hour in this garage.

Parking Update:
With construction going on all around the Campus area, parking has become hard to find. Here are a few tips for finding parking for your appointment.
  • Students can park at the Foundation Building and pay the fees.
  • Make sure you give your self enough time to get by all the construction and get here on time for your appointment
