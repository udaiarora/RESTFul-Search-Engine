REMINDERS
A few still available: Tuition waivers for Spring 2004—please turn in any last requests as quickly as possible to Catherine Lebihan at clebihan@asu.edu.

Please ensure that registration overload petitions include both the specific total number of credit hours the student would like to register for, as well as confirmation about the percentage of any RA or TA appointments which the student has received (or, when this is not applicable, please note that the student is not an RA or TA).

Graduate Admissions does not accept unofficial copies of transcripts to process applications. Without the official transcript, student files will not be released to the department for a recommendation. The application fee for new degree applications is $50.00 and $25.00 for re-admission and nondegree applications.

The August commencement ceremony has been cancelled. Students who graduate in August are invited to walk in the fall commencement ceremony.

For descriptions of Graduate Professional Development Initiatives, a comprehensive list of programs, and registration details, visit www.asu.edu/graduate/professionaldevelopment.
