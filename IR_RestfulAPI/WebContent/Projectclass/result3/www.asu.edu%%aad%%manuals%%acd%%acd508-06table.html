ACD 508–06 Table

The resigning faculty member or academic professional writes a letter of resignation to the executive vice president and provost of the university.

The executive vice president and provost of the university sends a letter of acknowledgment to the individual and a copy of both letters to the individual’s dean and department chair, and to the vice provost for academic personnel.

The resigning faculty member or academic professional:

  1. returns university property including keys and equipment, whether purchased with state or grant funds

  2. pays delinquent obligations such as parking and library fines

  3. returns photo ID card to Human Resources (HR)

  4. discusses insurance conversion rights and retirement options in HR

  5. returns parking decal to Parking and Transit Services

    and

  6. arranges to have user ID deleted from all ASU computer systems.
