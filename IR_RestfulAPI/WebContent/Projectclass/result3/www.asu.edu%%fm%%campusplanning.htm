University Real Estate Development Office

The University Real Estate Development Office provides physical planning and feasibility studies for the University including, ASU at the Tempe campus, Polytechnic, ASU at the West campus and Downtown Phoenix campuses.

  • Strategic direction for University Physical Planning
  • Conceptual and site feasibility studies
  • Internal and external planning studies including:
    • Program and space needs analysis
    • Environmental impact and sustainability analysis
    • Transportation analysis
    • Parking needs analysis
    • Infrastructure analysis
    • Market studies
    • Landscape concept development

  • Historic preservation
  • Neighborhood services

Comprehensive Development Plan

The Phoenix metropolitan area is experiencing phenomenal growth, which is expected to continue for several decades. As the valley grows, the need to meet the educational and cultural needs of its citizens grows as well.

To plan for this demand, ASU is developing a comprehensive development plan involving a detailed analysis of all four campuses: ASU at the West campus, ASU at the East campus, Downtown (Phoenix), and ASU at the Tempe campus.

This plan will focus on issues related to transportation, university access, blending university learning facilities with non-university facilities, and the satisfactory integration of the university into its local surroundings.
