Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

June 28, 1999

Present: Tom Keating, Molly McCartney, Maggie Mangini, Helen Seaton, Linda Shackle, Santos Vega

CAPS met at 11:00 AM in Social Sciences 410 to elect officers, determine a meeting schedule for the year, and to determine the issues we will address.

Chair: Tom Keating
Secretary: Helen Seaton
Web Master: Linda Shackle

The location of the committee's archives is unknown. Tom will check with Karl Weiss and Cecelia in the Provost's Office. Members continuing from last year were asked to check their files to see if they have anything for the archives.

Status of issues from the past year:

The Post-Continuing Review document went to the Academic Senate's Council of Deans. It is currently "On Hold" and no effort will be made to subject any AP to the process at this time.

Looked at individual college bylaws and constitutions to see if any indication that APs are treated differently or if they are in conflict with the Academic Senate Handbook. Tom will write up a report and send to the Provost.

Discussed how review committees are formed in the different colleges - elected or appointed.

New issues for this year:

Santos suggested we develop some kind of special recognition for APs similar to the Regents Professor.

Issue of uneven treatment of APs in science getting funding/grants but no time to develop the project or do research while carrying on ones regular job; no recognition - multiple bosses.

Maggie suggested that the committee devise a demographic databank on APs - one that could be queried. What information should be included and for what purpose?

Workshop for APs on how to put together a promotion packet.

Request money for a budget.

Meeting schedule: Start off with two meetings a month and then cancel if not needed. Decided to meet the first and third Wednesdays of the month from 11:30 to 1:00. Begin on July 21 with a lunch meeting at the University Club which will be arranged by Maggie. Next meeting on Sept. 15 at the University Club. Starting in October, we will meet on the first and third Wednesdays.

Helen Seaton, Secretary
