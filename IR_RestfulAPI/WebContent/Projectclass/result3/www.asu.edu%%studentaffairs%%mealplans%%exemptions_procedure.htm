Procedure for Exemptions

Review of Exemption Process

Step 1
Discuss the options that are available to you with Campus Dining by calling 480-727-DINE (480-727-3463).
Step 2
Obtain exemption application from Campus Dining staff via email.
  • The application will be in a PDF format. You can type directly into the various fields.
  • In order to view the form, Adobe Reader® version 5.0 or above must be used. Currently version 7.0 is available. If you do not have a version, a link will be provided in the email that will direct you to the download page. The program is free.
Step 3
Complete application and return (via fax, mail or drop it off in-person) to Campus Dining with appropriate documentation.
  • Please make sure you retain one copy for personal records.
  • Be sure to provide an explanation and justification of your need for an exemption.
  • Please include any documentation that supports your exemption request.
  • Please note that the exemption process will only proceed upon receipt of your exemption application.
Step 4
Campus Dining will review your exemption and pass along your exemption application to the Student Exemption Committee for further review if needed.
Step 5
If Campus Dining approves your exemption request, no further steps are necessary and Campus Dining will work out any details with you.

Student Exemption Committee

Step 1
If Campus Dining feels that further review is needed, they will pass along your exemption application to the Student Exemption Committee.
Step 2
The Student Exemption Committee meets every two weeks and reviews each case individually. They will approve, decline, request additional information, or request an in-person meeting with you based on your exemption application in order to make an informed decision.
Step 3
If the Student Exemption Committee declines your request for exemption, you will have until June 15th to choose a Meal Plan; otherwise you will be automatically enrolled in Plan D.
