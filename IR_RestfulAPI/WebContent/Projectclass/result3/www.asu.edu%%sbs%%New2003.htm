IRS Form 1098-T Tuition Statement
New for Calendar Year 2004

Beginning with calendar year 2003, the IRS Form 1098-T that Arizona State University is filing with the Internal Revenue Service includes the amount billed for qualified tuition and related expenses and the total of scholarships and grants administered and processed by ASU. Beginning with calendar year 2004, IRS Form 1098-T includes adjustments to tuition billed for a prior year and adjustments to scholarships or grants for a prior year.  See Explanation of Your IRS Form 1098-T Tuition Statement for a detailed explanation of the information being reported. See Form 1098-T Supplemental Information for a description of the supplemental information being provided on the ASU Interactive web site to assist you.
