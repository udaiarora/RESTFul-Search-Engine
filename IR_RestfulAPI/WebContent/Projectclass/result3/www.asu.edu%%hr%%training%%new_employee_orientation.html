New Employee Orientation

HR New Employee Orientation is offered to new benefits-eligible employees (50% time or more) of the ASU at the Tempe Campus, at the Polytechnic Campus, at the Downtown Phoenix Campus and at the West Campus. It introduces employees to University priorities and facilitates their involvement in the ASU community by acquainting them with the resources and services they need.

Sign on for ASU Services: New employees may submit payroll papers,complete I-9 verification, obtain a temporary Sun Card (Employee ID),obtain temporary parking, and self-subscribe for computer access.

Public Service Orientation: New Employees receive information about the policies and procedures that govern our employment at ASU. Public Service Orientation is mandated by the state of Arizona for all state employees.

ASU Overview: New employees experience ASU through a multi-media virtual tour of who and what we are, and how we accomplish our mission. In addition, new employees will hear about exciting programs, perks and resources they can take advantage of from day one to enhance their employment.

Benefits Overview: New employees will learn about medical, dental and retirement options open to them. Further information will be given on optional benefits such as short-term disability, supplemental life insurance, and various savings programs. During the session, enrollment forms will be filled out to select beneficiaries for retirement and life insurance plans, but decisions on specific health care and optional benefits can be made up to 30 days after the orientation session.

Please sign up for Orientation by calling (480) 965-4751

Orientation for new benefits-eligible employees will be held in the University Services Building, room 1302 on Mondays (except holidays) from 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, with a lunch break around noon. 

We look forward to welcoming all new faculty and staff!
