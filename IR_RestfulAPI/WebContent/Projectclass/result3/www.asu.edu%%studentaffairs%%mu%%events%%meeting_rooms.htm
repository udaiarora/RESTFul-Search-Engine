Meeting Rooms

The majority of the meeting rooms in the Memorial Union utilize "standard set-up" unless other arrangements have been made with an Event and Meeting Services staff member.

Please note that we do adhere to fire code regulations; therefore, requests for special set-up or for additional seating may not be approved. The following outlines maximum seating for standard set-ups. Select a room to see details.
