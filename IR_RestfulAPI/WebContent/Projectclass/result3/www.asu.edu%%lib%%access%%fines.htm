Paying Library Fines

Payment options

You have three options for paying your fines -- online with a Visa or MasterCard; in person at the Hayden Library billing office with cash, check, or credit card; or by mail with check or money order.

 

Online

Use ASU Interactive's secure server to pay your fines online with Visa or MasterCard.

  • Go to http://www.asu.edu/interactive.
  • Log in with your ASURITE ID and password
  • Scroll down to the "Financial Services" section and choose the link that says "Pay Your ASU Charges"
  • Choose the link that says "Student Accounts Receivable"
  • Click on the "Make a payment' link, and follow the instructions

When you pay online there may be delay of around three days for the library to see your account has been paid. If you are experiencing difficulties after you pay online, call the billing office at 965-3106.

 

Notes:

  • Community card holders are not able to pay online. Please come in to Hayden Library to pay your fines.
  • New fines take about three days to appear on the Accounts Receivable system. These new fines will not be able to be paid through ASU Interactive until they do appear in that system. To pay a fine before they appear on this system, please come into Hayden Library

 

In Person

Library Billing Office, Hayden Library
You can pay by cash, check, money order or credit card at the library's billing office. They are located on the lower concourse of Hayden Library, next to the Circulation desk. Their hours are:

 

 Monday - Tuesday 7am - 3:30 pm
  Wednesday - Friday 7am - 4 pm
  Saturday 9 am - 5 pm
  Sunday Closed

Circulation Desk, Hayden Library
You may pay fines at the Circulation desk after the billing office has closed, but you will only be able to pay by cash, check or money order.

If paying with cash, you will need the exact amount as the staff is not able to make change. Copy Service, located in the lower level of Hayden Library, is available to assist in making change.

When you pay in person you will be given an account "override" slip and you can resume checking out and renewing materials in person. Unfortunately you will not be able to renew online or in person for about ten days to two weeks. If you continue to experience difficulties after this time, please contact the billing office at 965-3106.

By Mail

Make check or money order payable to "Arizona State University " and mail to:

If you have received a billing statement, use the envelope provided.

Refunds

Any books that are paid for (either directly or through Financial Aid disbursement) and returned to the library within a year of payment are eligible for partial refund. After a year, the replacement payments are used by Collection Development to either replace the original item or purchase a different title. Refunds will not be issued beyond one year after payment. However, the Libraries will accept the books as donations to the collection.

For questions about billing or to appeal a charge, contact the billing office at 965-3106.

 

Any questions?

Please call the circulation desk at 480/965-3605 or 480/965-2600, or billing at 480/965-3106 for assistance with this process.
