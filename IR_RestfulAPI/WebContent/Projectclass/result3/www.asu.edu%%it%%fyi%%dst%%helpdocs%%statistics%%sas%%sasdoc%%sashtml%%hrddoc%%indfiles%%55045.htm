SAS Guide to Report Writing: Examples

Professional programmers at SAS Institute have packed this book with practical examples of report-writing programs that you can adapt and apply to your own work environment. This guide features report-writing procedures in base SAS software, base statistics procedures, the DATA step, and the COMPUTAB procedure from SAS/ETS software. Designed for intermediate to experienced SAS software users, this guide shows how to prepare reports for those who learn best by example.

232 pages.
