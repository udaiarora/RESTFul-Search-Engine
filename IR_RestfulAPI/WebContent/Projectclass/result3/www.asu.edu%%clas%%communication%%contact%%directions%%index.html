Parking

There is meter parking on the street. You can also park in the Tempe Center parking located on University and Mill (one block west of Myrtle) for $1.00 an hour. Stop at the kiosk to prepay for your time and place your ticket on your dash. Parking Structure 3 located on Myrtle in front of Stauffer Hall is for individuals with a permit. Do not park in this building or you will be ticketed. Please park in the street utlizing the meters, if availalbe, or park in the Tempe Center.

Commonly Requested Driving Directions

From Sky Harbor Airport

All major airlines fly into Sky Harbor International Airport in Phoenix,
just a 15-minute drive by car from campus.

  1. Take Sky Harbor Blvd. east to Loop 202.
  2. Take Loop 202 east to Scottsdale/Rural Road (Exit 7).
  3. Turn right onto Rural Road. Drive south to University.
  4. Turn right (west) onto University.
  5. Turn left onto Myrtle Avenue.
  6. There is meter parking on the street.

[TOP]

From Tucson / Southern Arizona

  1. Take Highway I-10 west to Highway US 60 east.
  2. Take Highway US 60 east to the Rural Road exit.
  3. Turn left (north) onto Rural Road to University.
  4. Turn left onto University.
  5. Turn left onto Myrtle Avenue.
  6. There is meter parking on the street.
