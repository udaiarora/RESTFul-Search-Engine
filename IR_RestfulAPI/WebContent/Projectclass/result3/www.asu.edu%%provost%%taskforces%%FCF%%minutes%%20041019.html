October 19, 2004
12:00 - 1:30 ADM B286

(Approved: November 9, 2004)

Present: Andrew Bremner, Martha Cocchiarella, Elaine Dahghanpisheh, Jeanne Dugan (Chair), Saundra Ealy, Jacques Giard, Nancy Gutierrez (liaison form the University Provost), Christine Helfers, Matt Isom, Beatrice Kastenbaum, Mirna Lattouf (academic Senate Rep.), Maria Mitkova, Michael Pearson

Not Present: Tom Schildgen, Donald Beggs, Jean Brown, Karen Geiger, Andrew Kirby, Robert Taylor, James Wenhart

Plan of Action

Update on Membership/welcome to new members

Michael Pearson is representative from the Polytechnic Academic Senate.

Maria Mitkova and Jacques Giard are newly appointed members.

Andrew Kirby will be West campus Academic Senate representative.

 

Minutes

There was not a quorum today, so the minutes from 9/24/04 could not be voted.

Minutes will be placed on the University web after approval.

For future meetings, the minutes will be sent with the agenda before the meeting. If a member will not be able to attend the meeting, changes can be sent before the meeting.

Vote on minutes from 9/24/04 will occur at the next meeting.

Minutes from each meeting will be sent with the agenda.

By-laws

We have 17 voting members, 13 is a quorum. No quorum was present today.

Two changes of bylaws were discussed (see attached for full statement of bylaws):

Under Voting
Change: Definition of quorum from 3/4 of all committee members to 2/3rds of all committee members and change 2/3rds majority vote needed on actions suggested to the Provost to 3/4 majority vote for actions suggested to the Provost.

Change: A simple majority vote of members present is required to elect the committee chair or approve the minutes.

The liaison from the Provost's office is seeking to complete representation to the committee.

The changes were moved and seconded. The vote will occur at the next meeting.

Old Business
(Gutierrez)
Update on Rolling Contracts

The latest draft was discussed. The committee made several suggestions that would clarify the policy; these will be incorporated into the final draft.

The draft will be discussed by the Deans.

Committee members will receive the final draft by email before meeting to discuss it.

Update on Sick Leave questions

State policy indicates that sick leave hours may not be donated to sick colleagues.

The way to help a colleague continues to be working with the chair to cover the colleague's need for release from responsibilities.

 

New Business
Academic Assembly Membership

Barbara Kerr will be coming to the November meeting to discuss a recommendation to the Academic Assembly focusing on contract faculty membership across campuses.

 

Agenda Items for this year

Beginning discussion suggested the following topics:

  • Professional development opportunities (leaves, grants, etc)
  • Issues surrounding one year contracts
  • Issues of concern to instructors
  • Salaries-where do we go from here?
  • Retirement requirements
