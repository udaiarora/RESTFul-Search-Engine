What's New

New Saturday Hours at Computing Commons & Coor Computing Sites

Beginning Saturday, June 3, 2006, Saturday hours at the Coor Hall computing site will be extended from 7 am to 10 pm. Saturday hours at the computing site in Computing Commons will be 12 pm to 5 pm. See the full Computing Sites schedule.

Classroom Training, Equipment Available for Summer Session

Information Technology Classroom Support encourages instructors to make plans early to receive training on the use of multimedia in classrooms and to reserve equipment, such as video projectors, laptops and DVD/VCR players for academic use on campus for the upcoming Summer Semester. To schedule training or to make a reservation for equipment, call (480) 965-3342 or send an email to classroom.support@asu.edu.

CPS Demonstrations

Are your students getting the message?
Do they understand what you just taught?
Are they showing up for class?
Are they listening past the first 15 minutes of lecture?
How do you know?

Let Tempe Campus Support help you monitor and adjust with eInstruction?s Classroom Performance System. The TCS Staff is ready to introduce you to new teaching tools and technologies to help you engage students during class, measure their understanding before they leave the room, re-teach where needed, administer quizzes, take attendance, and keep them from falling asleep. Introductory demonstrations, training, and start-up kits for teachers who adopt the system are FREE. Demonstrations can be scheduled anywhere. Contact Seth Levine in TCS at 5-0812 or Seth.Levine@asu.edu for more information.
