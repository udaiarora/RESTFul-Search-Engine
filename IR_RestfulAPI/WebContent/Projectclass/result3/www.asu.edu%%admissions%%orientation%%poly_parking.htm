ASU at the Polytechnic campus: Mesa, AZ

Check-In Information
All Orientation programs at ASU's Polytechnic campus are from 8:00 a.m. to approximately 3:00 p.m. Check-in begins at 8:00 a.m. Optional campus tours will be offered in the afternoon.

Important Notes
All students planning to take the Calculus Placement exam should arrive at 7:15 a.m. with their photo ID to have enough time to be checked in for the exam.

All students admitted to the Barrett Honors College need to attend the 8:30 a.m. Barrett Honors College Welcome.

Students must participate in the full-day on-campus Orientation program to complete the registration process.

Accommodations
For information on hotels close to ASU's Polytechnic campus, contact the Mesa Convention & Visitors Bureau.

800.283.6372
www.mesacvb.com

Parking and Directions
From Sky Harbor Airport: Take Loop 202 East from the airport. Merge onto Loop 101 South, and take the exit for the Superstition Freeway (US 60) heading east toward Mesa. Exit the US 60 at Power Rd. Take Power Rd. south to Williams Field Rd. Turn left onto Williams Field Rd. to enter the campus.

From Tempe: Head east on the Superstition Freeway ( US 60) toward Mesa . Exit the freeway at Power Rd. Take Power Rd. south to Williams Field Rd. Turn left onto Williams Field Rd. to enter the campus.

From Flagstaff: Take Interstate 17 (I-17) south toward Phoenix, Exit I-17 onto Interstate 10 (I-10) heading east. Take I-10 and exit onto the Superstition Freeway (US 60) heading east toward Mesa. Exit the freeway at Power Rd. Take Power Rd. south to Williams Field Rd. Turn left onto Williams Field Rd. to enter the campus.

From Phoenix: Take Interstate 10 (I-10) heading east. Exit onto the Superstition Freeway (US 60) heading east toward Mesa. Exit the freeway at Power Rd. Take Power Rd. south to Williams Field Rd. Turn left onto Williams Field Rd. to enter the campus.
