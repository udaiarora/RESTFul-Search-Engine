Revision Schedule for Catalog Course Information (Paper and Web)

See also “Course-Related Deadlines” for course-related approval deadlines and “May 2006 to May 2007: Important Catalog and Web Course Deadlines” for an annual calendar of GSC, CAPC, and TCCS meeting dates and course-related deadlines.

The rationale for these deadlines: to match other publications (e.g., print catalogs and Schedule of Classes).

Remember: the sooner I see course information, the sooner you see requests reflected in catalog drafts and the better your chances for getting approvals in time.
