Neuroimage Analysis Lab (NAL)


People in the lab:



  • Gene Alexander, Ph.D.
  • Jason Smith
Research focus:

  • NAL develops image-analysis techniques for applications such as characterizations of alterations in brain function and structure in persons affected by Alzheimer's and the ability of treatments to halt progression of this disorder.
