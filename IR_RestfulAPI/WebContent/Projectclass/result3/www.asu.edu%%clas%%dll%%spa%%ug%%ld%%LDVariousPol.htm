Overrides

There will be NO overrides for any Lower Division Spanish classes with the following exception:
  • Those students who have enrolled in Spa 101-202 and who are heritage speakers of Spanish may request an override for SPA 203 and 204. Please contact Dr. Juliet Falce-Robinson (Juliet.Falce-Robinson@asu.edu) for more information.
All other students should continue to monitor class openings through Sundial and ASU Interactive and add the appropriate classes as they become available.

Auditing


If you would like to audit a course, speak with the instructor regarding requirements for receiving a successful audit grade.



Grade Complaints


Grade complaints should be addressed to the Lower or Upper Division Spanish Language Program Coordinator. The request for a grade change must be in writing (a form will be provided).


Incompletes


INCOMPLETE GRADES will not be given without DOCUMENTED proof of a medical, legal or family emergency that prevents a student from completing the course. No incomplete grade will be issued to a student who is failing a course at the time of request.  Students wishing to complete an "I" should NOT register for the course again.  Speak to the instructor or section coordinator to make necessary arrangements. 

Make-Up Exams

Please be aware that no make-up exam opportunities will be provided by the Lower Division Spanish Program.  All exam dates are listed on the course schedule.  Make travel plans accordingly!
