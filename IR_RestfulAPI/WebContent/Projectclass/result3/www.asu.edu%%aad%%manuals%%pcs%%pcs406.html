PCS 406: Transferring Equipment to ASU

Purpose

To establish guidelines for receiving equipment from other institutions

Source

Property Control

Policy

ASU may receive equipment at no cost from other institutions. Costs may occur if the relinquishing university requires compensation for the property.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Department
  1. Forward to Property Control a list of equipment being transferred from another institution. Include appraised or estimated values for each item.
If the equipment does not pertain to a sponsored grant or contract:
  1. Advise the sponsored property coordinator accordingly.
Sponsored property coordinator
  1. Tag the equipment and add it to the property control system.
If the equipment pertains to a sponsored grant or contract:
  1. Record grant or contract number in property control system.
endtable
