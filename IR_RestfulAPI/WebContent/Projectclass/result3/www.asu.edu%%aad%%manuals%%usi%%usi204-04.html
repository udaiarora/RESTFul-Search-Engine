USI 204–04: Application for Graduation for Doctoral Candidates

Purpose

To notify the Graduation Section that a doctoral candidate intends to complete all course work and requirements for graduation at the end of a specific semester

Source

Division of Graduate Studies

Policy

A doctoral candidate must apply for graduation by the deadline dates specified in the ASU Graduate Catalog or pay a late fee.

A doctoral candidate must pay the appropriate fees at the time of application.

Procedure

The student:

  1. pays the graduation fee and obtains a receipt at Cashiering Services

    and

  2. takes the graduation fee receipt to the Graduation Section and provides a definite graduation date.

The Graduation Section:

  1. completes and signs the Graduate Application for Graduation form

  2. verifies that the student is meeting all graduation requirements outlined in the Doctoral Program of Study

    and

  3. files a copy of the application for processing in the semester in which graduation is anticipated.

Cross-Reference

For information about the doctoral program of study, see USI 204–02, “Master’s and Doctoral Programs of Study.”
