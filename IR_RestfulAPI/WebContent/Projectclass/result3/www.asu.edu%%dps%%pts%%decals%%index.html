Decal Sales

Anyone who parks or expects to park a vehicle on the ASU Tempe campus, either regularly or occasionally, must register each vehicle with ASU Parking and Transit Services by purchasing and displaying a valid ASU parking decal while the vehicle is on campus. Only one valid decal may be displayed at a time on a vehicle. Registration must occur before a vehicle is parked in any ASU decal lot.
