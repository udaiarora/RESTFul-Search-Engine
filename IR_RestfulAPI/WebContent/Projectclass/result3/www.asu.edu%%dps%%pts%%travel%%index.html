Carpooling Public Transit

Arizona State University is proud to contribute to the quality of life in our community and is dedicated to doing its part to help clean the air. ASU encourages the faculty, staff and students to use an alternate mode of transportation as often as possible. We can all make a difference in improving our air quality. Please do your part by participating when you can.

The Commuter Options program (formerly Travel Reduction Office) is located in room 103 of the Towers building, 525 S. Forest Avenue, at the corner of Fifth Street and Forest Avenue in Tempe. Business hours are from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday-Friday. For information or comments, please call (480) 965-1072 or e-mail TRPDEPT@asu.edu.

How easy is it?

Changing a habit is hard to do, but it does not take a life-altering commitment to make a difference. You can make a difference in our air quality if you use an alternate mode just once a week. Change your commute for a little stress relief! Studies show that commuting is one of the most stressful things we do in a day. So, try an alternative! Carpool, bus, bike, or walk, and see what a difference it makes in your life.
