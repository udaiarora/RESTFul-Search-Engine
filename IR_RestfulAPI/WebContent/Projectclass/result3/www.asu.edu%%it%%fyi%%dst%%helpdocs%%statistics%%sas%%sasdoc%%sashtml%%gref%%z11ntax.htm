The GREMOVE Procedure

Procedure Syntax

Requirements: The BY and ID statements are required.
PROC GREMOVE <DATA=input-map-data-set>
<OUT=output-map-data-set>;
BY <DESCENDING>variable-l
<...<DESCENDING>variable-n>
<NOTSORTED>;
ID variable(s);
