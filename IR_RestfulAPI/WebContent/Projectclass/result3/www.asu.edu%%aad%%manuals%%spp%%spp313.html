SPP 313: Human Immunodeficiency Virus Infection

Purpose

To restate Arizona State University’s policies assuring nondiscriminatory services to persons with human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) infection in all university programs and activities

To create an aware, sensitive climate in the campus community that is inclusive of persons with HIV infection

and

To reduce the occurrence of HIV infection through active, effective educational programs

Sources

Public Health Service Centers for Disease Control
Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 5-206
American College Health Association
Campus Health Service

Applicability

All employees

Policy

ASU’s policy for employees is contained in the University Student Initiatives Policies and Procedures Manual—USI 106–02, “Human Immunodeficiency Virus Infection.”
