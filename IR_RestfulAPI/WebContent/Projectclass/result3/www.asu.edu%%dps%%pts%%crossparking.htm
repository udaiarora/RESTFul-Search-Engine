Cross Parking Options at ASU Tempe

Cross parking is a privilege that allows any ASU decal holder to park outside of their assigned lot or structure anytime after 4:00pm.

As the campus continues to change and grow, cross parking has become a challenge in several locations, due to loss of spaces, increased evening activities and classes, and overall parking demand. For several parking areas, this has created issues that are no longer manageable if allowed to continue. Some of the problems include:

  • Decal holders often cannot find a space to park after cross parking hours begin.
  • Resident students impacted by cross parking are not able to access spaces they are assigned and required to park in.
  • Traffic congestions in and around lots and garages at peak times (4pm ? 6pm) create an unsafe environment.

PTS reminds the university community that FLASH Forward services run until 1 a.m., Monday ? Thursday, and until 10 p.m. on Friday, supporting those who attend evening classes/activities or visit the Student Recreation Center or Memorial Union.

Also, due to the PTS partnership with the Safety Escort Service, safe transportation to various campus locations is available free of charge seven days a week from 7:00 p.m. to 3:00 a.m. Call (480) 965-1515 for information on scheduling a ride.

Cross parking is also not permitted at anytime in the following locations:

  • Parking Structure 1(PS1) A PS 1 or PS 1E decal is required at all times Mon. ? Thurs. PS 1 is open to cross parking only on Fridays after 4 p.m. and weekends.
  • Parking Structure 3 (PS3) A PS 3 or PS 3E decal is required at all times Mon. ? Thurs. PS 3 is open to cross parking only on Fridays after 4 p.m. and weekends.
  • Parking Structure 6 (PS 6)
  • ASU Fulton Center Parking Structure
  • Lot 72
  • Resident Lot 50
  • Resident Lot 63
  • Any area reserved for ASU service vehicles
  • Disabled parking spaces are available only to disabled decal holders
