Horizonte’ celebrates 1-year mark on KAET

On Sept. 18, ASU’s KAET-TV/Channel 8 celebrates the one-year anniversary of “Horizonte,” the weekly public affairs program that focuses on Arizona issues through a Hispanic lens. The program launched a year ago with host José Cárdenas, who also serves as chairman of the law firm of Lewis & Roca, LLP.

Greg Giczi, KAET’s general manager, recognized the importance of the Hispanic perspective within Arizona ’s community and wanted a program that presented that perspective on Channel 8. After holding auditions for a host, he says, it was clear Cárdenas was the man for the job.

“José had the best combination of skills needed for the program,” Giczi says. “So much went into the initial launch, from putting together the production team, finding funding, to developing an audience. Primarily due to José, I believe ‘Horizonte’ was launched with instant credibility.”

In “Horizonte’s” first year, Cárdenas has interviewed many Arizona political leaders, including Gov. Janet Napolitano, Congressmen Ed Pastor and Jeff Flake, Phoenix Mayor Phil Gordon and Mesa Mayor Keno Hawker. Other memorable guests include leading journalists such as Ray Suarez from “The NewsHour with Jim Lehrer” and Univision’s Jorge Ramos.

“I think ‘Horizonte’ is important, in part, as a reflection of the growing impact and importance of Arizona’s Hispanic population,” Cárdenas says.

Soto, with KAET, can be reached at (480) 965-0579 or (susan.soto@asu.edu).
