HOUSING ASSISTANCE

(A no-cost service for ASU employees)

ASU Work/Life Balance Programs in partnership with NewTowN Community Development Corporation, a Tempe based private, non-profit organization is available to work with you to make home ownership the possible dream, by providing counseling and assistance to families of modest means in purchasing a home of their own. Spanish speaking counselor is available upon request.

511 W University Dr, #4
Tempe AZ 85281
Tel 480.517.1589, Fax 480.517.1490
www.NewTowNcdc.org

NewTowN's mission is to promote community stability and viability and to enhance the quality of life for Tempe residents through comprehensive community based development. Have you always wanted to own a home, but thought you couldn't? Let NewTowN help you prepare a home buyer's action plan.

For first-time home buyers or existing home owners NewTowN can assist with:

*Assessment of buying power
*Acquiring a credit report*Questions regarding credit reports
*Action plan for "cleaning up" credit and maintaining a clean credit report
*Advocate for you with lenders to get the lowest interest rate available
*Refinancing questions
*Budgeting

Thirty-minute appointments available on campus every first and third Wednesday of the month between 11:00 a.m. and 1:00 p.m. at the University Services Building. Contact Janet Russell at 480.965.9540 to schedule your appointment.

LAWYER REFERRAL SERVICE

 

Lawyer Referral Service is a service of theMaricopa County Bar Association. Legal problems can be puzzling when you don't know where to turn for answers. The Lawyer Referral Service can

* Find the lawyer that best fits your needs.

*Provide you with a 30-minute legal consultation with an attorney for a $35.00 administrative fee. (Fee is non-refundable once the appointment is scheduled.)

*Refer you to a lawyer that has professional malpractice insurance and is in good standing with the State Bar of Arizona and the Maricopa County Bar Association.

*Schedule an appointment with a lawyer located near your work or home.

To get a referral, call 602.257.4434. An interviewer will ask you a few questions and arrange for payment of your $35.00 fee. (Credit cards, money orders or cash) The interviewer will contact the right attorney for you and schedule your 30-minute consultation. The interviewer will then call you back to confirm the time and date of your appointment. Any additional legal services you may require beyond your 30-minute consultation would be determined by the attorney.
