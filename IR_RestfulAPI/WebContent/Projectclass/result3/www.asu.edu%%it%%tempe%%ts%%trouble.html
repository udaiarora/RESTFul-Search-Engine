No dial tone

[ Back To Top ]

  1. Is your phone plugged in? Check to make sure the handset cord is plugged in to both the handset and the phone. Then double-check the line cord to be sure it is plugged in to both the phone and the wall jack.
  2. Was the phone recently unplugged? If so, did it get plugged back in to the correct jack?
  3. Was the phone recently moved to a new location/jack (by someone other than a Telepone Services Technician)? If so, you will need to submit a Telephone Services Request (TSR) to have it programmed properly.
