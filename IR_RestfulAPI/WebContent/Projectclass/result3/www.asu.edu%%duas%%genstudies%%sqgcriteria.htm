III. The Following are not acceptable:

     SQ requirements cannot be met by courses:
  1. Presenting a qualitative survey of a discipline.
  2. Focusing on the impact of science on social, economic, or environmental issues.
  3. Focusing on a specific or limiting but in-depth theme suitable for upper-division majors.
