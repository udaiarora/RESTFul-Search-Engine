ACD 124: University Employee Recognition Program

Purpose

To provide information about the University Employee Recognition Program

Source

University policy

Applicability

Faculty
Academic and service professionals
Administrative and classified staff

Policy

The University Employee Recognition Program recognizes all employees for highly significant contributions, achievements, or service to the university.

Employee contributions are critical to the success of the university and shall be recognized through awards and celebrations. See the Human Resources Employee Resources Web page for details.

Award recipients may be recognized throughout the year and during Employee Recognition Week.
