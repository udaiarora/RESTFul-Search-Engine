SAS/GRAPH Software: Reference, Version 8

This title is a comprehensive reference to all the features and options available with SAS/GRAPH software. In addition to a detailed introduction to SAS/GRAPH, it includes complete information on each SAS/GRAPH statement and procedure. Each statement description includes example programs designed to show you the capabilities of the statement and its options. This title also includes complete information on the DATA step graphics interface and the annotate facility.

1300 pages.
