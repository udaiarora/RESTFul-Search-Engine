Arizona State University has released its crime statistics for the 2005 calendar year in accordance with the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act. The report can be viewed on-line. The crime statistics have been compiled in accordance with definitions taken from the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) Uniform Crime Reporting Handbook as required by Clery Act regulations and modified by the Hate Crime Statistics Act. This report is a valuable information resource that addresses ASU?s security policies and steps that members of the university community can take to improve their personal safety. The report includes contact information for numerous campus and community support organizations. A hard copy of the report is available upon request from the ASU police station at 628 E.
