WHY CHOOSE LAMBDA THETA PHI?
Lambda Theta Phi was born to promote the spirit of brotherhood, to protect the rights of Latino students in his/her quest for an education, to promote the rich Latino culture, and to maximize leadership potentials to provide guidance to our community.

Membership is open to all males, regardless of race, culture or creed. Lambda Theta Phi looks for men of vision, who can assist in our mission to accomplish the goals set forth by our founders.

Lambda Theta Phi, as an organization, is two fold. On one hand, we are a service organization. As such, we perform community service, educational workshops, mentor programs, fund raisers for charities, and much more. Lambda Theta Phi is also a social organization. We hold functions such as talent shows, weekend retreats, parties, and various other activities.

WHAT IS AN INFORMATION SESSION?
Lambda Theta Phi actively recruits new members both informally and formally. These sessions are held throughout the year and provide an opportunity for interested students to meet with brothers and to learn more about the fraternity.

Visit our Informational Session page to get the latest updates on scheduled meetings.

Our fraternity is sensitive to the commitments of university students and is willing to work with students that can not attend or participate in the schedules events.

INTEREST GROUPS OF LAMBDA THETA PHI
Interest Groups of Lambda Theta Phi are organized as part of the preliminary phase to the induction process. They are groups of interested men with three major goals:

  • To bond and learn to work as an organized group
  • To perform community service
  • To sponsor fund raising events

Members of the fraternity's interest groups work closely with Lambda Theta Phi brothers to accomplish these goals.

Recent activities include the following

  • Car Wash (October 15, 2005)
  • Desert Sky Mall Service (October 16, 2005)
  • Lambda Dinner
  • Blue Crab Feast
  • Club ASU

A LITTLE ABOUT OUR INDUCTION PROCESS
The Lambda Theta Phi process is unique, educational and spiritually uplifting.

Lambda Theta Phi determines its need of an induction process on different levels. New members or inductees begin the pledge period according to a defined schedule. When an inductee begins our induction process, it is time spent learning the history of Lambda Theta Phi, devoting time to studies, community service and becoming acquainted with the brotherhood and its traditions. The inductee will finish pledging once it is determined that he has completed the process. After being initiated into the Fraternity, the learning process continues. Each member is expected to attend formal meetings and be active in all fraternity functions. We expect members to exert a positive attitude in all aspects of life as well as a positive image in our communities.

We encourage all males to look into our organization not solely as a future prospect, but as someone who simply wants to get involved, have a stronger voice at their respective school and in their own community. The bottom line is we create leaders. Do you have what it takes?

If you are interested in getting additional information about Lambda Theta Phi, please contact our recruitment officer and take the first steps towards the brotherhood of Lambda Theta Phi, Fraternidad Latina, Inc.
