Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

June 25, 1998

Present: Karl Weiss, Ken Furuta, Molly McCartney, Tom Keating, Annette Voth, Gary Hembree, Helen Seaton

Excused: Margaret Mangini

1. The new meeting times will be the first and third Thursdays of the month at 1.

2. Tom and Molly were nominated as Co-Chairs for next year's Committee. Ken will continue to do the web pages. We will vote on these at the next meeting.

3. Issues for the upcoming year were discussed. They included:

  • Returning a CAPS representative as an ex-officio member of the Senate.
  • Completing the Post Continuing Review document's review by the beginning of the school year. The Committee may convene the Caucus and hold a vote afterwards.
  • When APs retire from the University, they must use their vacation or lose it. We are the only classification with that requirement.

4. The next meeting is July 23rd at 1pm.

5. The New Committee's e-mail addresses are:

  • Ken Furuta - kfuruta@asu.edu
  • Tom Keating - wtk1126@asuvm.inre.asu.edu
  • Karl Weiss - karl.weiss@asu.edu
  • Molly McCartney - molly.mccartney@asu.edu
  • Helen Seaton - helen.seaton@asu.edu
  • Margaret Mangini - maggie.mangini@asu.edu
