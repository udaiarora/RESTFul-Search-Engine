Location, Dates, and Times


The 2006 Summer Program will be offered June 12 through June 30 at Arizona State University’s Tempe Campus.

All classes meet Monday through Friday. The morning session is 8:30 – 11:30am and the afternoon session is 12:30 – 3:30pm.


Tuition and Fees


Application fee: $25, non-refundable.
Tuition: $425 per course. This includes all required books and course supplies.
Additional fees: If applicable, there will be additional fees for lunch and parking. Information will be included in the acceptance packet.
*There will be an additional $15 charge on all returned checks.


Financial Assistance


Programs for Talented Youth does offer a limited number of need-based financial assistance scholarships. For further information, see the Financial Assistance Form.


Lunch Program


Students enrolled in morning and afternoon classes are required to participate in the supervised lunch program. Students may choose to bring lunch or may purchase a meal plan. Additional information will be included in the acceptance packet.


Grade Level Placement


Students are placed in courses that correspond to the grade level completed at the end of the current school year. For example, if in school year 2005-2006 the student is in grade 6, that is the grade in which she/he will be placed during the summer program.


Student and Parent Orientation


Students and parents are required to attend an orientation program. The orientation will address key program policies and will offer opportunities to address questions. It will take place in the Memorial Union on Saturday June 3, 2006 from 9:00am - 11:00am. Further information will be included in the acceptance packet.
