    Remit Payment

    First, explore all your payment options and understand all of ASU's payment option policies. Please be aware of changes to ASU's payment option policies effective November 1, 2005. Visit the Student Business Services Web site for complete information. For more information about registration information, visit ASU's Registrar's Office Web site.

    To pay online (using an approved payment method), access ASU Interactive.

    To pay by mail (using an approved payment method). Please be sure to use the Method of Payment Form and send payment to:

    Important Notices

    Please be aware of changes to ASU's payment option policies effective November 1, 2005. Visit the Student Business Services Web site for complete information.

    Please be aware that ASU does not mail class schedules or tuition billing statements. Beginning spring 2006, you must pay your tuition/fees on time or you will be automatically enrolled in the Tuition Installment Plan. Visit the Student Business Services Web site for complete information.
