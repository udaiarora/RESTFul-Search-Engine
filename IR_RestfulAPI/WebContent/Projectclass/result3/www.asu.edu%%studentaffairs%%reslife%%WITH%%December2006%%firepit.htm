Renewal Period for Fall 07

Hello residents!

The Residential Life Operations staff would like to remind you to please wait until our Renewal Period to submit an application to live with us the next academic year! While we appreciate your diligence, we would like to keep the renewal process as fair as possible to all of our residents.

Our Renewal Period will begin in early Spring, 2007, and dates are to be determined.

We would also like to remind you that any meal plan changes and requests are to be made through the Campus Dining Office only.

Thank you.
