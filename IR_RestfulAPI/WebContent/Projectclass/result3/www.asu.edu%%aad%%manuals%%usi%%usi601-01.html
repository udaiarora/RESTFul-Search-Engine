USI 601–01: Clinical Services

Purpose

To address the clinical services provided by the staff at ASU counseling centers

Source

University policy

Policy

Counseling and Consultation (C&C) at the Tempe campus, the Student Health Service Counseling Center at the West campus, and Student Counseling Services at the Polytechnic campus provide psychological services that include clinical, developmental, preventive, and educational interventions. These services are limited and designed to support students’ academic goals. General health care and counseling services are available to faculty, staff, and students at the ASU Health Center on the Downtown Phoenix campus. See the counseling centers’ Web pages for detailed information on services provided at Counseling and Consultation at the Tempe campus, the Student Health Service Counseling Center at the West campus, and Student Counseling Services at the Polytechnic campus. For information about counseling and other health services at the Downtown Phoenix campus, see the ASU Health Center Web site.
