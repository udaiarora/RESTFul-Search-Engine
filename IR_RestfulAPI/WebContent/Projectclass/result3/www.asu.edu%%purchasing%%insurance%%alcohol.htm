Alcohol Permit Requirements

Requirements for Obtaining an Alcohol Permit

Consumption of alcoholic beverages on University property at special events is prohibited unless a "Permit to Serve Alcoholic Beverages on Campus" has been approved and signed by ASU Department of Public Safety and ASU Insurance Services. The original permit application must be submitted to ASU DPS fourteen (14) calendar days prior to the date of the event.

The steps below are listed to provide assistance to you in acquiring an alcohol permit to serve alcohol on ASU property.

Obtain a "Permit to Serve Alcoholic Beverages on Campus" from ASU Department of Public Safety or at www.asu.edu/dps/forms. Complete and submit the permit to the ASU Department of Public Safety fourteen (14) calendar days prior to the date of the event. The permit will be forwarded to ASU Insurance Services for review and approval. Proof of adequate insurance must be provided with the permit. The permit will only be signed if all insurance requirements are displayed on the certificate of insurance. The permit will be returned to ASU DPS for final approval. Approved applications must be displayed on site during the event.

Insurance requirements include:

Commercial General Liability--Bodily Injury, Death and Property Damage, Combined Single Limit: $1,000,000 per occurrence

Liquor Liability--Combined Single Limit: $1,000,000 per occurrence

Automobile Liability--Bodily Injury, Death and Property Damage, Combined Single Limit: $1,000,000 per occurrence

Worker's Compensation Insurance to cover obligation imposed by Federal and State statutes having jurisdiction of its employees engaged in the performance of the services, and Employer's Liability Insurance with a minimum limit of $1,000,000.

The State of Arizona, Arizona Board of Regents, and Arizona State University shall be named additional insureds for commercial general liability, liquor liability, and automobile liability coverage noted above.

Additional coverage may be necessary for special circumstances or large events.

The certificate holder shall be:

ASU Insurance Services
P.O. Box 875212
Tempe, AZ 85287-5212

For additional information, refer to Purchasing Services Policy and Procedure PURCH 705-04 and ASU Department of Public Safety Policy and Procedure DPS 202-03 at www.asu.edu/aad/manuals/dps/dps202-03.html. These requirements can also be found under Section III, Subsections A and B on the reverse side of the permit application. A sample permit can be reviewed at www.asu.edu/dps/forms/.
