HONORARY DEGREES COMMITTEE

The Honorary Degrees Committee reviews nominations and recommends candidates to the president. Membership includes nine members from the Tempe campus (five selected by the senate and four by the president) and four each from the West and Polytechnic campuses (two selected by the senate and two by the university provost, respectively). The chair of the committee is selected by the president and may come from any campus. All serve two-year terms that may be renewed.
