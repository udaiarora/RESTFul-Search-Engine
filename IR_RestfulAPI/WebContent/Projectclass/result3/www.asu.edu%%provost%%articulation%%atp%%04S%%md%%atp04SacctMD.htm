    ATP NOTES

    1. If ACC111 or ACC211 are taken, the student is likely to exceed 120 hours for graduation.
    2. The ACC230 and ACC240 sequence must be taken at one institution, either MCCCD or ASU.
    3. COM100 will be accepted in lieu of COM230. COM225 will be accepted in lieu of COM259. COM230 & COM259 are preferred. MCCCD's COM 225 and COM 230 also satisfies [L] requirements.
    4. Transferable as lower division credit only. Students must still meet the 51 hour requirement for upper division hours.
    5. If JAS225 has already been taken, the College of Business will accept it in lieu of the QBA221 requirement.
    6. Select [HU] and [L] courses that concurrently fulfill [C] and [H] requirements.
