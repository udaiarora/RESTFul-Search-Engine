BJ - Mechanical Engineering

"There is an emphasis on student success at ASU that cannot be found anywhere else."

BJ is a Mechanical Engineering student in the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering who is not only active in many extracurricular activities, but also has researched the similarities and differences of the manufacturing process in different parts of the world. He has been able to present his findings in both Europe and Asia. "ASU has given me an opportunity I would not have been able to have anywhere else." In March of 2006, BJ attended the Global Tech Leadership Symposium in Singapore and Shanghai with Richard Filley, director of the Global Futures Initiative, to further his knowledge on the subject.

BJ has interned with Boeing Commercial Airplanes in Seattle, Washington, and he is finishing his engineering degree with Boeing's tuition reimbursement scholarship. Upon graduating he will return to Boeing to pursue his professional career.

"With academic programs, student tutoring, an active student government, leadership opportunities, and faculty engagement, there are so many ways to call ASU home."
