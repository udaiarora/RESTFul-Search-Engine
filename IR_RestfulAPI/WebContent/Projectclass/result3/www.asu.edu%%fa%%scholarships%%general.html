ASU General Scholarship Application
General Info:
  • The ASU General Scholarship Application is used to identify qualified candidates for some limited awards.
  • Some private donors utilize the pool of applicants to select candidates for their scholarships.
  • Scholarships awarded through this application are highly competitive, and submission of this application by the deadline does not guarantee awarding of a scholarship.
Important Details:
  • Scholarship recipients are not selected based on GPA alone. Awards are based on an evaluation of the overall application.
  • Late applications will not be considered.
  • Scholarship awards will vary in amounts.
  • Scholarships awarded through this application process are not automatically renewable. The award is not an entitlement. An updated application must be submitted each year for consideration ( the application becomes available each November) in a highly competitive process. Receipt of the award in a previous year is NOT a factor in the selection process. Additional scholarship opportunities are available at www.asu.edu/fa/scholarships.
  • Upon successful submission, your application will be sent directly to the ASU Scholarship Office. Please print a copy of this application for your records.
  • For students who are selected awards will be posted to recipients' financial aid file, and may be viewed through ASU Interactive. Awards will be made by the end of May. If you are not selected for a scholarship you will not be notified.
  • Additional private donor scholarship funds may become available throughout the academic year. As such, we will continue to provide scholarship donors with qualified applicants.
Eligibility:
  • Undergraduate student
  • Arizona resident (non-resident students may be selected for private donor scholarships)
  • Admitted to a degree program
  • Some private donors using this applicant pool require a minimum 3.0 GPA.
  • Awards made with institutional funds will require a more competitive GPA due to the high number of applicants and exhaustible nature of these funds.
  • Full-time enrollment
    • If you are awarded a general scholarship and do not maintain full-time enrollment, your scholarship may be cancelled.
  • Recipients of other ASU scholarships will not be considered for the ASU General Scholarship. These students may still be considered by private donors for various awards.
Application:
  • Helpful Tips
    • The application will time-out at four hours.
    • Periodically save your work by clicking the "I Agree to the Terms Above & Submit Application/Save for Later" button at the end of the application.
    • You may return to your application for editing up to the deadline.
    • When pasting text from a word processing application, any formatting will be lost.
    • Applications will not be reviewed until the deadline has passed. At that time, all applications are final and will be evaluated as submitted.
  • Should you experience technical problems with the ASU General Scholarship Application, you may contact the SFA Scholarship Office at 480.965.4845 or via e-mail at scholarships@asu.edu.
