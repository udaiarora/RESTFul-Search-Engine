November 4 , 2005

Tempe campus to play host to NASCAR Day

On Nov. 9, Phoenix International Raceway will present a fascinating look at the sport, competition and business of NASCAR in a special program, open to the public, on ASU’s Tempe campus.

NASCAR Day at ASU is an educational program for ASU students and faculty, local business leaders, media and NASCAR fans that will include four sessions of panel discussion focusing on business and career opportunities within the sport.

Event Panelists

Confirmed panelists include:

• Erin Crocker – NASCAR’s premier female driver in development for Evernham Motorsports
• Jimmy Ellege – Crew Chief of the #41 Target Dodge, Chip Ganassi Racing
• Brett Bodine – Former NASCAR Cup driver and current NASCAR Competition Official
• Paul Alepa – Pit Stop Coordinator, Joe Gibbs Racing
• Al Shuford – Athletic Trainer, Joe Gibbs Racing
• Patrick Bernall – Jack Man Pit Crew Member, #45 Georgia-Pacific Dodge, Petty Enterprises
• Jill Gregory – Director, Sports Marketing, NEXTEL
• John Bickford, Sr. – President & General Manager, Jeff Gordon, Inc.
• John Miller – Vice President of Sponsorship, Roush Racing
• Fred Wagenhals – Chairman & CEO, Action Performance Companies
• Terry Dolan – Marketing Manager, Chevrolet Racing
• Don Rothwell – Director of Licensing, NASCAR
• Col. Thomas Nickerson – Director of NASCAR Program, #01 U.S. Army Chevrolet
• Jim Hannigan – Vice President of Licensing, Petty Enterprises
• Scott Rovn – Director of Sales & Marketing, Phoenix International Raceway

Panelists will consist of key players from many facets of the industry, including drivers, team owners, engineers, team and track sponsors, and public relations agencies. Other features of the program include interactive NASCAR exhibits, NASCAR show cars and displays from some of the most prominent NASCAR sponsors.

The event will take place at Gammage Auditorium from 4 – 7 p.m., Nov. 9. Admission is $10 for the general public and $5 for students.

Tickets for NASCAR Day at ASU are available by logging on to (www.ticketmaster.com), or by calling the ASU ticket office at (480) 965-3434.

As part of the event, Mill Avenue District businesses are getting in on the NASCAR action. Ten business partners have signed up to offer special discounts and incentives for all ticket holders. The Mill Avenue District businesses on board include: The Big Bang; Bison Witches Bar & Deli; Caffe Bar; Chester’s Harley-Davidson; Here On The Corner; The Library Bar & Grill; Mill Cue Club; Mill’s End Café; Mood Swings Salon; and Uno Chicago Grill. Ticket holders will receive the offer via coupon included with their event tickets.

Tickets are also available for the remaining ’05 events at PIR. The Checker Auto Parts 500 Weekend featuring the NASCAR AutoZone Elite Division - Southwest Series race (Nov. 10), the Chevy Silverado 150 - NASCAR Craftsman Truck Series race (Nov. 11), NASCAR Busch Series race (Nov. 12), and theChecker Auto Parts 500 presented by TECHRON - NASCAR NEXTEL Cup Series race (Nov. 13) can be purchased online at www.phoenixraceway.com or by calling the ticket office at 602-252-2227 or 1-866-408-7223.
