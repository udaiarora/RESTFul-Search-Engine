F.4: Supporting Material

Effective 5/15/92 .. Revised 4/19/94

SUPPORTING MATERIAL FOR PERSONNEL ACTIONS:


When appropriate, further documentation may be included in the personnel action packet to provide evidence in support of the application. Supporting materials include such documents as copies of publications, papers and significant projects; copies of title page, table of contents and/or programs for books, recitals or presentations; catalogs or brochures for exhibits; and letters of recognition or acknowledgment for service, professional activity or performance effectiveness. As many pieces of supporting material as are appropriate may be submitted with the packet, but because only two may be forwarded to the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University, the academic professional must clearly indicate which document(s) are to be forwarded.
