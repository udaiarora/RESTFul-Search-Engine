December 14, 2006

Accounts Payable forms get makeover

In response to the changes in travel, lodging and meal per diem rates effective Nov. 15, two ASU Accounts Payable forms have been updated: the ?Reimbursement Form for Guest Lecturers, Consultants, and Other Independent Contractors? and the ?Departmental Professional Services Order? form.

In addition to the rate changes, Accounts Payable also has included a new section for documenting the public purpose served. It is current policy that public purpose served to ASU by an expenditure be clearly documented on all disbursement requests.

The new forms are effective immediately and should be used for all new reimbursement requests to guest lecturers, consultants, and other independent contractors or professional services orders. The old forms will be accepted as backup to all accounts payable documents for a short transition period through Jan. 31. For more information, contact Janice Cron in Accounts Payable at (480) 965-3411 or (jcron@asu.edu).
