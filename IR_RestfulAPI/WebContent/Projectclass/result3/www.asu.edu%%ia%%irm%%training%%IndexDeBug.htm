DEBUG

Currently, IRM coordinates the monthly meetings of the Data Entry Benefactor User's Group. DEBUG is designed to bring Benefactor data entry users together to share information and address data entry issues. Attendance by individuals who have successfully completed Benefactor CORE training is mandatory.

The group is organized and led by Lisa Odle, the Training Coordinator for IRM. Meetings are scheduled for the second Wednesday of each month at 3:30 p.m. in the IRM JAD room (ASU Foundation building, suite 235) for one hour (except December).

DEBUG meetings are posted on the IRM Master Calendar. Meeting minutes are taken at all DEBUG meetings and are distributed via e-mail to all ASU Benefactor/Colleague users through the BENE-Q distribution list. Archived editions of DEBUG Meeting minutes are available through the links located on this web page.

Users can self-subscribe to the BENE-Q distribution list by sending an e-mail to the listserv. In the body of the e-mail note type: subscribe bene-q user name (for example: subscribe bene-q Sandra Beach) and remove any autosignature before sending the e-mail. Leave the Subject Line blank.

Self subscribe to BENE-Q now.

 

DOBUG

DOBUG is the companion users group to DEBUG. DOBUG is the Development Officers Benefactor User's Group. DOBUG is currently undergoing reorganization and is on hiatus. Archived meeting minutes for DOBUG are available from the IRM Help Desk.
