Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

June 14, 2005 ? Called to order 10:00 am

Members Present:  Barbara Trapido-Lurie (Chair), Beth Luey, Bill Sharp, Dan Stanton, Victoria Trotta , John  Wheatley

Victoria Trotta reported on the meeting of the PAC 20, which focused on the implementation of the new background check policy. Implementation will take place at the unit level, with the overall supervision of Human Resources. The new policy is available on the web as ACD 126.

 

Most of the meeting was devoted to a discussion of the final draft of the new ACD section 507. The committee raised a number of questions to be referred to Nancy Gutierrez and Mary Stevens. The committee agreed to meet again on Thursday, June 16, in hopes of completing its review.

 

The meeting adjourned at 11:45 a.m.

 

Thursday, June 16 - Reconvened at 10:00

 

In attendance: Beth Luey,  Bill Sharp, Dan Stanton, Barbara Trapido-Lurie, Victoria Trotta, and John Wheatley.

 

The committee completed its review of the new ACD 507, and its comments were forwarded to Nancy Gutierrez and Mary Stevens.

 

The committee commended the task force on its hard work and on the quality of the resulting document.

 

The committee also thanked outgoing members Beth Luey, Bill Sharp, Victoria Trotta, and John Wheatley for their outstanding service.

 

The meeting adjourned at 11:30 a.m.
