Request to Catalog Free Web Resources


(For Librarians at the West campus and Polytechnic campus libraries only.)

 

Librarians at Arizona State University at the West campus and Polytechnic Campus libraries should use this form to select free Internet resources for cataloging. The requests will then be submitted to Technical Services for cataloging. 


Any free Internet resource that requires licensing should be submitted through the Electronic Resources Coordinator in Collection Development at the Tempe campus Libraries, or should be licensed appropriately through the West or Polytechnic campus. This form will be used only to provide access to free web sites through the Libraries' catalog, and does not indicate inclusion of a link to the resource on any ASU web page outside the catalog.
