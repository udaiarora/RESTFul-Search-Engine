Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

April 6, 2004

Members Present:  Sue Hunter (Chair), Beth Luey, and Barbara Trapido-Lurie

 

Excused:  Carol Behl, Patricia Feldman, Victoria Trotta

Old business

Revisions to ACD 508-05 and 509-03:  Mary Stevens, ASU Deputy General Counsel, has requested one additional change to the text of ACD 509-03; she asked that wherever the document refers to ?legal counsel?, the word ?legal? be omitted ? because counsel can be provided by individuals other than lawyers. Sue Hunter will make these changes, and then will return both 509-05 and 509-03 to Nancy Gutierrez, requesting that the revisions be approved by the Provost.

 

The next steps will be: (1) ASU academic professionals will vote on the revisions, and then (2) the revised documents will be presented to the Academic Senate for their vote.

New business

Academic Professional Task Force progress report: The members present wondered when and how CAPS should expect to participate in working with the Task Force Proposals. (Amendment as of 04/12/04: Patricia Feldman explained that the Task Force recommendations were submitted to Provost Glick on April 5th. The provost will decide how to move forward with implementation, including the role of CAPS in this process.)

 

Upcoming elections for new CAPS members: Current members Carol Behl and Patricia Feldman will complete their terms this spring. Academic Professionals can vote online for next year's new CAPS members. The ballot will be available through April 16.

 

CAPS officers: CAPS officers for next year will be selected at the May meeting.

 

Meeting adjourned at 11:00 am.

 

Submitted by Barbara Trapido-Lurie

Minutes approved 04/19/04
