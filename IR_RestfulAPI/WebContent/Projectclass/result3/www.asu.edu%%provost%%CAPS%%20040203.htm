Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

February 3, 2004

Members Present:  Sue Hunter (Chair), Patty Feldman, Sue Hunter, Beth Luey

 

Excused:  Carol Behl, Barbara Trapido-Lurie, Victoria Trotta

Old business

Revisions to ACD 508-05 and 509-03 have been forwarded to the Provost?s office for approval. This is the first step in the process to approve changes to the ACD manual

New business

Patty Feldman gave an update on the work of the AP Task Force. The Task Force has been meeting regularly, twice a month, to address the charge given to the group. The Task Force expects to have a draft report and recommendations available by March 1, 2004 and a final report by April 5, 2004. The Task Force will hold open forums after March 1 to obtain input from academic professionals.

 

The Spotlight Speaker session for the spring semester will be an open forum to provide the opportunity for academic professionals to ask questions and make responses to the draft report from the AP Task Force. CAPS will make the date and room arrangements.

 

There was a discussion on the membership of CAPS in relation to the upcoming completion of committee member terms. One member is completing a term. It was noted the CAPS by-laws state there should be two alternate members; however, this year there was only one alternate.

 

 

Meeting adjourned at 10:30 am.

 

Submitted by Sue Hunter
