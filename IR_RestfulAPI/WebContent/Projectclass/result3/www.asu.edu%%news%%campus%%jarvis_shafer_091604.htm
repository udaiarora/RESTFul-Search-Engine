September 16, 2004

Jarvis, Shafer join Media Relations

Media Relations and Public Information is gearing up to support ASU’s increasing visibility and growing communications needs with two new staff members.

Terri Shafer joins the team as director of marketing and strategic communications, while John Jarvis is the new editor and publisher of ASU Insight.

Shafer previously served as director of marketing and public affairs for the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering.
Shafer joined ASU in 2003 after working in corporate marketing roles for more than 10 years and in corporate information systems for seven years.

Shafer says she is looking forward to seeing the university from a different perspective.
“The last year and a half in the Fulton School of Engineering has been a whirlwind,” Shafer says. “I’ve been able to work with great people on exciting projects during a time of dramatic change and growth. I’m delighted to join the exceptional team in Media Relations and to step up to new challenges and opportunities.”

Jarvis took over as editor and publisher of ASU Insight Aug. 23, replacing Christine Lambrakis, who now serves as the assistant director in the Office of Public Affairs at ASU’s East campus.

Jarvis has 17 years of experience in the newspaper field, with stints on news and sports desks at publications in Texas, Indiana and Arizona. Here in the Valley, he has served as the managing editor for World Publications Corp., which publishes close to 30 community newspapers, and as editor of Art-Talk, a monthly newspaper based in Scottsdale that details fine art trends in the Southwest.

“It’s an honor to be a part of the ASU family,” said Jarvis, whose wife, Cheryl, is an assistant professor of marketing in the W. P. Carey School of Business. “I’ve learned a lot about ASU during our four years of living here, and I’m eager to learn a lot more about the campus community as editor of ASU Insight.”

Shafer can be reached at (480) 965-3865 or (terri.shafer@asu.edu). Jarvis can be reached at (480) 965-9689 or (j.jarvis@asu.edu).

Media Relations and Public Information is part of the Office of Public Affairs.
