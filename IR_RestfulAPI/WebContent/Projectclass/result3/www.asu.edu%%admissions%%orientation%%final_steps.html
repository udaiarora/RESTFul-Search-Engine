What Should I Do After Orientation?

Review your Fall 2007 Class Schedule
www.asu.edu/interactive

Drop/Add
Make any additions, deletions or changes to your schedule by using the Drop/Add function by accessing ASU Interactive.

Pay Tuition
Pay your tuition. Your account charges, payment deadlines and payment method information will be available through ASU Interactive in late April.

Purchase ASU Parking Decal/Permit
Sign up at ASU Interactive. Students living in an ASU residence hall will be sent a parking information packet and application in June. Be sure to fill out the application and mail it back immediately.

Submit Final Official Transcript
Submit final official transcript to ASU as soon as possible.

ASU U-Pass
ASU partners with Valley Metro to provide free access to all Valley Metro bus routes. Read more about the program and when passes will be available at www.asu.edu/dps/pts.

ASU Welcome
Participate in events and activities on your campus to welcome you to ASU. Look for information online and in the mail in July. www.asu.edu/admissions/steps/welcomeprogram.html

Remember that classes begin August 20, 2007.
