IT Help Desk

If you need assistance or have a computing problem, you can come to the IT Help Desk or call at (480) 965-6500. The IT Help Desk can also assist with ASURITE User IDs.

Classroom Support

IT provides classroom support, training and equipment for many of the classrooms on ASU's Tempe Campus. See the Office of Classroom Management Web page for more information.

Computing Sites

IT provides four computing sites on ASU's Tempe campus and numerous locations where wireless is available. Printing information is also provided.

Instruction Support

The Information Technology Instruction Support Group offers hands-on training and workshops centered on enhancing instruction through the use of technology.
