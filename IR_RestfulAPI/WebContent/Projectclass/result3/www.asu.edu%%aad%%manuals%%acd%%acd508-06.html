ACD 508–06: Resignation of a Faculty Member or Academic Professional

Purpose

To describe the policy regarding the acceptance of faculty and academic professional resignations

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

The president has delegated to the executive vice president and provost of the university the authority to accept resignations from faculty and academic professionals. The executive vice president and provost of the university invites the resigning faculty member or academic professional to participate in an exit interview.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Resigning faculty member
or
academic professional
  1. Write letter of resignation to the executive vice president and provost of the university.
Executive vice president and provost of the university
  1. Send a letter of acknowledgment to the individual.

  2. Send a copy of both letters to the individual’s dean and department chair, and to the vice provost for academic personnel.
Resigning faculty member
or
academic professional
  1. Return university property including keys and equipment, whether purchased with state or grant funds.

  2. Pay delinquent obligations such as parking and library fines.

  3. Return photo ID card to Human Resources (HR).

  4. Discuss insurance conversion rights and retirement options in HR.

  5. Return parking decal to Parking and Transit Services.

  6. Arrange to have user ID deleted from all ASU computer systems.
endtable
