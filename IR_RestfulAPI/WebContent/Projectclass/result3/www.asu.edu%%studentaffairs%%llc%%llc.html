Living and Learning Communities @ ASU

The Living and Learning Communities (LLC) at ASU provide you with the opportunity to connect with others who share your academic and personal interests. As part of your First-Year Experience, the LLC can help you connect with resources to support your success in the first year and beyond. 


Click on the community links to find the one that is right for you.
