Getting Started
 

To finish this homework you will need:

  • Your ASURITE ID (check you admissions letter)

  • Your student ID number

  • A pencil and a piece of paper

  • About 45 minutes

Navigating this web site is easy.  Links will appear in maroon or blue or as a graphic.  Links to external sites will be opened in a new browser window (assuming your browser supports this function) so that you don't lose your place.  We recommend Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0 or higher, but other browsers will work (you just may not get all the fancy bells and whistles).

The lucky pencil graphic is your cue to jot something down as it is likely you'll be asked about it in your homework assignment.  

As you complete certain tasks or sections, a checklist will appear to the right that allows you to return to that particular part of the web site as needed.  Of course, you can use your browser's back button as well.

You can download our orientation packet here, but most of this information is contained in this Web site.
