Dynamics of Perception, Action, and Cognition (DPAC)
Graduate Applicants
Graduate applicants that are interested in joining DPAC must apply to Arizona State University through the Graduate College and one of the following departments. 
Psychology Department
 
Graduate applicants to the Psychology Department apply directly for the Ph.D. in the Cognitive Systems area.  It is advisable to contact the graduate advisor with whom you are most interested in working prior to submitting your application.  Applications are available through the Graduate Admissions Secretary, Lee McCracken (480-965-7156).  They also may be downloaded directly.  The deadline is December 1.
 
The following DPAC faculty are currently admitting graduate students:

Exercise Science and Physical Education
 
There are separate Master's and Doctoral programs in Exercise Science.  It is advisable to contact the graduate advisor with whom you are most interested in working prior to submitting your application.  Applications are available through the Graduate Admissions Secretary, Jill Butler (480-965-9580).  They also may be downloaded directly: M.S. or Ph.D.
 
Apply to the Exercise Science and Physical Education (ESPE) program if you wish to pursue an M.S. in Motor Development and Learning.
