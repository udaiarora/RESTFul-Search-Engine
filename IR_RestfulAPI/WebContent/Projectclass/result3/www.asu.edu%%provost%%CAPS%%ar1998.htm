Annual Report 1998

Members:
Annette Voth, Music Library - Co-Chair

Tom Keating, Psychology

Accomplishments

Job Announcements:
Successful in getting only Academic Professional (AP) job announcements forwarded to CAPS representative for this year. Annette was recipient. I do not know if this is still being done. This needs to be a constant reminder for CAPS to check into every year.

Faculty orientation of AP's:
AP greeting packet put together and Provost office will inform CAPS Via Tom when new AP's are hired. Welcome packet will them be sent to the new AP. End of year list turned over to people running faculty orientation to include the AP's. This is a difficult problem as we are dependent upon the Provost office for our information. If we do not know that there is a new AP we can not send out a packet. I would like this to be more automated rather than a personal touch to get this information forwarded to the committee.

ACD 506-09:
Revised and included in latest revision of ACD.

Academic Professional Job Category:
Questioned the inclusion of Post Doc's in the AP category with Dr. Harris. There are only nine categories allowed by the Board of Reagents and the Post Docs fit in the AP category better than any other. Looks like we are a catch all again! One positive result is that we can get a sorted list of APs with 50% or more full time equivalent from Barbara Mawhiney: Director Office of Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action.

Post-Continuing review:
CAPS has started a University wide document. This will outline the guidelines by which APs will be reviewed rather than being included in the units Faculty documents. PACAPS will be consulted in order to ensure this document is good for the entire Campus. Resulting document will be sent out to all AP's in order to get input before sending the final document off to the Provost's Office. Expected completion 98-99.

ACD 704-02:
Started work on getting AP's who retire their accumulated vacation upon retirement rather than loosing it. ACD 607-04 indicates that AP's will be paid for unused vacation time, but ACD704-02 stated that AP's must use the vacation time before they retire of they will lose it. Hoped resolution by mid-99.
