2001 Essay/Poetry Contest

Adam David, Grade 1
Teacher: Mrs. Sandra Matsumara
Pueblo del Sol Elementary
Sierra Vista District

First Place
Primary (Grades K-2)

 

Josh is a good leader because every time I get hurt Josh helps me up to see if I'm ok and if I am not Josh takes me to my dad to help me. Josh makes good choices for himself and I choose them because they are very good choices. Josh is not a cheater at any of the games we play together.
