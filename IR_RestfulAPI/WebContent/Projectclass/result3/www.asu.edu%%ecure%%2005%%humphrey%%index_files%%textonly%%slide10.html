Gap Analysis

Findings:

    • The vast majority of academic and non-academic research data fall outside the current interpretation and execution of the mandates of the National Library and National Archives (now the Library & Archives of Canada).

    • No other Canadian institution has a national mandate or the resources to address the current level of need for preserving research data.
