Pie Control: title2


the title2 attributes

Type: SASHELP.CLASSES.CTEXTLABEL_C.CLASS
Valid Values:

Details

Details

The title is a complex attribute. When you specify four titles, the bottom title may slightly overlap the graph. This is intended behavior for small region sizes, as there is a desired minimum size for the graph itself. To avoid the overlap, try increasing the region size.
