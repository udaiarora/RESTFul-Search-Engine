View the "ASU Generations: Homecoming" Video


(posted 10/29/04)

Click the image above to view "ASU Generations: Homecoming."

This week ASU Libraries will premier its second digital video production, " ASU Generations: Homecoming." The narrated video runs approximately ten minutes and features vintage film and photography from the University Archives collections documenting the history of Homecoming at ASU.


Grady Gammage, Jr. narrates the Homecoming coronation ceremony script originally read by his father in the 1940's. University Archives staff selected and digitized photos, and wrote and digitized the narrator's script. Technical wizardry of several staff from LIST enabled the editing, production and streaming of the digital video, available by clicking on the image at left. The video will also be shown via laptop in the ASU Libraries Homecoming tent Saturday November 6th.


Please join us for a special screening of "ASU Generations: Homecoming," and also the original 2003 "ASU Generations" video production, on Wednesday November 3rd at 12 noon in Hayden-133.
