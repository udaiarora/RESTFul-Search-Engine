Mission & Goals

The committee is a vital outreach service organized to promote awareness of the resources, programs, and common concerns of various museums, galleries and collections at ASU. Numerous facilities throughout the university manage collections and develop educational exhibitions in the sciences, humanities, and arts. The goals of the committee are:

  • To reach and attract diverse audiences on ASU's campus and in the Arizona community
  • To create and maintain a viable outreach program
  • To seek opportunities for university museums, galleries, and collections professionals to network and collaborate on development of educational exhibits and programs

Co-chairs: Penny Borszich, Kim Savage
Public Relations Partner: Judith Smith
