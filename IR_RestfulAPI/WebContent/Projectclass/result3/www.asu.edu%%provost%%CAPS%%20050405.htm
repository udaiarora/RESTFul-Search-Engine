Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

April 5, 2005 ? Called to order 10:10 am

Members Present:  Barbara Trapido-Lurie (Chair), Beth Luey, Bill Sharp, Dan Stanton, John  Wheatley

Absent:  Victoria Trotta

Old business

Although Victoria Trotta was absent, she forwarded information about the PAC-20 meeting to Barbara Trapido-Lurie, who will forward it to the committee.

 

Barbara Trapido-Lurie, reporting on the Academic  Senate meeting, noted that the election ended April 1 with results to be announced April 18. The Senate is continuing to work on improvements to the distribution of excess sick leave upon retirement and is discussing ways to implement the state-mandated background checks on new employees.

 

She also reported that the Spring Forum, featuring Barbara Kerr and Susan Mattson, president and president-elect of the Academic Senate, will be held on April 21 from 10:00 to 11:00.

 

Beth Luey and Bill Sharp reported on the continued progress of the implementation task force. The committee reviewed the current drafts of sections 507-03, 507-04, and 507-08. The comments will be reported to the task force. In addition, the committee discussed several larger issues relating to ACD policies.

 

Meeting adjourned at 11:15 am
