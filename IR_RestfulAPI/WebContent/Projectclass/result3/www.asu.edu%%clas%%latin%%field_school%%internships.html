Cancellation Policy for Summer Programs:

Students please note, your application is not considered complete until you pay the non-refundable program deposit by Visa or Mastercard. Once you submit your online application, you will be directed to our secure credit card processing website. You will be responsible for this non-refundable deposit unless you are not accepted to the program.
For cancellation fees and policies, please see the online brochure for your program under Eligibility.
To review cancellation fees and policies please click here to return to the Summer Programs list.

If you have further questions about the application process, deposits or cancellation fees before you submit your application please contact office at (480) 965-0101 or via email at study.programs@asu.edu. Thank you!
