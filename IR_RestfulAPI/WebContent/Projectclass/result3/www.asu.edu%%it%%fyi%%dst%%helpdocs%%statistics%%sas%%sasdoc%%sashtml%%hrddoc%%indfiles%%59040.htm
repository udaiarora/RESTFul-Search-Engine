SAS Technical Report R-108, Algorithms for the PRINQUAL and TRANSREG Procedures

Gives details of the internal algorithms used in the PRINQUAL and TRANSREG procedures. This report provides the six optimal scaling algorithms and the seven METHOD=name algorithms used in these procedures. This technical report is intended for advanced users who are familiar with the PRINQUAL and TRANSREG procedures and want to know the details of the internal algorithms used in these procedures.

28 pages.
