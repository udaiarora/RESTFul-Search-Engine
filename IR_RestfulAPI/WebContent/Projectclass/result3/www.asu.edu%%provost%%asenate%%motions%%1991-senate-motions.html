CodeSM #3, #4, #5, #6 and #7(111891)Revisions to the Constitution, Bylaw 1, Bylaw II and Bylaw III:
Under the current constitution, a review of the document must be conducted every four years, and a special committee performed this task during 1990-91. With some additional changes from the Senate, the recommendations from the Committee were approved. The Revised Faculty Constitution and Bylaws was approved by the Faculty Assembly by a mail ballot on February 11, 1992. A revised draft of the constitution was mailed to each member of the Faculty Assembly.

CodeSM #19(032392)Determination of a Catalog in effect for graduation.
The Senate recommends adoption of the Academic Program Articulation Steering Committee's proposal for a uniform policy among all universities, college and junior colleges, in the State of Arizona concerning the determination of the catalog in effect for graduation. A student may choose between this policy and using the year of their initial enrollment for a period of two years following the approval of this policy
