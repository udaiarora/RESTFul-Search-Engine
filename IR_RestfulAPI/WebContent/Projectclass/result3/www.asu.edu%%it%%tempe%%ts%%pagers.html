Pagers

To order a pager, submit a Telephone Services Request (TSR) to Telephone Services, mail code 0502. Be sure to include the Area/Org to be charged for the pager and an authorized signature on the form.

Various Pagers & Related Services

Digital Pager - $3.00 per month

  • Basic pager
  • receives numeric messages
  • holds 16 pages

Nationwide Digital Pager - $19.00 per month

  • Receives numeric messages within the United States, has numeric retrieval to retrieve numbers when pager has been turned off.

Nationwide Digital Pager with 800 Number - $24.00 per month

  • Same as above, with 800 number access

Alpha-Numeric Pager - $8.00 per month

  • receives numeric and printed messages

Alpha-Numeric Dispatch Center - $5.00 one-time activation, $.30 per call

  • Voice message service to dispatch alpha messages
  • maximum 200 characters per message

Express Mail - $2.00 per month

  • 15-second greeting
  • 30-second message length
  • 5 messages held in storage
  • 12-hour message storage

Page Mail - $4.00 per month

  • 15-second greeting
  • 60-second message length
  • 10 messages held in storage
  • 24-hour message storage

PageMail Plus - $6.00 per month

  • 30-second greeting
  • 2-minute message length
  • 20 messages held in storage
  • 72-hour message storage

Northern California Coverage - $3.25 per month

  • Adds extended coverage to digital pager

Southern California Coverage - $3.25 per month

  • Adds extended coverage to digital pager

Off Net Pager - $9.00 per month

  • Pager for the outlying area (Tucson, Flagstaff, etc.)

Group Call Pagers - $1.50 per month

  • More than one pager with the same phone number; in addition to pager cost
