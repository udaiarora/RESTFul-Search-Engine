Mission

Academic and Administrative Documents provides editorial leadership and technical expertise to improve, produce, and maintain accurate, consistent administrative manuals and general and graduate catalogs in print and on the World Wide Web for Arizona State University and its local, regional, and global communities.

Academic and Administrative Documents (AAD) has created a book on how to write a manual, Creating Administrative Manuals (CAM). AAD is available for informal consultation with university administrative units on the development and maintenance of internal manuals such as departmental or desk manuals.

Print catalog distribution

AAD produces the ASU General Catalog and the Graduate Catalog and arranges for their distribution to all other campus units that distribute the catalogs directly to users. AAD distributes catalogs to ASU employees who request them. AAD provides catalogs to community colleges, sends catalogs to selected libraries, and fills requests by students and others seeking older editions.

Undergraduate Admissions makes a free copy of the General Catalog available to each newly admitted freshman, transfer applicant, and nondegree applicant and to high school and community college counselors. The university Registrar’s Office supplies catalogs to readmitted students.

The Division of Graduate Studies makes a free Graduate Catalog available to each newly admitted graduate student.

The ASU Bookstore, ASU West Bookstore, and four non-ASU bookstores (The College Store, Rother’s University Book Store, the Student Book Center, and the Varsity Book Exchange) sell catalogs for $4.00 plus tax. The ASU Bookstore also sells catalogs to individuals who request them by phone or mail. The buyer pays an additional charge of $3.75 if requesting a first-class mailing.

To purchase a General Catalog or Graduate Catalog by mail, phone the ASU Bookstore at 480/965–7928.

Online catalogs

The ASU general and graduate catalogs are available online at the General and Graduate Catalogs Web page in PDF and HTML formats.
