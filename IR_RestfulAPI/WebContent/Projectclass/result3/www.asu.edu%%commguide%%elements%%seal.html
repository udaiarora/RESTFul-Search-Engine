Graphic Elements: University Seal

The official seal of Arizona State University is the official seal of the state of Arizona with the words “"Arizona State University 1885"” encircling it. Use of the seal is reserved for approved signage, formal and official documents such as diplomas, legal and official records, transcripts, programs for formal academic ceremonies, and any other legal agreements binding the university.

Use of the seal requires prior authorization from the ASU Brand Council and is not available to download. To contact the ASU Brand Council, email
