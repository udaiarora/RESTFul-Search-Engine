ACD 305–07: Faculty Office Hours

Purpose

To provide sufficient opportunities for students to seek assistance under conditions of privacy

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

Faculty

Academic professionals

Administrative staff, service professionals, and classified staff

Teaching assistants

Policy

Every individual who teaches courses is expected to post and maintain regular office hours. The schedule of office hours should be easily available to students. Faculty should also be available by appointment for students who cannot meet during posted office hours.
