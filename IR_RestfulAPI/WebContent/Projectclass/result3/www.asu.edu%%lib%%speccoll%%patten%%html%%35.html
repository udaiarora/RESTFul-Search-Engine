Dodoens, Rembert

1517-1585.

Historia frumentorum, leguminum, palustrium et aquatilium
herbarum ac eorum, quae eo pertinent.

Antverpiae: Ex officina Christophori Plantini, 1569. Includes 88 woodcut
illustrations by P. van der Borcht. Second edition, rev. and enl.--Cf.
Johnston, S. H. Cleveland coll. 101. References: Hunt 105; Johnston, S.
H. Cleveland coll. 101. Includes indexes. References: Hunt 105; Johnston,
S. H. Cleveland coll. 101. Includes indexes.

This is a second edition of PAT-34. The main difference seems to be
in the layout of the work, where now the second edition has more paragraphs,
making for easier reading. Dodoens's descriptions of grains, legumes, marshy
plants, and water plants give details about the plant, including histories,
and also list the common plant names in other languages, such as Greek,
German, Belgian, Italian, and French.
