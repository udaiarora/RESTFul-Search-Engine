Work directly with students, staff, faculty, and the general public on all phases of the readmission process, which includes providing general information and conveying university and registrar policies and procedures in person and by telephone and email. Provide customer service in a busy front counter environment. Evaluate academic credentials for undergraduate students applying for readmission to the university and determine if the student meets established academic requirements. Process student record requests such as grade changes and grade duplications/deletions. Evaluate and post incoming transcripts to readmitting and continuing student records. Maintain and enter student records utilizing ASU's student databases, MS Excel, and MS Word. Maintain an online personal calendar. Cross-train in diverse activities including areas such as in-person customer service, academic transactions, and data entry, to assist with variable peak processing demands.  
Two years of general clerical or related experience

Demonstrated customer service experience, in a high-volume service location preferred. Experience retrieving and updating information on a mainframe or relational database (e.g., PeopleSoft, ASU's Student Information System, Degree Audit Reporting System, and/or DARwin). Experience with: word processing; spreadsheet; email; and web browser software (e.g. MS Word, Excel, Outlook, and Internet Explorer). Experience working with university departments and/or advising units. Experience answering a multi-line telephone.
