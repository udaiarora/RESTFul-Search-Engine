ASU Computing policies



 
Site Home > Connecting

ASU provides students direct access to the World Wide Web in all the residence halls. Each room is equipped with one Ethernet port and one Ethernet cable for each student. Do not use a telephone cord.

Your computer must be equipped with an Ethernet card. Ethernet cards are often referred to as Network Interface Cards (NIC) or Network Adapters. This is the device that allows your computer to communicate with the network. It is like a modem, but does not use your phone line.

Please review our installation instructions to configure your TCP/IP and DHCP and to determine your Ethernet adapter address. If you change computers or get a new NIC card, refer to our installation instructions to reregister your MAC address.

Computer protection

It is your responsibility to obtain and install your own virus protection software. ASU offers virus protection software. Please visit the Computer Assistance Center site.
