Shannon Mertens, Grade 5
Teacher: Mrs. Cheryl Schaab
Howard K. Conley Elementary
Chandler Unified District

Third Place — Intermediate (Grades 3-5)

 

Mr. Narducci

Frank Narducci is the principal of Conley Elementary School. He built a microsociety for us. There is a Bank of America, we have our own accounts and it helps us with our math. There is a Basha's store where we can go in and write a check for an item in the store. There is a Salt River Project where we get to water the gardens and go around the school and check for lights out and doors locked for conservation. There is even a Postal Office where the kids in 2nd grade and lower get to sort the mail and put it in the teacher's box. I was chosen by my teacher to drop the mail in the mail box and pick up our mail. Mr. Narducci says attitude is everything. Also, if you work hard enough you will achieve it. He is really funny and nice but he also has the kids follow the rules. He has a wife named Vicki Narducci and a daughter named Emily. Mr. Narducci is always at every school event, even goes in our dunk tank. He is a really good sport even though he is a U of A fan. He wore an ASU Sun Devil Jersey for a whole day at school!! My world is a better world because he is my friend. He is a good principal and I like learning from him.
