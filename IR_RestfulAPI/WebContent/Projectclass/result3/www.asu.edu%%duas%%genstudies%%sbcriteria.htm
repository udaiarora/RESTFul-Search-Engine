The following types of courses are excluded from the SB area even though they might give some consideration to social and behavioral science concerns:

a. Courses with primarily fine arts, humanities, literary, or philosophical content.
b. Courses with primarily natural or physical science content.
c. Courses with predominantly applied orientation for professional skills or training purposes.
d. Courses emphasizing primarily oral, quantitative, or written skills.
