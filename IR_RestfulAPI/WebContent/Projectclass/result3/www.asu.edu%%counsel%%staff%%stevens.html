Mary C. Stevens has served as a university attorney in the General Counsel's Office since April, 1984. Prior to her employment with ASU, she was employed as an associate attorney with the law firm of James A. Ullman, P.C. which specialized in teacher employment matters and represented the teacher members of the Arizona Education Association. Following graduation from law school in 1980, she served as a law clerk to the Honorable James Duke Cameron, then Chief Justice of the Arizona Supreme Court on a one year appointment. Her specialization includes employment, personnel matters and civil rights matters. She regularly provides in-house training on employment rights, due process, civil rights and other employment rights to university committees and administrators. She has participated as a presenter at national conferences and workshops. Mary is a graduate of Arizona State University (J.D. 1980),(B.A. Elementary Education 1965) and the University of Denver (M.A. Theater 1969). She has been admitted to the practice of law in Arizona since 1980.
