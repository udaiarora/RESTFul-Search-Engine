PCS 1005 Table

The department:

  1. completes a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form, checking the Disposal/Salvage box in the “Activity” section

    Note: Forms are available in Property Control.

  2. records the ASU property control number on the PRA, indicating in the “Reason/Comments” block that the equipment is to be cannibalized

  3. obtains the signature of the department chair, dean, or director

    Note: No exceptions for signatures will be permitted.

  4. forwards the approved PRA to Surplus Property with the leftover property

    and

  5. retains the originating department copy for department files.

If sponsor-owned equipment appears on the PRA, Property Control notifies the sponsored property coordinator. The sponsored property coordinator checks the source of funds, title, and the sponsored account for specific regulations concerning disposition procedures.

If specific disposition instructions for cannibalization are required, the sponsored property coordinator contacts the sponsor.

If equipment is owned by ASU, Surplus Property:

  1. verifies that cannibalization has taken place

  2. approves the PRA, verifying equipment cannibalization

    3. and

  3. retains the original for department files.

Property Control removes the equipment from the property control system.
