LSAT

Law School Admission Test


Information
The Law School Admission Test provides a standard measure of acquired reading and verbal reasoning skills that law schools can use as one of several factors in assessing applicants. LSAT bulletins are available for pickup in our office or visit www.lsac.org for more information.
Schedule
Available Dates for Exam
  • February 10, 2007
Cost
The Law School Admission Test costs $118.00.
Time
The exam consists of five multiple choice sections and a writing sample. The total appointment time is approximately six hours.
Register
Registration deadlines are typically five weeks prior to the test. You can register online at www.lsac.org. Registration bulletins are also available for pickup in our office.
Retake
You may not take the LSAT more than three times in any two year period. This policy applies even if you cancel your score or it is not otherwise reported.
Admission
Walk in testing is not permitted. Exam entrance requires an admission ticket and one form of identification bearing both the examinee's photo and signature.
