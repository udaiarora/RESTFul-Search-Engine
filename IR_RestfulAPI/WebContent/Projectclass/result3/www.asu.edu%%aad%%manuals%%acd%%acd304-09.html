ACD 304–09: Evaluation of Teaching

Purpose

To provide an opportunity for student feedback on instruction

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

All individuals responsible for instruction including:

Ranked faculty (professors, lecturers, instructors)

Academic professionals

Administrative staff, service professionals, and classified staff

Teaching associates and assistants

Policy

Every instructor in every class every semester is required to administer a departmentally approved course evaluation form. The administration procedure should assure the students’ ability to respond forthrightly and anonymously. The evaluation should be distributed and collected by someone other than the instructor; the instructor should not see the evaluation or its summary prior to submitting grades for the course; and a copy of the results should be reviewed by the department chair
