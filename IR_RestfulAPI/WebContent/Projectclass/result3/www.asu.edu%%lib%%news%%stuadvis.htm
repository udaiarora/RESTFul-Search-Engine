Students … Come Dine with the Dean!


(posted 2/14/05)

The Libraries have created a brand new committee, the Library Student Advisory Committee (LSAC), to open a channel of communication between students and Sherrie Schmidt, the Dean of Libraries & University Librarian. The library will use the advice of students on the committee to enhance library resources and services.


The Library Student Advisory Committee will meet once each semester in Hayden Library (with food provided!). Student members are asked to make at least a one-year commitment to serving on the committee. Students must be users of one or more libraries on the Tempe campus and/or they should be users of the library's online resources. Can't make one of the formal meetings? Don't worry, there will be other opportunities to participate through such things as focus groups, online surveys and other library events.


Go to the LSAC web site to find out how you can participate in this important committee and dine with the Dean twice a year!
