The ASU Communication Guide is a one-stop resource for promoting the ASU brand on all web, print and electronic communication produced to support ASU’s mission. 

 

What’s New

The latest additions are detailed explanations of the branded visual styling, expanded color palette and photo guidelines.

[8/01/06] Presentation: Achieving Common Look and Feel on the ASU Web (PDF)

BRANDING is more than the how to's for using the ASU sunburst logo (although that is included here, too). It's a much broader concept including color, typography, fonts, photography and more. To help you produce communications that fully represent the ASU brand, the ASU Communication Guide is a collection of the guidelines (or standards that are expected), information about branding in general and a definition of the ASU brand, downloadable logos in several formats, a toolbox of templates and resources, and access to the ASU photo gallery, release forms and description of photographic services - everything you need to produce ASU-branded communications. The ASU brand is more than a logo or color scheme - it is a pledge, a demonstration of trust, to the people we value. Learn more >
