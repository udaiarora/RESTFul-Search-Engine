ECUADOR: The Amazon and the Andes Field School

Frequently Asked questions

Applying to the Program & other administrative details:

  • Who is eligible to join the program?
  • Do I need to be in good physical condition?
  • Is Financial Aid available?
  • What is the cancellation policy?
  • How do I make my flight arrangements?
  • May I stay in Ecuador longer or arrive earlier than the program dates?

Preparing for the Program:

  • Do I need a passport?
  • Will I need a Visa?
  • What are the health concerns for traveling to Ecuador and more specifically to the Amazon area?
  • What can I do to prepare for a safe trip?
  • How can I learn more about Ecuador?
  • What can I read to prepare myself for the Ecuador Summer Field School?
  • What should I pack?
  • Should I bring travelers checks?
  • How much spending money do I need to bring?
  • Should I bring a long distance telephone calling card? Will I be able to call home?

What to expect once you get to Ecuador:

  • What tribe or ethnic group will be hosting the class in the Amazon?
  • Where is the research site in the Amazon?
  • What are the living conditions in the Amazonian Napo Runa Community?
  • Is there plumbing or running water?
  • What is the food like?
  • Are kosher or vegetarian meals available?
  • Will we be hiking and camping out in the jungle?
  • What animals and birds will be see?
  • Are there snakes?
  • Are there noxious insects?
  • Do the native communities we will be visiting speak Spanish?
  • Is knowledge of Spanish required?
  • If I know Spanish will I be able to use it in Ecuador?
  • What is the danger from volcanic activity in the region?
  • Will I experience altitude sickness in the high Andes?
  • Is there any risk of violent crime or guerilla activity?
  • Are there drugstores available where I will be able to purchase toiletries?
  • Do I need to know how to swim or canoe?
  • Is there an airport exit tax?

If you have questions or concerns that are not addressed here, please contact Dr. Tod Swanson or Cesar Cordova directly and one of them will assist you.
