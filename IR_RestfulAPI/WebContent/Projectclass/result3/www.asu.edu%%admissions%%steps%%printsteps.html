Visit ASU
Visit ASU online and in person.
www.asu.edu/admissions/explore

Apply for admission
Read ASU’s undergraduate admission requirements and then apply by the priority application date.

Freshmen: apply by December 1 to be considered for merit scholarships. Applying online is the fastest way to have your application processed. www.asu.edu/admissions/howtoapply

Some majors have special requirements and early deadlines for admission to professional programs (junior/senior level). www.asu.edu/admissions/howtoapply/professional.html

Request transcripts and test scores

Transcripts:
Ask each high school and college you have attended to send your official transcripts directly to ASU. You must also request that a final official transcript be sent from your school directly to ASU upon graduation or completion of the current semester. Hand delivered transcripts are not official. Send your transcripts to the address shown:

Undergraduate Admissions
Arizona State University
PO Box 870112
Tempe, AZ 85287-0112.

Test Scores:
If your ACT or SAT score does not appear on your high school transcript, ask ACT www.act.org or the College Board (SAT) www.collegeboard.com to send your scores directly to Undergraduate Admissions for all campuses.

If you have taken AP, IB, and/or CLEP exams, request that your scores be sent directly to Undergraduate Admissions for all campuses.

Activate ASURITE UserID
After applying for admission, activate your ASURITE UserID as soon as you receive your UserID and activation code in the mail from Undergraduate Admissions or Admissions Services. www.asu.edu/asurite If you need help activating your ASURITE UserID, call 480.965.7788 for ASU’s Polytechnic and Tempe campuses or 602.543.8203 for ASU’s West campus. If you have already activated your ASURITE UserID and have problems, contact the IT Help Desk at or 480.965.6500.

Provide your e-mail address to ASU
While you are activating your ASURITE UserID, you will be asked if you have a non-ASU e-mail address and mailbox (such as Yahoo or Hotmail). If you do, enter it in the field provided.

Please note: Once you have been admitted to ASU, you are eligible to set up full ASU e-mail mailbox service (send, receive, read, and store e-mail on ASU servers). www.asu.edu/asurite

Verify MMR immunization
You must have verification of two MMR (measles [rubeola]/mumps/ rubella) immunizations on record at ASU, or you cannot register for classes. www.asu.edu/studentaffairs/health/nurseclinic/immunizations

Freshmen: complete this step before attending orientation. Transfers: complete this step before registering for classes.
