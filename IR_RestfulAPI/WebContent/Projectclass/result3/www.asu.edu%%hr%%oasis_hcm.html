Oasis HCM Update

PeopleSoft Talent Acquisition Manager (TAM):

PeopleSoft Talent Acquisition Manager (TAM) will go live on Monday, December 18, 2006 which will replace the legacy system that is currently heavily reliant on paper driven hiring processes. This new hiring process will only accept on-line applications for administrative, service professional and classified staff positions. In addition, correspondence with applicants can now be done through email, complimented by official letters.

In order to have access to the new system, training will be required. There will be three types of training available to employees:

• Demonstration training

• Online training

• Hands-on training

You may register to attend the demonstration training by clicking on the link you wish to attend at the OASIS training calendar (see link below). The on-line course will be available in ASU Blackboard - search for OASIS training. The first hands-on training class will be available on Wednesday, January 3, 2007.

These training sessions have been scheduled and can be viewed on the training calendar located at http://www.asu.edu/oasis/support/calendar_web.html.

Some of the highlighted benefits are: 1.) ability to review resumes immediately as applicants apply; 2.) only two approvals will be required instead of the current three; 3.) storage of resumes will be maintained in the system. This new hiring process will be more effective in our way of conducting business.

Jobs in the current process/legacy system will be closed on Friday, December 15th and departments will follow that process. From that point forward access to all legacy systems will be available as a reference tool beyond the December 15th, but all new positions will be created in the TAM system. PLEASE NOTE: DUE TO PEOPLESOFT MAINTENANCE, NO JOBS WILL BE POSTED ON DECEMBER 16th and 17th.

An appropriate message will be posted on the ASU HR job website announcing these dates and the new hiring process.

Please contact HR Staffing at 480-965-8844 for assistance in posting positions around the launch date (December 18th) or if you have any questions regarding this matter.
