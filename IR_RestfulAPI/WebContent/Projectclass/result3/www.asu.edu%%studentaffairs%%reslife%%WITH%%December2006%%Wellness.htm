Living Well at ASU

Arizona State University is dedicated to helping students lead healthy lives and reach their academic and personal goals by creating a healthy campus environment. Research has shown a direct link between students’ academic achievement and their state of health. At ASU we have developed programs and services that support students’ development and academic achievement, reduce the risk of individual illness or injury, enhance students’ capacity for adopting a healthy lifestyle, and engage students to become community leaders and health advocates.

We are committed to developing a healthy campus and helping students develop lifelong habits that improve their quality of life and will support their success in school, the workplace, and their community.

Campus Health Service , Campus Recreation, Counseling and Consultation , and Wellness and Health Promotion make up the Living Well Network and provide the following services:

Acupuncture
Alcohol/drug abuse education and treatment
Body image and eating disorder education and treatment
Depression and anxiety counseling
Fitness programs
Health and sports psychology
HIV education
Life planning
Medical care/immunizations/pharmacy services
Nutrition education and counseling
Physical therapy
Recreation and sports
Relationship counseling
Risk management programs
Sexual assault prevention
Sexual health resources
Spirituality and religious resources
Stress reduction programs and counseling
Tobacco education and cessation programs
Travel health and immunizations

We encourage you to look at our websites or stop in for more information about these services to help you be healthy and succeed in life!
