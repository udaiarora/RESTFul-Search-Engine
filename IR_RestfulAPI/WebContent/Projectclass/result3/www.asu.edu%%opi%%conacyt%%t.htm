January

  • Prepare all the graduate admission requirements in order to apply to ASU. Be aware that you will be required to complete the online general application, pay a $50 nonrefundable application fee, send official transcripts, send official test scores (TOEFL, GRE), have attained the equivalent of a U.S. bachelor?s degree from an international institution, send support materials (letter of recommendation, a statement of purpose, etc) and send proof of measles immunization. Please refer to FAQ section ?III ? Applying Students.?
  • Please refer to the following Web site for more information:
    http://www.asu.edu/graduate/admissions/domestic.html
  • Interested candidates must be admitted to ASU before submitting their application to CONACYT. Please begin by reviewing the requirements to apply to CONACYT.
  • CONACYT will receive applications for the first period beginning mid-January (please refer to CONACYT Web site for the exact date).

February

  • Check ASU general application deadline (please refer to the application requirements for more information). Please double-check dates with your department since they may vary between ASU colleges and departments.

March

  • The CONACYT application final deadline for the first period is in mid-March. Please refer to the CONACYT Web site for the exact date.

April

  • CONACYT will accept applications for the second period beginning early April. Please refer to CONACYT Web site for the exact date.
  • CONACYT acceptance notification (first period).

May

  • The CONACYT application final deadline for the second period is in mid-May. Please refer to CONACYT Web site for the exact date.

July

  • CONACYT acceptance notification (second period).

October/November

  • ASU/CONACYT Fellowship Program info session are held in Mexico for interested students. For more information contact the Office of Pan American Initiatives.

November

  • CONACYT?s annual call.
  • Contact your ASU department for more information on the ASU/CONACYT Fellowship program application process, requirements and deadline.

December

  • If CONACYT?s annual call occurs in December, apply to ASU per ASU?s deadline. After accepted to ASU, apply to CONACYT following CONACYT?s deadline requirements.
