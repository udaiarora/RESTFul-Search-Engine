Librarians' Handbook Revision Notices

Revised 1/15/2003

Jan. 15, 2003

Policy

Summary of Changes
E.2

Criteria 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on March 20, 2002. Change effective Jan. 15, 2003.

Under "Service," moved sentence "The academic professional should describe the nature and extent of activities ..." to another paragraph. Clarifies the definition of service activities by distinguishing between membership on Librarians' Council and service for Librarians' Council.
E.3

Requirements 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on March 20, 2002. Change effective Jan. 15, 2003.

Clarifies the section about the three criteria in the first paragraph. Modified and moved paragraphs 2-3 to new sections labeled "Job Performance" and "Professional Development and Contribution, and Service."
G.2.1

Probationary Evaluation 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on March 20, 2002. Change effective Jan. 15, 2003.

Expands the section about the letter which is to be written to the Chair of the Review Committee. Further defines the role of the Primary Evaluator in the review process.
G.2.2

Continuing Appointment/Promotion 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on March 20, 2002. Change effective Jan. 15, 2003.

Expands the section about the letter which is to be written to the Chair of the Review Committee. Further defines the role of the Primary Evaluator in the review process.

Summary of Changes

Criteria 		Revision

Makes the Parliamentarian and the Librarians' Council Page Master non-voting members of the Executive Committee.
B

Bylaws of Librarians' Council 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on October 18, 2000; Change effective May 16, 2001.

Clarifies duties of the Parliamentarian. Creates the position of Librarians' Council Page Master and defines the duties of that position.

Probationary Evaluation 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on Sept. 20, 2000; Change effective May 16, 2001.

Added new #4 to "Probationary Academic Professional" section requiring the AP to review and revise his/her job description and include a copy in the packet. Renumber #5 and #6 in this section.

Notification Process for Negative Recommendations 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on Sept. 20, 2000; Change effective May 16, 2001.

Section title changed from "Notification Process." Changed "Associate Dean" in first paragraph to "Assistant Dean for Personnel." Added "(where applicable)" after "Associate Dean."
H.2.1

Research Fund Policy and Procedures 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on Feb. 16, 2000; Change effective May 16, 2001.

Sixth paragraph under "Procedures" editted to allow expenditures other than those listed. "Personnel" changed to "student wages and contract personnel." "Typing" changed to "data input/capture."

September 13, 2000

Policy

Summary of Changes
D.2

Ranks 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on Feb. 16, 2000; Change effective Aug. 18, 2000.

Seperate section for Learning Resource Specialists was deleted. New sentences added to first paragraph to define three categories of academic professionals in the ASU Libraries -- Librarians, Archivists, and Learning Resource Specialists. The phrase "academic professionals" replaced "librarians" throughout the document to reflect all APs.
E.3

Requirements 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on Jan. 19, 2000; Change effective Aug. 18, 2000.

Under Retention of Probationary Academic Professionals, added "4. progress toward promotion to Associate, if currently at Assistant rank." Under Continuing Appointment added first sentence linking Continuing Appointment and Associate Rank. Under Continuing Appointment, added "3. fulfillment of the criteria for promotion to Associate, if currently at the Assistant rank; and".
G.2.2

Continuing Appointment 		Revision

Approved by Librarians' Council on Jan. 19, 2000; Change effective Aug. 18, 2000.

Deleted fourth paragraph with see reference to G2.3. Added new fifth paragraph -- "As described in E3, all continuing appointment applications for librarians at the assistant rank must be accompanied by an application for promotion to Associate Librarian." "Note" under Section B of The Academic Professional was amended to add the phrase "promotion to the next rank". See reference to G2.3 added to sections on the Primary Evaluator, Associate Dean, and Dean.
G.2.4

New Section: Letters of Reference 		Addition

Approved by Librarians' Council on Feb. 16, 2000; Change effective Aug. 18, 2000.

Adds new section to Handbook about Letters of References.
