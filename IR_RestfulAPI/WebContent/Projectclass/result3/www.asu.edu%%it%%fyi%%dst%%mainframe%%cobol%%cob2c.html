       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. TTIME.
       ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
       INPUT-OUTPUT SECTION.
       FILE-CONTROL.
       DATA DIVISION.
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01  DATE-RECORD.
           02 YR                                PIC IS 9(2).
           02 MN                                PIC IS 9(2).
           02 DY                                PIC IS 9(2).
       01  TIME-RECORD.
           02 HR                                PIC IS 9(2).
           02 MIN                               PIC IS 9(2).
           02 SEC                               PIC IS 9(2).
           02 HUNDRED                           PIC IS 9(2).
       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
       BEGIN-PARA.
           ACCEPT DATE-RECORD FROM DATE.
           DISPLAY 'THE MONTH IS ' MN UPON SYSOUT.
           DISPLAY 'THE DAY IS ' DY UPON SYSOUT.
           DISPLAY 'THE YEAR IS ' YR UPON SYSOUT.
           ACCEPT TIME-RECORD FROM TIME.
           DISPLAY 'THE TIME OF DAY IS ' HR ':' MIN UPON SYSOUT.
           STOP RUN.
