Creative, Healthy, Enjoyable, Fast: Easy Recipes for College Students

Pasta Salad

Needed: Pasta, vegetables you enjoy, can of sliced olives, salad dressing

Boil 8 ounces of any shaped pasta, drain and cool. Wash and cut into 1/2-1 inch chunks 2 cups of any combination of fresh vegetables , add to the pasta. Try broccoli, tomatoes, zucchini, green onions, bell peppers. Add a small can of sliced black olives (if u like). Add about 1/4 cup Italian or your favorite salad dressing .Refrigerate.

Pizza

Needed: Small Boboli, pita bread, flat bread, bagel or English muffin, Pizza sauce or pesto sauce, shredded mozzarella, diced vegetables(1/4 inch pieces).

Place your choice of "crust" on a paper plate or paper towel. Smear with a thin layer of pizza sauce. Top with shredded mozzarella and other ingredients desired. Microwave about 45-60 seconds or until cheese melts. Or bake in toaster oven 5-7 minutes at 350 degrees.

Soft Bean Tostado

Needed: Gordita or Fajita size flour tortillas, 16 oz. can of refried or whole pinto or black beans, grated cheese, salsa, chopped lettuce and tomatoes.

Heat beans on a covered plate/dish in the microwave for about 1 minute. Heat a stack of 2 tortillas for 20-30 seconds. Slap a scoop (1/4 cup) of beans on the center of each heated tortilla. Top with cheese, salsa, chopped lettuce and tomatoes. Fold and serve.

Taco Salad

Needed: Taco chips (baked or regular),1/2 bag of ready salad greens, shredded cheddar, 1/2 cup canned kidney beans, chopped tomato and other optional vegetables (cucumber, avocado, green peppers, etc.), salsa.

Layer ingredients as listed. Top with salsa.

Hot Pasta with Vegetables

Needed: Ready made spaghetti sauce, any shaped pasta, broccoli or zucchini and mozzarella cheese.

Boil 8 ounces of pasta noodles until tender but not mushy. While the pasta is cooking, wash and chop vegetables into 1/2 inch chunks. Add the vegetables to the pasta during the last minute of cooking (boiling) time. Drain the water. Add the sauce. Top with cheese.

Stir Fried Vegetables and Rice

Needed: Minute rice for quick cooking. Bag of frozen stir fry or fresh stir veggies, tamari or soy sauce.

Cook 2-4 servings of minute rice according to directions. Put about 1/2 inch of water in covered casserole dish. Add veggies. Cook until tender and and warm. Try about 2-4 minutes for fresh and 4-6 for frozen mixture. Drain and add sauce to taste. Easy since these sauces are full of sodium. Serve over rice.

Baked Potato

Needed: Baker sized potato, toppings.

Bake potato in microwave 4-6 minutes or in a toaster oven on 400 degrees for 30-40 minutes. Remove from oven, open and top with

1. Shredded cheddar, canned kidney beans and salsa.

2.Chopped steamed broccoli and shredded cheddar.

3.Sphagetti sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.

Note: Meat eaters can add chopped or sliced deli meats to any of the above recipes or can cook chopped chicken breast in the toaster oven or microwave and add to the recipe if desired.
