Summer Financial Aid

ASU can help with summer educational expenses.

Summer financial aid consideration is available to students who:

  • are enrolled in the summer term
  • meet eligibility criteria
  • and have a 2005-06 FAFSA on file.

Submit the Summer Financial Aid Application no later than August 5, 2006. Please contact the SFAO if you experience technical difficulties with the application.

Summer Funding Sources

  • Federal Pell Grant
  • University Grant
  • ASU Grant
  • Federal Perkins Loan
  • Federal Stafford Student Loans
  • Federal PLUS Loan
  • Federal Work-Study
  • Alternative Student Loans

Award Limits

If you received the maximum annual or aggregate award amount during the fall or spring semesters, you are ineligible for additional summer aid from that program.

  • Federal Pell Grant Limits
  • Federal Perkins Loan Limits
    • Undergraduate
    • Graduate
  • Federal Stafford Student Loan Limits

Federal Work-Study (FWS)

If you want to participate in the summer FWS program, please read the Summer FWS Information.

Study Abroad Students

Summer study abroad students must complete the steps noted on the Summer Sessions Study Abroad Programs Web site for financial aid consideration.

Tribal Students

To receive summer tribal funds, you must:

  • Be registered with your tribe
  • Submit a summer Needs Analysis Form to the Student Financial Assistance office
  • Complete the Summer Financial Aid Application
