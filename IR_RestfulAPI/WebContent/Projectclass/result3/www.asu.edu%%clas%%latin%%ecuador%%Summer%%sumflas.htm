ECUADOR: The Amazon and the Andes Field School

Foreign Language and Area Studies Fellowships
for the study of Quichua, Summer 2006
APPLICATION FORM
Arizona State University
Application Deadline: March 13th, 2006

Our Summer FLAS Fellowships are open to graduate students from any university interested in attending the ASU Andes and Amazon Quichua Summer Field School in Ecuador. Applicants must be citizens or permanent residents of the United States. Applicants must be accepted for enrollment or enrolled in a program that combines "modern" foreign language training with international / area studies or with the international aspects of professional or other fields of study. Preference is given to students who envision thesis research that would require or be strengthened by knowledge of the Quichua language. All things being equal, priority will be given to ASU students and to students from other FLAS granting institutions willing to make joint awards. The FLAS Fellowship provides tuition, program fees, and a stipend of $2,500.00 of which ASU retains $2,358.00 for room and board. The remainder amount of $142.00 is sent to the student. For a breakdown of the summer costs, please check out the summer costs page on the website.
