PTS 101: Decal Sales and Vehicle Registration

Purpose

To describe the vehicle registration and decal sales process at ASU

Sources

Arizona Revised Statutes § 15–1627
Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 5-102
ASU Parking and Vehicle Control Regulations

Applicability

Anyone operating or parking a vehicle at ASU at Tempe campus

Policy

Annual registration with the Decal Sales Office is required for all vehicles parked on university property, excluding those parked in areas designated for visitors.

Vehicle owners must register their vehicles at the Decal Sales Office and purchase the proper parking decal (and the gate-access keycard included with the purchase price) in order to park in designated areas on campus.

Decal holders who desire to park in the same lot the following year must renew their decals during the renewal period. Failure to renew during the renewal period may result in loss of parking privileges for that particular lot.

Faculty, staff, and students may purchase a parking decal for their vehicles in person or online. Decal renewals are also available using online services. Residence hall students cannot purchase or renew decals using online services.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Vehicle owners
  1. Register vehicles and purchase decals (with associated gate-access keycard) at the Decal Sales Office, Parking and Transit Services or online.

    Note:The office is located on Fifth Street between Forest and College avenues.

  2. Furnish ASU ID card (or driver’s license), completed decal application, and vehicle license plate number(s); pay by cash, check, or authorized credit card, or by payroll deduction for qualified faculty and staff.
Decal Sales Office
  1. Verify the eligibility and identity of applicants before accepting payment.

  2. Input the data into the parking system.

  3. Issue a receipt and the ASU Parking and Vehicle Control Regulations map with each decal and gate-access keycard.
Vehicle owners
  1. Display the proper decal in registered vehicle(s) in accordance with the ASU Parking and Vehicle Control Regulations.

  2. Ensure that each motor vehicle registered to the parking decal remains in compliance with State of Arizona emissions standards during the entire registration period.
endtable
