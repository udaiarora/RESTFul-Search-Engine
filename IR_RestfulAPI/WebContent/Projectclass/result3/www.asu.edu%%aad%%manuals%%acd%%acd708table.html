The faculty member or academic professional submits the military leave request to the chair, director, or appropriate supervisor.

The chair, director, or appropriate supervisor approves the leave and submits the Personnel Action form (PAF) to Human Resources (HR).

  1. If the leave does not exceed 30 calendar days in any two consecutive calendar years, the chair, director, or appropriate supervisor indicates a leave with pay on the PAF.

  2. If the leave exceeds 30 days in any two consecutive calendar years and vacation leave is not used, has been exhausted, or is not available, the chair, director, or appropriate supervisor indicates a leave without pay and its duration on the PAF.

  3. If the faculty member or academic professional enters into active service, the chair, director, or appropriate supervisor indicates an extended leave without pay on the PAF.

The chair, director, or appropriate supervisor maintains the appropriate departmental records indicating the type and duration of the leave.

The faculty member or academic professional contacts HR to arrange continuation of benefits, if desired.

HR adjusts all appropriate records.
