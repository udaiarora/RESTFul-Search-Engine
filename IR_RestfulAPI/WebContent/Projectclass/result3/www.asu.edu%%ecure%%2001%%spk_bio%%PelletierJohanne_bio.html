Johanne Pelletier
University Archivist and Director of Archives and Records Management
McGill University
Record Keeping and the Web

Bio

Johanne Pelletier has been University Archivist and Director of Archives and Records Management programming for McGill University (Montreal) since 1997. Prior to this position Ms. Pelletier was Archives Advisor for the Province of Ontario, providing archives and records management advisory services to over 500 public and private sector organizations. A graduate of McGill University (BA) and the University of Toronto (MA), Ms. Pelletier has been Chair of the Association of Canadian Archivists Program Committee (1997), Deputy-Secretary General of the Bureau of Canadian Archivists (1996 – 1997) and is currently Chair of the Society of American Archivists Education Committee. Ms. Pelletier is Chair of the McGill University Senate Standing Committee on Administrative Computing, and leads information seminars for McGill University employees on information management and privacy issues.
