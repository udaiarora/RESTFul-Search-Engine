ASU Orientation

Admitted degree-seeking undergraduate students are expected to attend ASU Orientation - an important step in making the transition to life as an ASU Sun Devil.  ASU Orientation will include academic advising, registering for classes, campus tours, and information about how you can stay on track for graduation.

Colleges and Schools may also send information to their admitted students encouraging them to make their reservation for ASU Orientation and providing additional information to help new students make the transition to ASU.

Learn more about ASU Orientation at your ASU campus or a location near you!

 

 

Note: Please be aware that some information contained within this Web site is subject to change or may need to be updated. For the most up-to-date information, please return to this page before attending your ASU Orientation program for the latest details, or contact New Student Orientation with any questions you may have.
