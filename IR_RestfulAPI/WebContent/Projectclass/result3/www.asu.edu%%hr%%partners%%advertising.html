Other important facts about advertising:

  • Recruitment & Staffing Services is available to assist in researching other advertising resources.

 

  • Types of ads: short, long and group ads are available. 
  • Recruitment & Staffing Services will develop the ad, send you proofs and the cost associated wit the ad.
  • Depending on the publication, some may require prepayment and others will send out invoices to the department/college.
  • The list of publications is continually updated.  If you would like to add a new resource, please contact Recruitment & Staffing Services.
  • The most popular advertising resources (print & online) are:
    • AZ Republic (Print)
    • Careerbuilder.com
    • Chronicle.com
    • Craigslist.com
    • Employment Guide (Print + Online)
    • HigherEdJobs.com
    • Jobing.com
