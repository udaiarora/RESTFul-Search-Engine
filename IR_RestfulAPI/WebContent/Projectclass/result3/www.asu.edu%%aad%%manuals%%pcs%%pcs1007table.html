PCS 1007 Table

The department:

  1. completes a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form, checking the Disposal/Salvage box in the “Activity” section

    Note: PRA forms are available in Property Control.

  2. records the ASU property control number (ASU PC#) on the PRA

  3. types or prints “Return of Loaned Equipment” in the “Reason/Comments” section of the PRA

  4. removes the ASU property control tag(s) from equipment, if possible, and affixes it to the PRA before returning the loaned equipment

    and

  5. forwards the signed PRA to Property Control for verification signatures.

Property Control removes the equipment from the property control system.
