Resetting Passwords

There are several passwords in the various ASU systems. Use the following list to determine which password you need help with.

  • ASURITE/Kerberos - Used for logging into IT computing sites and classrooms, the unix servers (general.asu.edu, stats.asu.edu, research.asu.edu, medusa.asu.edu, etc), myASU, ASU Interactive, ASURITE Activation and Service Management, Affiliate Web, making a PPP dial-up connection, or logging in at the Checkpoint VPN/SecuRemote window. There are several options for resetting this password, so please see our ASURITE/Kerberos password reset page for details.

  • ASURITE Windows Login - Used for logging into accounts on the Exchange servers for Outlook e-mail. Also used for logging into an office computer on the NT network. There are several options for resetting this password, so please see our Windows Login (Exchange/Outlook) password reset page for details.

  • DCE Authentication - Used for logging into the HR Web Applications webpages and the Graduate College's AUI. There are several options for resetting this password, so please see our DCE Authentication password reset page for details.

  • Administrative RACF/CICS - Used to login at the SOLVE screen and ASU screen when accessing SIS, DARS, HRMS, Advantage, Administrative MVS TSO, and several other systems on the server adm.asu.edu. Password resets are performed by the IT Help Desk. They can be requested via phone, email, or in person with valid photo identification. Requests made by phone or email will be fulfilled by an email sent to the user's Exchange mailbox or other ASU email address. Please see our Mainframe Login page for assistance with logging in or changing this password.

  • Academic RACF - Used for logging into Academic MVS TSO. Password resets are performed by the IT Help Desk. They can be requested via phone, email, or in person with valid photo identification. Requests made by phone or email will be fulfilled by an email sent to the user's Exchange mailbox or other ASU email address.

  • Advantage - 4-digit UserID and password used to login to the Advantage system. This login comes after the Administrative RACF/CICS login. Password resets are performed by the Advantage Helpline. They can be reached at 480-965-2334.

  • HRMS OperatorID and password - Used as an internal login for the HRMS system. This login comes after the Administrative RACF/CICS login. Password resets are performed by the HRMS Helpline. They can be reached at 480-965-0467.

  • Online Application for Admission - Used only to submit an online application for admission as a student to ASU. There is no way to lookup the UserID or reset the password. If either are forgotten or don't work, the user must start over from the beginning with a new login.

  • Novell - Used to login to an office computer on a Novell network. Password resets are performed by the user's local network technical support personnel.

  • CFA Online - Used for logging into the CFA Online system to take online tests. For password assistance, call 480-965-5886 or email asynch@asu.edu.

  • DataWarehouse - Used for logging into the DataWarehouse system on enterprise1.asu.edu. Password resets are performed by Data Administration. They can be reached at 480-965-2318.

  • ARS - Used for logging into the ARS system to submit problem reports. Password problems should be reported to ars-q@asu.edu.

  • Affiliate - Used for logging into the Affiliate Database. Password problems should be reported to affl-q@asu.edu.

  • WeBWorK - Used for logging into the WeBWorK webpage at webwork.asu.edu. This login is not controlled by the IT Help Desk. Please see documentation on the WeBWorK webpage for assistance. If you have problems logging into your class, contact your instructor to make sure you have access.

  • Registrar's PIN - Used to login to the SunDial automated phone system at 480-350-1500. Also used by former students who do not have active ASURITE UserIDs to login to ASU Interactive. PIN resets are performed by the Office of the Registrar. They can be reached at 480-965-3124 or registrar@asu.edu.

  • Library PIN - Used to login to the ASU Library system. For assistance setting up a PIN, contact the Hayden Library Reference Desk at 480-965-6164. PIN reset requests should be directed to the Hayden Library Circulation Desk at 480-965-3605.

  • Other - There are several other types of passwords that may be used for various reasons. Departmental computing labs often have their own login procedures. Professors sometimes setup class webpages on their personal space and create their own logins for the students of their classes. Departments may have their own applications that require users to login.
