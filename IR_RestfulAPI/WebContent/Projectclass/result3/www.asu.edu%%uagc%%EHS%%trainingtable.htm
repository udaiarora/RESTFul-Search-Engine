Environmental Health & Safety Training Table

Training for Academics, Research, General Campus, Service Units and Operations Employees

Job Functions That Require Training
Training
Job Function
Frequency
Asbestos Awareness Employees whose duties may expose them to asbestos or asbestos-containing materials at or above the Permissible Exposure Limit. Initially upon hire and then a refresher annually thereafter.
Biosafety Employees who perform any laboratory operations within academic, clinical, or research laboratories involving biological select agents or toxins. Initially upon hire and then a refresher annually thereafter.
Bloodborne Pathogens Employees who may be reasonably anticipated to come in contact with blood or other potentially infectious materials during the performance of their primary duties. Initially upon hire and then a refresher annually thereafter.
Coaching the SUV Driver, Defensive Driver or Off-Road Driving Individuals who operate high-occupancy vehicles on ASU business are required to attend any one of the three driver training courses. Prior to operating a high-occupancy vehicle.
Confined Space Individuals who may enter and conduct operations in confined spaces such as sewers, storage tanks and utility tunnels. Retraining required with changes in assignment, equipment, or procedures occur.
Electrical Safety Individuals that provide electrical distribution, installation of electrical distribution and utilize electrical equipment. Initially upon hire and whenever changes in assignment, equipment or procedures occur.
Fire Safety and Prevention Employees that work in areas where portable fire extinguishers are available for emergency use and are anticipated to fight an incipient fire Initially upon hire and then a refresher annually thereafter.
Hazard Communication University employees who may be occupationally exposed to hazardous substances. Initially upon hire and whenever a new physical or health hazard is introduced into their work area
Hazardous Waste Management Employees who generate any chemical/biological/radiological waste from research or laboratory operations. Initially upon hire, prior to working in a laboratory environment, and before preparing hazwaste for pick-up
Hazardous Materials in Transportation - Certification Employees preparing hazardous materials or dangerous goods for outbound shipment from ASU. Initially, then every two years for DOT and every three years for IATA. Training NOT provided by ASU.
Laboratory Safety Laboratory employees who perform any laboratory operations within academic, clinical, or research laboratories and who may receive or be exposed to hazardous chemicals while performing their duties. Initially upon hire and then a refresher annually thereafter
Laser Safety Individuals working at or visiting ASU that procure or use Class IIIb or IV lasers. Prior to working in a laboratory environment
Lock-out/Tag-out Employees who perform electrical shut-down procedures. Retraining required with changes in assignment, equipment, or procedures occur.
Machine Guarding Employees who operate powered construction machinery as part of their job. Retraining required with changes in assignment, equipment, or procedures occur.
Personnel Training and Spill Prevention Procedures - SPCC For fuel handling personnel to prevent oil discharges during fueling procedures. Annually for fuel handlers. Quarterly briefings are conducted for selected committee members
Powered Industrial Trucks Safety Employees who operate powered industrial trucks. Initially, then at least once every three years.
Radiation Safety Training Employees that want to procure, or need access to analytical x-ray equipment, accelerators, and other ionizing radiation producing equipment. Prior to working in a laboratory environment
Radioactive Material Training Employees working at or visiting ASU that procure, use, or dispose of radioactivity. Prior to working in a lab and then a refresher annually thereafter
Receiving Hazardous Materials/Dangerous Goods for Non-Lab Personnel Non-Lab employees who may handle and/or have contact with hazardous materials or dangerous goods (HM/DG) that are being received in their department. Initially upon hire within 90 days, then at least once every three years
Respiratory Training Employees that may be required to enter environments where airborne contaminants may be present, exposing them possibly to respiratory hazards during routine operations or possibly in unique situations during the course of duty. Initially upon hire and then a refresher annually thereafter.
Safety and Security for International Travelers Employees who travel internationally on University business. Prior to international travel.
Recommended Training
Back Safety Employees whose duties may place them at risk of back injury. As need determines.
Coaching the SUV Driver Employees operating an SUV while on ASU business. As need determines.
Defensive Driving Employees who may need to drive a State owned vehicle As need determines.
Off-Road Driving – A Coaching Program Employees who operate vehicles in off-road conditions on ASU business. As need determines.
Office Ergonomics Employees who may be at risk of injury due to repetitive work conditions As need determines.
