Recruitment Airfare Awards
for the 2007–2008 class

DEADLINE

Friday, November 17, 2006
Submit proposal to the office of Dr. Andrew N. Webber, Associate Dean, Division of Graduate Studies. Late proposals are unlikely to receive support.
PURPOSE
These awards provide $400 (maximum) airfare to assist in bringing top applicants to campus for recruitment visits. Academic units are responsible for providing lodging, meals, and local transportation (shuttle, taxi). Students will be reimbursed upon receipt of their original electronic ticket or ticket stub.

CONTACT
Pat Saldivar (psaldivar@asu.edu), Division of Graduate Studies, 965-5906

ELIGIBILITY
All units may apply in advance for funding. Only one proposal may be submitted per academic unit. Funding may be used:
  • To bring doctoral/MFA students to campus for a recruitment visit;

  • To recruit outstanding master's degree students the unit seeks to later admit to a doctoral program; and

  • To recruit excellent students who are underrepresented in their fields (master's or doctoral).
APPLICATION PROCESS
Academic units should submit a memorandum electronically to Andrew.Webber@asu.edu describing:
  • The number of Recruitment Airfare Awards or dollar amount you are requesting.

  • Your recruitment plan specifying how you will identify and proactively recruit top students.

  • Your plans for the recruitment visits.

  • How this award will help strengthen the excellence and diversity of your graduate student population.
Proposals will be evaluated consistent with the above criteria. In addition, for those units that have previously received Recruitment Airfare Awards, we will consider the number of airfare awards requested and used by your unit in the past three years and your success rate in enrolling those students.

Units will be notified of the results of this competition by Friday, December 15, 2006.
