Major Information Sheets

Standardized Major Information Sheets (SMISs) for ASU at the Downtown, Polytechnic, Tempe, and West campuses describe the requirements for majors and suggested programs of study for undergraduate degrees. SMISs are listed alphabetically by administrating unit, area of study, and degree.

SMISs are available in PDF format. You’ll want the free Adobe® Acrobat Reader to view the PDF versions. If you experience any problems viewing the PDF format, please contact the Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost. Any problems with content must be directed to the appropriate department.
