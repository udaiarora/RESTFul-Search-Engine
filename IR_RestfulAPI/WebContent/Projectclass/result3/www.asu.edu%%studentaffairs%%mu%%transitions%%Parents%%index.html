Welcome Parents and Family

This is an exciting time for you and your student. During their time at ASU they will encounter a variety of experiences that will benefit them academically, socially, and emotionally. As an ASU Sun Devil, your student will become a part of a long lasting tradition of academic excellence, diversity, community building, and involvement and personal growth.

We understand that as parents and families of ASU students you may have questions and occasional concerns. This webpage was developed to address your needs. It includes resources, contacts and answers to frequently asked questions for various campus departments.

Again we would like to welcome you and look forward to an exciting and successful year!
