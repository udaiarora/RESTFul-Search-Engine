If you are a non-degree seeking student who is admissible to ASU and may apply to a degree program in the future:

You may register for 1 - 8 semester hours each fall or spring semester, 7 semester hours for each 5-week summer session, 9 semester hours for the 8-week summer session, and 3 semester hours for winter intersession.

You may apply 15 hours taken in non-degree status to a degree program if you choose to pursue an undergraduate degree in the future.

You are not eligible for financial aid.

You may be eligible for academic advisement.

Complete the Non-Degree Checklist.

You should review Steps to Attending ASU as a degree seeking student, if you plan to apply to a degree program in the future.
