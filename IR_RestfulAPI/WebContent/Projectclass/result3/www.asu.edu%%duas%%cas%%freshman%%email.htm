ASU E-mail
 
If you haven't done so already, you will need to establish your ASU E-mail Account.  
  • Activate your ASURITE UserID; you will then be assigned an ASU e-mail account.  
  • Access your e-mail account via Emma.  Login with your ASURITE UserID and password; then select "IMAP E-mail" from the menu. To find out what your e-mail address is select "EPO Update".
  • If you would prefer to keep your existing account (e.g. hotmail, AOL), you may forward your ASU e-mail to your preferred account.  Login to Emma then select "IMAP Forwarding".
E-mail is the primary mode of communication at ASU, so check your ASU e-mail often for university announcements and class assignments.
Using your lucky pencil, write down your ASU e-mail address.
