Quino E. Martinez Scholarship Application

 

Description:

The Department of Languages & Literatures annually selects a student to receive this scholarship established in honor of Professor Quino E. Martinez.  Professor Martinez taught Spanish and Portuguese at ASU for 31 years, from 1957-1988.  He also was one of the ?original? television instructors on KAET where he hosted a Spanish language course.  The purpose of the scholarship is to support an outstanding student of Spanish and/or Portuguese who meets the criteria outlined below.  One scholarship of $1300 will be awarded; it will be disbursed over two semesters.

Criteria:

1.      Must be a full-time undergraduate student with a junior or senior class standing (having earned 56 or more credit hours) by time of enrollment in Fall 2007 semester.

2.      Must have a declared major in Spanish and/or course work in Portuguese. 

3.      Minimum overall cumulative grade point average required is 3.00.

4.      Must register for full-time status to continue the study of Spanish and/or Portuguese at ASU Tempe campus, Fall 2007 & Spring 2008.

 

Selection Process: It is the responsibility of the Department of Languages & Literatures to select the scholarship recipient. 

 

·         Students must submit:

1.      the scholarship application,

2.      transcripts and/or DARS report for the major, and

3.      a one-page essay describing: 

a)      involvement in community service,

b)      student involvement and activities,

c)      educational and career goals,

d)      specific area of interest in languages, and

e)      personal background.

·         The application and brief statement must be typed.

·         Complete the application and submit with supporting documentation to:
