Taking Quizzes

To take a quiz for one of the SunTutor modules, you must first enroll in the SunTutor course on my.asu (instructions below).

Tutorial quizzes are only available to ASU students, faculty, or staff.

Self-enrolling for SunTutor on my.asu

  1. Go to http://my.asu.edu.
  2. Enter your ASURITE ID and password and click on "Login". ASURITE IDs are the IDs that you use to access your university e-mail account or to use a computer in the Computing Commons.
  3. Click on the "Courses" tab in the upper left-hand corner
  4. Use the "Course Search" box and search for "suntutor"
  5. Your search results should show one course - Library: SunTutor Research Tutorial (LIB-SunTutor-001)
  6. Click on the "enroll" button to the right of the course name link on the self-enrollment page, click on the submit button
  7. Now, when you go to my.asu.edu, the course will show up when you click on the "courses" tab

Taking the quizzes

Quizzes for the SunTutor modules can be accessed only through my.asu.

  1. To take a quiz, click on the "Assignments" button
  2. Find the appropriate quiz that matches the module and click on the link
  3. Take the quiz following the instruction
