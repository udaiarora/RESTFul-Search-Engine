LSA Board and Committee Responsibilities

Board Positions

(Elected positions; serves for one year unless otherwise stated; attends monthly meetings)

President
Serves three-year term*: as President-elect, President, and Past-president
  • Presides at all meetings and exercises general supervision over the affairs and activities of the Association.
  • *In the following year, as immediate past president, he/she is available for consultation and conflict resolution, but is not required to serve on the Executive Board, but could serve on a voluntary basis.
  • Helps plan, organize, and set up events, including (but not limited to) shopping, decorating, preparing food, serving, cleaning up.
President Elect
Serves three-year term*: as President-elect, President, and Past-president
  • Assumes the duties of the President when the President is not available.
  • Helps plan, organize, and set up events, including (but not limited to) shopping, decorating, preparing food, serving, cleaning up.
  • Becomes President the following year, and then Past-President the year after.
    *See notation regarding past president under President above.
Secretary
  • Keeps the minutes of Board and General meetings and handles the official correspondence of the Association.
  • Helps plan, organize, and set up events, including (but not limited to) shopping, decorating, preparing food, serving, cleaning up.
Treasurer
Receives and disburses all Association funds, and maintains cashbox. Helps plan, organize, and set up events, including (but not limited to) shopping, decorating, preparing food, serving, cleaning up.

Committees

(Non-elected positions; serves one year term.)

Membership Committee
The Membership Committee is responsible for encouraging membership in the Association each year during the membership drive, and throughout the year. The membership chair is also responsible for maintaining a current list of active members.
Social Committee
The Social Committee handles refreshments and decorations for the various LSA events. The Social Committee plans and manages pot-luck meals, the Holiday Dinner, Spring Tea, and refreshments at the general meetings.
Courtesy Committee
The Courtesy Committee handles correspondence on behalf of the LSA on appropriate occasions. This committee sees that courtesies are sent to members upon appropriate occasions as outlined in the Courtesy Committee guidelines, and is responsible for sending out Spring Tea invitations to retired past members of the LSA.
Staff Lounge Committee
The Staff Lounge Committee is responsible for improving the general aesthetics of the lounges, periodically cleaning the appliances, replacing magazines, caring for the pictures, etc.
Plant Committee
The Plant Committee takes care of the plants in the lounges and smoking areas.
Program Committee
The Program Committee is chaired by the President-elect of the Staff Association. This committee organizes the programs to be held during those months in which there is no regularly scheduled event (potlucks, Tea, Holiday Dinner etc.).
Historian/Photo Albums
The Historian(s) attend all LSA functions and special events involving LSA members to take pictures. Historians also organize the photos into annual albums and set up the albums at the Spring Tea (and other events as requested). The LSA supplies the camera and reimburses expenses for film, processing and other album supplies.
