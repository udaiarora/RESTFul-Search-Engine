 Barrett Honors College

Admission Information and Application
For undergraduate students who want to study in the Barrett Honors College. The BHC application is completed in addition to the undergraduate application for admission to ASU. The BHC application deadlines are November 30, 2005, for the Spring 2006 semester and May 31, 2006, for the Fall 2006 semester. Regardless of these deadlines, prospective students are strongly encouraged to apply at their earliest convenience. Space is limited.
