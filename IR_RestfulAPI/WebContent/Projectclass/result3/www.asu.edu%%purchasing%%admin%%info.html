Purchasing & Business Services Departmental Philosophy


Our Mission Statement

Purchasing and Business Services serves University students, faculty, and staff by providing dependable, cost effective, timely, up-to-date, innovative, quality service with a commitment to honesty, courtesy, professionalism, and high ethical standards.


Who We Are

The Purchasing Department at Arizona State University was formed in 1946. In the 54 years since the forming of a centralized purchasing function at Arizona State University, the Purchasing Department has undergone significant changes in the areas of scope of responsibility, professionalism, work methods, system automation, and customer service.

Today, the department known as Purchasing and Business Services encompasses these service departments:

  • ASU Stores
  • Campus Card Services
  • Document Production Services
  • Furnishing Services
  • Lab Stores
  • Mail Services
  • Maintenance Stores
  • Procurement
  • Property Control
  • Surplus Property and Recycling
  • Real Estate Acquisition and Management
  • University Bookstore


    • What Our Values Are

    The Purchasing and Business Services Department at Arizona State University prides itself on the ability to meet the needs of our University community. Our motto is: "Yes, we can do that. Working together, we'll find a way." How do we work together? Our commitment to honesty, dependability, creativity and adherence to the ethical standards of our profession (s) defines our interaction with our colleagues, co-workers and customers. We embrace opportunities to change and improve in the interest of progress. We will strive to approach our work and our relationships with a positive attitude and a spirit of courtesy and cooperation.
