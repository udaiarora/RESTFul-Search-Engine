Departments that plan to pay foreign visitors (non-resident aliens) to perform services should contact the Tax Services Unit in the Financial Services office before any services are rendered. The United States Tax Code has separate provisions regarding these payments.

The person providing services must provide ASU with information and certifications so the university can comply with the withholding and reporting requirements associated with these tax laws. Depending on the visa status and the provisions, if any, of a tax treaty between the U.S. and the home country of the foreign visitor, certain documents must be completed. Failure to obtain the required information and certifications will result in lengthy payment delays and in, some cases, no payment at all.

Other offices to contact before contracting with a non-resident alien Include:

  • International Student Office
  • the International Programs Office and
  • the Human Resources Partners Group.
