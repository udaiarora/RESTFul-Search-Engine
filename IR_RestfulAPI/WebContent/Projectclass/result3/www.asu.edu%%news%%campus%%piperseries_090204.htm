Famous writers to visit, lecture at ASU

How does Alexander McCall Smith create memorable and lovable characters such as Mma. Precious Ramotswe, Botswana's intrepid female private detective and owner of the No. 1 Ladies Detective Agency? And what's E.L. Doctorow's secret for putting a spin on history and weaving tantalizing tales?

Smith and Doctorow will be among a fascinating lineup of authors at the Arizona State University Virginia G. Piper Center for Creative Writing's 2004-05 Distinguished Visiting Writers Series. One of the events to culminate the series is "Desert Nights, Rising Stars," the Piper Center's nationally recognized Writers Conference.

The series opens Oct. 8 (Friday) with a lecture by Gail Tsukiyama, author of such books as Dreamy Water, Night of Many Dreams and The Language of Threads, at 7:30 p.m. at the Scottsdale Center for the Arts, Stage 2, 7380 E. 2 nd St., Scottsdale. Tickets are $10.

Other speakers are:
. Former American Poet Laureate Robert Pinsky (1997-2000), author of such books as Democracy, Culture, and the Voice of Poetry , Saturday, Oct. 16, 7:30 p.m., Armstrong Hall (College of Law), ASU Tempe Campus. This event is free.

. Charles Baxter, author of Saul & Patsy, The Feast of Love and Burning Down the House: Essays on Fiction , 7:30 p.m., Thursday, Oct. 28, Desert Botanical Garden, 1201 N. Galvin Parkway, Phoenix.

. Edward Albee, Wednesday, Nov. 17, 5:30-7:30 p.m., Paul V. Galvin Playhouse, ASU Tempe Campus. The playwright will present a colloquium titled "Who's Afraid of Virginia Woolf? And The Goat or Who is Sylvia?"

. Alexander McCall Smith, author of the No. 1 Ladies' Detective Agency   series, and Sunday Philosophy Club: An Isabel Dalhousie Mystery , Friday, Nov. 19, 7:30 p.m. Old Main's Carson Ballroom, ASU Tempe Campus. Free.

. Stephen Cannell and Jana Bommersbach, Tuesday, Jan. 25, 7:30 p.m., Scottsdale Center for the arts, Piper Theatre. Tickets: $20.

. Piper Writers' House Grand Opening, all day, at the Piper House, with writers E.L. Doctorow and Amy Tan.

. E.L. Doctorow, author of Sweet Land Stories, Ragtime and City of God , Thursday, Feb. 10, 7:30 p.m., Scottsdale Center for the Arts, Piper Theatre. Tickets: $20.

. Poet Dave Smith, Tuesday, March 8, noon, Piper Writers' House, ASU Tempe Campus.

. Beckian Fritz Goldberg and Chase Twichell, Thursday, April 28, 7:30 p.m., Scottsdale Center for the Arts, Stage 2. Tickets: $10.

The ASU Writers Conference will be held March 9-12 on the ASU Tempe Campus. Speakers will include Denise Chavez, A.S. Byatt, Sarah Vowell and many other writers. Registration deadline is Feb. 23.

For more information about the lectures and conference, go to www.asu.edu/clas/pipercwcenter.
