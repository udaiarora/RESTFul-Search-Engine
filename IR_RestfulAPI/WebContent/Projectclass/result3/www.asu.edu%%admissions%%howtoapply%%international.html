Undergraduate Admission Application Timeline for International Students

To help you reach your education goals, ASU has established priority application dates. Following the timeline puts you on the best track for receiving information and completing the steps in your enrollment.

International: An international student is a student who is not a citizen of, or a permanent resident of, or a refugee in the United States of America (F & J Visas).

International Fall Spring Summer
Priority application date that ASU should have your completed application on file

May 1 (extended to June 1 for fall 2006 semester)

Approximately 3.5 months before classes begin

October 1

Approximately 3.5 months before classes begin		 International students must apply to begin in either fall or spring semester.*

*International students who have been admitted for the fall semester, may begin attending classes as early as summer session.


What should I do if I miss the application deadline?
Applications may be submitted after the priority application deadline, but ASU does not guarantee that they will be processed in time for admission. If you miss the priority application date, ASU encourages you to submit your application for the next semester.

Visit www.asu.edu/admissions/international for details about applying to ASU as an international student.
