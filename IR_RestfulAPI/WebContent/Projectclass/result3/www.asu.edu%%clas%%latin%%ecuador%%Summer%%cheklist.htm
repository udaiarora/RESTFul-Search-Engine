CANCELLATION POLICY

All students withdrawing from the program must provide a written statement to the Program Director, Dr. Tod Swanson.

  1. $275.00 of the $350.00 Program deposit is non-refundable.
  2. Refunds, on payments made plus the refundable portion of the deposit (if any), will be issued until April 4th, 2007.
  3. Students whose written withdrawal statement is received after April 4th, 2007 but prior to 30 days before the beginning of the program may be granted a partial refund.
  4. No refund will be granted for students whose written withdrawal statement is received within 30 days of the beginning of their program except in cases of medical or family emergencies.
