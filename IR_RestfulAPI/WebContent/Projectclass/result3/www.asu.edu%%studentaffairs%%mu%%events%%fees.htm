Rates & Fees

All registered student organizations and Arizona State University departments are exempt from meeting room charges in the Memorial Union. The exception to this guideline is when there is a registration fee, conference fee, admission charge, or donation requested at the door.

The following categories indicate the fee structure when applicable:

Category I
Registered student organization event when a fee, admission charge or donation is part of the event.
Category II
University related event with University participation when a fee, admission charge or donation is part of the event.
Category III
Non-university related event. Room/equipment charges apply to the category under all conditions.

A room rental fee will be waived when a complete meal has been purchased through Distinctive Catering for a specific event. This waiver only applies to groups utilizing a series of conference rooms in the Memorial Union or has requested a separate room for their meals to be served (if available). This waiver does not apply for one-time events (weddings, dances, etc.) or monthly meetings. Refreshment breaks are not considered a complete meal and do not therefore qualify for a room fee waiver. Entire conference rates are not waived for all rooms because there is a meal served. The waiver applies to only that room and that event during the conference when a meal is purchased.

Fees That Apply to all Categories

  • Cancellation of reservations within two working days of the event: $10
    If an on-campus group cancels the Arizona or Ventana Ballroom less than two weeks prior, then that client will be assessed a fee of 50% of what their rental would be for that ballroom.
  • Request for room within two working days of the event: $10
  • No show (client fails to call or use the room reserved): $10
    When there are a number of rooms as part of a reservation, no show fees will be negotiated.
  • Request for an alteration to a reservation containing 5 or more events that has been confirmed: $25

Conference Related Services and Fees

  • Vendor table fee in hallway: $10/per table
  • Delivery/storage of boxes: $4/per box
  • Excessive trash removal: $20 - $60
  • Piano tuning out of regular tuning schedule: $60
  • Phone installation: $75
  • Extended building hour past regular building schedule: $75

Late Equipment Requests (within 2 working days prior to event)

  • Level A: The request does not require set-up or technical assistance: no charge
    Examples: easels, sign stanchions.
  • Level B: The equipment requires set-up and assistance from staff: $15 per request
    Examples: overhead projector, VCR/monitor, data projector.
  • Level C: The equipment request involves three pieces or more. Set-up and assistance from staff is required: $20 - $50 depending upon number of items requested.
    Examples: Equipment may need to be retrieved from another client if no longer in use, or the building staff may need to pick up equipment at ASU Media Services if inventory is not available.

Back to Top

 

Late Request for Set-up Changes (within 2 working days prior to event)

  • Exempt campus users, as well as Categories I and II will be charged $10 - $25 for requests easily accommodated (qualified by labor costs).
  • Categories I and II will be charged a $25 - $50 fee for excessive set-up (qualified by labor costs). An example of a late, excessive set-up would include providing for a program with more than 200 chairs; more than 20 tables in any one ballroom; a complete set-up for a presentation when using Memorial Union microphones and house system connections; or special events when a special diagram must be followed.
