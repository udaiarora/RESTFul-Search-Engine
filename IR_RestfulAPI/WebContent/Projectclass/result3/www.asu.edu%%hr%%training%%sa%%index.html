SUPERVISORY ACADEMY

 

Our Vision

To be regarded as the number one provider of supervisory training for employees of the state of Arizona.

 

Our Mission

Provide Arizona State University’s supervisory staff with consistent, comprehensive, and effective supervisory training across all campuses in order to provide supervisors with the skills needed to effectively lead and manage Arizona State University’s workforce.

ACADEMY NEWS

The Learning and Performance Solutions Group (LPSG) is undertaking an initiative to improve and expand the learning opportunities that are being offered to ASU's faculty and staff.  As part of this initiative, we are making changes to the employee development supervision and leadership training programs.  These revisions include adding more training program options, changing the format of the classes, revising and adding to the classroom materials, expanding the delivery methods for the material, and improving the assessment tools used to measure participant performance.

Some things that future participants can look forward to are:

*A wider variety of classes that are tailored to specific skill levels and development needs *Greater flexibility in class schedules to reduce conflicts with work schedules

*Participants will have longer periods of time to complete the programs and can self-pace their training programs

*Increased hands-on learning and more skill practice

*Frequent evaluation of knowledge and skills

*In-depth coaching from the Human Resources training staff

To allow time for these changes to be made and work to be completed on the materials, we are postponing the start of the Spring 2007 supervision training programs.

As soon as the programs are ready to begin registering participants, we will post all of the logistical information about the programs (schedules, classes, any fees, etc.) on this site.

We apologize for any inconvenience this delay may cause.  If you have any questions about the changes to the programs please call the Learning and Performance Solutions Group at 480-965-4751 or email us at lps@asu.edu.

About The Program

Becoming a supervisor is often the first step a person takes into the realm of organizational leadership. Supervisory Academy is designed primarily to help new supervisors and managers develop and hone their front-line leadership and management skills; while at the same time equipping them with the foundational skills they need to grow and develop into future middle- and senior-level managers.

The program provides participants with an in-depth overview of the skills they need to be effective supervisors, while exposing them to real-life situations and obstacles they will face in their daily work at ASU.  The Academy provides the participants with the training and resources they will need to address these situations and be successful in their roles.  Participants are required to demonstrate their understanding of the concepts and ability to apply their skills in order to complete the course.

Along with the training and personal development, participants who successfully complete Supervisory Academy receive an ASU employment credit equivalent to one year of supervisory experience, they meet the prerequisites of the Certified Public Manager (CPM) program, and can earn continuing education credits.
