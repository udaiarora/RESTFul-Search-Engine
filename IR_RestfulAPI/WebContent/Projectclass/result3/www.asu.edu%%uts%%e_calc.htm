MATH PLACEMENT - CALCULUS

ASU Calculus Math Placement Test for placement into MAT 251, MAT 265 and MAT 270.

Information
This placement test is used to determine a student's readiness to take calculus courses at ASU. Prior to taking the Calculus Math Placement test, students should take the unified math placement test available at http://math.asu.edu/fym/placement.html . If a student desires to register for MAT 251, MAT 265, or MAT 270, the Calculus Math Placement exam must be completed and a passing score achieved prior to course registration. Students must answer 60% of the questions correctly to attain a passing score. Scores will be noted as pass/fail only. Further information concerning math placement requirements for Fall 2007 is available from the Math Department.
Schedule
This exam may be taken at Payne Hall, Room 301, on Friday afternoons at 2:30 pm or at ASU Orientation. Please visit the Orientation website soon for more information concerning Orientation for Fall 2007.
 
Cost
There is no fee associated with this examination.
Time
This exam is timed at 90 minutes and consists of 60 questions. The examination will begin and end promptly, testing time will not be extended for late arrivals.
Register
Students may register for the exam administered on Friday afternoons in the UTS office at Payne Hall, Room 301, by calling 480-965-7146 at least one working day prior to the desired test date, or you may take the exam as part of your Orientation acitivies.
Retake
You may retake the exam until a passing score is achieved. Retakes are available only at University Testing Services on Friday afternoons at 2:30 pm.
Admission
Walk in testing is not permitted. You should plan to arrive at least 15 minutes prior to the scheduled start of the exam. You must present a current photo ID (such as a driver's license, school ID, passport, or other government-issued photo ID card). If you have questions concerning your ID, please contact University Testing Services at 480-965-7146 prior to the exam date. No calculators are allowed.
