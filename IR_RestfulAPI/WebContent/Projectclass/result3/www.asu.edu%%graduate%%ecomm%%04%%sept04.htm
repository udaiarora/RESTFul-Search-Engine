— ANNOUNCEMENTS —

Format Advising Changes
Student Academic Services (SAS) provides DGS academic services to enrolled graduate students from program entry through graduation. SAS now includes format evaluation and format approval. MacKenzie Case is the SAS coordinator. Please call 5–3521 with questions.

Funding Opportunity
 Inquiries are being accepted through Dec. for P.E.O. Scholar Awards. These are one–time competitive awards for U.S./Canadian women either pursuing a graduate degree or engaged in advanced study or research who will make significant contributions in their field. Priority is given to women already established in graduate programs or advanced study or research. Each award is $10,000. An applicant must be nominated by a local P.E.O. chapter. For more information, email atedrick@sedona.net and use “P.E.O. Scholar Awards” in the subject line.

New Graduate Student Orientation a Success

More than 400 new graduate students, from half the country and almost two dozen foreign countries, attended this year. The event was made possible thanks to volunteers and academic departments/units, and those who contributed door prizes, including: Civil & Environmental Engineering, English, Nutrition (ASU East), Justice Studies & Social Inquiry, Music, Art, and the Graduate & Professional Student Association (GPSA). Thanks to everyone for making the event such a great success and welcoming new students to ASU.
