Introduction

Overview of SAS/ETS Software

SAS/ETS software, a component of the SAS System, provides SAS procedures for

  • econometric analysis
  • time series analysis
  • time series forecasting
  • systems modeling and simulation
  • seasonal adjustment
  • financial analysis and reporting
  • access to economic and financial databases
  • time series data management

In addition to SAS procedures, SAS/ETS software also includes a menu-driven system for interactive time series forecasting.
