2004 ASU Outstanding Doctoral Mentors

Professors Keith W. Kintigh and Douglas C. Montgomery have been named ASU Outstanding Doctoral Mentors for 2004 in recognition of their unbridled commitment to the learning, preparation, and intellectual and professional growth of their students.

Kintigh, a professor of anthropology, and Montgomery, a professor of industrial engineering, will be presented with the award by the Division of Graduate Studies and the ASU Foundation at an invitation-only reception on Feb. 10.

Congratulations to them both!

Clinic: Preparing for Graduate School on Feb. 3 has been canceled.

Revised guidelines for joint bachelor’s/ master’s programs now available! To provide academic units with increased academic program options, the Division is pleased to announce that the guidelines for joint bachelor’s/master’s programs have been revised. The revised guidelines include several critical changes. The guidelines may be found online at www.asu.edu/graduate/facstaff/docs/
BachelorsMastersGuidelinesJAN05.doc.

As always, if your unit is considering proposing a bachelor’s/master’s program, you are invited to contact Associate Dean Filiz Ozel or Gabriel Goltz.

Students must be registered to defend a thesis/dissertation. A student must be enrolled for at least one semester hour of appropriate graduate-level credit (such as 595, 795, or a course that appears on the approved POS) during the semester or summer session in which the student defends the thesis/ dissertation. Students will not be able to submit their document for format review or schedule their oral defense if they are not registered for the semester of their defense.

The deadline to apply for May 2005 graduation is March 31. Graduation deadlines and procedures for 2005 have been revised. For more information, go to www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/GradDdlns.

The last day to hold an oral defense of a thesis/dissertation for May 2005 graduation is April 22 (F). The 10 Working Day Calendar for 2005 has been revised. View the updates at www.asu.edu/graduate/generalinfo/GradDdlns.
