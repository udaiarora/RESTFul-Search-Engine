Services For Students

Borrowing materials

To check out materials, bring them to the Circulation/Check-out counters near the entrance/exit of each library. You will need either your ASU SunCard or another form of photo identification card for the check out process. Book returns are located outside Hayden (at the top of the stairs) and Noble (to the right/west of the front doors) Libraries and inside all campus libraries.

Individual Study Rooms

Individual Study Rooms are available for checkout at Hayden and Noble Libraries, on a four hour basis or ‘daily’ with the key due at closing. Rooms are not available for checkout between Midnight and 7:00 a.m.


These rooms are for single use/individual study and check out is on a first come first served basis. Check out is limited to ASU student, staff and faculty use only.


Late return fees will apply. Fees are listed on the key tag. You are responsible for returning key to the Circulation Desk, a return receipt is recommended


Group Study Rooms

Group study rooms are available at the Noble Science Library (several rooms on the second and third floors) on a first-come, first served basis. Individuals are welcome to use these rooms for private study, but group work has a higher priority. In the Noble Library, dry erase markers and erasers may be checked out at the circulation desk.
