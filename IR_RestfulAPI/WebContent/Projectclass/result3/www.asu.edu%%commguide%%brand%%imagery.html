Branding ASU: Brand Imagery

To help build the ASU Brand, high quality photography is essential. This photography should be used on main imagery within any medium. Whether the subject is the environment, still life, or a person, the quality and lighting reflects the quality and tone of the brand. The primary use of all photography should be in full color, with minimal background/foreground elements. Use only black and white photography when a certain application requires it (example: black and white ad).

See Photography Guidelines for more detail.
