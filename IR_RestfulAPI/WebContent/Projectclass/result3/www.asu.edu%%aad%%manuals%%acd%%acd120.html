ACD 120: “Second-Generation” Credit

Purpose

To define “second-generation” courses and describe ASU policies excluding credit for such courses

Sources

Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 2–205, 2–206
Graduate Council
Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

All academic units

Policy

In accordance with Board of Regents’ policies, ASU shall not grant academic credit for “second-generation” courses. This policy applies to all courses, regardless of the terminology used to name them. Many such courses serve useful purposes, but awarding ASU credit for them is inappropriate.
