ASU/Maricopa Community Colleges Alliance

The Arizona State University/Maricopa Community Colleges Alliance is a partnership designed to promote a seamless experience for students who want to earn an Associate Degree and a Baccalaureate Degree.

Building upon the historical success of the ASU/Maricopa partnership, the Alliance has expanded to develop formal agreements within key degree programs that help students graduate on time and help meet the demand in Arizona.

  • Program Articulation: Students enjoy assured guaranteed course transferability through established transfer agreements.
  • Academic Advising: Students will have access to academic advising at ASU and Maricopa Community Colleges.
  • Libraries: Students will have access to the volumes of materials available through the libraries at ASU and Maricopa Community Colleges.
  • Financial Aid: Consortium agreements between ASU and Maricopa Community Colleges allow students to pursue federal financial aid based on concurrent enrollment.
  • Student Events: Students will have the opportunity to attend various athletic and cultural events at ASU and Maricopa Community Colleges
