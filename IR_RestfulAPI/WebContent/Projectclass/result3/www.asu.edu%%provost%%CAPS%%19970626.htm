Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

June 26, 1997

  1. The minutes for the June 26 meeting were approved.

  2. Introduction of new members: Tom Keating, Political Science and Molly McCartney, CSSS are the newly elected members of CAPS beginning three-year terms. Ken Furuta, University Libraries, is replacing Cristina Gonzalez for the last two years of the term. Welcome! Gary also thanked departing members, Patricia Etter and Lise Hawkos for their service to the committee.

  3. Gary gave an overview of what the committee has been doing this year for the new members along with discussion of continuing concerns for the coming year.

  4. The current officers discussed the requirements of the various positions and from there we proceded to elect officers for next year:

    5. Co-Chairpersons: Annette Voth and Karl Weiss
    Secretary/Web Master: Ken Furuta
    Archivist: Molly McCartney
    Chair-Elect: to be elected at a later date

  5. The next meeting will be on Thursday, July 10, 1997.
