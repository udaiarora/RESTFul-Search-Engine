    Importing from IEEE and Web of Science

    Steps for importing citations from IEEE Xplore and ISI Web of Science:

     

    IEEE Xplore

    Complete your search in IEEE Xplore.  From results list, click in boxes to "select" items to save.

     

     

    Then scroll to the bottom of the page and click on "View Selected Records" button:

     

     

    From the "Download Citations" section from the left hand column, select either "Citation" or "Citation & Abstract" as the format to save, and click on "Download" button:

     

     

    Save file to desktop. Then, open it with Notepad (or any other viewer), and save as a text file.

    Log into Refworks, from "References" menu, choose "Import"

     


    Choose RIS Format as the Import filter/data source and choose IEEE as the database.

     

     


    Select Folder, if desired.  Then click Import button at bottom of screen.


    Web of Science

    Complete your search in Web of Science.  From results list, click in boxes to "Mark" items to save.

     

    When finished marking items, click on "Save" in the "Mark Records" box on the right hand side of the screen.

     

     

    Click on the "Marked List" button from the top navigation menu.

     

     

    Select the information fields to download , then click on "Export to Reference Software"

     

     

    Save file when prompted. 

     

    Log into Refworks, from "References" menu, choose "Import"

     

     

    Select "ISI (Insitute for Scientific Information" as Import Filter

     

     

    Select "Web of Science" as Database:

     

     

    Select Folder, if desired.  Then click Import button at bottom of screen.
