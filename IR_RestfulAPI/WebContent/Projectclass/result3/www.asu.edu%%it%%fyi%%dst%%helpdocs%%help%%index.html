Getting Help

  1. Online Consulting
    Consulting service for faculty, staff, and students covering a variety of computing and networking topics and is provided via email.

  2. Windows applications
    This has information related to a variety of statistical applications for Microsoft Windows.

  3. Online UNIX Help
    This has information related to a wide variety of topics from the Andrew File System (AFS) to Year2000.

  4. Online Manuals
    Some of the UNIX systems at ASU have vendor-supplied documentation available in a format suitable for interactive viewing.

  5. Statistics Hotline
    This service is available to anyone affiliated with ASU and who needs assistance with their ASU-related research.
