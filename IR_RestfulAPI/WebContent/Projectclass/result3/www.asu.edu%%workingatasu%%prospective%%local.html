Local Information

ASU's main campus is located in Tempe [pronounced Tem'pE] Arizona, adjacent to Phoenix and part of Maricopa County, the nation's fourth most populous county. Twenty-three communities form Greater Phoenix, also known as the "Valley of the Sun" or just the "Valley." Polytechnic campus is located in Arizona's third largest city, Mesa. West campus is located in west Phoenix, adjacent to Glendale. The ASU Downtown Center is located in Copper Square.
