Visitor Parking

A visitor to the Arizona State University Tempe Campus has four options for temporary parking:

  1. Meters for visits up to one hour in length at a rate of $1.50/hour.
  2. Attended structures for longer visits or for visits of unknown length at a rate of $2 per hour with a maximum exit fee of $8. ASU departments may purchase validation stamps at the Parking Decal Sales Office located in the Towers Building, Suite 109.

Only white all day stamps and orange one hour stamps are accepted for validation.

  1. Temporary permits for day-long visits utilizing surface lots. Depending on the lot location the rate is $4 to $6 per day.
  2. Pay & Display lots are $2/hour with a 12 hour maximum.

Meters and visitor lots are available on a first-come, first-serve basis and do fill up daily.  Best times for availability are early morning (before 9 a.m.) or later in the afternoon (after 2 p.m.).

Temporary permits may be purchased from Parking and Transit Services located at The Towers on 5th Street between Forest and College Avenue, Tempe, AZ. 

ASU students, faculty, and staff should purchase an ASU parking decal.
