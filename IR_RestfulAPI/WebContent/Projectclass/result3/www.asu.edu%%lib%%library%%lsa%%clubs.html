Clubs in the Libraries

Craft Club

Craft Club meets on Tuesdays in the Technical Services conference room (room 401K) from 12:00 to 1:00. We work on individual projects and enjoy each others company. Although the Craft Club is not part of the Staff Association, we sponsor Craft and Bake Sales for the LSA and sometimes work on group craft projects for the sale. We always welcome new members. There are no dues. Bring your craft, your lunch, yourself and join us!
