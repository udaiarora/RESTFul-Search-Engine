Site Home >Fall 2006 Move-In

All students must check in at Wells Fargo Arena (WFA) before proceeding to their assigned hall. Please refer to your roommate letter for the day and time you may check in.

WFA check in times are from 6 a.m. to 2 p.m. Aug. 15-17.

Scheduled days:
Tuesday, August 15 | Wednesday, August 16 | Thursday, August 17

Parking is available in Structure 7 and Lot 59 (please see check-in map). Leave and lock all belongings in vehicle. Proceed to WFA and collect room and mailbox keys, register for concierge/a la carte services and pick up parking decal (if pre-purchased). Please be sure to visit our Partner Showcase, where you will have the opportunity to experience and participate in the many services offered by our partners. Please see the enclosed Partner Showcase directory for more information. If you need to check in after 2 p.m. on the day you were assigned, please check in at your residence hall.

All rooms must be claimed by 5 p.m. on Saturday, August 19, or your room reservation will be cancelled and you will be assessed a $500 no-show fee. If you need to arrive after 5 p.m. on Saturday, August 19, please notify Residential Life in writing via U.S. Mail or fax.

PO Box 870212
Tempe, AZ; 85287-0212

or fax 480-965-1534.

The halls officially close for winter break at 5 p.m. on Friday, December 15. Each student is required to vacate their room 24 hours after their last final. Halls are open during Thanksgiving and Spring breaks. Halls open again for the spring semester on Saturday, January 13.
