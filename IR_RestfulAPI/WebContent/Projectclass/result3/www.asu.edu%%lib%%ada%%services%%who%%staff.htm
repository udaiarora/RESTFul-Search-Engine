Our Staff

 

 

 Jodie Milam has been the supervisor of ADA Services since May 2002, and has been with the ASU Libraries since 1991. Because of her warm, gentle manner and her willingness to give, Jodie is a favorite with staff and patrons alike! If you are nervous about using our massive library, please come in and meet Jodie - she will help you to feel comfortable in no time.
