Dear ASU Student,

According to our records, you did not successfully complete any of your classes for the summer 2006 semester. By law, you may be required to return all or part of the financial aid you received for this semester.

In order to calculate the amount that you must repay, we need to know:
1) that you did (or did not) begin attending each class;
2) the last day that you attended your class(es).

Documentation of these items must come from a university official; you cannot self-verify this information. Therefore, please have your professor e-mail us (thelwig@asu.edu) from his or her ASU e-mail account as soon as possible to report this information. This correspondence must include your name and the last four digits of your ASU ID number. If we cannot determine that you attended class, you will be required to repay the entire amount of aid disbursed to you for the semester. We must receive this information no later than Friday, August 25, 2006, at 5 p.m. Please note that this is a federal financial aid requirement and not an ASU institutional policy.

Please call us at (480) 965-3355 if you have any questions or feel free to visit one of our locations. Thank you in advance for your assistance in this matter. Act now!
