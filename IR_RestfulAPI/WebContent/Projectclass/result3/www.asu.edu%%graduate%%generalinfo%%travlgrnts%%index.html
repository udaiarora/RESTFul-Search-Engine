Conference Travel Grants
2006–2007


DEADLINES: 2006–2007

Friday, October 27, 2006: for travel November 16, 2006 through February 15, 2007
Friday, January 26, 2007: for travel February 16, 2007 through May 15, 2007
Friday, April 13, 2007: for travel May 16, 2007 through August 31, 2007
Friday, July 27, 2007: for travel September 1, 2007 through November 15, 2007
PURPOSE
Conference Travel Grants reimburse graduate students the cost of airfare (maximum $350) to support student participation in professional conferences. Students who are invited to attend small, prestigious professional development workshops should apply for Tripke Travel Awards.

CONTACT
Beatris Mejia (beatris.mejia@asu.edu), Division of Graduate Studies, 965-3521

ELIGIBILITY
Applicants must be regularly admitted graduate students who are in good standing with their academic unit and the Division of Graduate Studies (DGS). Under very unusual circumstances and upon prior approval, mileage will be reimbursed. No taxis or other local transportation will be reimbursed from these funds.

APPLICATION PROCESS
Approval is a two-step process. Applications are submitted initially to the student's academic unit for evaluation and prioritization in accordance with the unit's established criteria. They are then forwarded to the DGS for consideration. Because of limited funding, only a small number of requests can be funded.

Applicants must present evidence of matching funds from university sources other than the DGS (e.g., academic unit, Graduate and Professional Student Association).

Procedural Steps
  1. Complete and sign the application form. Funds from university sources other than the DGS must be shown.

  2. Complete the Travel Authorization form found at travel.asu.edu. Be sure to state how ASU will benefit from your participation (Purpose/Justification) and to sign and date the form.
  3. Submit your application to your academic unit for their prioritization and signature. Department chair/director or other person authorized to release departmental travel funds must sign the form. The academic unit forwards the top three applications to the DGS by the deadlines listed above. Please do not attach any additional information to the travel grant application or travel authorization form.

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS
Applications without all signatures, those with missing information, and those missing the original signed Travel Grant application will not be reviewed. Applications received after the deadline will not be considered. Decisions will be emailed to applicants approximately one week after the application deadline. If you have a change of address, please inform us as soon as possible.
