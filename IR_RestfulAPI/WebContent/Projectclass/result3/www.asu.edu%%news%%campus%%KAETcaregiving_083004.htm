August 30, 2004

KAET special to focus on caregiving

KAET-TV/Channel 8 will play host to "Arizona Caregiving Town Hall Meeting," a special community forum discussing long-term caregiving that will air at 8 p.m. Sept. 1.

The show will be a follow-up to the PBS special "And Thou Shalt Honor"- a program about providing long-term care that aired in 2002.

For the new KAET presentation, 100 caregivers, experts and policymakers from across the state discuss and find solutions to caregiving issues in Arizona. George and Nancy Mairs of Tucson, who were featured in "And Thou Shalt Honor," and Susan Goldwater Levine are featured guests, and Bill Heywood will be the moderator.

Throughout "Arizona Caregiving Town Hall Meeting," viewers are invited to call KAET to receive a free information packet focusing on Arizona resources.

The packet contains information about hospice care options, advance directives forms, tips for talking with your doctor, a glossary of frequently used terms, advance health care planning questions and answers, and other pertinent information. Also included is a comprehensive CD-ROM from AARP on the subject of caregiving.

"Arizona Caregiving Town Hall Meeting" offers valuable information on a variety of home caregiving topics.
