ACD 802: Keys

Purpose

To provide necessary keys for faculty members and academic professionals

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

Requests for building and office keys should be made through the chair or director of the faculty member’s or academic professional’s department or division.

Upon termination, keys must be returned to the employee’s supervisor. Before leaving the campus, faculty members and academic professionals should make sure that they are credited with having returned their keys.

Cross-Reference

For information on assistance with retrieval of keys from a locked car, see the Parking and Transit Services Policies and Procedures Manual—PTS 701, “Motorist Assistance.”
