Photos for the 2007–2008 Catalogs

Academic and Administrative Documents (AAD) continually strives to improve the photo content in the General Catalog and Graduate Catalog. To that end we again request your assistance.

How You Can Help

We feel that it is important to continue to work closely with you, the coordinators, in gathering photos that could be used. Over the past years many coordinators have contributed photos that enhanced the look of their particular college section. Therefore, we encourage you to keep an eye out for photos that represent your unit well. If you become aware of an individual photo, please let us know, or if someone in your college or department has a pool of photos, please put us in touch with that person.

Guidelines for Photos

We prefer these formats, ranked by preference:

  1. Scans of color photos (originally scanned or digitally shot at 300 dpi or higher)
     
  2. Original color prints
     
  3. Original black and white prints
     
  4. Color slides
     

The photos should

•Be sharply focused (not grainy)
•Have good contrast
•Be accompanied by an explanatory photo caption (including names of individuals in the photo) and the name of the photographer
•Highlight faculty and student diversity

Photo Placement in Catalog Drafts

Our goal is to have photos in place for the optional review—in January for the General Catalog and in March for the Graduate Catalog.

Submitting Photos

•Coordinators are encouraged to submit photos with the first and second drafts.
•Photos can be delivered by hand to AAD.

To discuss potential photos and ideas for publication, please call John MacIsaac, associate editor, at 5-9358, or send e-mail to macisaac@mainex1.asu.edu.asu.edu.
