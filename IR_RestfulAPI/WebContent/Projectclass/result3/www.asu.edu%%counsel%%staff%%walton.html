Matt Walton is an Associate General Counsel for Arizona State University. He comes to ASU after six years as the University Counsel to the University of Northern Colorado. Prior to that, he served as the head of the Education Unit of the Colorado Attorney General's Office. Matt also has experience in private practice, as a magistrate for the Denver County court system and as a Deputy in the Denver Office of the Colorado Public Defender. He is a graduate of the University of Denver College of Law (J.D. 1985) and the University of Michigan (B.A. 1982).
