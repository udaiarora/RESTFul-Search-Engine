329.  

ALFONSO X. KING OF CASTILE AND LEON, 1221-1284. CONCORDANCES AND TEXTS OF THE ROYAL SCRIPTORIUM MANUSCRIPTS.
CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 4811
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 112 F
INDEX OR GUIDE: Pamphlet in binder with fiche.
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: These microfiche represent some 20,000 pages of text and their corresponding concordances and frequency counts. See the Online catalog record for details on the contents.
SUBJECTS: Spain – History – Medieval Period; 13c

330.  

COLECCION DE DOCUMENTOS INEDITOS PARA LA HISTORIA DE ESPANA.
CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 56
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 112 VE
INDEX OR GUIDE: Julian Paz, Catalogo de la Coleccion de Documentos Ineditos para la Historia de Espana (DP 3 .C693 vol. 1-2 Stacks). This work describes in detail the contents of each volume and contains a number of indexes: persons, places and regions as well as archival sources of the documents. There is also a detailed list of contents in Raymond Foulche-Delbosc, Manuel de l’hispanisant, vol. 2, pp. 113-179 (Z 2681 .A1 F7 Stacks).
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Consists of archival and manuscript materials (chronicles, diplomatic correspondence, biographies, Inquisitional proceedings) for the study of Spain and her possessions for the 15th through the 17th centuries (including many sources on Spanish America). The collection is not only indispensable to the study of Spanish history from the union of Castille and Aragon in 1493 to the last of the Spanish Hapsburgs, but also contains important materials for research in German, Austrian, French, English and Portuguese history. Topics include biographies of important persons such as Bartolome de Las Casas, information on the silk weavers of Toledo, a description of the Viceroyalty of Naples, the Council of Trent and the conquests of Mexico and Peru. Some of the microfiche for the first volume appear to be missing. Have most of the set in hard copy, DP 3 C69 Stacks.
SUBJECTS: Spain – History; Latin America; 15c; 16c; 17c

331.  

COLONIAL HISPANIC LEGAL DOCUMENTS, 18TH – 19TH CENTURIES.
CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 9359
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 13 R
INDEX OR GUIDE: None
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Spanish and Portuguese pamphlets, broadsides, official gazettes and manuscripts dealing with Mexico, Peru, Puerto Rico and Portugal. Within each jurisdiction, the documents are arranged by category: briefs, canon laws, laws and statutes and miscellanea. Included are royal decrees, ordinances and regulations of local character, petitions to the king, records of inquiries and royal answers to petitions. Valuable for the study of history, law, politics, Indian relations and the Church.
SUBJECTS: Spain – History; Latin America; Portugal; 18c; 19c

333.  

HISPANIC CULTURE SERIES: BOOKS BEFORE 1601.
CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 3752
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 259 R (129 VE)
INDEX OR GUIDE: Hispanic Culture Before 1601: Short-title List (Z 2682 .H5x Micro Ref.) This is a reel guide to reels 61 to 259 of the collection. Titles are not listed in alphabetical order so entire list must be scanned to locate a particular title. Micro Ref. also has a card file for this set, consisting of cards for each title, filed in order as they appear on the reels, covering reels 1-195 (overlapping the printed reel guide).
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: All areas of knowledge are included, e.g., indexes of prohibited books, the works of Bartolome de las Casas on the colonization of America, Encina’s Cancionero and other works invaluable for study of the Renaissance and the discovery of the New World. Some reels have title: “Spanish, Portuguese and American Books Printed Before 1601.” A collection of books based on British Museum, Catalogue of Books Printed in Spain and of Spanish Books Printed Elsewhere Before 1601 (Z 2682 .B85x 1989 Ref.); and Simon Diaz’ Bibliografia de la Literatura Hispanica (PQ 6021 .S5x Ref.).
SUBJECTS: Spain; Latin America – History; Renaissance; 15c; 16c

335.  

SPAIN. SERVICIO HISTORICO MILITAR. DOCUMENTOS DE LA “DIVISION AZUL.”
CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 3830
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 8 R
INDEX OR GUIDE: Reel 1 contains a chronological list of documents.
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Official documents concerning the Spanish Blue Division, which fought on the side of the Germans in World War II as “volunteers.”
SUBJECTS: Spain – History; Spanish Blue Division; World War II; 20c; 1940s

336.  

SPANISH LEGAL DOCUMENTS, 15TH – 19TH CENTURIES.
CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 9358
NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 36 R
INDEX OR GUIDE: Reel 1 contains index cards describing each document.
SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Contains six categories: briefs, canon law notarial instruments, opinions and judgements, laws and statues and miscellanea. Documents are written in either old Spanish, Castilian, Latin or dialects such as Catalonian or Aragonese. The majority are briefs, i.e., forensic writings related to disputes on inheritance, titles of nobility, taxes, church privilege etc. Includes items on the Inquisition.
SUBJECTS: Spain – Law; 15c; 16c; 17c; 18c; 19c
