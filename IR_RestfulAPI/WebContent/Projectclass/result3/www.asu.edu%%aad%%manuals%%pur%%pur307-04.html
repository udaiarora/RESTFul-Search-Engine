PUR 307–04: Retaining Consultants and Independent Contractors

Purpose

To specify the process needed to retain consultants and independent contractors

Sources

Arizona Board of Regents Policy Manual - 3-803
University policy

Applicability

All uses of consultants and independent contractors

Background

Purchasing and Business Service personnel participated on a Total Quality Service Team to simplify the process of retaining consultants and independent contractors while still complying with the university procurement code established by the Board of Regents, Arizona conflict of interest laws, and the federal tax code.

Policy

Consultants and independent contractors will be retained through the procedures on the Purchasing and Business Services Web site.
