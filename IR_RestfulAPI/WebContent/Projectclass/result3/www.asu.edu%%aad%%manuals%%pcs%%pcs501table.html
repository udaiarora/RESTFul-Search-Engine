The department:

  1. completes a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form (see PCS 501A), checking the “Intradepartment movement” box in the “Activity” section

  2. records the ASU property control number, if one exists, on the PRA

    Note: Forms are available in Property Control.

  3. forwards the signed PRA to Property Control for verification signatures

    and

  4. retains the originating department copy.
Property Control signs the PRA, verifying relocation within the department, and retains the Property Control copy for departmental files.

If the Department of Facilities Management movers are not needed, Property Control updates records with the new location of the asset(s).

If the Department of Facilities Management movers are needed, Property Control forwards the original and the remaining copies to the Department of Facilities Management.

The Department of Facilities Management verifies that the numbers are correctly entered on the PRA or, if missing, writes them on the original (Property Control).

If ASU property control numbers are affixed to the equipment, the Department of Facilities Management:

  1. relocates the equipment for the requesting department

  2. signs the PRA upon completion of the move

    and

  3. forwards the original to Property Control.
Property Control:

  1. matches the original with the PRA copy previously signed by the department

  2. reviews the original and the copy for changes

    and

  3. updates Property Control records.
