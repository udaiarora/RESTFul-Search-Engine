Background

Jenny Mueller-Alexander is the Reference Librarian and Bibliographer for the following disciplines: Economics, Marketing, Health Management & Policy, Information Systems, and Community Resources & Development. Her academic training is in German Languages & Literatures and library science. She has worked as a business librarian most of her career. Additionally she has been a Medical and Sciences Librarian.

 

Ms. Mueller-Alexander's current research interest concerns the state and local documents for finding business information. The two geographical areas she focuses on for this are Arizona and Mexico.
