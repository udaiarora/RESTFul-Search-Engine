About ASU Job Openings

Arizona State University welcomes you to our Job Opportunities page and encourages you to participate in our recruitment processes for staff, service professional and administrative positions. This page provides general information regarding job opportunities and methods of applying for positions of interest. Recruitment and hiring of all faculty, faculty associate, post doctoral, and academic professional positions is done at the department level in each college or library. Please contact the appropriate department for more information.

Arizona State University is comprised of three anchor campuses and an extended campus in the Greater Phoenix area.

  • Arizona State University at the Tempe Campus - located in Tempe, AZ
  • Arizona State University at the Polytechnic Campus - located in Mesa, AZ at the former Williams Air Force Base
  • Arizona State University at the West Campus - located in northwest Phoenix, AZ
  • School of Extended Education - located in downtown Phoenix, AZ

Arizona State University at the Tempe Campus Human Resources is responsible for the recruitment efforts of ASU at the Tempe campus, ASU at the Polytechnic campus and the School of Extended Education. Current job opportunities for these locations may be seen on our Jobs on the Web or reviewed on the computers located in the Human Resources Customer Service Center. 

Application Procedure:
Individuals interested in employment opportunities with Arizona State University may apply by submitting a (typed) resume for Posted positions. You may apply for current, Posted positions by following the "To Apply" instructions listed on the job vacancy notice. Positions may vary in their application requirements. Failure to submit the requested information may result in elimination for further consideration.

ASU encourages all individuals to participate in our recruitment processes. We offer a generous benefits package including medical & dental insurance plans, paid vacation & sick days, paid holidays, tuition waiver and retirement programs.

Arizona State University at the West campus job opportunities are the responsibility of ASU at the West campus Human Resources.
