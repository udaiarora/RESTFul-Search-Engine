University Design Team Phase I Report and Recommendations

Please note below, the revised list of units/programs that replied to the University Design Team (UDT) recommendations. Some reports that arrived after the deadline may not be included in the synopses at this time. Please be assured that these reports will be included in any discussion concerning the future of Arizona State University. One proposal was more in keeping with the Phase II report and will be included with comments for that version. The Phase I report and synopses are available at: www.asu.edu/provost/designteam/report/

  1. We received individual responses from approximately 80 members of the main campus faculty and staff, 32 from the west campus faculty and staff and 3 from the east campus faculty and staff.
