Primary Sources in History: China

45. CHINA COAST NEWSPAPERS, 1822-1911.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm AN 8 [rest of number varies: see online catalog]  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 105 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: Research Guide to China Coast Newspapers, 1822-1911 (Z6958 C5 K5 Stacks).  Guide has history of China-Coast newspapers; annotated list of the papers; a list of editors and publishers with brief biographical information; a bibliography; and the following indexes: 1: chronological, 2: alphabetical by title, 3: general index to the guide and its contents.  There is no subject index to the contents of the newspapers, which are listed by title in the online catalog.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: A collection of newspapers published in China.  SUBJECTS: China; Asia; 19c; 20c

46. DOCUMENTS ON CONTEMPORARY CHINA, 1949-1975: CULTURAL REVOLUTION, RED GUARD TRANSLATIONS.     CALL NUMBER: University of Arizona Microfiche 258.   NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:            INDEX OR GUIDE: Microfiche Guide 258.  A complete bibliography and subject index of the translations that comprise this collection.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: This collection of Red Guard literature consists of about 1,500 translations from newspapers, periodicals, books, pamphlets, leaflets, etc., which were either published or sponsored by the Red Guard.  SUBJECTS: China - History - Revolution, 1949; Red Guard; 20c; Asia.

47. MARSHALL, GEORGE. COMPLETE RECORDS OF THE MISSION OF GENERAL GEORGE C. MARSHALL TO CHINA. DECEMBER 1945-JANUARY 1947.  CALL NUMBER: Center for Research Libraries (Request at Interlibrary Loan).  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 50 R       INDEX OR GUIDE: Guide available.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: President Truman appointed General Marshall a special envoy to China with instructions to arrange a cease-fire agreement between Chiang Kai-shek's Nationalist troops and Chinese Communist forces.  General Marshall returned to become Secretary of State without having reached a solution to the conflict.  These papers are sources for studying the Chinese political and military situation following World War II, as well as U. S. policy during this period.  Contains minutes of Marshall's meetings with Chiang Kai-shek, Chou En-Lai and other leaders and reports and memoranda prepared for Marshall by U. S. diplomatic personnel.  Reveals American perceptions of events in China and provides insights into the failure of postwar U. S. policy there.  Includes a file prepared by John Carter Vincent, who had been in China since the early 1940s.   SUBJECTS: China - Politics; Foreign Relations; United States; 1940s; 20c; Chiang Kai-shek; Chou En-lai.

48. PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH. BOARD OF FOREIGN MISSIONS. BOARD LETTERS TO MISSIONS; CHINA LETTERS, 1851-1861.       CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 3831-3832 NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 9 Reels  INDEX OR GUIDE: Reel 1 starts with a calendar or chronological list of the letters, with some excerpts indicating the contents.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Representative subjects: dedication of a new church; contrast of Chinese and Western educational methods; Romanizing the Chinese language; riots and rebellions; the Chinese form of government.  These are manuscript letters.     SUBJECTS: Presbyterian Church; China - Missions; 19c; Education; Politics; Education; Asia.

49. PRESS CUTTINGS ON ASIAN AFFAIRS: CHINA, 1963-1970  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 9105  NUMBER OR REELS/FICHE: 49 R  INDEX OR GUIDE:  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Clippings from newspapers, mostly in Asian languages.  Includes occasional English and French articles.  Filmed in monthly segments.      SUBJECTS: China - History; Asia; 1960s

50. SPECIAL STUDIES SERIES. CHINA. 1970-1980.   CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 7773    NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 8 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 777.55 C44668x Guide. Micro Ref.  Reel guide/subject index.  Contains abstracts of the reports.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Sixty-nine studies by "think tanks" such as the Institute for Defense Analysis, Rand Corp, etc., originally written to provide current data and analyses of China for the executive departments of the U. S. government.  Sections cover both China and Taiwan.  Chinese section includes papers on land reform, military strategy, cultural; revolution, foreign policy, Sino-Soviet relations, nuclear weapons, and works of Mao Tse-Tung.  The dates in the title refer to the dates of publication of these studies rather than the periods dealt with.  SUBJECTS: China; Taiwan; Asia; 1970s

51. U. S. CIA REPORT. CHINA, 1946-1976.  CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm DS 777.55 C44655x            NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 6 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 777.55 C44655x Guide.  Contains a table of contents, reel index and a subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Reports generated by the U. S. Central Intelligence Agency on China, India and Pakistan.  Covers agriculture, balance of payments, Chinese Communist Party, Cultural Revolution, foreign policy, economic and political conditions, India-Pakistan war, Indochina, military issues.  Information on Mao Tse-tung, Lin Piao, Indira Gandhi, Chou En-lai and Chiang Kai-shek.  Important events during this period include continuous conflicts between Mainland China and Taiwan; China's entry into the Korean War; and her victory in Vietnam.  Also covers relations between China and the Soviet Union. SUBJECTS: China; India; Pakistan; Asia; Cold War; Korean War; Vietnam War; 20c

52. U. S. DEPARTMENT OF STATE. OFFICE OF CHINESE AFFAIRS, RECORDS, 1945-1955. CALL NUMBER: Center for Research Libraries (Request at Interlibrary Loan)  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 41 R            INDEX OR GUIDE: Printed Guide.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: The Office of Chinese Affairs, which was charged with the operational control of American policy toward China, amassed information on virtually all aspects of life there immediately before, during and after the revolution.  These papers provide insights into numerous domestic issues in Communist and Nationalist China, U. S. containment policy and Sino-American relations.  There are three categories of records: Political, Economic and Top Secret.  The latter category includes policy statements, reports, correspondence and intelligence estimates pertaining to such topics as Soviet policy toward the Chinese Communist regime.  SUBJECTS: United States - Foreign Affairs - China; Economic Conditions; Politics; Soviet Union; 1940s; 1950s; 20c; Communism.

53. U. S. MILITARY INTELLIGENCE REPORTS, CHINA, 1911-1941.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 8060            NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 15 R           INDEX OR GUIDE: DS 773.89 U6x Stacks. A reel and subject index. SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Reports of American military attaches on key events in China: revolution of 1911; Twenty-one demands; Japanese intervention; struggle between Kuomintang and Communists; the Long March; and World War II. SUBJECTS: World War II; China; 20c

54. ALMANACH DE GOTHA: ANNUAIRE GENEALOGIQUES, DIPOMATIQUES ET STATISTIQUES, 1767-1863.  CALL NUMBER: Microcard 4x6 S228       NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 75 VE (501 F)  INDEX OR GUIDE:  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Published annually.  A standard handbook, in which coverage varies over the years.  Until 1940, included two main sections: 1) Annuaire Genealogique, which gives genealogies of the royal and princely houses of Europe; and 2) Annuaire Diplomatique et Statistique, which gives statistical and descriptive information about the various countries of the world, with lists of the principal executive, legislative and diplomatic officials of each.      SUBJECTS: Europe; Kings and Rulers - Genealogy; 18c; 19c; 20c

55. BRITAIN AND EUROPE SINCE 1945.            CALL NUMBER: Center for Research Libraries (ask at Interlibrary Loan).  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:  INDEX OR GUIDE: Copies of the guides are available from the Center.  Includes a list of organizations whose publications have been filmed; introductory material about the integration of Britain and Europe; and chronological author and title indexes.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Includes British source material on European integration since 1945.  Material is produced by a number of organizations interested in Britain's entry into the Common Market and the ramifications of that action on international affairs.  Base set covers 1945-1972 with annual updates.            SUBJECTS: Common Market; Europe; European Integration; European Economic Community; 20c 56. BULGARIA. STATISTICHESCKI GODISHNIK, 1956-1970.    CALL NUMBER: Microform HA 1621 S8  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 87 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Hardcopy, 1979-  . HA 1621 S8  SUBJECTS: Bulgaria-Statistics; 20c

57. CATHOLIC REFORMATION  CALL NUMBER: Center for Research Libraries (Request at Interlibrary Loan).  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:  INDEX OR GUIDE: Lists of works included available from CRL.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Consists of about 190 separate works dealing with the Catholic Reformation in the 16th and 17th centuries.  The emphasis is on philosophy, theology and controversy.  Includes a number of biographies or major figures of the Catholic Reformation in France.  Valuable sources for an understanding of the period between the end of the Middle Ages and the Enlightenment.  Most of the works are in Latin, some in French.           SUBJECTS: Catholic Church - Reformation; 16c; 17c

58. EUROPEAN OFFICIAL STATISTICAL SERIALS ON MICROFICHE.  CALL NUMBER: Cataloged separately in the Online Catalog  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:  INDEX OR GUIDE:  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: ASU has selected titles for East Germany, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Rumania and Yugoslavia.  Some cover as early as the Nineteenth century while others are more recent.  For a full list, do a series search in the Online Catalog.  The University of Arizona has a more complete collection of these serials (Microfiche 212 U of A) and a printed list of the titles included (HA 1107 .E89 U of A).       SUBJECTS: Central Europe; East Germany; Czechoslovakia; Hungary; Rumania; Yugoslavia; Statistics; 19c; 20c

59. EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT. DEBATES, 1980-1984.  CALL NUMBER: Microfiche JN 32 .036            NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:         INDEX OR GUIDE: Name and subject index (in both bound volumes and fiche).      SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Full text of debates in English.  An Annex to Official Journal of the European Communities.  Have hardcopy, 1973-1980 on periodicals floor.  Same call number as fiche.            SUBJECTS: Europe; European Parliament; European Communities; 20c

60. HARVARD UNIVERSITY. DICTIONARY CATALOGUE OF THE BYZANTINE COLLECTION OF THE DUMBARTON OAKS RESEARCH LIBRARY.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 9047  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 12 R  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: A film of the library's card catalog, which contains 172,000 cards.  Author, title and subject cards are inter-filed in one alphabet.  The 84,000 volume collection contains books on all aspects of Byzantine and medieval civilization, as well as the late Graeco-roman world, early and medieval Islam and the world of the Orthodox Slavs.  The library contains literary and historical texts in the original languages, including primary sources in Greek, Latin, Coptic, Syriac, Arabic, Persian, Turkish, Georgian, Armenian and Old Slavonic.  In addition to studies in political, social and economic history, there is a large section of titles on art, archaeology, church history and liturgy, as well as canon and civil law.  Also covered are the auxiliary disciplines of epigraphy, numismatics, palaeography and papyrology.  This catalog may be used as a comprehensive bibliography on this subject, to identify relevant titles, to verify bibliographical information and to locate books to borrow on interlibrary loan. SUBJECTS: Medieval History; Byzantine Empire; 4c; 5c; 6c; 7c; 8c; 9c; 10c; 11c; 12c; 13; 14c; 15c

61. HUNGARY. STATISTICAL YEARBOOK, 1872-1965.  CALL NUMBER: Microfiche HA 1201 A523  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 488 F  INDEX OR GUIDE:          SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Have later in hard copy    SUBJECTS: Hungary; Europe; 19c; 20c 62. ITALIAN BOOKS BEFORE 1601.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 3756          NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 275 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: Z 2342 I8x Micro Ref. "Italian Books Before 1601: A Short-title List" is a reel guide only.  Books were not filmed in any order and there is no author or title index.  In order to locate a specific title, it is necessary to scan the whole guide.  Micro Ref. has a card file, consisting of cards for each title, arranged as they appear on the reels, for reels 1-143.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: The collection is based on, but not restricted to, titles listed in: British Library, Short-title Catalogue of Books Printed in Italy and of Italian Books Printed in Other Countries from 1465 to 1600 (Z2342 .B86x and Supplement - in Stacks).  Includes important 15th and 16th century books in all subjects, e.g., Papal documents, works of humanists such as Ficino and Pico della Mirandola, editions of Greek and Latin texts, Celsus' De Medicina and other works invaluable for study of the Renaissance.  SUBJECTS: Italy - History; Renaissance; 15c; 16c

63. MARTENS, GEORGE FRIEDRICH. RECUEIL DE TRAITES D'ALLIANCE, DE PAIX, DE TREVE AND SUPPLEMENT.  CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 3x5 4958 and 4959 NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 126 F            INDEX OR GUIDE: Chronological table at end of each volume.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: The most complete collection of international agreements and negotiations for the late 18th - early 19th centuries.            SUBJECTS: Treaties - Collected Works; Europe - Politics; 18c; 19c

64. MICROFILM CORPUS OF THE INDEXES TO PRINTED CATALOGUES OF LATIN MANUSCRIPTS BEFORE 1600 A.D. CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 8558    NUMBER OR REELS/FICHE: 39 R           INDEX OR GUIDE: Cranz, F. Edward, Microfilm Corpus (Z 6601 .A1 M5x Ref.)  A hard copy bibliography of the works whose text appears on the microfilm.  Contains full bibliographical description of all the catalogs, a number of indexes (cities, names), and locations of the works cited in various libraries.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: The purpose of this work is to facilitate the first stage of a search for Latin manuscripts of a given author or work.  It makes available the indexes to authors and works found in the catalogs listed in Paul O. Kristeller's Latin Manuscript Books: A List of the Printed Catalogues and Unpublished Inventories of Extant Collections [LMB] (Z 6601 .A1 .K7 stacks).  The films are arranged in the order of listing in LMB. SUBJECTS: Manuscripts, Latin - Catalogs; Latin Manuscript Books; Medieval History; 10c; 11c; 12c; 13c; 14c; 15c.

65. MIGNE, JACQUES PAUL. PATROLOGIAE CURSUS COMPLETUS, SERIES GRAECA. CALL NUMBER: Microcard 4x6 2860  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE:  INDEX OR GUIDE: Index at end of microfiche.            SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Early documents of the Christian Church, from the 1st to the 15th centuries (to 1439). SUBJECTS: Ancient History; Mediaeval History; Christian Church - History; 1c; 2c; 3c; 4c; 5c; 6c; 7c; 8c; 9c; 10c; 11c; 12c; 13c; 14c; 15c

66. OSS STATE DEPT. (OSS INTELLIGENCE AND RESEARCH REPORTS, PTS 5 AND 10) POSTWAR EUROPE, 1945-1949, 1950-1961.            CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm D 1051 P68x and D1051 E9x            NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 21 R           INDEX OR GUIDE: D 1051 P68x Guide and D1051 E9x Guide.  Reel/subject indexes. SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Declassified research reports on economic, social, military and political conditions; European recovery.  Covers Albania, Austria, Baltic States, Belgium, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Great Britain, Greece, Hungary, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Rumania, Sweden and Yugoslavia.  The 1950-61 supplement has sections on Eastern and Western Europe in addition to individual countries and biographical articles on leaders such as Konrad Adenauer and Aneurin Bevan.        SUBJECTS: World War II; Europe; 20c

67. PARIS. PEACE CONFERENCE, 1919-1920. RECUEIL DES ACTS DE LA CONFERENCE. CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 3823   NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 18 R (45 VE)  INDEX OR GUIDE: A general table of contents (Liste des volumes) is included in the "Preliminary Note" on reel 1.   SCOPE AND CONTENTS: This once top-secret collection of records consists primarily of summaries in French of the proceedings of the various commissions of the Conference, including drafts of documents as they were presented and revised.  The collection is divided into 8 parts (resolutions, commission proceedings, treaties, etc.) which in turn are subdivided into numerous sections.  More detailed tables of contents are found at the beginning of each section, some of which are indexed by subject. SUBJECTS: Paris Peace Conference; World War I; 20c

68. RECUEIL DES HISTORIENS DES CROISADES.    CALL NUMBER: Microfiche 4x6 23  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 207 F (16 VE)       INDEX OR GUIDE:   SCOPE AND CONTENTS: A collection of source material for the history of the Crusades.  Includes material in various languages (Arabic, Greek, Latin) with translation into French at bottom of each page.    SUBJECTS: Crusades - History; Medieval History; 11c; 12c; 13c

69. RENAISSANCE RHETORIC: KEY TEXTS, 1479-1602, FROM THE BODLEIAN LIBRARY.  CALL NUMBER: PN 185 r46X Micro Mixed Media        NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 144 f  INDEX OR GUIDE: Guide is in binder with the fiche.  Contains list of authors and works with fiche number; biographical sketch of authors; and a bibliography.            SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Reproduces 54 published works, arranged by author.  Contains texts of works by a variety of authors, mostly in foreign languages including poets, diplomats, lawyers, philosophers, courtiers, businessmen and professors. SUBJECTS: Renaissance; Rhetoric; Europe; 15c; 16c

70. REPARATION PAPERS OF THE ALLIED POWERS COMMISSION.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 6390  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 6 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: D 649 G3 G8x Micro Service.  Reel guide; no subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: German economic conditions, 1918-1930.  Includes complete statement of Germany's postwar obligations, meetings of the Committee of Experts, details of the Dawes Plan and the Young Plan and an examination of military restrictions.  Reports on the Reichsbank, German industries and the Ruhr district.   SUBJECT: Europe; World War I; Germany; 20c; 1920s

71. SPECIAL STUDIES SERIES. EUROPE AND NATO, 1970-1980.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 8820            NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 11 R           INDEX OR GUIDE: UA 646.3 E86x Micro Ref.  Subject index with abstracts.           SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Reports written by "think tanks "on Eastern and Western Europe.  Includes information on strategy, political stability, NATO, Warsaw Pact, nuclear weapons, neutral countries and the U. S. military situation in Europe.          SUBJECTS: Europe; NATO; 20c; 1970s

72. U. S. CIA RESEARCH REPORTS. EUROPE, 1946-1976. CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm D 1058 E85x   NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 4 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: D 1058 E85x Guide.  Lists reports by subject, geographically and chronologically.            SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Reports from the U. S. Central Intelligence Agency concerning internal economic and political situations and topics such as left wing groups (particularly communists), electoral groups, Greek Civil War; economic reconstruction of Europe after World War II.  Information on Harold Wilson, Harold Macmillan, Charles de Gaulle and other leaders.  Reports on foreign relations, attitudes toward NATO, relations with Soviet Union, attitudes toward Indochina war.  Covers Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Great Britain, France, Germany, Greece, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Spain, Sweden. SUBJECTS: Europe; 20c

73. U. S. JOINT CHIEFS OF STAFF. EUROPEAN THEATER, 1942-1945. RECORDS OF THE JOINT CHIEFS OF STAFF, PT. 1.  CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm D 756 G8x          NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 14 R            INDEX OR GUIDE: D 756 G8x Guide.  Reel/subject index.        SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Covers World War II military campaigns in Europe (e.g., Overlord); effects on bombing on Germany; surrender terms; conversations with neutrals (Portugal and Spain).  SUBJECTS: Europe; World War II; 20c; 1940s

74. U. S. JOINT CHIEFS OF STAFF. EUROPE AND NATO, 1946-1953.  RECORDS OF THE JOINT CHIEFS, PT. 2  CALL NUMBER: Doc. Serv. Microfilm D 1065 U5 U56x         NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 9 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: D 1065 U5 U56x Guide.  Reel/subject index.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Primarily military, defense matters; military aid program; occupation and administration of Germany.  Covers Austria, Balkans, Finland, Great Britain, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Italy, Spain and Yugoslavia.         SUBJECTS: NATO; Europe; 20c

75. ULTRA DOCUMENTS.  CALL NUMBER: Microfilm 6833  NUMBER OF REELS/FICHE: 152 R  INDEX OR GUIDE: D 810 S7 U48x Micro Service.  Reel guide only: no subject index.  For each reel, gives serial numbers andn dates covered.  Our guide covers only reels 1-104.  SCOPE AND CONTENTS: Copies of intercepted and decrypted World War II German messages enciphered by the Enigma machine.  Center for Research Libraries has rest of set.  SUBJECTS: World War II; Europe; 20c; 1940s
