Scheduling an Appointment

Students needing to meet with their DRC Disability Access Consultant should call to schedule an appointment through the DRC receptionist at 480-965-1234 (Voice) 480-965-9000 (TTY). Generally appointments will be scheduled for the following day.

Students who are taking classes on the Polytechnic campus, can schedule to meet their DRC Disability Access Consultant in one of three ways:

  • In person

  • Scheduled phone appointment

  • Via email


    • The Polytechnic campus also has a liaison to respond to questions and facilitate academic support services and accommodations on that campus. Please refer to:

    ASU at the Polytechnic campus:
    Irene Rodriguez, Office Specialist, Sr.
    480.727.1039 (V), 480.727.1009 (TTY)

    Every DRC Disability Access Consultant has a pre-scheduled daily walk-in hour to accommodate students who have quick questions or who need to see their Disability Access Consultant on days they are not scheduled for appointments. Walk-in appointments are meant to be short in nature (approximately 10 minutes) to accommodate more students. Please see your Disability Access Consultant for his/her walk-in schedule.
