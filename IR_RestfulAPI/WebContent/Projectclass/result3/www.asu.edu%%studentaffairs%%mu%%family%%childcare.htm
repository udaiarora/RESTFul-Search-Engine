Child Care

We believe that effectively managing family, work and school is challenging for everyone. Child and Family Service's (CFS) services are designed to make it a little easier. We can offer support and information, which is sometimes all you need. If you need more help, we can refer you to others on and off campus. CFS offers expertise, information and educational materials to assist you in finding the most age-appropriate programs.

Contact DeDe Grogan, Referral Specialist, at DeDe@asu.edu or 480-965-9515 for consultation on:

  • On and Off Campus Child Care
  • Temporary/Emergency/Sick Child Care/Nanny Agencies
  • School-Age Resources (Public and Private)
  • Playgroups/Family Activities
  • Special Needs
  • Tutoring
  • Family Assistance: Financial, Counseling, Support Groups, etc.

Contact Maureen Duane, Program Coordinator Sr., at Maureen.Duane@asu.edu or 480-965-9723
for consultation on:

  • CCAMPIS Child Care Subsidy and CCAMPIS Emergency Fund
  • Campus Children's Center
  • Lactation Services
  • ASU Dependent Care issues and policies
  • Advocacy for Families
  • CFS Advisory Board
  • Elder Care Support Group and referrals
  • Lending Library
Referrals provided by Child and Family Services do not imply an endorsement or recommendation by Arizona State University and Child and Family Services. You are urged to visit the licensing agency to review center records, interview potential caregivers and check references. The Office of Child Care Licensure can be reached at 602-364-2539. This web site also includes searchable facility information.
Choosing Child Care
Choosing a child care center, family home, or in-home provider is often the most difficult decision a family can make. CFS has created the Choosing Child Care document to help families make these decisions, based upon what is most age-appropriate for their child(ren).
  • Choosing Child Care (in MS Word format)

Whatever child care option you have chosen, listed below are sample forms that can not only help to have a successful arrangement, but can also help in those unplanned emergency situations:

  • Caregiver Consent form for Medical Treatment
  • Emergency Information
  • Family Profile
  • Interview Questions
  • Buzz- words to help in a search for a Nanny
  • Sample Contract for a Nanny
