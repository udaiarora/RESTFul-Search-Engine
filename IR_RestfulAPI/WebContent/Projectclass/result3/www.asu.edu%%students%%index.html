SUN DEVIL SPOTLIGHT

ASU students will be able to get a fresh start with with a customizable start page based on technology delivered as part of the Google Apps for Education suite. The new ASU start page, available at http://my.asu.edu/beta, is a richer and more dynamic version of ASU's existing portal, myASU, providing students with interactive tools, announcements, ASU news, and access to course information and interactive services. The new page is part of ASU’s ongoing alliance with Google.
