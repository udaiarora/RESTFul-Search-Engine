Human Resources - New Employee

Parking Decal Information

Parking and Transit Services is located in the Towers building at 525 S. Forest (between Fifth and College Streets) Suite 105. There is metered parking on Forest Street. Call Decal Sales at (480) 965-6124 with questions. Decal Sales Office hours: Monday - Friday 7:30 am to 4:30 pm.

How to obtain a Temporary Parking Permit:

Decal Sales provides new benefits-eligible employees with two weeks of FREE parking in designated lots. This will give you time to make permanent parking arrangements. You may receive a temporary parking permit at your scheduled session of Benefits Eligible New Employee Orientation in the University Services Building, room 1302 or from the Decal Sales office in the Towers Building.

To obtain a temporary parking permit directly from the decal sales office, please bring the following:

  • ASU ID Number (if issued already) or a letter from your department that verifies that you are a new benefits-eligible employee
  • License Plate Number and Make Model of Car
  • Photo Identification

How to obtain a Permanent Parking Decal:

AFTER your data has been entered into the HRMS payroll system and you have an ASU ID number, you can purchase a Permanent Parking Decal at your scheduled session of Benefits-Eligible New Employee Orientation or at the Decal Sales office.  You will need to bring the following:

  • ASU ID number
  • Correct ARIZONA License Plate Information (you must obtain AZ plates)
  • Photo Identification
  • Acceptable Form of Payment (Payroll deduction, Cash, Check, or Credit Card)

You can also pick up information about the Motorist Assistance Program and the Free Local Area Shuttle (FLASH) circulator bus system that services ASU.

Arizona Motor Vehicle Division:
For information about registering your vehicle in Arizona, visit the Arizona Motor Vehicles Division website or call DMV at (602) 225-0072.
