Fall Commencement: Thursday, December 14, 2006, 10:00 am

Please NOTE: Be sure to review this important information regarding traffic delays and road closures in Tempe.

All Graduates:

University Commencement represents the culmination of a student's academic achievement. It is a time of celebration and reflection for students, families, friends, faculty and staff. It brings together a diverse community to share in the joy of accomplished goals.

ASU Spring and Fall Commencement ceremonies take place in Wells Fargo Arena. The doors to the Wells Fargo Arena open one hour prior to the ceremony. Doctoral and Bachelor Degree candidates enter through the lower west ramp. Master Degree candidates and Juris Doctor Candidates enter through the lower east ramp.

The ceremony will start promptly and will run approximately 90 minutes for the Fall ceremony and 2 hours for the Spring. No tickets are required. The event is open to the public.

University commencement and convocation ceremonies are special events for all involved. Please show respect for everyone who has come to share in the experience. Individuals who engage in inappropriate or disruptive behavior may be removed from the event.

For information about disabled seating or parking, please contact the Office of University Ceremonies at (480)965-3565.

If you have any questions, please contact the University Ceremonies Office at (480) 965-3565 or via email at commence@asu.edu.
