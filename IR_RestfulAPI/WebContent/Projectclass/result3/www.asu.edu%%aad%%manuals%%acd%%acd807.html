ACD 807: University Vehicles

Purpose

To provide university vehicles, or reimbursement for operating costs of private vehicles, for use on official university business

Source

Office of the Executive Vice President and Provost of the University

Applicability

Faculty
Academic professionals

Policy

Faculty and academic professionals may be authorized to use a university vehicle, subject to the restrictions noted below:

  1. University vehicles are to be used for authorized university business only.

  2. Every operator of a university vehicle shall have a valid driver’s license issued by the state of Arizona, or a license issued by the state or country in which the operator is a legal resident.

  3. Any faculty member or academic professional who disregards safety regulations while operating a vehicle on university business may be subject to disciplinary action and may incur a financial liability for claims arising from such negligence.

  4. A Motor Pool Request form shall be used when requesting a vehicle from the University Motor Pool. The request must be approved by the department chair or director and routed through the Motor Pool.

  5. All drivers of private vehicles on university business for which operating costs are reimbursed must have a valid certificate of vehicle liability insurance.

  6. Anyone authorized to travel into Mexico must obtain special Mexican automobile insurance through the university risk manager.

Cross-Reference

For information on regulations for persons traveling on official university business, see the Financial Services Policies and Procedures Manual—FIN 500, “Travel.”
