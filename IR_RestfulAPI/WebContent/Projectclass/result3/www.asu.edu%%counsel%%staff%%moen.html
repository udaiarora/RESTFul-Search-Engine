Lisa Moen joined the Office of General Counsel in July 2004. Lisa serves as a Legal Assistant to Associate General Counsel Art Lee. Her duties include legal assistance in commercial and real estate matters, contracts, employment, student affairs and intercollegiate athletics. Prior to joining the Office of General Counsel, Lisa was employed at the Arizona State University Libraries for eight years. She also worked as a legal secretary and legal assistant since 1984. Lisa has an Associates degree in Legal Assistant Studies and a Bachelor of Arts degree.
