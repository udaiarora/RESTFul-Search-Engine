The undergraduate student meets with an academic advisor, who processes a degree audit and reviews it with the student.

The student obtains signatures of approval on the Declaration of Graduation form from the advisor and the dean’s representative, and gives the completed, signed Declaration of Graduation form to the college.

The college forwards the approved Declaration of Graduation to the Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus, which updates the Student Information System (SIS) to show that the Declaration of Graduation has been approved.
