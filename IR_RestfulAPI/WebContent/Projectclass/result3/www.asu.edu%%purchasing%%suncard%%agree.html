If you only want to add funds to a Sun Dollars account you do not need to register with the On-line Card Office. You will need to know the Campus ID number (9-digit ASU ID) and the cardholders name as contained in the Sun Card database (in most cases this will be what is printed on the card). Also note that "First Name" in many cases will actually be first name and middle name or initial.

If you are the cardholder and you want full access to the site you will need to register. As above, you will need to know your Campus ID number (9-digit ASU ID) and your name exactly as it is in the Sun Card database.

Once you complete your transaction be sure to print out the electronic receipt (or save a copy of it). Without this receipt it may be difficult or impossible to trouble-shoot any problems that may occur. Finally, be sure to logout (via the hyperlink on the page) when you have completed your transaction. This will ensure that your information on the site is secure.
