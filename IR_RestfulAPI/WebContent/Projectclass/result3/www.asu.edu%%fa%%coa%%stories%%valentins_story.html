Valentin's Story

Valentin is pursuing a Master of Accountancy and Information Systems through the W.P. Carey School of Business. While an undergraduate here at ASU, he said, "The Barrett Honors College, my study abroad experience and living in the residence hall made my ASU experience very worthwhile.

"The business program improvements and additional funding also really affected my education." Valentin's positive experiences as an undergraduate helped guide his decision to apply to the graduate program and continue his education at ASU.

For several years, Valentin was a START (Student Admissions Relations Team) member, providing general information to prospective students and parents at the General Information desk in the Student Services Building on the Tempe campus. Having immigrated from Bulgaria with his family five years ago, speaking with Valentin reveals his pronounced European accent. "People always ask me where I am from which helps start a conversation. My accent is a great ice breaker. Because I have experienced and been through a lot, I can relate to different personalities and easily find common ground."
