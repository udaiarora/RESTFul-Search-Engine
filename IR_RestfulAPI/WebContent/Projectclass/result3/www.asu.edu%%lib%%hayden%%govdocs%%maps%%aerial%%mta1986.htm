Maricopa County Tax Assessor Photos 1986 Index Map


The map below shows the ASU Libraries' holdings of Maricopa County Tax Assessor Photos for 1986. Photo scales vary: 1:4800 (pink), 1:7200 (blue) and 1:14,400 (yellow). This map was developed in the ASU Libraries' Map Collection using ArcView, a geographic information system software, and data from ALRIS and ESRI. For more information about other Aerial Photos available in the ASU Libraries' Map Collection, visit the Aerial Photos home page.
