Archives & Special Collections


Luhrs Gallery


As a vital outreach service, the Department of Archives and Special Collections produces exhibitions intended for the university community and the public by showcasing archival materials, including historical photographs, manuscripts, and ephemera. Exhibits are drawn from the primary source materials of the department by the Exhibits Curator. Traveling exhibits are available through the Exhibits of Arizona. All exhibits on display are available during regular
library hours.


Additional information on museum and gallery exhibits at the ASU Campus can be viewed at http://www.asu.edu/museums/
