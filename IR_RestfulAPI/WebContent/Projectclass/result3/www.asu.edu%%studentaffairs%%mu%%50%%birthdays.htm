Birthday Parties

Many special events have arisen at Arizona State University because of student, staff, and faculty interest and dedication. One of these events includes the Memorial Union birthday parties. Members of the ASU's community worked together to assemble large-scale celebrations including themed events & activities, an oversized birthday cake for all to share, and many more fun activities.

Past MU birthday parties have included the following themes:
  • 1956 - Memorial Union Grand Opening
  • 1957 - MU 1st Birthday Celebration
  • 1958 - Around the World in 80 Days
  • 1959 - South Pacific
  • 1960 - Alice in Wonderland
  • 1961 - San Francisco
  • 1962 - Seattle World's Fair
  • 1963 - The Sands of Time
  • 1964 - Shakespeare Festival
  • 1965 - New Orleans Mardi Gras
  • 1966 - Festival of the World
  • 1981 - MU 25th Anniversary Celebration
  • 2006 - MU 50th Anniversary Celebration
