Effective: 11/1/2006

The Student Affairs Policies and Procedures Manual (STA) and the Student Enrollment Services Policies and Procedures Manual (SES) have merged. As of November 1, 2006, the material previously found in these manuals is housed in the University Student Initiatives Policies and Procedures Manual (USI).

All SES policies have been converted to the USI prefix, prompting a revised date of 11/1/2006 for all policies. To determine changes to specific policies formerly housed in SES, see the SES MRN. To determine specific new policy numbers, see the SES Table of Contents, which provides links to the corresponding USI policies.

All STA policies have been converted to the USI prefix, and STA 202–02 through 1303 have been renumbered, prompting a revised date of 11/1/2006 for all policies. To determine changes to specific policies formerly housed in STS, see the STA MRN.To determine specific new policy numbers, see the STA Table of Contents, which provides links to the corresponding USI policies.
