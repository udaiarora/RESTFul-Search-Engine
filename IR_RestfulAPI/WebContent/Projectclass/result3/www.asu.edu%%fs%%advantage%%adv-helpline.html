     ADVANTAGE Financial Systems Helpline
    

The Advantage Helpline provides support for the Advantage financial system. The types of support include telephone and e-mail help for questions about the programs, inputting and completing orders, and requesting access and training.

The Helpline staff are available from 8 am until 12 pm and from 1 pm until 5 pm, Monday through Friday. They may be contacted by telephone at (480) 965-2334, by email at AdvantageHelp@asu.edu, or by fax at (480) 965-2625.

When leaving messages by phone or sending e-mails or faxes, please be sure to include your name, phone number, Advantage User ID, and a description of your request or problem. If the question concerns a document, please mention the type of document and the document number, as well as your question or a description of the problem.
