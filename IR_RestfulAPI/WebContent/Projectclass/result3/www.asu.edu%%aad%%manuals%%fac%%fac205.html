FAC 205: Lock Shop/University Key Policy

The primary responsibility for overall security of buildings and grounds rests with the ASU Department of Public Safety. Please see the Department of Public Safety Policies and Procedures Manual— DPS 206, “Building Access Services” for the university key control system policy and procedures.

To order new keys and to have existing locks rekeyed, complete the Key Action Form and deliver it to the ASU Department of Public Safety.

To order desk or cabinet keys, e-mail (FACMAN) or send an interdepartmental memo.
