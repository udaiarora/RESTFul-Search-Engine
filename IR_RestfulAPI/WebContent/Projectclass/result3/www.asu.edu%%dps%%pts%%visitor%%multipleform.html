Reservations may only be made by campus departments for visitors with arrival times between 7am-5pm Monday thru Friday. Reservations are not for ASU Main students, staff, or faculty members. Our office needs to be in receipt of requests two working days prior to the arrival date. Requests for larger groups (more than 20 guests) need to be made at least two weeks prior to the reservation date and should be submitted online.
