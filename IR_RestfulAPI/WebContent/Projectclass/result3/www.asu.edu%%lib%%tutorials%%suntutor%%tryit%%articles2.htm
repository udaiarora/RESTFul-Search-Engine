Try It 2

When you go to a database page, you will see a "Web" button. Click on the button to use the database.

If there is more than one "Web" button, choose the button that best describes where you are -- on campus or off-campus.

If you click on the button for off-campus, you will be asked to enter your ASURITE ID and password. This is your ID that you use to access your university e-mail account.

To do the Try It, click on the appropriate button in the right-hand frame.
