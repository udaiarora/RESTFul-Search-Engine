Faculty Associate Pool

The Department of Languages and Literatures at Arizona State University seeks pool applicants for Faculty Associate in the following languages: Arabic, Chinese, French, German, Ancient Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Latin, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Spanish, Swedish, Thai, Vietnamese.

Required Qualifications:
Masters' Degree is required, or Native or near-native fluency in the respective language and relevant post secondary teaching experience.

Desired Qualifications:
Experience in teaching at the post-secondary level of the language to be taught.

Duties:
Teach Lower and Upper Division courses in their respective languages for 2006-2007 academic year.

Application Deadline:
Ongoing

Application Procedure:
Send letter of intent and current vitae, copies of teaching evaluations or a write-up by a supervisor confirming teaching evaluations, Letter of recommendation must be sent to :

Dr. Joe Cutter, Chair
Department of Languages & Literatures
Arizona State University
PO box 870202
Tempe, AZ 85287-0202

General Information:
One semester contract. Number of courses is contingent upon department needs, funding and sufficient enrollment. Salary is based on degree and teaching experience and will be determined at time of application. Hiring range will be between $712 and $1068 per credit hour.

Arizona State University is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action employer.
