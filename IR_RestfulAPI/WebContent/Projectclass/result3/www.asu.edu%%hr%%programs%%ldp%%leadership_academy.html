The Leadership Academy

Academy Overview

The Leadership Academy is part of the nationally recognized Leadership Development Program at ASU. The Academy meets approximately every other Friday morning during the academic year. The program provides an opportunity to improve leadership competencies, an overview of University operations and related matters (such as the budget process, legislative action, and relationships with the Board of Regents), and an excellent forum for networking. Participants can expect to leave the program with a new network of peers and a significantly increased understanding of University operations.

NOTICE: The Leadership Academy program is currently being redesigned in order to create a more competency-driven program that is in close alignment with the goals of the New American University. We anticipate launching a new Leadership Academy sometime in spring 2007.
