Douglas Sylvester
Associate Professor
Arizona State University College of Law
Access and Ethics Panel


Douglas Sylvester is an associate professor at Arizona State University School of Law. Prior to joining the faculty, Professor Sylvester was an attorney in Baker & McKenzie?s Global E-Commerce practice group specializing in advising emerging and established information technology companies on domestic and international regulatory issues. Professor Sylvester is a former Bigelow Fellow and lecturer-in-law at the University of Chicago and Northwestern University Law Schools. Professor Sylvester has written and spoken extensively on e-commerce, intellectual property, and international law questions.
