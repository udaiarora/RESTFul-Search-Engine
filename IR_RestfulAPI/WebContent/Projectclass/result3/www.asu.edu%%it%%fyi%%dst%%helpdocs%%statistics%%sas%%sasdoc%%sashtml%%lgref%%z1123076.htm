DUR


Returns the modified duration for an enumerated cashflow

Category: Financial

Syntax
Arguments
Details
Examples

Syntax

DUR(y,f,c(1), ... ,c(k))

Arguments

y
the effective per-period yield-to-maturity, expressed as a fraction.
Range: y > 0

f
the frequency of cashflows per period.
Range: f > 0

c(1), ... ,c(k)
a list of cashflows.
