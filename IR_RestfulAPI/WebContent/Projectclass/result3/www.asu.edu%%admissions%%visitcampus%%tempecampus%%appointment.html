Admissions Appointment

What is an Admissions Appointment?
The Talk and Tour is designed to answer most of your general questions about the admissions process and student resources at ASU.

During an admission appointment you will meet with an Undergraduate Admissions Counselor to address any specific questions you may have regarding the admissions process or address any special circumstances you may have.

Please note that an Admissions Counselor is not able to evaluate transcripts. After you are admitted to ASU, you are eligible for academic advising.

Does ASU require Admissions Appointments or Interviews?
No. ASU does not require students to see an Admissions Counselor or attend an interview for admission.

How do I schedule an Admissions Appointment?
After reading this information, if you feel you need to meet with an Admissions Counselor, please call 480.965.7788 (select option 5). Please note, admissions appointments are available on Mondays and Fridays only. Admissions appointments and campus visits are not scheduled on national holidays and/or ASU's academic holidays.
