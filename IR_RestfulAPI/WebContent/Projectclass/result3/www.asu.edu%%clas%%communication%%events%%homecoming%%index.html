HDSHC Welcomes Back Forensic Alumni!

Saturday, October 21

10am - 12:30 pm  ASU Block Party and CLAS Alumni Picnic

Event description:  The CLAS Picnic will be an open house, allowng alumni to attend anytime between

Location:  Old Main Alumni Lawn

Cost: $12/per person for food & drinks.  Purchase your food tickets at

https://clas.asu.edu/alumni/homecoming/2006/Homecomingtickets.aspx or just hang out for free.

 

10am - 12 pm  HDSHC Showcase & Work Session with ASU Forensics Team

Event description:  Directed by ASU alumnus Michael Graupmann, this event will feature performances by current members of the award-winning ASU Forensics team and past alumni, including Eric Dominquez, John Parsi, Emily Seftel, Jeremy Smith, and Ashley Deahl.  The students will provide critiques of current performances of Dramatic Duo, Oral Interpretation of Poetry and After Dinner Speaking.  ASU Forensics boasts a proud reputation of having numerous national champions in each of these events and other CLAS alumni will have the opportunity to witness these outstanding performances as well as to socialize with current and past team members and coaches.

Location:  Old Main Alumni Lawn
Stauffer Hall Building A, Room 412
