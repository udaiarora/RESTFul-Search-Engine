Miles Campos -- Blogger and Sinophile


Miles Campos is a Chinese junior who studied abroad in Beijing, China for the summer of 2006. He was accompanied by several students from ASU as well as U of A and other schools in around the U.S.

The ASU Study Abroad in China program includes 6 weeks of classes in Beijing and 3 weeks travelling all around China.


Miles chose to keep a blog to help him document the things he has done during his time overseas, for memories, and to give family and friends an update on his life while away from home.
