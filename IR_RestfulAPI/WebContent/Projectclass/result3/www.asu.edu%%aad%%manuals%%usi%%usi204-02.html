USI 204–02: Master’s and Doctoral Programs of Study

Purpose

To establish a program of study for graduate students relating to the specific degree requirements for graduate degree completion and graduation

Source

Division of Graduate Studies

Policy

Graduate students working toward a master’s or doctoral degree must file an official Master’s Degree Program of Study form or a Doctoral Program of Study form, as appropriate, at the earliest possible date after admission with regular status to a specific graduate degree program.

Courses and requirements on the program of study are subject to approval by the graduate student’s supervisory committee, department chair, and the Division of Graduate Studies.

The graduate student must file a Petition to the Division of Graduate Studies if any changes to the program are necessary.

ProceduresD

Filing a Master’s or Doctoral Program of Study

Responsibility
Action
Graduate student
  1. Complete and submit to the department the master’s or doctoral Interactive Program of Study (iPOS) online at ASU Interactive.

  1. If assistance is needed, talk with the committee chair, committee members, or graduate coordinator.

  2. Make any necessary revisions and resubmit if necessary.

  3. Verify that the iPOS meets the specified requirements and submit it to the Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus.
  4. Help students complete any necessary revisions, online course changes, or petitions.
Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus
  1. Verify that the iPOS meets the requirements specified in the ASU Graduate Catalog.

  2. Reroute the iPOS to the department for revisions when necessary.

  3. Submit the student’s iPOS to the Division of Graduate Studies once it has been verified.

  4. Process online petitions or course changes for the student when needed.
Division of Graduate Studies
  1. Approve or disapprove the program.

  2. Return the approved program of study form to the Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus for processing.

  3. Mail a copy of the approved program of study to the student.

  4. File the approved program of study.

Filing a Petition to the Division of Graduate Studies

Responsibility
Action
Graduate student
  1. Use online format to file course changes and petitions, and submit them to the department for approval.

  1. If assistance is needed, check with the committee chair, committee members, or graduate coordinator.
Division of Graduate Studies, Graduation Section, or Records & Registration at the West campus
  1. Verify that the petition adjusts the program of study to meet the requirements specified in the ASU Graduate Catalog.

Division of Graduate Studies
  1. Forward the original of the petition adjusting the program of study to the Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus.
Graduation Section or Records & Registration at the West campus
  1. Process online petitions and course changes once submitted or reroute them to the department for revisions if needed.
