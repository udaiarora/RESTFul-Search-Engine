PCS 502: Transferring Equipment between Departments at Zero Cost

Purpose

To keep track of capital equipment transferred between university departments

Source

Property Control

Policy

Individuals desiring to transfer capital equipment between departments must prepare a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form. For large quantity transfers, Property Control may authorize an attachment to the PRA listing the capital equipment being transferred. Alternately, department personnel may use the online PRA form to transfer equipment. For more information, contact Property Control at property-q@asu.edu.

Note:This policy is not related to Internal Purchase Orders.

ProcedureD

Responsibility
Action
Department relinquishing equipment
  1. Complete a Property Relocation Authorization (PRA) form (see PCS 501A), recording the ASU property control number (ASU PC#), if one exists. Obtain the approval of both the releasing and receiving departments.

    Note:Forms are available in Property Control.

  2. Send the PRA to Property Control.
If the Department of Facilities Management movers are not needed:
Property Control
  1. Update Property Control records to reflect the new custodial unit, location, and other pertinent data.
If the Department of Facilities Management movers are needed:
  1. Retain the Property Control copy for departmental files and forward the original and the remaining copies to the Department of Facilities Management.
Department of Facilities Management
  1. Verify that the numbers are on the PRA or write them on the original.
If ASU PC#s are affixed to the equipment:
  1. Relocate the equipment for the requesting department.

  2. Sign the PRA upon completion of the move.

  3. Forward the original to Property Control.
Property Control
  1. Match the original with the copy retained in step 4. Review the original and the copy for changes and reconcile discrepancies with the Department of Facilities Management.

  2. Update Property Control records to reflect the new custodial unit, location, and other pertinent data.
endtable
