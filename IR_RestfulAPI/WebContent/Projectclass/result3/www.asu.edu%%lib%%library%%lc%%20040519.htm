Minutes of Meeting

 

May 19, 2004
Hayden C6A

 

A quorum was present.

I. Approval of Minutes (April 21, 2004)


– Minutes were approved.


II. Chair & Executive Committee's Report- Scott Herrington


The Chair offered remarks about the past year, especially with regard to the AP Task Force work. Committee members were thanked for their service.

III. Dean’s Report –Sherrie Schmidt


The following announcements were made:

1. Mimmo Bonanni was awarded continuing appointment and promotion.

2. The Dean thanked all who participated in the first candidate’s interview for Associate Dean for Library Services. The second candidate will be on campus on Thursday and Friday.

3. The Dean spoke to the place of the University Libraries in One University, Many Places. The libraries for each campus will be staffed independently (i.e. not with Tempe campus staff). The Provosts for each campus will be critical to decisions regarding the libraries and service levels will be defined by each campus. However, services should be optimized with no duplication from campus to campus. Porgrams moving to different campuses may have their materials moved. The Dean mentioned that the University Provost is supportive of hiring an Associate Dean for Collections.

IV. Committee Reports

a.       Personnel Committee – Brad Vogus

     Work is being done to revise the Orientation document. A transition meeting will be held on July 15.

b.       Research and Professional Development Committee – Sue Hunter

     The committee is continuing to allocate travel funds. Jenny Duvernay will be taking over the committee work when Sue leaves.

c.    Committee on Academic Professional Status – Sue Hunter 

     Dan Stanton will be the CAP’s representative next year.

d.    Nominations and Elections Committee- Phil Konomos

     Election results have been distributed. Winners were congratulated.

V. Old Business

The second reading of the Sabbatical Leave procedures (I.1) was done. Discussion ensued. Two revisions to the text were made. The amended document was approved.

VI. New Business

Deborah Koshinsky proposed a resolution for consideration in honor of the retirees. The resolution was accepted. The resolution reads as follows:

A RESOLUTION

 

IN HONOR OF:

 

George Bell              

Vladimir Borovansky   

Sandra Bray            

Jane Conrow             

Diane Moore             

Lois Schneberger      

Helen Seaton

 

 

WHEREAS, these seven librarians have served the University Libraries at Arizona State University for a combined total of almost 180 years; and

WHEREAS, these seven librarians have contributed to the growth and development of the University Libraries in substantial and dynamic ways; and

WHEREAS, these seven librarians have served, with devotion and skill, on many committees, both within the University Libraries and in the University at large; and

 

WHEREAS, there can be no doubt that the University Libraries and Arizona State University have benefited greatly from their considerable service.

 

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Academic Professionals of the Arizona State University Libraries:

 

THANK George, Vladimir, Sandra, Jane, Diane, Lois, and Helen for their dedication, commitment, and service to the library profession.        

           

Passed by acclamation, May 19, 2004

Librarians’ Council, University Libraries, Arizona State University

 

Meeting adjourned 3:40pm.
