Process

Whether a unit needs communication materials or website development from scratch, redesign, or updated content, we generally follow the same basic steps to ensure that both the team and the client agree on and understand the scope and direction of the project. The most successful projects are completed with equal collaboration, communication and attention to deadlines by both the team and the client.

The steps listed here represent the steps of a successfully completed project.  

For more information, contact our project manager.

Process Steps
Initial Contact/Communication
Design Brief (filled out by client)
Project Plan written (team)
Review/Feedback (client)
Initial Comps/Sketches (team)
Review/Feedback (client)
Revisions to Comps (optional)
Review/Feedback (client)
Converstion to HTML/Build Templates
Content Miagration
Review/Feedback (client)
Go Live
Index and Setup Google Keymatches
Closing Feeback/Evalution
