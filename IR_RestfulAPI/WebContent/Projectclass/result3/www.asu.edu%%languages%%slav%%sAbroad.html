There are many opportunities for study in Slavic countries through various universities and academic institutions. What follows is a list of several programs that we feel merit further inspection. As with any major investment of time or money, we would like to stress the importance of careful planning and thorough research to those who wish to persue Slavic studies oversees.

The University of Arizona also offers several popular Study Abroad programs for Russian and Slavic language students.
