Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

June 6, 2001

Present: Carol Behl, Maggie Mangini, Annie Platoff, Helen Seaton, and Linda Shackle.

The CAPS Committee met at 10:00 a.m. in Room 214 (Gila) of the Memorial Union. Maggie conducted the meeting in the absence of Santos Vega. Two new members, Carol and Annie, were introduced.

The minutes of the May 2 meeting were read and approved.

The members for next year are: Continuing - Stefan Myhajlenko, Linda Shackle, and Santos Vega. New - Carol Behl who will be representing the Academic Associates, Pat Feldman, and Annie Platoff.

After consultation between Santos Vega, Chair of CAPS, and Darby Shaw, Pat Feldman was appointed as the 6th member of the committee. The CAPS members present discussed and approved Pat's appointment to the committee. It is our understanding that Pat will be serving a two-year term since she is filling out the remainder of Victor Becerra's term. Helen Seaton will send Pat a copy of the minutes and point out the two-year term so that there is no confusion.

Santos has had no response yet from Dr. Hackett about the letter concerning voting rights for Academic Associates. At the August 1 meeting, it should be noted that this matter is still pending.

Another matter that is pending is the professional development workshop for career advancement or for moving up in the ranks for APs which this committee discussed for the current year but did not accomplish.

Carol Behl announced that she has moved from the Biology Department to a new position at Computer Science Engineering.

Helen Seaton
Secretary
