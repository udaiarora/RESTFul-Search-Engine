A vibrant campus is emerging in the heart of Phoenix, bringing to the city an essential element that promises to ultimately rival the urban campuses of metropolitan cities worldwide.

ASU at the Downtown Phoenix campus is the newest element in a decades-long urban renaissance that adheres to the tradition that built a great American city.

Just as Phoenix itself was built on the foundations of the ancient Hohokham civilization, a new downtown is emerging in an urban center that already features such cultural gems as the Orpheum and Herberger Theaters, the new Phoenix Convention Center and such major arenas for sports as the U.S. Airways Center and Chase Field.

As rich as Downtown Phoenix is with opportunities for culture, growth and excitement, the new ASU campus will add its own blend of learning, research and student life to this mixture, elements that will create a 24-hour vibrancy within the city's urban core.
