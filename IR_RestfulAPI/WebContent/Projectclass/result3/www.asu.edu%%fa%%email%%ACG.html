Academic Competitiveness Grant (ACG) - Act Today!

This is an electronic announcement, please do not reply to this e-mail notice. If you have problems viewing this page visit www.asu.edu/fa/email.

Dear ASU Student,

ASU has received information from your Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) indicating that you may meet the criteria for the Academic Competitiveness Grant (ACG), one of our new aid programs.

In order to award this grant, we must verify that you completed a rigorous program of study in high school. To obtain the documentation we need, please provide us with one of the following:

  • An official copy of your final high school transcript that shows all coursework and grades completed through graduation.
    OR
  • Have your high school complete a High School Verification of Rigorous Coursework form which states that you successfully completed the rigorous coursework requirement. For home-schooled students the letter must be notarized and their parent(s) are the certifying authority.

The form or official final transcript must be submitted to our office at the following address in an envelope sealed by your high school:

ASU Student Financial Assistance
ACG Coordinator
PO Box 870412
Tempe, AZ 85287-0412

We cannot award this grant if we do not receive one of the requested items.

Please call or visit one of our locations if you have questions concerning this grant or other financial aid matters.
