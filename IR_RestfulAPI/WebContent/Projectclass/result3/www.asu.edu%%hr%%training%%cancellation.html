Cancellation Policy

Don't be a No-Show! Because most of the learning opportunities have waiting lists, please cancel as soon as you know you will be unable to attend. Even a last minute cancellation is better than a no-show. (All cancellations within 24 hours of class start time must be made by telephone.) We can often get someone in from the waitlist on very short notice.
