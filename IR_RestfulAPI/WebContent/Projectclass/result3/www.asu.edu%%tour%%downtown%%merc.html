Mercado A-F

Known previously as the ASU Downtown Center, the Mercado accommodates University College’s extended education and distance learning programs, the College of Nursing’s simulation labs, the College of Public Programs’ advanced public executive program and the offices of various ASU organizations and initiatives such as the Stardust Center for Affordable Homes and the Family, Arizona Prevention Resources Center, Office of Youth Preparation and Building Great Communities.  The Mercado, located between Van Buren and Monroe Streets and Seventh and Fifth Streets, is comprised of six Spanish-style buildings accommodating a variety of ASU classrooms and offices.  Some space is leased to local businesses and government agencies.
