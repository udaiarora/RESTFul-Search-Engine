Blackberry

BlackBerry® offers leading wireless solutions, providing access to a wide range of applications on a variety of BlackBerry devices, as well as BlackBerry enabled devices, around the world.

Blackberry Licenses

System: Server

Availability: See setup instructions below

Restrictions: Faculty & Staff only

Form: Blackberry Site License

Setting Up Blackberry Service

  • You MUST obtain your Blackberry and obtain at LEAST a data service. Verify with your provider that this data service CAN be used with a Blackberry Enterprise Server.
  • Activate your device on the Wireless Network (and check to be sure it's working). Your carrier will provide instructions for this process when you purchase the plan and/or device. Verizon, for example, requires you to dial *228 and follow the instructions. This is required even if you have opted out of phone service on the device.
  • If you already have a device, you must be sure that you are running at least Blackberry Software v4.0 or 2.7 (for Java and C++ Devices) on the handheld.
  • You will need an Exchange E-Mail Account.
  • You will need to purchase a Blackberry Enterprise Server Client Access License. The cost is $75, the vendor code for this purchase is: COMPUTING. Complete the Blackberry License form and fax to 965-5171 ATTN: Patti Dawson.
  • When you have purchased your Client Access License from the IT Help Desk, you will receive an e-mail with your wireless activation password once your account has been created on the Blackberry Server.
