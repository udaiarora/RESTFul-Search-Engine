Committee on Academic Professional Status (CAPS)

Minutes of Meeting

December 4, 1997

Present: Karl Weiss, Gary Hembree, Ken Furuta, Molly McCartney, Tom Keating, Annette Voth

1. The Minutes were accepted as amended.

2. The welcome packet letter will be sent from CAPS, not by the Provost's Office.

3. Annette reported on the meeting with the Vice-Provost and Barbara Mawhiney about the inclusion of postdoctoral positions with APs. They noted that ABOR set a limited number of personnel categories and that they did not have much flexibility.

They also provided a printout of all APs on campus. That list does not include Deans, however.

In the discussion about the welcome letter the feeling was against including it in the Provost's packet.

4. Annette reported on the Academic Senate. There was no update on ACD 506-09.

5. Karl reported on the Committee of Committees. There are six candidates for CAPS.

6. The Meeting scheduled for December 17th was cancelled unless otherwise notified.

Submitted by Ken Furuta
