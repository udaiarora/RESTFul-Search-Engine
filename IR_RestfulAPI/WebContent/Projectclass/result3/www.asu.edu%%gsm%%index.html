ASU Graphic Standards

Almost every university in the country has adopted a "brand" much like most of corporate America.  Arizona State University has been ahead ov the curve as one of the first universities with online graphic standards and later with the development of the ASU Communication Guide, incorporating tools and resources with the standards to better support ASU's communication professionals.

The standards are in place to ensure quality and consistency across all units and campuses that develop ASU web, electronic and print communications.
