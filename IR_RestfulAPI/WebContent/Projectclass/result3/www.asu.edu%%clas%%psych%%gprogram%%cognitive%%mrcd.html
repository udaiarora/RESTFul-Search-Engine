Memory, Reasoning, & Cognitive Development (MRCD) Lab


People in the lab:



  • Susan Somerville, Ph.D.
  • Stanley Parkinson, Ph.D.
  • Suzie Khalil
Lab facilities:

  • The MRCD comprises a room, fitted with one-way mirrors located withing the Child Study Laboratory, suitable for testing memory, reasoning and spatial skills in children; and other laboratory space for testing adults.

Current projects:

  • Representation of objects in photographs and drawings
  • Short-term longitudinal study of children's acquisition of expertise
