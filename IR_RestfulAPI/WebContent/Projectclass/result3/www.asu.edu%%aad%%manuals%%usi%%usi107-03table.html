The ASU University Technology Office assigns an @asu.edu e-mail at the point of the student’s application for admission to ASU.

The student:

  1. either activates the ASU mailbox and begins using the assigned @asu.edu e-mail address or goes to the electronic post office and forwards @asu.edu e-mail to a preferred e-mail address (such as @hotmail.com, @aol.com, etc.)

    Note: Having e-mail redirected does not change the individual’s responsibility for reading and responding to official communications sent to the ASU e-mail address. Information and warnings about forwarding are available at The Mailbox on ASU’s Information Technology Web site.

  2. reads ASU e-mail policies at The Mailbox

  3. updates the forwarding order as needed

    and

  4. retrieves e-mail messages regularly from the @asu.edu address and acts upon the messages appropriately.

If the student wishes to prevent the release of the @asu.edu e-mail address, in addition to other directory information, he or she should obtain, complete, and submit a Withhold Directory Information Request form, which is available online.
