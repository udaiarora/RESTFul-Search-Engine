Process for Applying to Statistics Certificate Program

 

If you are not presently admitted to the ASU Graduate College:

 

  1. Fill out an application for nondegree admission on the ASU Graduate College website at the address

http://www.asu.edu/graduate

 

  1. Download the application from the Committee on Statistics on the website at the address

 

http://www.asu.edu/graduate/statistics

 

  1. Fill out this application and mail with a copy of all your undergraduate and graduate transcripts to:

 

Dr. Douglas Montgomery, Co-Director Committee on Statistics

Department of Industrial Engineering

Arizona State University

Tempe, AZ  85287-5906

(480) 965-3836

 

 

 

 

If you are presently admitted to the ASU Graduate College:

 

  1. Download and fill out the application for the Committee on Statistics on the website at the address

http://www.asu.edu/graduate/statistics

 

 

  1. Mail your complete application to:

 

Dr. Douglas Montgomery, Co-Director Committee on Statistics

Department of Industrial Engineering

Arizona State University

Tempe, AZ  85287-5906

(480) 965-3836

 

 

If you have any questions, send them to statistics@asu.edu or phone 480-965-2671.
