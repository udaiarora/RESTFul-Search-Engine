Schedule of Classes

The online Schedule of Classes allows students to search for classes by using several selection criteria, including campus on which course is offered, course prefix/number, and course prefix and range of numbers. The selection can be for open classes only or all available classes. Additionally, some alternate formats for viewing class information and the published Schedule of Classes are provided, including PDF formats of the entire published Schedule of Classes and a list of all open sections, grouped by college, which gets updated nightly.


Extended Education Schedule

The School of Extended Education provides a searchable online schedule of classes and programs held throughout the Valley. Credit classes, degree programs, and professional continuing education programs are offered days, evenings, and weekends at off-campus sites, via the Internet and television, and by independent learning.
