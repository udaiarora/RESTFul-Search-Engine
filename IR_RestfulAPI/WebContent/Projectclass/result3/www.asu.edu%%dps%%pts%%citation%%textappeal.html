Parking Citation Appeal
Arizona State University
PCAB Policy: A decision of the hearing officer may be appealed to the Parking Citation Appeals Board. The Board is composed of representatives from the faculty, staff, and student body. Appeals to the Board must be filed at the Parking Appeals Office within fourteen (14) calendar days after the rendition of the Hearing Officer?s decision. Receipt for payment from the University Cashier for the indicated amount must be presented to the Parking Services Officer before an appeal to the Board may be filed. Refunds will be made following a favorable decision by the Board. For further information, contact the PCAB Secretary at 965-6808.
