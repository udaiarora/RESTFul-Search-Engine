International Students

To help you get information and answers to your questions about ASU, please use these tips and resources.

For Admission Information and Answers to Questions:

  • Please read the admission requirements and application procedures for international students.
  • Please refer to the most frequently asked questions for international students online for answers to commonly asked questions.
  • Ask a question online.
  • Check your admission status online by using ASU Interactive. (Please note, you must have your ASURITE UserID and password to check your admission status.)

For Application for Admission and ASU Materials:

  • Apply online or download/print the application for admission for international students.
  • Request ASU materials online (materials will be mailed to you).
