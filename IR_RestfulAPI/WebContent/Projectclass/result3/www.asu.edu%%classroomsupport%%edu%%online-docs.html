Classroom Equipment Guides & Online Tutorials

To help faculty and others become more familiar with the media equipment available in ASU classrooms, Classroom Support provides online classroom equipment guides for viewing and downloading in PDF format. Along with the equipment guides, we provide a Flash tutorial on using the Mediated Teaching Station (MTS) in the Brickyard and Coor Hall classrooms, as well as the AMX Control Panel in a few classrooms. Please click on one of the icons below to view either the PDF or Flash tutorial for your classroom.

If you have any questions regarding a tutorial or would like hands-on training, please contact us at 965-3342 or by email.
