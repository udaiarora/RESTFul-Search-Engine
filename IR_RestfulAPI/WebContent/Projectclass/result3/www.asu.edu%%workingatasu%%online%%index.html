Online Services

Class Roster and Grade Reporting

Rostergrades [http://www.asu.edu/rgapp]

Also known as “Rostergrades,” this online application enables faculty to be completely self-sufficient in obtaining their class rosters and in submitting early warnings, mid-term deficiencies, and final grades.   Support is provided by Roster Contacts who are found in each academic department and school and who are thoroughly trained on the application.

Using Class Roster and Grade Reporting

Information is available at this web site on the following topics:

  • Instructions for faculty
  • List of Roster Contacts (by campus, college, and department/school)
  • FAQ’s
  • Help for Roster Contacts

Looking for the old Class Roster Reporting System?

Because it was in an older, proprietary software that the university no longer supports, the old roster system is no longer available.
