Special Course-Related Notes:
Do not mark any catalog drafts for course changes. E-mail Katty McIntosh with all changes in omnibus courses, teaching method, or semester offered, along with an effective date. All other changes require official approval and must be submitted through ACRES.

You can view the most recent approved courses in the database.

A PDF draft of all catalog courses (pending and approved), updated periodically, is also available.

What’s in this site? This site contains useful instructions and other information for adding, changing, and deleting courses electronically using ACRES. Instructions, schedules, handouts, and other information for designated catalog coordinators and their assistants to use in revising the general and graduate catalogs on schedule can be found at the Catalog Coordinator Seminar Site.
