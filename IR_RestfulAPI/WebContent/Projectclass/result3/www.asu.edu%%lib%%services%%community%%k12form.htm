Request for K-12 Group Tours


Due to ASU student usage, tours of the University Libraries are not available during the first four and last four weeks (including finals week) of the Fall and Spring semesters. Tours are also available during Intersessions, Summer Sessions, and Spring Break. (See the ASU Academic Calendar). Before submitting a request for a tour, please read the School Group Visits page.
