Community Outreach

  • ASU in the Community Website - www.asu.edu/community
    Arizona State University is making a difference in the lives of community members every day. Hundreds of outreach programs provide needed educational, cultural, legal, and health care services throughout the state, making ASU an integral part of the community.  

    ASU in the Community is an online resource of ASU’s community outreach programs and activities. This site demonstrates ASU’s commitment to the region as a New American University, while also serving as a tool for identifying specific programs and linking to a network of people engaged in the community.
