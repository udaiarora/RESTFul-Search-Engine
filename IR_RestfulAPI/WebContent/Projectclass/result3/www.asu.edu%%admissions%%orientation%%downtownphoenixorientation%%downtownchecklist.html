Program Checklist - ASU Orientation 2006

Print Tip: For best results, print in landscape format.

This page provides details about ASU Orientation for students enrolled beginning fall 2006 in degree programs offered at ASU's Downtown Phoenix campus.

Review this checklist and complete the necessary steps prior to attending your ASU Orientation. If you have questions, contact New Student Orientation by calling 480.965.2880 or via e-mail at . Please print this checklist, as well as your online reservation confirmation, and bring them with you to your ASU Orientation program.

Pre-Orientation Homework

You must complete the Pre-Orientation Homework before attending ASU Orientation, including the online Math Placement Test and all other placement tests, as appropriate.

TOP

Submit MMR Immunization Form

You must have verification of two MMR (measles [rubeola] /mumps/rubella) immunizations on record at ASU, or you cannot register for classes. Freshmen should complete this step before attending ASU Orientation.

Use the ASU Immunization Form that ASU sends to you with your admission certificate or print the ASU Immunization Form.

To verify that ASU has received and recorded your immunization documentation before you attend ASU Orientation, go to ASU Interactive, log in, and view your ASU Orientation Summary. Please be aware that it takes approximately two days from the time ASU receives your documentation to record it in the system. Therefore, you must send/fax your MMR documentation well in advance of your scheduled Orientation program.

What to Bring to Orientation

  • Photo ID.
  • There is a $25 fee for an ASU photo ID, please bring check or cash to Orientation.
  • Print-out of your program confirmation (obtained from the online confirmation when you register).
  • Print-out of your completed Pre-Orientation Homework.
  • Print-out of parking and directions for your program location.
  • Print-out of your Unified Math Placement Exam Score.
  • A copy of this checklist.
  • Sweatshirt or light jacket (Program rooms are air conditioned).
  • Backpack (for carrying items you collect throughout day).
  • If you have completed or are currently enrolled in college courses, bring a copy of your enrollment or a transcript for your ASU academic advisor to review.

TOP

What's Next?

Please review and complete important next steps to ensure your smooth transition to life at ASU. Many of these important tasks can, and should, be completed as soon as possible!

  • Apply for on-campus housing.
  • Apply for financial aid and scholarships.
  • Pay tuition and fees.
  • Submit final official transcript to ASU.
  • Attend a Welcome Program in August 2006. (Look for this year's details and reservation information in July on the web and in the mail.)
  • Begin classes in August 2006.

TOP

Program Payment, Cancellations or Changes, and Special Needs

Program payment: Students requesting the check payment option have 10 business days to remit their payment to New Student Orientation.

Cancellations or Changes:

  • If you would like to change your program date or cancel your ASU Orientation session, you must notify New Student Orienation in writing (by mail, fax or online) so that the cancellation or Orientation Change Request is received at least five business days before your scheduled Orientation date.
  • Each change to your program date or cancellation will be assessed a $15 processing fee.
  • A $15 processing fee will be charged for all returned checks and denied credit cards.
  • If you wish to add a guest or substitute a guest, you must submit your Orientation Change Request in writing or online.
  • Any Orientation refund requests will be processed in July 2006 upon conclusion of the ASU Orientation season with required documentation. Please note that in order to receive a refund for a cancellation you must submit your request in writing (by mail, fax or online) 5 business days before the date of the program you wish to cancel.
  • In order to provide you with the appropriate academic advising, it is important that we have the correct information about the major you have chosen. If the major displayed in your reservation confirmation is incorrect, or has changed, please contact New Student Orientation or submit an Orientation Change Request to update your major's information at least five business days before your scheduled ASU Orientation program. Note: Changes to your major cannot be made during your Orientation session. Requests for changes to the major are updated every 24 hours. If you have not already made your Orientation reservation, please contact New Student Orientation to change your major before making a reservation.

Special Needs: If you have special needs call 480.965.2880 or e-mail at least 10 business days before your scheduled program. Special needs include: disabled parking, assistance with tours, language interpretation and/or a special meal.
