Wireless Networking Now Available at Hayden


(posted 12/8/03)

Hayden Library’s 24-hour study area (room C-55) is now equipped for wireless access! The system’s range extends far beyond the room itself so that laptops can be used both outside and inside in areas adjacent to C-55. If you are considering using this new service, an IEEE 802.11b or an IEEE 802.11b-g compatible wireless network card is required. These cards can be purchased relatively inexpensivley through the bookstore. For a review of various network cards or for recommendations on selecting a card, visit IT’s Network Card Review page at http://asu.edu/comm/wlan/review.html. Other important information about getting connected can be found at http://www.asu.edu/it/fyi/comm/wlan/connect.html.
