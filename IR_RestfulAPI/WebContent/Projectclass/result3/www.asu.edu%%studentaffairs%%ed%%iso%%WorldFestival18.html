18th Annual World Festival

ASU’s 18th annual World Festival will celebrate the heritage and traditions of countries and cultures from around the world, Feb. 23 from 10:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. on the Hayden Library Lawn. Student organizations will have booths selling food, providing information and displaying crafts and artifacts from their home countries.


Professional and student group entertainers will perform on a stage from 11:30 a.m. until 1 p.m., sharing music from their cultures. ASU is home to about 3,000 international students from almost 150 countries.


The event is free and open to the public. Paid parking is available at meters and in designated visitor lots. Sponsors are the ASU International Student Office and Educational Development. For more information, call 480-965-7451 or e-mail iso@asu.edu.
