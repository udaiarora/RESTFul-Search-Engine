Presidio MBA Students Launch DriveNeutral to Offset Greenhouse Gas Emissions
Business Wire via Yahoo! Finance
For about the cost of a tank of gas, drivers can now do their part to fight global warming. Through the DriveNeutral program created by enterprising MBA students at Presidio School of Management, individuals can purchase a decal certifying that they have neutralized the impact of their vehicles' greenhouse gas emissions for an entire year.
