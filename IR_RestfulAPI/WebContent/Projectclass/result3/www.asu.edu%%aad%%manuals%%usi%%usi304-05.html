USI 304–05: Student Job Vacancy

Purpose

To establish job posting requirements that ensure equal opportunity and nondiscrimination for all interested students

Source

Student Financial Assistance Office/Student Employment

Policy

Public posting of student job vacancies is required by the university’s policy on nondiscrimination and equal employment opportunity. All student jobs are posted on the Student Employment Web site. All jobs must be posted:

  1. for a minimum of two working days

    or

  2. until the employer has interviewed three applicants for the posted position.

When one of these two requirements has been met, the employer may offer the job to the student.

Student Employment position posting requirements must be met before any commitment to hire is made. The purpose of posting requirements is to ensure fair, equal, and nondiscriminatory employment opportunity for all interested students.

Posting may be waived in a rehire situation only if:

  1. the former employee returns to the same job and department within the same semester

    or

  2. the student is continuing in the same job from fall to spring, spring to summer, spring to fall, or summer to fall.
