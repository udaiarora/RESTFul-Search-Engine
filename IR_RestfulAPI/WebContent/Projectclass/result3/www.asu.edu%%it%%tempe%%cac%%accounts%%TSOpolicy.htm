Academic TSO Data Retention Policy

Academic MVS/TSO UserIDs created after 1 March 2000 are subject to the same retention policy as ASURITE UserIDs. As such, your Academic MVS/TSO UserID remains active only while you are affiliated with the university. When your affiliation ends, the UserID is suspended (inactivated); however, the UserID and its data files continue to exist for an additional eight months. If you remain continuously unaffiliated for eight months, the UserID is removed and its data files are deleted.

If you re-establish an affiliation with the university before the UserID and data files are deleted, it is your responsibility to contact the IT Help Desk to reactivate the account.

Note: The Academic MVS/TSO UserID and the ASURITE UserID are two separate entities, even if they have the same value. You must inform the IT Help Desk of both UserIDs when you re-establish affiliation; otherwise you may not be able to access your data on MVS/TSO.

You are encouraged to retain adequate backup copies of your files at all times. Before ending your affiliation with the university, copy all your data files to some form of removable media and/or transfer the files to another system for which you will have continued access. Once the UserID is suspended you no longer have access to the data files thereon. Eight months after affiliation ends, all data files are removed from the system and cannot be recovered. If you need access to the data files after affiliation ends but before the files are deleted, you can contact the IT Help Desk for temporary access to the files for purposes of making backup copies.
