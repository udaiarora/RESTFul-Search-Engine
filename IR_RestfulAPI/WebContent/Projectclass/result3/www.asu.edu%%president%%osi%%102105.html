Prescott College to Offer PH.D in Education with Sustainability Focus

EFS West

Beginning this fall, Prescott College will offer a Doctor of Philosophy in education, with a focus in sustainability education.  The four year, low residency program centers on self-directed independent study, offering students flexibility in designing their own studies within the areas of Sustainability Education.  The College's philosophy stresses experiential learning and self-direction within an interdisciplinary curriculum.
