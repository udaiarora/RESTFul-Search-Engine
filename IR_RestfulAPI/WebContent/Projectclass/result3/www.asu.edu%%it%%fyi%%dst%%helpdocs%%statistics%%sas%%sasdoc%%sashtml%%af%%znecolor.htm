Chart Control: outlineColor


the outline color for chart elements

Type: Character
Valid Values:
Editor: sashelp.classes.coloreditor.frame

Details

Details

Bars are outlined when the viewMode=2D and chartStyle=Filled. Bars are also outlined when viewMode=3D or 3DPerspective and you supply a list of colors.
