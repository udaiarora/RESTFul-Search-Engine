Human Resources - Professional Development

Clayton Taylor

Clayton Taylor is a customer contact center operations and program/project management professional with Fortune 500 company experience.  He is an experienced team and project leader who is skilled at coaching, developing and motivating work teams to achieve high productivity and quality goals.  He has a proven track record of managing multiple projects from concept to closeout, as well as designing and implementing innovative processes which have enabled organizations to grow and improve profitably.  Clayton is a Six Sigma Black Belt with two field delployments.

Clayton joined the Learning and Performance Solutions Group as a Senior Program Coordinator in December 2004.  He is responsible for coordinating the Supervisory Academy, strengthening the new-hire orientation program, and working on various additional initiatives for the Office of Human Resources.  Clayton has a Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Phoenix.
