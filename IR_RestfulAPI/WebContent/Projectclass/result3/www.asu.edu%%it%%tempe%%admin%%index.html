Admin Computing

  • Advantage

    • The Financial Services Office provides training on the ADVANTAGE financial accounting system used by Arizona State University faculty and staff.

  • Affiliate

    • The Affiliate Management System is designed to collect and update name, address, and other demographic information about any person or organization doing business with ASU. The System stores this information in the Affiliate database. ASU production systems and departmental applications may interface with the database to update and/or obtain this data.

  • Benefactor (ASU IP only)

    • Benefactor database system is maintained by Information Resources Management. Information Resources Management supports the information and computing resources of Public Affairs.

  • COEUS

    • Coeus is a computer system that stores information about proposals, awards, sponsors, and more. This information can be searched and sorted in many useful ways. It is all maintained in a centralized database at ASU, which Coeus accesses over a network. It can also be used to generate documents like award notices and cover letters, obtain approvals electronically, and even send proposals directly to a sponsor.

  • DARS

    • ASU Degree Audit Reporting System (DARS) is maintained by Division of Undergraduate Academic Services.

  • Data Warehouse

    • The Data Warehouse is a read only database containing integrated student, financial, and employee data for use in decision support. Data Administration is responsible for developing policies, standards, definitions, and conventions governing the creation and use of enterprise administrative data. Enterprise administrative data are data needed by the university for reporting, management analysis, and essential operating activities.

  • HRMS

    • The Human Resources Management System (HRMS), handled by Human Resources, is a mainframe computer system used to record all personnel actions.

  • ProSAM

    • ProSAM software is used by financial aid departments throughout ASU, and is supported by Information Technology. Software is available for download at the Software Licensing Web site.

  • Rosters

    • Also known as â??Rostergrades,â?? this online application enables faculty to be completely self-sufficient in obtaining their class rosters and in submitting early warnings, mid-term deficiencies, and final grades. Support is provided by Roster Contacts who are found in each academic department and school and who are thoroughly trained on the application.

  • SAAR

    • Student Accounts and Accounts Recievable (SAAR) system is maintained by Information Technology and Student Business Services. Software is available for download at the Software Licensing Web site.

  • Sybase 12

    • Connection software Sybase 12 is necessary for using ASU Data Warehouse, provided by Information Technology. Software is available for download at the Software Licensing Web site.
