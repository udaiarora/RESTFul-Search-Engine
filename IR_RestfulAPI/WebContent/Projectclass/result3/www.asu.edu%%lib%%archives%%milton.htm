Dawn of a New Day

Milton Snow Photograph Collection


Milton Snow worked for the United States Indian Service at Window Rock. His professional photographs were used for publications by the U.S. Office of Indian Affairs in such publications as Along the Beale Trail: A Photographic Account of Wasted Range Land. The photographs on exhibition are of a personal and artistic nature, at the same time they are historical documentation. Milton captured the essence of the Navajo culture through images of the weaver, the silversmith and the sheep herders. The collection of photographs was produced in collaboration with Hazel Dreis, portfolio design with lettering by Archie Begay.
