FIN 204–01 Table

Advantage Approval Matrix

The Advantage User ID for each approval signature must be included on Form A so Advantage Security can ensure that the person signing has the authority to approve the addition/change/deletion.

When establishing a new agency/org, a New Account Application Form must be completed (see also FIN 202, “Establishment of Agency/Orgs”). The approval signature of the new org manager or higher-level authority is required, along with his or her Advantage User ID.

In the case of a new org manager, the approval signature of the department chair or higher-level authority over the agency/org is required, along with his or her Advantage User ID.

A change in the org signer(s) other than org manager requires the approval signature of the present org manager or higher-level authority, along with his or her Advantage User ID. If the previous org manager will remain an authorized signer on the agency/org(s) and retained, if warranted, on the Advantage security tables, this should be indicated.

Note: For org manager changes on sponsored project agency/orgs, certain additional approvals may be required. Consult your Office for Research and Sponsored Projects Administration site officer.

Procedure

The requesting department completes an Advantage Form A to initiate org manager or authorized signer change(s) and obtains all required approval signatures. If adding, changing, or deleting an org manager and/or authorized signer for an established agency/org, the department forwards the form to the Advantage Security section of Financial Services for updating of Advantage tables.

If establishing an org manager and/or authorized signer(s) for a new agency/org, the department forwards the completed document(s), including all required signatures, to Financial Services.

Financial Services or the Office for Research and Sponsored Projects Administration:

  1. reviews the completed document(s) for completeness and accuracy

  2. updates Advantage tables as deemed necessary

    and

  3. files ASU Advantage Form A document for updating/record retention purposes.
