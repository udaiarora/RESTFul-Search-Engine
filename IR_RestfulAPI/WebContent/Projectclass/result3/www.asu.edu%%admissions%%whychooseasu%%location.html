Top Location

Located in a large growing metropolis in the Grand Canyon state, ASU is a prime location for you to pursue your academic interests and gain access to numerous employment and internship opportunities. You can also explore and experience all the natural wonders that Arizona has to offer.

Metropolitan Phoenix

Phoenix, the fifth largest city in the United States, and the surrounding area provides ASU students with cultural events, internships, shopping, entertainment, and sporting events making ASU an ideal for a university setting. In addition, there are relevant, exciting, and challenging job and career opportunities for students and graduates.

TOP

ASU's Multiple Campuses

Scheduled to open beginning fall 2006, ASU at the Downtown Phoenix campus is currently being established in the heart of downtown Phoenix placing it near the major centers of business and government in this growing metropolitan area.

ASU at the Polytechnic campus is located in Mesa, and it is surrounded by an excellent combination of resorts, museums, and airparks, making it an enriching atmosphere to learn and grow.

ASU at the Tempe campus is just minutes from downtown Phoenix, Scottsdale, and Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport. The campus is adjacent to downtown Tempe's Mill Avenue, home to unique and familiar shops and restaurants. ASU students, as well as visitors in Tempe, can also enjoy the benefits of ASU's Wireless access in Tempe. Tempe has been voted one of the top 10 college towns by the New York Times.

ASU at the West campus is situated in an ideal location in Northwest Phoenix, near a growing area with the Arrowhead Regional Mall, Historic Downtown Glendale, the NFL's Arizona Cardinals new football stadium as well as the Phoenix Coyotes new ice hockey arena.

Arizona

Arizona is a great state to explore. Miles of trails for hiking, biking, and horseback riding are within minutes of Phoenix.

  • The Four Peaks mountain area offers unparalleled hiking, beauty, and exciting river rafting, just an hour away.
  • The Grand Canyon, one of the natural wonders of the world, is approximately four and a half hours from ASU's Tempe Campus.
