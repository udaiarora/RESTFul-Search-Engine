Jenny Mehmedovic
Assistant to the Vice Provost for Information Services
University of Kansas
From Paper to Paperless: Records Management in a Decentralized Environment


Jenny Mehmedovic is Assistant to the Vice Chancellor for Information Services at the University of Kansas. She provides overall coordination of IT policy, ensuring policy development that serves all campus constituencies and maintains currency with new technologies. She provides education to the user community in the areas of access, privacy, security, and intellectual property. She chairs a campus-wide task force addressing records management issues in the digital era and represents the state’s Regents institutions on state-wide committees relating to electronic and web-based records management. She holds a Master of Business Administration and a BA in Anthropology & Spanish.
