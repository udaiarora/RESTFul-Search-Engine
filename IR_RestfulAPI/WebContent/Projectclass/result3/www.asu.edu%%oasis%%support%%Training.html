TRAINING

January 2007

January finds us training those who hire employees and advise students.

To learn how to Hire Employees using Peoplesoft, you can take either the on-line course, the hands-on course, or the demonstration course. They present the same material in three ways. Select the type of training you prefer. You can register for the hands-on sessions or access the on-line courses via the training calendar link to your right.

For Advisors there are three courses - They are NOT the same material.

Intro to PeopleSoft for Advisors will show you where student data resides in PeopleSoft It is STRONGLY recommended as pre-requisite work for Advisor 101.

Advisor 101 lets you practice using the system to find student data and to prep for advising.

Advisor 201 teaches you how to use the new MACs system, how to give permission overrides, how to change a student's program or plan and how to view a student's milestones.
Advisor 101 is a pre-requisite for this class, but you don't have to take them the same day.
Sign up via the training calendar.
