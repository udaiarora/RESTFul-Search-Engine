Please note that the deadline for submissions is November 3, 2006 for this contest.

Arizona State University will sponsor its 18th Annual Martin Luther King, Jr. Celebration on Tuesday, January 30, 2007. A highlight of our breakfast celebration is the recognition and awards ceremony for Arizona K-12 students who are contest winners; entries will be selected on the basis of best depiction of this year's MLK Celebration theme:

"Beyond the Dream: Building Communities through Servant-Leadership"

Download a copy of Martin Luther King, Jr.'s 1963, "I Have a Dream" speech (PDF Format) which can be shared with your staff and students. The ideals expressed in the speech are cross-curricular and can be integrated into many of your ongoing teaching/learning opportunities. Students should be encouraged to read the speech before responding to the theme "Beyond the Dream: Building Communities through Servant-Leadership".

  • Two categories
    • Essay/Poetry - Individual student entries
    • Poster/Bookmark - Individual student entries
  • Awards and Prizes
    • Savings Bonds ($150, $100, or $75) for Essay/Poetry and Poster/Bookmark Awards
  • Entry Forms
    • Official entry forms may be downloaded here:
         Individual Essay/Poetry
         Individual Poster/Bookmark
  • Entry Deadline: November 3, 2006
We would appreciate your sharing this material with your staff and colleagues and look forward to receiving student entries representing your school. Thanks, once again, for your active involvement in a campus tradition - the Annual MLK Celebration at Arizona State University.
