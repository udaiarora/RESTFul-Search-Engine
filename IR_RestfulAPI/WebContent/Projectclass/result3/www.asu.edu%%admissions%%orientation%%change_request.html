ASU Orientation Change Request

ASU Orientation change requests must be submitted to New Student Orientation in writing. You may do this in one of the following ways:

  • Reschedule your date online through ASU Interactive.
  • Submit changes/updates to your ASU Orientation reservation by filling out the online form below and selecting "Submit." (Please be aware that this form will be transmitted via an unsecured transaction. Read the ASU Privacy Statement. If you have concerns about sending your information via an unsecured transaction, you may fax or mail your request.) For an up-to-date list of available programs, log in to ASU Interactive.
  • Fax or mail your request by printing the downloadable .pdf version of the Orientation Change Request Form. Fax or mail the completed form to New Student Orientation.

If you have any further questions, please contact New Student Orientation at 480.965.2880.
